Hotori arrived with the "Misty Tipsy Style" banner on April 29, 2026, instantly shifting the power dynamics of Hethereau’s combat landscape. While her primary role is categorized as a Sub-DPS and Support, her kit includes enough utility to serve as a secondary healer, making her one of the most slot-efficient characters in the game. Her power revolves around the "Non-Closed Timepiece," a specialized energy resource that allows her to record ally actions and replay them during a devastating time-stop window. 

Whether you are looking to maximize your Blossom reaction damage or seeking a way to bypass difficult boss mechanics through pure time manipulation, this guide covers the optimal setups for Hotori.

## Overview

![Overview](/img/hotori-best-team-nte/inline-1.webp)


Hotori is an S-Rank Cosmos character specializing in time manipulation and field utility. Unlike traditional attackers who rely on raw multipliers, Hotori’s value is multiplicative, scaling based on the strength of the teammates she supports.

**Key Strengths:**
*   **Time Stop Mechanic:** Her Ultimate, "World's Tide," pauses the instance timer, allowing for uncontested damage windows during timed challenges like the Hunter Exchange trials.
*   **Skill Recording:** Through "Present Replay," she can double the efficiency of ally Support and Redirect skills, effectively letting your team use their best abilities twice.
*   **Role Compression:** She provides Cosmos DMG, Break reduction, and healing, freeing up slots in your four-person squad for more specialized damage dealers.
*   **Reaction Synergy:** She is the "missing piece" for Blossom-based teams, specifically those utilizing [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte)’s Vita Buds.

The core of playing Hotori effectively is managing her Non-Closed Timepiece energy. This gauge fills as she and her allies perform actions. Once it reaches 60 units, she can activate her recording state; at 120 units, she can freeze time entirely. Mastering this rhythm is the difference between a mid-tier support and an S+ tier goddess of the battlefield. ## Skills

![Skills](/img/hotori-best-team-nte/inline-2.webp)


Hotori’s kit is designed around the concept of "The Echo." She doesn't just fight; she forces the timeline to repeat her team's most successful moments.

| Skill Type | Name | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Attack** | Misty Moon Style | A 5-hit umbrella-katana combo dealing Cosmos DMG. The 'Crescent' plunging attack scales with height. |
| **Esper Skill** | Present Replay | Consumes 60 energy to record ally Support/Redirect skills for 5s. Overwrites previous records on reuse. |
| **Ultimate** | World's Tide | Consumes 120 energy. Stops time for 5s, pausing the timer and replaying all recorded attacks with a Finisher. |
| **Passive 1** | Half Moment | A Critical Riposte triggered by perfect dodges, dealing Cosmos DMG and reducing enemy Break. |
| **Passive 2** | Ghost Orchid Crest | Allows Blossom reactions (Vita Buds) to continue firing during Time Stop for massive damage. |
| **Passive 3** | Time Takes Away | Grants 30% DEF ignore during the Time Stop window (requires Ascension/Awakening). |

### Skill Priority and Usage
When upgrading Hotori’s talents, the priority should always be **Ultimate > Skill > Basic Attack**. 

The **Ultimate (World's Tide)** is her most impactful tool. Because it pauses the actual game timer, it is invaluable in endgame content where "Clear within X seconds" is a requirement. During this 5-second window, character switching is disabled, meaning Hotori must stay on the field. However, any projectiles or "automated" skills (like Nanally's buds) launched before the freeze will continue to hit the target.

**Present Replay** requires careful timing. You should activate this skill immediately before your primary DPS uses their most powerful Support or Redirect ability. Hotori "records" these timings and then executes them simultaneously when she triggers her Ultimate. If you misclick or record a low-impact attack, you can reuse the skill to overwrite the record, provided you have the energy.

## Best Build

![Best Build](/img/hotori-best-team-nte/inline-4.webp)


The goal of a Hotori build is to maximize her "Finisher DMG" and ensure she has the energy regeneration required to keep her Timepiece full. Because her Ultimate allows her to ignore a portion of enemy DEF, stacking Crit and ATK% yields higher returns than on other supports.

### Best Arcs (Weapons)
1.  **Marching Beyond Time (5-Star):** Hotori’s signature weapon. It provides a massive boost to Cosmos DMG and specifically increases the damage of "recorded" attacks during World's Tide. It also generates a small amount of Non-Closed Timepiece energy every time an ally uses a skill.
2.  **Fluff of Fearlessness (4-Star):** A solid secondary option that increases Crit Rate. It helps ensure that her "Half Moment" ripostes and Ultimate finishers consistently land critical hits.
3.  **The Forgotten (4-Star):** A F2P-friendly sword that focuses on energy regeneration. While it lacks the raw power of the signature Arc, it allows for much higher Ultimate uptime.

### Best Disks (Artifacts)
The **Lost Radiance (4pc)** set is currently the undisputed best-in-slot for Hotori. 
*   **2pc Bonus:** Increases Cosmos DMG by 15%.
*   **4pc Bonus:** After using an Esper Skill, the character ignores 20% of the enemy's DEF for 10 seconds. This stacks with Hotori’s "Time Takes Away" passive, allowing her to bypass 50% of a boss's armor during her Ultimate window.

**Recommended Stats:**
*   **Head:** HP (Fixed)
*   **Chest:** ATK% or Crit Rate (Aim for a 1:2 Crit Ratio)
*   **Sub-stats:** Crit DMG > Crit Rate > ATK% > Cosmos DMG.

## Teams

![Teams](/img/hotori-best-team-nte/inline-5.webp)


Hotori thrives in teams that can capitalize on her time-stop window. Since she records ally skills, she performs best with characters who have high-impact, short-duration abilities.

### 1. Ultimate Blossom Burst
This is Hotori's "Premium" team and currently one of the highest-DPS compositions in Neverness to Everness.
*   **Main DPS:** [Nanally](#TODO:keyword:nanally-best-build-nte)
*   **Sub-DPS/CC:** Jiuyuan
*   **Tank/Utility:** Zero
*   **Support:** Hotori

**How it works:**
Nanally creates Vita Buds that trigger Blossom reactions. Normally, these reactions are limited by enemy movement and internal cooldowns. However, Hotori's **Ghost Orchid Crest** passive allows these buds to continue firing while time is frozen. Jiuyuan provides the necessary crowd control to group enemies together, while Zero ensures the team survives long enough to build Hotori’s energy. The rotation involves setting up Nanally’s buds, recording them with Hotori’s "Present Replay," and then unleashing "World's Tide" for a screen-clearing explosion of Cosmos and Blossom damage.

### 2. Cosmos Tri-Cycle
A team focused on elemental resonance and rapid energy cycling.
*   **Main DPS:** Nanally
*   **Energy Battery:** Hathor
*   **Sub-DPS:** Jiuyuan
*   **Support:** Hotori

**How it works:**
By running three Cosmos characters, you maximize the elemental resonance bonuses. Hathor acts as a dedicated battery, feeding energy directly to Hotori to ensure her Non-Closed Timepiece is always full. This team focuses on "Ultimate Spam," keeping the enemy in a near-constant state of slowed or stopped time. Jiuyuan is included here to increase the Blossom cap, allowing Nanally’s reactions to hit even harder during the frozen windows.

### 3. F2P Time Control
A highly accessible team for players who pulled Hotori but are missing other 5-star units.
*   **Main DPS:** Mint
*   **Tank:** Zero
*   **Sub-DPS:** Skia
*   **Support:** Hotori

**How it works:**
Mint provides consistent damage that is easy to record. Skia offers additional off-field pressure that complements Hotori’s Sub-DPS role. While this team lacks the explosive power of the Blossom reaction, it is incredibly safe. The combination of Zero’s shielding and Hotori’s time manipulation makes it very difficult for enemies to land hits on your squad.

## Strategy and Rotations

To get the most out of Hotori, follow this standard combat rotation:
1.  **Build Energy:** Start the fight with your Main DPS and Tank to generate Non-Closed Timepiece energy.
2.  **The Setup:** Once Hotori has 60 energy, switch to her and activate **Present Replay**.
3.  **The Record:** Quickly switch to your DPS (like Nanally) and use their highest-damage Support skill.
4.  **The Freeze:** Switch back to Hotori. Once she has 120 energy, trigger **World's Tide**.
5.  **The Finisher:** During the 5-second freeze, Hotori will automatically replay the recorded skill. Use her basic attack chain to shred the enemy's Break gauge. The "Time Takes Away" passive will ensure your final katana strike deals massive damage.

Hotori is a complex character, but she rewards players who understand the flow of time in Hethereau. By pairing her with the right Arcs and a strong Blossom-focused team, you can trivialise even the most difficult boss encounters in Neverness to Everness.
