# Dr. Ratio Best Team Build HSR Guide
> 📝 **Editorial Note:** This guide is AI-assisted and game data evolves rapidly. Please cross-reference with in-game information. _Updated: 2026-05-13._


Dr. Ratio, also known as Veritas Ratio, is a prominent member of the Intelligentsia Guild who debuted as a free gift to the community during the Version 1.6 update. As an Imaginary-type character following the Path of The Hunt, he excels at dismantling elite bosses and high-health targets through a unique mechanic that triggers follow-up attacks based on the number of debuffs currently affecting the enemy. Unlike traditional Hunt characters who rely solely on direct skill damage, Dr. Ratio’s performance scales exponentially when paired with Nihility or Harmony characters capable of weakening the opposition.

## Dr. Ratio Overview

Dr. Ratio is a 5-star Imaginary character following the Path of The Hunt. His primary identity centers on being a "Follow-up Attack" (FUA) specialist. His kit is designed around the concept of logical deduction; the more "flaws" (debuffs) an enemy has, the more likely Dr. Ratio is to find an opening for a powerful stone pillar strike.

Key points regarding his gameplay include:
*   **Follow-up Mastery:** A significant portion of his total damage output comes from his Talent, "Cogito, Ergo Sum," which triggers after he uses his Skill.
*   **Debuff Dependency:** To guarantee his follow-up attacks, an enemy needs at least three debuffs. Having more debuffs further increases his Crit stats and raw Damage dealt.
*   **Single-Target Precision:** As a Hunt character, he lacks AoE capabilities but possesses some of the highest single-target multipliers in the game, making him a premier choice for Memory of Chaos and Apocalyptic Shadow.
*   **Accessibility:** Because he was distributed for free to all players during Version 1.6, he remains one of the most widely owned and utilized 5-star DPS units in the game.

## Dr. Ratio Skills and Trace Priority

Understanding Dr. Ratio’s skill priority is essential for resource management. His damage is heavily backloaded into his Talent, meaning players should focus their Trace materials there first.

| Skill Type | Name | Effect Summary |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Basic Attack | Mind is Might | Deals Imaginary DMG (100% ATK) to one enemy. |
| Skill | Intellectual Midwifery | Deals Imaginary DMG (150% ATK) and rolls for a Follow-up Attack. |
| Ultimate | Syllogistic Paradox | Deals Imaginary DMG (240% ATK) and applies Wiseman's Folly. |
| Talent | Cogito, Ergo Sum | 40% base chance (+20% per debuff) to launch a Follow-up (270% ATK). |
| Technique | Mold of Idolatry | Creates a dimension that slows enemies and reduces their SPD by 15%. |

### Trace Priority
1.  **Talent (Cogito, Ergo Sum):** This is your primary damage source. Leveling this increases the multiplier of the follow-up attack, which you will be triggering almost every turn.
2.  **Skill (Intellectual Midwifery):** The Skill itself deals respectable damage, but more importantly, it is the trigger for the Talent.
3.  **Ultimate (Syllogistic Paradox):** The Ultimate applies "Wiseman's Folly." When teammates attack an enemy with this mark, Dr. Ratio launches a follow-up attack. This allows him to deal damage even when it isn't his turn.
4.  **Basic Attack:** This should be your lowest priority. Dr. Ratio wants to use his Skill every single turn to maximize his damage and Energy regeneration.

### Major Traces (Ascension Passives)
*   **Summation:** This provides a permanent stack of Crit Rate (2.5%) and Crit DMG (5%) whenever Dr. Ratio uses his Skill on a debuffed target. With 6 stacks, he gains a free 15% Crit Rate and 30% Crit DMG, significantly lowering his relic requirements.
*   **Inference:** Using his Skill has a 100% base chance to reduce the target's Effect RES. This counts as a debuff itself, helping Dr. Ratio self-enable his Talent's success rate.
*   **Deduction:** If a target has 3 or more debuffs, Dr. Ratio deals 10% more DMG per debuff, capping at a 50% DMG increase. This is why reaching the 5-debuff threshold is the "gold standard" for his team builds.

## Dr. Ratio Ascension and Trace Materials

To fully ascend Dr. Ratio and max out his Traces, you will need to farm materials from the Antimatter Legion and specific Stagnant Shadows.

### Ascension Materials
To reach Level 80, you require the following:
*   **Suppressing Edict x65:** Obtained from the Stagnant Shadow: Shape of Puppetry located in the Xianzhou Luofu's Artisanship Commission.
*   **Antimatter Legion Drops:** 15 Thief's Instinct, 15 Usurper's Scheme, and 15 Conqueror's Will.
*   **Credits:** 308,000 for ascension alone (approx. 880,000 total including leveling).

### Trace Materials
Maxing out all Traces requires a significant investment in the Hunt Calyx:
*   **Hunt Materials:** 18 Arrows of the Beast Hunter, 69 Arrows of the Starchaser, and 139 Arrows of the Demon Vanquisher.
*   **Weekly Boss:** 12 Past Evils of the Borehole Planet Disaster (from the "Old Crater" Echo of War).
*   **Tracks of Destiny:** 8 units.

## Best Build for Dr. Ratio: Relics and Light Cones

The best build for Dr. Ratio focuses on capitalizing on the debuffs applied by his teammates. Because his kit provides a large amount of free Crit stats through "Summation," players can focus more on Crit DMG and ATK% in their relic sub-stats.

### Best Light Cones
1.  **Baptism of Pure Thought (5-star Signature):** This is his best-in-slot by a wide margin. It increases Crit DMG and provides additional DMG and DEF ignore based on the number of debuffs on the enemy.
2.  **Worrisome, Blissful (5-star):** Topaz’s signature cone is excellent for Ratio. It provides Crit Rate and Follow-up DMG, and it applies a "Tame" debuff which helps Ratio reach his debuff requirements.
3.  **Cruising in the Stellar Sea (5-star F2P):** Available from Herta’s Store, this is the best free option. At Superimposition 5, it provides a massive 16% Crit Rate (doubled against enemies below 50% HP) and an ATK% buff upon killing an enemy.
4.  **Swordplay (4-star):** A strong choice for single-target encounters. Since Ratio hits the same target multiple times, he can ramp up the DMG stacks quickly.

### Best Relic Sets
*   **4pc Pioneer Diver of Dead Waters:** This set was designed specifically for characters like Dr. Ratio. It increases DMG against debuffed enemies and doubles its Crit bonuses when the wearer inflicts a debuff. Since Ratio's "Inference" trace inflicts a debuff, he can maintain 100% uptime on these buffs.
*   **2pc The Ashblazing Grand Duke + 2pc Pioneer/Wastelander:** A flexible hybrid build if you lack a good 4pc Pioneer set. This focuses on boosting follow-up damage and raw ATK or Imaginary DMG.

### Best Planar Ornaments
*   **Duran, Dynasty of Running Wolves:** The premier choice for follow-up teams. It grants stacks of Follow-up DMG whenever any ally launches a follow-up attack. At 5 stacks, it provides an additional 25% Crit DMG.
*   **Inert Salsotto:** A classic choice that provides 8% Crit Rate and 15% Ultimate/Follow-up DMG. It is easier to farm but slightly weaker than Duran in high-synergy teams.

### Recommended Relic Stats
| Gear Piece | Main Stat Priority |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Body** | Crit DMG (preferred) or Crit Rate |
| **Feet** | SPD (to reach 134 breakpoint) or ATK% |
| **Sphere** | Imaginary DMG Boost |
| **Rope** | ATK% |

**Sub-stat Priority:** Crit DMG > Crit Rate > ATK% > SPD. Note that Dr. Ratio gains up to 15% Crit Rate from his traces, so ensure you do not over-cap on Crit Rate (aim for roughly 70-75% on the stat sheet).

## Best Team Compositions for Dr. Ratio

Dr. Ratio is not a self-sufficient hypercarry in the same way characters like Jingliu might be. He requires teammates who can apply debuffs consistently to ensure his 100% follow-up trigger rate.

### 1. Premium Follow-Up Team (The "IPC" Team)
This is widely considered the strongest team in the game for single-target scenarios.
*   **Main DPS:** Dr. Ratio
*   **Sub-DPS:** Topaz & Numby (Applies "Proof of Debt" debuff, boosts FUA damage)
*   **Support:** Robin (Massive ATK buffs, team-wide Action Advance, extra DMG on every hit)
*   **Sustain:** Aventurine (Applies "Unnerved" debuff, launches FUAs to stack Duran/Robin energy)

In this team, Topaz and Aventurine provide the necessary debuffs for Ratio to trigger his Talent every turn. Robin’s Ultimate allows the entire team to take extra turns, leading to a cascade of follow-up attacks that can delete bosses in a single cycle.

### 2. F2P Debuff Team
For players without limited 5-star supports, this team focuses on shredding enemy defenses.
*   **Main DPS:** Dr. Ratio
*   **Support:** Pela (Equip her with "Resolution Shines As Pearls of Sweat" for extra debuffs)
*   **Support:** Tingyun (Provides Energy for more Ultimates and massive ATK/DMG buffs)
*   **Sustain:** Gallagher (His Ultimate and Enhanced Basic apply two separate debuffs)

Pela is the MVP here, as her AoE DEF shred and the "Ensnared" debuff from her Light Cone provide enough stacks for Ratio to function at peak efficiency.

### 3. Hypercarry Debuff Team
This team focuses on maximizing Ratio's personal damage through elite Nihility support.
*   **Main DPS:** Dr. Ratio
*   **Support:** Silver Wolf (The queen of debuffs; she can apply up to 5-7 debuffs alone)
*   **Support:** Sparkle (Provides Skill Points and massive Crit DMG buffs)
*   **Sustain:** [Fu Xuan](/characters/fu-xuan-best-relic-build-hsr-20260513) (Provides Crit Rate and survivability)

Silver Wolf is the best individual partner for Dr. Ratio when fighting enemies that lack an Imaginary weakness, as she can force the weakness onto them while maintaining a 100% follow-up trigger rate.

## Dr. Ratio Eidolon Guide

While Dr. Ratio is exceptionally strong at E0, his Eidolons provide significant quality-of-life improvements and damage scaling.

*   **E1 (Pride Comes Before a Fall):** Increases the maximum stacks of "Summation" by 4 and provides 4 stacks immediately at the start of battle. This gives Dr. Ratio an extra 10% Crit Rate and 20% Crit DMG (Totaling 25% CR / 50% CD), making him much easier to build.
*   **E2 (Divine Proportion):** When his Talent's follow-up attack hits a target, it deals extra Imaginary DMG equal to 20% of ATK for every debuff the target has (up to 4 debuffs). This adds a flat 80% ATK scaling to every follow-up.
*   **E4 (Blind Sniffing):** When his Talent's follow-up attack is triggered, Dr. Ratio additionally regenerates 15 Energy. This drastically improves his Ultimate uptime, allowing for more "Wiseman's Folly" triggers.
*   **E6 (Vincit Omnia Veritas):** Increases the trigger count of "Wiseman's Folly" by 1 and increases the DMG of his follow-up attacks by 50%. This turns Dr. Ratio into a machine gun of follow-up attacks, often ending fights before the enemy can take a second turn.

For most players, E0 is perfectly sufficient. If you are looking for a stopping point for investment, E1 offers the best value for your Stellar Jades by easing relic requirements.
