## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/how-to-farm-currency-nte-20260518/inline-3.webp)


Managing your wealth in NTE requires navigating a dual-stamina system. **Character Pixels** are used for combat activities like Anomaly Zones to farm gear and EXP, while **City Stamina** is dedicated to urban hobbies and business management to earn Fons.

### Farming Annulith: The Gacha Lifeblood

Annulith is the currency most players will prioritize, as it is the only way to purchase Solid Dice for limited character banners like "The Ichi-daime." 

1.  **Daily Exploration Guide:** Completing four tasks in the Exploration Guide grants 60 Annulith daily. These tasks usually involve simple actions like logging in, spending Character Pixels, or earning a small amount of Fons.
2.  **Anomaly Commissions:** These are weekly combat challenges found in the city. Clearing these provides a bulk sum of 420 Annulith per week.
3.  **Bonds and Achievements:** Reaching Affinity Level milestones with characters and completing urban achievements (like finding secret locations) provide one-time Annulith rewards.
4.  **Nacupeda’s Punishment:** A unique one-time source of Annulith comes from the "Mhm…?" achievement. By choosing "Coins, love 'em, grab some!" at Nacupeda’s Pool, you trigger a punishment that awards a small amount of Annulith.

### Mastering Fons: From Hobbies to Heists

Fons is used for nearly every interaction in Hethereau. While early game Fons may seem abundant, the costs of high-end car parts and property renovations scale quickly.

**City Stamina Efficiency**
Every point of City Stamina is worth roughly 1,000 Fons. You should never let your City Stamina cap out.
*   **Fishing:** Catching and selling fish is the fastest way to dump stamina if you have high-tier bait.
*   **City Delivery:** Driving packages across districts is the most efficient method for players who enjoy exploration, as it also levels up your mailbox for bonus weekly rewards.
*   **Taxi Services:** Providing rides to NPCs is a consistent way to spend stamina while moving between mission objectives.
*   **Owner's Selection:** This minigame allows you to spend 12 stamina every 30 seconds if you use characters with specialized life skills like **Mint**, **Baicang**, and **[Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte)**.

### The Pink Paws Heist: 1 Million Fons Strategy

Unlocked at **City Tycoon Rank 10**, the Pink Paws Heist is a bi-weekly event located at the Pink Paws Bank HQ in the New Herland District. It does not cost City Stamina, making it mandatory for all players.

*   **The Timer:** You have 12 minutes to collect as much loot as possible.
*   **Priority Targets:** Focus on **Golden Enemies** and **Golden Statues**. Standard enemies provide negligible Fons; ignore them to save time.
*   **Extraction:** You must reach a **Red Phone Booth** to extract before the timer hits zero. If you fail to extract, you lose all Fons collected during that run.
*   **Strategy:** At the 2-minute mark, stop looting and begin moving toward the nearest active booth. In the final 90 seconds, booths can close randomly, so always have a backup exit planned.

### Business Management: Cafe by Origen Passive Income

The Cafe by Origen unlocks at **City Tycoon Rank 4**. This system provides passive Fons income that accumulates even when you are offline.

*   **Management Level:** As you collect revenue, your Management Level increases, unlocking more menu slots and the ability to own up to five different cafe locations.
*   **Employee Synergy:** Assigning Espers to the cafe boosts hourly earnings. Characters like **Nanally** and **Sagiri** have specific passive traits that increase customer traffic or reduce ingredient costs.
*   **Restocking:** The cafe can run for up to 72 hours before requiring a manual restock of ingredients like coffee beans and cocoa powder.

### Specialty Farming: Carrota and Mhm! Coins

Specialty currencies are used for character power spikes rather than general spending.

*   **Carrota:** Farmed in the **Rabbit Hole [Anomaly Zone](https://ntecodex.com/faq)** (New Herland District). Bronze, Silver, and Gold Carrota are used in the "Rewind" gacha to obtain Modules for your character's Console. Higher difficulty stages (Hunter Level 30+) are required for Gold Carrota.
*   **Mhm! Coins:** Obtained daily at **Nacupeda’s Pool** in Illusion Town by selecting "Make a Sincere Wish." After collecting 100 Mhm! Coins, you can purchase the S-Class Arc **"Your Happiness is Priceless"** from the exchange shop.
*   **Beetle Coins:** Earned primarily through Tycoon Level milestones (5,000 to 7,500 per level). These are spent in the **Hunter Exchange** to buy Annulith and high-tier upgrade materials.

### Daily and Weekly Farming Checklist

To maximize your income, follow this routine:

**Daily Tasks:**
*   Log in and claim the Event/Login bonus.
*   Complete 4 Exploration Guide tasks (60 Annulith).
*   Visit Nacupeda's Pool for 1 Mhm! Coin.
*   Collect Cafe by Origen profits and restock ingredients.
*   Spend all Character Pixels on Anomaly Zones (Rabbit Hole for Carrota).
*   Spend all City Stamina on Hobbies (Fishing/Delivery).

**Weekly Tasks:**
*   Complete all Anomaly Commissions (420 Annulith).
*   Clear the Pink Paws Heist (Bi-weekly, 1,000,000 Fons cap).
*   Defeat the weekly boss **Mammon** for Fons and upgrade materials.
*   Check the Hunter Exchange for Annulith purchases using Beetle Coins.

### NTE Tycoon Leveling Guide
Your farming efficiency is directly tied to your **City Tycoon Rank**. Raising this rank increases your City Stamina cap, unlocks more Cafe properties, and grants access to the Pink Paws Heist. To level up, you must accumulate total Fons and reach "House Collection" milestones by decorating your properties with furniture.

### Best Arcs for Support and DPS
While farming currency is essential, spending it on the right Arcs (weapons) determines your combat success. The **Arc Research Program** uses Tri-Keys (160 Annulith each) to pull for S-Rank weapons. For F2P players, the Mhm! Coin exchange for the "Your Happiness is Priceless" Arc is the most reliable way to secure a high-tier support weapon without spending Annulith.

### Anomaly Commission Walkthroughs
Anomaly Commissions are dynamic city events that spawn high-risk foes. To clear these quickly for your weekly Annulith, use characters with high "Break" capabilities like **Mint** or **Jiuyuan**. Jiuyuan’s Redirect Skill is particularly effective for clearing groups of "Rainmen" enemies during commissions and heist runs.
