# NTE v1.1 Psyche Resonance Explained: Best Teams & Awakening Guide

In Neverness to Everness (NTE), building a powerful roster involves more than just leveling up your favorite characters; it requires a deep understanding of the Awakening system and how specific elements interact during the Esper Cycle. As of the v1.1 update, which arrived on June 3, 2026, the meta has shifted significantly toward teams that leverage the Resonance bonuses of Psyche characters alongside the newly released Chaos unit, Lacrimosa.

Psyche character Resonance refers to the elite power spikes unlocked at Awakening levels A3 and A6 for characters like Fadia and Haniel. In v1.1, these bonuses specifically highlight the meta-defining synergy between Psyche units and the Chaos element, allowing for devastating reactions that can shred even the toughest anomaly bosses.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/nte-v1-1-psyche-resonance-explained/inline-2.webp)


The term "[Psyche Resonance](https://ntecodex.com/faq)" is often used by the community to describe a two-fold system: the character-exclusive bonuses unlocked at Awakening levels 3 (A3) and 6 (A6), and the elemental synergy between Psyche and Chaos characters. These Resonance nodes transform Psyche units from simple supports into high-utility "Survival" hybrids or potent buffers.

In Version 1.1, these synergies have become the dominant meta due to the release of Lacrimosa, an S-Rank Chaos character who triggers the "Discord" and "Nova" reactions when paired with Psyche supports like Fadia. To maximize these effects, players must collect character duplicates, known as Mind Shards, to unlock the A3 and A6 Resonance tiers. These tiers provide unique passive abilities—such as Fadia's The Unified Saint—that offer team-wide stat boosts or enhanced damage redirection.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/nte-v1-1-psyche-resonance-explained/inline-3.webp)


The combat system in Neverness to Everness revolves around the Esper Cycle, a wheel of six elements where adjacent types react to produce powerful effects. Psyche is a "Bottom Trio" element, meaning it primarily interacts with Chaos (to trigger Nova) and Lakshana (to trigger Stain).

### The Awakening System: Unlocking A3 and A6 Resonance

The Awakening system is the primary method for enhancing a character's base kit. While levels A1, A2, A4, and A5 typically provide standard stat increases or minor skill tweaks, the A3 and A6 levels are designated as "Resonance" nodes. These nodes grant transformative passives that can redefine a character's role in a team.

For Psyche characters, these Resonance bonuses are particularly impactful because they often scale with the team's overall offensive or defensive capabilities. To reach these levels, you must obtain Mind Shards by pulling duplicates of the character from the gacha system.

### Psyche Elemental Synergy: Nova, Stain, and Discord

In the v1.1 update, the "Dreamwalk Corridor" challenges favor teams that can trigger Nova and Discord reactions. These reactions are the backbone of the Psyche meta.

*   **Nova (Psyche + Chaos):** This reaction marks the target for 5 seconds. When the effect expires, the target takes a massive burst of Mental damage. This is ideal for bosses with high physical resistance.
*   **Stain (Psyche + Lakshana):** Triggering Stain increases the Psyche and Lakshana damage taken by the enemy by 20% for 12 seconds. This is the go-to reaction for teams featuring Fadia and Hathor.
*   **Discord (Psyche + Chaos + Incantation):** This is a "Trio Reaction." It occurs when a target is affected by both Nova and Scorch (Chaos + Incantation) simultaneously. Discord deals massive Stagger damage and heavily reduces the enemy's Break gauge, making it essential for breaking elite enemies in the Dreamwalk Corridor.

### Version 1.1 Meta: The Fadia and Lacrimosa Core

The release of Lacrimosa on June 3, 2026, has solidified the Psyche-Chaos synergy. Lacrimosa is a Chaos-element damage dealer who relies on stacking Nightmare debuffs on enemies. When paired with a Psyche support like Fadia, she can trigger Nova reactions consistently.

**Character Spotlight: Fadia**
Fadia remains the premier Psyche unit in v1.1. Her role as a Survival character allows her to redirect 50% to 75% of the damage taken by her allies to herself. At A3, her Curser of Blessings resonance increases her Max HP by 30%, significantly raising her damage-soaking threshold. Her A6 Resonance, The Unified Saint, provides a permanent 10% ATK buff to the party, making her a dual-threat defensive and offensive support.

**Character Spotlight: Haniel**
If you lack Fadia, Haniel is a powerful alternative. Her Resonance focuses on scaling team Crit DMG, which synergizes perfectly with Lacrimosa's high-frequency Chaos attacks.

### How to Farm Mind Shards for Psyche Resonance

Unlocking A3 and A6 Resonance requires a significant investment in Mind Shards. These are primarily obtained through the following methods:

1.  **Gacha Duplicates:** Every time you pull a duplicate of a character you already own, you receive Mind Shards for that specific unit. S-Rank characters like Fadia require more luck (or Fons investment) to reach A6.
2.  **City Tycoon System:** While you cannot directly buy Mind Shards for S-Rank characters here, progressing your businesses in City Tycoon provides the Fons (non-paid currency) needed to perform more pulls on limited banners like Lacrimosa's "Fading Reverie."
3.  **Event Rewards:** Major updates like "Dreamwalk Corridor" often include character selectors or specific Shard rewards in the event shop. Check the Scarborough Fair in-game menu to see the current exchange rates.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the difference between Awakening and Resonance in NTE?
Awakening is the general system of enhancing characters using Mind Shards, spanning levels A1 to A6. Resonance refers specifically to the major power spikes found at the A3 and A6 tiers, which unlock unique passive abilities like Fadia's team-wide ATK buff.

### Is Fadia or Haniel better for a Lacrimosa team?
Fadia is generally considered superior for high-difficulty content because her Survival role and damage redirection keep Lacrimosa alive. However, if you are speed-running and have a high-investment team, Haniel's Crit DMG Resonance can provide higher overall damage output.

### How do I trigger the Discord reaction?
To [trigger Discord](https://ntecodex.com/faq), you must apply both the Nova (Psyche + Chaos) and Scorch (Chaos + Incantation) effects to the same target simultaneously. This requires a three-element team and careful management of your Esper Cycle swaps to ensure both debuffs are active at once.

### Can I get Mind Shards without spending money?
Yes, you can earn Fons, the game's non-paid currency, through the City Tycoon system and daily commissions. These Fons can be used to pull on banners, which provide the Mind Shards needed for Awakening. Additionally, some A-Rank character Shards are available through login rewards and event shops.
