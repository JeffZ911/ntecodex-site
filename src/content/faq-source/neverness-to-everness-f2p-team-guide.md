## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/neverness-to-everness-f2p-team-guide/inline-3.webp)


To master F2P play, you must look beyond character levels and focus on the **Esper Cycle**. NTE combat is designed as a wheel of six elements. You cannot simply pick your four favorite characters and expect to win; they must be neighbors on the elemental wheel to trigger reactions.

### Understanding the Esper Cycle for F2P

The Esper Cycle consists of Cosmos, Anima, Incantation, Chaos, Psyche, and Lakshana. Reactions only occur when you swap between adjacent elements. 

| Reaction Name | Elements Required | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Blossom** | Cosmos + Anima | Spawns autonomous "Lo-bin" turrets that attack enemies. |
| **Hexed** | Anima + Incantation | Deals 20% follow-up damage based on damage taken in 12s. |
| **Nova** | Chaos + Psyche | Deals a massive burst of mental damage after a 5s delay. |
| **Scorch** | Incantation + Chaos | Inflicts a 15-second Damage-over-Time (DoT) burn. |
| **Remora** | Cosmos + Lakshana | Slows enemy movement and attack speed significantly. |
| **Discord (Triple)** | Incant. + Psyche + Chaos | Massive Break Meter shred and stagger potential. |
| **Charge (Triple)** | Lakshana + Cosmos + Anima| Grants 10 Ultimate energy when Blossom turrets hit. |

For F2P players, the **Cosmos-Anima-Incantation** arc of the wheel is the most accessible because the free characters (Zero, Mint, and Adler) sit exactly in those positions.

### Deep Dive: The Top 3 F2P Team Compositions

#### 1. The "Blossom" Starter Squad (Zero, Mint, Haniel, Adler)
*   **Focus:** Sustained AOE Damage.
*   **Rotation:** Start with Zero's Skill -> Swap to Mint (Triggers Blossom) -> Use Mint's Ultimate -> Swap to Adler (Triggers Hexed and Shields) -> Swap to Zero (Refill meters with Ultimate).
*   **Why it works:** This is the most balanced team for exploration. Mint’s AOE clears trash mobs, while Adler’s shields keep you alive against elite anomalies.

#### 2. The "Economy" Carry Team (Chiz, Zero, Aurelia, Haniel)
*   **Focus:** High Single-Target DPS.
*   **Rotation:** Use Haniel's buff -> Zero's Skill -> Swap to Chiz (Main DPS) -> Aurelia (Burst).
*   **Why it works:** Once you unlock Chiz from City Tycoon, she becomes your strongest hitter. She is a Cosmos-element Gas-type user who deals massive damage with her Yokai Hammer. Pairing her with Zero allows for "Double Cosmos" resonance, which increases the crit rate of all Cosmos abilities.

#### 3. The "Discord" Stagger Team (Adler, Aurelia, Sakiri/Baicang)
*   **Focus:** Staggering Bosses.
*   **Rotation:** This team requires using your S-Rank Selector on either **[Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide)** or **Baicang**. 
*   **Why it works:** By combining Incantation (Adler/Sakiri) and Psyche (Aurelia/Haniel), you can trigger the Discord reaction. This is the most efficient way to deplete a boss’s Break Meter, leading to long windows where the enemy is incapacitated and takes 150% increased damage.

### How to Maximize the City Tycoon System for Chiz

Chiz is unique because she cannot be pulled from the gacha. She is exclusively tied to the **City Tycoon** management system. To unlock her and her "Awareness" (duplicates), you must level up your Tycoon Rank by managing properties and completing urban activities.

1.  **Passive Income:** Unlock the "Cafe by Origen" at Tycoon Level 4. Assign characters with high management stats to generate Fons while you are offline.
2.  **City Stamina:** Unlike combat stamina, City Stamina resets weekly. Always spend it on "Races" and "Heists" first, as these provide the highest XP for your Tycoon Level.
3.  **The Chiz Milestone:** You get your first copy of Chiz at Level 18. Her signature Arc (weapon) unlocks at Level 21. By reaching Level 45, you will have enough materials to reach A6 Awakening, making her statistically superior to most S-Rank characters at A0.

### Best F2P Arcs (Weapons) and Gear for Early Game

In the early game, do not waste your Fabricated Dice on the weapon banner. The game provides several "Growth Arcs" through the Hunter Exchange

*   **Zero:** Use the **[Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-24]** or the standard 4-star "Antique Blade."
*   **Chiz:** Her signature Yokai Hammer is free at Tycoon Level 21. Use a generic "Heavy Impact" Arc until then.
*   **Haniel:** Equip any Arc that provides a flat ATK% bonus, as her buff does not care about Crit or Elemental damage.

For gear, focus on the **Solid** set for Zero and the **Gas** set for Chiz. These sets provide a 15% damage bonus to their respective physical states, which is the most consistent way to boost F2P damage before you can farm endgame "Anomalous Echoes."

### S-Rank Selector Strategy: Who to Pick?

After 50 pulls on the Standard Board, you receive an S-Rank Selector. For F2P players, there are two primary choices

1.  **Jiuyuan (Anima):** The best choice for most players. She is a direct upgrade to Mint and has perfect synergy with Zero and Chiz for the Blossom reaction. Her "Control" capabilities allow you to group enemies, making Chiz’s hammer swings much more effective.
2.  **Sakiri (Incantation):** Pick her if you prefer the "Discord" playstyle. She is an elite support buffer who makes Adler much more viable in the late game.

Avoid picking **Daffodil** or **Hathor** as your first selector unless you happen to pull their signature Arcs early; they require specific Chaos or Lakshana teammates that are currently difficult for F2P players to acquire.
