### Understanding the Echo System

Echoes are the "remnants" of Tacet Discords (TDs) that you defeat in the world. When an enemy is defeated, there is a base chance for it to leave behind a spectral image that you can "absorb." Once absorbed, these Echoes can be equipped to your Resonators in five available slots. Each Echo has a "Cost" (1, 3, or 4), and your total equipped cost cannot exceed the limit determined by your Data Bank level (initially 8, increasing to 12 at higher levels).

### The Importance of Echo Rarity and Stats

The rarity of an Echo—ranging from Green (2-star) to Gold (5-star)—dictates its maximum level and the number of sub-stats it can hold. 
*   **Green (2-star):** Max level 10, 2 sub-stats.
*   **Blue (3-star):** Max level 15, 3 sub-stats.
*   **Purple (4-star):** Max level 20, 4 sub-stats.
*   **Gold (5-star):** Max level 25, 5 sub-stats.

Because Gold Echoes offer significantly higher base stats and allow for five "Tuned" sub-stats, your primary goal is to reach Data Bank Level 15 as quickly as possible, as this is the threshold where Gold Echoes begin to drop.

### Maximizing Your Data Bank Level Fast

Your Data Bank level is the single most important factor in Echo farming. It determines your "Base Drop Rate" (the chance an enemy leaves an Echo) and your "Enhanced Drop Rate" (the rarity of that Echo). At Data Bank Level 20, you have a 20% base drop rate and a 100% chance for that drop to be Gold rarity.

To level up fast, focus on the Echo Gallery in your menu. Every time you collect a rarity of an Echo you haven't owned before, you gain Data Bank XP. 
1.  **Hunt New Monsters:** Use the "Detect" function in the Echo Gallery to find monsters you haven't captured at their highest possible rarity.
2.  **Complete Milestones:** Check the Data Bank tasks for additional XP.
3.  **Prioritize Rarity:** If you have a Blue version of a monster, finding the Purple or Gold version will grant a significant XP boost.

### Efficient Overworld Farming Routes

For 1-cost and 3-cost Echoes, certain regions offer much higher density than others. 
*   **Desorock Highland:** This area is dense with 3-cost Echoes like the Roseshroom and Flautist. It is a prime location for farming the Void Thunder and Sun-sinking Eclipse sets.
*   **Central Plains:** Excellent for 1-cost Echoes (like the Tick Tack and Hoarcat) which are essential for filling out the remaining slots of your set bonuses.
*   **Whining Aix's Mire:** A high-level zone with a high concentration of Elite (3-cost) enemies. 

When farming 3-cost Echoes, focus on the "pity" system. The community has observed a hidden mechanic where if you do not receive a drop from a 3-cost enemy, your internal "pity" increases, making a drop guaranteed by the 4th kill of that specific cost class.

### Tacet Fields: Spending Waveplates Wisely

Tacet Fields are specialized combat challenges marked on the map. Each run costs 60 Waveplates. Unlike overworld farming, Tacet Fields guarantee Echoes from specific sets. For example, one Tacet Field might only drop Echoes from the "Molten Rift" and "Sun-sinking Eclipse" sets.

**Reward Scaling by Union Level:**
*   **UL 40:** Guaranteed Purple/Gold Echoes, basic Sealed Tubes and Tuners.
*   **UL 50:** Increased Gold Echo drop rate and more Advanced Sealed Tubes.
*   **UL 60:** Maximum efficiency for Premium Sealed Tubes and Gold Tuners.

Because Waveplates are a limited resource, it is generally recommended to avoid heavy Tacet Field farming until you reach Union Level 40+, where Gold Echoes and Gold Tuners become more common.

### Echo Fusion and Recycling Strategy

The "Data Merge" feature (accessible through the Data Bank menu) allows you to recycle 5 unwanted Echoes to receive 1 or 2 new random Echoes. This is a vital way to "reroll" the trash Echoes you accumulate while farming.
*   **Strategy:** Only merge Echoes that are Purple rarity or lower once you reach Data Bank level 17. 
*   **Outcome:** The rarity of the Echoes produced by Data Merge is determined by your current Data Bank level, not the rarity of the Echoes you put in. You can turn 5 Green Echoes into a Gold Echo if your Data Bank is high enough.

### Boss Farming (4-Cost)

4-cost Echoes come from Overlord and Calamity class bosses (e.g., Thundering Mephis, Crownless, Bell-Borne Geochelone). These bosses have a 2-minute respawn timer.
*   **The 2-Minute Cycle:** You do not need to spend Waveplates to get the Echo drop. You only spend Waveplates if you want the boss's material rewards (e.g., Group Abyssal Core).
*   **Targeting Stats:** Since 4-cost Echoes are the only ones that can roll Crit Rate or Crit Damage as a main stat, you may need to kill these bosses dozens of times. Simply defeat the boss, wait for the remnant to appear, absorb it, and move to the next boss or wait 2 minutes for a respawn.

### Co-op Farming: Doubling Your Efficiency

If you have cleared your map of specific 3-cost enemies (like the Cyan-Feathered Heron), you can join a friend's world. If both players are present when a monster dies, both players can see and absorb their own individual Echo drop. This is the most efficient way to farm 3-cost Echoes with specific elemental damage bonuses, as it bypasses the 24-hour overworld respawn timer.

### How to Level and Tune Your Echoes

Once you have farmed a Gold Echo with the correct main stat (e.g., Aero Damage on a 3-cost Echo for Jiyan), you must level it using Sealed Tubes. 
*   **Sealed Tubes:** These come in various tiers (Basic, Medium, Advanced, Premium) and provide Echo EXP.
*   **Tuning:** Every 5 levels, you can use "Tuners" to reveal a sub-stat. 
*   **Priority Sub-stats:** Most DPS characters look for Crit Rate, Crit Damage, ATK%, and Energy Regen. 
*   **Important:** Do not waste Gold Tuners on Purple Echoes. Gold Tuners are a finite resource and should be saved exclusively for 5-star Echoes with the correct main stats.

### Managing Your Echo Inventory

With an inventory limit of 2,000 Echoes, you will quickly find yourself overwhelmed with "trash" drops. 
*   **Locking:** Immediately lock any 3-cost Echo that has a matching Elemental Damage main stat (e.g., a Void Thunder Echo with Electro DMG bonus). 
*   **Filtering:** Use the filter tool to find Echoes with HP or DEF main stats on DPS sets and mark them for Data Merge. 
*   **Data Merge Efficiency:** As of 2026-05-12, the Data Merge system remains the most effective way to clear inventory while hunting for elusive 3-cost elemental pieces.
