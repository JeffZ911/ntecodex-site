Ganyu remains one of the most powerful Cryo DPS characters in [Genshin Impact](https://ntecodex.com/guides/nte-vs-genshin-impact-graphics-comparison-gear), capable of clearing endgame content like the Spiral Abyss with her devastating Frostflake Arrows. While premium five-star weapons like Amos' Bow or The First Great Magic offer the highest damage ceilings, Ganyu is remarkably friendly to players who do not wish to spend Primogems on the weapon banner.

# What Is the Best F2P Bow for Ganyu in Genshin Impact?

Selecting the right weapon for Ganyu involves balancing her massive ATK requirements with the specific needs of her playstyle. Because Ganyu relies heavily on her Level 2 Charged Attacks, the best free-to-play (F2P) options are those that provide significant buffs to Charged Attack damage or massive ATK% percentages to fuel her Frostflake Blooms.

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/best-f2p-bow-for-ganyu-genshin/inline-1.webp)


When looking for the best F2P bow for Ganyu, players generally aim to maximize her personal damage without relying on gacha-exclusive five-star pulls. The selection process involves evaluating weapons that can be obtained through blacksmith forging, in-game shops, or as rewards for exploration.

### Identifying the most effective free-to-play (F2P) weapon options for Ganyu
The primary contenders for Ganyu's F2P slot are the Prototype Crescent, Hamayumi, and Song of Stillness. Each of these weapons is craftable, meaning players can eventually reach Refinement Rank 5 (R5) simply by playing the game and collecting weapon billets. Additionally, the Blackcliff Warbow serves as a high-value "stat stick" for those with enough Masterless Starglitter, while the 3-star Sharpshooter's Oath provides a surprising amount of power for early-game accounts.

### Understanding how Ganyu's unique Charged Attack mechanics influence weapon choice
Ganyu’s damage is split between the initial arrow hit and the "Bloom" that occurs shortly after. This Bloom scales with her total ATK and benefit from Cryo DMG bonuses. Because the Bloom is considered Charged Attack damage, weapons that specifically buff this category are highly valued. Furthermore, Ganyu's ability to hit "weak points" (headshots on humanoid enemies) makes certain weapon passives significantly more powerful than they appear on paper.

### Evaluating craftable bows versus shop-purchasable options like the Blackcliff Warbow
Craftable bows are generally preferred because they do not cost "pull currency." However, the Blackcliff Warbow offers Crit DMG as a secondary stat, which can make balancing Ganyu's artifact stats much easier. The trade-off is the cost: 24 Masterless Starglitter is equivalent to nearly five Intertwined Fates, making it a "budget" choice rather than a strictly free one.

### Considering the impact of different playstyles (Melt vs. Freeze) on weapon rankings
Weapon rankings for Ganyu shift depending on her team composition. In a Freeze team (often called "Morgana"), Ganyu uses her Elemental Burst frequently, making weapons that don't penalize energy usage more attractive. In a Melt team, Ganyu often ignores her Burst to ensure she doesn't steal the Pyro aura from her targets, allowing her to utilize weapons like the Hamayumi to their full potential.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/best-f2p-bow-for-ganyu-genshin/inline-2.webp)


The "best" F2P bow for Ganyu depends on your ability to aim and your specific team composition. However, for the majority of players, the **Prototype Crescent** is the overall best F2P bow if you can consistently hit weak points. If you prefer a more consistent experience or play Melt Ganyu, the **Hamayumi** is the superior choice.

### Quick Summary: The Top 3 Picks

| Weapon | Primary Stat | Best Use Case | Passive Benefit (at R5) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Prototype Crescent** | ATK% | Freeze Teams / Skilled Aimers | +72% ATK for 10s after hitting a weak point. |
| **Hamayumi** | ATK% | Melt Teams / General Use | +24% Charged ATK DMG (doubled to 48% at 100% Energy). |
| **Song of Stillness** | ATK% | Teams with a Healer | +32% DMG for 8s after being healed. |

### Which Bow Should You Craft First?

If you are a new player or an mobile player who finds aiming at heads difficult, **Song of Stillness** is currently the most recommended starting point. It is the Fontaine craftable bow and offers a very generic, easy-to-trigger damage buff as long as you have a healer like Bennett, Diona, or Kokomi on the team.

If you are confident in your aiming skills and are fighting humanoid enemies or Hilichurls, **Prototype Crescent** offers the highest theoretical damage. For players focusing strictly on a Melt Ganyu build (using Xiangling and Bennett), **Hamayumi** is the gold standard of craftable weapons because it synergizes with the "no-Burst" playstyle of Melt teams.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/best-f2p-bow-for-ganyu-genshin/inline-3.webp)


The effectiveness of Ganyu's weapons is tied closely to her artifact sets and the specific enemies she faces. Below is a breakdown of why these specific bows rank where they do.

### Prototype Crescent: The High-Skill Ceiling Choice
The Prototype Crescent has been a staple for Ganyu since the game's launch. Its passive, "Unreturning," grants a massive 36% ATK buff at R1 and a staggering 72% ATK buff at R5 when a Charged Attack hits a weak point.

*   **Pros:** Highest potential ATK buff of any F2P weapon. The buff snapshots, meaning if you use Ganyu's Burst while the buff is active, the Burst will maintain that extra damage for its entire duration.
*   **Cons:** Useless against enemies without weak points. If you cannot trigger the passive, the weapon becomes a basic stat stick with low value.

### Hamayumi: The King of Melt Ganyu
Introduced with the Inazuma region, the Hamayumi is designed for characters who rely on Normal and Charged Attacks. Its passive, "Full Draw," increases Charged Attack DMG by 12% (R1) or 24% (R5). This effect is doubled when the character's Energy is at 100%.

*   **Melt Synergy:** In Melt teams, Ganyu's Burst can actually lower your total damage by consuming the Pyro aura applied by Bennett or Xiangling. Because you rarely use her Burst in these teams, Ganyu stays at 100% Energy easily, maintaining the 48% DMG buff (at R5) indefinitely.
*   **Freeze Anti-Synergy:** In Freeze teams, Ganyu's Burst is a massive part of her total DPS. Using the Burst drops your Energy to zero, cutting the Hamayumi's effectiveness in half.

### Song of Stillness: The New Fontaine Contender
Song of Stillness is often overlooked but serves as a middle ground between the Prototype Crescent and Hamayumi. Its passive triggers when the wielder is healed, granting a flat DMG% increase.

*   **Consistency:** Unlike the Prototype Crescent, it works against all enemies (including bosses). Unlike the Hamayumi, it doesn't care about your Energy levels.
*   **Requirement:** You must have a healer on the team. Fortunately, most Ganyu teams include Bennett (Melt), Diona (Freeze/Shield), or Kokomi (Freeze), making this requirement easy to meet.

### Blackcliff Warbow: The Starglitter Alternative
The Blackcliff Warbow is available in the Paimon's Bargains shop on a rotating monthly basis. It is the only F2P-accessible bow that provides Crit DMG as a substat.

*   **When to buy:** Buy this if your artifact luck has been poor and you cannot reach a 1:2 Crit Rate to Crit DMG ratio.
*   **The Passive Problem:** Its passive requires you to kill an enemy to gain an ATK buff. This makes it excellent for "mobbing" (fighting many small enemies) but effectively useless against single-target bosses in the Spiral Abyss.

### Melt Ganyu vs. Freeze Ganyu: Choosing the Right Bow
The interaction between your bow and your artifact set is crucial.

1.  **Freeze Ganyu (Blizzard Strayer):** Since Blizzard Strayer provides massive amounts of Crit Rate, you should focus on weapons with ATK% or Crit DMG substats. Prototype Crescent is usually the winner here because the Freeze team relies on Ganyu's Burst, which benefits from the Prototype's massive ATK buff.
2.  **Melt Ganyu (Wanderer's Troupe / Shimenawa):** These sets focus entirely on Charged Attack DMG. Hamayumi is the preferred choice here because it stacks even more Charged Attack DMG bonuses, which are multiplicative with the Melt reaction's high damage floor.

### Refinement Value: R1 vs. R5
For craftable weapons, refinements are everything. A Prototype Crescent at R1 is significantly weaker than a 5-star weapon. However, at R5, it can rival or even outperform some R1 5-star bows in specific scenarios.

| Weapon | R1 Passive Buff | R5 Passive Buff | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Prototype Crescent | 36% ATK | 72% ATK | High (Huge jump) |
| Hamayumi | 12% (24%) CA DMG | 24% (48%) CA DMG | High (Doubles value) |
| Song of Stillness | 16% DMG | 32% DMG | Medium |
| Blackcliff Warbow | 12% ATK per stack | 24% ATK per stack | Low (Too expensive) |

### Weak Point Mechanics: The Prototype Crescent's Weakness
The biggest drawback of the Prototype Crescent is that many of the game's toughest enemies do not have weak points. If an enemy lacks a weak point, you cannot trigger the 72% ATK buff.

**Enemies/Bosses without Weak Points:**
*   **Slimes:** All varieties.
*   **Specters:** All varieties.
*   **Elemental Constructs:** Hypostases (Anemo, Electro, etc.).
*   **Weekly Bosses:** Andrius (Wolf of the North), Dvalin (while on the platform), Azhdaha, and the All-Devouring Narwhal.
*   **World Bosses:** Golden Wolflord, Thunder Manifestation, and most mechanical constructs like the Perpetual Mechanical Array.

If you find yourself frequently fighting these enemies, the Hamayumi or Song of Stillness will provide much more consistent results.

### How to Farm Northlander and Midlander Bow Billets
To craft these weapons, you need Bow Billets. These are rare drops from Weekly Bosses (Trounce Domains).
*   **Northlander Billets:** Drop from Mondstadt, Liyue, and Inazuma bosses (Dvalin, Andrius, Childe, Azhdaha, Signora).
*   **Midlander Billets:** Drop from Sumeru and Fontaine bosses (Scaramouche, Apep, Narwhal, Arlecchino).
*   **Conversion:** You can use the Alchemy Bench to convert a Midlander Billet into a Northlander Billet (or vice versa) using two Dream Solvents. Dream Solvents are also random drops from Weekly Bosses.

![Related](/img/best-f2p-bow-for-ganyu-genshin/inline-4.webp)


Maximizing Ganyu requires more than just a good bow. You must also align her artifacts and team members to support her specific weapon choice.

### Best Artifacts to Pair with F2P Bows
*   **Blizzard Strayer (4-Piece):** The best set for Freeze Ganyu. It provides 15% Cryo DMG and up to 40% Crit Rate against frozen enemies. This allows you to use an ATK% bow like Prototype Crescent without worrying about low Crit Rate.
*   **Wanderer's Troupe (4-Piece):** The standard for Melt Ganyu. It provides 80 Elemental Mastery and 35% Charged Attack DMG. It pairs perfectly with the Hamayumi.
*   **Shimenawa's Reminiscence (4-Piece):** An alternative for Melt Ganyu. It provides 50% Charged Attack DMG at the cost of 15 Energy. **Note:** Do not use this with Hamayumi, as the energy drain will prevent you from getting the Hamayumi’s double damage bonus.

### Top F2P Team Compositions for Ganyu
1.  **F2P Freeze:** Ganyu, Sucrose (Crowd Control), Barbara (Free Hydro application/Healing), and Rosaria or Kaeya (Cryo Resonance).
2.  **F2P Melt:** Ganyu, Xiangling (Pyro application), Bennett (ATK buff/Healing), and Lynette or Shielder (like Layla or Noelle).

### Ganyu Talent Leveling Guide
To get the most out of your F2P bow, you must prioritize your talent levels correctly.
1.  **Normal Attack: Liutian Archery:** This is Ganyu's most important talent. It increases the damage of her Frostflake Arrows and the resulting Blooms. Level this to 10 (Crown) first.
2.  **Celestial Shower (Burst):** Vital for Freeze teams. Level this to 8 or 9.
3.  **Trail of the Qilin (Skill):** This is your lowest priority. It provides a taunt and some energy particle generation, but the damage is secondary. Level this to 6 and leave it until the others are finished.

### Comparison with 5-Star Bows
While F2P bows are excellent, you may eventually pull a 5-star weapon.
*   **Amos' Bow:** This is Ganyu's "signature" standard weapon. It is significantly better than any F2P option due to its massive Charged Attack DMG scaling based on arrow flight time.
*   **Aqua Simulacra:** Provides massive Crit DMG. It is better than Blackcliff Warbow in every scenario but requires you to be closer to enemies.
*   **The First Great Magic:** A newer 5-star that rivals Amos' Bow, providing high ATK and Charged Attack DMG buffs.

If you obtain any of these three, they should generally replace your craftable F2P bow immediately. However, until that day comes, an R5 Prototype Crescent or Hamayumi will serve you exceptionally well in all of [Genshin Impact](https://ntecodex.com/guides/nte-vs-genshin-impact-graphics-comparison-gear)'s challenges.
