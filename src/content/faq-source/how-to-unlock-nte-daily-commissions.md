To maximize your account progression in Neverness to Everness (NTE), establishing a consistent daily routine is the most effective way to secure a steady stream of Annulith and Hunter EXP.

# How to Unlock Daily Commissions in Neverness to Everness (NTE)

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/how-to-unlock-nte-daily-commissions/inline-1.webp)


In Neverness to Everness, players often look for the "Daily Commission" system common in other urban open-world RPGs to begin their daily resource grind. However, NTE uses specific terminology that can be confusing for newcomers. The system most players are looking for is officially known as **Participation Tasks**, which are housed within the **Exploration Guide**. 

These tasks serve as the primary engine for account progression. By completing small, repeatable objectives each day, you earn the points necessary to claim high-value rewards like Annulith (premium currency) and significant amounts of Hunter EXP. Because Hunter Level gates almost every major feature in Hethereau—including character level caps and advanced City Tycoon mechanics—unlocking these dailies early is a top priority.

### What are Daily Commissions in NTE?
Daily Commissions in NTE are referred to as Participation Tasks. Unlike traditional side quests that require manual pickup from NPCs, these tasks are objective-based and track your activity automatically as you play. Whether you are spending Fons at a local shop, defeating anomalies in the streets, or managing your businesses, these actions contribute toward your daily completion goal.

### When do you first encounter the Daily Quest system?
You will first see references to the Exploration Guide and Participation Tasks during the early tutorial phases in the Hesperia district. However, the menu remains locked until you prove your capability as a Hunter through the initial story arc. Most players will notice the "70" icon on their HUD early on, but interacting with it will provide a prompt stating that the system is currently unavailable.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/how-to-unlock-nte-daily-commissions/inline-2.webp)


To unlock Daily Commissions (Participation Tasks) in Neverness to Everness, you must complete the main story quest **Prologue - Part 2**. Specifically, you must finish the **"Film Anomaly"** questline, which involves investigating supernatural occurrences within the Hesperia and Bridge Crossings districts.

Once this quest is cleared, the Exploration Guide becomes fully accessible via the in-game menu or by clicking the stylized "70" icon on the top-right of your screen. While there is no hard Hunter Level requirement to unlock the system, the natural progression of the story typically places players between **Hunter Level 12 and 15** by the time they finish the necessary prologue steps.

| Requirement | Detail |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Main Story Progress** | Complete Prologue - Part 2 ("Film Anomaly" questline) |
| **Menu Location** | Exploration Guide (Stylized '70' Icon) |
| **Daily Reset Time** | 5:00 AM (Server Time) |
| **Primary Rewards** | Annulith, Hunter EXP, Fons, Beetle Coins |

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/how-to-unlock-nte-daily-commissions/inline-3.webp)


Unlocking and completing your dailies is not just about the immediate rewards; it is about the long-term efficiency of your Character Pixels and City Energy. Understanding the nuances of the Participation system will help you clear your checklist in less than fifteen minutes.

### The Prologue - Part 2 Walkthrough
The path to unlocking dailies begins after your first encounter with the antique shop crew. The "Film Anomaly" questline is a multi-stage investigation that introduces you to the "Urban Weird" atmosphere of Hethereau.

1.  **The Eibon Meeting:** Speak with Adler at the Eibon antique shop to receive the investigation details.
2.  **Moby-Dick Bookstore:** Teleport to Hankaku Plaza and meet with Leila. This stage involves detective work to identify the source of a shifting spatial anomaly.
3.  **The Eden Apartment Investigation:** Head to the Eden Apartment building and take the elevator to the 4th floor. You will need to investigate clues (a laptop and specific books) to trigger the appearance of a blue bird anomaly.
4.  **Wall Green Slope:** Follow the quest markers to the Bridge Crossings district. You must navigate the wooded rock paths of Wall Green Slope to find the "Gold Doors," which serve as the entrance to the anomaly zone.
5.  **Defeating Morphix:** The quest culminates in a combat encounter against the boss, Morphix. Once Morphix is defeated and the anomaly is stabilized, the game will trigger a tutorial sequence for the Exploration Guide, officially unlocking your Participation Tasks.

### Understanding the Participation Points System
The Participation system operates on a point-scaling mechanic. Every day, you are presented with a list of tasks that each grant a specific amount of Participation Points. Your goal is to reach **100 Participation Points** to unlock all five reward chests for the day.

The rewards are distributed at 20-point intervals (20, 40, 60, 80, and 100). These chests contain:
*   **Annulith:** The essential currency for pulling on banners like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte)’s "The Ichi-daime."
*   **Beetle Coins:** Used for specific shop exchanges.
*   **Dreamless Seeds:** A material required for certain character and Arc enhancements.
*   **Hunter EXP:** The primary way to increase your account level.

### Daily Task Breakdown: EXP and Fons
The Exploration Guide typically features four core objectives that provide the bulk of your points and Hunter EXP. Clearing all four core tasks awards a total of 1,000 Hunter EXP.

| Task Name | Requirement | Hunter EXP Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Daily Login** | Log into the game after reset | 200 EXP |
| **Fons Accumulation** | Earn 10,000 Fons from any source | 200 EXP |
| **Pixel Consumption** | Spend Character Pixels in Anomaly Zones | 300 EXP |
| **Activity Goal** | Reach 100 Participation Points | 300 EXP |

Beyond these core tasks, you can also gain points by defeating open-world enemies, using a character's Ultimate ability, or restocking ingredients in your cafe.

### Managing Character Pixels for Daily Efficiency
**Character Pixels** are the primary stamina resource in NTE, capped at **240 Pixels**. They regenerate at a rate of **1 Pixel every 6 minutes**, meaning it takes exactly 24 hours to go from zero to a full cap. 

Since one of the main daily tasks requires consuming Pixels, you should always spend them on Anomaly Zone dungeons (like Houdini's Magic Stage or the Bubble Can Factory) before they hit the cap. Spending 80 Pixels on a double-reward run is the fastest way to satisfy the daily requirement while farming the materials needed to upgrade your characters' Arcs.

### NTE Daily Reset Times by Region
Neverness to Everness operates on a fixed server reset schedule. All daily tasks, login rewards, and participation chests refresh at **5:00 AM server time**. It is important to note that the reset is tied to the server's clock, not your local device time.

| Server Region | Time Zone Baseline | Reset Time (Local) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Asia** | UTC +8 | 5:00 AM |
| **Europe** | UTC +1 | 5:00 AM (WET) / 6:00 AM (CET) |
| **America** | UTC -5 | 5:00 AM (EST) / 2:00 AM (PST) |

## Related

![Related](/img/how-to-unlock-nte-daily-commissions/inline-4.webp)


While Participation Tasks are the formal "commissions" of NTE, a complete daily routine involves several other unmarked activities found throughout Hethereau. These provide unique currencies and combat buffs that cannot be obtained through the Exploration Guide.

### Nacupeda’s Pool: The Daily Wish Mechanic
Located along **Davidia Avenue** in the southwest section of Illusion Town (near the Bridge Crossing), Nacupeda’s Pool offers a daily interaction that is vital for long-term Arc progression. 

When interacting with the pool, you are given two choices. You should always select **"Make a Sincere Wish."** Doing so rewards you with **Mhm! Coins** and a random "Nacupeda’s Blessing" (which can occasionally grant a gold capsule for the DSD POP gacha). Accumulating 100 Mhm! Coins allows you to purchase the S-Rank Arc **"Your Happiness is Priceless"** from the Arc Shop. Selecting the "grabby" option instead will result in common furniture or low-value items like lettuce.

### The Witch’s House: Fortune Reading Rewards
The Witch’s House is located in a park on **Fox Window Street**, southwest of the Hankaku Street Wertheimer Tower. Inside, you can speak to the NPC Blackbird to receive one of three daily divinations.

| Fortune Type | Effect / Reward |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Bless** | Provides a significant combat buff (e.g., +20% Crit DMG) to all characters for the day. |
| **Treasure Hunt** | Marks the exact location of an undiscovered Oracle Stone on your map. |
| **Lost Tales** | Identifies a hidden quest location; completing the quest grants Hunter EXP and achievements. |

### Cafe Management and Passive Income
Once you reach **City Tycoon Level 4**, you unlock **The Cafe by Origen**. This is your primary source of passive Fons income. A standard daily routine should include collecting your accumulated earnings and restocking ingredients. Upgrading the cafe and researching new recipes increases the Fons-per-hour rate, which helps satisfy the "Earn 10,000 Fons" daily Participation Task without any active effort.

### Daily Character Bond and Affection Gifts
Increasing your Bond with characters like Haniel or Chiz unlocks unique story chapters and combat benefits. You are limited to **10 gifts per day** across your entire roster, with a maximum of **3 gifts per individual character**. Since this limit does not roll over, you should give gifts daily to your primary team members to maximize Affection gains.

### Weekly Resets: Anomaly Pilgrimage and Realm of Greed
While most tasks are daily, two high-priority activities reset every Monday at 5:00 AM:
*   **Anomaly Pilgrimage:** These are weekly boss challenges that provide the rarest materials for high-level Arc ascension.
*   **Realm of Greed:** Accessed via the Wiener Apartment by interacting with **Blind Mammon**. This combat challenge is one of the best sources for large sums of Fons.

### The Ultimate NTE Daily Routine Checklist
To ensure you never miss a resource, follow this checklist every time you log in:
1.  **Claim Login Rewards:** Open the "Events" tab in your in-game phone.
2.  **Check the Exploration Guide:** Review your Participation Tasks for the day.
3.  **Spend Character Pixels:** Clear Anomaly Zones to prevent stamina capping.
4.  **Visit Nacupeda’s Pool:** Make a "Sincere Wish" for Mhm! Coins.
5.  **Visit The Witch’s House:** Get a "Bless" buff or "Treasure Hunt" location.
6.  **Manage the Cafe:** Collect Fons and restock the menu.
7.  **Give Affection Gifts:** Use your 10 daily gift slots on your favorite characters.
8.  **Spend City Energy:** Use any remaining City Energy on Hethereau Hobbies like street racing to earn extra Annulith.
