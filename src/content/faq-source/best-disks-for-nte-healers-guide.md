In Neverness to Everness, the equipment system is centered around the Console. Players slot in Cartridges (the "disks") into specific Module slots. These Cartridges come in different "Types" (I, II, and III), and matching these types to your character's Console layout is vital for activating set bonuses. A common mistake among new players is ignoring the Module Type requirements, which can prevent a 4-piece set bonus from triggering even if four pieces are equipped.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/best-disks-for-nte-healers-guide/inline-3.webp)


The effectiveness of a healer is not just determined by their base kit but by the synergy between their Cartridges and their specific ability scaling. Below is a deep dive into the mechanics of the top sets and how to build the current roster of healers.

### Thea's Night Tavern: The Gold Standard for Medics
Thea's Night Tavern is found in the "Midnight Urban" domain. Its synergy is straightforward: 
*   **2-Piece Effect:** HP +10%.
*   **4-Piece Effect:** Healing Bonus +20%.

Most healing abilities in NTE, such as Edgar's "Wild Current," scale directly with the caster's Max HP. By providing both raw HP and a final Healing Bonus multiplier, this set offers the highest effective healing per second (HPS) in the game. It is particularly effective on characters who lack consistent "Burst" healing and instead rely on "Healing Over Time" (HOT) effects.

### Speedy Hedgehog: Trading Raw Healing for Team Damage
Speedy Hedgehog is the preferred set for endgame "cycle" teams. 
*   **2-Piece Effect:** Charge Efficiency +12%.
*   **4-Piece Effect:** After using an Ultimate, all team members gain a 15% ATK buff for 12 seconds.

In a game where Ultimates provide significant I-frames (invincibility frames) and utility, having 12% extra Charge Efficiency is massive. When equipped on a unit like Haniel, who has a low-cost Ultimate, you can maintain near 100% uptime on the 15% ATK buff. This makes Speedy Hedgehog a "force multiplier" rather than just a defensive tool.

### Tiny Big Adventure: The HP-Scaling Powerhouse
This set is often overlooked because it does not mention "Healing" in its description. However, for Psyche-element healers:
*   **2-Piece Effect:** HP +10%.
*   **4-Piece Effect:** Increases Max HP by an additional [information unavailable]% based on the character's Break Intensity.

For Fadia, whose healing is a secondary effect of her damage redirection, having the largest possible HP pool is more beneficial than a Healing Bonus. If Fadia dies, the team loses its primary defensive layer, making Tiny Big Adventure the safer bet for high-difficulty content.

### Optimizing the Console: Module Types and Bonuses
The Console system in NTE uses three Module Types: Type I, Type II, and Type III. 
*   **Type II Modules** typically focus on defensive stats (HP, DEF).
*   **Type III Modules** are the rarest and offer the highest base stat rolls for HP% and Healing Bonus.

To trigger the 60% Max HP Console bonus—a hidden milestone that significantly boosts a healer's effectiveness—you must equip at least two Type III Modules with HP% as the main stat. This is often referred to by the community as "reaching the health cap."

### Edgar Build Guide: The Dedicated Healer
Edgar is a Cosmos-element healer who excels at sustained party-wide recovery.
*   **Primary Skill:** 'Wild Current' — Creates a field that heals based on Edgar's Max HP.
*   **Ultimate:** 'Finnegan's Wake' — A massive burst heal that also cleanses debuffs.
*   **Recommended Set:** 4-piece Thea's Night Tavern.
*   **Stat Priority:** HP% Main Stat on Type II and Type III Modules. Look for Charge Efficiency sub-stats to keep 'Finnegan's Wake' available for emergencies.

### Jiuyuan Build Guide: The Hybrid Sub-DPS Healer
Jiuyuan is an Anima-element unit who bridges the gap between damage and support.
*   **A1 Synergy:** At Resonance Level 1 (A1), Jiuyuan gains a damage boost based on her Healing Bonus.
*   **Recommended Set:** 4-piece Thea's Night Tavern (for pure healing) or 4-piece Fireflies and the Forest (for hybrid DPS).
*   **Stat Priority:** ATK% Main Stat with Healing Bonus on the Type III Module. This allows her to contribute to the Blossom reaction while keeping the team healthy.

### Fadia Build Guide: The Survival Specialist
Fadia is a Psyche-element unit who functions more like a "Paladin."
*   **Mechanic:** She redirects 30% of damage taken by allies to herself.
*   **Recommended Set:** 4-piece Tiny Big Adventure.
*   **Stat Priority:** Pure HP%. Because she takes damage constantly, she needs the highest possible health pool to avoid getting one-shot by boss mechanics.

### Haniel Build Guide: The Universal Support
Haniel is the most versatile support in the Psyche element.
*   **Mechanic:** Rapid Ultimate cycling and energy battery for the team.
*   **Recommended Set:** 4-piece Speedy Hedgehog.
*   **Stat Priority:** Charge Efficiency is the only stat that matters here. Aim for at least 160% Charge Efficiency to ensure she can trigger the Speedy Hedgehog buff every rotation.

### Main Stat and Sub-Stat Optimization Strategy
When farming Cartridges, prioritize the following main stats for your healer's Console:
1.  **Module Type I:** Always Flat HP (Fixed).
2.  **Module Type II:** HP% (Primary) or Charge Efficiency (if using Speedy Hedgehog).
3.  **Module Type III:** Healing Bonus% (Primary) or HP%.

For sub-stats, focus on:
*   **Tier 1:** HP%, Charge Efficiency.
*   **Tier 2:** Flat HP, Break Intensity.
