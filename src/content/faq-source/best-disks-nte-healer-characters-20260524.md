### Defining Healers and Sustain in NTE

In Neverness to Everness, "sustain" is a broad category that includes dedicated healers, hybrid damage dealers with healing capabilities, and tanks that use damage redirection or shielding. Unlike traditional RPGs where a healer only heals, NTE healers often contribute to the Esper Cycle—the game's elemental reaction system—by applying Cosmos or Anima status effects while keeping the party's HP bars full.

### The Role of Disks (Cartridges) in Character Building

Disks, officially known in-game as Cartridges, are the primary equipment system for customizing character stats. Each character has a specific Module layout where these disks are slotted. Set bonuses are triggered by equipping 2 or 4 disks of the same series. For healers, these bonuses typically focus on Outgoing Healing, HP, ATK (for scaling healers), and Charge Efficiency.

### Understanding Module Layouts for Set Bonuses

Every character in NTE has a unique Module grid. Some characters have a "Linear" layout, making it easy to slot in 4-piece sets, while others have "Fragmented" layouts that favor 2-piece/2-piece combinations.

For healers, always check the **Central Module** slot. This slot often provides a multiplier to the 4-piece set bonus of whatever disk is equipped there. If you are using Speedy Hedgehog on Edgar, ensure the highest-rarity disk is in the Central Module to maximize the Charge Efficiency gain.

### Optimal Sub-stats for Endgame Healer Disks

Once you have your 4-piece set, you must optimize the sub-stats through the enhancement system. For healers, the priority list is generally consistent across the board:
1.  **Charge Efficiency:** Essential for keeping healing Ultimates available.
2.  **Scaling Stat (ATK% for Edgar, HP% for Fadia):** Increases the raw healing/shielding numbers.
3.  **Flat HP:** Provides a safety net for the healer themselves.
4.  **Resistance:** Reduces the chance of the healer being stunned or interrupted during a casting animation.

### The Esper Cycle Synergy

Healers are not just for survival; they are catalysts for the **Esper Cycle**.
*   **Blossom (Anima + Cosmos):** This reaction creates a field that deals continuous damage and reduces enemy resistance. If you run Jiuyuan (Anima) and Edgar (Cosmos), your healing actions are simultaneously shredding enemy defenses.
*   **Charge (Anima + Cosmos + Lakshana):** This is the premier energy-generation reaction. Having a healer with high Charge Efficiency (from Speedy Hedgehog) ensures this cycle never breaks, providing infinite energy for your main DPS.

### Healing vs. Shielding: Which is Better?

In the current endgame mode, **Beyond the Rails**, shielding is generally slightly better than pure healing for the first 5 floors. This is because many enemies in the early stages have "One-Shot" mechanics that can bypass a healer's periodic ticks. For the most difficult content, a "Dual Sustain" approach is recommended: use Fadia for her shields and damage redirection, and Edgar for his raw healing to cleanse the Corrosion ticks.
