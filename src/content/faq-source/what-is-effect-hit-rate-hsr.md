Whether it is Pela’s defense shred, Kafka’s Shock, or [March 7th](https://ntecodex.com/guides/best-gear-for-long-session-material-grinding)’s Freeze, EHR acts as the primary tool to overcome an enemy's natural defenses against debuffs.

Understanding EHR is critical for optimizing team compositions that rely on Damage over Time (DoT), Defense reduction, or Crowd Control (CC). For characters like Black Swan or Jiaoqiu, EHR is not just a utility stat—it is directly tied to their damage output through specific passive traces.

### Why EHR Matters for Your Build

In Honkai: Star Rail, combat efficiency is often measured by how quickly you can dismantle an enemy's defenses. It removes the element of "RNG" (random number generation) from your rotations, allowing you to plan moves with the certainty that your debuffs will land.

### Common Misconceptions About 100% Base Chance

The most common point of confusion for new players is the difference between "Base Chance" and "Fixed Chance." When a skill says it has a 100% base chance to Burn an enemy, many assume it will always happen. However, this 100% is merely the numerator in a formula that includes the enemy's Effect RES. If a boss has 40% Effect RES, that 100% base chance effectively becomes a 60% actual chance unless you have built EHR to counter the resistance.
