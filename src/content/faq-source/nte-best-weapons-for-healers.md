## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/nte-best-weapons-for-healers/inline-3.webp)


The weapon system in Neverness to Everness, known as Arcs, is more complex than simple stat sticks. Each Arc belongs to a specific "Type" (Synthesis, Solid, Gas, Liquid, or Plasma), and matching these types to a character's combat style is essential for activating secondary passives. For healers, the priority is often finding an Arc that matches their primary scaling stat while providing enough energy regeneration to keep their Ultimate abilities active.

### Understanding Arc Types and Healer Synergy

Arcs are categorized by physical states that dictate their general stat distribution. Healers typically gravitate toward **Solid** and **Synthesis** types. 

*   **Solid Type Arcs:** Usually offer higher base Defense and HP stats. These are ideal for healers like Fadia, who often take redirected damage from teammates.
*   **Synthesis Type Arcs:** These are rarer, high-tier Arcs that provide hybrid stats. They often combine offensive boosts (like Mental DMG) with massive survivability buffs.
*   **Gas and Liquid Types:** These are more niche for healers, often used by Anima-based supports like Jiuyuan who need higher ATK stats to fuel their healing throughput.

Matching the Arc Type to the character's preferred style ensures that you unlock the "Resonance" bonus, which can provide an additional undisclosed to healing effectiveness.

### S-Rank Meta: Eternal Waltz and Good Boy’s Grand Adventure

If you are lucky enough to pull an S-Rank Arc, your healer's efficiency will skyrocket. **Eternal Waltz** is currently the most sought-after weapon for Fadia. Its primary draw is the passive ability that increases the wearer's Max HP by up to 32% at max refinement. Because Fadia’s damage redirection and healing are tied directly to her health pool, this Arc makes her nearly unkillable while ensuring the rest of the team stays topped off.

**Good Boy’s Grand Adventure** serves a different role. It is the "battery" Arc. While it provides decent sustain, its main utility lies in its Charge Efficiency. Healers using this Arc can generate energy for the entire team, and upon casting a healing skill, it grants a Team ATK buff. This is the preferred choice for speed-running high-level Anomalies where offensive momentum is just as important as survival.

### A-Rank and Budget Alternatives: Song of the Veil and Call of the Twisted City

Not every player will have access to S-Rank Arcs. Fortunately, the A-Rank **Song of the Veil** is a highly capable substitute. It focuses on situational sustain, providing a burst of healing whenever a team member falls below 40% HP. This "safety net" mechanic is invaluable during boss fights with high-damage phases.

**Call of the Twisted City** is the standout B-Rank weapon. In many other games, B-Rank weapons are fodder, but in NTE, the specific "Redirect Skill" bonus on this Arc makes it mathematically superior to some A-Rank options for Edgar and Fadia during the early game. It is easy to refine to Level 5, making it a very cost-effective investment for F2P players.
