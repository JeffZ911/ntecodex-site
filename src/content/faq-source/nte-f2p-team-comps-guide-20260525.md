### Mastering the Esper Cycle for F2P

The Esper Cycle is the heartbeat of NTE combat. Each character generates "Cycle Energy" through basic attacks and skills. When a character's icon flashes at 100%, swapping them in triggers a "Cycle Entry" move. For F2P players, the goal is to trigger elemental reactions during these entries.

*   **Blossom (Cosmos + Anima):** Triggered when Zero (Cosmos) follows Mint or Haniel (Anima). It creates a floral explosion that deals AoE damage and reduces enemy resistance.
*   **Hexed (Anima + Incantation):** Triggered when Adler (Incantation) follows an Anima unit. It applies a Damage-over-Time (DoT) effect that scales with Adler’s Defense.
*   **Discord (Nova + Scorch):** A "Trio Reaction" that requires specific elemental combinations. While harder for F2P players to trigger consistently, using Adler’s Incantation skills followed by a Chaos entry from Aurelia can mimic these high-tier effects.

### The Starter Team: Zero, Mint, Adler, and Haniel

This team is provided entirely for free within the first two hours of gameplay. 

**Esper Zero** functions as the anchor. As a Cosmos-element carry, Zero has a high Cycle Rate, meaning they generate energy faster than most units. Use Zero to group enemies with their "Cosmic Pull" skill before swapping.

**Adler** is the backbone of F2P survivability. His "Bose-type shields" scale based on his total Defense stat. Unlike other shielders, Adler’s shield also applies an Incantation aura that deals minor damage to nearby enemies, making him a "passive" DPS contributor.

**Haniel** provides the "Hootie" summon. Positioning Hootie is critical; the summon provides a Team ATK buff as long as you stay within its radius. For F2P players, Haniel's burst is often the only source of reliable healing, so save it for when Adler’s shield is breached.
