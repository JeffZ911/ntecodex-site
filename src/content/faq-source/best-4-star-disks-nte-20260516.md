This guide identifies the best 4-star (A-Rank) Disks—officially known as Arcs—and Cartridge sets to optimize your characters in Neverness to Everness (NTE).

# Best 4-Star Disks for Neverness to Everness (NTE): Top A-Rank Gear

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/best-4-star-disks-nte-20260516/inline-1.webp)


Players reaching the mid-game of Neverness to Everness often find themselves at a crossroads: should they invest resources into 4-star (A-Rank) gear or wait for elusive S-Rank drops? Specifically, the community frequently asks:

*   What are the best 4-star "Disks" (officially known as Arcs) in Neverness to Everness?
*   How do 4-star Disks compare to the free S-Rank Arcs available through exploration in Hethereau?
*   Which A-Rank Disks provide the best value for F2P players during early to mid-game progression?

### Defining Disks vs. Arcs vs. Cartridges

In Neverness to Everness, the terminology for gear can be confusing for new Hunters. The community often uses "Disks" as a catch-all term, but the game officially distinguishes between two main equipment types:

1.  **Arcs (The "Disks"):** These are the primary weapons. They provide a Base ATK stat, a secondary stat (like Crit Rate or ATK%), and a unique passive ability. Arcs come in rarities of B (Blue), A (Pink/4-Star), and S (Orange/5-Star).
2.  **Cartridges (The Gear Sets):** These function like artifacts or relics. You equip them to a character’s Console to activate 2-piece and 4-piece set bonuses.
3.  **Modules:** These are smaller, Tetris-like pieces that you slot into the Console. The shape and placement of these Modules are what activate your Cartridge set bonuses.

### The Importance of A-Rank Gear in Version 1.0

While S-Rank Arcs like "Ready-Ready" ([Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte)’s signature) or "The Last Rose" offer the highest ceiling, A-Rank Arcs are essential for several reasons. First, they are much easier to "Refine" (Ascend), allowing you to reach Rank 5 passives that can sometimes outperform a Rank 1 S-Rank Arc. Second, the resource cost for leveling A-Rank gear is significantly lower, making them the primary choice for secondary and tertiary team members in endgame content like the Anomaly Commissions.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/best-4-star-disks-nte-20260516/inline-2.webp)


The current meta for 4-star (A-Rank) gear focuses on maximizing specific elemental damage types and team-wide utility. While top-tier S-Rank Arcs like **The Rain That Shook the World** and **Raging Flames** are often recommended for F2P players because they can be obtained for free through specific missions, they are technically 5-star items. For true A-Rank (4-star) options, **Watch Your Heads!** and **Clear Skies** are the premier choices for DPS.

For Support characters, **Time Bandit** and **Hethereau's Keeper** (sometimes referred to as Hurus Keeper) are the top-tier 4-star choices. These Arcs focus on accelerating Ultimate rotations and providing flat ATK buffs that benefit the entire squad.

When it comes to Cartridge sets (the gear equipped to the Console), **Street Boxer** is the best for Lakshana-based Crit builds, while **Shadow Creed** provides the most consistent ATK buffs for characters who rely on their Skills.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/best-4-star-disks-nte-20260516/inline-3.webp)


Navigating the gear system in Neverness to Everness requires understanding how Arcs interact with the "Esper Cycle"—the game's elemental reaction system. Choosing a 4-star Disk isn't just about the raw ATK; it's about how the passive effect synergizes with reactions like **Blossom**, **Stain**, and **Nova**.

### Best 4-Star DPS Disks: Damage & Crit Focus

For players who haven't yet pulled a signature S-Rank Arc from "The Ichi-daime" banner or other limited pools, these A-Rank options provide the necessary firepower for Hethereau's street combat.

*   **Watch Your Heads!:** This is the gold standard for Lakshana-element characters. Its passive increases ATK and specifically boosts damage against enemies affected by the **Remora** or **Stain** reactions. Since these reactions are easy to maintain with characters like Skia, this Arc provides near-constant uptime on its damage bonuses.
*   **Clear Skies:** If you are running an Anima-focused team, Clear Skies is your best friend. It provides a flat boost to Anima damage dealt by Ultimates and Redirect Skills. It is particularly effective on Mint, whose kit allows for consistent Skill cycling.
*   **Oraora!:** This Arc is a versatile choice for characters who spend a lot of time on-field using Basic Attacks. It provides a stacking damage bonus to Basic Attacks and unleashes additional Incantation DMG (equal to 200% of ATK) whenever the wearer successfully triggers a Parry Attack.

### Best 4-Star Support Disks: Energy & Buffing

Support characters in NTE are judged by how quickly they can generate energy for the team and how much they can amplify the Main DPS's numbers.

*   **Hethereau's Keeper:** This unique Arc provides a flat ATK boost and has a high-utility passive that summons "Officer Whisker" to assist in combat. Beyond the flavor, it increases damage dealt specifically against boss-type anomalies, making it a "damage package" that supports the whole team's efficiency.
*   **Time Bandit:** Energy management is crucial in Version 1.0. Time Bandit increases Charge Efficiency, allowing your supports to cast their Ultimates more frequently. This is vital for characters like Sakiri, who need their Ultimate to control the battlefield.

### Best 4-Star Defensive Disks: HP & Shielding

Survival becomes a priority in high-level Anomaly Commissions where enemies can one-shot glass-cannon builds.

*   **Umbrella:** This is the premier defensive A-Rank Arc. It provides a significant HP boost and strengthens shields when the wearer's HP is high. It is the best-in-slot 4-star option for Adler, whose shielding scales directly with his defensive stats.
*   **Dangerous Game:** A niche but powerful choice for "berserker" playstyles. It increases damage when the wearer's HP is low, creating a high-risk, high-reward scenario for tankier characters who can survive at low health.

### How to Obtain 4-Star Disks: Anomaly Commissions & Gacha

Acquiring A-Rank Arcs is more straightforward than hunting for S-Ranks. You can obtain them through:

1.  **The [Scarborough Fair](https://ntecodex.com/faq):** This is the primary [gacha system](https://ntecodex.com/faq) where you can spend your earned currency to pull for Arcs.
2.  **Anomaly Commissions:** These are repeatable combat challenges found throughout Hethereau. Higher-tier commissions have a high probability of dropping A-Rank Arcs and Cartridges.
3.  **Arc Collection Packs:** By earning keys through exploration and mission completion, you can open packs of ten Arcs at a time, which frequently contain A-Rank items.

### The Console System: Modules and Cartridges Explained

Equipping an Arc is only half the battle. To maximize your 4-star Disk, you must pair it with the correct **Cartridge** set on the character's Console.

*   **Street Boxer:** The best set for Lakshana DPS. The 2-piece bonus grants +10% Lakshana DMG, while the 4-piece bonus increases Crit Chance by 14%. If your team triggers Remora or Stain, that bonus doubles to 28% for 20 seconds.
*   **Shadow Creed:** A universal powerhouse for Skill-reliant characters. The 4-piece bonus increases the wearer's ATK by 25% for 20 seconds after casting a Skill.
*   **Speedy Hedgehog:** The mandatory set for supports. It provides +12% Charge Efficiency and grants the entire team a 15% ATK buff for 20 seconds after the wearer casts an Ultimate.

To activate these bonuses, you must fit **Modules** into the Console. Modules come in different shapes (II, III, and IV types). You must slot the specific shapes required by the Cartridge to turn on the set effects. This "Tetris" mini-game is a core part of NTE's build optimization.

### Optimizing Sub-stats: What to Look for in Early Game

When farming Cartridges and Modules, prioritize the following sub-stats for your 4-star builds:

1.  **Crit Rate / Crit DMG:** Essential for all DPS characters.
2.  **Cycle Intensity:** This stat determines the damage dealt by Esper Cycle reactions (like Nova or Scorch). High Cycle Intensity is often more valuable than raw ATK for reaction-heavy teams.
3.  **Break Intensity:** Crucial for characters designed to deplete the enemy's Break Gauge quickly.
4.  **Charge Efficiency:** The priority for all Support and Hybrid characters.

## Related

![Related](/img/best-4-star-disks-nte-20260516/inline-4.webp)


As you progress past Hunter Level 30, you will want to start replacing your 4-star Disks with the free S-Rank options hidden throughout Hethereau.

### Free S-Rank Arc Locations

*   **The Rain That Shook the World:** While technically S-Rank, this is Esper Zero's signature weapon. It can be obtained through the "Limited Press Arc Pack" in the Gift Center or through high-level gacha pools. It provides massive Cosmos DMG and Cycle Intensity.
*   **Raging Flames:** The signature Arc for Hathor. You can get this for free by defeating the **Headless Rider** boss. To find him, drive a 4-wheeled vehicle to the crossing at Fox Window Street at 23:00 (11 PM) in-game time and follow the marker into the Anomaly Realm.
*   **Day Off:** An excellent F2P S-Rank Arc that increases Charge Efficiency and restores Ultimate energy upon defeating enemies. This is a top-tier choice for the Protagonist in mob-heavy fights.
*   **Blow up the Crowd:** A powerful off-field support Arc that increases the active character's ATK while the wearer is swapped out.

### NTE Character Tier List (Version 1.0)

To know which 4-star Disks to invest in, you must understand where your characters sit in the current meta. As of May 2026:

*   **S-Tier:** Nanally, Chiz, Esper Zero (Cosmos), Haniel.
*   **A-Tier:** Hathor, Skia, Sakiri, Adler.
*   **B-Tier:** Mint, Aurelia, Baicang.

### Beginner Tips for Hethereau Exploration

*   **City Tycoon Rewards:** Progress through the City Tycoon management system early. It is one of the most reliable ways to unlock Chiz and high-quality gear without relying on gacha.
*   **Stamina Management:** Use your "Character Pixel" (stamina) primarily on Anomaly Commissions that drop the **Speedy Hedgehog** and **Shadow Creed** sets, as these are the most versatile for a growing roster.
*   **Fons Management:** Some Arcs, like **Contemplative Cat**, scale their damage based on how much "Fons" (currency) you have in your inventory. Avoid over-spending on cosmetics if you are using these specific Disks.
