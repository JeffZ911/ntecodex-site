The weapon system in Neverness to Everness is built around "Arcs," which function as the primary equipment for Espers. To master F2P progression, you must understand how these Arcs interact with character types and where to find the high-impact variants hidden in the city districts.

### Understanding Arc Types and Rarities

Arcs are categorized into five distinct types: Solid, Plasma, Gas, Liquid, and Synthesis. For an Esper to gain the full benefit of an Arc, the weapon type must match the Esper's combat style. Equipping a Solid Arc on a Plasma-type Esper will result in a significant loss of passive synergy, even if the raw stats are high.

Rarity in NTE is split primarily between A-Rank (4-star) and S-Rank (5-star). While S-Rank Arcs have higher base ATK and more powerful passives, they are harder to "Sync" (refine). A Sync Level 5 A-Rank Arc often outperforms a Sync Level 1 S-Rank Arc because the passive percentages scale aggressively with each duplicate merged into the weapon.

### How to Farm Arcs: Anomaly Zones and City Tycoon

Farming in NTE is less about repetitive dungeon crawling and more about district-specific progression. 

**Anomaly Zones:** These are localized distortions in the overworld. To farm Arcs like "Raging Flames," you must locate the Anomaly Beacon in Davidia Avenue. These zones have weekly reset timers. Clearing them at higher "Distortion Levels" increases the drop rate for Arc shards.

**City Tycoon:** This is the business management mini-game within NTE. By upgrading shops and managing staff in the Illusion Town district, you earn Carrota Coins. These coins are the only currency accepted in the Tycoon Exchange, where you can purchase S-Rank Arcs like "Contemplative Cat" and various Sync materials.
