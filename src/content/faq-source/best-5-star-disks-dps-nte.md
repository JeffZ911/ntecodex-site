Optimizing your damage output in Neverness to Everness (NTE) hinges on the synergy between your Esper's elemental kit and their equipped Cartridges. As players reach the mid-to-late game of Version 1.0, transitioning from Purple (A-rank) to Gold (S-rank) 5-star disks becomes the primary method for overcoming high-difficulty anomalies in Hethereau.

# Best 5-Star Disks for DPS in Neverness to Everness (NTE)

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/best-5-star-disks-dps-nte/inline-1.webp)


What are the highest-performing 5-star disks (officially known as Cartridges) for DPS characters in Neverness to Everness?

How do the 2-piece and 4-piece set bonuses impact damage output for S-rank Espers like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) and Baicang?

Which disks provide the best scaling for critical hits, elemental damage, and defense penetration?

In Neverness to Everness, gear is divided into Cartridges and Modules. While the Cartridge provides the primary set identity and main stat, the Modules act as the "Tetris blocks" that fit into your Console to activate these bonuses. For DPS roles, the choice of a 5-star disk can mean the difference between a sluggish rotation and a high-speed clear. Players often look for sets that offer multiplicative scaling—such as Critical Damage (CRIT DMG) or Defense Ignore (DEF Ignore)—rather than simple additive Attack (ATK) buffs, as these scale better against high-level bosses.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/best-5-star-disks-dps-nte/inline-2.webp)


The best 5-star disks for DPS in NTE are the **Fireflies and the Forest** set (Anima DPS), **Lost Radiance** (Cosmos DPS), and **Diabolos** (Chaos DPS). For general ATK-scaling characters or sub-DPS units, the **Speedy Hedgehog** set is a versatile alternative that provides a team-wide 15% ATK bonus.

Nanally, the current meta-leading DPS, performs best with the **Fireflies and the Forest** set. This set grants a base 10% Anima DMG bonus and can stack up to 56% bonus CRIT DMG through its 4-piece effect. Cosmos-type damage dealers like Chiz and Zero should prioritize the **Lost Radiance** set for its 25% DEF ignore capability, which activates for 20 seconds after using an Ultimate. For Chaos-element characters like Daffodil, the **Diabolos** set is the premier choice, offering up to 24% Chaos Resistance (RES) shred when paired with Scorch or Nova reactions.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/best-5-star-disks-dps-nte/inline-3.webp)


The gear architecture in Neverness to Everness is more complex than standard RPG equipment systems. It requires a dual focus on the Cartridge (the disk itself) and the Modules (the geometric shapes that power the disk).

### Understanding the Cartridge and Module System

Every Esper has a "Console" grid. To activate a 4-piece set bonus, you must equip the 5-star Cartridge and then slot in enough matching Modules to fill the requirement. Modules come in various geometric shapes (Type II, Type III, and Type IV), resembling Tetris blocks. 

A 5-star (Gold) Cartridge typically requires four Modules of the same set to be placed within the Console grid. Because the grid has limited space, high-tier Modules (S-rank) are often larger and provide significantly higher stat rolls but require careful arrangement to fit. The Cartridge itself provides one Main Stat, such as Anima DMG Bonus or ATK%, while the Modules provide two fixed stats (Flat ATK and Flat HP) and four randomized substats.

### Deep Dive: Fireflies and the Forest (Anima Meta)

This set is the current gold standard for Anima-element DPS characters like Nanally and Jiuyuan. 
*   **2-Piece Bonus:** 10% Anima DMG.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** Grants 8% CRIT DMG whenever a nearby enemy takes Anima DMG from the team. This effect stacks up to 7 times, totaling a massive 56% CRIT DMG.

The strength of Fireflies and the Forest lies in its ease of maintenance. Because Nanally’s kit involves high-frequency hits—especially when her "Underboss" companion is summoned during her Ultimate—she can reach maximum stacks in less than two seconds. The stacks last for 10 seconds and remain active even if the character is swapped off-field, making it excellent for quick-swap rotations.

### Deep Dive: Lost Radiance and Diabolos (Cosmos & Chaos)

For characters that do not rely on Anima, the [meta shifts](https://ntecodex.com/guides/best-gear-genshin-6-6-meta-shifts) toward defense and resistance penetration.

**Lost Radiance** is designed for Cosmos Espers like Chiz, [Hotori](/characters/hotori-best-team-nte), and the main protagonist, Zero. The 25% DEF ignore is one of the strongest multipliers in NTE because it bypasses the natural damage reduction of high-level bosses. Since the buff lasts 20 seconds after an Ultimate, it covers the entire burst window of most Cosmos rotations.

**Diabolos** is the specialized set for Chaos characters, most notably Daffodil and Lacrimosa. Chaos damage thrives on elemental reactions. The base 12% Chaos RES ignore is helpful, but the set truly shines when you participate in a **Scorch** (Chaos + Incantation) or **Nova** (Chaos + Cosmos) reaction. Triggering these reactions doubles the shred to 24%, effectively removing the elemental resistance of most standard enemies and significantly weakening elite anomalies.

### Optimizing the Tetris Console Grid

To maximize a DPS build, fitting your Modules into the Console is a puzzle that directly affects your power. 
1.  **Prioritize S-Rank Modules:** Even if an A-rank Module fits better, the stat ceiling of an S-rank (Gold) Module is significantly higher. 
2.  **Shape Management:** Use Type II (2-cell) and Type III (3-cell) modules to fill the corners of your grid, saving the large Type IV (4-cell) modules for the center. 
3.  **Stat Synergy:** For a DPS Cartridge, the Main Stat should almost always be the corresponding Elemental DMG Bonus (e.g., Anima DMG for Nanally). If your CRIT Rate is below 50%, you may temporarily use a CRIT Rate main stat Cartridge until your Module substats are better optimized.

### DPS Stat Priority (Main Stats vs. Substats)

| Gear Piece | Recommended Main Stat | Priority Substats |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cartridge** | Elemental DMG % (Anima/Cosmos/Chaos) | CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK%, Flat ATK |
| **Modules** | Flat ATK / Flat HP (Fixed) | CRIT Rate, CRIT DMG, ATK%, [Energy Regen](https://ntecodex.com/guides/best-gear-for-gacha-theorycrafting-math-20260707) |

### When to Start Farming 5-Star Disks

Farming Cartridges consumes "Character Pixels" (stamina). It is highly recommended to wait until you reach **Tycoon Level 40** before heavily investing your stamina into Cartridge domains. At Tycoon Level 40, the [drop rates](https://ntecodex.com/faq) for Gold (5-star) Cartridges and Modules increase significantly, guaranteeing at least one S-rank drop per run. 

Before Level 40, players should focus on leveling their Arcs (weapons) and Esper levels. Using the "Hunter Exchange" unlocked at Tycoon Rank 6 is a good way to get baseline gear, but the true "endgame" grind for perfect substats should be reserved for the post-Level 40 phase.

## Related

![Related](/img/best-5-star-disks-dps-nte/inline-4.webp)


Maximizing your DPS involves more than just picking the right disk set; you must also consider how your Arcs and team reactions trigger your gear's passives.

### Arc (Weapon) Synergy for DPS

The Arcs you equip act as the primary stat sticks for your Espers. For a character like Nanally, the 5-star Arc **"Ready-Ready"** is considered Best-in-Slot (BiS). This Arc provides a high base CRIT Rate substat, which allows you to focus purely on CRIT DMG substats in your Module grid. 

Another powerful option is **"Hethereau's Keeper,"** which boosts ATK% and increases damage dealt specifically to bosses. It also summons a companion, Officer Whisker, who deals off-field damage. This off-field damage can help maintain stacks for sets like "Fireflies and the Forest" or "Crimson: Twin Butterflies" even when your main DPS is dodging or repositioning. For F2P players, the S-rank Arc **"Song of the Whale"**—obtained by defeating the Sea Prisoner boss in the Coastal Highway—provides a solid 12% ATK boost and extra damage against "Broken" enemies.

### Elemental Reactions and Gear Procs

Many 5-star disks require specific reactions to reach their full potential.
*   **Blossom (Anima + Cosmos):** This reaction is vital for Nanally and Zero teams. It creates a field that deals continuous damage, helping to rapidly stack the "Fireflies and the Forest" CRIT DMG buff.
*   **Scorch (Chaos + Incantation):** Essential for the "Diabolos" set. Pairing a Chaos DPS like Daffodil with an Incantation support like [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) or Baicang ensures the 24% RES ignore is always active.
*   **Stain (Incantation + Cosmos):** This reaction is the key to maximizing the "Devil's Blood: Curse" set, which boosts total damage by 36% against stained targets.

### Substat Optimization via Rewinding

Once you have your 5-star disk set and Modules in place, the final step is "Rewinding." In the gear enhancement menu, you can use specific materials to reroll the substats on your Modules. For a DPS build, you are hunting for "Double Crit" lines—Modules that possess both CRIT Rate and CRIT DMG. 

Because NTE allows you to see all four substats on a Module immediately upon acquisition, you should only spend resources "Rewinding" Modules that already have at least one high-value stat like CRIT Rate or ATK%. Avoid wasting materials on Modules with purely defensive stats (DEF/HP) unless you are building a survival-based character like Fadia.
