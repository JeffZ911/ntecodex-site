### Mastering the Pity System and Chronicled Wish

The gacha system, or "Wishes," is how you acquire new characters and weapons. Understanding "Pity" is vital for Free-to-Play (F2P) players. For Character Event Banners, you are guaranteed a 5-star character at 90 pulls (though "soft pity" usually kicks in around pull 74). If your first 5-star is not the featured character, you have lost the "50/50," and your next 5-star is guaranteed to be the featured unit.

The Chronicled Wish banner is an additional system that periodically features older characters and weapons from specific regions. This banner uses a "Fate Point" system; if you do not get your chosen 5-star on the first try, your next 5-star is guaranteed to be your selection.

### Resin Management: From Fragile Resin to [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-14]

Original Resin is the energy currency used to claim rewards from bosses and domains. In 2026, the cap is 200 Resin, regenerating at a rate of 1 every 8 minutes.

1.  **AR 1-44:** Use Resin on Ley Line Outcrops (for Mora and EXP books) and World Bosses for ascension materials.

3.  **Fragile Resin:** Never spend this resource before AR 45. Save it to "burst farm" a complete 5-star artifact set once you unlock the highest-level domains.

### Navigating Teyvat: From Mondstadt to the Snezhnaya Border

The journey begins in Mondstadt, the city of Freedom. As a new player, you should follow the linear path of the Archon Quests: Mondstadt -> Liyue -> Inazuma -> Sumeru -> Fontaine -> Natlan -> Snezhnaya.

*   **Statues of the Seven:** Interacting with these unlocks portions of the map and increases your stamina when you offer Oculi (Anemoculus, Geoculus, etc.).
*   **Adventure Encounters:** In 2026, you can complete your "Daily Commissions" simply by exploring, opening chests, or completing event objectives. This "Encounter Points" system allows you to skip repetitive daily combat tasks.
*   **Teleport Waypoints:** Unlock every waypoint you see. Fast travel is essential for efficient material gathering.

### Adventure Rank (AR) and World Level

Your Adventure Rank determines the strength of the enemies in your world and the quality of the loot they drop. Every few levels, you will face an Ascension Quest to increase your World Level. Do not be afraid to increase your World Level; while enemies get tougher, the increased drop rates for rare materials are necessary for character progression.

If you find the game too difficult after an ascension, you can lower your World Level by one in the main menu, though this will slightly reduce reward quality.
