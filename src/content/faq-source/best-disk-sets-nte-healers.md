In Neverness to Everness, "Disks" are officially known as Cartridges, and they serve as the primary gear system for enhancing your characters' stats and utility. For players focusing on sustainability, the most effective Disk Sets (Cartridges) for healers are those that either maximize raw healing output or provide powerful team-wide buffs to maintain momentum in combat.

Selecting the right gear involves understanding whether your healer prioritizes raw healing potency or utility. Pure healers like Edgar often look for sets that increase the Healing Bonus stat directly, while hybrid supports like [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) or Haniel might lean toward sets that improve Charge Efficiency to ensure their Ultimate is always ready.

Furthermore, the choice of Disk Set is heavily influenced by the character's scaling. In NTE, healers typically fall into two archetypes: HP-scaling (like Fadia) or ATK-scaling. Equipping an HP-focused set on an ATK-scaling healer results in significantly diminished performance, making it vital to match the Cartridge’s set bonus to the character's specific kit requirements.

### Defining 'Disks' in the NTE Gear System
While the community often uses the term "Disks," the game refers to these items as Cartridges. These are equipped within the "Console" interface. A complete build consists of one Cartridge, which determines the set bonus, and several Modules—Tetris-like blocks that fit into a grid. To activate the most powerful "Legend" effects of a Cartridge, you must successfully fit four Modules of the correct shape and type into the character's Console.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/best-disk-sets-nte-healers/inline-3.webp)


The gear system in Neverness to Everness is a multi-layered puzzle involving the Console, Cartridges, and Modules. Unlike traditional gear systems, NTE requires you to physically arrange "Tetris" pieces to unlock the full potential of your healer.

### Top-Tier Cartridge Sets for Healers

#### The Knight's Tavern: The Survival Standard
The Knight's Tavern is the go-to set for early-game survival and high-difficulty content where enemy damage is unpredictable. The 20% Healing Bonus provided by the 4-piece Legend effect is a multiplicative stat, meaning it scales much better than raw HP or ATK in the late game. If your Edgar is struggling to keep the team's health bars full during boss fights in the New Herland District, switching to this set is the most immediate fix.

#### Speedy Hedgehog: The Offensive Support
In the current meta, Speedy Hedgehog is often considered the best-in-slot for any support that can maintain high Ultimate uptime. The 12% Charge Efficiency from the 2-piece bonus helps cycle the Ultimate faster, while the 15% team-wide ATK buff ensures that your healers are contributing to the team's total DPS. Since this buff lasts for 20 seconds, healers with short Ultimate cooldowns can keep this bonus active almost indefinitely.

#### Tiny Big Adventure: The Fadia Specialist
Fadia operates differently than other healers. She uses a "Damage Redirection" mechanic to absorb hits meant for her teammates. Because her healing and survivability scale exclusively with her health pool, Tiny Big Adventure is essential. The set provides a stacking Max HP bonus whenever she takes damage—which happens constantly due to her redirection passive. With the 4-piece effect, Fadia can reach HP totals that make her effectively unkillable while providing massive heals based on that inflated health bar.

### The Tetris System: Optimizing Module Placement
Activating a Cartridge's Legend effect requires placing four Modules of specific shapes into the Console. Each character has a unique Console grid, and Modules come in different types (Type II, III, and IV).

*   **Type II Modules:** Typically 2-cell shapes. These are preferred by Fadia, whose Console passives grant extra HP for every Type II Module equipped.
*   **Type III Modules:** Larger, 3-cell shapes (often L-shaped or T-shaped). Support characters like Sakiri gain significant ATK or Elemental DMG bonuses from these.
*   **Type IV Modules:** [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-17].

When building a healer, you must balance the Cartridge's shape requirements with the character's Module Type preference. For example, if you equip four Type III Modules to activate Speedy Hedgehog on a character who prefers Type II, you may lose out on the character's native Console stat bonuses.

### Stat Priorities: Main Stats and Sub-stats
For dedicated healers, the **Healing Bonus** main stat on a Cartridge is almost always superior to HP% or ATK%. This is because Healing Bonus acts as a final multiplier on the healing calculation.

**Healer Stat Priority List:**
1.  **Healing Bonus (Main Stat):** Essential for the Cartridge slot.
2.  **Charge Efficiency:** Vital for ensuring your healer can cast their Ultimate during emergencies.
3.  **Max HP% / ATK%:** Choose based on your character's specific scaling (HP for Fadia, ATK for Edgar).
4.  **Universal DMG Bonus:** A useful sub-stat that helps your healer contribute minor chip damage during the Esper Cycle.

### NTE Healer Tier List
In the current 1.0 meta, **Fadia** is ranked as the lone S-Tier survival character. Her ability to redirect damage and her massive HP pool make her the most "comfy" pick for high-tier content like the Rabbit Hole or Beyond the Rails. **Jiuyuan** sits in the S-Tier for utility, though her healing is locked behind her first Awakening. **Edgar** is a solid B-Tier or C-Tier option; while his raw healing is high, he lacks the defensive utility or damage redirection found in higher-ranked Espers.

### How to Farm Legendary Cartridges
To obtain high-rarity (S-Rank) Cartridges like The Knight's Tavern or Tiny Big Adventure, you must navigate the **Rabbit Hole** Anomaly Zone. This challenge is located on the south side of the **New Herland District**. 
*   **Cost:** 40 Character Pixels per run.
*   **Tip:** You can claim double rewards to save time, which is highly recommended when farming for specific Module shapes to complete your Legend sets.
*   **Rewards:** In addition to Cartridges, you will earn Carrota and upgrade materials necessary for leveling your Console.

### Alternative Healing Methods
If you find yourself without a dedicated healer in your party, NTE provides several out-of-combat recovery options
*   **ReroRero Phone Booths:** These act as fast travel points and automatically restore the entire team's HP when approached.
*   **Wertheimer Towers:** Similar to phone booths, these towers provide full team healing and serve as landmarks throughout Hethereau.
*   **Food Items:** You can assign healing food to your shortcut menu to restore HP mid-combat.
*   **Dormitory:** Resting in your bed at the Eibon Antique Shop base will instantly fully heal all characters.

### Best Team Compositions for Healers
Healers play a crucial role in the **Esper Cycle** system, which relies on elemental reactions.
*   **Blossom Reaction:** Triggered by combining Anima and Psyche elements. Fadia (Psyche) is a perfect partner for Anima DPS units like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) or Mint to maintain constant Blossom procs, which can provide additional energy and sustain.
*   **Stain Reaction:** Often triggered by Lakshana or Incantation supports. Using a healer like Edgar in a team with Sakiri allows you to maintain the Stain effect on enemies, increasing the team's overall elemental application while staying safe behind Edgar's consistent healing.
