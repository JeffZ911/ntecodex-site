### Currency-Based Arcs: City Tycoon and Mhm Coin Exchange

NTE features a deep business management sim called City Tycoon. By managing shops and increasing your "Fons" (business revenue), you unlock unique rewards.

*   **Contemplative Cat:** At City Tycoon Level 15, you receive this S-Rank Arc. It is unique because its passive increases the rewards gained from open-world activities, making it a great "farming" weapon for early-game progression.
*   **Your Happiness is Priceless:** This Arc is available in the Mhm Coin shop. Mhm Coins are earned by participating in weekly "Urban Legend" events and completing daily commissions. It costs 1,200 Mhm Coins, which typically takes a F2P player about three weeks of consistent play to accumulate.

### Top F2P Arcs for DPS, Support, and Break Roles

For a balanced F2P team, you should focus your resources on upgrading one Arc for each role.

*   **DPS Role:** Focus on **Raging Flames** or **Your Happiness is Priceless**. These provide the highest raw damage multipliers. Raging Flames is specifically better for Plasma-type Espers, while Your Happiness is Priceless is a universal fit for any element.

### Weapon Leveling and Breakthrough Tips for F2P Players

Leveling Arcs requires "Arc Exp" materials and "Fons" (gold). 

1.  **Don't Spread Resources:** In the early game (Player Level 1-30), only level three Arcs. Resources become extremely scarce once you reach the Level 50+ Breakthrough stage.
2.  **Breakthrough Materials:** These materials are farmed in "Anomaly Rifts." As an F2P player, always use your daily "Stamina" (Energy) on these rifts during double-drop weekend events.
3.  **Recycling:** If you obtain a better S-Rank Arc, you can recycle your old A-Rank Arc to recover 80% of the invested EXP materials. This is a vital strategy for maintaining power while transitioning to late-game gear.
