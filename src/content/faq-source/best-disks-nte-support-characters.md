### Optimizing the Console and Module System

In NTE, you do not just "equip" a Cartridge; you place Modules into a grid called the Console. These Modules come in different "Tetris" shapes categorized as Types II, III, and IV.

*   **Type II Modules:** Usually square or rectangular, these often carry the primary stats like ATK or HP.
*   **Type III Modules:** L-shaped pieces that typically provide secondary utility stats like Charge Efficiency.
*   **Type IV Modules:** T-shaped or complex pieces that often hold the "Set Bonus" trigger.

To activate the 4-piece Legendary effect of a set like Speedy Hedgehog, you must fit four Modules of that set into the Console grid. For support characters, the layout should prioritize "fitting" as many Charge Efficiency sub-stat Modules as possible, even if it means using a lower-rarity Module to complete the "puzzle."
