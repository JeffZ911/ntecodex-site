For example, the Cunning Hares faction members all benefit significantly from being grouped together, providing extra energy generation and damage buffs that are vital for sustaining long encounters.

## Detailed Explanation

### Team 1: The Cunning Hares (Anby / Billy / Nicole)

The Cunning Hares team is the gold standard for F2P players. The synergy here is straightforward: Anby is the "on-field" agent who uses her N3 -> EX Special combo to build Daze. Once the enemy is stunned, Nicole is swapped in to use her EX Special, which creates a small black hole that groups enemies and applies a DEF reduction debuff.

Billy is the finisher. His ultimate and chain attacks have massive multipliers. Because Billy is a Physical Attack agent, he benefits from the "Assault" anomaly, which deals a burst of damage and interrupts enemy actions. The key to this team is Anby's "Additional Ability," which increases her Energy Generation when another Cunning Hares member is present, allowing her to use her stun-heavy EX Special more frequently.

### Team 2: Belobog Electric Burst (Anby / Anton / Nicole)

This team swaps Billy for Anton, a powerful A-rank Attack agent from Belobog Heavy Industries. While Anton is not a "starter," he is widely available through early-game pulls. This team focuses on the "Shock" status effect. When Anby deals Electric damage, she contributes to the Shock anomaly meter.

Once the enemy is Shocked and Stunned, Anton enters his "Burst Mode" (activated by his EX Special). In this state, Anton's attacks deal significantly increased damage to Shocked enemies. Nicole remains in the team to provide DEF Shred and grouping, ensuring Anton's drills hit as many targets as possible.

### Team 3: Physical Anomaly Shred (Piper / Lucy / Corin)

This is a specialized team that moves away from the traditional Stun-focus and moves toward Anomaly application. Piper is an Anomaly agent who uses a "spin-to-win" mechanic to build up Physical Anomaly extremely fast. This triggers "Assault" and undisclosed for massive damage.

Lucy, an A-rank Support from the Sons of Calydon, provides a team-wide ATK buff using her Guarded Pigs. This buff is essential for Corin, who acts as the "Burst DPS." When Piper triggers a stun or an anomaly proc, Corin swaps in to use her chainsaw-revving EX Special, which deals increased damage to stunned targets.

### Team 4: The Ice Support Core (Anby / Soukaku / Corin)

Soukaku is arguably the strongest A-rank Support in Zenless Zone Zero. Her "Fly the Flag" ability allows her to transfer a portion of her own ATK to the next agent who enters the field via a Chain Attack or Quick Assist. This buff is massive, often reaching over 500-1000 bonus ATK.

In this F2P setup, Anby stuns the target, Soukaku triggers her flag buff, and then Corin is swapped in to receive the ATK boost. Even though Corin is Physical and Soukaku is Ice, the raw stat increase from Soukaku's kit makes Corin's chainsaw attacks devastatingly powerful.

### Team 5: The 'New Era' F2P Powerhouse (Anby / undisclosed / Nicole)

As the game evolves, new A-rank agents are introduced. In this composition, Anby and Nicole provide the foundational Stun and Support roles. The inclusion of undisclosed suggests a shift toward newer mechanics that may involve high-frequency attribute application. Until the official release of this specific agent, players should stick to Billy or Anton in the third slot to maintain Faction bonuses with Nicole or Anby.

### Combat Rotations and Chain Attack Priority

To maximize F2P team efficiency, you must follow a specific rotation:
1.  **Lead with your Stunner (Anby):** Stay on field until the enemy's Daze bar is nearly full. Use N3 -> EX Special to maximize Daze per point of Energy.
2.  **Apply Debuffs/Buffs (Nicole/Soukaku):** Just before the stun triggers, or during the Chain Attack sequence, ensure your Support agent uses their ability.
3.  **Chain Attack Sequence:** When a Chain Attack is triggered, the order should always end with your **Attack agent**. For example: Anby -> Nicole -> Billy. This ensures Billy is the one on field when the enemy is fully stunned and debuffed.
4.  **The Burst Window:** Once the enemy is stunned, dump all Energy with your Attack agent (Billy's EX Special or Corin's held EX Special).

### How to Get More Free Characters in ZZZ

Zenless Zone Zero offers several ways to expand your roster without spending money.
*   **Story Progression:** Completing Chapter 2, Intermission, grants you **Ben Bigger**, a Fire Defense agent who is excellent for survivability.
*   **[Hollow Zero](https://ntecodex.com/guides/zzz-3-0-hollow-zero-tactical-buff-comparison):** By participating in the weekly Hollow Zero runs, you can earn "Z-Merits," which can be traded in the Logistics Shop for **Soukaku** Mindscape Cinemas or other rewards.
*   **Permanent Events:** The "Shidian Kick" event is a permanent addition that rewards players with Soukaku for completing specific combat trials.
*   **Monthly Shop:** The "Signal Shop" allows you to spend "Residual Signal" (earned from any pulls) to buy two specific A-rank agents every month on a rotating schedule.

### Best Bangboo Pairings for Budget Teams

Bangboos are free units obtained through "Boopons" earned via gameplay. They provide significant combat assistance.
*   **Amillion:** The best Bangboo for the Cunning Hares. It triggers a physical chain attack and provides bonuses when Cunning Hares members are present.
*   **Bagboo:** The top choice for Physical teams (Piper/Corin/Billy). It focuses on dealing Physical damage and building Anomaly.
*   **Rocketboo:** Excellent for teams using Ben Bigger, as it focuses on Fire damage.
*   **Exploreboo:** A generic but useful Bangboo that provides random buffs/healing, making it a safe pick for any F2P team.

### F2P vs. Premium: When to Pull for S-Rank Agents

While F2P teams can clear most content, S-rank agents provide a "quality of life" improvement. As an F2P player, you should save your Encrypted Master Tapes for agents that fill a gap in your roster.
*   **The 300-Pull Selector:** After 300 pulls on the Standard Banner, you can choose any S-rank agent. **Lycaon** (Stun) or **Rina** (Support) are the best choices for F2P players, as they significantly upgrade Ice or Electric teams, respectively.
*   **Targeting Synergy:** If you have built a strong Soukaku, pulling for an Ice Attacker like **Ellen Joe** is a wise investment. If you prefer the Cunning Hares playstyle, **Zhu Yuan** (Ether/Attack) is the perfect upgrade for Billy, as she shares a faction with Nicole.
