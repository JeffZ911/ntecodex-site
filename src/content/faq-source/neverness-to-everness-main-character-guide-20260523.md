### Character Identity: The Appraiser (Zero)

The Appraiser serves as a bridge between the mundane world of Hesperia and the supernatural chaos of the "Blockades." Employed by the Hesperia Intelligence Agency and operating out of the Eibon Antique Shop, the protagonist is tasked with identifying and neutralizing anomalies. 

Unlike other Espers who possess specialized, narrow abilities, the Appraiser exhibits a broad spectrum of power. This narrative flexibility translates into gameplay where the protagonist can fit into almost any team composition, filling gaps in damage or utility as needed.

### How to Change Gender: The 'In the Reflection' Quest

Neverness to Everness stands out for its "Fluid Identity" system. If you find yourself regretting your initial choice of male or female protagonist, you are not stuck. To unlock the ability to swap, you must reach the Hanaku Street district and locate Good Joe's Vintage Shop.

1.  **Triggering the Quest**: Once you reach Account Level [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-23], a rumor will appear in your Hesperia Intelligence Agency (HIA) handbook regarding a "Haunted Mirror."
2.  **Locating the Mirror**: Head to Good Joe’s Vintage Shop. In the back room, you will find a large, ornate mirror that serves as the centerpiece of the "In the Reflection" quest.
3.  **Completing the Trial**: You must enter a solo Blockade instance where you fight a shadow version of your current character.
4.  **Swapping**: After the quest is complete, you can interact with the mirror at any time in the Eibon Antique Shop to switch between the male and female versions of the Appraiser. This change is cosmetic and does not reset your level, skills, or equipment.

### The Esper Cycle: Elemental Synergies

The Appraiser’s true strength lies in how they interact with other characters like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) or [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide). To maximize this, you should always aim to use the Appraiser's Ultimate right before switching to your main burst DPS. This ensures the enemy is in a "Weakened" state, taking increased damage from the incoming character's abilities.

Outside of combat, the Appraiser is your avatar for the extensive life simulator features in Neverness to Everness. Your performance in the main story and side quests unlocks various lifestyle upgrades:
