### Zero: The Versatile Cosmos Protagonist

Zero is your primary gateway into the Esper Cycle. As a Cosmos-element unit, Zero does not suffer from the typical elemental disadvantages found in the Anima-Psyche-Incantation triangle. Their kit is classified as "Solid," meaning they excel at breaking enemy poise and maintaining field presence.

In the early game, you should focus on Zero's ability to trigger reactions. Because Cosmos acts as a universal catalyst, Zero can help trigger **Charge** (when hitting enemies affected by Incantation) or **Blossom** (when interacting with Anima or Psyche fields). ### Chiz: The Free S-Rank Main DPS

Chiz is arguably the most important free unit in the game. While Zero is versatile, Chiz is a dedicated damage dealer who uses the **Grain Market** mechanic to dominate the battlefield. ### Mint, Adler, and Haniel: The Core Support Trio

While the S-rank units provide the damage, the A-rank support trio ensures your team stays alive and buffed.

1.  **Mint (Anima):** Your primary healer. Mint’s skill creates a "Botanical Zone" that restores health to the active character over time. In the first three Anomaly Zones, Mint is essential because enemy damage scales aggressively.
2.  **Adler (Incantation):** A crowd-control specialist. Adler’s abilities can pull smaller enemies together, making it easier for Chiz to hit them with her Grain Market bursts. Adler is obtained at the Eibon Antique Shop after completing the "Echoes of the Past" story beat.
