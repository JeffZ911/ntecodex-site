## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/how-to-defeat-tempest-mephis-wuwa/inline-2.webp)


Tempest Mephis is an Electro-attribute world boss located in an underground cavern in the Central Plains region, specifically northwest of Qichi Village. To enter the arena, you must jump into a large crater that contains an upward-spiraling air current and descend to the bottom.

To defeat it, you should avoid using Electro DPS characters like Calcharo or Yinlin as your primary damage dealers, as the boss possesses high Electro resistance. Instead, opt for Glacio, Fusion, or Aero Resonators to maximize damage output. The core strategy revolves around mastering the "Weakness Halos"—the gold rings that appear during the boss's dash attacks. Successfully timing your attacks during these windows will deplete the boss's Vibration Strength (the white bar under its health), eventually stunning it for a significant damage window. Key moves to monitor include the zigzag dash, the red laser beam (which requires a sideways dodge), and the Judgment Cut sequence where the boss teleports rapidly.

### Location: Finding the Underground Cavern

Unlike many world bosses that sit in open fields, Tempest Mephis is hidden beneath the surface of the Central Plains. 

1.  **Teleportation:** Start by teleporting to the Resonance Beacon located northwest of Qichi Village. 
2.  **The Crater:** Head further northwest until you find a massive, jagged crater in the ground. You will see a blue air current rising from the center.
3.  **The Descent:** Do not be deterred by the air current; you can glide down through it. As you reach the bottom, the cavern opens up into a large, circular arena where the Tempest Mephis manifests.
4.  **The Beacon:** There is a Resonance Beacon located inside the cave near the boss. Ensure you activate this immediately so you can fast-travel directly to the boss for future farming sessions without having to jump down the crater again.

While you can technically win with an Electro team, the fight will take significantly longer, increasing the risk of mechanical errors.

*   **Aero:** Excellent for breaking Vibration Strength and maintaining mobility.

### Combat Mechanics: Mastering the Parry and Dodge

The Tempest Mephis fight is a rhythm-based encounter. It is highly aggressive and will punish players who spam attacks blindly.

#### The Weakness Halo (Parry)
The most critical mechanic is the gold ring that appears around the boss. This is the Weakness Halo. When this ring appears, you must land a hit on the boss just as the outer ring shrinks to meet the inner circle.
*   **The Zigzag Dash:** The boss will crouch and then dash in a zigzag pattern. A gold ring appears right before the final strike. Parry this to deal massive Vibration Strength damage.
*   **Aerial Slam:** When the boss jumps high into the air, wait for the gold ring to appear as it descends. Timing a mid-air attack or a well-timed ground strike will knock it out of the air.

#### Major Move Sets and Counters
*   **Red Laser Beam:** The boss charges Electro energy in its palm and fires a concentrated beam. This move cannot be parried. You must dodge to the left or right. If you are caught in it, it hits multiple times and can easily down a low-HP Resonator.
*   **Judgment Cut (Teleport Slashes):** The boss disappears and reappears rapidly, leaving slashes in the air. The best defense here is to sprint in a circle or time consecutive dodges. The final hit of this sequence often features a parry window.
*   **Tail Swipe:** If you stay behind the boss for too long, it will perform a 360-degree tail swipe. This move has a very short tell, so watch for the boss's hips shifting before the spin.

#### Managing Vibration Strength
The white bar under the boss's HP represents its posture. Once this bar is depleted through parries and heavy attacks, the boss enters a "Weakened" state. During this time, it takes increased damage and remains stationary for several seconds. Save your Resonance Liberations (Ultimates) specifically for this window to maximize efficiency.

### Tactical Hologram: Difficulty Modifiers

In the Tactical Hologram: Tempest Mephis challenge, the boss becomes significantly more dangerous. Starting at Difficulty 4 and above, new mechanics are introduced:
1.  **Shadow Clones:** The boss will summon a clone that mimics its attacks. This means you may have to dodge two lasers or parry two dashes in rapid succession.
2.  **Increased Aggression:** The windows between attacks are shortened, requiring perfect stamina management.
3.  **One-Shot Potential:** At Difficulty 6, many of the boss's heavy attacks will instantly defeat a character regardless of their DEF stats.

### Tempest Mephis vs. Thundering Mephis

Players often confuse these two bosses as they share a similar model and name. However, their Echo skills and rewards differ significantly:
*   **Tempest Mephis:** Focuses on Heavy Attack damage buffs. Use this for characters whose kits revolve around heavy strikes.
*   **Thundering Mephis:** Focuses on Resonance Liberation (Ultimate) damage and Electro damage buffs. This is often preferred for Yinlin or quick-swap Electro teams.
*   **Farming Tip:** You can equip both on different characters in the same team to stack different Electro buffs, but you can only have one "active" transformation skill per character.

### Other Central Plains Bosses

The Central Plains is a high-traffic area for farming. While you are in the area for Tempest Mephis, you might also consider hunting:
*   **Impermanence Heron:** Located to the east, providing Havoc ascension materials.
*   **Feilian Beringal:** Found in the Giant Banyan area (technically Dim Forest but close to the southern border), providing Aero materials.

### Echo Farming Tips

To increase your efficiency when farming for a Gold-tier Tempest Mephis Echo:
1.  **Data Bank Level:** Ensure your Data Bank is at least Level 15. This is the threshold where Gold (5-star) Echoes have a 50% chance to drop. At Level 20, the drop rate for Gold Echoes increases to 80% or higher.
2.  **Enhanced Drop Rate:** Use your "Enhanced Drop Rate" chances from the Guidebook on this boss if you are specifically looking for a Crit Rate main stat piece, as 4-cost Echoes are the hardest to optimize.
3.  **Multiplayer:** If you are struggling with the higher difficulty versions or the Tactical Hologram, you can invite friends to your world. However, note that Tactical Hologram rewards are one-time first-clear bonuses, whereas the world boss rewards can be farmed as long as you have Waveplates.
