Mastering the progression systems in Hethereau requires a clear understanding of how different levels interact to power up your account and your Espers. This guide breaks down exactly how to increase your Resonance Level in Neverness to Everness (NTE) to unlock new story content and maximize your combat potential.

# How to Increase Resonance Level in Neverness to Everness (NTE)

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/how-to-increase-resonance-level-nte/inline-1.webp)


In Neverness to Everness, "Resonance Level" is a term that players often use interchangeably to describe three distinct progression systems. To optimize your gameplay, you must distinguish between your account's overall growth, the difficulty of the world around you, and the individual power peaks of your characters.

*   **Hunter Level:** This is your account-wide progression rank. It dictates your access to Main Episodes, determines when you can upgrade your Espers, and serves as the primary gate for all other systems.
*   **Appraisal Level:** Often referred to as the "World Level," this tier governs the strength of enemies in the open world and the quality of rewards dropped in Anomaly Zones. Increasing this level is necessary to raise the level cap of your characters and weapons.
*   **Character Resonance (Awakening):** This refers to the individual "stars" or "constellations" of an Esper. It is a vertical investment system where obtaining duplicate characters allows you to unlock powerful passive nodes and skill enhancements.

Understanding the interplay between these three systems is the foundation of efficient progression.

### Hunter Level vs. Character Resonance

While Hunter Level is earned through active play—such as completing quests and spending stamina—Character Resonance is primarily tied to the [Scarborough Fair gacha](https://ntecodex.com/faq) system and specific high-level rewards from the City Tycoon management mode. You cannot "grind" Character Resonance through combat; it requires specific resources called Mind Shards. Conversely, a high Character Resonance level on a character like Daffodil will not help you unlock new story chapters if your Hunter Level is too low.

### Why Increasing Your Level is Critical

Progression in NTE is strictly gated. If you do not maintain a steady increase in your Hunter Level, you will find yourself unable to progress the narrative or participate in limited-time events. Furthermore, the Appraisal Level acts as a multiplier for your efficiency. Staying at a low Appraisal Level means you are spending the same amount of Character Pixels (stamina) for significantly inferior rewards compared to a player at a higher tier.

| Term | Scope | Primary Source | Major Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hunter Level** | Account | Quests, Daily Tasks, Stamina | Unlocks story and Appraisal Quests |
| **Appraisal Level** | World | Ascension Quests at HL 20/40/50 | Better loot drops and higher level caps |
| **Character Resonance** | Individual | Mind Shards (Duplicates) | Unlocks A3/A6 unique passive effects |

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/how-to-increase-resonance-level-nte/inline-2.webp)


To increase your account-wide Resonance (Hunter Level), you must focus on activities that grant Hunter EXP. The most efficient methods include completing Main Episodes, finishing Spinoff Quests, and consistently clearing your Daily Participation Tasks in the Exploration Guide.

To increase individual Character Resonance, you must acquire Mind Shards for that specific Esper. These are obtained by pulling duplicates in the [Scarborough Fair](https://ntecodex.com/faq) gacha or by reaching milestone levels in the City Tycoon business simulation. Once you have the required Mind Shards, navigate to the Esper's "Awakening" menu to activate the next Resonance tier.

**Top 5 Sources for Hunter EXP:**
1.  **Main Episodes:** 500 – 2,000 EXP per mission.
2.  **Daily Participation Tasks:** Up to 1,500 EXP per day.
3.  **Anomaly Zones:** 5 Hunter EXP per 1 Character Pixel spent (1,200 EXP daily at the 240 cap).
4.  **Spinoff Quests:** 300 – 800 EXP per mission.
5.  **Wertheimer Tower Synchronization:** 100 – 200 EXP per tower discovered.

To move past level caps at Hunter Level 20, 40, and 50, you must complete specific Ascension Quests. Failure to complete these missions will cause excess Hunter EXP to be stored, but your Appraisal Level and Esper level caps will remain frozen.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/how-to-increase-resonance-level-nte/inline-3.webp)


The progression loop in Neverness to Everness is designed to reward both narrative completion and daily consistency. By balancing these activities, you ensure that your Appraisal Level keeps pace with your Hunter Level, preventing a bottleneck in character power.

### The Hunter Level & Appraisal Loop

The relationship between Hunter Level and Appraisal Level is the core of the NTE experience. As you gain Hunter EXP from various sources, your Hunter Level rises. At specific intervals—most notably at levels 20, 40, and 50—you will hit a "soft cap." At this point, your Appraisal Level must be increased through a dedicated Ascension Quest.

Increasing your Appraisal Level raises the base level of all monsters in Hethereau. While this makes exploration more dangerous, it is the only way to access higher-tier materials in Anomaly Zones. For example, undisclosed materials required for Level 60+ Esper Ascension only drop once you have reached the appropriate Appraisal tier.

### Maximizing Daily Hunter EXP

For players who have cleared the available Main Episodes, the Participation Task system becomes the primary driver of growth. This system resets daily and rewards you for activities you are likely already performing.

**Daily Participation Checklist:**
*   **Log in to the game:** 100 Points
*   **Spend 120 Character Pixels:** 200 Points
*   **Complete 1 Anomaly Hunt:** 150 Points
*   **Perform 1 City Tycoon Task:** 100 Points
*   **Interact with a ReroRero Phone Booth:** 50 Points

Reaching the maximum daily point threshold grants a total of 1,500 Hunter EXP and a small amount of undisclosed currency.

### Ascension Quests: Breaking the Level Cap

When you reach the Hunter Level milestones (20, 40, 50), a unique quest will appear in your mission log. These Ascension Quests typically involve a combat trial within a specialized Anomaly Zone. You will be required to defeat several waves of enemies and a final boss within a strict time limit. 

Completing these quests immediately raises your Appraisal Level. It is highly recommended to upgrade your main combat team’s weapons and Arcs to their current maximum before attempting these trials, as the difficulty spike in the open world following an Appraisal increase can be jarring if your team is under-geared.

### Character Resonance & The Awakening System

Character Resonance is the primary way to customize and significantly boost the power of your Espers beyond simple leveling. Each character has six Resonance Tiers, labeled A1 through A6.

*   **A1 & A2:** Usually provide flat stat increases to Attack, HP, or Crit Rate.
*   **A3 (First Resonance Effect):** This is a major milestone. For a character like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte), this might fundamentally change how her skill interacts with the environment or provide a massive energy regeneration boost.
*   **A4 & A5:** Further enhance skill levels and base statistics.
*   **A6 (Second Resonance Effect):** The final tier, often granting a transformative power that defines the character's "best-in-slot" status for endgame content.

To unlock these, you need Mind Shards. While most Mind Shards come from the Scarborough [Fair gacha](https://ntecodex.com/faq), F2P players should focus on the City Tycoon rewards.

### The Role of City Tycoon in Progression

City Tycoon is not just a side-activity; it is a vital source of Resonance materials. By managing businesses in Hethereau, you earn Tycoon EXP. 

Reaching **City Tycoon Level 18** is a critical goal for many players, as it rewards free Mind Shards for Chiz. This allows players to reach Chiz’s A1 or A2 Resonance without spending a single pull on a banner. Additionally, the Fons (gold) generated by your businesses is required for every Hunter Level and Esper Ascension upgrade, making business management a mandatory part of the "leveling" process.

### Exploration and Hidden EXP Sources

If you are just a few hundred EXP away from your next Hunter Level, Hethereau is filled with minor sources of experience:
*   **Wertheimer Towers:** Synchronizing these not only reveals the map but provides a quick burst of EXP.
*   **ReroRero Phone Booths:** These interactive objects often trigger short dialogues or mini-events that reward small amounts of Hunter EXP and Mhm! Coins.
*   **Gifts from 21:** Scattered throughout the city, these collectibles provide cumulative rewards as you find more of them.
*   **Nacupeda's Pool:** Spending Mhm! Coins here for "Luck" can occasionally trigger hidden world quests that offer substantial EXP rewards.

## Related

![Related](/img/how-to-increase-resonance-level-nte/inline-4.webp)


Increasing your Resonance Level is only one part of the character growth equation in Neverness to Everness. To truly maximize your effectiveness in combat, you must look at how these levels interact with other systems.

### Character Ascension and Leveling

While Hunter Level sets the *cap*, Character Ascension is the act of actually raising an Esper's level. This requires materials gathered from Anomaly Hunts. Even if you are Hunter Level 40, your characters will remain at Level 1 unless you feed them the necessary EXP materials and Ascension items. Always prioritize ascending your "Main DPS" first to ensure you can clear the higher-difficulty Anomaly Zones unlocked by your new Appraisal Level.

### Managing Your Stamina Systems

NTE uses a dual-stamina system: **Character Pixels** and **City Energy**.
*   **Character Pixels:** Used for combat-related rewards (Anomaly Zones, Bosses). This is your primary source of repeatable Hunter EXP.
*   **City Energy:** Used for City Tycoon actions. While this doesn't grant Hunter EXP directly, it provides the Fons and Mind Shards needed to actually implement the power gains you've unlocked.

Efficient players will ensure neither cap is ever reached, as wasted stamina is essentially wasted account progression.

### Combat Resonance vs. Account Resonance

It is important to distinguish the permanent "Character Resonance" (Awakening) from "Combat Resonance" mechanics. Characters like Daffodil utilize a "Resonance Stack" mechanic during real-time battle. This is a combat resource generated by using skills in a specific order. While increasing your Character Resonance (A1-A6) will often make it easier to generate these stacks or make them more powerful, they are a temporary buff during a fight and not a permanent level.

| Level 1-19 | 0 | 20 |
| Level 20-39 | 1 | 40 |
| Level 40-49 | 2 | 50 |
| Level 50-59 | 3 | 60 |

By following this roadmap and prioritizing your Daily Participation Tasks, you will ensure a steady climb through the Hunter Levels while keeping your Espers strong enough to handle the increasing threats of Hethereau.
