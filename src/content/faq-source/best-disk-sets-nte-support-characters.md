With the launch of Neverness to Everness (NTE) on April 29, 2026, players have quickly discovered that team damage is dictated less by raw stats and more by how effectively your support units can cycle their buffs.

# Best Disk Sets for NTE Support Characters: A Complete Cartridge Guide

In Neverness to Everness, the difference between a struggling team and a meta-defining squad often comes down to the gear equipped on your support Espers. While your primary damage dealers like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) or [Hotori](/characters/hotori-best-team-nte) hog the spotlight, units like Haniel and [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) provide the mechanical backbone that allows those carries to shine. This guide focuses on the most effective "disk sets"—officially known in-game as Cartridges—to maximize your team's utility, energy generation, and offensive output.

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/best-disk-sets-nte-support-characters/inline-1.webp)


When building a support unit in NTE, players typically ask: What are the most effective Cartridge sets for support units? Unlike traditional RPGs where you might simply stack health on a healer, NTE requires a more nuanced approach to gear. You must consider which Cartridge bonuses provide the highest utility for team-wide buffs and energy generation, as well as how specific support characters like Haniel, Sakiri, and Fadia benefit from different gear configurations.

### Understanding the Role of Support Gear in NTE
Support characters in NTE are not just there to heal or provide shields; they are the primary drivers of the "Esper Cycle." This system rewards players for swapping characters frequently to trigger elemental reactions like Scorch, Blossom, and Nova. Therefore, the best support gear focuses on two primary goals: increasing the frequency of Ultimates (via Charge Efficiency) and providing massive, team-wide stat multipliers that persist even when the support character is off-field.

### Cartridges vs. Disk Sets: Terminology Explained
While many players coming from other gacha titles refer to these items as "disks" or "artifacts," the official term in Neverness to Everness is **Cartridges**. These items are equipped in the character's **Console** and serve as the base for your build. However, a Cartridge alone does nothing; you must slot in **Modules**—the Tetris-like pieces—to activate the Cartridge’s 2-piece and 4-piece set bonuses. Throughout this guide, we will use these terms interchangeably to help you navigate the community meta.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/best-disk-sets-nte-support-characters/inline-2.webp)


The absolute best universal disk set for support characters in NTE is the **Speedy Hedgehog** Cartridge set. This set is widely considered S-Tier because it addresses the two most critical needs of a support: faster Ultimate uptime and team-wide damage amplification. 

Speedy Hedgehog provides a 12% boost to Charge Efficiency as its 2-piece bonus, allowing your support to regenerate Ultimate energy much faster. Its 4-piece bonus is even more potent, granting a massive 15% Team ATK buff for 20 seconds after the wearer uses an Ultimate. Because most support rotations in NTE last roughly 15 to 20 seconds, this allows for near-permanent buff uptime if your Charge Efficiency is high enough.

For characters who rely more on their Skills or swap-in "Redirect" abilities than their Ultimates, **Shadow Creed** is the top alternative. It offers a 10% ATK boost to the wearer (2-piece) and a significant 25% ATK buff for 20 seconds upon casting a Skill or Redirect (4-piece). Survival-focused supports like Fadia or Adler may prioritize sets that enhance HP or Shield strength, such as **Kingdom's Guard** or **Thea's Night Tavern**, though Speedy Hedgehog remains the meta choice for offensive utility in timed challenges like the Rabbit Hole.

### The S-Tier Choice: Speedy Hedgehog
Speedy Hedgehog is the "gold standard" for support gear in version 1.0. The reason it dominates the meta is its synergy with the game's core loop. In NTE, you want your supports to enter the field, use their Skill, fire their Ultimate, and swap out. Because the 15% ATK buff from the 4-piece set affects all allies, your main DPS can take the field and immediately benefit from the increased damage. Note that this effect does not stack; if you have two characters wearing Speedy Hedgehog, only one buff will apply.

### The A-Tier Alternative: Shadow Creed
Shadow Creed is often misunderstood as a DPS set because of its wearer-only ATK buff. However, for "Sub-DPS" supports who contribute a meaningful amount of damage while setting up reactions, Shadow Creed is excellent. It is particularly strong on characters with short Skill cooldowns or those who rely on high-frequency "Redirect" swaps to maintain their presence.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/best-disk-sets-nte-support-characters/inline-3.webp)


To truly master the NTE gear system, you need to look beyond the set bonuses and understand how the Console and Module systems interact with your support's stats.

### Speedy Hedgehog: The Ultimate Team Buffer
The 12% Charge Efficiency from the 2-piece Speedy Hedgehog set is more impactful than it appears on paper. In NTE, Ultimate generation is tied to "Charge," which is separate from "Cycle Energy." While Cycle Energy is gained through basic attacks and skills to trigger character swaps, Charge is what fuels your big cinematic attacks. 

By stacking Charge Efficiency on a support character, you ensure that their Ultimate is ready every time their swap-in cooldown ends. This creates a "perfect rotation" where the 20-second ATK buff from the 4-piece set is refreshed just as it expires, effectively providing your team with a permanent 15% damage floor increase.

### Shadow Creed: Maximum Skill Utility
Shadow Creed’s 25% ATK buff is massive but restricted to the wearer. This makes it the premier choice for supports who scale their utility off their own ATK stat. For example, if a character's shield or healing scales with their total ATK, Shadow Creed provides more "survival utility" than a pure HP set might. It is also the go-to set for characters who act as "Shield Breakers," where their personal damage output is necessary to deplete the enemy's Break Meter quickly.

### Optimizing the Console: Modules and Tetris Shapes
The Console system is where NTE gear complexity truly resides. Every character has a unique grid—a "Tetris" board—where you must fit Modules to activate your Cartridge. A standard S-Rank Cartridge requires four specific Module types (usually a mix of Type II, III, and IV) to activate its full 4-piece bonus.

To optimize a support Console, you should aim for the following:
1.  **Module Type Synergy:** Characters have "Console Bonuses" that trigger based on the *type* of modules installed. For example, Haniel gains a 10% ATK bonus for every Type III Module equipped.
2.  **Stat Priority:** For the center Cartridge piece, always look for **Charge Efficiency** as the main stat. For the individual Modules, prioritize **Cycle Intensity** (which boosts [reaction damage](https://ntecodex.com/guides/nte-chaos-vs-psyche-reaction-damage-gear-guide)) and **Break Intensity** (which helps stun bosses).
3.  **Shape Efficiency:** Use the "Rewind" system to gamble for Module shapes that take up the least amount of space (like 1x2 or 1x3 blocks) so you can fit more Modules into the grid beyond the four required for the set bonus.

### Haniel Build Guide: The F2P Support Queen
Haniel is a Psyche-element support that every player receives for free. She is arguably the best user of Speedy Hedgehog in the early game. 
*   **Set:** 4-pc Speedy Hedgehog.
*   **Console Focus:** Fit as many Type III Modules as possible to trigger her 10% ATK-per-module passive.
*   **Main Stats:** ATK% and Charge Efficiency.
*   **Playstyle:** Swap Haniel in to trigger a Nova reaction, use her Skill to pull enemies together, and immediately fire her Ultimate to trigger the Hedgehog team buff.

### Sakiri Build Guide: Crowd Control and Buffing
Sakiri is an Incantation-type support who excels at grouping enemies. Her gluttonous companion, Kiroumaru, deals significant "Break" damage.
*   **Set:** 4-pc Speedy Hedgehog or 4-pc Shadow Creed.
*   **Console Focus:** Prioritize **Break Intensity** substats.
*   **Main Stats:** Incantation DMG% and Cycle Intensity.
*   **Playstyle:** Sakiri should be used to initiate the "Scorch" reaction. By wearing Speedy Hedgehog, she provides the ATK buff while her companion holds enemies in place for your main DPS.

### Fadia and Adler: Survival Support Gear
Fadia (Psyche) and Adler (Incantation) are survival-focused units. While you can still run Speedy Hedgehog on them for the offensive buff, some high-difficulty content in the "Rabbit Hole" may require more tankiness.
*   **Fadia:** Benefits greatly from **Thea's Night Tavern** (+10% HP, +20% Healing Bonus) because her healing scales directly with her Max HP.
*   **Adler:** Works best with **Kingdom's Guard** (+15% DEF, +20% Shield Strength) since his walking-stick-generated shields scale with his DEF stat.

### Farming the Rabbit Hole: Where to Get Best-in-Slot Cartridges
To get these sets, you must farm the **Rabbit Hole Anomaly Zone** located south of the New Herland District. 
*   **Stamina Cost:** Each run costs 40 Character Pixels.
*   **Rarity:** S-Rank (Gold) Cartridges only begin dropping consistently once you reach Hunter Level 30.
*   **The Rewind System:** After farming the Rabbit Hole, you will earn **Carrota Coins**. Take these to the "Rewind" arcade machine in your Console menu. This is where you "pull" for Modules. Use the "Hard" difficulty setting (unlocked at Level 30) to ensure you get Gold-tier Modules with four substats visible from Level 1.

Gearing your support is only half the battle. To truly optimize your team, you must pair your Cartridges with the right Arcs and understand how they interact with the Esper Cycle.

### Top Arcs for NTE Supports
Arcs are the weapons of NTE. For support characters, you aren't looking for CRIT Rate; you are looking for energy generation and team buffs.

*   **Good Boy's Grand Adventure:** This is the signature Arc for Sakiri. It provides a base 45% ATK boost and up to 30% Charge Efficiency. More importantly, it grants a team ATK buff after an Ultimate, which stacks with the Speedy Hedgehog set for massive damage windows.
*   **Stellar Veil:** The best option for Haniel or Fadia. It boosts Psyche DMG and provides a stacking CRIT DMG buff that can be passed to the team through certain reaction chains.
*   **Hethereau's Keeper:** A "Solid" type Arc that is excellent for Adler, providing a flat ATK boost and summoning a companion to deal continuous damage while Adler is off-field.

### Understanding Esper Cycle Reactions
The gear you choose impacts how your reactions perform.
*   **Blossom (Anima):** Greatly affected by the wearer's ATK. If your Anima support wears Shadow Creed, the Blossom reaction deals significantly more damage.
*   **Scorch (Incantation):** Relies on high Cycle Intensity. Ensure your Incantation supports have high Cycle Intensity substats on their Modules.
*   **Nova (Psyche):** The strongest reaction for crowd control. Since Nova triggers frequently, Speedy Hedgehog is ideal for maintaining the buff through constant Psyche applications.
