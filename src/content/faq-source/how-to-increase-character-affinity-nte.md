The Bond system in Neverness to Everness is a relationship-tracking mechanic that measures the level of trust and intimacy between the protagonist, Esper Zero, and the various Espers recruited throughout the game. Players often wonder how to navigate the 10-tier Bond Level structure and what the most efficient methods are for reaching the maximum affinity with their favorite characters.

Key questions regarding affinity progression include

*   **What is the Bond system in Neverness to Everness?** It is a progression track for each character that unlocks lore, interactions, and combat bonuses.
*   **How do I raise the affinity level of my Espers?** Affinity is primarily increased through daily gifting, urban interactions, and the "Home Time" apartment system.
*   **Are there daily limits or caps on affinity progression?** Yes, there is a hard cap of 10 total gifts per day across all characters, and a sub-limit of 3 gifts per individual character.
*   **What are the specific benefits of reaching Bond Level 10?** Reaching the maximum level grants a unique character outfit (skin), exclusive profile cosmetics, and the final tier of combat stat bonuses.

### Understanding Bond Levels and Affinity Points (AP)

Every companion in NTE follows a 10-tier progression path. To move from one level to the next, you must accumulate a specific amount of Affinity Points (AP). While early levels require only a few hundred points, the climb to Level 10 is a long-term investment, requiring a total of 56,000 AP per character.

Progression is gated by milestones. For example, reaching Level 4 changes your status with a character to "Familiar," while Level 6 upgrades the relationship to "Close Friend." These milestones are not just titles; they serve as keys to unlock more intimate interactions like home visits or specific dating locations in Hethereau.

### The Importance of Relationship Progression in Hethereau

In the supernatural urban landscape of Hethereau, your bonds serve a functional purpose. High affinity levels provide permanent stat increases, such as percentage-based boosts to ATK, HP, or Critical Rate, which apply whenever that character is in your active party. Furthermore, the Bond system is the only way to acquire certain "Loungewear" outfits, which characters wear during the Home Time phase of the game.

## Direct Answer

The primary way to increase affinity in Neverness to Everness is by giving daily gifts, with a strict limit of 10 total gifts per account per day and 3 gifts per character. To maximize efficiency, you should always prioritize a character's "Preferred Gifts," which grant significantly more AP than standard items.

Universal gifts are the most efficient tools for progression. **"A Handwritten Letter"** is the single best item in the game, granting a massive **+2000 AP** to any character, while **"Fluffy Cotton"** provides a reliable **+400 AP** boost. Beyond gifting, supplemental AP can be earned through urban activities such as Movie Theater dates, Ferris Wheel interactions, and random "Passenger Events" triggered while using the taxi system.

For the fastest possible progression, you must level up **Esper Zero’s "Five-Star Paragon" life skill**. This skill provides a passive percentage boost (up to 5% per tier) to all affinity gains from gifts, ensuring that every item you give has a higher impact.

## Detailed Explanation

Increasing affinity in NTE requires a blend of daily resource management and active exploration. Because of the daily gifting caps, missing a day of interactions can significantly delay your progress toward unlocking a character's Level 10 skin.

### The Daily Gifting Strategy

Gifting is the cornerstone of the Bond system. Every character has a list of "Favorites" that can be viewed in their Bond menu. Giving a favorite item usually doubles or triples the AP gained compared to a neutral gift. For instance, giving Mint a standard snack might grant 50 AP, but giving her the "Waltz" music track provides a much larger boost.

To optimize your daily 10-gift limit
1.  Identify your top three priority characters.
2.  Give each of them 3 of their favorite gifts (9 gifts total).
3.  Use the final 10th gift slot on a fourth character or a universal item.

### Universal Gifts: Handwritten Letters and Fluffy Clouds

If you want to bypass the need to farm specific items for every character, focus on universal gifts.

*   **A Handwritten Letter (+2000 AP):** This is the "gold standard" of gifts. It is universally loved by the entire cast. You can obtain these through the Warp Exchange or by completing Circle Bounties. Unlike standard gifts, some versions of the Handwritten Letter are [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-24] regarding whether they bypass the daily 10-gift cap, though most players report they count toward the daily 3-per-character limit.
*   **Fluffy Cotton (+400 AP):** This item is highly valued by characters like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte). It can be generated passively through the "Compressed Boom Boom Cloud" furniture in your apartment.

### Urban Interactions: Dates, Taxis, and the Cinema

Once you reach Bond Level 2, you unlock the ability to invite characters to ride in your car as passengers. This is the simplest way to gain "passive" affinity while exploring Hethereau. Additionally, the taxi system occasionally triggers "Passenger Events" if you own the character currently appearing in the taxi pool, providing a small AP bonus for completing the ride.

High-level bonds (Level 4 and above) unlock "Urban Dates." These are specific locations on the map, such as the **Floe Cinema** or the **Hethereau Ferris Wheel**, where you can trigger unique cutscenes. A cinema date using a "Floe Cinema Ticket" grants a flat +400 AP and is a great way to supplement your daily gifting.

### The "Home Time" Apartment System

Reaching **Bond Level 4** is a major milestone because it unlocks the **Home Time** feature. This allows you to invite a character to live in one of your owned properties, such as the Wiener Apartments or Fenglin Villa. 

While in the apartment, characters will change into their casual loungewear and roam the rooms. You can engage in "Interactive Actions" which provide free AP once per day. These interactions include
*   **Hand-holding**
*   **Ear Care (ASMR interaction)**
*   **Sitting back-to-back**
*   **Playing Rock Paper Scissors**

These interactions are essential for free-to-play players as they do not consume Fons or gift items, though they provide smaller AP amounts (typically around +100 AP).

### Esper Zero's Life Skill: Five-Star Paragon

One of the most overlooked aspects of the affinity system is the protagonist's own skills. Under the Life Skills tab for Esper Zero, you will find the **Five-Star Paragon** skill. You should prioritize leveling this skill as early as possible.

*   **Tier 1:** +5% Affection from gifts (Bond Level 4 or below).
*   **Tier 2:** +5% Affection from gifts (Bond Level 6 or below).
*   **Tier 3-5:** Increases the Bond Level cap and further enhances gift efficiency.

Without this skill, you are essentially losing thousands of potential AP over the course of a month. Maxing this skill allows you to reach Bond Level 10 significantly faster than players who ignore it.

### Best Characters to Max First
When deciding who to prioritize, look at the stat bonuses. **Mint** is a popular first choice because her Bond rewards include a significant ATK% boost that benefits the entire team. **Nanally** is also a high priority for many players due to her status as a top-tier DPS, though she is notably one of the most expensive characters to gift, as her favorite items (like Blazing Crimson) cost more Fons at the florist than others.

### Farming Fons for Gifts
Buying gifts daily can drain your Fons quickly. To keep up with the costs, ensure you are clearing your daily commissions and participating in the "Coinicles Anomaly Zone" for extra currency. A full day of buying 400-AP gifts can cost upwards of 50,000 Fons, so budget accordingly.

### The Version 1.0 Cap for Aurelia
It is important to note that in the current version 1.0 of Neverness to Everness, **Aurelia's Bond Level is capped at Level 2**. While you can still give her gifts, you will not be able to progress her relationship to the "Home Time" phase until a future content update. Do not dump all your Handwritten Letters into her until this cap is raised.

### Unlocking the Compressed Boom Boom Cloud
To generate **Fluffy Cotton** for free, you must unlock the "Compressed Boom Boom Cloud" furniture. This is obtained by completing the side quest "The Fluffy Anomaly" in the Miguel District. Once placed in your apartment, it generates one Fluffy Cotton every 24 hours, allowing you to stockpile these +400 AP gifts for your favorite Espers.
