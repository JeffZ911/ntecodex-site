Choosing the right weapon for Bennett is the single most important decision for maximizing your team's damage potential in Genshin Impact. As of May 2026, despite the arrival of new regions and mechanics, the quest for the perfect free-to-play (F2P) sword remains a priority for every traveler.

# What Is the Best F2P Sword for Bennett in Genshin Impact?

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/best-f2p-sword-bennett-genshin-impact/inline-1.webp)


When looking for the best free-to-play (F2P) sword for Bennett, players are typically balancing two conflicting needs: maximizing the Attack (ATK) buff provided by his Elemental Burst and ensuring he has enough Energy Recharge (ER) to use that Burst on every single rotation. Because Bennett’s "Fantastic Voyage" scales exclusively off his Base ATK—which is only determined by his character level and the white ATK number on his weapon—a sword with a high Base ATK is mandatory. However, a high-attack sword is useless if Bennett cannot generate enough energy to actually cast his ultimate.

In the current meta of May 2026, identifying the most effective F2P options involves looking at craftable weapons from Sumeru and Fontaine, as well as reward-based weapons that do not require Gacha pulls. We must also consider how these weapons perform in newer endgame modes like the Stygian Onslaught, where energy management is even more punishing than in the classic Spiral Abyss.

### Why Choosing the Right F2P Sword Matters

Bennett remains the "True Pyro Archon" for a reason. His ability to consolidate healing and a massive flat ATK buff into one character slot is unmatched. However, using the wrong sword can result in a buff that is 100 to 200 points lower than it should be, or worse, a "dead" rotation where your main DPS loses their damage window because Bennett’s Burst isn't ready. For F2P players who cannot rely on 5-star weapons like Aquila Favonia or Skyward Blade, choosing a weapon with the correct stat distribution is the difference between clearing Floor 12 and falling short.

### Defining 'Free-to-Play' in the Current Meta

In 2026, "Free-to-Play" weapons include:
*   **Forgeable/Craftable Weapons:** Swords you can make at any blacksmith using Billets and regional ores.
*   **Fishing Rewards:** Weapons obtained by trading specific fish at regional Fishing Associations.
*   **Quest Rewards:** Weapons given directly for completing world quests or story arcs.
*   **3-Star Weapons:** Items commonly found in chests or through low-level exploration.

While some Gacha 4-star swords like The Alley Flash offer higher stats, they are not strictly F2P as they require luck or "Wishes" to obtain. This guide focuses on weapons that any player can guaranteedly acquire through gameplay and time.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/best-f2p-sword-bennett-genshin-impact/inline-2.webp)


The **Sapwood Blade** is the best overall F2P sword for Bennett in 2026. It offers a high Base ATK of 565 at Level 90, which is the second-highest available for a 4-star sword, and features an Energy Recharge substat (30.6%) that helps Bennett reach his high energy requirements.

The **Fleuve Cendre Ferryman** (the "metal pipe" from Fontaine fishing) is the best alternative for players who are struggling with energy management. While its Base ATK is lower (510), its massive 45.9% Energy Recharge substat and passive ER boost make it the most comfortable choice for solo-Pyro Bennett builds.

For players who have not yet reached Sumeru or Fontaine, the **Prototype Rancour** serves as a viable "stat stick" due to its 565 Base ATK, though it provides no Energy Recharge. For absolute beginners, the 3-star **Skyrider Sword** is the best entry-level option for its ER substat.

### The Top Pick: Sapwood Blade
The Sapwood Blade is widely considered the gold standard for F2P Bennett. It hits the "sweet spot" of high Base ATK and useful substats. Its passive, "Forest Sanctuary," creates a Leaf of Consciousness when a Dendro-related reaction is triggered. While Bennett himself is Pyro, he can easily trigger Burning or Burgeon in modern Dendro teams, allowing a teammate to pick up the leaf for a 60–120 Elemental Mastery (EM) buff. Even in non-Dendro teams, the 565 Base ATK makes it superior to almost every other free option.

### The Runner-Up: Fleuve Cendre Ferryman
The Fleuve Cendre Ferryman is favored in team compositions where Bennett is the only Pyro unit and lacks a "battery" (like Xiangling or Raiden Shogun) to help him charge his Burst. While you sacrifice 55 points of Base ATK compared to the Sapwood Blade—which translates to a slightly smaller ATK buff—the 45.9% ER substat is often more valuable for ensuring 100% Burst uptime in high-pressure environments like the Stygian Onslaught.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/best-f2p-sword-bennett-genshin-impact/inline-3.webp)


To understand why these swords are ranked this way, we must look at the math behind Bennett’s kit.

### Understanding Bennett's ATK Buff Mechanics

Bennett’s Elemental Burst, *Fantastic Voyage*, provides an ATK Bonus based on his **Base ATK**. It is a common mistake to stack ATK% on Bennett's artifacts (like an ATK% Sands or Circlet) to increase this buff. **This does not work.**

*   **Base ATK =** (Bennett’s Character Level Base ATK) + (Weapon’s Base ATK)
*   **Buff Calculation:** The field grants a percentage of this total Base ATK to any character standing inside it. 

Because the weapon's Base ATK is the only external factor that increases the buff, a sword with 565 Base ATK (like Sapwood Blade) will always provide a larger damage boost than a sword with 510 Base ATK (like Fleuve Cendre Ferryman) or 454 Base ATK (like Favonius Sword), assuming equal talent levels.

### Deep Dive: Sapwood Blade Stats and Passive

The Sapwood Blade is part of the Sumeru "Forest" weapon series. Its Level 90 stats are:
*   **Base ATK:** 565
*   **Substat:** 30.6% Energy Recharge
*   **Passive (R1-R5):** After triggering a Dendro reaction, a "Leaf of Consciousness" appears for 10 seconds. Picking it up grants 60 (up to 120 at R5) Elemental Mastery for 12 seconds.

For Bennett, the refinement level (R1 to R5) is not strictly necessary unless you are playing him in a Burgeon or Vaporize-Dendro hybrid team where the EM buff matters. For pure buffing, an R1 Sapwood Blade is just as effective as an R5 copy.

### Deep Dive: Fleuve Cendre Ferryman and the Fishing Grind

The Fleuve Cendre Ferryman is often called the "Pipe" by the community. Its Level 90 stats are:
*   **Base ATK:** 510
*   **Substat:** 45.9% Energy Recharge
*   **Passive (R5):** Increases Elemental Skill CRIT Rate by 16% and increases Energy Recharge by 32% for 5 seconds after using an Elemental Skill.

When you factor in the passive, Bennett can effectively reach over 77% Energy Recharge from this weapon alone after tapping his Skill. This makes it the ultimate "lazy" build weapon, allowing you to run an HP% Sands for more healing instead of an ER Sands if your substats are good enough.

### Prototype Rancour: Why It's Only a Temporary Fix

Every player receives a Prototype Rancour for free upon reaching Adventure Rank 10. It has 565 Base ATK, matching the Sapwood Blade. However, its substat is Physical DMG Bonus, which provides zero benefit to Bennett’s support capabilities. 

You should only use Prototype Rancour if you have not yet unlocked Sumeru or do not have the "Tale of the Desert" blueprint. Once you can craft the Sapwood Blade, you should switch immediately to gain the 30.6% Energy Recharge, which makes building Bennett significantly easier.

### How to Unlock Sumeru Craftable Blueprints (Tale of the Desert)

Unlocking the Sapwood Blade is tied to the **Aranyaka** questline, one of the longest quest chains in the game. You do not need to finish the entire questline, but you must reach the point where you unlock the "Tree of Dreams" in Vanarana.

1.  **Start the Quest:** Complete the "Woodland Encounter" quest in Sumeru to begin the Aranyaka series.
2.  **Obtain Stories:** Complete specific world quests within the Aranyaka chain (such as *Varuna Gatha* or *Vimana Agama*) to receive an item called **"Stories of You and the Aranara."**
3.  **Visit Aravinay:** Travel to the Tree of Dreams in northeast Vanarana. Next to the tree, you will find an Aranara named **Aravinay** who runs a shop.
4.  **Exchange for Blueprint:** Trade one "Stories of You and the Aranara" for the **Tale of the Desert** blueprint.
5.  **Forge:** Use the blueprint in your inventory, then visit any blacksmith.

**Materials Required to Craft Sapwood Blade (R1):**
*   1x Midlander Sword Billet
*   50x Crystal Chunk
*   50x White Iron Chunk
*   500x Mora

### Optimal Energy Recharge Thresholds for F2P Builds

Regardless of which sword you choose, Bennett needs a specific amount of Energy Recharge to function. In 2026, the recommended thresholds are:
*   **With Raiden Shogun or Triple Pyro:** 160% – 180% ER.
*   **With Xiangling (Standard National Team):** 180% – 200% ER.
*   **Solo Pyro Bennett:** 220%+ ER.

If you use the Sapwood Blade, you will almost certainly need an **Energy Recharge Sands** artifact to hit these numbers. If you use the Fleuve Cendre Ferryman, you might be able to use an **HP% Sands** if your other artifacts have high ER substats.

### Best 5-Star Upgrades for Bennett

If you happen to pull a 5-star sword, these are the rankings for Bennett:
1.  **Aquila Favonia / Mistsplitter Reforged:** Both have 674 Base ATK, the highest in the game. They provide the maximum possible buff but require heavy ER investment from artifacts.
2.  **Skyward Blade:** 608 Base ATK and 55.1% ER. This is arguably Bennett's best-in-slot (BiS) weapon because it combines high attack with massive energy.
3.  **Absolution:** undisclosed.

### Recommended Artifact Stats for Support Bennett

To complement your F2P sword, use the **4-piece Noblesse Oblige** set. This set provides an additional 20% ATK buff to the whole team when Bennett uses his Burst, stacking with his own flat ATK buff.

*   **Sands:** Energy Recharge (or HP% if ER is met)
*   **Goblet:** HP%
*   **Circlet:** Healing Bonus or HP%
*   **Substat Priority:** Energy Recharge > HP% > Flat HP.

### Top F2P Team Comps Featuring Bennett

Bennett’s versatility allows him to anchor several F2P-friendly teams:
*   **Hyperburgeon:** Bennett (Sapwood Blade), Dendro Traveler, Xingqiu, and Collei.
*   **The Classic National:** Bennett, Xiangling (The Catch), Xingqiu, and Sucrose.
*   **Melt Bennett:** Bennett, Kaeya, Rosaria, and Lynette.

### Future-Proofing: Natlan and Snezhnaya Swords

As we move deeper into the 5.x and 6.x patches, new regional weapons have appeared. The Natlan craftable sword, **Flute of Ezpitzal**, features a DEF% substat and a low Base ATK of 454, making it a poor choice for Bennett. 

Regarding the Snezhnayan (Nod-Krai) craftable sword series, the specific stats and names for the 6.0 weapon set are undisclosed. However, based on historical trends, unless a new sword with 565+ Base ATK and an ER substat is released, the Sapwood Blade will likely remain the king of F2P Bennett weapons through the end of the Teyvat chapter.
