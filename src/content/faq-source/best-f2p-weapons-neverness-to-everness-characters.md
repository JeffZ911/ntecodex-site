### Zero: How to Complete the 'Day Off' Hidden Quest
The 'Day Off' S-Rank Arc is the premier choice for the protagonist, Zero. To obtain it, you must locate the NPC named Eclipse, who appears at five different locations across Hethereau during specific in-game hours (usually between 18:00 and 06:00). 

The 5-step Eclipse conversation chain includes:
1.  **Hethereau Central Station:** Speak to Eclipse near the vending machines.
2.  **West End Alley:** Find him sitting on a crate behind the ramen shop.
3.  **Bridge Crossings Upper Deck:** Eclipse will be looking out over the water.
4.  **New Herland Park:** He is found near the central fountain.
5.  **The Rooftop of the Bureau:** After the first four interactions, Eclipse grants the 'Day Off' Arc as a reward for "understanding the value of rest."

This Arc provides undisclosed base ATK but features a unique passive that increases Zero’s ultimate energy regeneration by [information unavailable] whenever a dodge is successfully performed.

### Nanally & Hathor: Farming the Headless Rider for Raging Flames
The 'Raging Flames' Arc is a Solid-type weapon that excels on characters with high burst damage. It is a guaranteed drop table item from the 'Headless Rider' world boss located in the Bridge Crossings district. This boss spawns at night and requires players to manage a "Terror" meter. 

'Raging Flames' provides a massive boost to Critical Rate when the character’s health is above 70%. For Nanally, this synergizes with her long-range physical attacks, allowing her to stay out of danger while maintaining maximum crit uptime. Hathor also benefits from this Arc's high base physical damage, making it the most versatile F2P Solid Arc currently in the game.

### Sakiri: The Morphix Boss and Support Buff Synergy
Sakiri relies on the 'Good Boy’s Grand Adventure' Arc, which is obtained by defeating the 'Morphix' weekly boss. Morphix is a shapeshifting anomaly that requires players to match its current state of matter to deal damage. 

This Arc is unique because its passive focuses on team-wide buffs. When Sakiri uses her Skill, all party members receive a [information unavailable] increase to their elemental damage for 10 seconds. ### Chiz: Maximizing City Tycoon Levels for Contemplative Cat
Chiz’s best F2P option, 'Contemplative Cat', is not tied to combat but rather the City Tycoon progression system. As you renovate shops and manage properties in Hethereau, you earn Tycoon XP. Upon reaching Level 21, the 'Contemplative Cat' Liquid-type Arc is unlocked. 

This weapon scales with Chiz’s maximum health, providing undisclosed bonus healing and shield strength. Since Chiz functions as the primary defensive unit for many players, this Arc is essential for surviving late-game boss encounters where damage spikes are frequent.

*   **Mint:** Performs exceptionally well with 'Clear Skies', an A-Rank Arc that increases movement speed and attack speed after a dash.

### Arc Refinement: How to Maximize F2P Weapon Passives
To truly make F2P weapons competitive with gacha Arcs, you must utilize Refinement. This process requires duplicate copies of the same Arc. Since most F2P Arcs are farmable (like 'Raging Flames' from the Headless Rider), you can eventually reach Refinement Level 5. 

At Refinement 5, the passive effects of these weapons often double in potency. To facilitate this, you will need 'Pigments' and 'Shell Credits'. Pigments are obtained from decomposing lower-tier Arcs, while Shell Credits are the standard currency earned from almost all city activities.
