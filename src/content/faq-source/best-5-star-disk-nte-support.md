### Defining the Support Role in NTE

In Neverness to Everness, support characters aren't just there to heal; they are the engines that drive your team's "Burst Window." A support's primary job is to generate Energy (Charge), apply elemental status effects, and provide stat boosts that allow your main DPS to shred enemy shields. Unlike DPS units that focus on CRIT and Elemental DMG, supports prioritize utility stats that keep the team's rotation fluid.

### Why 5-Star (S-Class) Disks Matter

While 4-star (A-Class) disks are sufficient for clearing the main story, 5-star disks—often referred to as S-Class Cartridges—are essential for the "Beyond the Rails" endgame mode. These disks offer significantly higher base stats and, more importantly, 4-piece set bonuses that can increase total team damage by over 20%. Investing in 5-star support disks ensures your team has enough "Charge Efficiency" to use Ultimates as soon as their cooldowns expire.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/best-5-star-disk-nte-support/inline-3.webp)


Understanding why these disks dominate the meta requires looking at how stats interact with NTE's combat systems. The difference between a "good" build and an "optimal" build often comes down to how well your support disks feed into your DPS character's needs.

### Top Tier: Speedy Hedgehog Breakdown

Speedy Hedgehog is the gold standard for a reason. The 2-piece bonus provides a 12% boost to **Charge Efficiency**. In the high-intensity combat of NTE, "Charge" is the resource required to cast Ultimates. Without sufficient Charge Efficiency, your supports will often find their Ultimates are still on cooldown or undercharged when your DPS is ready to strike, leading to "dead air" in your rotation.

The 4-piece bonus is where the set truly shines. When the wearer casts their Ultimate, every member of the party receives a 15% ATK buff for 20 seconds. This is a "global" buff, meaning it stacks with the character's own innate buffs. For a character like Sakiri, who already provides elemental resonance, adding a flat 15% ATK boost makes her an indispensable part of any high-tier composition.

### The Offensive Alternative: Shadow Creed

While Speedy Hedgehog is great for the whole team, **Shadow Creed** is the preferred choice for supports whose buffs scale directly off their own Attack stat. Haniel is the prime example of this. Her healing and damage-boosting capabilities are calculated based on her total ATK.

By using Shadow Creed, you maximize Haniel's personal ATK stat, which in turn increases the value of the buffs she passes to the rest of the team. If your team lacks a high-investment DPS and relies on "stat-checking" enemies, the raw power provided by Shadow Creed can often outweigh the utility of Speedy Hedgehog.

### Niche Support: Tiny Big Adventure for Tanks

Not every support is focused on damage. Defensive supports like **Fadia** prioritize team survival. **Tiny Big Adventure** is the premier 5-star disk for these "Tank-Support" hybrids.
*   **2-Piece Effect:** Increases Max HP by 15%. This is vital for Fadia, as her shield durability scales directly with her total health.
*   **4-Piece Effect:** After using a Combat Skill, team Shield Strength is increased by 20%. This makes your team nearly invulnerable during boss "Enrage" phases, allowing you to ignore mechanics and continue attacking.

### Optimal Stat Distribution for Supports

When farming for these 5-star disks, the set bonus is only half the battle. You must also look for specific Main Stats and Sub-stats to ensure your support doesn't fall behind.

**Recommended Main Stats:**
*   **Slot 1 (Fixed):** HP
*   **Slot 2 (Fixed):** ATK
*   **Slot 3 (Variable):** Charge Efficiency % (Priority #1) or ATK%
*   **Slot 4 (Variable):** Elemental DMG Bonus (if the support does off-field damage) or HP%
*   **Slot 5 (Variable):** CRIT Rate % or Healing Bonus %

**Sub-stat Priority:**
1.  **Charge Efficiency:** Aim for at least 140% total Charge Efficiency on supports.
2.  **Flat HP / HP%:** Essential for surviving late-game Anomaly zones where one hit can take 50% of your health.
3.  **Flat DEF / DEF%:** Often overlooked, but necessary for the "Midas Arc Workshop" challenges.
