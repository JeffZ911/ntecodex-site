# How to Unlock Chiz in Neverness to Everness

To [unlock Chiz](https://ntecodex.com/faq) in Neverness to Everness, you must reach Tycoon Level 18 within the City Tycoon system. Unlike gacha-exclusive units, Chiz is a free S-Rank Cosmos DPS rewarded for progressing through Hethereau's business simulation. Once you reach this milestone, you can claim her directly from the City Tycoon rewards menu.

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/how-to-unlock-chiz-nte/inline-1.webp)


Players often wonder how to obtain Chiz, given her high ranking on current character tier lists. In the 1.0 launch version of Neverness to Everness (NTE), Chiz occupies a unique position as a powerful S-Rank character that does not require "scarborough fair" pulls or premium currency to obtain. Instead, she serves as the primary reward for engaging with the game's deep life-simulation mechanics.

As a Cosmos-element Damage dealer, Chiz excels at sustained DPS through her unique "Grain" resource system. Her combat style is heavily influenced by her "Gas" Arc compatibility, making her a versatile pick for players who prefer tactical, resource-based rotations over pure button-mashing. Because she is a permanent progression reward, she remains available to all players regardless of the current limited-time banner rotation.

### Who is Chiz in Neverness to Everness?

Chiz is introduced as the Lobby Manager for the Pink Paws Bank in the New Herland District. While she may appear bashful or even overwhelmed by her administrative duties, her combat performance tells a different story. In battle, she utilizes "Exiled Swordplay" to generate Grain, which she then spends on powerful "Redirect Skills" to demolish Anomalies.

### Is Chiz Available via Gacha Summons?

Chiz is not available through the standard or limited-time gacha banners. According to official progression guides, she is strictly tied to the City Tycoon system. This makes her the most accessible S-Rank DPS for Free-to-Play (F2P) players, as her entire kit—including her signature weapon and all six Awakening levels—can be earned by playing through Hethereau's hobbies and management tasks.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/how-to-unlock-chiz-nte/inline-2.webp)


The path to unlocking Chiz is linear but requires a significant time investment in Hethereau’s urban activities. The primary requirement is reaching Tycoon Level 18. This level is not reached through combat experience (Hunter EXP) but through the accumulation of Fons and the completion of specific management objectives.

### The Tycoon Level 18 Milestone

Reaching Tycoon Level 18 requires you to have earned a cumulative total of 5,332,600 Fons throughout your account's history. It is important to note that this requirement tracks "Total Fons Earned," meaning you can freely spend your money on cars, apartments, and gear without delaying your progress toward unlocking Chiz. In addition to the currency requirement, you must reach 50 Footprint Points in the Unheard Shores region by completing exploration tasks and finding collectibles.

### Where to Claim Your Free Chiz

Once you meet the level 18 requirements, open the main menu and navigate to the **City Tycoon** interface (accessible via the F5 shortcut on PC). Within the rewards tab, you will see a progression track. Click on the Level 18 icon to claim your first copy of Chiz.

Beyond the initial unlock, the City Tycoon system provides everything needed to maximize her potential:

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/how-to-unlock-chiz-nte/inline-3.webp)


The City Tycoon system is a comprehensive business simulation that runs alongside the main RPG elements of Neverness to Everness. To unlock Chiz quickly, you must master the loop of earning Fons and spending City Stamina effectively.

### Mastering the City Tycoon System

You unlock the City Tycoon system after completing the main story quest involving the "Viewfinder" enemy and the antique shop crew. Following this, the quest "Good Morning, Hethereau" will appear in your log. Completing this quest introduces you to Chiz herself and grants you the debit card necessary to track your Tycoon progress.

The system is governed by City Stamina, a resource that is separate from your regular combat-related stamina. Unlike regular energy that refills daily, City Stamina only resets weekly, making it the most valuable resource for players rushing to Level 18.

### Fastest Ways to Earn Tycoon XP

Tycoon Level increases are triggered by hitting specific "Total Fons Earned" thresholds and completing "Guide Quests." To maximize your XP gain, focus on these activities in order of efficiency:

1.  **Guide Quests:** Each Tycoon Level has a specific objective, such as purchasing a certain property or reaching a racing milestone. Prioritize these, as they are mandatory for the level-up to trigger.
2.  **Property Management:** Owning businesses like the Cafe by Origen generates passive Fons. While the initial investment is high, the long-term returns are essential for hitting the multi-million Fons requirements of higher levels.
3.  **Hethereau Hobbies:** Racing and Fishing provide significant Fons for a relatively low time investment. Racing, in particular, offers permanent rewards and "Star Rewards" that provide large chunks of currency upon first completion.

### Pink Paws Heist Strategy and Routes

The Pink Paws Heist is the single most efficient way to farm Fons in NTE, but it is limited by a [bi-weekly cap of 1,000,000 Fons](https://www.reddit.com/r/NevernessToEverness/comments/optimal_heist_route/). This mode is unlocked at Tycoon Level 10 and can be played solo or in multiplayer.

To maximize your take-home pay, follow the "Optimal Fons Route":
*   **Ground Floor (G):** Quickly clear the initial guards and head for the elevators.
*   **Lower Ground 1 (LG1):** Focus on the special jump puzzles that lead to hidden vaults. These contain higher-density Fons stacks than the main corridors.
*   **Lower Ground 2 (LG2):** Navigate the Laser Room carefully. Tripping alarms spawns elite guards that waste time.
*   **Gold Bosses:** Defeating "Gold-tier" enemies is the fastest way to hit the 150k–200k Fons per run mark. These bosses have specific spawn patterns that favor teams who split up to cover multiple vault entrances.

### Farming Fons: The Currency of Progression

If you have hit your bi-weekly Heist cap, you can still farm Fons through several other methods:
*   **Blind Mammon Anomalies:** These are world events that appear randomly. Defeating a Blind Mammon can yield roughly 40,000 Fons per encounter.
*   **Daily Anomaly Commissions:** Completing your four daily commissions provides a steady stream of about 400,000 Fons per week.
*   **Beyond the Rails:** This endgame mode offers one-time Fons rewards for clearing specific combat floors.
*   **Warp Pieces:** Collecting and turning in Warp Pieces found during exploration can net over 400,000 Fons.

### Unlocking Chiz's Signature Weapon and Awakenings

Once you unlock Chiz at Level 18, your goal should shift to Level 21 to obtain her signature weapon, the Contemplative Cat. This Gas-type Arc is tailor-made for her kit, providing a 2.5% Cosmos DMG boost for every 100,000 Fons you currently hold in your inventory (capping at 10 stacks or 1,000,000 Fons).

Continuing to level up the Tycoon system will eventually grant you enough copies to reach Awakening 6 (A6). At A6, Chiz becomes one of the most powerful units in the game, rivaling limited S-Rank characters in raw damage output.

After unlocking Chiz, the next step is optimizing her gear and team to handle Hethereau's toughest Anomalies. Her performance scales dramatically with the right build and synergies.

### Best Chiz Build and Arc Compatibility

Chiz's kit revolves around her "Grain" mechanic. Her Basic Attack, **Exiled Swordplay**, generates Grain, which is then consumed by her **Redirect Skill** to deal massive Cosmos damage. Her Ultimate, **Zero-Sum Game**, enters the "Grain Market" state, where she can deal burst damage based on her current Grain investment.

*   **Best-in-Slot Arc:** Contemplative Cat. This is her signature weapon and should be equipped as soon as you reach Tycoon Level 21.
*   **Recommended Cartridge:** Lost Radiance (4-Piece). This set provides a 10% Cosmos DMG bonus and allows Chiz to ignore 25% of an enemy's defense for 20 seconds after using her Ultimate.
*   **Primary Stats:** Focus on CRIT DMG and ATK%. Since she gains a significant amount of Cosmos DMG from her weapon and modules, increasing her crit consistency is vital.

### Top Team Compositions for Chiz

Chiz functions best as a Main DPS in teams that can trigger frequent Esper Cycles. She has excellent synergy with characters who can provide off-field elemental application.

1.  **The "Top-Trio" Cycle:** Chiz (Cosmos), Hathor (Lakshana), and Jiuyuan (Anima). This team covers three core elements, enabling Blossom and Remora reactions. Hathor provides CRIT Rate buffs, while Jiuyuan groups enemies for Chiz's AoE attacks.
2.  **Ultimate Uptime Team:** Chiz, Zero, Jiuyuan, and Hathor. Zero's ability to instantly trigger Esper Cycles allows Chiz to generate Ultimate energy at a rapid pace, ensuring her "Grain Market" state is active as often as possible.
3.  **F2P Friendly:** Chiz, [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide), and Zero. This team uses the free units provided early in the story to support Chiz's damage output through basic elemental reactions.

### Other Free S-Rank Characters in NTE

While Chiz is the most prominent free S-Rank, Neverness to Everness offers other high-rarity rewards through various systems. For example, Zero is provided during the tutorial as a Cosmos-element support, and certain weapons can be earned through the "Beyond the Rails" challenge mode. However, Chiz remains the only S-Rank Damage dealer whose entire power ceiling (A6) can be reached purely through the City Tycoon system.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I get Chiz if I miss her "banner"?
Chiz does not have a limited-time banner. She is a permanent reward within the City Tycoon system. You can unlock her at any time by reaching Tycoon Level 18, regardless of which characters are currently featured in the Scarborough Fair gacha.

### What is the fastest way to get Fons for Tycoon Level 18?
The fastest method is the Pink Paws Heist, which allows you to earn up to 1,000,000 Fons every two weeks. Supplement this by completing daily Anomaly Commissions and hunting for Blind Mammon world events, which provide consistent currency without using your weekly City Stamina.

### Does spending Fons lower my Tycoon Level?
No, the City Tycoon system tracks your "Total Fons Earned" over the lifetime of your account. You can spend your Fons on cars, furniture, or upgrades without losing progress toward Level 18 or your Chiz rewards.

### Is Chiz better than gacha S-Rank characters?
Chiz is widely considered a "T0" or top-tier DPS, especially at higher Awakening levels. While limited characters like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) may offer higher burst damage, Chiz's ability to be fully maxed out for free makes her the most reliable and powerful unit for most players' accounts.
