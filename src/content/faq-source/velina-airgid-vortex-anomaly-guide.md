# Velina Airgid Vortex Anomaly Guide: Neverness to Everness

[Velina Airgid](https://ntecodex.com/faq) and the Vortex mechanic are not currently featured as playable features in Neverness to Everness (NTE); instead, they are associated with the game Zenless Zone Zero. This guide details the actual Anomaly Commissions and supernatural [exploration mechanics](https://ntecodex.com/guides/nte-vs-zzz-urban-exploration-gear-comparison) found within the city of Hethereau in Neverness to Everness.

### Common Player Confusion: NTE vs. ZZZ

The most frequent source of confusion for new Appraisers is the similarity between Neverness to Everness and Zenless Zone Zero (ZZZ). Both games feature urban environments, supernatural phenomena, and high-fidelity action combat. However, characters like [Velina Airgid](https://ntecodex.com/faq) are exclusive to the roster of Zenless Zone Zero, where she serves as an S-Rank Wind Anomaly Agent in the External Strategy Department.

In Neverness to Everness, the term "Anomaly" refers to the supernatural entities and reality-warping events that players investigate as part of their daily routine at the Eibon antique shop. While both games use the word, the mechanics differ significantly.

### Defining 'Anomaly' in Neverness to Everness

In the world of NTE, an Anomaly is not a character class but a supernatural manifestation that disrupts the peace of Hethereau. These occurrences can range from invisible invaders to environmental puzzles that defy the laws of physics. Players take on the role of an unlicensed Anomaly Hunter (or Appraiser) to track, contain, and nullify these threats using their Esper abilities.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/velina-airgid-vortex-anomaly-guide/inline-2.webp)


[Velina Airgid](https://ntecodex.com/faq) is currently not featured as a character or entity within Neverness to Everness; she is an S-Rank Wind Anomaly Agent belonging to the [Roscaelifer External Strategy Department](https://zenless.hoyoverse.com/en-us/news/123456) in Zenless Zone Zero. Similarly, the "[Airgid Vortex](https://ntecodex.com/faq)" is not present as a weapon or mechanic in NTE as of the April 29, 2026, release.

In Neverness to Everness, "Anomalies" refer to the supernatural entities and environmental puzzles found throughout districts like New Herland and Illusion Town. Players must use the Anomagram—a specialized tracking tool—to locate these phenomena and resolve them to earn rewards such as Fons, Annuliths, and Beetle Coins.

### The Factual Status of Velina Airgid in NTE

While some players have searched for [Velina Airgid](https://ntecodex.com/faq) within the NTE character database, she is not part of the 16 confirmed launch characters. The wind-based gameplay some associate with her "Vortex" style is instead found in characters like Nanally, an Anima-element character who uses Fangs and Claws to deliver high-speed follow-up attacks.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/velina-airgid-vortex-anomaly-guide/inline-3.webp)


Neverness to Everness centers on an urban open-world experience where the city of Hethereau is plagued by supernatural "Anomalies." As an Appraiser, you are tasked with investigating these disturbances. Unlike traditional combat-only missions, Anomaly Commissions often involve environmental interaction, puzzle-solving, and utilizing the specific Esper skills of your team.

### How to Use the Anomagram to Track Anomalies

The Anomagram is the essential interface for any Anomaly Hunter. It functions as a digital map that highlights active supernatural disturbances across Hethereau's districts. When you open the Anomagram, you will see various icons indicating the difficulty and type of anomaly.

1.  **Scanning:** Navigate to districts like Miguel or the Bridge Crossings to trigger the Anomagram's proximity sensor.
2.  **Tracking:** Select a commission from the list to mark its rough location on your HUD.
3.  **Investigation:** Upon reaching the area, you may need to use Esper vision to reveal hidden entities or environmental clues.

### Step-by-Step Guide: Solving Common NTE Anomalies

Many anomalies in Hethereau are more than just fights. They require specific steps to "nullify" the phenomenon.

#### The 'Lonely Player' (Wandering Puppets) Walkthrough
This Anomaly Commission in Illusion Town is a prime example of the game's puzzle-combat hybrid.
*   **Location:** Illusion Town.
*   **Objective:** Locate and interact with a series of spectral puppets that only appear under certain conditions.
*   **Step 1:** Use a character with high mobility, like Nanally, to reach the rooftops where the first puppet resides.
*   **Step 2:** Follow the sound of the music box to find the subsequent puppets hidden behind destructible environmental objects.
*   **Step 3:** Defeat the final manifestation to clear the anomaly and claim your rewards.

#### Plot Hole Repair Project: Puzzle Mechanics
In the New Herland District, you will encounter "Plot Hole" anomalies. These require you to "Change Your Perspective" by standing in specific locations to align fragmented environmental pieces into a coherent image. This mechanic is vital for progressing through the 11 available commissions in that region.

### High-Risk Anomaly Zones: Farming and Account Progression

For players looking to maximize their account strength, High-Risk Anomaly Zones offer the best farming opportunities. These zones feature tougher enemies and more complex hazards but drop rare materials like specialized upgrade materials (for related gear) and significant amounts of Hunter EXP.

### Best NTE Characters for Anomaly Hunting

Not all characters are equally suited for every anomaly. Choosing the right team can simplify difficult puzzles or high-health boss encounters.

*   **Nanally (Anima):** Her Ichidame's Authority allows her to maintain constant pressure with follow-up attacks, making her excellent for clearing waves of minor supernatural entities quickly.
*   **Lacrimosa (Chaos):** A versatile S-Rank character who can copy enemy skills and switch between melee and ranged forms. Her "Heist" ability is particularly useful for anomalies that require specific elemental interactions you might otherwise lack in your party.
*   **Mint (Anima):** An A-Rank character who serves as a "living dictionary." While her combat stats are lower, she excels in synergizing with Zero and providing utility in exploration-heavy commissions.

### Weapon (Arc) Drops from Anomaly Commissions

Completing these commissions is the primary way to obtain Arcs, the weapons used by Espers. For example, Nanally's performance is significantly boosted by her signature Arc, which enhances her follow-up attack damage. Other Arcs, such as those used by Baicang or Fadia, can be farmed through specific world bosses found at the end of long anomaly quest chains.

### Hethereau Exploration: Finding Hidden Supernatural Entities

Beyond the marked commissions, Hethereau is filled with hidden encounters. Using an interactive map can help you find all five explorable districts, including the countryside and amusement centers. These hidden anomalies often reward players with Beetle Coins, a currency used to purchase rare upgrade materials and cosmetics for your character's urban life, such as car modifications or business upgrades.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Velina Airgid coming to Neverness to Everness?
There is no official confirmation of [Velina Airgid](https://ntecodex.com/faq) appearing in Neverness to Everness. She is a character specifically designed for Zenless Zone Zero by HoYoverse, whereas NTE is developed by Hotta Studio.

### How do I trigger the Vortex mechanic in NTE?
The Vortex mechanic does not exist in Neverness to Everness. Players looking for similar battlefield control should use Anima-element characters like Nanally or explore environmental hazards that create wind-based displacement.

### What are the best rewards from Anomaly Commissions?
The most valuable rewards include Fons for character leveling and rare Arcs (weapons) dropped by anomaly bosses. Additionally, players can earn Beetle Coins for the urban life simulation aspects of the game.

### Where can I find the Anomagram in the game menu?
The Anomagram is typically accessed through the main map interface or a dedicated hotkey. It allows Appraisers to see a comprehensive list of active supernatural disturbances in their current district.
