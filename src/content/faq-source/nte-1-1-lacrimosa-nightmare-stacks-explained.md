## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/nte-1-1-lacrimosa-nightmare-stacks-explained/inline-2.webp)


Nightmare stacks are a unique Chaos Damage-over-Time (DoT) debuff applied by Lacrimosa's attacks. Unlike standard elemental statuses, this debuff functions as a stacking "curse" that eats away at an enemy's health bar independently of Lacrimosa’s active presence on the field.

The debuff caps at 10 stacks per enemy target. At the base Awakening level (A0), the Nightmare DoT has a fixed 50% Crit Rate. This is a crucial mechanic to understand: it means the DoT ticks ignore your character's total Crit Rate stat from equipment and instead rely entirely on your Crit DMG for scaling. To maximize this damage, players should prioritize Crit DMG and ATK% in their builds.

These stacks serve as the primary engine for the Discord reaction, which occurs when Chaos interacts with Incantation and Psyche elements. Triggering Discord provides Lacrimosa's highest burst damage windows, effectively "detonating" the accumulated pressure of the Nightmare stacks for massive damage.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/nte-1-1-lacrimosa-nightmare-stacks-explained/inline-3.webp)


The efficacy of a Lacrimosa build depends on how quickly you can reach the 10-stack threshold. Because her damage is back-loaded into the DoT ticks and the Discord reaction, a slow ramp-up time can lead to significant DPS loss in timed challenges.

### How to Apply 10 Nightmare Stacks Fast

Application mechanics are tied to her Basic Attacks and her specialized skills. Every hit from her melee-focused Basic Attack sequence applies 1 stack of Nightmare. Given that her melee combo features rapid, multi-hit sequences, staying in close range is the fastest way to build stacks manually. 

In her ranged mode, her projectiles also apply stacks, but at a slightly slower interval. This mode is better suited for maintaining the 10-stack cap from a distance during boss phase transitions or when an enemy is surrounded by hazardous ground effects.

### The Fixed 50% Crit Rate Mechanic

The most unique part of the Nightmare mechanic is the fixed 50% Crit Rate. In most urban open-world RPGs, DoT effects cannot crit at all. Neverness to Everness breaks this mold by allowing Lacrimosa’s ticks to crit half the time by default. This makes her uniquely powerful even with low-investment gear, as you do not need to hunt for Crit Rate substats. Instead, every point of Crit DMG you acquire directly increases the ceiling of those 50% "lucky" ticks.

### Triggering Discord Reactions with Nightmare Stacks

The [Discord reaction](https://www.reddit.com/r/NevernessToEverness/comments/1eb2216/nte_combat_mechanics_elements_and_reactions/) is the pinnacle of Lacrimosa’s combat loop. When Nightmare stacks are active on a target, triggering Discord by cycling through Incantation and Psyche teammates deals 400% of Lacrimosa's ATK as bonus damage. This reaction essentially checks for the presence of the Nightmare status; the more stacks present, the more consistent the overall damage output becomes during the reaction window. Her passive ability, "Tomato Banquet," further amplifies this damage based on the total number of Chaos-related debuffs on the enemy.

### Optimizing 'The Last Rose' and Chaos Thorn Stacks

For players with her signature Arc, "The Last Rose," the Nightmare mechanic gains an additional layer. This Arc converts Nightmare DoT ticks into "Chaos Thorn" stacks for Lacrimosa herself. These thorns grant up to +120% Crit DMG at 10 stacks, creating a synergistic loop: the Nightmare DoT ticks (which have a fixed 50% Crit Rate) generate thorns, which then increase the Crit DMG of those very same ticks.

### Nightmare Damage Scaling Comparison

*   **A0 (Base):** Standard DoT damage with 50% fixed Crit Rate.
*   **A1 (First Awakening):** Increases Nightmare DMG by 50%. This is the most significant power spike for players looking to use her as a primary DPS.
*   **A5 (Max Awakening):** Introduces a HP-cap reduction mechanic. Nightmare ticks at this level can bypass certain boss defensive thresholds, making it invaluable for the highest floors of the endgame towers.

### The Instant 10-Stack Boss Rotation

To achieve maximum efficiency, follow this rotation:
1.  **Open with Skill 3:** This instantly applies 5 Nightmare stacks to all enemies in the vicinity.
2.  **Melee Combo:** Perform a full Basic Attack sequence in melee mode to add the remaining 5 stacks.
3.  **Swap to Teammates:** Switch to an Incantation character (like [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide)) and then a Psyche character (like Haniel) to trigger the Discord cycle.
4.  **Return to Lacrimosa:** Use her Ultimate to refresh the duration of the 10 stacks before they expire, maintaining the damage pressure.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I increase the 50% Crit Rate of Nightmare stacks?
No, the 50% Crit Rate is a fixed mechanic for the Nightmare status effect and does not scale with your character's Crit Rate stat. To increase the damage of these crits, you must focus on increasing your Crit DMG stat through Arcs and Cartridges.

### Does the Nightmare DoT snapshot Lacrimosa's stats?
Yes, the Nightmare stacks calculate their damage based on Lacrimosa’s ATK and Crit DMG at the moment they are applied or refreshed. This means you should ensure all team buffs are active before using her Skill 3 or Ultimate to apply or refresh stacks.

### What happens if I apply more than 10 stacks?
The stack count will remain at 10, but applying a new stack will refresh the duration of the entire 10-stack debuff. This is why it is important to keep attacking or use her Ultimate to ensure the stacks never fall off the target.

### Is Lacrimosa viable without her signature Arc?
While "The Last Rose" provides a massive 120% Crit DMG boost, Lacrimosa remains a top-tier Chaos DPS with F2P options like "Shiny Days." Her core mechanic of fixed crit rates makes her very floor-friendly for players who haven't perfected their gear yet.
