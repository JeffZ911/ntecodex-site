# Best Disks for Support in Neverness to Everness (NTE): Full Guide

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/best-disks-for-nte-support/inline-1.webp)


Players often ask which "disks" they should equip on their support Espers to maximize team performance. In Neverness to Everness, the term "disks" generally refers to two distinct but interconnected gear systems: Arcs and Cartridges. Arcs serve as the primary weapon for each character, providing base stats and unique passive skills, while Cartridges are set-based equipment that slots into the character's Console to provide powerful team-wide buffs or defensive scaling.

Equipping support-oriented gear sets is essential for two reasons. First, they provide massive stat boosts, such as team-wide Attack (ATK) increases or enemy Defense (DEF) reduction, which allow your main DPS to reach their maximum damage ceiling. Second, they enhance survivability through shields and healing bonuses, ensuring your team can withstand the aggressive attack patterns of bosses like the Nestbound Bird or the Headless Rider.

To get the most out of your roster, you must prioritize specific gear for top-tier supports:
*   **[Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide):** Needs high Charge Efficiency and team ATK buffs.
*   **Adler:** Requires DEF-scaling gear to strengthen his shields.
*   **Edgar:** Relies on HP-scaling and healing bonus sets to keep the party healthy.
*   **Haniel:** Benefits from off-field ATK buffs and break intensity.

### Understanding the terminology: Arcs vs. Cartridges

Arcs are the "weapons" of Neverness to Everness. Each Arc belongs to a specific type—Gas, Liquid, Plasma, Solid, or Synthesis—and must match the Esper's type to be equipped. Arcs provide a flat ATK value and a secondary stat like CRIT Rate, DEF, or HP. Their passive "Arc Skills" often trigger based on the character's abilities, such as casting an Ultimate or a Redirect Skill.

Cartridges are the "armor" or "relic" system. They are equipped in sets of two or four to activate "Epic" or "Legendary" bonuses. However, unlike traditional gear systems, activating these bonuses requires you to slot specific "Modules" into a character's Console. The Console is a grid-based system where you must fit Tetris-like Module shapes to complete the Cartridge's requirements.

### The role of Support Espers in the 1.0 Meta

In the current 1.0 meta, support Espers are the backbone of the "Esper Cycle" system. By rotating through your supports and triggering elemental reactions like Scorch, Nova, and Hexed, you build up energy and damage multipliers for your main attacker. Supports like Sakiri and Adler are not just there to provide utility; they also contribute to the "Break Gauge," helping to stagger enemies faster so your DPS can unleash their full burst.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/best-disks-for-nte-support/inline-2.webp)


The best support "Disk" (Arc) for defensive utility is the **Umbrella**, an A-Rank Synthesis Arc that provides a massive DEF substat and strengthens shields. For offensive support, the best S-Rank Arc is **Blow Up the Crowd**, which grants the active character up to an 18% ATK buff while the wearer is off-field.

When it comes to Cartridges, the absolute Best-in-Slot (BiS) set for offensive buffers is **Speedy Hedgehog**. Its 4-piece Legendary bonus provides a 15% team-wide ATK buff for 20 seconds after the wearer uses an Ultimate. For defensive supports who scale with Defense, such as Adler, **Kingdom's Guard** is the mandatory choice due to its 15% DEF boost and 20% shield intensity increase.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/best-disks-for-nte-support/inline-3.webp)


The gear system in Neverness to Everness is deep, requiring a balance between raw stats and the "Tetris" layout of the Console. To maximize your support units, you must understand how these sets interact with character-specific mechanics.

### Deep Dive into Speedy Hedgehog

Speedy Hedgehog is the premier support Cartridge because it addresses the two most critical needs of a buffer: energy and scaling. 
*   **2-piece Effect:** Grants +12% Charge Efficiency (Energy Recharge). This is vital because support ultimates, like Sakiri's "Feast of Gluttony," often have high energy costs (120 Energy). Higher efficiency ensures you can cycle your Ultimate every rotation.
*   **4-piece Effect:** After casting an Ultimate, all allies receive a 15% ATK buff for 20 seconds. This effect does not stack, meaning you should only run one Speedy Hedgehog set per team. If you have two supports, the second should run a different set like Shadow Creed or Kingdom's Guard.

### Kingdom's Guard Synergy

Kingdom's Guard is designed specifically for characters who convert their defensive stats into utility. Adler is the primary benefactor here. His skill, "Evil's Bane," creates a shield that scales directly with his DEF. 
*   **2-piece Effect:** Increases DEF by 15%.
*   **4-piece Effect:** Increases the wearer's shield strength by 20%. 
By stacking DEF main stats on the Modules used to activate this set, Adler can provide shields that absorb thousands of damage, allowing your DPS to ignore minor enemy hits and continue their attack strings uninterrupted.

### Shadow Creed as an Alternative

Shadow Creed is often overlooked but serves as an excellent set for sub-DPS supports who need to contribute damage while cycling. 
*   **2-piece Effect:** +10% ATK.
*   **4-piece Effect:** Increases the wearer's ATK by 25% for 20 seconds after casting a Redirect Skill. 
This is particularly useful for Haniel. Since Haniel's signature Arc, "Blow Up the Crowd," already buffs the team while she is off-field, Shadow Creed allows her to deal significant damage during the brief window she is on-field to cast her skills.

### Optimizing the Console: Module Shapes and Stats

The Console system is where you finalize your build. To activate a 4-piece Cartridge bonus, you must slot four Modules that match the Cartridge's required shapes into the grid. 
*   **Speedy Hedgehog:** Requires four "Corner" Modules. These are typically Type III or Type IV modules. Support characters should prioritize Type III modules because they often carry Charge Efficiency or HP/DEF substats.
*   **Kingdom's Guard:** Requires specific Type III shapes (Horizontal, Vertical, and L-shapes). 
*   **Stat Priority:** For supports, the "Main Stat" on the Cartridge is crucial. 
    *   **Sakiri:** Target ATK% or Incantation DMG.
    *   **Adler:** Target DEF% Main stats.
    *   **Edgar:** Target Healing Bonus or HP% Main stats.

### Best Arcs for Support Espers

Choosing the right Arc (weapon) is just as important as the Cartridge set.
*   **Good Boy's Grand Adventure (Sakiri BiS):** This S-Rank Gas Arc is perfect for Sakiri because she naturally applies control effects (Suppress) with her Ultimate. This triggers the Arc's secondary buff, providing a total of 16% Team ATK on top of the 18% Charge Efficiency.
*   **Tears Beneath the Mask (Warning Gaze):** This Arc is a top-tier defensive utility choice. It applies the "Warning Gaze" debuff to enemies hit by the wearer's Ultimate, reducing the damage they deal by 18% for 20 seconds. This is a life-saver in high-difficulty Anomaly Zones where bosses can one-shot squishier DPS units.
*   **Call of the Twisted City (Edgar BiS):** For A-Rank healers, this Liquid Arc is unrivaled. It provides a massive 37.5% HP boost at level 80, which directly increases Edgar's healing output from his "Wild Current" skill.

### Farming Guide for Support Disks

Acquiring high-rarity support gear requires consistent farming in the New Herland District.
1.  **Cartridges:** These are farmed in the **Rabbit Hole Anomaly Zone**. Each run costs 40 Character Pixels (stamina). Higher stages unlock as your Hunter Level increases, eventually dropping S-Rank (Orange) Cartridges.
2.  **Modules:** Modules are obtained through the **Rewind** system. You collect "Carrotas" from Rabbit Hole domains and spend them in the Rewind menu to "roll" for random Module shapes and stats. 
3.  **Hunter Exchange:** You can purchase specific S-Rank Cartridges like Shadow Creed and Speedy Hedgehog from the Hunter Exchange once you reach City Tycoon Rank 6. This requires managing your properties in Hethereau and completing city-life activities to earn Fons.

Choosing the right disks is only half the battle. To truly master support in Neverness to Everness, you need to understand how these units fit into the wider combat system.

### Recommended Support Team Compositions

Support gear is most effective when paired with complementary elemental types to trigger the **Esper Cycle**.
*   **The Scorch Team:** Sakiri (Support) + [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) (DPS) + Adler (Shielder). Sakiri buffs Nanally's ATK while Adler provides the shields necessary for Nanally to stay on-field. The interaction between Sakiri's Incantation and Nanally's attacks triggers Scorch, dealing massive damage over time.
*   **The Discord Team:** Haniel (Support) + Sakiri (Support) + Daffodil (DPS). This team focuses on high-frequency rotations. With both supports running ATK-buffing Arcs and Speedy Hedgehog, Daffodil can reach absurd damage numbers during her burst window.

### How to Upgrade Arcs and Cartridges

Upgrading your disks is essential for mid-to-late game progression.
*   **Arc Mixing:** You can "Mix" duplicate copies of an Arc to increase its passive effect level (up to level 5). For example, the Warning Gaze damage reduction on "Tears Beneath the Mask" increases from 18% to 30% at max mixing level.
*   **Cartridge Leveling:** Leveling a Cartridge only increases its Main Stat. Sub-stats are fixed and unlock every five levels (at +5, +10, +15, and +20). Because sub-stats don't increase in value when leveling, you should only invest resources into Cartridges that have high initial rolls in useful stats like Charge Efficiency or ATK%.
*   **Module Rewind:** If you have a Module with the right shape but bad stats, you can use the Rewind system to "re-roll" the sub-stats using Carrota Coins. This is the primary way to min-max your Console layout.

By focusing on the Speedy Hedgehog and Kingdom's Guard sets while equipping Arcs like Good Boy's Grand Adventure or Umbrella, you will create a support foundation capable of carrying your team through any challenge Hethereau has to offer.
