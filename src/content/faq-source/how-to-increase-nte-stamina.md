## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/how-to-increase-nte-stamina/inline-1.webp)


Players often ask "how do I get more stamina" without realizing that Neverness to Everness utilizes three distinct resource pools. Depending on whether you are trying to sprint longer, farm more upgrade materials, or engage in urban side activities, you will need to target a different system.

The yellow bar on your UI represents **Exploration Stamina**, which dictates your ability to sprint, climb, and dodge. The blue bar represents **Character Pixels** (often called Energy), which is consumed when claiming rewards from domains and bosses. Finally, the purple bar represents **City Stamina**, a weekly resource used for urban "hobbies" like racing or running a cafe.

Confusion often arises because these systems recover at different rates and require entirely different progression paths to upgrade. If you find yourself unable to climb the skyscrapers in the Misaki District or running out of rewards in the Paradoxical Domains, you are likely hitting a cap in one of these three categories.

### What is Exploration Stamina?
Exploration Stamina is the traditional open-world movement resource. It is consumed by actions such as sprinting, swimming, climbing, and mid-air gliding. In the early game, this pool is relatively small, making vertical traversal in the dense urban landscape of Hesperia difficult. Increasing this cap is a permanent account upgrade that allows for more fluid movement and faster traversal through the city's high-rise architecture.

### What are Character Pixels (Energy)?
Character Pixels serve as the "gacha energy" or "resin" equivalent in Neverness to Everness. This resource is spent to acquire rewards from combat challenges, such as the Echoes of the Past or weapon ascension material domains. Unlike movement stamina, Character Pixels recover over time (at a rate of 1 every 6 minutes) up to a hard cap. Increasing this cap allows you to store more energy for long sessions, though it does not inherently increase the recovery rate.

### What is City Stamina?
City Stamina is a unique system tied to the "Everness" side of the game. It is used to participate in Hethereau Hobbies—mini-games and management sims that provide Fons (the main currency) and Tycoon experience. Activities like high-speed racing, taxi driving, and managing the local cafe consume this resource. Unlike the other two, City Stamina is largely managed on a weekly refresh cycle, though it has a daily recovery component tied to your City Tycoon rank.

### Upgrading the Witch's House with Oracle Stones

The Witch's House is the most important landmark for exploration-focused players. Located in the coastal area southwest of the Hankaku Street district, this hub acts as the primary repository for your exploration progress.

To increase your yellow stamina bar, you must hunt for Oracle Birds. These are small, glowing avians scattered across the rooftops, alleys, and parks of Hesperia. Catching a bird grants you Oracle Stones. Once you have a sufficient amount, you can offer them at the Witch's House to increase your "Witch's Favor" level.

Reaching **Level 3** at the Witch's House is a critical milestone for every player. At this level, the game unlocks a passive radar feature that reveals nearby bird locations on your mini-map. This significantly accelerates your ability to find stones and maximize your movement stamina.
