# How to Farm NTE Currency Fast: The Ultimate Guide to Annulith and Fons

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/how-to-farm-nte-currency-fast/inline-1.webp)


To master the economy of Neverness to Everness (NTE), players must first understand the distinction between premium resources and local city funds. Efficient farming isn't just about grinding combat; it’s about leveraging the Hethereau social systems and the City Tycoon engine.

**What are the primary currencies in Neverness to Everness (NTE)?**
The economy revolves around two main pillars: **Annulith**, the premium currency used for gacha pulls, and **Fons**, the primary in-game gold used for everything from character upgrades to purchasing luxury vehicles and apartments. Secondary currencies like **Beetle Coins** and **Mhm Coins** gate specific progression items and rare weapons (Arcs).

**How can players maximize their daily and weekly currency gains?**
Maximizing gains requires completing the **Circle Bounty** (the seasonal battle pass) daily tasks and spending **City Stamina** before the weekly reset. High-efficiency players prioritize activities with the highest Fons-per-minute return, such as the **Pink Paws Heist**.

**What are the most efficient methods for farming gacha pulls and upgrade gold?**
For gacha pulls, clearing the world map to 100% and finishing the main story are the fastest one-time sources of Annulith. For gold (Fons), the **Cafe by Origen** provides the best passive return, while the character **Chiz** offers a unique way to "extract" money directly from enemies during combat.

**How does the City Tycoon system impact your overall wealth in Hethereau?**
The City Tycoon system acts as a multiplier for your income. As you raise your Tycoon Level, you unlock higher-tier business opportunities, increase your City Stamina cap, and gain access to the **Hunter Exchange**, where Fons can be traded for premium materials.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/how-to-farm-nte-currency-fast/inline-2.webp)


The fastest way to farm premium currency (**Annulith**) in Neverness to Everness is through consistent daily login rewards, completing the main story chapters, and hunting high-tier achievements in the Exploration Guide. These sources provide the bulk of the 160 Annulith required for a single gacha pull.

To farm **Fons** (gold) fast, prioritize the **Pink Paws Heist** at the Pink Paws Bank. This activity unlocks at **Tycoon Level 10** and allows you to secure a massive 1,000,000 Fons burst every two weeks. For long-term wealth, you must manage the **Cafe by Origen**; upgrading this business creates a passive income stream that generates Fons even while you are offline.

Additionally, you should spend your **City Stamina** on Hethereau Hobbies such as fishing, racing, or taxi services. These activities convert stamina into Fons at a fixed **1:1,000 ratio**, meaning 100 City Stamina is worth exactly 100,000 Fons. Finally, always convert your Annulith into **Solid Dice** for limited character banners, as these are the most valuable pull tokens in the game.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/how-to-farm-nte-currency-fast/inline-3.webp)


Farming in Neverness to Everness is split between active gameplay and passive management. To reach the late-game economy where you can afford 10,000,000 Fons for a luxury villa or thousands of Annulith for an S-Rank character, you must utilize every system available in Hethereau.

### Farming Annulith for Gacha Pulls

Annulith is the lifeblood of your roster. Each pull on a character banner costs 160 Annulith, and a 10-pull requires 1,600.

*   **Circle Bounty Daily Quests:** This is your most reliable income stream. Completing the daily tasks in the Circle Bounty menu provides roughly 60 Annulith per day, alongside 1,000 Battle Pass EXP. 
*   **Exploration Rewards:** The city of Hethereau is divided into several districts, including Illusion Town and the New Herland District. Each zone contains hidden chests, anomaly puzzles, and "Lost Pieces" that provide one-time Annulith rewards. Using the interactive map to clear these zones to 100% is the fastest way for a new player to accumulate enough for their first 90-pull pity.
*   **Participation Tasks:** Found within the Exploration Guide, these tasks reset daily and offer a tiered reward system. Reaching 100 Participation Points grants a final chest containing Annulith and Beetle Coins.

### Maximizing Fons via the City Tycoon System

Fons is the "common" currency, but the amounts required for high-end vehicles and character ascension are staggering.

*   **The Pink Paws Heist:** Located at the Pink Paws Bank, this is the single largest source of immediate Fons. It is an extraction-style minigame where you loot the bank's vaults while fighting off "Rainmen" enemies. The heist has a bi-weekly cap of 1,000,000 Fons. High-efficiency players focus on "Golden Mobs" and "Golden Safes" to hit this cap in just a few runs.
*   **Cafe by Origen:** This management simulator is your primary engine for wealth. Once unlocked at Tycoon Level 4, you can assign employees (characters like Mint or [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte)) to run the shop. To maximize profits, you must upgrade your recipe slots and ensure you restock ingredients every 24 hours. A fully upgraded cafe can generate over 50,000 Fons passively per day.
*   **Chiz Farming:** The character Chiz has a unique passive ability where her charged basic attack "extracts" Fons directly from world enemies. While this has a daily cap (roughly 30,000 to 40,000 Fons) and a lifetime cap of 500,000 Fons, it is a great way to top off your wallet while completing combat commissions.

### The Role of City Stamina in Wealth Accumulation

NTE uses a dual-stamina system. While **Character Pixels** are used for combat rewards and character materials, **City Stamina** is reserved specifically for the City Tycoon system.

*   **Weekly Reset:** City Stamina resets weekly (Sunday 8:00 PM UTC). Any unspent stamina is lost, so you must deplete it before the reset.
*   **Hethereau Hobbies:** Use your City Stamina on activities like fishing, taxi driving, or city delivery. Every point of City Stamina spent translates to 1,000 Fons. If you are short on time, the "Manager's Special" at the cafe or quick delivery routes are the fastest ways to dump stamina for cash.

### Secondary Currencies: Beetle Coins and Tri-Keys

Beyond the main two, you must track specialized currencies to optimize your gear.

*   **Mhm Coins:** Visit **Nacupeda’s Pool** in Illusion Town daily. By interacting with the fountain and "Making a Sincere Wish," you receive 1 to 5 Mhm Coins. Collecting 100 of these allows you to purchase the **"Your Happiness is Priceless"** S-Rank Arc (weapon) from the Arc Shop for free.
*   **Beetle Coins:** These are earned through the Circle Bounty and Anomaly Commissions. They are primarily used in the Hunter Exchange to buy high-level upgrade materials that cannot be purchased with Fons.
*   **Tri-Keys:** These are the specific tokens for the **Arc Research Program** (weapon gacha). You should never spend Annulith on these unless you have already secured your target character, as the game provides a steady supply of Tri-Keys through monthly shop resets and Tycoon Level rewards.

Understanding how to farm currency is only half the battle; knowing where to spend it ensures you don't hit a progression wall.

### NTE Gacha System and Pity Explained

The gacha in Neverness to Everness is called the **Scarborough Fair**. It uses a "Dice Board" system where your character moves across a board to land on rewards.

*   **Hard Pity:** You are guaranteed an S-Rank character at **90 pulls**.
*   **No 50/50:** Unlike many other urban RPGs, NTE does not have a 50/50 system on its limited banners. If you pull an S-Rank on a limited character banner, it is guaranteed to be the featured character.
*   **Dice Conversion:** 160 Annulith = 1 Solid Die (Limited) or 1 Fabricated Die (Standard). Never use Annulith for Fabricated Dice, as the game rewards these frequently through progression.

### Best Items to Buy with Fons

In the early game, Fons should be treated as a progression tool rather than for cosmetics.

1.  **Shop Upgrades:** Prioritize buying new Cafe locations and upgrading your management level. This increases your passive income.
2.  **Vehicle Performance:** Upgrading your car's engine and handling allows you to win higher-tier races, which provide better one-time Annulith rewards.
3.  **Character Ascension:** Ascension materials for your main DPS should always be bought from the Fons shop if you are short on Character Pixels.
4.  **Avoid Early Cosmetics:** Do not spend Fons on expensive apartment furniture or character outfits until your Tycoon Level is at least 25 and your main team is fully leveled.

### How to Increase Your Tycoon Level Fast

Your Tycoon Level gates your earning potential. To raise it quickly
*   **Complete the Exploration Guide:** This acts as a checklist for city activities.
*   **Spend City Stamina:** Every point of City Stamina spent contributes to your Tycoon EXP.
*   **Property Investment:** Buying your first apartment and the first Cafe location provides a massive jump in Tycoon EXP.
*   **Warp Pieces and Lost Pieces:** When you pull duplicate characters or Arcs, you receive Warp Pieces or Lost Pieces. These can be used in the **Fair Exchange** section of the Mall. Prioritize buying the monthly discounted Solid Dice and Tri-Keys to further your farming efficiency.
