Furthermore, you must exhaust your **240 Character Pixels (Stamina)** every day on Anomaly Zones, such as **Houdinii's Magic Stage** or the **Bubble Can Factory**, to earn roughly 1,200 XP daily as a byproduct of material farming. For long-term growth, engage with the **City Tycoon** system and turn in **Oracle Stones** at the **Witch's House** to maintain a steady flow of account experience.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/how-to-level-up-fast-nte/inline-3.webp)


The progression system in Neverness to Everness is a dual-layered mechanic. Your **Hunter Level** represents your overall account experience, while the **Appraisal Level** acts as the World Tier. Increasing your Hunter Level is the only way to trigger the Appraisal Level Up quests, which in turn raise the level cap for your Espers and Arcs.

### Mastering the Exploration Guide & Daily Quests
The Exploration Guide is the most critical menu for consistent leveling. Upon completing Prologue - Part 2, you unlock the Participation Tasks tab. Every day at 5:00 AM (Server Time), four specific tasks refresh. These typically include:
1. **Daily Login:** 200 XP.
2. **Earn 10,000 Fons:** 200 XP.
3. **Consume Character Pixels:** 300 XP.
4. **Reach 100 Participation Points:** 800 XP.

Completing all of these ensures a total of 1,500 XP. Because these tasks often overlap with other activities—like spending stamina or managing your cafe—they should be the first thing you check upon logging in.

### Spending Character Pixels Efficiently
Character Pixels are the lifeblood of character growth. You generate 1 Pixel every 6 minutes, totaling 240 Pixels every 24 hours. While it is tempting to farm for high-end gear immediately, you should follow the **"Level 30 Rush"** strategy. 

Before Hunter Level 30, Legendary (gold) Cartridges and Modules do not drop from Anomaly Zones. Therefore, you should spend your Pixels exclusively on **Elevation Materials** and **Ability Upgrades**. 
* **Houdinii's Magic Stage:** Farm this for Character and Arc EXP materials (Dye).
* **Bubble Can Factory:** Farm this for Arc Ascension materials like Appleseeds.

Spending all 240 Pixels daily provides a side benefit of approximately 1,200 Hunter XP, which is essential for hitting the next Appraisal milestone.

### The Witch's House and Oracle Stone Turn-ins
Located southwest of the first Vimma Tower in Bridge Crossings, the **Witch's House** is home to an NPC named Blackbird. As you explore Hethereau, you will encounter **Oracle Ravens**. Interacting with these birds causes them to fly away, leaving behind **Oracle Stones**.

These stones are a "hidden" XP source. Turning them in to Blackbird in batches provides significant Hunter XP milestones. Additionally, Blackbird offers Divinations that can reveal the locations of treasure chests or hidden side quests, further fueling your XP gain.

### Anomaly Commissions and the Anomagram
While exploring the city, check your **Anomagram** for Anomaly Commissions. These are short, combat-focused or puzzle-based tasks scattered across districts like Hankaku Street. Each commission typically rewards between 160 and 300 XP. These are excellent for filling the gap when you are just a few hundred XP away from a Hunter Level increase.

### Appraisal Level Up Quests and XP Capping
Do not ignore the Appraisal Level Up quests. These appear at Hunter Levels **20, 30, 40, 45, 50, and 55**. If you reach these levels and do not complete the associated quest, your Hunter XP will "cap," and any additional XP earned will be wasted. 

Completing these quests immediately is vital because they scale the rewards you get from Anomaly Zones. For instance, reaching Appraisal Level 3 (Hunter Level 30) is the requirement to start seeing S-Rank Consoles and Cartridges in the **Rabbit Hole** Anomaly Zone.

### City Tycoon: Passive and Active XP Gains
NTE's unique urban management system, City Tycoon, is more than just a side activity. Every time you increase your Tycoon Level, you receive Hunter XP rewards. 
* **Cafe by Origen:** Unlock this at Tycoon Level 4 to start generating Fons passively. 
* **Pink Paws Bank Heist:** Unlock this at Tycoon Level 11 for high-yield Fons and XP tasks.
* **Chiz:** Reaching Tycoon Level 18 grants you the 4-star Esper Chiz for free, who has unique passives for farming Fons in combat.

By managing your properties and completing Tycoon Objectives (such as buying the **Novis ST-X 950** or inviting characters like Mint to your apartment), you gain a steady stream of XP that doesn't require Character Pixels.

Leveling your account is only the first step. To survive the higher Appraisal Levels, you must translate that XP into combat power.

### Character and Arc Progression
Once your Hunter Level increases, you must manually ascend your Espers and Arcs. Character Ascension requires materials like **Paradoxical Whispers**, dropped by world anomalies. Arcs (the weapon system) require **Mixing** to increase their passive intensity. Always prioritize your main DPS—such as **[Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte)** from "The Ichi-daime" banner—to ensure you can clear Anomaly Zones at higher difficulties quickly.

### Essential Traversal Tips (Novis ST-X 950)
Fast leveling requires fast movement. While **Vimma Towers** and **Phone Booths** provide fast travel points (and 50-100 XP each upon first activation), the **Novis ST-X 950** is your primary tool for navigating between mission markers. Use Nanally’s **"Ichi-daime's Authority"** skill to walk on walls and ceilings to reach Oracle Ravens hidden in high-altitude spots that are otherwise inaccessible.

### Upcoming Events and Limited-Time XP Bonuses
Keep an eye on the **Anomagram** for limited-time events. Developers often introduce "Double Drop" periods for Anomaly Zones or special City Festivals that provide bonus Hunter XP for completing urban activities. Participation in these events is often the fastest way to bridge the gap between Level 40 and 50.
