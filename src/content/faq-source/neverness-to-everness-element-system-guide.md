## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/neverness-to-everness-element-system-guide/inline-3.webp)


The Esper Cycle is the heart of NTE’s combat. It rewards players for active character swapping and precise timing rather than simply staying on a single powerful DPS unit.

### The Six Elements of Hethereau

Every character in NTE is assigned one of the six Attributes. These elements are not just for flavor; they dictate which characters can effectively work together. The elements are arranged in a fixed circle:

1.  **Cosmos**
2.  **Anima**
3.  **Incantation**
4.  **Chaos**
5.  **Psyche**
6.  **Lakshana**
7.  (Back to Cosmos)

Reactions only occur between neighbors. For example, a Cosmos character can react with an Anima character or a Lakshana character. However, a Cosmos character cannot directly trigger a Duo Reaction with a Chaos character because they are on opposite sides of the wheel.

### Mastering the Esper Cycle Wheel

To trigger a reaction, you must first fill the **Esper Meter** of the active character. This bar is located around the character’s portrait. Once the meter is full, the portraits of your off-field characters will glow, indicating a "Cycle Swap" is available.

*   **Cycle Rate:** This stat determines how quickly a character generates Esper energy. High Cycle Rate is vital for support characters who need to trigger reactions frequently.
*   **Cycle Intensity:** This stat scales the effectiveness of the reaction itself. For instance, a higher Cycle Intensity will increase the damage dealt by a Scorch reaction or the slow percentage of a Remora reaction.

### Advanced Trio Reactions: Charge and Discord

While Duo Reactions are the bread and butter of combat, Trio Reactions are the "finishers" used for elite enemies and bosses. A Trio Reaction is triggered when you complete a sequence of three adjacent elements (e.g., Cosmos -> Anima -> Incantation).

*   **Charge:** Usually associated with the "Top Trio" (Cosmos, Anima, Lakshana). Successfully completing this cycle grants a massive burst of Ultimate energy to the entire team.
*   **Discord:** Associated with the "Bottom Trio" (Incantation, Chaos, Psyche). This is the primary tool for shield breaking. It deals immense "Stability Damage," which helps deplete the enemy's Break Gauge significantly faster than standard attacks.

### Filling the Esper Meter: Parries and Cycle Rate

You cannot spam reactions constantly; you must earn them. The Esper Meter fills through:
1.  **Basic Attacks and Skills:** Consistent damage generates a steady flow of energy.
2.  **Perfect Parries:** Successfully parrying an enemy attack instantly grants a large chunk of Esper Meter, often allowing for an immediate Cycle Swap.
3.  **Active Skills:** Certain characters have skills specifically designed to boost their own Cycle Rate or provide energy to the team.
