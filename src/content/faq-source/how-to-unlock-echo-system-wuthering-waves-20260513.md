Capturing and equipping the souls of defeated enemies is the core progression mechanic in Wuthering Waves. You cannot progress far without these "Echoes," so knowing exactly when and how the system becomes available is essential for every new Rover.

# How to Unlock the Echo System in Wuthering Waves

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/how-to-unlock-echo-system-wuthering-waves-20260513/inline-1.webp)


Players often arrive in Solaris-3 wondering exactly when they can start their monster-collection journey. Because the Echo system serves as the primary "gear" or "artifact" system for your Resonators, unlocking it early allows you to begin building stats and gaining active combat abilities immediately.

### Identifying the Echo System Unlock Point
The ability to absorb Echoes is not available the second you start the game. You will spend the first 30 to 45 minutes of the Prologue learning basic combat and movement. The system only becomes active once you have established your presence in the main city and met with the researchers responsible for studying Tacet Discords. You will know the system is about to unlock when the narrative shifts toward investigating a specific "First Resonance" event outside the city walls.

### Prerequisites for New Players
There are no complex Union Level (UL) requirements or hidden side quests needed to access Echoes. As long as you follow the main story markers, the game will force you into the unlock tutorial. You cannot bypass this system, nor can you "miss" it by exploring too far; the Pangu Terminal on your character's back remains inactive for absorption until the specific quest script triggers.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/how-to-unlock-echo-system-wuthering-waves-20260513/inline-2.webp)


The Echo system is unlocked during the Main Quest **"Utterance of Marvels: II"** in the Prologue (Chapter 1, Act 1). You do not need to reach a specific Union Level to start using the system; simply follow the main story campaign until you arrive at the city of Jinzhou.

The system is introduced through a scripted tutorial involving the characters **Baizhi** and **Chixia**. Shortly after your arrival at the Academy in Jinzhou, you are sent to a nearby field to investigate Tacet Discord activity. Your first Echo absorption is a guaranteed, scripted event during this "First Resonance" phase, where the game teaches you how to use the Pangu Terminal to capture a defeated monster's frequency. Once this tutorial is complete, you are free to roam the overworld and capture any Echoes you encounter.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/how-to-unlock-echo-system-wuthering-waves-20260513/inline-3.webp)


The Echo system is deeply integrated into the SOL3 world logic. Understanding how to unlock its full potential requires mastering the Data Bank, which acts as your collection level and determines the power of the gear you find.

### The Prologue Quest Walkthrough
To reach the unlock point, follow these steps:
1.  Complete the opening combat sequence and meet Yangyang and Chixia.
2.  Follow the quest markers to reach the main hub city, Jinzhou.
3.  Visit the Academy to meet Baizhi, who explains the nature of Tacet Discords and the Pangu Terminal.
4.  Proceed to the Gorge of Spirits area as directed by the quest "Utterance of Marvels: II."
5.  Defeat the designated group of enemies.
6.  Look for the yellow, glowing hologram left behind by the defeated monster.
7.  Interact with it to "Absorb" the Echo.

Once this interaction finishes, the Echo tab in your menu becomes accessible, and you can begin equipping these creatures to your Resonators.

### Mastering the Data Bank Leveling System
Your Data Bank level is the most important number in Wuthering Waves regarding Echoes. It dictates the maximum rarity of Echoes you can find and your base drop rate. You increase this level by collecting unique Echoes and finding higher-rarity versions of Echoes you already own.

| Data Bank Level | Max Rarity Unlock | Max Echo Cost | Notable Milestone |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | 2-Star (Green) | 8 | Initial Unlock |
| 2 | 2-Star (Green) | 10 | First Cost Increase |
| 5 | 3-Star (Blue) | 10 | Blue Rarity Drop Chance |
| 8 | 4-Star (Purple) | 10 | Purple Rarity Drop Chance |
| 9 | 4-Star (Purple) | 12 | Maximum Cost Limit Reached |
| 15 | 5-Star (Gold) | 12 | **Gold Rarity (Legendary) Unlock** |
| 17 | 5-Star (Gold) | 12 | 50% Chance for Gold Drops |
| 20 | 5-Star (Gold) | 12 | 100% Enhanced Drop Rate |

### The Echo Cost System Explained
Every Echo has a "Cost" associated with its Class. You cannot simply equip five massive boss Echoes at once. Your Resonator has a total Cost capacity that starts at 8 and eventually caps at 12 as you level up your Data Bank.
*   **Common Class:** 1 Cost (Small mobs)
*   **Elite Class:** 3 Cost (Mid-sized monsters)
*   **Overlord/Calamity Class:** 4 Cost (World Bosses and Weekly Bosses)

A standard endgame build usually follows a 4-3-3-1-1 distribution, totaling exactly 12 cost.

### Rarity Tiers and Drop Rate Milestones
The rarity of an Echo is color-coded: Green (2-star), Blue (3-star), Purple (4-star), and Gold (5-star). Higher rarities provide significantly higher base stats and allow for more "Tuning" slots.
*   **Data Bank Level 15** is the "Golden Milestone." This is when 5-star Echoes begin to drop. Most players rush to this level by hunting every unique monster on the map.
*   **EXP Values:** You gain 10 XP for a 2-star, 10 XP for a 3-star, 15 XP for a 4-star, and 20 XP for a 5-star. If you capture a 5-star version of a monster you've never caught before, you instantly gain the XP for all lower tiers as well.

### Absorption Mechanics: How to Capture Monsters
After defeating a Tacet Discord in the overworld, there is a base chance (starting at 6% and rising to 20% at Data Bank Level 10) that it will leave behind a golden "reverberation." You must manually walk up to this hologram and press the interact key to absorb it into your Pangu Terminal. If you do not absorb it, the Echo will eventually despawn.

## Related

![Related](/img/how-to-unlock-echo-system-wuthering-waves-20260513/inline-4.webp)


Once the system is unlocked, the real gameplay begins. Building a powerful team requires more than just catching monsters; it involves matching sets and optimizing sub-stats.

### Top 5 Early Game Echoes to Target
1.  **Crownless:** An Overlord class Echo (4-cost) that provides Havoc damage. Excellent for the Rover.
2.  **Inferno Rider:** A 4-cost Echo that allows Chixia or other Fusion characters to transform and ride a motorcycle, dealing massive AOE damage.
3.  **Bell-Borne Geochelone:** A 4-cost defensive Echo essential for Baizhi or Verina, providing a shield and damage reduction for the whole team.
4.  **Violet-Feathered Heron:** A 3-cost Elite Echo that is great for parrying and dealing Aero damage.
5.  **Hoartoise:** A 1-cost Common Echo that provides a small heal, useful for early-game survivability when you lack dedicated healers.

### How to Level Up and Tune Your Echoes
Echoes are leveled using "Sealed Tubes" (EXP materials). For every 5 levels an Echo gains, a "Tuning" slot is unlocked. You must use "Tuners" (specific items matching the Echo's rarity) to reveal a random sub-stat, such as Crit Rate, Energy Regen, or Basic Attack DMG Bonus. 
*   **2-Star (Green):** Max Level 10, 0 Tuning slots.
*   **5-Star (Gold):** Max Level 25, 5 Tuning slots.

### Sonata Effects: Building Your First Set
Echoes belong to specific sets called Sonata Effects. Equipping 2 or 5 unique Echoes from the same set grants powerful bonuses.
*   **Lingering Tunes:** Increases ATK and Outro Skill DMG.
*   **Sierra Gale:** Increases Aero DMG.
*   **Rejuvenating Glow:** Increases Healing Bonus and buffs team ATK when the user heals.
*   **Crown of Valor:** A high-end set used by characters like Augusta and Iuno (introduced in later 2025/2026 updates) to boost Echo Skill DMG and Crit Rate.

### Farming Routes and Tacet Fields
To find specific Echoes, use the **Guidebook**. Under the "Echo Gallery" or "Detect" tab, you can select a monster, and the game will place a tracking marker on your map for the nearest spawn. 
*   **Tacet Fields:** These are specialized combat challenges that cost 60 Waveplates to claim. They are the most efficient way to farm both high-rarity Echoes and the EXP materials/Tuners needed to upgrade them.
*   **Co-op Farming:** You can join a friend's world to farm Echoes. Loot is individual, meaning if an Echo drops for you, it does not take away the drop from your friend. This allows groups to clear the entire map daily for maximum Data Bank XP.
