## Detailed Explanation

To understand which game offers better value, we must break down the specific tiers and mechanics of their respective weekly systems.

### HSR 4.3: Cyclical Extrapolation Reward Tiers
In Honkai: Star Rail 4.3, the Cyclical Extrapolation is a weekly-resetting challenge within the Divergent Universe. Each week, a new set of Equations, Weighted Curios, and Blessings is curated to provide a unique gameplay experience.

**Weekly Reward Breakdown:**
*   **Stellar Jades:** 225 Jades are awarded for the first clearance of the week at any difficulty.
*   **Synchronicity Points:** These points advance your Synchronicity Level, which offers a one-time track of rewards including Star Rail Passes and Tracks of Destiny.
*   **Planar Ornaments:** By using a Save File from a successful run, players can spend Trailblaze Power to directly claim Planar Ornaments from boss encounters without re-running the entire mode.
*   **Lost Echoes:** Used for upgrading the Inspiration Circuit, which provides permanent buffs within the Divergent Universe.

The difficulty is governed by the Threshold Protocol. Higher protocols do not necessarily increase the Stellar Jade count but do provide more relic experience and synthesis materials, making them a target for endgame min-maxers.

### NTE: Weekly Anomaly and Urban Management Rewards
Neverness to Everness takes a different approach by splitting rewards between combat-focused Anomaly Commissions and passive urban gameplay.

**Anomaly Hunter System:**
Players track urban legends and supernatural phenomena across Hethereau. Completing these Anomaly Hunts and Pilgrimages rewards players with Anulith. 160 Anulith is equivalent to one Fabricated Die, the item required for a single pull on limited banners.

**Urban Management Income (City Tycoon):**
One of NTE's standout features is the City Tycoon system. Players can invest in and manage various businesses:
*   **Cafe by Origen:** Players assign Espers like Mint or Sakiri to manage the shop. Depending on the Esper's Life Skills, the cafe generates passive Fons and occasional Anulith bundles.
*   **Tofu Delivery:** A manual minigame where players deliver goods using the [ST79 vehicle](https://www.reddit.com/r/NevernessToEverness/comments/1d1n2m9/time_to_deliver_some_tofu/). High performance in these deliveries results in Fons and character upgrade materials.
*   **Taxi & Racing:** Other "Hethereau Hobbies" like taxi driving and street racing provide additional avenues to earn currency for purchasing cars and properties.

### The Fair vs. Warp: Gacha Economy Comparison
The most significant difference between the two games lies in their gacha architecture. HSR uses the traditional "Warp" system, while NTE uses "The Fair" (also known as Scarborough Fair).

**NTE's "The Fair" Advantages:**
*   **No 50/50:** The single most pro-consumer move in NTE is the removal of the 50/50 coin flip. If you pull an S-rank on a limited banner, it is guaranteed to be the featured character.
*   **Board Visualizer:** Instead of simple button presses, players move across a Board visualizer. Spending dice moves you forward 1–6 tiles, and you receive rewards based on the tile you land on.
*   **Pity Mechanics:** Hard pity is set at 90 pulls. However, the Board Modification triggers at 70 pulls, dramatically increasing the S-rank frequency.

### Efficiency Guide: Maximizing Weekly Gains in Hethereau
To maximize rewards in NTE, players should follow a specific weekly checklist:
1.  **Clear Weekly Anomaly Dungeons:** These provide the largest bulk of Anulith bundles for minimal time.
2.  **Optimize Cafe Staff:** Ensure you are using Espers with the best Life Skills for your Cafe by Origen. This maximizes passive Fons income.
3.  **Perform Daily City Deliveries:** These commissions are essential for stacking up resources fast.
4.  **Collect Oracle Stones:** While exploring for missions, keep an eye out for Oracle Stones, which appear in the open world as exploration loot.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How many Stellar Jades can I get from HSR 4.3 Cyclical Extrapolation?
You can earn 225 Stellar Jades per week by completing a single clearance of the Cyclical Extrapolation mode. This is independent of the difficulty level, meaning you can secure the primary rewards even on lower Threshold Protocols.

### Does Neverness to Everness have a 50/50 system?
No, Neverness to Everness has completely removed the 50/50 mechanic for its limited character banners. If you obtain an S-rank character on a limited board, it is guaranteed to be the featured rate-up unit.

### What is the best way to earn Anulith in NTE?
The most consistent F2P methods include completing Anomaly Commissions, advancing your City Tycoon level, and finishing the main story quests. Passive income from the Cafe by Origen also provides a steady stream of Fons that can be used to purchase certain pull-related items.

### Who are the best characters to use for Urban Management in NTE?
Espers like Mint, Sakiri, and Nanally are highly effective for shop management. Each character possesses specific Life Skills that can boost the efficiency and revenue of your businesses in Hethereau.
