### How to Get Pyrois for Free (ZZZ 3.0)
To [unlock Pyrois](https://ntecodex.com/faq) in Zenless Zone Zero, you must reach the Roscaelifer floating island, a classified airspace zone added in Version 3.0. Pyrois is fundamentally different from other Agents as he represents the combat manifestation of the Proxy protagonists, Wise and Belle.

The process involves completing the Main Story Season 3 Chapter 1, titled "A Sleepwalker's Confession." Once the "Triumphant Return" combat tutorial is finished, Pyrois is added to your roster. Additionally, players can earn his signature W-Engine, Sol Exuvia, and his first two Mindscapes (M1 and M2) by increasing their **Interboo account level** through exploration and puzzles in the Roscaelifer district.

### Unlocking Every Free Character in Neverness to Everness
Neverness to Everness offers a robust path for free-to-play (F2P) players to build a competitive team. The primary S-Rank units are distributed through story progression and side activities:

1.  **Zero (S-Rank, Cosmos):** This is your starting character. Zero is a versatile Main DPS who utilizes the Cosmos element. He is automatically added during the prologue.
2.  **Chiz (S-Rank, Cosmos):** Unlike Zero, Chiz requires active participation in the city's economy. She is the face of the City Tycoon system.
3.  **A-Rank Support Units:** To round out your team, you can unlock Haniel (pre-registration mail), **Aurelia** (Day 3 login), Mint (Prologue completion), and Adler (unlocked at the Eibon Antique Shop).

### The City Tycoon System: Getting Chiz for Free
Chiz is arguably the most valuable F2P unit because she can be fully Awakened (A6) without spending a single Annulith. The City Tycoon is a management mini-game where you earn **Fons** (city currency) by running businesses like the **Cafe by Origen**, completing delivery missions, or winning car races.

You unlock the first copy of Chiz at Tycoon Level 18. As you continue to level up your empire, you receive additional copies and her specialized Arcs at the following milestones:
*   **Level 18:** 1st Copy of Chiz
*   **Level 24, 30, 33, 37, 41, 45:** Additional copies for full Awakening.
*   **Level 21, 27, 35, 39, 43:** Unlocks for her signature Arc.

### NTE Gacha Mechanics: The Scarborough Fair Board
Neverness to Everness uses a unique [gacha system](https://ntecodex.com/faq) called [Scarborough Fair](https://ntecodex.com/faq). Instead of a standard "pull" animation, players roll dice to move a piece called a **Chuppa** across a board. Each tile landed on provides a reward, ranging from upgrade materials to S-Class characters.

There are two primary types of dice:
*   Fabricated Dice: Used for the **Strange Encounters** Standard Board.
*   **Solid Dice:** Used for Limited Boards (featured characters).

The game provides a Free S-Rank Selector after you complete 50 rolls on the Standard Board. This is part of the "Anomaly Hunter’s Journey" event designed to help new players establish a core team.

### Best S-Rank Selector Picks for Beginners
When you reach the 50-pull milestone or check your launch mail, you will have to choose between six S-Rank units. Based on the early 1.0 meta, here is how the options stack up:

*   Sakiri: Widely considered the **S+ Tier** pick. She is a powerhouse in quick-swap teams and has excellent longevity.
*   Jiuyuan: Received a significant buff in the launch hotfix, making her a top-tier DPS for Blossom-reaction teams.
*   **Baicang:** Essential for players looking to build a "Firefly Hyper" composition.
*   **Hathor:** A high-damage unit that works well in "Hyper" carries, though some players have reported mechanical bugs with her targeting at launch.
*   **Daffodill & Fadia:** Solid niche picks that excel in specific elemental synergy teams like Nova or Hex.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Pyrois a permanent free character in ZZZ?
Yes, Pyrois is a permanent addition to the Zenless Zone Zero roster for any player who completes the Roscaelifer story arc. Unlike limited event characters, the requirements to unlock him are tied to the main campaign progression in Version 3.0.

### How long does it take to reach City Tycoon Level 18 in NTE?
Most players can reach Tycoon Level 18 within the first week of casual play. By focusing on high-yield activities like the **Pink Paws Heist** and managing your **Cafe by Origen** efficiently, you can unlock Chiz relatively quickly without spending real money.

### Can I get more than one S-Rank selector in Neverness to Everness?
At launch, players effectively received two opportunities for a free S-Rank. The first is the guaranteed selector after 50 pulls on the Standard Board, and the second was a May 3rd commemorative gift sent to all global players' mailboxes.
