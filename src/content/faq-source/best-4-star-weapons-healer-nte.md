In the high-stakes urban supernatural combat of Neverness to Everness (NTE), keeping your squad healthy is the difference between clearing a high-level Anomaly Commission and a total team wipe. While S-Rank Arcs like Eternal Waltz offer staggering stat ceilings, the current 1.0 meta provides several accessible 4-star (A-Rank) options that can carry a healer through the most difficult endgame content.

# Best 4-Star Weapons for Healers in Neverness to Everness (NTE)

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/best-4-star-weapons-healer-nte/inline-1.webp)


Choosing the right Arc for your sustain characters requires a deep understanding of how healing scales in Neverness to Everness. Players often ask which weapons provide the most efficient HP scaling and whether certain utility-focused Arcs are better than pure healing output. Specifically, the community focuses on three core questions:

*   What are the most effective 4-star Arcs (weapons) for healer and sustain characters in NTE?
*   Which weapons provide the best balance of HP scaling and healing bonuses?
*   Are there specific 4-star options that benefit hybrid healers like Esper Zero or Fadia?

### The Importance of Arcs for NTE Healers
In NTE, Arcs are more than simple stat sticks; they are the primary source of passive effects that trigger during the game’s "Redirect Skill" and "Ultimate" windows. For healers, an Arc's substat—usually HP% or Charge Efficiency—directly dictates the potency of their restoration. Because healing often scales off a character's Maximum HP, choosing an Arc with a high HP% substat is usually the first priority for characters like Edgar.

### Identifying Healer Archetypes in Neverness to Everness
Not all sustain units in NTE heal in the same way. We currently categorize healers into three distinct archetypes:
1.  **Pure Healers (e.g., Fadia, Edgar):** These characters focus almost entirely on restoring the party's health through channeled skills or massive Ultimate bursts. They prioritize HP% and Healing Bonus.
2.  **Hybrid Sustain (e.g., Jiuyuan at A1):** These characters provide grouping or sub-DPS utility while offering supplemental healing. They often prefer Arcs that boost Redirect Skill damage alongside sustain.
3.  **Energy/Utility Healers (e.g., Esper Zero):** These units focus on keeping the team’s Ultimate energy full while providing just enough healing to survive. They prioritize Charge Efficiency.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/best-4-star-weapons-healer-nte/inline-2.webp)


The current best-in-slot 4-star Arc for pure healing output is **Call of the Twisted City**, thanks to its massive HP% substat and its "Cardboard Castle" passive. For teams struggling with high-damage bosses, **Tears Beneath the Mask** provides unparalleled defensive utility by reducing enemy damage output. If your team relies on frequent Ultimate usage, **Be Happy** serves as the premier support choice for energy management.

### The Top Recommendation: Call of the Twisted City
Call of the Twisted City is widely considered the gold standard for 4-star healer weapons. Its HP% substat is one of the highest in the A-Rank category, providing a massive boost to healers whose kits scale with health. The 12% Healing Bonus (scaling to 20% at R5) is easy to maintain, as it triggers whenever a character uses their Redirect Skill—the core mechanic of most healer rotations.

### Best for Utility: Tears Beneath the Mask
While listed as an A-Rank utility Arc in many 1.0 guides, Tears Beneath the Mask is essential for survivability. It applies the "Warning Gaze" debuff to enemies hit by the wearer's Ultimate. This reduces the enemy's damage output by 18% for 20 seconds, effectively providing a "soft heal" by preventing damage before it happens.

### Best for Energy Management: Be Happy
Be Happy is the go-to choice for players who find their Ultimates are never ready when they need them. It boosts Charge Efficiency and provides a team-wide ATK increase following an Ultimate. This makes it an excellent choice for Esper Zero or any sustain unit in a "Hyper-carry" team composition where the main DPS needs every buff possible.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/best-4-star-weapons-healer-nte/inline-3.webp)


The effectiveness of a healer in NTE is determined by how well their Arc interacts with their specific kit and the team's rotation. Below is a detailed breakdown of the top 4-star options and their character synergies.

### Call of the Twisted City: Stats and Mechanics
Call of the Twisted City is a Liquid-type Arc that excels on characters who spend time on the field channeling their skills. The "Cardboard Castle" effect is particularly potent because the 10-second duration is refreshed every time a Redirect Skill is used.

**Refinement Scaling (Cardboard Castle):**
*   **Rank 1:** +12% Healing Bonus
*   **Rank 2:** +14% Healing Bonus
*   **Rank 3:** +16% Healing Bonus
*   **Rank 4:** +18% Healing Bonus
*   **Rank 5:** +20% Healing Bonus

For a character like Edgar, whose "Wild Current" skill is a long-channel heal, this Arc ensures that the healing bonus stays active for the entire duration of his sustain window. With a base HP substat of 37.5%, it can push a well-built Edgar toward the 50,000 HP threshold, making him nearly impossible to kill while he supports the team.

### Tears Beneath the Mask: Maximizing Team Survival
Tears Beneath the Mask is often classified as a Gas-type Arc. Its primary value lies in the "Warning Gaze" mechanic. Unlike pure healing, which is reactive, Warning Gaze is proactive. By reducing enemy damage by up to 30% at Rank 5, it allows your team to stay on the offensive without fearing a "one-shot" mechanic from bosses like the Nestbound Bird. This Arc is highly recommended for Fadia if you are running her in a Synthesis/Gas hybrid setup.

### Be Happy: Balancing Healing with Offensive Buffs
Though it is often found in the B-Rank pool, Be Happy is a "hidden gem" for support units. In NTE, the "Charge" reaction is one of the strongest ways to cycle through Ultimates. By equipping Be Happy, your healer becomes a battery for the rest of the team. The team-wide ATK buff it provides after an Ultimate cast ensures that your healer is contributing to the team's total DPS, not just their health bars.

### Real Music: The Hybrid Healer's Choice
Real Music is a Liquid-type Arc that focuses on Redirect Skill DMG. While this might seem counterintuitive for a healer, it is the best 4-star option for Jiuyuan (especially at A1). Jiuyuan’s kit involves dashing across the battlefield and dealing multiple instances of Anima DMG. Since her healing is often a secondary part of her kit, Real Music allows her to maintain high damage pressure while still benefiting from the Liquid-type stat scaling that her kit requires.

### How to Optimize Healer Arcs with Anomaly Furniture
In the city-building and apartment customization aspect of Neverness to Everness, players can place "Anomaly Furniture" to further boost their Arcs. For healers, you should prioritize furniture sets like "Thea's Night Tavern" modules. These modules provide additional HP% and Healing Bonus percentages that stack additively with your Arc's stats. When your healer Arc is reaching Rank 5, the combination of weapon passives and furniture bonuses can increase total healing output by over 50% compared to a base setup.

### F2P Acquisition: How to Get These Arcs
You do not need to spend Riftcrystals to obtain these powerful 4-star Arcs.
*   **Scarborough Fair (Arc Exchange):** Call of the Twisted City can be purchased using U-00NE currency, which is earned by recycling lower-tier Arcs.
*   **Anomaly Commissions:** Tears Beneath the Mask is a guaranteed reward for completing the "Call of the Lost" commission and defeating the Nestbound Bird anomaly.
*   **City Tycoon Rewards:** Progressing through the urban exploration and management mini-games often rewards A-Rank Arc selectors, allowing you to pick the specific weapon your healer needs.

## Related

![Related](/img/best-4-star-weapons-healer-nte/inline-4.webp)


As you progress into the late-game of NTE, you may eventually look to replace your 4-star Arcs with S-Rank alternatives. However, the 4-star options listed above remain viable even in the hardest tower climbs.

### Best S-Rank Arcs for Sustain Characters
If you manage to pull an S-Rank Arc, look for **Eternal Waltz** or **Your Happiness is Priceless**. Eternal Waltz is the direct upgrade to Call of the Twisted City, offering a 41.25% HP substat and a passive that increases Mental DMG and Max HP by up to 32%. Your Happiness is Priceless is the ultimate support Arc, providing massive team-wide buffs that far exceed the 4-star "Be Happy" Arc.

### NTE Healer Tier List Overview
In the current 1.0 meta, **Fadia** is widely regarded as the best pure healer due to her protective barriers and prayer abilities. **Edgar** follows closely as a highly dependable Cosmos healer who is easy to build. **Jiuyuan** sits in the A-tier as a flexible support who becomes a healer at Awakening 1 (A1), while **Esper Zero** is a solid early-game choice that eventually gets outscaled by specialized sustain units.

### Essential Team Synergies for Healers
Healers in NTE perform best when paired with high-damage units that can take advantage of the windows of safety they provide.
*   **[Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) & Fadia:** Fadia’s shields allow Nanally to stay on the field longer to complete her high-damage rotations.
*   **[Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) & Edgar:** Edgar’s channeled healing keeps Sakiri’s summons alive, ensuring constant battlefield control and taunts.
*   **Jiuyuan & [Hotori](/characters/hotori-best-team-nte):** Jiuyuan’s grouping abilities combined with Hotori’s energy refill mechanics create a "perma-Ultimate" loop that is currently dominating the speed-clear meta.
