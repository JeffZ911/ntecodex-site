### Defining the Main DPS Role in Version 6.x
In the 2026 meta, the "Main DPS" is no longer defined simply by who stays on the field the longest. Instead, the role is defined by "Field Time Efficiency." With the introduction of characters like Mavuika and Kinich, the Main DPS is often a character who can front-load massive amounts of damage within a specific window—usually defined by a state like Nightsoul’s Blessing. Artifacts now focus on maximizing these specific windows of power rather than providing flat, unconditional stat boosts.

### The Evolution of Artifact Powercreep
Early Genshin Impact artifacts like Gladiator’s Finale provided generic 18% Attack bonuses. Modern sets have evolved to provide massive "hidden" stats. For example, the Obsidian Codex provides a staggering 40% CRIT Rate, which is nearly double the value of a standard CRIT Rate circlet. This shift means that building a Main DPS in 2026 is less about finding "good pieces" and more about completing 4-piece sets that fundamentally change the character’s stat ceiling.

### The Natlan Meta: Obsidian Codex and Nightsoul
The Obsidian Codex has redefined the CRIT ratio standards for Main DPS characters. The 2-piece set provides a 15% DMG increase while the character is in the Nightsoul’s Blessing state and on the field. The 4-piece set is where the true power lies: whenever the character consumes 1 Nightsoul point while on the field, their CRIT Rate is increased by 40% for 7 seconds.

Because Natlan characters like Mavuika consume Nightsoul points constantly during their combat state, this 40% buff has near 100% uptime. This allows players to run a CRIT DMG circlet and focus almost exclusively on CRIT DMG substats, often pushing the 2026 "Hypercarry" ceiling to over 300% CRIT DMG while maintaining a consistent 100% CRIT Rate.

### The Fontaine Legacy: HP Drain and Marechaussee
Marechaussee Hunter remains the most efficient domain to farm for any player who owns Furina. Its 4-piece bonus provides a 36% CRIT Rate buff when the character's HP increases or decreases. Since Furina’s Elemental Skill constantly drains the party's HP and her Burst rewards healing, any Main DPS paired with her can maintain the full 36% CRIT Rate bonus. This makes Marechaussee Hunter a "universal" set for older characters like Hu Tao or even Xiao, provided they are played in a Furina-centric "FFXX" (Furina, Faruzan, Xianyun, Xiao) or similar archetype.

### Fragment of Harmonic Whimsy: The Bond of Life Specialist
For characters that utilize the Bond of Life mechanic, such as Arlecchino and Clorinde, Fragment of Harmonic Whimsy is the undisputed BiS. The 4-piece set increases the character's DMG by 18% for 6 seconds when the value of a Bond of Life increases or decreases, stacking up to 3 times for a total of 54% DMG bonus. This set is highly niche but provides one of the highest raw damage multipliers in the game for the characters that can actually use it.

### Substat Priority Tier List
In the 2026 meta, the priority for Main DPS substats has shifted toward "Stat Balancing" rather than just "CRIT Fishing."

1.  **CRIT Rate (to 100%):** With the prevalence of 36-40% CRIT Rate bonuses from sets, hitting the 100% cap is now the baseline for high-end builds.
2.  **CRIT DMG:** Once the 100% cap is reached, all remaining offensive value should go here.
3.  **Primary Scaling Stat (ATK%, HP%, or DEF%):** Ensure you aren't neglecting your base scaling; a 300% CRIT DMG build with no ATK will underperform.
4.  **Elemental Mastery (EM):** Crucial for reaction-based DPS (Vaporize, Melt, Quicken).
5.  **Energy Recharge (ER):** The "hidden" DPS stat. If you cannot Burst on cooldown, your DPS drops to zero during your downtime.

### Energy Recharge (ER) Requirements
As of Version 6.x, many Main DPS characters have "Energy Hungry" Bursts. For example, Mavuika typically requires roughly 130-140% ER if she is the solo Pyro unit, while Raiden Shogun still targets 250%+ to maximize the Emblem of Severed Fate bonus. Always prioritize meeting your ER threshold before chasing higher CRIT numbers.
