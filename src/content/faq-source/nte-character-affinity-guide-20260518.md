### How does the character affinity (Bond) system work in Neverness to Everness?
The Bond system tracks your relationship progress with each individual character. As you accumulate affection points, you progress through 10 distinct tiers. Each tier unlocks new voice lines, profile information, and gameplay benefits. The system is gated by daily limits to prevent players from maxing out relationships instantly, making consistent daily engagement the only path to high-level affinity.

### What are the best ways to increase character affection points quickly?
The most effective method is utilizing the daily gifting system, specifically by matching characters with their favorite high-rarity items. Beyond gifting, players can earn points through social interactions such as car rides, hangouts, and specific mini-games. Efficiency is further improved by investing in the protagonist's (Esper Zero) life skills, which provide a permanent multiplier to all affection gains.

### What rewards and features are unlocked by reaching higher Bond levels with characters like Mint and Sakiri?
Reaching higher Bond levels provides a mix of premium currency and character-specific upgrades. Players can earn up to 80 Annulith per character as they reach specific milestones. High affinity also grants permanent stat bonuses, such as the ATK% increase for Mint. Furthermore, Bond Level 4 is the threshold for "Home Time," a feature that allows characters to move into your owned properties, unlocking intimate interactions like hand-holding and specific domestic animations.

### Daily Gifting Limits and Optimization Strategy

Because you are limited to 10 gifts per day, you must choose your targets wisely. If you are trying to maximize Mint for her ATK% bonus, you should use 3 of your 10 daily slots on her every day. The rarity of the gift determines the base affection points:

*   **Blue (Rare) Gifts:** 100 base points.
*   **Purple (Epic) Gifts:** 300 base points.
*   **Gold (Legendary) Gifts:** 800 base points.

Giving a character their "Favorite" gift provides a 50% bonus to these base values. Always ensure you are giving the correct item type to avoid wasting your limited daily gift slots.

### The Esper Zero 'Five-Star Paragon' Buff

Esper Zero, the player character, possesses a life skill tree that directly impacts social progression. The "Five-Star Paragon" skill should be a priority for any player focusing on the Bond system. This skill has five tiers of effectiveness:

*   **Tier 1:** +5% Affection Gain from all sources.
*   **Tier 2:** +10% Affection Gain from all sources.
*   **Tier 3:** +15% Affection Gain from all sources.
*   **Tier 4:** +20% Affection Gain from all sources.
*   **Tier 5:** +25% Affection Gain from all sources.

At Tier 5, a Gold gift that normally gives 1,200 points (800 base + 400 favorite bonus) will instead grant 1,500 points. Over the course of the 56,000 points required for Level 10, this skill saves the player dozens of gifts and days of effort.

### Unlocking Home Time and Interactive Actions

One of the most anticipated features of the Bond system is "Home Time." Once a character reaches Bond Level 4, an option appears in their character menu to "Invite to Property." You must own at least one apartment or house to use this feature.

Once a character is living in your home, you can engage in unique interactions that are unavailable in the open world:
1.  **Domestic Actions:** Characters will interact with furniture you have placed, such as sitting on sofas or browsing bookshelves.
2.  **Interactive Mini-games:** You can play Rock Paper Scissors or engage in "Ear Care" sessions, which provide small daily affection boosts (separate from the gifting limit).
3.  **Physical Affection:** At Level 2, you can initiate car rides where the character sits in the passenger seat. At Level 4, you unlock hand-holding during home visits. Level 8 unlocks the "Hug" interaction.
