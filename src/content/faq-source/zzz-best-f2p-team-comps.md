## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/zzz-best-f2p-team-comps/inline-3.webp)


Success in Zenless Zone Zero's combat system relies on a specific rhythm: building Daze to stun the enemy, applying buffs or debuffs, and then unleashing a high-damage burst window. This "Holy Trinity" of Stunner, Support, and Attack is the foundation of every successful F2P team.

### Core Mechanics: Faction and Attribute Synergies

Every agent in Zenless Zone Zero possesses an "Additional Ability" that remains inactive unless specific conditions are met. To activate these powerful passives, you must pair an agent with another character from the same Faction (e.g., Cunning Hares) or the same Attribute (e.g., Electric). 

For F2P players, these synergies are non-negotiable. An unoptimized team of three S-Rank characters with no active passives will often be outperformed by a well-synergized A-Rank F2P team. For example, Anby’s Additional Ability significantly increases her Energy Generation when she hits an enemy with her third basic attack, but only if she is paired with another Electric or Cunning Hares character. Without this, her ability to use EX Special Attacks—and thus her ability to stun enemies quickly—is severely diminished.

### The Starter Powerhouse: Anby, Billy, and Nicole

The Cunning Hares trio is the most cohesive F2P unit in the game. Because they all belong to the same faction, all three Additional Abilities are active by default.

*   **Anby (Stun):** Her primary job is to stay on the field and build the Daze meter. Her "Bolt" combo (three basic attacks followed by a Special Attack) is the most efficient way to stun enemies.
*   **Nicole (Support):** Once the enemy is stunned, Nicole enters the field. Her EX Special Attack creates a small black hole that groups enemies and applies a defense shred of 20% to 40% [exact value depends on skill level]. This makes enemies significantly more vulnerable to the coming burst.
*   **Billy (Attack):** Billy is a Physical powerhouse who excels during the stun window. His Ultimate has one of the highest DMG multipliers among A-Rank agents, especially when the enemy is grouped by Nicole.

**Rotation Guide for the Cunning Hares:**
1.  **Lead with Anby:** Use Basic Attack x3 -> EX Special Attack to build Daze.
2.  **Trigger Chain Attack:** Once the enemy is stunned, trigger the Chain Attack.
3.  **End with Nicole:** Ensure Nicole is the last or second-to-last in the chain to leave her "Energy Field" on the ground.
4.  **Burst with Billy:** Switch to Billy and use his Ultimate or EX Special Attack while the enemy is stunned and Nicole's DEF shred is active.

### Belobog Electric: Anton, Ben, and Anby

If you pull Anton Ivanov from your initial discounted "Star-Repped" banner, you can form the Belobog Electric team. This squad is exceptionally durable thanks to Ben Bigger, the game's only dedicated Defense agent available for free.

Ben provides a shield to the entire team and increases the Critical Rate of the active character while his shield is active. When paired with Anton—who enters a "Burst Mode" that deals massive Electric DMG—this team becomes a boss-killing machine. Anby remains the Stunner here because she shares the Electric attribute with Anton, keeping all passives active.

### Ice Support Excellence: Building Around Soukaku

Soukaku is widely regarded as one of the strongest units in the game, regardless of rarity. Her "Fly the Flag" mechanic allows her to transfer a portion of her own ATK (up to a cap) to the next character that takes the field via a Chain Attack or Quick Assist.

For F2P players, Soukaku can be paired with Corin Wickes (the free Physical Attack agent) and Anby. While this team lacks a unified Faction, Anby and Soukaku provide enough utility to carry Corin. Corin deals massive "Saw" DMG to stunned enemies, and when boosted by Soukaku’s ATK transfer, her DPS can rival S-Rank attackers like Lycaon or Rina.

### Physical Shred: Corin and Piper F2P Variants

If you happen to pull Piper Wheel (an A-Rank Physical Anomaly agent), you can create a "Physical Shred" team with Corin and Nicole. Piper is excellent at building the "Assault" anomaly, which deals a massive chunk of Physical DMG and briefly stuns the enemy. 

In this setup, Nicole provides the grouping and DEF shred, Piper builds the Anomaly meter, and Corin enters the field to execute her EX Special Attack on the "Assaulted" target. This team relies less on traditional stuns and more on rapid Anomaly procs to bypass high-defense bosses.

### Endgame Strategy: Clearing Shiyu Defense as F2P

Shiyu Defense is the ultimate test for any F2P account because it requires two separate teams starting from Floor 7. This is where roster depth becomes an issue for non-spenders.

To succeed, you must split your free characters effectively. A common strategy is:
*   **Team 1:** Anby, Billy, Nicole (The Cunning Hares). This team handles the side with Ether or Physical weaknesses.
*   **Team 2:** Ben, Soukaku, Corin (or Anton). This team utilizes Ben's survivability and Soukaku's massive buffs to carry the second half.

Focus your limited resources on leveling your two main Attackers (Billy and Corin/Anton) to the maximum level first. Support and Stun units can function effectively even if they are 10 levels behind, as long as their Core Passives and W-Engines are upgraded.
