# How to Unlock Daily Quests in Neverness to Everness (NTE)

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/nte-how-to-unlock-daily-quests-20260515/inline-1.webp)


Accessing daily missions is the first major hurdle for any new Appraiser arriving in the metropolitan sprawl of Hethereau. While the open world is available almost immediately, the systems that reward consistent play are gated behind specific narrative progress. Players often find themselves wondering what the exact story milestone is to trigger these rewards and where they are hidden within the game's dense, smartphone-inspired user interface.

### The Importance of Dailies in NTE

In Neverness to Everness, daily quests serve as the primary engine for account growth. They are the most reliable source of Hunter EXP, which determines your Hunter Level (account level). Since many features, such as advanced Anomaly Zones and high-level shop inventories, are locked behind your Hunter Level, skipping even a single day of tasks can significantly delay your access to end-game content. Furthermore, dailies provide Annuliths—the premium currency required to pull for new characters and Arcs—making them indispensable for free-to-play players.

### Common Confusion for New Appraisers

Many players mistake the "Circle Bounty" (the game's battle pass) for the primary daily quest system. While the Circle Bounty does have daily objectives, the core daily rewards are actually found within the "Participation Tasks" system. Another common point of confusion is the unlock timing; because the game encourages exploration early on, it is easy to wander away from the main questline and delay unlocking these essential features for hours.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/nte-how-to-unlock-daily-quests-20260515/inline-2.webp)


To unlock daily quests in Neverness to Everness, you must complete the main story questline titled **"Prologue - Part 2"** (specifically the quest "Good Business, Better Treasure"). Once this narrative milestone is reached, the daily reward system, officially known as **Participation Tasks**, becomes available.

You can access these tasks through your in-game phone by selecting the **Exploration Guide** icon. This icon is easily identifiable as a stylized "70" located on the main menu. For most players, reaching this point takes approximately one to two hours of focused gameplay. Completing a full set of dailies provides a total of 1,000 Hunter EXP, along with a steady stream of Fons and Annuliths.

### NTE Daily Unlock Summary

| Feature | Requirement | Location | Reset Time |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Participation Tasks** | Complete Prologue - Part 2 | Exploration Guide (Icon '70') | 5:00 AM (UTC+8) |
| **Circle Bounty Dailies** | Reach Hunter Level 5 | Circle Bounty Menu | 5:00 AM (UTC+8) |
| **Login Rewards** | Reach Eibon Antique Shop | Events Tab (Phone) | 5:00 AM (UTC+8) |
| **Character Pixels** | Natural Unlock | Anomaly Zone Menu | 1 Pixel every 6 mins |

### The Prologue - Part 2 Milestone

The specific quest that triggers the unlock is the conclusion of the "Good Business, Better Treasure" arc. During this mission, you interact with the Eibon Antique Shop and the Wertheimer Tower. Once you finish the final dialogue with Kodak and return to your dormitory to rest, the system notification for Participation Tasks will appear. If you are currently exploring the city but haven't seen the daily menu yet, check your quest log to ensure you haven't left the Prologue unfinished.

### Where to Find Your Daily Tasks

The UI in Neverness to Everness is designed to mimic a modern smartphone. To find your dailies, press the ESC key (PC) or tap the phone icon (Mobile) to bring up the main interface. Look for the Exploration Guide—it is the icon that resembles a stylized "70" or a dial. Inside this menu, navigate to the second tab on the left-hand side. This screen lists your active Participation Tasks and your progress toward the daily reward chests.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/nte-how-to-unlock-daily-quests-20260515/inline-3.webp)


The daily system in Neverness to Everness is split into two distinct but overlapping categories: **Participation Tasks** and **Circle Bounty** missions. Understanding how these interact is key to efficient progression.

### Understanding Participation Tasks

Participation Tasks are the primary "Daily Quests" that most players refer to. Instead of having to complete every single task on the list, NTE uses a "Participation Point" system. Each task you complete awards a set number of points (usually 10, 20, or 30). Your goal is to reach **100 Participation Points** to unlock all five daily reward chests.

Typical Participation Tasks include:
*   **Daily Login:** Simply opening the game (20 points).
*   **Spend 100 Character Pixels:** Using your stamina in Anomaly Zones (30 points).
*   **Earn 10,000 Fons:** Collected through combat, selling items, or shop income (20 points).
*   **Use an Ultimate Ability:** Activating a character's burst during combat (10 points).
*   **Interact with a City Hobby:** Playing a mini-game like racing or rhythm games (20 points).

By reaching the 100-point threshold, you claim a total of 60 Annuliths and a significant amount of Fons and upgrade materials.

### Circle Bounty vs. Exploration Guide

While Participation Tasks give you immediate rewards, the **Circle Bounty** is the NTE version of a Battle Pass. It has its own set of daily missions that refresh at the same time. These missions do not give Annuliths directly but instead provide **Circle Bounty EXP**.

*   **Participation Tasks (Exploration Guide):** Focus on Hunter Level EXP, Annuliths, and immediate materials.
*   **Circle Bounty Daily Quests:** Focus on Battle Pass levels to unlock seasonal rewards like "Fabricated Dice" (gacha pulls) and "Gold Carrota."

Completing your daily Participation Tasks will usually fulfill the requirements for your Circle Bounty dailies automatically, so you don't need to go out of your way to do both separately.

### Server Reset Schedule

Neverness to Everness operates on a global reset timer. All daily tasks, shop limits, and login bonuses refresh at **5:00 AM (UTC+8)**. Depending on your region, this may fall in the late evening or early morning.

### Maximizing Hunter EXP Gains

Hunter Level 14 is a critical milestone in the early game because it acts as a soft gate for Chapter 3 content. If you find yourself unable to progress the story because your Hunter Level is too low, your daily Participation Tasks are the fastest way to bridge the gap. Each full completion provides 1,000 Hunter EXP. When combined with spending your **Character Pixels** (stamina), you can gain roughly 1.5 to 2 levels per day in the early-to-mid game.

## Related

Beyond the standard Participation Tasks, a veteran Appraiser’s daily routine in Hethereau involves several other activities that provide passive income and long-term character growth.

### The Ultimate NTE Daily Routine

To optimize your account, you should follow a specific checklist every time you log in. The game does not always flag these activities in the Exploration Guide, but they are vital for staying competitive.

### NTE Daily Checklist Priority

| Priority | Activity | Reason |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **High** | Spend Character Pixels | Prevents stamina capping (240/day) |
| **High** | Participation Tasks | Main source of Annuliths and 1k EXP |
| **Medium** | Nacupeda's Pool | Progress toward free S-Rank Arcs |
| **Medium** | The Witch's House | Daily buffs and hidden quest triggers |
| **Low** | Cafe by Origen | Collect passive Fons and restock |
| **Low** | Chiz Fons Farming | Hit the 40,000 daily Fons cap |

### Stamina Management: Character Pixels vs. City Stamina

NTE uses a dual stamina system. **Character Pixels** are your primary combat stamina. They regenerate at a rate of 1 every 6 minutes, totaling 240 pixels every 24 hours. You should spend these in Anomaly Zones to farm materials. **City Stamina** is used for "Hethereau Hobbies" (mini-games) and resets weekly on Mondays. Always prioritize spending your Pixels first, as they cap much faster.

### Passive Income: The Cafe by Origen

Once you unlock the Cafe by Origen, you gain access to a passive Fons generator. You must visit the cafe daily to collect your earnings and restock ingredients. If the cafe runs out of stock, your passive income stops entirely. This is also where you can manage your "Tycoon Level," which increases the efficiency of your city-based businesses.

### Daily NPC Interactions: Witch's House and Nacupeda's Pool

Two specific locations in Southwest Hethereau offer unique daily benefits:

*   **The Witch's House:** Located near Bridge Crossing, the Witch offers a daily fortune reading. Choosing the "Lost Tales" fortune can reveal a hidden quest, while "Bless" provides a combat damage buff that lasts until the next reset.
*   **Nacupeda's Pool:** This fountain provides a daily reward based on your dialogue choice.

### Nacupeda's Pool Dialogue Options and Rewards

| Dialogue Choice | Reward Type | Long-Term Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **"Make a Sincere Wish"** | Mhm! Coin | Save up for an S-Rank Arc |
| **"Coins, love 'em, grab some!"** | Furniture/Fons | Cosmetic items for your dormitory |

### Weekly Tasks to Watch For

While dailies are the focus, certain systems in NTE refresh every Monday at 5:00 AM (UTC+8).
*   **Ebisu's Auction:** A weekly event where you can bid on rare Arcs and upgrade materials using Fons.
*   **Anomaly Pilgrimage:** These are weekly bosses that provide high-tier character ascension materials. You are limited to three reward claims per week.
*   **Realm of Greed:** Accessed via the Mammon decoration in your home, this challenge rewards massive amounts of Fons based on the difficulty cleared.

By integrating these tasks into your daily and weekly cycle, you ensure that your Appraiser level and character power continue to scale alongside the challenges of Hethereau.
