Ensuring your frontline stays standing during high-intensity Anomaly hunts is the difference between a successful raid and a total team wipe. In Neverness to Everness (NTE), the "Tank" role is defined by how well you manage your Console's Cartridges and Modules to maximize damage mitigation and team-wide protection.

# Best Disk Sets for Tanks in Neverness to Everness (NTE Guide)

## What Are the Best Disk Sets for Tank Characters in Neverness to Everness?

![What Are the Best Disk Sets for Tank Characters in Neverness to Everness?](/img/best-disk-sets-for-tank-nte/inline-1.webp)


In Neverness to Everness, "Disk Sets" are officially referred to as Cartridges. These are the primary gear pieces that dictate your character's set bonuses and stat ceiling. Unlike traditional gear systems where you simply equip an item, NTE uses a "Console" system. You must slot a Cartridge and then arrange specific Tetris-like shapes called Modules on a grid to activate the 2-piece and 4-piece set bonuses.

As of the April 29, 2026 launch, the tank meta is dominated by two primary archetypes: shield-based mitigation and HP-based damage redirection. Survival in endgame content like the Rabbit Hole or high-tier Anomaly raids requires more than just high health; you need specific stats like Shield Intensity and DEF Scaling to counteract the aggressive "Break" mechanics used by bosses.

The launch-day roster features two standout survival specialists:
*   **Adler:** An A-Rank Incantation support who provides massive DEF-scaling shields.
*   **Fadia:** An S-Rank Psyche tank who redirects 50% of team damage to herself and scales entirely off Max HP.

While characters like Edgar (Cosmos) provide some sustain-hybrid utility, Adler and Fadia are the current gold standards for pure team protection.

### Understanding NTE's Gear System: Cartridges vs. Modules

The Console system is the heart of character progression. Every Esper has a unique grid layout where you must fit your gear.
*   **Cartridges:** These are the "anchor" of your build. They provide a single Main Stat (like DEF% or HP%) and four Sub Stats. Most importantly, they define the set bonus.
*   **Modules:** These are shaped stat-fillers. Every Module has fixed Main Stats (Flat ATK and Flat HP) but carries four random Sub Stats. To activate your Cartridge's 4-piece bonus, you must fill the grid with Modules of a specific shape (Type II, III, or IV) as dictated by the Cartridge's requirements.

### The Role of Tanks in the Esper Cycle Meta

Tanks in NTE do not just soak up damage; they facilitate the "Esper Cycle." This combat system rewards you for rotating through your team to trigger elemental reactions like Scorch (Incantation + Chaos) or Nova (Psyche + Chaos). A well-built tank like Adler allows your main DPS to stay on the field longer without dodging, while Fadia turns the damage she takes into a "Destructive Experience" debuff that reflects damage back at the enemy.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/best-disk-sets-for-tank-nte/inline-3.webp)


### Top-Tier Survival Cartridges: Stats and Mechanics

Choosing the right set requires looking at the "scaling tag" on your character's skills. If a skill says "Damage scales with 80% of DEF," Kingdom's Guard is mandatory. If it says "Restores HP based on 10% of Max HP," Tiny Big Adventure is the priority.

**Kingdom's Guard**
This set is designed for characters who generate "Blessing" shields. The 15% DEF boost directly increases the base value of the shield, while the 20% Shield Intensity acts as a final multiplier, making the shield significantly harder for enemies to "Break." In the current 1.0 meta, this is the most efficient way to ignore boss mechanics.

**Tiny Big Adventure**
This is a "ramping" set. Every time the wearer takes damage, they gain a stack of 4% Max HP. Since Fadia is constantly taking redirected damage from her teammates, she hits the 10-stack limit (40% total Max HP) almost instantly. This creates a massive health pool that makes her nearly impossible to kill, even in high-level Anomaly Zones.

### Best Arcs (Weapons) to Complement Tank Disk Sets

Your Arc choice should mirror your Cartridge's stat focus.
*   **Umbrella (A-Rank):** The best F2P option for Adler. It provides a DEF% secondary stat and strengthens shields by 10% as long as the wearer's HP remains above 50%.
*   **The Fool's Spring (A-Rank):** A high-tier choice for Adler that increases ATK by 18% while a shield is active. Since Adler's shields have high uptime, this allows him to contribute meaningful sub-DPS damage.
*   **Eternal Waltz (S-Rank):** Fadia’s signature Arc. It provides the highest base HP in the game and a massive boost to Psyche damage based on total HP.
*   **Bittercake (B-Rank/A-Rank):** A solid early-game filler that grants a 26% DEF boost for 10 seconds after taking damage.

### Module Grid Strategy: Maximizing the Console System

Activating a 4-piece set bonus is only half the battle. To truly optimize a tank, you must use the "Preference Bonus." Every character has a preferred Module type that grants extra stats for every piece of that type slotted into the Console.

*   **Adler’s Preference:** Type III Modules. Each Type III Module slotted grants Adler an additional +12% DEF. By filling his grid with Type III pieces, you can stack up to +72% DEF before even considering substats.
*   **Fadia’s Preference:** Type II Modules. Each Type II Module grants her +6% Max HP. A full grid of Type II pieces can provide a total of +36% Max HP.

When farming, prioritize Modules with "DEF%" or "HP%" substats. In NTE, substat values are fixed based on the size of the Module (e.g., a 2-block Type II piece always provides 1.60% Crit Rate or a specific flat HP value), so focus on the *type* of stat rather than chasing a high roll.

### Character Build: Adler (The Unbreakable Butler)

Adler is an Incantation-element support whose kit revolves around "Karma."
1.  **Skill (Evil's Bane):** Consumes Karma stacks to provide a "Shield of Blessing" to the active character.
2.  **Ultimate (Tranquility):** Summons five Sunyas to deal area damage and instantly generates Karma stacks at higher Awakening levels.
3.  **Optimal Setup:** 4-Piece Kingdom's Guard. Focus on DEF% Main Stats on your Cartridge. Fill the rest of the Console with Type III Modules to maximize the +12% DEF per piece bonus.

### Character Build: Fadia (The Immortal Damage-Sharer)

Fadia is a Psyche-element S-Rank who excels in "Discord" or "Nova" team compositions.
1.  **Passive (All Eyes on Me):** Redirects 50% of damage taken by allies to Fadia.
2.  **Skill (Existence):** Deals Psyche damage and marks the strongest enemy with "Destructive Experience," causing them to share the damage Fadia receives.
3.  **Optimal Setup:** 4-Piece Tiny Big Adventure. Use HP% Main Stats and prioritize Type II Modules to capitalize on her +6% HP preference bonus.

### Where to Farm Tank Disks: Rabbit Holes and Hunter Level 30+

Cartridges are primarily farmed in the **Rabbit Hole Anomaly Zone**, located in the southern section of the New Herland District.
*   **Stamina Cost:** 40 Character Pixels per run.
*   **Drops:** Specific Rabbit Hole stages rotate daily, dropping different Cartridge sets. Check the "Hunter Exchange" or the in-game guide to see which stage currently features Kingdom's Guard or Tiny Big Adventure.
*   **Modules:** These are obtained through the **Rewind** feature in your Console menu. You spend Carrota Coins (earned from the Rabbit Hole) to pull for random Module shapes.
*   **Rarity Gates:** S-Rank (Orange) Modules only become available once you reach **Hunter Level 30** and unlock the "Hard" difficulty in the Rewind system.
