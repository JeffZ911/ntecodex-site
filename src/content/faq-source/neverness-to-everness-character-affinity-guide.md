In Neverness to Everness (NTE), the Character Affinity system—officially known as the "Bond" system—is a core social mechanic that allows players to build intimacy with their Espers. By increasing Affinity Points (AP), players can transition their relationships from mere acquaintances to "Heartbonds," unlocking exclusive lore, dating interactions, and significant stat bonuses.

Efficiently increasing AP requires a mix of daily gifting, urban activities, and combat participation. However, the system is governed by strict daily limits: players can only provide 10 gifts total across their entire roster each day, with a maximum of 3 gifts per individual character. This guide explores how to navigate these restrictions, where to find the best gifts, and what rewards await you at the peak of Level 10 Bond.

### Understanding the Bond Level System

The Bond system features 10 distinct levels, though some characters in the current v1.0 version, such as Aurelia, are temporarily capped at Level 2. For most of the launch roster, the journey to Level 10 is a marathon rather than a sprint. To reach the maximum Affinity rank, a character requires roughly 56,000 AP. 

As you progress, the titles associated with your relationship change, reflecting the growing trust between the protagonist (Esper Zero) and their companions. These levels act as "gates" for Hethereau’s urban content. For example, you cannot invite an Esper to your apartment or go on a movie date until you reach "Familiar" status at Level 4.

### Methods to Increase Affinity: Gifting, Combat, and Hangouts

While gifting is the fastest route, it is not the only way to earn AP. A balanced approach ensures you are making progress even after hitting your daily gift limit.

1.  **Daily Gifting:** This remains the gold standard. A favorite gift typically yields between 400 and 2000 AP. Tapping an item in the Bond menu will reveal its source, usually a shop in Hethereau.
2.  **Combat Participation:** Keeping a character in your active party during missions and open-world encounters provides a small, passive trickle of Bond EXP.
3.  **Urban Interactions:** Once you reach Level 2, you can invite characters to ride "Shotgun" in your vehicle. Driving around the city and visiting landmarks provides incremental AP.
4.  **Bond Text Messages:** Occasionally, characters will send you text messages marked with a heart icon. Responding to these and completing the associated mini-quests or meetings provides a significant AP chunk without consuming gift slots.

### Universal Gifts: The Handwritten Letter Shortcut

If you want to bypass the need to farm specific items for 15+ different characters, you should focus on Universal Gifts. These items are accepted by every character at a high AP value.

*   **A Handwritten Letter (+2000 AP):** This is the most powerful gift in the game. It is primarily obtained through the "Warp Exchange" or as a reward from the "Circle Bounty" (the NTE battle pass). It is highly recommended to stockpile these for limited S-Class characters like Nanally or [Hotori](/characters/hotori-best-team-nte).
*   **Fluffy Cotton (+400 AP):** A more accessible universal gift. You can farm Fluffy Cotton for free by interacting with the "Compressed Boom Boom Cloud" anomaly located inside your player housing (Wiener Apartments or Eden Apartments).
*   **Floe Cinema Tickets (+400 AP):** These are favored by a wide variety of characters, including Mint and Chiz, making them a great multi-purpose item to keep in your inventory.

### Unlocking Dates and Special Interactions

The "Urban Life" aspect of Neverness to Everness truly opens up at Bond Level 4. Once you reach this milestone, you can use your phone to invite characters to specific "City Hangouts." 

*   **Movie Theater:** Taking a character to the Floe Cinema allows you to choose a film genre. Matching the genre to the character's personality (e.g., Action for Nanally) results in a higher AP bonus.
*   **Ferris Wheel:** A classic date spot that triggers unique dialogue sequences and photo opportunities.
*   **Home Invitations:** At Level 4, you can invite characters to stay at your apartment. This unlocks "Home Time," where characters will roam your property in their unique loungewear.
*   **Heartbond Scenes:** At Level 8 and Level 10, characters like Nanally and Mint unlock exclusive cinematic dates that conclude their personal story arcs.

### Best Esper Builds
To make the most of your high-affinity characters, ensure they are equipped with the correct Arcs. Nanally benefits most from high-impact physical Arcs, while Mint excels with Liquid-attribute resonance.

### City Tycoon & Fons Farming Guide
Buying gifts at Hillside Blooms or the Miguel District Bookstore requires a steady supply of Fons. Participating in the City Tycoon system—specifically the "Heist" mini-games and shop management—is the most efficient way to fund your gifting habits. High-level Tycoon ranks also unlock free characters like Chiz.

### NTE Combat Mechanics: Esper Cycles
Bonding improves your synergy, but understanding the Esper Cycle is what wins battles. Swapping between Solid, Liquid, and Gas attributes at the correct timing allows for massive elemental reactions that can clear anomalies in seconds.
