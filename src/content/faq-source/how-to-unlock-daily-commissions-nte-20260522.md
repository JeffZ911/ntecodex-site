Players arriving in Hethereau often find the initial hours of the game focused strictly on the narrative and tutorial systems. Access to the recurring daily tasks that define the long-term progression of an urban open-world RPG does not happen immediately upon starting the game. ### The Importance of Dailies for F2P Players

For players who do not intend to spend real money, Daily Commissions represent the most reliable source of Annulith, the premium currency used for pulling on character and weapon banners. Beyond currency, these tasks provide a steady stream of Appraisal EXP, which is required to increase your player rank and unlock higher-level world rewards, more difficult anomalies, and advanced story chapters. Neglecting these tasks even for a few days can significantly delay your access to end-game content and character upgrades.

### Defining 'Daily Commissions' in Hethereau

In Neverness to Everness, "Daily Commissions" is a community term used to describe a combination of two specific systems found within the Exploration Guide: Daily Quests and Participation Tasks. Unlike some games that force you to travel to a specific NPC to pick up tasks, NTE integrates these directly into your urban lifestyle. They involve a mix of combat, city exploration, and business management activities that reflect your role as an anomaly investigator and a business owner in Hethereau.

### The 'Film Anomaly' Milestone

The "Film Anomaly" quest involves investigating a series of supernatural occurrences within a local Hethereau movie theater. This quest serves as a tutorial for the "Reality Folding" mechanics and introduces the first major combat challenges involving elite anomalies. Upon defeating the boss at the end of this sequence and returning to the safehouse, the game will trigger a UI tutorial explaining the Exploration Guide. This menu serves as your central hub for all daily and weekly activities.

### Server Reset Times for NTE Dailies

All daily activities, including quest refreshes and shop restocking, occur at 04:00 AM server time. The specific time zone depends on the server you selected during account creation:

*   **North America:** 04:00 AM ET
*   **Europe:** 04:00 AM CET
*   **Asia:** 04:00 AM GMT+8

If you are logged in during the reset, you may need to teleport to a different district or restart the client to see the updated list of Daily Quests in your Exploration Guide.

The daily loop in Neverness to Everness is designed to be completed in approximately 15 to 20 minutes, depending on how you choose to spend your Character Pixels. The system is split between fixed objectives and a flexible "Participation" meter.

### Accessing the Exploration Guide Menu

To access your dailies, open the main menu (ESC on PC, or tap the P-Phone icon on mobile) and select the "Exploration Guide" icon, which resembles a small notebook. Within this menu, the "Daily" tab lists four specific tasks that change every day, while the "Participation" tab tracks your overall activity across all game systems.

Each day, the game assigns four specific tasks. While these can vary slightly, the most common rotation includes:

1.  **Daily Login:** Automatically completed when you sign in.
2.  **Earn 10,000 Fons:** Can be completed by selling items, completing world events, or collecting revenue from your businesses.
3.  **Consume Character Pixels:** Requires spending at least [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-22] Pixels on anomaly suppressions or material domains.

### Participation Tasks vs. Daily Quests

The Participation system is a secondary reward track. You earn Participation Points by engaging in almost any activity in Hethereau, including:
*   Completing a City Commission (20 points)
*   Spending 20 Character Pixels (10 points)
*   Winning a street race (15 points)
*   Modifying a vehicle (10 points)

The goal is to reach 100 points daily to claim all five reward chests. The final chest always contains the bulk of the daily Annulith reward.

### Nacupeda’s Pool: Making a Sincere Wish

Located in the central urban district of Hethereau, Nacupeda’s Pool is a daily interaction point that many new players overlook. By spending a "Mhm! Coin"—which can be found through exploration or purchased at convenience stores—players can "Make a Wish."

This provides a random buff that lasts for 24 hours, such as:
*   Increased Fons gain from combat.
*   Reduced Character Pixel cost for specific domains.
*   Movement speed increases within the city.

### The Witch’s House: Daily Fortunes and Buffs

The Witch’s House is a specialized shop and interaction hub. Once per day, you can have your fortune read. This is not just flavor text; the "Fortune of the Day" determines which elemental types receive a damage bonus in the open world. Additionally, the Witch sells a limited stock of rare ingredients used for high-level cooking, which resets at the 04:00 AM server time mark.

## Related

Beyond the daily grind, NTE features several weekly and seasonal systems that tie into your daily activities.

### Weekly Special City Commissions (The Old Mailbox)

Every Monday, the "Old Mailbox" in the residential district refreshes with three Special City Commissions. these are longer, narrative-driven tasks that offer significantly higher rewards than standard dailies, including rare weapon upgrade materials and large quantities of Fons. It is best to complete one of these per day alongside your standard dailies to spread out the workload.

### Circle Bounty: Maximizing Seasonal Progression

The Circle Bounty is the Neverness to Everness version of a Battle Pass. Both your Daily Quests and Participation Tasks contribute "Bounty Points." By completing your dailies consistently, you can reach the maximum Circle Bounty level (Level 50) well before the season ends, securing the featured 4-star weapon and extra Annuliths.

### City Energy and Urban Hobbies

Separate from Character Pixels, "City Energy" is used for non-combat hobbies like car tuning, arcade games, and urban exploration challenges. City Energy refreshes daily, and using it often contributes to your Participation Points. If you are close to the 100-point milestone but out of Character Pixels, participating in a hobby is the best way to bridge the gap.

### Ebisu’s Auction House: Weekly Rare Items

Ebisu’s Auction House features a rotating stock of high-end car parts and rare character Arcs. While the stock refreshes weekly, there is often a "Daily Deal" section that offers a 50% discount on a random material. Checking this daily can save you thousands of Fons in the long run.
