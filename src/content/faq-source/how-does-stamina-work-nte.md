# How Does Stamina Work in Neverness to Everness? Full Guide
> ⚠️ **Notice:** Auto-generated content. May contain approximations or minor inaccuracies in supporting details. Help us improve via the comment section below. _Last reviewed: 2026-05-14._


In Neverness to Everness, stamina is not a single resource but a trio of independent systems that govern your combat growth, economic power, and [world exploration](https://ntecodex.com/guides/nte-vs-zzz-open-world-exploration-gear-comparison). Unlike traditional gacha games that gate all content behind one "Energy" bar, NTE splits these into Character Pixels, City Stamina, and Exploration Stamina. Understanding the nuances of each is vital for optimizing your daily and weekly routines in Hethereau.

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/how-does-stamina-work-nte/inline-1.webp)


Players often find themselves confused by the UI, which displays multiple icons and timers. The core questions most new Hunters have are:

*   **What are the different types of stamina in Neverness to Everness (NTE)?**
*   **How do Character Pixels and City Stamina differ in their usage and recovery?**
*   **How can players optimize their stamina consumption to progress faster?**

Each system has its own regeneration rules and specific sinks. While Character Pixels are your daily bread and butter for strengthening your Espers, City Stamina is a weekly-limited resource that fuels your rise through the Tycoon ranks.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/how-does-stamina-work-nte/inline-2.webp)


NTE uses three distinct stamina systems: Character Pixels (for combat and farming), City Stamina (for tycoon activities), and Exploration Stamina (for movement).

*   **Character Pixels** are the primary combat resource. They regenerate at a rate of 1 every 6 minutes, capping at a baseline of 240. It takes exactly 24 hours to recover a full bar from zero.
*   **City Stamina** is a weekly resource dedicated to Hethereau Hobbies and Tycoon progression. It does not regenerate over time; instead, it resets fully every Monday at 4:00 AM (UTC+8).
*   **Exploration Stamina** is the circular bar seen while sprinting, dodging, or climbing. It regenerates rapidly when not in use and can be permanently increased by submitting Oracle Stones at the Witch's House.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/how-does-stamina-work-nte/inline-3.webp)


The split nature of NTE's stamina means you are rarely "out of things to do." If you have spent all your Character Pixels farming for Arc ascension materials, you can still spend the rest of your evening engaging in the City Tycoon system or hunting for hidden Oracle Stones in the Miguel District.

### Character Pixels: Combat & Progression

Character Pixels are the gatekeeper for Esper power. Every time you enter an [Anomaly Zone](https://ntecodex.com/faq)—whether it is Houdini's Magic Stage for EXP or the Rabbit Hole for Cartridges—you must spend Pixels to claim rewards.

A standard [Anomaly Zone](https://ntecodex.com/faq) run costs **40 Character Pixels**. However, most players prefer the "Double Reward" toggle, which consumes **80 Character Pixels** to grant twice the loot in a single combat encounter. This is a significant time-saver but does not change the Pixel-to-loot efficiency.

**Character Pixel Recovery Options:**

| Method | Restore Amount | Daily Limit | Cost |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Natural Regeneration** | 1 per 6 mins | 240 / 24h | Free |
| **De-noise Solution** | 60 Pixels | None | Item Consumption |
| **Annulith (Premium)** | 60 Pixels | 6 times | 60 Annulith |

### City Stamina: Tycoon & Hobbies

City Stamina represents your "social energy" in Hethereau. It is spent exclusively on **Hethereau Hobbies**, which include mini-games like City Delivery, fishing, and passenger transport. Spending this stamina is the only way to earn Fons (the city's main currency) outside of passive income from the Cafe by Origen.

One of the most important milestones for this system is **Tycoon Level 16**. At this level, the efficiency of your City Stamina increases drastically, allowing you to generate roughly 1,000 Fons per 1 point of stamina spent. This makes it significantly easier to fund expensive purchases like the Novis ST-X 950 motorcycle or luxury apartments.

**City Stamina Cap by Tycoon Level:**

| Tycoon Level | City Stamina Cap |
| :--- | :--- |
| Level 1 | 100 |
| Level 5 | 200 |
| Level 10 | 350 |
| Level 16 | — |
| Level 23 | — |

To recover City Stamina before the weekly reset, you must use a **Refresh Recharge** item (restores 100) or spend Annulith. Note that Annulith refills for City Stamina are capped at 10 per week, and the cost increases after the 3rd and 6th recharge.

### Movement & Exploration Stamina

Exploration Stamina is the only system that does not require items or timers to refill. It governs how long you can sprint, how many walls you can climb, and—most importantly—how many times you can dodge in combat.

Proper management of this bar is the difference between life and death in high-level Anomaly Commissions. If you deplete your bar while sprinting toward a boss, you may find yourself unable to dodge their opening "Break" attack.

### How to Increase Your Stamina Caps

NTE allows players to permanently expand their stamina pools through world progression and housing.

1.  **Character Pixel Cap:** While the base is 240, you can increase this by purchasing properties (like the Eden apartment) and placing specific "Anomaly Furniture." Items such as the **Dream Weaver's Knot** or the **Cloud Buddy** furniture can push your cap toward 280, giving you more "buffer time" before the bar stops regenerating.
2.  **City Stamina Cap:** This is tied directly to your **Tycoon Level**. Earning Fons and completing Tycoon tasks (like buying your first car or opening the Coffee Shop) will raise your rank and your weekly stamina limit.
3.  **Exploration Stamina Cap:** This is increased by visiting the **Witch's House** (located southwest of Hankaku Street). By turning in **Oracle Stones**—which are obtained by finding "birds" or ravens hidden throughout Hethereau—you receive **VitalMax Energy Bars**. Each bar consumed permanently increases your max exploration stamina.

### Stamina Recovery Items & Methods

*   **De-noise Solution:** The primary item for Character Pixels. These are often rewarded through the Exploration Guide's Daily Quests or the Circle Bounty (Battle Pass).
*   **Refresh Recharge:** Used for City Stamina. These are rarer and typically found in the Hunter Exchange shop or as rewards for major Tycoon milestones.
*   **Annulith:** The premium currency. While it can refill both types of stamina, it is generally recommended to save Annulith for character banners unless you are pushing for a specific progression breakpoint.

## Related

Managing your stamina is just one part of the NTE loop. To fully optimize your account, you should also look into how these systems interact with your overall levels.

### Hunter Level vs. Appraisal Level

While spending Character Pixels grants **Hunter Level EXP**, which increases your world tier and character level caps, it does not directly affect your **Appraisal Level**. Appraisal Level is increased by discovering new items, completing the "Lost Tales" from the Witch's House, and filling out your Collection Level. You need both to unlock the highest-difficulty Anomaly Zones.

### Maximizing Fons with City Stamina

If you are short on time, the best Hethereau Hobbies for spending City Stamina are **City Delivery** and **Races**. While all hobbies provide roughly the same Fon-per-stamina ratio, these activities can be completed much faster than fishing or long-distance passenger transport. At Tycoon Level 10, you also unlock the **Pink Paws Heist**, which provides a massive influx of Fons without consuming any stamina at all—make sure to clear this every week.

### Anomaly Zone Material Priority

Early in the game, your Character Pixels should be spent in the following priority:
1.  **Houdini's Magic Stage:** For character and Arc EXP.
2.  **Bubble Can Factory:** For Arc ascension materials (essential for breaking level caps).
3.  **Rabbit Hole:** Only once you reach the level required for Orange (Legendary) Cartridges. Farming Purple gear is often considered a waste of Pixels in the long run.
