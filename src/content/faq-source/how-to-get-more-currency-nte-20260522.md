### Maximizing the City Tycoon System

The City Tycoon system acts as your account’s overall "economic level." As you perform actions in the city—such as shopping, completing side quests, and discovering landmarks—you earn Tycoon EXP.

Leveling up this system is mandatory for anyone looking to farm Annulith. Every 5 levels, the game rewards you with a bulk payout of 240 Annulith. Furthermore, reaching specific Tycoon milestones unlocks higher "Fons Multipliers." At lower ranks, a delivery mission might pay 5,000 Fons, but at higher Tycoon ranks, that same mission can pay upwards of 15,000 Fons for the same amount of effort.

### High-Stakes Farming: The Pink Paw Heist

The Pink Paw Heist is a unique weekly activity that separates NTE from other urban RPGs. Unlike most resource stages, this does not consume City Stamina. Instead, it is a skill-based challenge where you must infiltrate a secure vault and extract as many Fons as possible before the timer expires or security overwhelms you.

The rewards are tiered based on the total value of assets stolen. At the highest tier, a successful run can net you 1,000,000 Fons. Because this is a weekly lockout, it is the single most important activity for players looking to buy expensive vehicles or high-end apartments in the Diamond District.

### Converting City Stamina Efficiently

City Stamina is a regenerating resource used for most "official" jobs in Hethereau. If you have a surplus of stamina, you should convert it into Fons through the most efficient channels.

*   **Online Racing:** Offers the highest Fons-per-stamina ratio if you consistently place in the top three.
*   **City Delivery:** A more consistent, lower-stress method that provides a flat rate of Fons and Tycoon EXP.
*   **Owner's Selection:** A specialized combat challenge that provides Fons along with weapon enhancement materials.

Currently, the standard conversion efficiency sits at roughly 1:1000. If an activity offers less than 1,000 Fons per 1 Stamina point, it is generally considered inefficient unless it provides other rare materials.

### Earning Gacha Pulls: Annulith and Dice Sources

Annulith is the currency you will likely guard most closely. While Fons can be farmed infinitely through time and stamina, Annulith has a more defined ceiling.

1.  **Exploration Stars:** Each district in Hethereau has a completion percentage. Finding all Oracle Stones, tracking down hidden Ravens, and interacting with every Phone Booth triggers star rewards. Each milestone typically grants 60 to 100 Annulith.
2.  **Hunter Level Milestones:** As you increase your combat Hunter Level, check the rewards menu. High-tier milestones often bypass Annulith and give you **Solid Dice** directly.
3.  **Riftcrystals:** This is the "paid-only" currency. Riftcrystals convert to Annulith at a 1:1 ratio.
4.  **Tri-Keys:** These are rare items used to unlock special "Anomaly Chests" found in the open world, which contain small amounts of Annulith and rare upgrade materials.

### Exploration and Achievement Hunting

Neverness to Everness rewards players who poke into every corner of the map.
*   **Ravens:** These are collectible urban spirits. Collecting sets of them provides a significant Annulith payout at the Raven Association NPC.
*   **Oracle Stones:** These puzzles are scattered across rooftops and alleys. Each one solved provides a small amount of Annulith (usually 5-10) and contributes to district completion.
*   **Phone Booths:** Interacting with these often triggers "Urban Legends" (side quests) that have high currency payouts.
