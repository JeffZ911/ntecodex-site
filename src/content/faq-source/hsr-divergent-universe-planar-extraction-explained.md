# HSR Divergent Universe Planar Extraction Explained: The NTE Player's Guide

HSR's Planar Extraction allows players to skip full Simulated Universe runs by using weekly save files to challenge bosses directly for ornaments. In NTE, the Rabbit Hole and Rewind systems offer similar farming efficiency by streamlining how players acquire Cartridges and optimize Module shapes.

*   **What exactly is the Planar Extraction mechanic in Honkai: Star Rail's Divergent Universe?** This feature was introduced to alleviate the "grind fatigue" associated with the original Simulated Universe, where players had to clear multiple floors just to reach a single reward node.

### Defining Divergent Universe: A Human Comedy

The Divergent Universe represents a significant shift in HSR’s endgame. Unlike the traditional Simulated Universe, it emphasizes speed and flexibility. It introduces the "Arithmetic Mapping" feature, which automatically levels up characters, Light Cones, and Traces to a baseline standard, allowing players to test new team compositions without fully investing resources first.

### The Evolution of Planar Ornament Farming

Before the Divergent Universe, farming Planar Ornaments required a full 15-20 minute run through the Simulated Universe. The introduction of Planar Extraction changed this by decoupling the "run" from the "reward." Now, once a player completes a run for the week, the game remembers their Blessings and Curios, letting them jump straight to the final boss for the remainder of that week.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/hsr-divergent-universe-planar-extraction-explained/inline-2.webp)


Planar Extraction is a quality-of-life feature in HSR's Divergent Universe that bypasses the need for a full dungeon crawl. By using a "Save File" generated from a completed Ordinary Extrapolation or Cyclical Extrapolation run, players can challenge the final boss of a specific Planar Ornament set immediately.

Players use a 'Save File' from a completed run to jump straight to a boss fight, preserving all the overpowered buffs they collected. This ensures that even the hardest Threshold Protocol levels become farmable if the initial run was strong enough. Each extraction costs 40 Trailblaze Power or 1 Immersifier to claim rewards, mirroring the cost of standard Calyxes or Caverns of Corrosion.

In Neverness to Everness, the equivalent "quick-farm" is the Rabbit Hole domain for Cartridges and the Rewind system for Modules. While HSR focuses on skipping the path to the boss, NTE focuses on streamlining the acquisition of specific gear shapes and stats through a localized domain system and a secondary "controlled gacha" for gear refinement.

### Quick-Reference: HSR Planar Extraction Costs and Requirements

*   **Unlock Requirement:** Clear World 3 of Simulated Universe and complete the "Divergent Universe: The Human Comedy" quest.
*   **Stamina Cost:** 40 Trailblaze Power per reward claim.
*   **Alternative Currency:** 1 Immersifier.
*   **Save File Reset:** Every Monday at 4:00 AM server time.
*   **Maximum Rewards:** Unlimited, as long as the player has stamina/Immersifiers.

### NTE's Answer to Efficient Gearing

Neverness to Everness approaches efficiency through the Rabbit Hole system. Located primarily in [Cape Square](https://www.ign.com/games/neverness-to-everness) within the city of Hethereau, these domains function as localized combat challenges. Once discovered, they allow for rapid farming of Cartridges, which provide the primary set bonuses for characters. To complement this, the Rewind system allows players to spend Carrota Coins to "re-roll" or acquire specific Modules, reducing the need to repeat the same combat encounter hundreds of times for a single perfect piece.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/hsr-divergent-universe-planar-extraction-explained/inline-3.webp)


The depth of these systems lies in how they handle player power and itemization RNG. In HSR, the power is temporary (within the run), whereas in NTE, the power is permanent (the gear itself), but the "fit" of the gear is the primary challenge.

### Mastering HSR Save Files for Maximum Efficiency

The "Save File" in Divergent Universe is the cornerstone of efficient farming. When a player finishes a run, the game records every Equation, Blessing, and Curio obtained. 

1.  **Ordinary vs. Cyclical Extrapolation:** Ordinary Extrapolation is the standard mode where players can climb Threshold Protocols (difficulty levels). Cyclical Extrapolation is a weekly challenge with fixed buffs. A Save File can be created from either, but players usually aim for a high-synergy Ordinary run to make Planar Extraction trivial.
2.  **Support Characters:** HSR allows players to use one Support Character from a friend during Planar Extraction. This is a massive advantage for players who may not have a top-tier DPS for a specific boss's elemental weakness.
3.  **Threshold Protocols:** The higher the protocol level cleared in the Save File, the more Synchronicity Points a player earns, which levels up the Divergent Universe permanent reward tree.

### NTE Rabbit Hole: Farming Cartridges in Cape Square

In Neverness to Everness, the Rabbit Hole serves as the primary source for Cartridges. Unlike HSR’s Simulated Universe, which is a separate menu-based mode, Rabbit Holes are physical locations in the open world of Hethereau. 

The farming loop is straightforward:
*   Travel to [Cape Square](https://www.ign.com/games/neverness-to-everness).
*   Interact with the Rabbit Hole Anomaly.
*   Select the difficulty tier based on your Hunter Level.
*   Spend 40 Character Pixels to claim Cartridges and Modules.

### The Rewind System: How NTE Handles Module Substats

The most innovative part of NTE's gearing is the Rewind system. If a player fails to get the specific Module they need from a Rabbit Hole, they can use the Rewind mechanic. This acts as a "controlled gacha" where players use Carrota Coins to pull for specific gear pieces.

| Module Tier | Carrota Coin Type | Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Easy (Blue)** | Bronze Carrota Coin | Low-level Rabbit Holes / Exploration |
| **Normal (Purple)** | Silver Carrota Coin | Mid-level Rabbit Holes / Appraiser Level Rewards |
| **Hard (Gold)** | Gold Carrota Coin | High-level Rabbit Holes / City Tycoon Management |

This system ensures that even if a player has "bad luck" in the domain, their time is still rewarded with currency that can eventually guarantee the piece they need.

### Consoles and Tetris Gearing: The NTE Innovation

The Console is where NTE separates itself from HSR. In HSR, you simply equip an ornament. In NTE, characters have a Console grid (e.g., a 4x4 or 5x5 square). Modules come in various shapes—resembling Tetris blocks. 

To activate Cartridge set bonuses, players must fit the corresponding Modules into the Console grid. This adds a spatial puzzle element to gearing. A "perfect" piece isn't just one with high stats; it's one with the right shape to fit alongside your other high-stat Modules. Managing this grid is a core part of the Appraiser Level progression.

### Efficiency Showdown: Which Game Respects Your Time More?

HSR's Planar Extraction is faster for pure "daily" completion. A single boss fight can take under two minutes with a good Save File. NTE's Rabbit Hole takes slightly longer (3-5 minutes) because it involves waves of enemies and traversal within the domain. 

However, NTE's Rewind system offers a more consistent path to a "perfect" build. In HSR, you are entirely at the mercy of the Planar Ornament drop RNG unless you use the rare Self-Modeling Resin. NTE players can actively target shapes and stats through the Carrota Coin exchange, making the long-term grind feel more purposeful.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I use HSR Planar Extraction without clearing the Divergent Universe first?
No, you must complete at least one full run of Ordinary or Cyclical Extrapolation during the current week to create a Save File. Once that file is created, you can use Planar Extraction as many times as your Trailblaze Power allows.

### What happens to my NTE Modules if I replace them in the Console?
When you replace a Module in the Console, the old Module is returned to your inventory. You can then use it on another character or use it as experience material to level up other Modules. If the Module is no longer needed, it can sometimes be dismantled for Beetle Coins.

### Is there a limit to how many Carrota Coins I can earn in NTE?
While you can earn Carrota Coins through daily activities and Rabbit Holes, the most consistent source is the City Tycoon urban management system. The amount you can earn is generally tied to your Hunter Level and the number of businesses you manage within Hethereau.
