### Overview of Version 1.1 Reward Categories

The rewards in Version 1.1 are split into four primary categories: 
1.  **System Rewards:** These include maintenance compensation and bug fix bonuses sent directly to the in-game mail.
2.  **Activity Rewards:** Time-limited login events and daily check-ins that provide gacha currency and upgrade materials.
3.  **Gacha Milestones:** Cosmetic rewards and character-specific items earned by reaching specific pull counts on the "Fading Reverie" and "Forsaken Path" boards.
4.  **Content Rewards:** Rewards earned from exploring the new Sunward Island region and completing the Chapter 5 story missions.

### Eligibility Requirements for 1.1 Rewards

To qualify for the bulk of these rewards, Appraisers must have created their character before the maintenance window on June 3, 2026. Additionally, several event-based rewards are gated behind story progression. Accessing the new Sunward Island map and the Corridor of Echoes permanent anomaly zone requires completing the Version 1.1 main story episode, "Dreamwalk Corridor."

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/nte-1-1-dreamwalk-corridor-rewards-faq/inline-3.webp)


The reward structure of Version 1.1 is designed to keep players engaged throughout the six-week patch cycle, with the first phase focusing on the new S-rank character Lacrimosa and the second phase shifting to Chaos.

### Maintenance and Login Bonuses

Upon opening the game after the June 3 update, players will find two separate mail items containing 300 Annulith each. The first is standard downtime compensation, while the second is a bug fix compensation for issues identified in the previous version.

The **Circle Gifts** event is the primary free gacha source this patch. Between June 3 and July 8, logging in for a total of seven days grants a cumulative 10 Solid Dice. These dice are used for limited character banners, making them highly valuable for those aiming for Lacrimosa or Chaos.

### Chapter 5 Story and Corridor of Echoes Rewards

Completing the new story chapter, "Dreamwalk Corridor," is essential for unlocking recurring rewards. The story takes players to Sunward Island, a coastal region that serves as a departure from the urban sprawl of Hethereau. 

Once the main story is finished, players unlock the **Corridor of Echoes**, a permanent anomaly zone. This zone functions as a recurring source of **Fons** (the primary in-game currency) and upgrade materials required for character ascension.

### Fading Reverie Gacha Milestone Breakdown

The **Fading Reverie** banner features Lacrimosa, an S-rank Chaos-element character. Unlike standard banners, NTE's limited boards include a milestone system that rewards high-investment players with cosmetic items.

| Pull Milestone | Reward |
| :--- | :--- |
| **50 Pulls** | "Tomato Duo" Glider |
| **120 Pulls** | "Ketchup Cruise" Vehicle Livery |
| **200 Pulls** | "Gilded Rhapsody" Lacrimosa Outfit |

These rewards are cumulative, meaning players who reach the 200-pull guarantee will receive all previous milestone items. A similar system is expected for the **Forsaken Path** banner featuring Chaos in Phase 2.

### Sunward Travelogue and Limited-Time Events

The "Sunward Travelogue" event encourages exploration of the new Sunni Island map. By completing specific "Strange Tales" and finding collectibles like Oracle Stones, players can earn:
*   **Annulith:** Totaling around 300 from exploration tasks.
*   **Chaotic Dye:** Used for customizing vehicle and character appearances.
*   **Expansion Cores:** Critical for upgrading the Appraiser's equipment.
*   **Exclusive Furniture:** Themed items for the Pegasus Residence.

Another major event, **"What's Baking,"** allows players to participate in the Bagel SNS system. Completing cooking challenges and social interactions rewards players with the **"Feast Champion" title** and additional Fons.

### Porsche Collaboration: How to Get the 918 Spyder

The NTE × Porsche collaboration is a centerpiece of Version 1.1. The **"Everdriving" Mystery Box** event is the only way to obtain the Porsche 918 Spyder. This system uses a scaling cost model where players can spend either Fons or Annulith to open boxes.

| Box Number | Fons Cost | Annulith Cost |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1st Box** | 50,000 | 50 |
| **Subsequent**| Scaling Increase | Scaling Increase |
| **15th Box** | [Guaranteed All Rewards] | [Guaranteed All Rewards] |

The total pool contains 15 boxes. Opening all of them guarantees the 918 Spyder, the "Perfect Partners" outfit for Appraiser Zero, and the **"Heartbeat Moment"** avatar frame. While F2P players can earn enough Fons to open several boxes, a full guarantee typically requires a mix of saved Fons and Annulith.

### Active Version 1.1 Redeem Codes

To help bridge the gap for pulls, several redeem codes were released alongside the "Dreamwalk Corridor" update. These codes must be entered in the "Account" section of the settings menu.

*   **DREAMWALK0603:** Grants Annulith and upgrade materials (Expires June 25).
*   **TOMATO100:** Grants Fons and character EXP items (Expires June 25).
*   **RACENOLIMIT:** Grants vehicle-related materials and Fabricated Dice (Expires June 25).

## Frequently Asked Questions

![Frequently Asked Questions](/img/nte-1-1-dreamwalk-corridor-rewards-faq/inline-4.webp)


### How many free pulls can I get in NTE 1.1?
Players can earn approximately 10 Solid Dice from the 7-day login event and over 2,000 Annulith by completing all Sunward Island exploration and limited-time events. Combined with maintenance compensation, this totals roughly 25 to 30 pulls for the limited banners.

### Is the Porsche 918 Spyder permanent?
The Porsche 918 Spyder is a limited-time collaboration vehicle available only during the Everdriving Mystery Box event from June 3 to July 8, 2026. Once the event ends, it is currently unknown if the vehicle will return in future rotations.

### What is the difference between Solid Dice and Fabricated Dice?
Solid Dice are used specifically for limited character banners like Lacrimosa’s, while Fabricated Dice are generally used for the standard banner or weapon-focused boards. Version 1.1 provides both through various event milestones.

### How do I unlock Sunward Island?
You must complete the main story mission "A Research in Scarlet" from Version 1.0. Once completed, the Version 1.1 "Dreamwalk Corridor" episode will become available, leading you to the new coastal region.
