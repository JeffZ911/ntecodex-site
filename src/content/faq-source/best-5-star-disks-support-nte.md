Optimizing your support Espers with the correct gear is the most effective way to multiply your team's damage output and ensure smooth rotations in high-level content. This guide breaks down the best 5-star disks—officially known as Cartridges—to equip on your support units to dominate the current meta.

# Best 5-Star Disks for Support in Neverness to Everness (NTE)

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/best-5-star-disks-support-nte/inline-1.webp)


In Neverness to Everness, the gear system is divided into two primary components: Cartridges (the base "disk") and Modules (the Tetris-style blocks that fit inside). When players look for the "best 5-star disks for support," they are typically searching for the 4-piece set bonuses that provide the most utility to the entire party.

The top-rated 5-star support Cartridges are those that offer team-wide ATK buffs, increased Energy regeneration (Charge Efficiency), or defensive utility like shields. Because NTE relies on a "Cycle" system—where you rotate between three characters to trigger elemental reactions like Blossom, Hex, or Nova—support disks must facilitate fast Ultimate uptime and provide buffs that persist even when the support character is off-field.

### Defining 'Disks' in the NTE Gear System
In the game’s UI, "Disks" are referred to as **Cartridges**. These are the primary gear items you farm using Character Pixels (stamina). Every Cartridge has one Main Stat and four Sub-stats. To activate the powerful set bonuses (2-piece and 4-piece), you must slot specific **Modules** into the character's **Console**. The Console is a grid-based board where you arrange these Modules in a puzzle-like fashion. A support "build" is only complete when the Cartridge's requirements are met by the shapes and types of the Modules equipped.

### The Role of Support in the Esper Cycle
Support characters in NTE are not just healers or shielders; they are "Enablers." Their primary job is to build the **Esper Cycle** meter and apply buffs that allow your Main DPS (like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) or Chiz) to deal maximum damage. The best support disks are those that trigger their effects upon casting a Skill or an Ultimate, as these actions are the core of every combat rotation.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/best-5-star-disks-support-nte/inline-2.webp)


The current meta for support Espers is dominated by sets that increase team-wide offensive stats. The absolute best 5-star support disk set is **Speedy Hedgehog**. It is the gold standard because it provides both Charge Efficiency for the wearer and a significant ATK buff for the entire team.

For supports who focus on Skill usage rather than Ultimates, **Shadow Creed** is the premier choice. Defensive supports, such as Adler, prioritize **Kingdom's Guard** for its shield-scaling bonuses. Meanwhile, the Main Character (Zero) often runs **Lost Radiance** to take advantage of Cosmos-type damage and DEF-ignore utility.

### Best-in-Slot (BiS) Recommendations by Character

*   **Sakiri (S-Rank, Incantation):** **Speedy Hedgehog** is her undisputed BiS. Her kit revolves around her Ultimate ("Feast of Gluttony"), which groups enemies and applies debuffs. Adding a 15% team-wide ATK buff on top of her natural utility makes her the strongest support in the game.
*   **Haniel (A-Rank, Psyche):** **Speedy Hedgehog** is also the best choice here. Haniel is a highly accessible support who buffs ATK based on her own Base ATK. The Charge Efficiency helps her keep her speaker, "Hootie," on the field more consistently.
*   **Adler (B-Rank, Physical/Shield):** **Kingdom's Guard** is essential. Adler’s shields scale with his DEF, and the 20% boost to shield strength is vital for surviving "High Risk Commission" boss fights.
*   **Zero (Cosmos):** **Lost Radiance** is the preferred 5-star set. Since Zero is a hybrid who often stays on the field to bridge cycles, the 25% DEF ignore after their Ultimate provides a massive boost to the team's total damage.

## Detailed Explanation

Understanding why certain sets are "best" requires looking at how stats scale in the endgame. In Neverness to Everness, damage is calculated through a complex formula involving ATK, Elemental DMG%, and Enemy DEF. Support disks are designed to manipulate these variables in your favor.

### The Speedy Hedgehog Meta Breakdown
Speedy Hedgehog is currently the most used support set for one reason: **uptime**. The 4-piece bonus lasts for 20 seconds, which is a very generous window in NTE's fast-paced combat. Most Ultimates have a cooldown between 15 and 25 seconds, meaning a well-built Sakiri or Haniel can maintain a near-permanent 15% ATK buff for the Main DPS.

Furthermore, the 2-piece bonus of 12% Charge Efficiency is crucial. Energy (Charge) is generated by dealing damage and triggering reactions. Without Charge Efficiency, supports often find themselves waiting for their Ultimate to ready while the DPS is already prepared for a burst window. This set synchronizes the entire team's rhythm.

### Optimizing the Console: Cartridges vs. Modules
A 5-star Cartridge is useless without the correct Modules. Each Cartridge set has "Required Pieces"—specific types of Modules (Type II, III, or IV) that must be present in the Console to activate the set bonus.

*   **Type II Modules:** Usually smaller, 2-block shapes.
*   **Type III Modules:** 3-block Tetris shapes. These are highly valued for Sakiri, as her Console gives a **10% Incantation DMG bonus for every Type III Module** equipped.
*   **Type IV Modules:** Large 4-block shapes that provide the highest flat stats but are harder to fit into the grid.

To maximize a support like Sakiri, you should aim for a Speedy Hedgehog Cartridge and fill the Console with as many Type III Modules as possible. This allows her to contribute significant chip damage while her buffs are active.

### Sub-stat Strategy for Support Disks
Unlike other gacha games where sub-stats are hidden until you level the item, NTE shows all four sub-stats the moment a Cartridge or Module drops. This makes farming much more efficient. For support characters, you should prioritize the following stats:

1.  **Charge Efficiency (Right Disk Slot):** This is the most important main stat for any support. It ensures you can spam Ultimates.
2.  **HP% / DEF%:** Essential for survivability, especially for characters like Adler who scale directly off these stats.
3.  **ATK%:** Important for Haniel, as her team-wide buff scales with her own ATK.
4.  **Cycle Intensity:** A niche but powerful stat that increases the damage dealt by elemental reactions (Blossom, Nova, etc.) triggered by that character.

### Farming 5-Star Disks in Rabbit Holes
To get 5-star (S-Rank) Cartridges, you must farm the **Rabbit Hole** challenges located in Cape Square, south of the New Herland District. These domains cost Character Pixels to enter. 

For Modules, you use the **Rewind** system in the Console menu. This is a separate gacha-like system that consumes **Carrota Coins**.
*   **Bronze/Silver Carrota Coins:** Used for lower-tier Modules.
*   **Gold Carrota Coins:** Used for the "Hard" Rewind difficulty, which is the only way to reliably get 5-star (Gold) Modules with perfect shapes.
You earn Carrota Coins by completing daily activities and dismantling unwanted Modules.

### The 'Rewind' System and Gold Carrota Coins
The Rewind system allows you to "roll" for new Modules. At Hunter Level 30, you unlock the Hard difficulty, which guarantees higher-rarity drops. If you are trying to min-max a support, you should save your Gold Carrota Coins until you reach this level. This ensures you aren't wasting resources on 4-star gear that will eventually be replaced.

## Related

![Related](/img/best-5-star-disks-support-nte/inline-4.webp)


Gearing a support Esper involves more than just Disks. To truly reach the "ceiling" of support utility, you must pair your 5-star Cartridges with the right Arcs (weapons) and understand the Resonance system.

### Best Support Arcs (Weapons) to Pair with Disks
While Disks provide the set bonuses, Arcs provide the raw stats and unique passive effects.
*   **Good Boy's Grand Adventure (S-Rank):** The best support Arc for Incantation characters like Sakiri. it provides 18% Charge Efficiency and an additional 16% team ATK buff after an Ultimate. When paired with Speedy Hedgehog, the total ATK buff reaches 31%.
*   **Blow up the Crowd (S-Rank):** The signature Arc for Haniel. It provides a stackable ATK buff to the active character whenever Haniel deals off-field damage with her speaker.
*   **Tears Beneath the Mask:** A defensive alternative that reduces incoming damage for the whole team, ideal for high-difficulty boss encounters where survivability is an issue.

### NTE Support Tier List Overview
Currently, **Sakiri** and **Haniel** sit at the top of the support tier list (S+ and S respectively). Sakiri is favored for her superior crowd control and the fact that her buffs are easy to trigger. Haniel is the best F2P-friendly option because the game gives players multiple copies of her through pre-registration rewards and story progression, making it easy to unlock her **Awakenings**.

### Advanced Gearing: The Resonance System
Once you have your 5-star Disks and Arcs, the final step is **Resonance**. By using duplicate Cartridges or specific materials, you can "Awaken" your gear. For support disks, Resonance often unlocks additional Charge Efficiency or increases the duration of the 4-piece set bonus. This is strictly endgame content and should only be attempted once you have a stable team of level 50+ Espers.

### Building the Ultimate 'Blossom' Team
A popular meta team involves using **Sakiri** (Support), **Zero** (Hybrid/Support), and **Nanally** (Main DPS). 
1.  **Sakiri** enters first, uses her Skill to group enemies, and her Ultimate to trigger the **Speedy Hedgehog** buff.
2.  **Zero** enters to apply Cosmos stacks and trigger the **Blossom** reaction.
3.  **Nanally** enters and uses her Skill/Ultimate to "pop" the stacks, dealing massive Anima damage.
This rotation is only possible if both Sakiri and Zero have high Charge Efficiency from their 5-star Disk sets.
