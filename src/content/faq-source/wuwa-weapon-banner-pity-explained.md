Understanding the pity system in Wuthering Waves is the most effective way to manage your Astrite and ensure your Resonators are equipped with their best-in-slot gear. Unlike many other gacha titles, Wuthering Waves offers a highly transparent and player-friendly weapon banner system that removes the frustration of "losing" to off-banner items.

# Wuthering Waves Weapon Banner Pity Explained: Rates, Guarantees, and Rules

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/wuwa-weapon-banner-pity-explained/inline-1.webp)


Players frequently ask several key questions regarding the weapon banners in Wuthering Waves to avoid wasting their premium currency. The most common inquiries include:

*   How many pulls are required to guarantee a 5-star weapon in Wuthering Waves?
*   Does the weapon banner have a 50/50 system like the character banner?
*   How does pity carry over between different weapon banners?
*   What are the specific rates for 4-star and 5-star weapons?

Navigating these systems requires an understanding of "Hard Pity," "Soft Pity," and the specific currencies used for different banner types. This guide breaks down every mechanic to help you plan your pulls effectively.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/wuwa-weapon-banner-pity-explained/inline-2.webp)


The Wuthering Waves weapon pity system is designed to be straightforward. The hard pity for a 5-star weapon is exactly 80 pulls. This means if you have not received a 5-star weapon in 79 consecutive attempts, the 80th pull is guaranteed to be a 5-star weapon.

Crucially, the featured weapon banner in Wuthering Waves does not utilize a 50/50 system. When you trigger a 5-star weapon on a limited banner, it is guaranteed to be the featured signature weapon. This 100% guarantee makes the weapon banner significantly more predictable than the character banner.

For 4-star items, a guarantee is triggered every 10 pulls. Pity progress is persistent and carries over between banners of the same type—for example, progress made on a current limited weapon banner will transfer to the next limited weapon banner. In the Standard Weapon Banner (Winter Brume), you can select a specific 5-star weapon from the standard pool, and you are guaranteed to receive that specific choice at 80 pulls.

| Mechanic | Featured Weapon Banner | Standard Weapon Banner |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **5-Star Hard Pity** | 80 Pulls | 80 Pulls |
| **5-Star Guarantee** | 100% (Featured Weapon) | 100% (Selected Weapon) |
| **4-Star Pity** | 10 Pulls | 10 Pulls |
| **Base 5-Star Rate** | 0.8% | 0.8% |
| **Currency Used** | Forging Tide | Lustrous Tide |

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/wuwa-weapon-banner-pity-explained/inline-3.webp)


### The 100% Guarantee Mechanic

The defining feature of the Wuthering Waves weapon banner system is the 100% guarantee for featured 5-star weapons. In the character "Event Convene," players face a 50/50 chance where the first 5-star pulled may not be the featured character. If the 50/50 is lost, the next 5-star is guaranteed. 

The "Weapon Targeted Convene" skips this hurdle entirely. If you see the gold resonance animation on a limited weapon banner, you have successfully obtained the featured weapon, such as Jiyan's signature "Verdant Summit" or Yinlin's "Stringmaster." This mechanic removes the need for a "path" system or multiple pities to secure a signature weapon, making it one of the most accessible weapon gacha systems in the genre.

### Standard Weapon Selector (Winter Brume)

The Standard Weapon Targeted Convene, titled "Winter Brume," functions differently than the standard character banner. Instead of pulling from a randomized pool of all 5-star weapons, the game allows you to set a "Targeted" weapon.

You can choose one of the following five standard 5-star weapons:
*   **Emerald of Genesis** (Sword)
*   **Lustrous Razor** (Broadblade)
*   **Static Mist** (Pistols)
*   **Abyss Surges** (Gauntlets)
*   **Cosmic Ripples** (Rectifier)

Once a weapon is selected, the 80-pull pity applies specifically to that weapon. You can change your selection at any time before hitting the 5-star, and your pity count will remain intact. This ensures that players can always obtain a high-tier weapon that matches their primary damage dealer without relying on luck.

### Soft Pity vs. Hard Pity Thresholds

While the hard pity is set at 80 pulls, the actual probability of obtaining a 5-star weapon increases significantly before that point. This is known as "Soft Pity." 

Based on community data and historical pull tracking, the base rate of 0.8% begins to climb around pull 65 to 70. Most players find that they receive their 5-star weapon between 65 and 75 pulls. While the official documentation only specifies the 80-pull limit, the statistical likelihood of reaching pull 80 without a 5-star is quite low. If you are planning your Astrite budget, it is safer to assume you will need approximately 70 pulls, though you should always have enough for 80 to account for the worst-case scenario.

### Pity Carry-over and Reset Rules

Pity in Wuthering Waves is categorized by banner type. It is essential to distinguish between these categories to understand where your "hidden" progress lies:

1.  **Limited Weapon Banner Pity:** This carries over from one limited weapon banner to the next. If you pull 40 times on a limited weapon banner and do not get a 5-star, you will start the next limited weapon banner with 40 pity already built up.
2.  **Standard Weapon Banner Pity:** This is independent and only tracks progress on the "Winter Brume" banner.
3.  **Reset Rule:** Once you pull a 5-star weapon, the pity counter for that specific banner type resets to zero immediately. This happens regardless of whether you hit the weapon at 10 pulls or 80 pulls.

### Currency Guide: Forging Tides and Lustrous Tides

Managing your pull currency is vital for maximizing the pity system. Wuthering Waves uses different "Tides" for different banners:

*   **Forging Tides (Limited):** Used for the Featured Weapon Targeted Convene. These are usually purchased with Astrite (160 Astrite per Tide).
*   **Lustrous Tides (Standard):** Used for the Standard Weapon Targeted Convene and the Standard Character Convene. These are often given as rewards for Union Level milestones, Battle Pass levels, and through the shop's monthly reset.

You should generally avoid spending Astrite on Lustrous Tides. Because the standard weapon banner is permanent and the game provides Lustrous Tides through gameplay, it is more efficient to save your Astrite for Forging Tides to secure limited-time signature weapons.

### 4-Star Pity Rules

The 4-star pity system ensures that you receive a 4-star item (either a weapon or a Resonator) at least once every 10 pulls. On the Featured Weapon Banner, there are usually three featured 4-star weapons that have a "Rate-Up." 

When you pull a 4-star item on a limited weapon banner, there is a 50% chance it will be one of the three featured 4-star weapons. If the first 4-star you pull is not one of the featured weapons, the next 4-star you pull is guaranteed to be one of the featured ones. This 4-star guarantee also carries over between banner rotations.

### How to Get Free 5-Star Weapons

Wuthering Waves provides several avenues for players to acquire 5-star weapons without spending Astrite. The most significant of these is the **Union Level 45 Reward**. 

As part of the "Awakening Journey" permanent event, players receive a "5-Star Standard Weapon Supply Chest" upon reaching Union Level 45. This chest allows you to choose one of the five standard 5-star weapons (the same pool as the Winter Brume banner). 

**Union Level Rewards Summary:**
*   **Union Level 5-35:** Various Lustrous Tides and Astrite.
*   **Union Level 45:** 5-Star Standard Weapon Supply Chest and 1,000 Astrite.
*   **Union Level 80:** [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-15].

Because of this free chest, many players choose to wait until Union Level 45 before spending their Lustrous Tides on the standard weapon banner. This allows you to see which weapon you get from the chest first, then use your selector banner to get a different weapon or a duplicate for "Syntonization" (refinement).

### Best Standard Weapons to Target

If you are using your Standard Weapon Targeted Convene or your Union Level 45 chest, certain weapons offer more universal value than others.

1.  **Emerald of Genesis (Sword):** Widely considered the best standard weapon. It provides a Crit Rate sub-stat and increases Energy Regeneration. Since many top-tier characters use swords (such as Rover, Sanhua, and Yangyang), this is a high-value pick.
2.  **Static Mist (Pistols):** Another high-tier option that provides Crit Rate. It also features a unique mechanic that restores Energy to the next character switched in, making it excellent for sub-DPS characters like Chixia or Mortefi.
3.  **Lustrous Razor (Broadblade):** A solid choice for Broadblade users like Jiyan or Calcharo if you do not have their signature weapons. It provides ATK% and increases Resonance Liberation damage.
4.  **Abyss Surges (Gauntlets) & Cosmic Ripples (Rectifier):** These are more niche and should generally only be picked if you main a character that specifically requires these weapon types.

### Managing Afterglow and Oscillated Corals

Every time you pull on a weapon banner, you receive secondary currencies: **Afterglow Corals** and **Oscillated Corals**.

*   **Afterglow Corals:** Obtained when pulling 4-star or 5-star items. These can be used in the "Adversity Exchange" shop to purchase additional Forging Tides or Lustrous Tides. More importantly, they can be saved to purchase "Wavebands" for characters or specific weapon materials.
*   **Oscillated Corals:** Obtained when pulling 3-star weapons. These should be used every month to buy the discounted Forging Tides and Lustrous Tides from the "Item Exchange" shop. This shop resets monthly, and purchasing these pulls is the most efficient use of this currency.

### Checking Your History

To calculate how close you are to pity, you can use the in-game "Convene History" button located at the bottom left of the Convene screen. 

The history log tracks every pull you have made, but note that there is often a delay (up to 1 hour) between making a pull and it appearing in the log. To calculate your pity:
1.  Open the history for the specific banner you are interested in.
2.  Count the number of pulls starting from the most recent entry back to your last 5-star weapon.
3.  Subtract that number from 80 to see exactly how many pulls remain until your hard pity guarantee.

By monitoring your history and understanding the 100% guarantee on featured banners, you can ensure that you never miss a signature weapon for your favorite Resonator.
