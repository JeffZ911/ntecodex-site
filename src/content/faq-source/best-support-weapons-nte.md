In the neon-drenched streets of Hethereau, your choice of Arc defines whether your team survives the supernatural anomalies or falls to the urban shadows. Selecting the best support weapons for Neverness to Everness (NTE) requires balancing off-field buffs, energy generation, and specialized scaling for healers like Fadia.

# What are the Best Support Weapons in Neverness to Everness?

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/best-support-weapons-nte/inline-1.webp)


Identifying the top-tier support Arcs currently available in NTE involves looking beyond raw damage to find utility that empowers your entire squad. Unlike Main DPS weapons that focus on critical stats, support weapons prioritize team-wide Attack (ATK) buffs, Ultimate energy regeneration, and defensive utility like shields or healing bonuses.

In the current meta, the distinction between different support types is vital. We categorize these into:
*   **Off-field Buffers:** Arcs that provide stat increases to the active character while the wearer is swapped out.
*   **Energy Batteries:** Weapons that maximize Charge Efficiency to ensure the team can cycle through Ultimate abilities without downtime.
*   **Defensive Anchors:** Arcs that scale with HP or DEF to boost the effectiveness of healers and shield-generating Espers.
*   **Break Supports:** Specialized weapons that increase Break Intensity, allowing the team to shatter enemy defenses faster.

Addressing weapon types is also mandatory. In NTE, characters are restricted to specific Arc types—Solid, Gas, Synthesis, Liquid, or Plasma. A support build is only effective if the Arc type matches the Esper’s compatibility, such as pairing Gas-type Arcs with [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) or Synthesis-type Arcs with Fadia.

### Defining the Support Role in NTE
Supports in NTE do not just sit on the sidelines; they enable the high-damage rotations of characters like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) or Jiuyuan. A support Esper's primary job is to trigger "Redirect Skills" and "Support Skills" that activate weapon passives. For instance, certain Arcs summon autonomous entities like the "Black Tome" or "Officer Whisker," which provide automated assistance while you focus on your primary attacker.

### Why Choosing the Right Arc is Critical for Endgame
As you progress through City Tycoon and tackle higher-level Anomaly Commissions, enemy health pools and Break gauges become significantly more difficult to manage. Without the correct support weapon to provide off-field ATK scaling or defensive mitigation, your Main DPS will struggle to clear content within the required time limits. The right Arc can provide up to a 14% ATK boost to your active character simply by having the support Esper present in the party.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/best-support-weapons-nte/inline-2.webp)


The best overall support weapon in Neverness to Everness is **Blow up the Crowd** due to its unparalleled off-field ATK buffs that benefit any active DPS. For teams focused on fast rotations and energy sustain, **Good Boy's Grand Adventure** stands as the premier Gas-type choice, offering high Charge Efficiency and team-wide ATK scaling. 

Healers and tanks that scale with HP, most notably Fadia, should prioritize **Eternal Waltz**. This Arc provides a massive HP substat and a Mental DMG bonus that triggers after casting an Ultimate. For F2P players, **Be Happy** (B-Rank) and **Umbrella** (A-Rank) are the most reliable early-game options for survivability and shield strengthening.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/best-support-weapons-nte/inline-3.webp)


### S-Rank Powerhouses: Buffers and Batteries
**Blow up the Crowd** is currently the gold standard for support utility in NTE. While it possesses a high base ATK of 512, its true value lies in the "Beat King" effect. This passive increases the active character's ATK by 10% while the wearer is off-field. If the wearer deals damage, they can stack this bonus further, up to an additional 3.2% per stack. This makes it the ideal weapon for Haniel, who can drop her abilities and swap out, leaving your Main DPS with a significant offensive advantage.

**Good Boy's Grand Adventure** focuses on "Charge Efficiency," a stat that dictates how quickly an Esper gains energy for their Ultimate. At base level, it provides an 18% boost to Charge Efficiency. Furthermore, casting an Ultimate with this Arc equipped grants the entire team a 10% ATK buff for 20 seconds. If the Ultimate controls or CCs enemies—a common trait for Gas-type characters like Sakiri—the buff increases by an additional 6%.

### Healing and Survivability: The Best Defensive Arcs
For defensive supports, HP scaling is often more important than ATK. **Eternal Waltz** is the signature weapon for Fadia and provides a 20% Max HP increase. Since Fadia’s "Furnace of Guilt" passive drains Max HP from enemies to heal her team, this Arc directly increases the team's total effective health. After Fadia uses her Ultimate, "Agony to Euphoria," the Arc also boosts Mental DMG by 10%, allowing her to contribute meaningful sub-DPS damage while tanking.

If you are looking for shield utility, the A-Rank Arc **Umbrella** is a surprisingly potent alternative. It provides a 52.5% DEF substat and strengthens shields by 10% as long as the wearer's HP remains above 50%. This is particularly effective for shield-heavy supports like Adler.

### The Role of Break Intensity in Support Builds
Breaking an enemy's gauge is the fastest way to deal massive damage in NTE. **Youthful Fantasy** is a Liquid-type Arc that specializes in this mechanic. It increases Break Intensity by 60 and features a unique "Black Tome" seal. Using Support Skills on your team unleashes chains; once two chains are broken, the Black Tome is summoned for 20 seconds. This tome designates enemies and deals Chaos DMG equal to 200% of the wearer's ATK whenever the target takes Break Damage. This synergy makes it the best choice for Daffodil or Edgar when they are played in a support capacity.

### F2P Support Arcs: Maximizing Value with Lower Rarity
Not every player will pull multiple S-Rank Arcs from the Scarborough Fair. Fortunately, several B-Rank and A-Rank options provide excellent utility:
*   **Be Happy (B-Rank):** A Gas-type Arc that restores 12% of the wearer's HP upon defeating an enemy. It is a solid choice for early-game survivability.
*   **Call of the Twisted City (A-Rank):** A Liquid-type Arc that grants a 12% Healing Bonus for 10 seconds after the wearer uses a Redirect Skill. This is the go-to budget option for healers who lack their signature S-Rank Arcs.
*   **First Step to Success (B-Rank):** A Solid-type Arc that provides a simple 12% ATK boost for 10 seconds after a Redirect Skill, making it a decent placeholder for offensive supports.

### How to Refine Support Arcs for Maximum Team Utility
In NTE, refinement is known as "Mixing." You can upgrade an Arc from T1 to T5 by consuming duplicate copies of the same weapon. For support Arcs, Mixing is often more impactful than leveling, as it scales the percentage-based team buffs.

## Related

### Best Support Characters in NTE
Weapon choice is only half the battle; you must pair these Arcs with the right Espers.
*   **Fadia (Synthesis):** The premier "Survival" role character. She excels with **Eternal Waltz** and the **Tiny Big Adventure** Cartridge set.
*   **Haniel (Solid):** A versatile buffer and healer. She is the best user of **Blow up the Crowd** for maximizing team DPS.
*   **Sakiri (Gas):** A control-oriented support who thrives with **Good Boy's Grand Adventure**, using her CC to trigger the Arc's secondary ATK buffs.
*   **[Hotori](/characters/hotori-best-team-nte) (Solid):** A newer addition to the roster who utilizes the "Wastelab" mechanic of **Marching Beyond Time** to stack Ultimate Crit DMG for the team.

### How to Farm S-Rank Arcs for Free
While the Fair is the primary way to get Arcs, many S-Rank weapons are farmable:
1.  **Anomaly Commissions:** High-level commissions often drop specific S-Rank Arcs as first-time clear rewards.
2.  **Field Bosses:** Defeating the **Never-Ending Arachne** boss in the Ebisu Auction House is a guaranteed way to obtain the **Eternal Waltz** Arc for free.
3.  **City Tycoon Progression:** Completing world exploration milestones and business management tasks in Hethereau rewards players with Miracle Boxes and high-tier Arcs.

### Weapon and Cartridge Synergy Guide
To truly optimize a support, you must match your Arc with the correct Cartridges and Modules. For HP-scaling supports like Fadia, the **Tiny Big Adventure** 4-piece set is mandatory, as it provides a stacking HP% buff when taking damage. If you are using an energy-focused weapon like **Good Boy's Grand Adventure**, look for Cartridges that provide "Ultimate Energy Recovery" or "Cycle Intensity" to ensure your support can keep their weapon passives active 100% of the time.

As Neverness to Everness continues to update, keep an eye on new Arcs released during limited banners, such as the "Bright Moon Special Arc Research Program," which recently introduced **Marching Beyond Time**. Future-proofing your support builds involves maintaining a diverse collection of Arcs across all five types (Solid, Gas, Synthesis, Liquid, Plasma) to ensure you can support any new Main DPS character that joins your roster.
