With the urban supernatural landscape of Hethereau now open to the public, players are transitioning from the post-apocalyptic wilds of Solaris-3 to a neon-drenched metropolis. # Neverness to Everness Beginner Tips Guide: Essential 2026 Starter Info

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/wuthering-waves-beginner-tips-guide-nte/inline-1.webp)


What are the most important tips for players starting Neverness to Everness (NTE) in 2026? As a spiritual successor to the high-intensity combat found in [Wuthering Waves](https://ntecodex.com/guides/wuthering-waves-2-6-vs-2-5-meta-gear-guide-20260706), NTE introduces several new layers of complexity, including a business management economy and a board-game-inspired gacha system. Beginners often ask how the combat elements differ from the Attribute system in Wuthering Waves and what specific milestones should be reached in the first 10 hours of gameplay to ensure long-term account health.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/wuthering-waves-beginner-tips-guide-nte/inline-2.webp)


Success in Neverness to Everness requires a shift in focus from pure exploration to a balance of urban management and tactical combat. To optimize your start, prioritize the main story until you reach Hunter Level 10, which unlocks the Bureau of Anomaly Control and the full City Tycoon suite. Unlike the 50/50 pity system in [Wuthering Waves](https://ntecodex.com/guides/wuthering-waves-2-6-vs-2-5-meta-gear-guide-20260706), the NTE "Scarborough Fair" gacha guarantees the featured S-class character (such as [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) or Hotori) at 90 pulls with no chance of losing to a standard unit. 

Combat mastery revolves around the Esper Cycle, where you must chain elemental reactions like "Blossom" and "Discord" to bypass enemy shields. For traversal, save 250,000 Fons early to purchase the Novis ST-X 950 motorcycle; its ability to be summoned off-road makes it significantly more versatile than early-game cars for navigating Hethereau’s vertical architecture. Finally, aim for City Tycoon Level 16 as quickly as possible to double the efficiency of your weekly City Stamina.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/wuthering-waves-beginner-tips-guide-nte/inline-3.webp)


The transition from the open-world exploration of [Wuthering Waves](https://ntecodex.com/guides/wuthering-waves-2-6-vs-2-5-meta-gear-guide-20260706) to the "supernatural urban" setting of NTE involves learning several unique systems. While Wuthering Waves focuses on Echo farming and parry-heavy combat, NTE emphasizes elemental synergy and economic scaling.

### 1. Progression: Main Story and Hunter Level
In Neverness to Everness, your account progress is measured by your Hunter Level. Similar to the Union Level in [Wuthering Waves](https://ntecodex.com/guides/wuthering-waves-2-6-vs-2-5-meta-gear-guide-20260706), your Hunter Level gates your ability to ascend characters and access higher-tier Anomaly Hunts. 

The first 10 hours should be dedicated almost exclusively to the Episode Quests, specifically the "Prologue: A New Destination." Completing this arc unlocks the Eibon Antique Shop, which serves as your base of operations. By Hunter Level 10, you gain access to the Bureau of Anomaly Control (BAC) commissions. These are the primary source of character experience materials and "Fons," the game's main currency. Stalling the main story to explore Hethereau early is a common mistake that slows down your access to the Garage and Housing systems.

### 2. Combat Mastery: The Esper Cycle and Reactions
Combat in NTE is built on the Esper Cycle, a system of six elements that interact in "Duo" and "Trio" combinations. Unlike [Wuthering Waves](https://ntecodex.com/guides/wuthering-waves-2-6-vs-2-5-meta-gear-guide-20260706), where elements primarily provide damage type variety and Intro/Outro buffs, NTE elements create tangible field effects that can freeze time or generate massive energy.

The six elements are categorized into a wheel:
*   **Lakshana (Gold):** Focused on Order and slowing enemies.
*   **Cosmos (Silver):** The primary element for light-based damage and time manipulation.
*   **Anima (Green):** Deals with life-force and status-spreading.
*   **Incantation (Red):** High-force physical and elemental impact.
*   **Chaos (Purple):** Dark-matter manipulation and debuffs.
*   **Psyche (Azure):** Mind-based attacks and crowd control.

**Table: Esper Duo and Trio Reactions**

| Reaction Name | Element Combination | Combat Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Blossom** | Cosmos + Anima | Creates tracking projectiles that deal continuous damage. |
| **Remora** | Cosmos + Lakshana | Marks enemies, slowing their attack animations by 40%. |
| **Stain** | Lakshana + Psyche | Increases the target's vulnerability to all Esper damage. |
| **Discord** | Incantation + Chaos + Psyche | Triggers a massive explosion that ignores 30% of enemy DEF. |
| **Charge** | Lakshana + Cosmos + Anima | Rapidly regenerates Ultimate Energy for the active character. |

Understanding the "Cycle Rate" stat on your gear (known as Arcs) is vital. High Cycle Rate allows characters to fill their Esper Meter faster, enabling more frequent swaps to trigger these reactions.

### 3. Economy: City Tycoon and Fons Management
City Tycoon is the management layer of NTE. It allows you to buy properties, manage businesses, and customize vehicles. The most critical milestone for a beginner is **City Tycoon Level 16**. 

At Level 16, you unlock a passive that increases both the cost and the rewards of City Stamina activities by 100%. This is a massive time-saver for busy players, allowing you to burn through your weekly stamina in half the time while receiving double the Fons and upgrade materials. Do not spend your Fons on luxury car liveries or high-end furniture until you have reached this level and secured your primary combat team's Arcs.

### 4. Traversal: Vehicle Summoning and Customization
While [Wuthering Waves](https://ntecodex.com/guides/wuthering-waves-2-6-vs-2-5-meta-gear-guide-20260706) uses wall-running and grappling hooks, NTE relies on vehicles. However, not all vehicles are created equal. Early-game cars like the C2000 are restricted to paved roads; you cannot summon them if you are standing on grass or a sidewalk.

The **Novis ST-X 950 motorcycle** is the "meta" choice for beginners. Priced at 250,000 Fons at the Regalia Dealership in the New Herland district, it is the first vehicle you can acquire that supports off-road summoning. This allows you to cut across parks and alleys to reach objectives faster.

### 5. Gacha Strategy: Scarborough Fair and Version 1.0 Banners
The NTE gacha, called "Scarborough Fair," is presented as a board game. You spend "Solid Dice" (Limited) or "Fabricated Dice" (Standard) to move a piece called a Chuppa across the board. 

A major advantage for F2P players is the **lack of a 50/50 mechanic**. On a limited banner, such as Nanally's "The Ichi-daime" board, if you land on an S-class character tile or reach the 90-pull hard pity, you are guaranteed to get the featured character. 

**Version 1.0 Banner Schedule:**
*   **Phase 1 (April 29 – May 13, 2026):** Features **Nanally** (Anima/Plasma). She is a top-tier DPS who uses electromagnetic force to walk on walls and ceilings, making her invaluable for Hethereau's vertical exploration.
*   **Phase 2 (May 13 – June 3, 2026):** Features **Hotori** (Cosmos/Solid). Hotori is a time-manipulation specialist capable of freezing enemies in place, offering the highest crowd control in the current meta.

### NTE vs Wuthering Waves: Key Differences for New Players
Players coming from [Wuthering Waves](https://ntecodex.com/guides/wuthering-waves-2-6-vs-2-5-meta-gear-guide-20260706) will notice that NTE is less about "farming the world" for Echoes and more about "managing the city" for Fons. In Wuthering Waves, your power growth is tied to the Data Bank and defeating Tacet Discords. In NTE, while you still hunt Anomalies, your power is heavily influenced by the Arcs (weapons) and Cartridges (gear) you purchase or craft through the City Tycoon system.

The gacha predictability is also a significant departure. [Wuthering Waves](https://ntecodex.com/guides/wuthering-waves-2-6-vs-2-5-meta-gear-guide-20260706) players are used to the 80-pull pity with a 50/50 chance. NTE’s 90-pull guarantee with no 50/50 makes it much easier to plan your pulls months in advance. However, NTE requires more currency management, as you must balance spending Annulith on pulls versus saving Fons for city-based upgrades.

### Recommended Character Build Guides
For those who pulled Nanally in Phase 1, her best-in-slot weapon is the **Ready-Ready Arc**, available in the Arc Research Program. This weapon synergizes with her Plasma-type damage by increasing electromagnetic adhesion duration. For those waiting for Hotori, prioritize saving for her signature S-class Arc, as her time-stop duration scales directly with the weapon's "Momentum" stat.

**Checklist: Daily and Weekly Tasks for Optimal Account Growth**

*   **Daily:** Complete 4 Anomaly Commissions for Hunter EXP and Annulith.
*   **Daily:** Visit the Witch’s House southwest of Hankaku Street to turn in any birds found; reach Level 3 to unlock bird visibility on the mini-map.
*   **Daily:** Check the Eibon Antique Shop for the "Daily Special" Arc upgrade material.
*   **Weekly:** Use all City Stamina on street races or "Heather Hobbies" to maximize Fons generation.
*   **Weekly:** Clear the "Secret Fair" segment of the Scarborough Fair board if you have accumulated enough Warp Pieces.
