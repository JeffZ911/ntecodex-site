Building any character in NTE involves two primary systems: Arcs and Cartridges.

**Cartridges (Gear Sets):**

*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Usually a flat 15% boost to an attribute or stat like ATK, HP, or Defense.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** Provides a mechanical change, such as "Increases damage by 20% for 10 seconds after switching characters" or "Restores 5 energy when a critical hit occurs."
