Pyrois is the first-ever S-Rank Ether Attack Agent that HoYoverse has distributed entirely for free. Narratively, the character is revealed to be the Etheric Other combat form of the protagonist (Phaethon), allowing players to finally take the Proxy directly into the Hollows for combat.

### Unlocking Pyrois: A Sleepwalker's Confession Walkthrough
The [ZZZ 3](https://ntecodex.com/guides/best-gear-for-zzz-3-0-hexerei-secret-rite-rewards).0 update marks the beginning of Season 3, shifting the focus away from New Eridu's streets to the floating classified zone of [Roscaelifer](https://zzz.hoyoverse.com/en-us/news/124434). To begin the unlock process for Pyrois, you must first clear the Eridan Sunset epilogue from the previous patch.

Once in Roscaelifer, the story follows the "A Sleepwalker's Confession" arc. The pivotal moment occurs during the [Celestial Nexus](https://zzz.hoyoverse.com/en-us/news/124434) commission, where the Proxy twins (Belle and Wise) manifest their Etheric forms. Upon completing the third part of this chapter, Pyrois is automatically added to your Agent list. 

Furthermore, players can unlock Mindscapes for Pyrois by collecting Pixel Fairy Tales throughout the new map, and his signature W-Engine, [Sol Exuvia](https://zzz.hoyoverse.com/en-us/news/124434), is earned by leveling up the new [Interboo](https://zzz.hoyoverse.com/en-us/news/124434) account system.

### Neverness to Everness: How to Get Chiz for Free
In NTE, the character Chiz is the most dependable manager at the Pink Paws Bank and a formidable Cosmos-attribute DPS. Unlike Pyrois, who is a pure story unlock, Chiz is the ultimate reward for engaging with the City Tycoon simulation mechanics.

To reach Level 18 and claim her, you must optimize your weekly City Stamina. This stamina resets every Monday and should be spent on high-yield activities like the Pink Paws Heist, which unlocks at Level 10. By Level 18, you will have accumulated the necessary 5.3 million Fons and exploration footprints to secure her.

### Understanding NTE's 'The Fair' Gacha vs. ZZZ's Pity System
NTE's summoning system, known as The Fair, utilizes a unique Dice Board visualizer. Instead of simple card flips, you spend dice to move across tiles. 

Key mechanics of The Fair include:
*   **No 50/50:** Unlike ZZZ, NTE has completely removed the 50/50 on limited banners. If you hit an S-Rank on a limited board, it is guaranteed to be the featured character.
*   **Base Rate:** The S-Rank rate is 1.87%, which is higher than ZZZ's 0.6% base rate.
*   **Soft Pity:** At 70 pulls, the board undergoes a "Modification," jumping the S-Rank rate to 19.59%.
*   **Hard Pity:** A guaranteed S-Rank occurs at 90 pulls.

### Farming Resources for Future NTE Banners

1. **City Tycoon Hunter Exchange:** Converting excess Fons into Annulith.
2. **Beyond the Rails:** Clearing high-level floors in the Fracture Circle.
3. **Daily Commissions:** Standard supernatural investigation tasks in Hethereau.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I get Pyrois in ZZZ for free?
You can get the S-Rank Agent Pyrois for free by progressing through the [ZZZ 3](https://ntecodex.com/faq).0 main story. Specifically, you must complete the chapter titled "A Sleepwalker's Confession" in the Roscaelifer region. This character is a permanent story unlock and does not require Polychromes or banner pulls.

### Is the NTE S-Rank Selector permanent?
The S-Class Selection promotion on the Standard Board in Neverness to Everness is a one-time bonus for new accounts. It activates after you have performed 50 pulls on the "Strange Encounters" board using Fabricated Dice. Once used, the selector option disappears from the banner interface.

### What is the best character to pick from the NTE S-Rank Selector?
Most players and guides recommend choosing Sakiri from the S-Rank Selector. She is a universal buffer with powerful crowd-control abilities that can group enemies together, making her invaluable for both early-game exploration and late-game challenge modes like the Fracture Circle.

### Does Neverness to Everness have a 50/50 system?
No, Neverness to Everness has removed the 50/50 mechanic for its limited character banners. The "The Fair" gacha system guarantees that the first S-Rank you pull on a limited board will be the featured character, provided you reach the 90-pull hard pity or land on the character's tile on the dice board.
