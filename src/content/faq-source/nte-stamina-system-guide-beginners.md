# Neverness to Everness Stamina System Guide for Beginners

Managing your resources in Neverness to Everness requires a clear understanding of how the game splits its energy systems. Unlike many other urban RPGs that rely on a single energy pool, NTE utilizes three distinct systems to govern combat, economic growth, and world traversal. This guide breaks down how to balance Character Pixels, City Stamina, and Action Stamina to maximize your daily gains.

When starting your journey in Hethereau, you will likely encounter several questions regarding resource management:

*   **What are the different types of stamina in Neverness to Everness (NTE)?** The game uses Character Pixels for combat rewards, City Stamina for the Tycoon system, and Action Stamina for physical movement like sprinting and climbing.
*   **How do Character Pixels and City Stamina differ in use and regeneration?** Character Pixels regenerate every few minutes and are spent daily for character growth, while City Stamina is a weekly resource dedicated to the city's economy and life-simulation features.
*   **What is the best way for beginners to manage their daily energy to avoid wasting resources?** Beginners should prioritize spending Character Pixels to avoid hitting the 240 cap and focus on weekly Tycoon activities to unlock high-tier rewards like the S-Rank character Chiz.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/nte-stamina-system-guide-beginners/inline-2.webp)


Neverness to Everness features three distinct stamina systems: Character Pixels (Combat), City Stamina (Tycoon), and Action Stamina (Movement).

*   **Character Pixels** serve as the primary resource for Anomaly Zones and boss rewards. These regenerate at a rate of 1 point every 6 minutes, totaling 240 Pixels over a full 24-hour period.
*   **City Stamina** is exclusive to "Hethereau Hobbies," such as street racing, fishing, and deliveries. Unlike the daily combat stamina, City Stamina resets weekly every Monday at 4:00 AM UTC+8.
*   **Action Stamina** is a classic regenerating bar visible during exploration. It is consumed by sprinting, dodging, and climbing, and it recharges naturally without the need for items or currency.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/nte-stamina-system-guide-beginners/inline-3.webp)


The dual-economy design of Neverness to Everness ensures that your combat progress and your "city life" progress do not compete for the same energy. However, this means you must track two separate timers to ensure you aren't leaving rewards on the table.

### Character Pixels: Combat and Grinding

Character Pixels are the lifeblood of your team's power. Almost every upgrade material—from Hunter XP and Beetle Coins to Arc ascension items—requires the consumption of Pixels to claim.

The primary location for spending this resource is the Anomaly Zone. These zones, such as Houdini's Magic Stage, offer various combat challenges. A standard run typically costs 40 Character Pixels. For players looking to save time, the "Double Reward" option allows you to spend 80 Pixels in a single run to receive twice the loot.

**Anomaly Zone Costs and Hunter XP Yields**

| Activity Type | Pixel Cost | Hunter XP Yield | Primary Rewards |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Standard Anomaly Run | 40 Pixels | 200 XP | Character/Arc XP, Beetle Coins |
| Double Reward Run | 80 Pixels | 400 XP | Doubled Materials |
| World Boss Reward | [Information unavailable] | [Information unavailable] | High-tier Ascension Mats |

Because the cap is strictly set at 240, you must spend your Pixels at least once every 24 hours. If you remain at the cap, you lose approximately 10 Pixels every hour that could have gone toward your Hunter Level.

### City Stamina: The Tycoon Economy

City Stamina is tied directly to the Hethereau Tycoon system. This system represents your influence and wealth within the city. Spending City Stamina on "Hethereau Hobbies" earns you Fons (the city's primary currency) and Tycoon Level experience.

Unlike Character Pixels, you do not need to log in every few hours to manage City Stamina. Since it resets weekly, you can choose to spend your entire pool in one long session or spread it across the week. However, reaching specific Tycoon Level milestones is critical for account progression. For example, reaching Tycoon Level 18 is the requirement to unlock Chiz, a powerful S-Rank Cosmos character, for free.

**City Tycoon Activities and Stamina Costs**

*   **Street Racing:** Costs approximately 30–40 stamina per race depending on performance.
*   **Fishing:** Costs 1 stamina per fish caught (converts to 1,000 Fons baseline).
*   **Deliveries:** Variable costs based on distance and package type.
*   **Cafe Management:** undisclosed.

At Tycoon Level 16, a major efficiency boost is unlocked: the Stamina Cost Multiplier. This allows you to spend double the City Stamina on activities for double the rewards, significantly cutting down the time required to clear your weekly pool.

### Action Stamina: Movement and Combat Utility

Action Stamina is the only system that does not require a currency to refill. It governs your physical limits within the open world. In combat, managing this bar is vital for survival, as dodging and certain air attacks (like Hathor's) will drain it rapidly.

A unique interaction exists with the character [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte). Her skill, "Colucci Howling Technique" (which triggers the "Ichi-daime's Authority" state), allows her to ignore gravity and run up walls or across ceilings. While most characters drain stamina while dashing upward, Nanally’s specialized kit allows her to recover Action Stamina while climbing during her skill duration, making her the premier choice for vertical exploration in Hethereau's skyscraper-dense environment.

### Stamina Recovery and Management Tips

If you find yourself short on energy, the game provides several ways to replenish your stocks, though some are more cost-effective than others.

**1. Using Recovery Items**
*   **De-noise Solution:** Restores 60 Character Pixels. These are often rewarded through Daily Tasks or limited-time events. It is best to save these for when you unlock a new Appraisal Level, allowing you to farm higher-tier materials immediately.
*   **Refresh Recharge:** Restores 100 City Stamina. These are rarer and should be used specifically to push for Tycoon Level milestones before the weekly reset.

**2. Premium Refills (Annulith)**
You can spend Annulith, the game's premium currency, to refresh your stamina. However, there are diminishing returns and daily limits.

**Annulith Refresh Costs for Character Pixels**

| Refresh Number | Annulith Cost | Pixels Restored |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1st - 3rd | 60 Annulith | 60 Pixels |
| 4th - 6th | 120 Annulith | 120 Pixels |

For City Stamina, players can refresh up to 10 times per week using Annulith, though the cost increases significantly with each subsequent purchase.

**3. Progression Gating**
Your stamina efficiency is tied to your Hunter Level and Appraisal Level. Higher Appraisal Levels increase the difficulty of Anomaly Zones but also significantly improve the quality of the drops. Spending all your De-noise Solutions at Appraisal Level 1 is generally considered a mistake; wait until Appraisal Level 3 or higher to get the best value for your items.

Beyond the basics of the stamina bars, several other systems interact with your daily routine in Neverness to Everness.

### NTE Tycoon Leveling Guide

To increase your Tycoon Level quickly, focus on high-yield Fons activities. While fishing is the fastest way to burn City Stamina (1 point per catch), deliveries and racing often provide better Fons-to-stamina ratios. Reaching Level 18 should be your primary goal during the first two weeks of play to secure Chiz. Once you reach Level 16, ensure you toggle the "Double Consumption" mode to save time.

### Character Progression and Beetle Coins

Character Pixels are also used to farm Beetle Coins, the secondary currency used for leveling up Arcs and character abilities. While Fons (from City Stamina) are used for city-based purchases like cars and furniture, Beetle Coins are strictly for combat power. If you find your characters hitting a wall in Anomaly Zones, check if you have neglected your Beetle Coin farming.

### Hethereau Hobbies Rewards List

Participating in hobbies doesn't just give Fons; it also unlocks unique collectibles.
*   **Racing:** Unlocks car modification parts and paint jobs.
*   **Fishing:** Provides ingredients for cafe recipes and rare aquarium fish.
*   **Heists:** undisclosed.

By balancing your daily Character Pixel spend with your weekly City Stamina goals, you can ensure a steady stream of both combat power and urban wealth.
