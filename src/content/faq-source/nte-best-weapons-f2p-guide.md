# NTE Best Weapons for F2P Players: Top Arcs Guide

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/nte-best-weapons-f2p-guide/inline-1.webp)


What are the best weapons for free-to-play (F2P) players in Neverness to Everness (NTE)? Since the game’s global launch on April 29, 2026, many players have looked for ways to optimize their damage without relying on the gacha system. Finding high-rarity Arcs (weapons) without spending Tri-Keys or Lunaria is a major priority for progression. This guide focuses on which farmable S-rank Arcs are considered best-in-slot (BiS) for specific characters and how to acquire them through in-game activities like Anomaly Commissions and world boss hunts.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/nte-best-weapons-f2p-guide/inline-2.webp)


Neverness to Everness is exceptionally F2P-friendly, offering over 10 farmable S-rank Arcs through Anomaly Commissions, world bosses, and side activities. The best overall F2P weapon is **Day Off** (Solid), which is obtained via a hidden multi-stage quest involving the NPC Eclipse and is ideal for the Main Character (Zero). Other top-tier F2P choices include **Youthful Fantasy** (Liquid) for Daffodil, **Raging Flames** (Plasma) for [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) or Hathor, and **Contemplative Cat** (Gas) for Chiz. For those using Jiuyuan, the S-rank Arc **Reality Refuge** is the premier farmable choice. 

F2P players should prioritize "Mixing" (refining) these farmable Arcs to Rank 5. In NTE, a max-refined farmable S-rank often outperforms a Rank 1 gacha signature weapon due to the massive scaling of passive effects at higher Mixing levels.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/nte-best-weapons-f2p-guide/inline-3.webp)


### Understanding the Arc System: Types and Rarities

In Neverness to Everness, weapons are known as Arcs. These are categorized into five distinct types: **Solid, Plasma, Gas, Liquid, and Synthesis**. Each character in NTE is hard-locked to one of these types. For example, the Main Character (Zero) can only equip Solid-type Arcs, while a character like Chiz requires Gas-type Arcs. This makes it crucial to farm the correct weapon for your main DPS rather than just any S-rank you find.

Arcs come in three primary rarities: S-Rank (Gold), A-Rank (Purple), and B-Rank (Blue). While S-rank Arcs have the highest base ATK and strongest passives, they are harder to refine. However, because NTE allows you to farm specific S-rank Arcs repeatedly through the Anomaly Hunt system, F2P players can eventually reach Rank 5 (max refinement) on these powerful weapons.

### Top Farmable S-Rank Arcs for F2P Players

#### Day Off (Solid)
This is widely considered the best F2P weapon for Zero. It offers a massive 30% ATK increase at Rank 1, which scales up to 50% at Rank 5. Its unique "Arc: Eclipse" effect creates a 40-second zone where every enemy defeated restores 6 to 10 Ultimate Energy. 

To obtain **Day Off**, you must complete a hidden quest involving the NPC **Eclipse**. Start by visiting the Bureau of Anomaly Control (BAC) in Area M10 between 8:00 AM and 12:00 PM in sunny weather. You will then need to find her in several unmapped locations, including the roof of the photo studio on Hankaku Street, a rooftop in Illusion Town, and finally at the top of the Tikal Bridge supports.

#### Raging Flames (Plasma)
Ideal for Lakshana-element characters like Hathor and Nanally, this Arc provides a high base ATK of 666 at level 80 and a 24% CRIT DMG substat. Its passive increases Lakshana damage by 15% and boosts Redirect Skill and Ultimate damage by 10% for 10 seconds after an Ultimate is cast.

You can farm this by defeating the **Headless Rider** world boss. This boss spawns at **Coastal Highway Z42** (south of Hankaku Street) specifically at **8:10 PM** in-game time. You must be in a vehicle to trigger the chase event that leads to the boss portal.

#### Youthful Fantasy (Liquid)
This is the signature-tier F2P Arc for Daffodil. It focuses on increasing Break Intensity and Chaos damage. It is earned by completing **The Book of Post-Adolescence** Anomaly Commission in the New Herland District.

#### Contemplative Cat (Gas)
Best for Chiz, this Arc increases Cosmos damage by 2.5% to 4.5% for every 100,000 Fons (the standard currency) the wearer holds, stacking up to 10 times. It is a reward for reaching **City Tycoon Level 21**, rewarding players who engage with the city management and business simulation aspects of the game.

Before you have the Hunter Level or gear to take on S-rank world bosses like the Headless Rider, you will need A-rank "bridge" Arcs. These are easier to obtain from the Arc Shop or common Anomaly Commissions.

### How to Refine (Mix) Arcs Without Gacha

The "Mixing" system is the NTE equivalent of refinement or resonance. To increase an Arc's Rank, you must consume a duplicate of that same Arc. 
*   **Rank 1 to Rank 5:** Each rank significantly boosts the percentages on the weapon's passive effect.
*   **Farming Duplicates:** For world boss Arcs like **Raging Flames**, you can challenge the boss once per week (or use specific reset items) to get more copies. 
*   **Arc Shop:** The Mall features an Arc Shop where you can exchange **Mhm Coins** (earned from Nacupeda’s Pool activities) or **Tri-Keys** for specific S-rank and A-rank duplicates.

### Where to Find Anomaly Commissions and Hidden Quests

Anomaly Commissions are the primary way to unlock the "Anomaly Hunt" versions of bosses for farming. 
*   **Headless Rider (Ride Assault):** Located at Coastal Highway Z42. Triggered by driving a car at 8:10 PM.
*   **Swallowtail (Let's Go See a Movie):** Located inside the Floe Cinema in New Herland. Enter the projection room at the back to find the hidden portal. This boss drops the **Reality Refuge** Arc.
*   **Morphix (Pawn Moonlight):** This is a weekly boss located in the **Green Wall Slope** area of Bridge Crossings. You must complete the "Pawn Moonlight" spinoff quest to unlock it. Morphix drops materials for the **Good Boy's Grand Adventure** Arc.

### How do I get more Tri-Keys for the Arc Research Program?
Tri-Keys are earned through weekly missions, clearing the higher floors of the Wertheimer Index (the endgame tower), and as rewards for increasing your Hunter Level. You can also purchase a limited number of Tri-Keys each month using Beetle Coins in the standard exchange shop.

### What are the best F2P team compositions in NTE?
A highly effective F2P team consists of **Zero** (Main DPS/Solid), **Nanally** (Sub-DPS/Plasma), **Aurelia** (Support/Psyche), and **Edgar** (Healer). This team covers multiple elements (Cosmos, Lakshana, Psyche) and utilizes the free S-rank Arcs like Day Off and Raging Flames effectively.

### Is it worth pulling for signature weapons if I have the farmable S-ranks?
Generally, for F2P players, it is not worth pulling on the weapon banner. A Rank 5 farmable S-rank Arc like **Day Off** provides utility and damage that is very close to a Rank 1 gacha signature weapon. It is better to use your Lunaria to secure new characters, as the farmable Arcs in NTE are significantly more powerful than those in other contemporary open-world gachas.

### Where can I find the weekly boss Morphix for Good Boy's Grand Adventure?
Morphix is located at the **Green Wall Slope** in the Bridge Crossings district. To unlock the fight, you must complete the **Moonlight Pawnbroker** special episode. Once defeated, you can fast-travel directly to the boss arena from your map to farm the Good Boy Stamps and Arc shards required for the **Good Boy's Grand Adventure** Gas-type weapon.
