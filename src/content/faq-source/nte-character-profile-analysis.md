## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/nte-character-profile-analysis/inline-2.webp)


Neverness to Everness features a launch roster of over 16 playable Espers categorized by Rarity (S-Class and A-Class), Attribute (Esper Type), and Arc Compatibility. The combat system is built around the "Esper Cycle," a mechanic where switching between adjacent Attributes on the elemental wheel—such as Cosmos to Anima or Incantation to Chaos—triggers powerful reactions like Blossom or Scorch.

The current 1.0 meta is dominated by S-Class units who provide unrivaled synergy or raw power. **Nanally** stands as the premier Main DPS due to her Anima-based follow-up attacks and "Underboss" summon. **Hotori** serves as the ultimate Sub-DPS and buffer, utilizing time-manipulation to replicate ally skills. **[Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide)** is the meta's most essential support, offering massive crowd control through her "Eating Mode" and team-wide attack buffs.

For free-to-play players, the game is remarkably generous. **Zero** (the protagonist), **Mint** (obtained via story), and **Chiz** (unlocked through the City Tycoon system) are all highly viable. Chiz, in particular, is a top-tier Cosmos DPS whose power scales with your in-game funds, making her a "high-investment" character that rewards dedicated progression rather than just gacha luck.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/nte-character-profile-analysis/inline-3.webp)


Understanding a character's profile in NTE goes beyond their rarity. To maximize effectiveness in endgame content like "Beyond the Rails," you must align their Attribute, Arc type, and team synergy.

### The Attribute & Arc Compatibility System

There are six unique Esper Attributes in NTE: **Cosmos, Anima, Incantation, Chaos, Psyche, and Lakshana**. These Attributes form a circular "Esper Wheel." Triggering a reaction requires switching to a character with an adjacent Attribute when the Esper Meter is full.

Equally important is the **Arc Type**, which functions as the character's weapon category. The five confirmed Arc types are **Solid, Liquid, Gas, Plasma, and Bose**. A sixth type, Synthesis, is [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-15]. Matching the correct Arc to an Esper is critical for unlocking stat bonuses provided by the Console system.

### The Esper Cycle Reaction Map

| Reaction | Attributes Involved | Primary Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Blossom** | Cosmos + Anima | Deals AoE damage and creates Vita Buds |
| **Hexed** | Anima + Incantation | Increases damage taken by enemies |
| **Scorch** | Incantation + Chaos | Massive Fire-based Damage over Time (DoT) |
| **Nova** | Chaos + Psyche | Reduces enemy defense |
| **Stain** | Psyche + Lakshana | Increases damage from Psyche/Lakshana |
| **Remora** | Lakshana + Cosmos | Reduces enemy movement and attack speed |
| **Charge** | Trio: Lakshana, Cosmos, Anima | Rapidly restores Ultimate Energy |
| **Discord** | Trio: Psyche, Chaos, Incantation | Reduces enemy Break cap percentage |

### Nanally: The Anima Powerhouse and 1.0 Meta Queen

Nanally is the first limited S-Class character (featured on "The Ichi-daime" banner) and currently holds the title of the game's strongest Main DPS. Her profile is defined by her Anima Attribute and Plasma Arc compatibility.

*   **Core Mechanic:** Her Skill, "Ichi-daime’s Authority," allows her to ignore gravity and walk on walls or ceilings for 12 seconds. In this state, she gains a 30% Crit DMG buff.
*   **Ultimate:** "Colucci Ultimate Technique" summons an **Underboss**. This summon coordinates its strikes with Nanally’s basic attacks, effectively doubling her hit count and dealing massive follow-up damage.
*   **Meta Standing:** She is the benchmark for damage. When paired with characters who trigger the **Blossom** reaction, her damage ceiling becomes unrivaled.

### Hotori: Time Manipulation and Skill Replication

Hotori is an S-Class Cosmos character using a Solid Arc. She is a technical Sub-DPS whose kit centers on the "Non-Closed Timepiece" energy gauge.

*   **Skill Replication:** Her Skill, "Present Replay," records the Support or Redirect skills used by her allies over a 5-second window.
*   **Time Stop:** Her Ultimate, "World’s Tide," stops time for 10 seconds. During this period, the stage timer in modes like "Beyond the Rails" freezes, and Hotori replays all recorded ally skills simultaneously while dealing high-frequency Cosmos damage with her katana.
*   **Utility:** Hotori is essential for "Burst Windows," allowing a team to dump an entire rotation's worth of damage into a frozen target.

### Sakiri: Unrivaled Crowd Control and Team Buffs

As an Incantation-type character wielding a Gas Arc, Sakiri is the backbone of most high-tier team compositions. She belongs to the Eibon Antique Shop faction and uses her oversized hammer, Kiroumaru, to dictate the flow of battle.

*   **Eating Mode:** By holding her basic attack, Sakiri mounts Kiroumaru. She can move freely while the hammer pulls in and devours weaker enemies. Devoured enemies are removed from the field for a 180-second "digestion" period.
*   **Ultimate Buff:** Her Ultimate, "Feast of Gluttony," pins enemies to the ground with gravity and grants all other team members an ATK buff equal to 30% of Sakiri’s Base ATK.
*   **Meta Role:** She is the premier "grouper" and offensive buffer, making her indispensable for AoE-heavy challenges.

### Chiz: The High-Investment Prodigy from Pink Paws Bank

Chiz is a unique Cosmos S-Class character who can be obtained for free by reaching City Tycoon Level 10. Unlike other characters, her damage is tied to the "Grain" system and your current Fons (in-game currency).

*   **Scaling:** As you progress through the Pink Paws Bank heist missions and increase your wealth, Chiz’s damage multipliers increase.
*   **Quick-Swap Potential:** At higher Awakening levels (A3+), Chiz becomes a potent quick-swap DPS who can adapt to almost any team structure. She is the gold standard for free-to-play investment.

### Zero & The Free-to-Play Roster Analysis

The protagonist, **Zero**, is an exceptionally versatile unit. As an "Appraiser," Zero has the unique ability to trigger an "Instant Cycle," allowing for faster reaction setups than most other characters.

*   **Mint:** An A-Class Anima DPS given during the story. While her raw numbers are lower than Nanally's, her "Mint Tornado" skill provides excellent mobility and respectable AoE damage for early-to-mid game progression.
*   **Adler:** An A-Class Chaos unit focused on survival. His ability to generate shields makes him a safe pick for players struggling with the difficulty spikes in the "Fracture Circle" of Beyond the Rails.

### Character Progression: Consoles, Cartridges, and Awakenings

A character's profile is further refined through the **Console System**. This involves equipping **Cartridges** (which provide main stats like Anima DMG% or Crit Rate) and **Modules** (Type I, II, and III). 

*   **Modules:** Equipping high-tier Type III Modules often unlocks set bonuses, such as increased Attribute damage or energy regeneration.
*   **Awakenings (A1-A6):** Obtaining duplicate copies of a character unlocks powerful passive upgrades. For example, Nanally’s A1 ("Gang Formation") grants her Ultimate Energy for every follow-up attack, significantly increasing her Ultimate uptime. Jiuyuan’s specific A1 effect and potential healing capabilities are [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-15].

### Meta Analysis: Building Teams Around the Esper Cycle

The most effective teams in the current meta focus on "Trio Reactions." 

1.  **The Blossom-Charge Team:** Consists of Nanally (Main DPS), Zero (Sub-DPS), and Jiuyuan (Support). This team focuses on the Anima/Cosmos/Lakshana side of the wheel to trigger both Blossom for damage and Charge for infinite Ultimate uptime.
2.  **The Discord-Scorch Team:** Typically features Sakiri (Support), Baicang (Main DPS), and Daffodill (Breaker). This team utilizes the Incantation/Chaos/Psyche trio to trigger Discord, which shreds enemy Break gauges, followed by Scorch for massive DoT.
