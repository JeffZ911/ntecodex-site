# Best Disks for NTE Tank Characters: Top Cartridge Builds (2026)

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/best-disks-nte-tank-characters-guide/inline-1.webp)


In Neverness to Everness (NTE), optimizing a "tank" character involves navigating a multi-layered equipment system. Players often ask: What are the best 'Disks' (officially known as Arcs) for tank and survival characters? Furthermore, which equipment sets (Cartridges) maximize the effective HP and damage redirection for S-Class characters like Fadia and A-Class options like Adler? 

To answer these questions, one must understand the Console and Module systems. These Tetris-style mechanics are the primary way to enhance defensive stats like HP%, DEF%, and Cycle Intensity. Finding the right balance between these components determines whether your tank simply absorbs damage or provides the utility necessary to keep the rest of the team alive.

## Direct Answer

In Neverness to Everness, "Disks" technically refers to the Arc system, which functions as the character's weapon. The actual gear sets that provide passive bonuses are called Cartridges. For pure survival and damage redirection, the **Tiny Big Adventure** Cartridge set is the undisputed best-in-slot (BiS) for Fadia, offering a total HP boost of up to 50% when fully stacked. For utility-focused tanks like Adler, the **Speedy Hedgehog** set is the superior choice, as it provides team-wide ATK buffs and essential Charge Efficiency.

Regarding Arcs (Weapon Disks), the top choices for tanks are **Eternal Waltz** (Fadia’s signature Synthesis-type Arc) and **Your Happiness is Priceless** (a Solid-type Arc that provides massive DEF and random team support effects).

### Understanding the Console and Cartridge System

The equipment system in Neverness to Everness is split into two main parts: the **Cartridge** and the **Modules**. The Cartridge defines the set bonus (2-piece and 4-piece effects), while the Modules provide the raw stats and determine which character-specific passives are activated.

Unlike traditional RPGs where you simply equip a piece of gear, NTE uses a "Console" grid. Each Cartridge requires a specific configuration of Modules to activate its 4-piece Legendary effect. These Modules come in different shapes and types (Type I, II, III, and IV). To get the most out of a tank, you must fit these shapes into the Console grid like a puzzle while prioritizing the correct Module types to trigger the character's unique Console buffs.

### Fadia: The Ultimate Redirection Tank Build

Fadia is currently the premier S-Class tank in the NTE 1.0 meta due to her "All Eyes on Me" passive. This ability allows her to redirect a significant portion of damage taken by her teammates to herself. Because this redirected damage is calculated based on her own durability, stacking HP is the most efficient way to build her.

**Best Cartridge: Tiny Big Adventure**
*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Increases HP by 10%.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** Whenever the wearer takes damage, their Max HP increases by 4% for 10 seconds, stacking up to 10 times. Casting an Ultimate instantly grants all 10 stacks.
*   **Why it works:** At max stacks, Fadia gains a 40% HP increase on top of the 10% base set bonus. Since her damage redirection and "Lilith form" healing scale directly with her Max HP, this set makes her nearly unkillable in high-level Anomaly Raids.

**Best Arc: Eternal Waltz**
As a Synthesis-type Arc, Eternal Waltz provides a massive 41.25% HP substat at Level 80. Its passive, "Arachne," further increases Max HP by 20% and boosts Mental DMG by 10% after using an Ultimate. This synergy ensures Fadia not only survives but also contributes meaningful Psyche damage during her burst windows.

### Adler: Shielding and Utility Support Build

Adler serves as a high-utility A-Class tank who excels at providing shields and enabling faster Ultimate rotations for the team. While he lacks the raw damage redirection of Fadia, his ability to mitigate incoming hits through shields makes him a staple for F2P players and specific Incantation-based team comps.

**Best Cartridge: Speedy Hedgehog**
*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Increases Charge Efficiency by 12%.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** After the wearer casts an Ultimate, all allies receive a 15% ATK buff for 20 seconds.
*   **Why it works:** Adler’s primary goal is to maintain shield uptime. The Charge Efficiency from the 2-piece set ensures he can cast his Ultimate more frequently, while the 4-piece ATK buff provides the offensive utility that tanks need to stay relevant in time-limited challenges.

**Best Arc: Your Happiness is Priceless**
This Solid-type Arc is a defensive powerhouse. It provides a 38.5% DEF substat and increases HP by up to 24% at base refinement. The "Nacupeda" effect is particularly strong for Adler, as it grants a random beneficial effect upon casting an Ultimate: either a massive team heal, a shield based on the wearer's Max HP, or a targeted heal for the lowest-HP ally.

### Optimal Module Placement and Tetris Strategies

To maximize a tank's potential, you must pay attention to the **Module Types** required by their Console. 

1.  **Type I and II Modules:** These are typically smaller (2-cell) shapes. For Fadia, equipping Type II Modules is essential because her Console provides specific HP scaling bonuses for every Type II Module placed.
2.  **Type III Modules:** These are 3-cell shapes (often "L" or "I" shapes). Adler benefits from Type III Modules, which often align with Incantation-specific passives that increase Shield Strength or Cycle Intensity.
3.  **The Tetris Strategy:** When farming for Modules in the **Rewind** system, look for "Gold" (Legendary) Modules that have HP% or DEF% as their main stat. Because you have limited space on the Console grid, prioritize Modules that satisfy the Cartridge's shape requirements first, then fill the remaining gaps with the specific Type (I-IV) that matches your character's passive buff requirements.

### Endgame Stat Breakpoints for Tanks

For players tackling Level 60+ Anomaly Raids, hitting certain stat thresholds is more important than simply having the right sets.

*   **HP Threshold (Fadia):** Aim for at least 45,000 HP. With a fully stacked *Tiny Big Adventure* set and *Eternal Waltz*, reaching 55,000+ HP is possible, which allows her to comfortably redirect 75% of incoming team damage.
*   **DEF Threshold (Adler):** Aim for 2,500+ DEF. Since Adler's shields scale with his defensive stats, this ensures the shield doesn't break after a single boss hit.

### Best Teams for NTE Tanks

Tanks in Neverness to Everness are rarely just "meat shields"; they are essential for triggering elemental reactions.

*   **Fadia's Nova Team:** Fadia (Psyche) pairs exceptionally well with Chaos characters like **Daffodil**. Together, they trigger the **Nova** reaction, which deals massive area-of-effect damage and applies the "Stain" debuff to enemies. Adding a support like Haniel further increases the team's Mental DMG output.
*   **Adler's Incantation Team:** Adler (Incantation) is best paired with other Incantation or Anima characters to focus on high-frequency shield generation and Break efficiency.

### How to Farm High-Tier Cartridges

High-tier gear is primarily obtained through the **Rewind** system. 
1.  **Gold Modules:** These are the rarest and provide the highest stat rolls. You can "gamble" for specific shapes using Bronze Coins earned from clearing urban anomalies.
2.  **Legendary Cartridges:** These drop from high-level Guardian Bosses. For *Tiny Big Adventure*, you must farm the **Never-ending Arachne** boss. For *Speedy Hedgehog*, look for the **Justice Executioner** world boss or specific Anomaly Vision stages.

### NTE Character Tier List: Survival Category

In the current 1.0 meta, the survival category is dominated by a few key names. While DPS characters often get the spotlight, a well-built tank is the difference between clearing a floor of the Tower or failing due to a one-shot mechanic.

*   **S-Tier:** Fadia (Unrivaled damage redirection and HP scaling).
*   **A-Tier:** Adler (Excellent F2P shielding and utility).
