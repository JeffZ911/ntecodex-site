Many players find themselves confused when they arrive in Sumeru City for the first time, only to find that Katheryne does not immediately offer them local daily commissions. If you are looking to earn Sumeru-specific rewards and achievements, you likely have several questions regarding the transition.

### Common Challenges for New Sumeru Explorers

The most common issue is the "missing" quest. Unlike Mondstadt and Liyue, where commissions are essentially unlocked by default as you level up your Adventure Rank, Sumeru follows the model established in Inazuma. It requires the completion of a specific chapter of the main story before the "unlock quest" becomes available. Furthermore, the unlock is not instantaneous; there is a time-gate tied to the real-world server reset that often catches players off guard.

Another point of confusion involves the Adventurer's Handbook. Even after unlocking the region, your commissions may still appear in previous nations if your settings are not manually updated. This leads to players trekking through Sumeru while still being forced to fight Hilichurls in Windrise for their daily Primogems.

### The Importance of Daily Commissions for Sumeru Progression

Unlocking Sumeru daily commissions is vital for several reasons:
*   **Reputation Experience:** Completing commissions in Sumeru is one of the primary ways to gain reputation points with the Corps of Thirty, which eventually unlocks the Dendroculus Resonance Stone and the Sumeru wings.
*   **Exclusive Achievements:** Sumeru features a vast array of hidden achievements tied to specific commission RNG, such as the "Garcia’s Paean" questline and the "Scholarly in Sumeru" quiz.
*   **Local Materials:** Many Sumeru commissions reward you with regional specialties or enemy drops (like Fungal Spores or Chaos Bolts) that are necessary for ascending Sumeru characters and weapons.
*   **Lore and World-Building:** These daily tasks often provide deeper insight into the lives of the Akademiya scholars and the Eremite mercenaries.

## Direct Answer

To unlock Sumeru daily commissions, you must follow these four essential steps:

1.  **Complete Archon Quest Chapter III: Act II:** Finish the quest titled "The Morn a Thousand Roses Brings," specifically the final sub-quest "Denouement."
2.  **Wait for the Daily Server Reset:** The unlock quest will not appear immediately after finishing the Archon Quest. You must wait until 4:00 AM server time the following day.
3.  **Complete "Adventure Takes Courage!":** Once the server resets, a new World Quest will appear in your log. Visit Katheryne at the Adventurer's Guild in Sumeru City to complete this brief dialogue quest.
4.  **Update the Adventurer's Handbook:** Open your Handbook (F1 on PC), navigate to the "Commissions" tab, and change the "Preferred Region" to Sumeru.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/how-to-unlock-sumeru-daily-commissions/inline-3.webp)


The process of unlocking commissions in the Dendro nation is more narrative-heavy than in earlier regions. Because Sumeru's story involves a complex temporal loop, the game gates your daily activities until the "Samsara" of the Sabzeruz Festival is officially resolved.

### Prerequisite: Completing the Sabzeruz Festival Arc

Before you can think about daily tasks, you must resolve the crisis in Sumeru City. This occurs during **Archon Quest Chapter III: Act II, "The Morn a Thousand Roses Brings."**

This act is famous for its "Groundhog Day" mechanic where the Traveler and Paimon are stuck in a repeating cycle of the Sabzeruz Festival. You must progress through all the stages of the festival, including the "Continuity of the Sabzeruz Festival" and the various "Days" of the loop. The critical trigger point is the sub-quest **"Denouement."** In this phase, you finally break the cycle, help Nilou perform her dance, and confront the Akademiya's influence over the Akasha Terminal.

Once the "Denouement" quest is marked as complete in your quest log, you have satisfied the narrative requirement. However, don't be alarmed if the Adventurer's Guild doesn't have any new options for you right away.

### The 'Adventure Takes Courage!' World Quest Walkthrough

The quest **"Adventure Takes Courage!"** is the official gateway to Sumeru dailies. It is a very short World Quest that involves no combat.

1.  **The Wait:** After finishing Chapter III: Act II, finish any other active dialogue. Check your quest log. If "Adventure Takes Courage!" isn't there, you must wait for the server reset at 4:00 AM (based on your specific server: America, Europe, or Asia).
2.  **Locating Katheryne:** Head to Sumeru City. The Adventurer's Guild is located on the northeast side of the city, situated on "Treasure Street." The nearest Teleport Waypoint is the one located near the Akademiya; from there, you can glide down to the lower level where the guild stall is located.
3.  **The Interaction:** Speak with Katheryne. She will acknowledge your arrival in Sumeru and explain that the Adventurer's Guild is now ready to process commissions for you in this region. 
4.  **Completion:** The quest completes immediately after the dialogue ends, rewarding you with some Adventurer's Experience and Mora.

### Configuring the Adventurer's Handbook

Simply completing the quest does not guarantee that tomorrow's commissions will be in Sumeru. Genshin Impact defaults to your previous selection (or "Random") unless you manually intervene.

*   **Step 1:** Open the **Adventurer's Handbook**. On PC, the shortcut is F1. On PlayStation, it is found in the Shortcut Wheel. On Mobile, tap the book icon in the top right.
*   **Step 2:** Click on the **Commissions** tab on the left-hand sidebar.
*   **Step 3:** Look for the dropdown menu labeled **"Choose preferred region in which to do commissions."**
*   **Step 4:** Select **Sumeru** from the list.

Once selected, the handbook will display a message stating: "Preferred region set. Changes will take effect on the next daily reset." This means if you change the setting at 10:00 AM, today's commissions will stay where they are, but tomorrow's will be in Sumeru.

Unlocking commissions is just the start of your daily routine in Sumeru. There are several interconnected systems that you should activate simultaneously to maximize your efficiency.

### Unlocking Sumeru Reputation and Bounties

Shortly after unlocking commissions, you should seek out the Sumeru Reputation system. This is managed by an NPC named **Effendi**, located in the Citadel of Regzar in Sumeru City. 

To unlock this, you must complete the World Quest **"Corps of Thirty Recruitment."** This quest typically becomes available after you have made significant progress in the Archon Quest (Chapter III: Act II). Once unlocked, you can perform weekly Bounties and Requests in Sumeru to earn Reputation XP. Reaching higher levels provides the "Dendroculus Resonance Stone" instructions, the "Dendro Treasure Compass" diagram, and the "Wings of the Forest" glider.

### Best Sumeru Commissions for Hidden Achievements

Sumeru is famous—or perhaps infamous—for its complex commission-based achievements. Many of these require you to complete a specific daily task multiple times or make specific choices.

| Commission Name | Achievement Name | Quick Requirement |

| **Questions and Answers** | Scholarly in Sumeru | Answer all 6 of Ziryab's questions correctly across multiple instances. |
| **Garcia's Paean: Echo of Someone** | Principia Arithmetica | Complete this specific part of Garcia's chain three times. |

The **Garcia's Paean** chain is particularly notable. It actually begins as a World Quest in Liyue at Wangshu Inn. You must complete the Liyue portion before the daily commissions will ever appear in Sumeru. If you haven't seen Garcia in Sumeru yet, head back to Liyue and check if his introductory quest is still sitting in your log.

### Transitioning to Fontaine Daily Commissions

Once you have exhausted the achievements in Sumeru, you will eventually want to move on to the Hydro nation. Unlocking Fontaine commissions follows a nearly identical pattern. You must complete **Archon Quest Chapter IV: Act II "As Light Rain Falls Without Reason."** 

Following the completion of that Act, a World Quest titled **"Another Horizon's Adventure"** will appear after the daily reset. Just like with Sumeru, you will visit the local Katheryne (in the Court of Fontaine) to register. The mechanics remain consistent: finish the story, wait for the reset, talk to Katheryne, and update your Adventurer's Handbook. 

By mastering the Sumeru unlock process, you are preparing yourself for the standard operating procedure used in every major region update in Genshin Impact. Keeping your Handbook updated ensures you are always working toward the latest achievements and reputation rewards available in Teyvat.
