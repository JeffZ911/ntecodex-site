# How to Unlock Daily Missions in Neverness to Everness (NTE)

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/how-to-unlock-daily-missions-nte/inline-1.webp)


Many new Appraisers in Hethereau are looking for the specific point where daily missions become available. While the game introduces its urban open-world mechanics early on, the structured daily loop is not accessible from the very first login. Understanding when and how to unlock these tasks is vital for long-term progression.

Daily missions are the primary source of free Annulith (the premium gacha currency) and Account EXP (Hunter EXP). Without these, players will find themselves hitting a wall when trying to increase their Appraisal Level or pull for high-tier Espers. The game does not immediately grant access to the daily loop during the initial tutorial phase, requiring players to focus on the main narrative first.

### Why are Daily Missions Important in NTE?
In Neverness to Everness, daily missions serve as the backbone of your resource economy. They provide a steady stream of Fons for character upgrades and Beetle Coins for shop purchases. More importantly, they offer a guaranteed daily income of Annulith, which is required to purchase Solid Dice for limited character banners or Tri-Keys for the Arc Research Program. By completing these tasks, you also earn Hunter EXP, which increases your account level and unlocks higher Appraisal Levels, effectively raising the level cap for your characters and Arcs.

### When Do Most Players Reach the Unlock Point?
Most players will reach the unlock point for daily missions within the first 2 to 4 hours of gameplay, depending on how much they focus on the main story versus exploring the side streets of Hethereau. If you prioritize the "Prologue" quests, you can have your daily routine established by the end of your first play session.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/how-to-unlock-daily-missions-nte/inline-2.webp)


Daily missions in Neverness to Everness are unlocked by completing the main story quest: **Prologue - Part 2**. Specifically, you must finish the "Film Anomaly" questline within the Prologue to trigger the tutorial for the Exploration Guide. Once this quest is resolved and you have established your office operations, the game will prompt you to open the Exploration Guide for the first time.

Once unlocked, daily tasks are found under the **Participation Tasks** tab in the Exploration Guide menu. You can access this menu at any time by using the in-game phone interface or the **F4** shortcut on PC. The menu icon for Participation Tasks is a stylized "70" logo located on the left-hand side of the Exploration Guide screen.

### Quick Summary: Daily Mission Unlock & Details

| Requirement | Details |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Unlock Quest** | Prologue - Part 2 (Film Anomaly) |
| **Menu Location** | Exploration Guide > Participation Tasks (F4) |
| **Reset Time** | 5:00 AM Server Time |
| **Primary Rewards** | Annulith, Hunter EXP, Fons, Beetle Coins |
| **Daily Point Cap** | 100 Participation Points |

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/how-to-unlock-daily-missions-nte/inline-3.webp)


The "Prologue - Part 2" requirement involves navigating the early supernatural incidents in Hethereau and establishing your office as a functional hub for anomaly investigation. The "Film Anomaly" quest acts as the final gate; it introduces the core combat and investigation mechanics you will use throughout the game. After the cutscenes conclude and you return to the city streets, the Exploration Guide tutorial will automatically trigger.

### Step-by-Step: Navigating the Exploration Guide
1. **Open the Phone:** Press Escape (PC) or tap the phone icon (Mobile).
2. **Select Exploration Guide:** Look for the compass-like icon or press F4.
3. **Locate Participation Tasks:** On the left sidebar, click the icon that looks like a stylized number 70.
4. **Track Objectives:** The screen will list several tasks. You can click "Go" on any task to be directed to the relevant map location or menu.
5. **Claim Rewards:** As you complete tasks, the bar at the bottom fills. You must manually click the five reward chests to claim your items.

### Understanding Participation Points and Rewards
Participation Tasks function on a point system where you need 100 Participation Points daily to claim all five reward chests. Unlike some RPGs where you must finish every single listed task, NTE allows you to pick and choose the activities you prefer to reach the 100-point cap.

**Participation Task Point Values and EXP Rewards**

| Task Name | Points Awarded | Key Rewards |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Daily Login | 20 Points | Hunter EXP, Fons |
| Earn 10,000 Fons | 20 Points | Hunter EXP, Beetle Coins |
| Consume Character Pixels | 30 Points | Hunter EXP, Annulith |
| Complete 1 City Incident | 30 Points | Hunter EXP, Annulith |
| Complete 1 Hethereau Hobby | 20 Points | Hunter EXP, Fons |

### The 'Hidden' Daily Routine: Nacupeda and The Witch
Beyond the Exploration Guide, there are "Hidden Dailies" that are not explicitly listed in the Participation menu but are essential for long-term power. 

**Nacupeda's Pool** is a critical stop located in the park west of Davidia Avenue in the Bridge Crossing (Illusion Town) district. Look for a yellow crown icon on your map. Once there, interact with the fountain and select **"Make a Sincere Wish."** This grants you a "Mhm! Coin" and a random daily buff. Collecting 100 Mhm! Coins allows you to purchase the S-Class Arc **"Your Happiness is Priceless"** from the exchange shop. This is currently one of the only ways to obtain a guaranteed S-Rank weapon through purely free-to-play daily activity.

**The Witch's House** provides daily fortunes that can reveal undisclosed or provide temporary combat buffs. Increasing your Divination Level to 10 at the Witch's House by turning in Oracle Stones is a long-term goal that improves the quality of these daily fortunes.

### Managing Character Pixels and City Energy
Neverness to Everness uses a dual-energy system. **Character Pixels** (Stamina) regenerate at a rate of 1 every 6 minutes, totaling 240 per day. These are primarily spent in Anomaly Zones to farm character EXP, Arc materials, and Cartridges. Spending these Pixels is the fastest way to hit your daily Participation Point cap. A standard Anomaly Zone run costs 40 Pixels, while the double-reward option costs 80.

**City Stamina** (or City Energy) is a separate resource that resets weekly rather than daily. This is used for Hethereau Hobbies like fishing, taxi services, and delivery routes. While not a daily regeneration resource, you should spend a portion of it each day to ensure you don't hit the weekly cap and waste potential Fons income. The baseline conversion is 1 City Stamina to 1,000 Fons.

### Fons Farming: The Chiz Daily Cap
Fons is the lifeblood of the City Tycoon system. While you can earn Fons through many activities, certain characters like **Chiz** have specific mechanics tied to daily income. Chiz has a daily Fons ability cap of 500,000; once you reach this limit through her specific talents or associated missions, you will not earn additional Fons from those specific sources until the next reset.

### Circle Bounty and Seasonal Progression
The **Circle Bounty** (Battle Pass) daily tasks are separate from Participation Tasks but usually overlap significantly. Completing your dailies will typically grant you 1,000 Circle Bounty EXP per day. The weekly cap for the Circle Bounty is 12,000 EXP, so staying consistent with your daily Participation Tasks ensures you hit this cap without needing to grind heavily on the weekends.

### Checklist: The 'Perfect' Daily Routine (Priority 1-5)
1. **Login & Events:** Claim the daily login bonus from the Event tab in the phone menu.
2. **Nacupeda’s Wish:** Visit Nacupeda's Pool in Bridge Crossing for your Mhm! Coin.
3. **Participation Tasks:** Spend Character Pixels in Anomaly Zones and complete one City Incident to hit the 100-point cap.
4. **City Tycoon:** Restock and collect profits from the **Cafe by Origen**.
5. **Affection:** Give 10 gifts to your favorite Espers to increase Bond levels.

## Related

![Related](/img/how-to-unlock-daily-missions-nte/inline-4.webp)


Once you have mastered the daily loop, you will begin to encounter weekly systems that offer even higher-tier rewards. These are typically gated by higher Appraisal Levels and require more time to complete than the standard daily tasks.

### Weekly Reset Checklist
The weekly reset occurs every Monday at 5:00 AM. Key activities to track include:
*   **undisclosed:** High-tier weekly boss challenges that provide materials for upgrading Esper abilities.
*   **Realm of Greed:** Located in your first apartment, this involves the Mammon furniture mechanic to earn furniture-related currency and Fons.
*   **Special Delivery:** Accessed via the **Old Mailbox** furniture in your second apartment, these weekly commissions provide unique upgrade resources.
*   **City Stamina Drain:** Ensure all weekly City Stamina is converted into Fons via Hobbies before the Monday reset.

### City Tycoon and Hethereau Hobbies
The City Tycoon level is independent of your Hunter Level and is raised by spending City Stamina and managing businesses like the Cafe by Origen. As you increase this level, you unlock more Hethereau Hobbies, such as the **Pink Paw Heist**. The Pink Paw Heist is a bi-weekly activity with a massive 1,000,000 Fons cap, making it the most efficient way to fund high-end vehicle purchases and real estate upgrades.

### Character Affection and Bond Systems
Character Affection gifts are limited to 10 per day. These are crucial for unlocking **Bond Encounters** (marked by character icons on the map) and **Movie Dates**. Increasing Affinity Level to the maximum (Level 10) provides unique character cosmetics, specialized Arcs, and permanent stat boosts for that Esper.

### Comparison: Daily vs. Weekly Task Rewards

| Reward Type | Daily Source | Weekly Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Annulith** | Participation Tasks (60+) | — |
| **Fons** | Cafe, Login, 10k Task | Pink Paw Heist, City Stamina |
| **Hunter EXP** | Participation Tasks | Weekly Bosses, Main Story |
| **Beetle Coins** | Participation Tasks | — |
| **Upgrade Mats** | Anomaly Zones (Pixels) | Realm of Greed, Old Mailbox |
