## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/how-to-unlock-daily-quests-nte/inline-3.webp)


The path to unlocking your daily routine is integrated into the narrative of your arrival in Hesperia. You cannot skip these steps, as the game uses the Prologue to introduce the "Anomaly" concept and the basic functions of your urban headquarters.

### Completing Prologue - Part 2: Key Milestones

To reach the unlock point, you must follow the main story markers through the first two parts of the Prologue. This journey begins with your arrival at the Hesperia Central Station and your first encounter with the supernatural phenomena plaguing the city.

1.  **Hesperia Arrival:** Follow the initial navigation markers to meet your primary contacts.
2.  **Tutorial Combat:** You will be forced into several scripted encounters to learn about elemental reactions and character switching.
3.  **The First Containment:** You must complete the "Anomaly Containment" tutorial, which teaches you how to seal minor rifts in the city.
4.  **Meeting the Team:** The Prologue - Part 2 concludes after a major dialogue sequence at the central agency hub. Once the quest "A New Normal" (or its localized equivalent) is marked as complete, a notification will appear on the right side of your screen announcing that "Participation Tasks" are now available.

### Understanding Participation Tasks vs. Circle Bounty

It is vital to distinguish between these two systems, as they overlap significantly.
*   **Participation Tasks:** These are your standard "dailies." You earn Activity Points by completing specific actions. Reaching 100 Activity Points grants the maximum daily reward.
*   **Circle Bounty:** This is the seasonal progression system. While Participation Tasks provide the points needed to level up the Circle Bounty, the Bounty itself contains weekly and seasonal challenges that are much more time-consuming than the daily tasks.

### Daily Login and Event Rewards

Beyond the active tasks, NTE features a standard login reward system. However, unlike some other titles, these are not always automatically claimed. You must manually open the "Events" tab to claim your daily check-in bonus. During special launch windows or patches, such as the "The Ichi-daime" banner featuring [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte), additional login calendars may be active simultaneously.

### Spending Character Pixels (Stamina) Efficiently

One of the core daily tasks involves consuming "Character Pixels." This is the NTE stamina system used to claim rewards from Anomaly containment zones, boss fights, and material domains. To optimize your daily routine, you should never let your Character Pixels sit at the maximum cap. 

A common strategy for new players is to spend Pixels on "Fons Farming" if they are unsure which character to upgrade. Fons is the universal currency used for everything from buying coffee to upgrading high-end Arcs (weapons).

### Nacupeda's Pool and The Witch's House

Once you have more freedom in Hesperia, two specific locations become essential for your daily checklist.

*   **Nacupeda's Pool:** Located in the northern district, this area allows you to perform a daily "wish." This is a free interaction that provides a random assortment of low-to-mid-tier crafting materials.
*   **The Witch's House:** Tucked away in an alleyway in the East District, visiting the Witch allows you to receive a daily fortune. These fortunes often grant a temporary buff to your movement speed in the city or a slight increase in the drop rate of specific open-world materials for 30 minutes.

### City Tycoon: Cafe by Origen and Passive Income

NTE differentiates itself with the City Tycoon system. Your daily routine should include a visit to the **Cafe by Origen**. This is your primary source of passive income.
*   **Collecting Profits:** The Cafe accumulates Fons over time based on your City Tycoon level.
*   **Management:** You can assign characters to work shifts at the Cafe. Characters with specific "Urban Traits" will increase the efficiency of Fons generation or reduce the "City Energy" consumption rate.

### Hethereau Hobbies: City Delivery and Fishing

The "Hethereau Hobbies" system provides additional daily activities that are more relaxed than combat. 
*   **City Delivery:** Use your vehicle to deliver packages across Hesperia. This is a quick way to earn Participation Points if you are short on time.
*   **Fishing:** Certain spots along the Hesperia waterfront offer daily fishing quotas. These fish can be exchanged for rare cooking ingredients or sold for Fons.

As you increase your City Tycoon level through daily management, you unlock more complex hobbies. At Level 8, players gain access to the **Pink Paws Heist** minigame. ### Stamina Management: Character Pixels vs. City Energy

It is important to manage both of your "stamina" bars. Character Pixels are for combat and character growth, while **City Energy** is used for Tycoon activities like deliveries and shop management. City Energy regenerates separately from Character Pixels, meaning you can still participate in the City Tycoon economy even after you have exhausted your combat stamina for the day. Effective players balance both to ensure they are progressing their account level and their economic standing in Hesperia simultaneously.
