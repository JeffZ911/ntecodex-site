### Is Neverness to Everness Multiplayer?

Yes, Neverness to Everness features a comprehensive multiplayer system. It is primarily a cooperative experience where up to four players can join a single world instance. In this shared space, you can explore the city, participate in combat encounters, and engage in various urban activities like racing and high-stakes heists. While the main narrative remains a solo experience, the majority of the open-world content and specialized "Anomaly" challenges are designed with co-op in mind.

## Direct Answer

To unlock multiplayer in NTE, you must reach **Hunter Level 8**. This is your primary account level that tracks your overall progression and combat experience. In addition to the level requirement, you must also complete the main story chapter titled **"Prologue II: Good Business, Better Treasure"**. This chapter serves as the final part of the game's introductory sequence, where you are introduced to the core business management and urban exploration mechanics.

Once these conditions are met, the Multiplayer app will automatically appear on the second page of your in-game phone. You can access this by hitting the "ESC" key on PC or tapping the phone icon on mobile devices, then swiping to the right.

The process typically takes **25 to 35 minutes** of active gameplay from the start of a new save. If you focus strictly on the "Yellow" main story markers and skip optional dialogue, you can reach this milestone even faster.

Unlocking the multiplayer feature is just the beginning of the social experience in Neverness to Everness. The system is integrated into the "Smartphone" interface, which acts as the hub for all your menus, including characters, inventory, and settings.

### How to Level Up Fast to Hunter Level 8

To reach Hunter Level 8 as quickly as possible, focus on the following activities:

1.  **Main Story Quests:** These provide the largest chunks of Hunter EXP. Completing the initial tutorial combat against the first Anomaly and meeting [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) will jump-start your leveling.
2.  **Anomaly Containment:** While exploring Hethereau during the prologue, you will encounter minor "glitches" or Anomalies in the environment. Defeating these enemies provides substantial EXP and upgrade materials.
3.  **City Exploration:** Discovering new fast-travel points and landmarks in Hethereau grants small amounts of EXP that add up quickly during the first thirty minutes of play.
4.  **Commissions:** Check the "Ichi-daime" notice board early on. While most high-tier commissions are locked, the introductory tasks are simple and yield the necessary EXP to push you from Level 6 to Level 8.

### How to Invite and Join Friends in NTE

Once the Multiplayer app is unlocked, you can connect with other players using the **UID (User Identification) system**. Your UID is a unique 9-digit code found in the top-left corner of the main menu or on your profile card within the phone interface.

**Step-by-Step Invite Process:**
1.  Open your in-game phone and swipe to the second page.
2.  Select the **Multiplayer** icon.
3.  To add a friend, go to the **Friends** tab and enter their 9-digit UID.
4.  Once they accept the request, their name will appear in your Friends List.
5.  Click on their profile and select "Request to Join World."
6.  Alternatively, you can click "Invite to World" to have them join your current session.

The player who sends the invitation or whose world is being joined acts as the "Host." The Host's world state determines which world bosses are available and what time of day it is in Hethereau.

### What Can You Do Together in Hethereau?

Multiplayer in NTE is more than just fighting enemies. The urban setting allows for a variety of lifestyle activities:

*   **Open-World Exploration:** You can drive through the streets together. NTE features a "Vehicle Sharing" mechanic where a passenger can ride in the host's car, or players can bring their own customized vehicles into the host's instance.
*   **Anomaly Combat:** High-level Anomalies found in the city outskirts or within "Distortion Zones" are significantly easier to manage with a full party of four.
*   **Lifestyle Minigames:** Several social activities are available in the city's entertainment districts.

### Multiplayer Restrictions and Limitations

While the multiplayer system is expansive, there are several key limitations to keep in mind:

*   **Main Story Missions:** You cannot progress through the main "Yellow" story quests while in a multiplayer session. If you or the host starts a story-critical cutscene, the session may be temporarily suspended or guests may be placed in a spectator mode.
*   **World Progress:** Only the Host's world progress is saved regarding chest collection and environmental puzzles. If you find a rare chest in a friend's world, you will receive the rewards, but it will not count as "collected" in your own world.
*   **Level Scaling:** The game utilizes a "Dynamic Scaling" system. If a Level 50 player joins a Level 10 player's world, the higher-level player's stats will be adjusted downward to ensure the content remains challenging and the lower-level player isn't completely overshadowed.
*   **Server Lock:** You can only play with players on the same server. If you are on the NA (North America) server and your friend is on the EU (Europe) server, you will not be able to find their UID or join their world.

### Cross-Platform and Cross-Play Support

Neverness to Everness was built with cross-play as a core feature. Players on **PC, PlayStation 5, and Mobile (iOS/Android)** can all play together in the same session without any restrictions. As long as you are all in the same region, the platform you choose does not affect your ability to join friends. 

The game also supports **cross-save**. By linking your account to a Hotta ID or a third-party login, you can start a session on your PC at home and continue the same co-op session on your mobile device while on the go.

However, there are competitive elements in the **Street Racing** mode and certain leaderboard-based challenges in the **Pink Paws Heist** events. These allow players to compete for the fastest times or highest scores without engaging in direct combat with one another.
