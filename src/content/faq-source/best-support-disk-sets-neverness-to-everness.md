Optimizing your team’s support units is the fastest way to clear high-tier Anomaly Zones in Hethereau City. While your main DPS often takes the spotlight, the right combination of Cartridges and Modules on your buffers determines how often you can trigger the Esper Cycle and keep your team alive.

# What Are the Best Disk Sets for Support in Neverness to Everness?

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/best-support-disk-sets-neverness-to-everness/inline-1.webp)


In Neverness to Everness (NTE), "Disk Sets" are officially referred to as **Cartridges**. These are the primary gear pieces that determine your character's set bonuses, while **Modules** are the Tetris-like pieces you slot into the **Console** to activate those bonuses. Understanding the distinction is vital: Cartridges provide the set effect, but you must fit the correct number and type of Modules into the Console grid to actually trigger the Epic (2-piece) and Legendary (4-piece) buffs.

Support characters in the current meta generally fall into two categories: **Buffers** (who increase team damage) and **Sustainers** (who provide shields, damage redirection, or healing). Characters like [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) and Haniel are pure buffers, while Fadia, Adler, and Edgar focus on keeping the team standing. 

Choosing the right Cartridge set is critical because support units often have high energy costs for their Ultimates. Without sets that provide **Charge Efficiency** or team-wide ATK buffs, your DPS will struggle to maintain high numbers during the 20-second burst windows that define endgame combat.

The primary support characters currently in the roster include:
*   **Sakiri:** S-Rank Incantation Buffer/CC.
*   **Haniel:** A-Rank Psyche Buffer.
*   **Edgar:** A-Rank Cosmos Healer.
*   **Fadia:** S-Rank Psyche Sub-DPS/Tank.
*   **Adler:** A-Rank Incantation Shielder.
*   **Mint:** A-Rank Anima Support/Sub-DPS.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/best-support-disk-sets-neverness-to-everness/inline-2.webp)


The **Speedy Hedgehog** set is the undisputed best-in-slot (BiS) Cartridge for almost all offensive buffers in Neverness to Everness. It provides the essential Charge Efficiency needed to cycle Ultimates and grants a massive team-wide ATK boost. For characters who rely on their Skills rather than their Ultimates to buff, **Shadow Creed** is the premier secondary choice. Sustainers like Fadia thrive with **Tiny Big Adventure**, while pure healers like Edgar should prioritize **Thea's Night Tavern** for the Healing Bonus.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/best-support-disk-sets-neverness-to-everness/inline-3.webp)


The gearing system in Neverness to Everness is a "build puzzle" where the Console acts as the board. A Cartridge set does not grant its bonuses simply by being equipped; you must fill the character's Console with Modules to meet the "Required Pieces" threshold. For most Legendary (4-piece) effects, you need to slot in specific Module Types (II, III, or IV) that match the Cartridge's requirements.

### The Speedy Hedgehog Meta

Speedy Hedgehog is the gold standard for support units in Version 1.0. Its 2-piece **Epic Bonus** provides +12% Charge Efficiency, which is the most valuable stat for supports who need to keep their Ultimate uptime at 100%. The 4-piece **Legendary Bonus** increases all allies' ATK by 15% for 20 seconds after the wearer casts their Ultimate.

This set is particularly powerful because it matches the typical 20-second cooldown of most support Ultimates. For example, Sakiri’s Ultimate, *Feast of Gluttony*, has a 20-second cooldown. By running Speedy Hedgehog, Sakiri can effectively maintain a permanent 15% ATK buff for the entire team, provided she has enough Charge Efficiency to fill her energy bar within that window. It is important to note that this effect does not stack; if you run two characters with Speedy Hedgehog, only one buff will apply.

### Alternative Sets for Niche Supports

While Speedy Hedgehog dominates, other sets provide specialized value:

*   **Shadow Creed:** This set is ideal for "quick-swap" supports or offensive buffers like Haniel if you find yourself using her Skill (*Paranormal Cannon*) more frequently than her Ultimate. The 4-piece bonus grants the wearer a 25% ATK increase for 20 seconds after casting a Skill. Since many support buffs scale off the support's own ATK stat, this self-buff indirectly boosts the numbers they provide to the DPS.
*   **Tiny Big Adventure:** This is the definitive set for Fadia. Fadia's utility comes from her ability to redirect 50% of incoming team damage to herself. Tiny Big Adventure increases Max HP by 4% every time the wearer takes damage, stacking up to 10 times. Since Fadia is constantly taking "redirected" hits, she reaches the 40% Max HP cap almost instantly, which in turn increases her survivability and the damage of her Psyche-scaling attacks.
*   **Kingdom's Guard:** Specifically designed for Adler and other shield-dependent units. The 2-piece bonus provides +15% DEF, and the 4-piece bonus increases Shield effects by 20%. Since Adler’s *Shield of Blessing* scales directly off his DEF, this set provides a double-layered buff to team survivability.

### Optimizing the Console: Module Placement Tips

Activating your Cartridge is only half the battle. To maximize your support, you must pay attention to the **Module Type** bonuses. Each character has a "Preferred Module Type" that grants a flat stat bonus (often Max HP or CRIT Rate) if you use that specific shape.

For example, Sakiri typically prefers Type III Modules. Speedy Hedgehog requires four Modules to activate its Legendary effect. If you use four Type III Modules to activate Speedy Hedgehog on Sakiri, you not only get the ATK buff but also trigger Sakiri’s internal Console bonus.

### Substats: Cycle Intensity vs. Charge Efficiency

For supports, the priority of substats differs significantly from DPS units. You should prioritize the following hierarchy:

1.  **Charge Efficiency:** The most critical stat. It ensures your Ultimate is ready as soon as the cooldown ends.
2.  **Cycle Intensity:** This stat increases the effectiveness of elemental reactions triggered during the **Esper Cycle**. For supports like Haniel, high Cycle Intensity makes the *Nova* or *Stain* reactions deal significantly more damage when the DPS swaps in.
3.  **HP% / DEF%:** Essential for sustainers like Fadia or Adler to ensure they don't get staggered or defeated while redirecting damage.
4.  **Flat ATK / ATK%:** Only a priority if the character’s buffs scale off their own ATK (like Haniel or Sakiri).

### Farming Strategy

To acquire these sets, you must spend **Character Pixels** (stamina) in **Anomaly Zones**. The most efficient way to farm for support gear is the **Rabbit Hole [Anomaly Zone](https://ntecodex.com/faq)** located in the **New Herland District**. 

The Rabbit Hole is unique because it drops both Cartridges and **Carrota Coins**. Carrota Coins are used in the **Rewind** system, which is the only way to obtain high-rarity (S-Rank) Modules. When farming, prioritize completing the 4-piece set first, regardless of the substats. The 15% team-wide ATK buff from a "bad" Speedy Hedgehog set is still more valuable than a perfect set of random Modules that provides no team utility.
