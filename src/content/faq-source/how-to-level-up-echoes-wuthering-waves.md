## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/how-to-level-up-echoes-wuthering-waves/inline-1.webp)


Echoes serve as the primary gear system for every Resonator in [Wuthering Waves](https://ntecodex.com/guides/tactical-simulacrum-event-rewards-wuthering-waves-gear-guide), providing essential stat boosts and unique active skills. However, unlike traditional RPG gear where you simply equip a higher-rarity item, Echoes require a multi-stage investment process to reach their full potential. Players often find themselves asking: how do you increase the level of Echoes efficiently?

The process involves two distinct but interconnected systems: Leveling (which increases the Echo's base stats) and Tuning (which reveals hidden sub-stats). To level up an Echo, you must consume specific materials known as Sealed Tubes along with Shell Credits. Tuning, on the other hand, requires Tuner materials of the corresponding rarity.

Understanding this system is vital because the resource cost for Echo enhancement scales exponentially. While it is easy to reach level 10, the jump from level 20 to level 25 is significantly more expensive. New players often face the challenge of running out of Sealed Tubes or Shell Credits because they invested too heavily in low-rarity (Green or Blue) Echoes that are eventually replaced by Gold (5-star) variants.

### The Importance of Echo Leveling for Combat Power
Leveling an Echo primarily boosts its Main Stats, such as Attack, HP, or Elemental Damage Bonus. For a main DPS character like Jiyan or Jinhsi, a level 25 Gold Echo can provide over 30% Elemental Damage or a substantial Crit Rate boost. Without leveled Echoes, your Resonators will lack the raw numbers needed to survive high-level Tacet Discord (TD) encounters. Furthermore, leveling is the prerequisite for Tuning; you cannot unlock the powerful sub-stats—like Crit Damage, Energy Regen, or Resonance Skill DMG Bonus—without first hitting specific level milestones.

### Common Challenges for New Players
The most frequent hurdle is the "Tuner Bottleneck." While Sealed Tubes (EXP) can be found through exploration and various shops, Premium Tuners are a finite resource heavily tied to Tacet Field farming and events. Another challenge is the Data Bank level. Since your Data Bank determines the maximum rarity of Echoes you can absorb, many players waste resources leveling Purple Echoes to level 20, only to unlock Gold Echoes shortly after. Learning when to stop investing in "transitional" gear is a hallmark of an optimized account.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/how-to-level-up-echoes-wuthering-waves/inline-2.webp)


To level up Echoes in [Wuthering Waves](https://ntecodex.com/guides/tactical-simulacrum-event-rewards-wuthering-waves-gear-guide), you must navigate to the **Resonator** screen, select the **Echo** tab, and click on a specific Echo to open the **Upgrade** menu. You will then use **Sealed Tubes** to provide EXP and **Shell Credits** to pay for the enhancement.

The maximum level an Echo can reach is strictly determined by its rarity (Rank). As you increase your Data Bank level, you will unlock higher-rarity Echoes that can reach higher level caps and possess more sub-stat slots.

| Echo Rarity (Rank) | Color | Max Level | Max Sub-stat Slots |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Rank 2 | Green | 10 | 0 (Cannot be Tuned) |
| Rank 3 | Blue | 15 | 3 |
| Rank 4 | Purple | 20 | 4 |
| Rank 5 | Gold | 25 | 5 |

### Quick Step-by-Step Upgrade Guide
1. **Open the Echo Menu:** From the main terminal, select "Resonators" and then the "Echo" sub-menu.
2. **Select an Echo:** Choose the Echo you wish to improve. Note that it is usually more efficient to level Echoes that are already part of a matching Sonata Effect set.
3. **Apply Sealed Tubes:** Click "Upgrade" and select your Sealed Tubes. You can use the "Auto-Select" feature, but be careful not to over-invest EXP beyond the current level cap.
4. **Tune at Milestones:** Every 5 levels (5, 10, 15, 20, 25), you must switch to the "Tuning" tab to unlock a sub-stat using Tuners.

### Summary of Required Materials
*   **Sealed Tubes:** These come in four tiers (Basic, Medium, Advanced, Premium) and are the primary source of Echo EXP.
*   **Tuners:** Required to reveal sub-stats. You must use the Tuner rarity that matches the Echo (e.g., Premium Tuners for Gold Echoes).
*   **Shell Credits:** The standard currency used for almost all upgrades in Solaris-3. Since Version 1.1, the cost of Echo leveling has been reduced by approximately 75%, making the process much more accessible.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/how-to-level-up-echoes-wuthering-waves/inline-3.webp)


The Echo system is designed to be a long-term progression mechanic. While the basics are straightforward, optimizing your resources requires understanding the EXP values, the Tuning RNG, and the most efficient farming routes.

### Understanding Sealed Tube Tiers
Sealed Tubes are the "food" for your Echoes. They are categorized by the amount of EXP they provide. It is important to note that while lower-tier tubes are common, you will need thousands of them to max out a full team of Resonators.

*   **Basic Sealed Tube (Green):** 500 EXP
*   **Medium Sealed Tube (Blue):** 1,000 EXP
*   **Advanced Sealed Tube (Purple):** 2,000 EXP
*   **Premium Sealed Tube (Gold):** 5,000 EXP

The cost of leveling increases significantly at higher levels. For example, taking a Gold Echo from level 0 to level 10 requires roughly 8 to 9 Advanced (Purple) Sealed Tubes. However, taking that same Echo from level 20 to level 25 requires 13 Premium (Gold) Sealed Tubes. This exponential growth is why many veteran players suggest the "+10 Checkpoint" strategy: level an Echo to 10, tune it twice, and if the sub-stats are poor, stop there to save resources.

### The Tuning System and Sub-stat RNG
Tuning is where the real power of an Echo is unlocked. Every 5 levels, a new sub-stat slot becomes available. To "Tune" the Echo, you spend 10 Tuners of the appropriate rarity. 

The sub-stats are pulled from a pool that includes:
*   Crit Rate % and Crit Damage %
*   ATK % and Flat ATK
*   Energy Regen %
*   Resonance Skill / Basic / Heavy / Liberation DMG Bonus %
*   HP %, Flat HP, DEF %, and Flat DEF

Because you cannot "re-roll" a single sub-stat, if you roll undesirable stats like Flat DEF or HP on a DPS Echo, you may want to consider discarding that Echo and starting over with a fresh base.

### Data Bank Influence on Echo Potential
Your Data Bank level is the single most important factor in your Echo journey. 
*   **Level 1-7:** You primarily find Green and Blue Echoes. Do not invest heavily here.
*   **Level 8-14:** Purple Echoes become common. These are good for mid-game progression.
*   **Level 15+:** Gold Echoes begin to drop. This is when you should start using your Premium Tuners and Gold Sealed Tubes.
*   **Level 20/21:** This is currently the milestone for the highest drop rates for 5-star Echoes (80% drop rate at level 20).

Increasing your Data Bank level is done by capturing unique Echoes and unlocking higher rarities of Echoes you already own. If you have a 3-star version of a monster, finding the 4-star version will grant you Data Bank EXP.

### Farming Routes for Upgrade Materials
To keep up with the demand for Sealed Tubes and Tuners, you must engage with specific activities:
1. **Tacet Fields:** These are the primary source of Echo materials. For 60 Waveplates, you receive a bundle of Sealed Tubes, Tuners, and random Echoes from specific sets. This is the most efficient way to spend your stamina once your Resonators are ascended.
2. **The Guidebook (Milestones):** Completing Guidebook tasks and Data Bank level rewards provides a significant initial influx of Premium materials.
3. **Shops:** The Souvenir Shop in Jinzhou, the Item Exchange (using Oscillated Coral), and the Hazard Record store all sell Sealed Tubes and Tuners.
4. **Events:** [Wuthering Waves](https://ntecodex.com/guides/wuthering-waves-2-6-vs-2-5-meta-gear-guide-20260706) frequently runs limited-time events that offer "Echo Starter Kits" containing high-tier Sealed Tubes.

### Resource Management: When to Stop Leveling Purple Echoes
A common mistake is taking Purple (4-star) Echoes to level 20. While they are useful during the mid-game, the resources spent on them are better saved for Gold Echoes. As a rule of thumb, only level Purple Echoes to 10 or 15 to get through the main story and early Union Level challenges. Once you hit Data Bank level 15, pivot all resources to Gold Echoes.

### Echo Recycling and EXP Efficiency
If you have a leveled Echo that you no longer need (perhaps you found a better one or the sub-stats rolled poorly), you have two choices:
1. **Feeding (Direct):** You can use a leveled Echo as EXP material for a new one. This returns approximately 80% of the invested EXP, but you lose all the Tuners spent on it.
2. **Recycling (Bag):** By opening your Inventory/Bag and using the "Recycle" (trash icon) feature, you can convert leveled Echoes back into Sealed Tubes. This returns roughly 75% to 80% of the EXP and—crucially—refunds about 30% of the Tuners used. 

For high-level Gold Echoes, **Recycling via the bag is generally preferred** because Tuners are often the primary bottleneck for endgame builds.

## Related

![Related](/img/how-to-level-up-echoes-wuthering-waves/inline-4.webp)


Leveling your Echoes is only half the battle. To truly optimize a Resonator, you must understand how Echoes interact with each other and the character's kit.

### Best Echo Main Stats for DPS vs Support
When choosing which Echo to level, look at the Main Stat first.
*   **Main DPS (e.g., Jiyan, Yinlin):** Look for Crit Rate or Crit Damage on the 4-cost Echo. For 3-cost Echoes, Elemental Damage Bonus (e.g., Aero DMG, Electro DMG) is almost always superior to ATK %.
*   **Supports (e.g., Verina, Baizhi):** Look for Healing Bonus on the 4-cost Echo and Energy Regen % on the 3-cost Echoes.
*   **Sub-DPS (e.g., Mortefi, Sanhua):** Energy Regen % is vital to ensure their Resonance Liberation is available every rotation.

### How to Increase Data Bank Level Fast
The fastest way to reach Data Bank level 15 is "Rarity Skipping." Don't waste time farming every monster at 2-star rarity. Instead, focus on reaching the Union Level required to unlock higher world phases. Once you can drop 4-star Echoes, go on a "world tour" and kill one of every monster type. Capturing a 4-star Echo for the first time gives you the EXP for the 2-star and 3-star versions simultaneously. Additionally, use the **Echo Merge** feature in the Data Bank menu to turn 5 low-rarity Echoes into a random new one, which can trigger new rarity discoveries.

### Optimizing Echo Cost for End-game Builds
Every Resonator has an Echo Cost Limit of 12 (at max Data Bank level). This limit defines how you level your sets.
*   **The 4-3-3-1-1 Build:** This is the current meta for DPS characters. It uses one 4-cost (Boss), two 3-cost (Elite), and two 1-cost (Common) Echoes. This setup allows for two Elemental DMG bonuses, which provides the highest damage ceiling.
*   **The 4-4-1-1-1 Build:** Often used if you cannot find good 3-cost Echoes with the correct elemental stat. It uses two Boss Echoes, but since you can only have one "Active" Echo skill, the second 4-cost is purely for stats.

### Common Echo Set Bonuses (Sonata Effects)
Always ensure you are leveling Echoes that belong to the correct set. Mixing sets usually results in a significant loss of power.
*   **Void Thunder:** Increases Electro DMG (Best for Yinlin/Calcharo).
*   **Sierra Gale:** Increases Aero DMG (Best for Jiyan/Feixian).
*   **Sun-sinking Eclipse:** Increases Havoc DMG (Best for Rover-Havoc/Danjin).
*   **Moonlit Clouds:** Increases Energy Regen and provides an ATK buff to the next character (Best for most Sub-DPS).
*   **Rejuvenating Glow:** Increases Healing and provides a team-wide ATK buff (Best for Verina/Baizhi).

By following these guidelines and managing your Sealed Tubes carefully, you will ensure that your Resonators are always prepared for the challenges of Solaris-3. Focus on the +10 checkpoint, prioritize your Data Bank level, and always recycle through your bag to keep your Tuner supply healthy.
