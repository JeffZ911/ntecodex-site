To obtain 5-star Disks, players must participate in high-level **Anomaly Hunts** and the **Beyond the Rails** endgame mode.

### Frequently Asked Questions About NTE Sustain Builds

**Are 4-star Disks viable placeholders for endgame content?**
Yes, 4-star Disks can be used to clear early stages of Beyond the Rails, but the stat gap between 4-star and 5-star Disks is roughly 30%. You should prioritize getting the correct 2-piece set bonus on 4-star Disks while you farm for 5-star replacements.

**How do Esper Cycle reactions impact equipment?**
Specific reactions like **Charge** (Anima + Cosmos + Lakshana) grant 120 Ultimate Energy instantly. If your team is built to trigger Charge frequently, you can shift your Disk sub-stats away from Energy Regeneration and toward more HP% or Healing Bonus.

**Does Healing Bonus affect shields?**
No. Healing Bonus only increases the restoration of lost HP. For shielders like Adler, you should prioritize the **Kingdom's Guard** set (DEF +15%) or sets that specifically mention "Shield Effect +20%."

**What is the "Nova" reaction mentioned in Fadia builds?**
Nova is triggered by combining Psyche and Chaos elements. It creates a shared damage link between enemies. While Fadia herself doesn't need specific Disks to trigger it, running her with a Chaos unit like Daffodill allows her to use her damage redirection more offensively.
