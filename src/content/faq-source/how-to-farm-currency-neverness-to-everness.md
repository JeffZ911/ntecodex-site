### Farming Annulith: The Path to S-Rank Characters

Annulith is the lifeblood of your roster. To maximize your pulls on banners like "The Ichi-daime," you must exploit every available source.

**One-Time Sources:**
The most significant influx of Annulith comes from the initial clear of the Main Story chapters and Side Stories. Each major mission typically yields a substantial amount of Annulith upon completion. Additionally, Hethereau is filled with exploration rewards. Opening Common, Precious, and Luxurious chests throughout the city districts provides a steady stream of Annulith. Completing world puzzles—often involving the manipulation of urban anomalies—also grants small but consistent amounts.

**Repeatable Sources:**
Once the initial world rewards are exhausted, your primary source of Annulith shifts to the **Anomaly Hunt**. This is a repeatable combat challenge that resets weekly. Achieving high scores or clearing specific difficulty tiers in Anomaly Hunts provides a reliable weekly "salary" of Annulith. Daily login bonuses and "Daily Errands" (the game's version of daily missions) provide [information unavailable] Annulith per day, totaling enough for several pulls per month.

### Earning Beetle Coins: Character and Arc Progression

Beetle Coins are consumed in almost every character-related action, from ascending an Esper to enhancing an Arc. While they seem abundant early on, the costs scale exponentially at higher levels.

The most efficient way to farm Beetle Coins is through **Daily Commissions**. These are short combat or traversal tasks found on the city map. Using your **Esper Pixels** to clear "Wealth Anomalies" in the Anomaly Zone is the fastest method for bulk farming when you are in a pinch. However, since Esper Pixels are also needed for talent materials, it is recommended to only farm Beetle Coins this way during "Double Drop" event periods.

### The Urban Grind: Farming Fons for Cars and Property

Fons is what separates Neverness to Everness from traditional gacha games. This currency is used exclusively for the "Everness" side of the game—purchasing high-performance vehicles, buying furniture for your apartment, and paying for car tuning.

**Cafe Management:**
The most effective long-term strategy for Fons is the Cafe Management system. Early in the game, you unlock a storefront that generates Fons passively. 
*   **Upgrading:** Use Fons to upgrade the cafe’s interior and kitchen equipment. Higher-tier cafes generate more Fons per hour.
*   **Staffing:** Assigning characters with high "Hospitality" or "Management" stats to the cafe increases the efficiency of passive generation.
*   **Menu:** Researching new recipes through exploration allows you to sell higher-priced items, further boosting Fons yield.

**Pink Paws Heist:**
For those who need a large injection of cash quickly, the Pink Paws Heist is the premier activity. This is a bi-weekly event where players infiltrate specific high-security zones to recover "Anomaly Artifacts." Successfully completing a heist can net up to 1,000,000 Fons depending on the difficulty and the amount of loot secured.

### Specialized Farming: Carrota and the Rewind System

The **Module System** is NTE’s version of gear or equipment. To optimize your characters, you must participate in the **Rewind** gacha, which specifically grants character Modules.

Since Modules provide massive percentage boosts to stats like Crit Rate and Anomaly Damage, farming Carrota is a mandatory endgame activity for any player looking to clear high-level content.
