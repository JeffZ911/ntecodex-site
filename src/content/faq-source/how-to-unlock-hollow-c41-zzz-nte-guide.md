# How to Unlock Hollow C41 in ZZZ and NTE Anomaly Commissions

### Quick Answer
To [unlock Hollow C41](https://ntecodex.com/faq) in Zenless Zone Zero, reach Inter-Knot Level 32 and complete the Main Story Chapter 3: The Midnight Pursuit. Afterward, you must finish the [Prepare for the Gold Rush](https://zenless.hoyoverse.com/en-us/news/125123) commission during the Camellia Golden Week event. In Neverness to Everness, similar high-stakes content is accessed by increasing your Hunter Level and investigating supernatural phenomena within the Miguel District.

### The Confusion Between ZZZ Hollows and NTE Anomalies
Because both games feature urban-fantasy settings with supernatural "zones" that players must investigate, it is easy to confuse the terminology. In Zenless Zone Zero, players enter "Hollows"—disaster zones filled with Ethereals—using a grid-based navigation system called the Hollow Deep Dive (HDD). In Neverness to Everness, the player takes on the role of an Appraiser who deals with "Anomalies" that manifest directly in the open world of Hethereau. While [Hollow C41](https://ntecodex.com/faq) is a specific, event-gated location in ZZZ, the Anomaly Commissions in NTE serve as the primary method for engaging with the game's supernatural combat and exploration loop.

### Unlocking Hollow C41 in Zenless Zone Zero
Hollow C41 became a focal point for players during the [Camellia Golden Week](https://zenless.hoyoverse.com/en-us/news/125123) event. Unlike standard Hollows that can be accessed via the HDD system at any time, Hollow C41 is tied to a specific event narrative. 

To begin the process, you must ensure your Inter-Knot Level is at 32 or higher. This level is achieved by completing daily commissions, side quests, and spending Battery Charge. Once the level requirement is met, the "Prepare for the Gold Rush" commission will appear in your schedule. This quest leads you into Hollow C41, where the gameplay shifts from standard combat to a more complex puzzle-solving experience involving the collection of "Golden Flowers." Successfully navigating this zone is essential for obtaining limited-time rewards like Polychromes and specialized W-Engines.

### Unlocking Anomaly Commissions in Neverness to Everness
In Neverness to Everness, the system is more integrated into the open-world environment. As an Appraiser, you do not just select a mission from a menu; you often encounter Anomalies while driving through the streets of Hethereau.

To unlock the full range of Anomaly Commissions, players must focus on their Hunter Level. This is the primary progression metric that dictates which supernatural threats you are qualified to handle. The initial set of commissions is unlocked after completing the prologue and the first series of tasks in the Miguel District. As you reach higher milestones, you will gain access to more dangerous zones, including those located near the city's most prominent landmarks and the central skyscraper.

### NTE Hunter Level Progression Milestones
Progression in NTE is less about a linear story and more about your standing within the Appraiser community.
*   **Early Game:** Focus on the Miguel District to unlock basic combat commissions.
*   **Mid-Game:** Reach the required Hunter Level to investigate the "Plot Hole" phenomena, which act as the game's version of high-difficulty dungeons.
*   **Endgame:** High-risk Anomaly Zones require a team of well-built Espers and high-rank Arcs (weapons).

### Comparing ZZZ Hollows and NTE Anomaly Zones
The two systems offer different takes on urban supernatural investigation. ZZZ uses a stylized, retro-tech interface where the "Proxy" guides agents through the Hollow. The difficulty in Hollow C41 often comes from the environmental hazards and the "Pressure" mechanic, which penalizes players for staying in the Hollow too long.

Conversely, NTE offers a seamless transition from urban exploration to supernatural combat. When you enter an Anomaly Zone in Hethereau, the world around you may distort—cars might float, or the gravity might shift—but you remain in the same physical space. Characters like Nanally, who utilizes high-impact physical attacks, or Chaos (Lakshana), who features spatial manipulation abilities, are vital for clearing these zones efficiently.

**Key NTE Characters for Clearing Anomalies:**
*   **[Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte):** A powerful physical attacker capable of breaking through the defenses of heavy Anomaly entities.
*   **Chaos (Lakshana):** Provides essential mobility and spatial control, making it easier to manage large groups of enemies in distorted environments.
*   **Mint:** A versatile character often used for utility and elemental reactions to exploit enemy weaknesses.

The rewards for these activities also differ. While ZZZ players hunt for Polychromes to pull for new agents, NTE players seek Annulith and Fons to upgrade their Espers and Arcs.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I still access Hollow C41 after the event ends?
Typically, event-specific Hollows like Hollow C41 are only available for the duration of the associated event, such as Camellia Golden Week. However, some narrative elements or simplified versions of the map may be added to the permanent Hollow Deep Dive system in later updates. Check the official ZZZ news for any announcements regarding permanent archival.

### What is the fastest way to increase Hunter Level in NTE?
The most efficient way to increase your Hunter Level is to consistently complete Anomaly Commissions and daily urban tasks throughout Hethereau. Engaging in world exploration and discovering hidden supernatural sites also provides significant experience. Prioritizing the main story missions in the Miguel District will unlock higher-tier commissions more quickly.

### Who is the best character for soloing Anomaly Zones in NTE?
Characters with high survivability and area-of-effect damage, such as Nanally, are often preferred for soloing. Chaos (Lakshana) is also a top-tier choice due to her ability to avoid damage through spatial manipulation. Your choice should depend on the specific elemental weaknesses of the Anomalies you are facing.
