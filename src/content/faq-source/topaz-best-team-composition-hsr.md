Topaz & Numby have evolved from a niche sub-DPS into the cornerstone of the most dominant archetype in Honkai: Star Rail: the Follow-Up Attack (FUA) meta. By providing a unique vulnerability debuff that specifically amplifies follow-up damage, Topaz acts as both a damage dealer and a specialized support for the game's most powerful strikers.

# Topaz Best Team Composition HSR: The Ultimate Follow-Up Guide

## Question Restated

![Question Restated](/img/topaz-best-team-composition-hsr/inline-1.webp)


When building a team around Topaz & Numby, players often focus on three primary questions to maximize their investment:

**What are the most effective team compositions for Topaz & Numby in the current Honkai: Star Rail meta?**
The meta has shifted significantly since Topaz's debut. While she was initially paired with units like Clara or Jing Yuan, the introduction of the "IPC Core" (Aventurine and Dr. Ratio) and the high-speed stack mechanics of Version 2.5 characters like Feixiao have redefined her ceiling.

**How does Topaz's role as a Hunt character shift between a Main DPS and a Follow-Up Attack (FUA) support?**
Unlike traditional Hunt characters like Seele, who demand all the team's resources to carry, Topaz functions as a "force multiplier." In single-target scenarios, she can act as a Main DPS, but her true value lies in her "Proof of Debt" debuff, which allows a second damage dealer to reach astronomical damage numbers.

**Which characters maximize the 'Proof of Debt' debuff provided by Numby?**
Not all follow-up attacks are created equal. Topaz prefers partners who trigger follow-ups frequently rather than one large hit per cycle. This frequent triggering accelerates Numby’s action, creating a feedback loop of continuous attacks.

## Direct Answer

![Direct Answer](/img/topaz-best-team-composition-hsr/inline-2.webp)


The absolute best team composition for Topaz & Numby is the **Premium IPC Team**, consisting of **Dr. Ratio (Main DPS), Robin (Harmony), and Aventurine (Preservation)**. This team leverages the synergy between Topaz’s debuffs, Dr. Ratio’s high-frequency follow-ups, Robin’s team-wide Action Advance, and Aventurine’s defense-scaling follow-up attacks.

In the Version 2.5+ meta, Topaz has also become the premier partner for **Feixiao**. Because Feixiao’s Ultimate relies on the total number of attacks launched by the team to generate "Flying Aureus" stacks, the high-frequency nature of Topaz and Numby makes them the most efficient stack generators in the game.

For multi-target scenarios like Pure Fiction, Topaz shifts to a support role for Erudition units such as **Jade, Himeko, or Herta**. By marking an elite enemy with Proof of Debt, Topaz helps these AOE specialists clear high-HP targets that would otherwise survive their area-of-effect sweeps.

F2P players who missed the premium IPC units can still find great success pairing Topaz with **Moze** or **March 7th (The Hunt)**. These units are designed to synergize with frequent attacks and provide a budget-friendly way to engage with the FUA playstyle.

## Detailed Explanation

![Detailed Explanation](/img/topaz-best-team-composition-hsr/inline-3.webp)


The power of Topaz & Numby stems from their unique interaction with the "Proof of Debt" status. Understanding how this mechanic interacts with the rest of the roster is key to mastering her team compositions.

### Analysis of the 'Proof of Debt' Mechanic
Proof of Debt is a debuff applied by Topaz's Skill that increases the Follow-Up Attack damage taken by a single target by 50%. This is a "Vulnerability" multiplier, which is calculated separately from standard DMG% buffs. Because it occupies its own layer in the damage formula, it provides a much more significant boost than adding more Attack or Elemental DMG to a character who is already saturated with those stats.

### The Synergy Loop
Topaz does not play in a vacuum. Her kit creates a "Synergy Loop" with her teammates:
1.  **Numby's Action Advance:** When an ally hits an enemy afflicted with Proof of Debt using a follow-up attack, Numby’s action is advanced by 50%. 
2.  **Teammate Triggering:** Frequent follow-ups from allies like Dr. Ratio or Feixiao bring Numby forward, causing Numby to attack more often.
3.  **Passive Stacking:** Numby’s attacks count as follow-up attacks, which in turn trigger teammate passives. For example, every time Numby attacks, Aventurine gains a "Blind Bet" stack. Once Aventurine reaches 7 stacks, he launches his own follow-up, which then advances Numby again.

### The 'Debuff Threshold' for Dr. Ratio
One of the most common points of confusion in Topaz team building is her synergy with Dr. Ratio at low investment. Dr. Ratio requires 3 debuffs on a target to guarantee his follow-up attack (100% chance) and 5 debuffs to maximize his damage bonus.
*   **Topaz E0S0:** Provides only 1 debuff (Proof of Debt).
*   **Topaz S1 (Worrisome, Blissful):** Adds a second debuff (Tame).
*   **Topaz E1:** Adds a third debuff (Debtor).

If your Topaz is E0S0, she cannot solo-support Dr. Ratio’s debuff requirement. In this scenario, you must rely on your sustain (like Aventurine’s Ultimate or Gallagher’s debuffs) or use the "Trend of the Universal Market" Light Cone on a Preservation unit to bridge the gap.

### Checklist: Sources of Debuffs in a Topaz/Ratio Team
*   **Topaz Skill:** Proof of Debt (1 Debuff)
*   **Topaz E1:** Debtor (1 Debuff)
*   **Topaz S1:** Tame (1 Debuff)
*   **Dr. Ratio Skill:** Effect RES Down (1 Debuff, 100% base chance)
*   **Aventurine Ultimate:** Unnerved (1 Debuff)
*   **Aventurine S1:** Blind Bet follow-up debuff (1 Debuff)
*   **Robin E1:** undisclosed (Note: Robin's E1 provides Res Pen, which is a buff, not a debuff on the enemy).

### The Premium IPC Team: Ratio, Robin, and Aventurine
This is widely considered the "Gold Standard" for follow-up teams. Robin is the lynchpin here because her Ultimate, "Vox Harmonique, Opus Cosmique," provides a massive Attack buff and causes her to deal additional Physical damage every time an ally attacks. Since Topaz, Numby, Ratio, and Aventurine all attack multiple times per cycle, they trigger Robin’s coordinated attacks more than any other team, leading to massive total damage output.

### The New Meta: Topaz and Feixiao Synergy
With the release of Feixiao, Topaz has found a second "best" partner. Feixiao’s kit revolves around her Ultimate, which requires 12 stacks of "Flying Aureus" to reach maximum potential. She gains 0.5 stacks every time an ally attacks an enemy. 
Because Numby is constantly being advanced by Feixiao’s follow-ups, and Topaz herself has a high attack frequency, this duo generates stacks for Feixiao faster than any other combination in the game. This team typically drops Dr. Ratio in favor of Feixiao as the primary carry.

### Pure Fiction Specialists: Jade, Himeko, and Herta
In Pure Fiction, the goal is wave clear. While Topaz is a Hunt (single-target) character, she excels here by pairing with Jade. Jade can designate Topaz as her "Debt Collector." Since Topaz and Numby attack so frequently, they quickly build stacks for Jade’s AOE follow-up attack. This allows the team to handle both the small fodder enemies (via Jade/Herta) and the high-health bosses (via Topaz/Numby).

### F2P and Budget Team Alternatives
If you do not have the limited 5-star supports, you can use the following:
*   **March 7th (The Hunt):** Her "Master" mechanic allows her to launch follow-up attacks whenever her designated Master (Topaz) attacks. This creates a very fast-paced duo that is excellent for Breaking enemies.
*   **Moze:** As a 4-star Lightning Hunt character, Moze "departs" the field and deals additional damage whenever an ally attacks his "Prey." This is highly synergistic with Topaz’s high hit-count.
*   **Asta:** She provides a team-wide Speed buff and a Fire DMG buff, both of which Topaz utilizes perfectly.

### Support and Sustain Alternatives: Ruan Mei, Huohuo, and Fu Xuan
While Robin and Aventurine are the best-in-slot choices, other units perform admirably:
*   **Ruan Mei:** Excellent for Break-focused FUA teams (Topaz + March 7th).
*   **Huohuo:** Provides Energy Regeneration, which is vital if you are relying on Topaz’s Ultimate to keep Numby in the "Windfall" state.
*   **Lingsha:** A newer sustain unit who features her own follow-up attacks, making her a direct upgrade over Gallagher in FUA-centric compositions.

Optimizing Topaz goes beyond just picking the right teammates; she requires specific gear to facilitate the high-frequency attack loops her teams rely on.

### Topaz Best Light Cones
1.  **Worrisome, Blissful (5-Star Signature):** This is transformative. It provides Crit Rate, increases Follow-Up damage, and applies the "Tame" debuff. The extra debuff is often the difference between a Dr. Ratio team working or failing.
2.  **Baptism of Pure Thought (5-Star):** Dr. Ratio’s signature cone. While it doesn't provide the team utility of her own signature, it offers the highest personal damage for Topaz.
3.  **Cruising in the Stellar Sea (5-Star F2P):** Available from the Herta Shop, this provides a massive Crit Rate boost and an Attack buff upon defeating enemies.
4.  **Final Victor (4-Star):** A strong F2P option from the Forgotten Hall shop that rewards the multiple hits in Topaz’s kit with stacking Crit DMG.

### Topaz Best Relics and Ornaments

| Gear Type | Best-in-Slot | Reasoning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Relic Set (4-Pc)** | **The Ashblazing Grand Duke** | Increases ATK based on the number of hits in a follow-up attack. Numby’s multi-hit attacks max this out instantly. |
| **Relic Set (Alternative)** | **Pioneer Diver of Dead Waters** | Best if Topaz is E1S1, as she can maintain the 4-piece set's double Crit bonuses via her constant debuffing. |

### Speed Tuning and Breakpoints
Topaz generally wants to be fast, but her speed requirements depend on her teammates.
*   **134 SPD:** The standard breakpoint for two actions in the first cycle.
*   **160+ SPD:** Optimal if running her with characters who don't have their own follow-ups, as Topaz needs to move more to advance Numby manually.
*   **Base SPD (Slowpaz):** If paired with high-frequency attackers like Feixiao or Dr. Ratio, Topaz can sometimes run ATK% boots. Because her teammates advance Numby so much, her own personal speed becomes less critical than the raw power of her hits.

### Simulated Universe: Best Blessings for Topaz on the Path of Elation
In the Simulated Universe, Topaz is the queen of the **Path of Elation**. Key blessings to look for include:
*   **"Twenty-First Military Rule":** Recovers 1 Skill Point when a follow-up attack is launched. With Topaz and Numby, this makes your team virtually Skill Point infinite.
*   **"Champion's Dinner: Cat's Cradle":** Turns Ultimates into follow-up attacks. This allows Robin and other supports to benefit from Topaz’s Proof of Debt.
*   **"Auto-Harmonica: Whitest Night":** Causes follow-up attacks to trigger "Aftertaste" damage, which scales massively with Topaz’s vulnerability debuff.

By aligning Topaz with high-frequency attackers and the right debuff-heavy gear, you can unlock a team that doesn't just take turns, but creates a continuous stream of attacks that can dismantle the game's toughest bosses.
