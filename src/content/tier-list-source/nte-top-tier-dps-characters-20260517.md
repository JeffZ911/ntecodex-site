## Methodology

![Methodology](/img/nte-top-tier-dps-characters-20260517/inline-1.webp)


Ranking characters in NTE involves testing units across three primary metrics: raw damage per second (DPS), Break efficiency against the Stagger Bar, and their contribution to the Esper Cycle. Testing occurs primarily within high-level Anomaly Hunter commissions and world bosses located in the Hethereau districts.

The Esper Cycle is the heart of NTE combat. Unlike traditional action RPGs where one character stays on field indefinitely, NTE rewards rapid swapping to trigger elemental reactions. We evaluate DPS units based on how well they initiate or capitalize on these reactions:
*   **Blossom (Cosmos/Anima):** High-frequency damage ticks.
*   **Scorch (Incantation):** Stacking Damage over Time (DoT) effects.
*   **Nova (Chaos/Psyche):** Massive burst damage that scales with the target's missing Stagger Bar.

### The Role of the Stagger Bar in DPS Rankings
In NTE, enemies possess a Stagger Bar (white bar below health) that mitigates incoming damage. High-tier DPS characters are those who can either shred this bar quickly or deal astronomical damage once the enemy enters the "Broken" state. A character like Daffodill ranks high despite lower raw numbers because her Break efficiency creates massive damage windows for the rest of the team.

## S Tier

![S Tier](/img/nte-top-tier-dps-characters-20260517/inline-2.webp)


S-Tier characters represent the peak of the current meta. These units are either indispensable for clearing high-tier content or offer such high scaling that they can brute-force through elemental resistances.

### Nanally: The Queen of Follow-up Attacks
Nanally remains the gold standard for DPS in NTE. Her unique mechanic involves her "Underboss" summon—a spectral figure that emerges from her briefcase to perform follow-up attacks whenever Nanally or a teammate uses a Resonance Skill. This allows Nanally to provide massive off-field pressure while another character is active, effectively doubling the team's damage output during the Esper Cycle. Her Anima alignment makes her the perfect partner for Jiuyuan or Mint to trigger Anima Resonance.

### Chiz: Why the Tycoon System is Mandatory for F2P Players
Chiz is the premier F2P (Free-to-Play) carry, primarily because her Awakening levels (A1-A6) are grindable through the Tycoon business simulation system in Hethereau. Her Yokai Hammer features massive horizontal hitboxes, making her the best character for clearing waves of "Anomaly" mobs. While her attack animations are slower than sword users, her "Weight of the World" passive increases her damage based on the target's current Stagger Bar, allowing her to hit harder as the fight progresses.

### Jiuyuan and Hotori: The New Meta of Burst and Record
Jiuyuan is a burst-heavy sword specialist whose value skyrocketed with the discovery of her A2 (Awakening 2) potential. At A2, her "Intel Turns Into Blades" passive allows a portion of her damage to be converted into team-wide healing, or conversely, for healing received to boost her next Ultimate's damage. Hotori, the newest addition to the roster, has introduced the "Record" mechanic. ### Baicang and the Scorch Meta
Baicang is the centerpiece of the Incantation element. His "Power Word" rotations involve applying stacks to enemies that detonate when hit by a specific elemental reaction. In long-form boss fights, Baicang’s Scorch DoT scaling can actually outpace Nanally's burst, provided the player can maintain the "Power Word" stacks without interruption. He is currently the best counter for bosses with high physical resistance.

### Daffodill: The Essential Shield Breaker
While Daffodill's raw DPS numbers look underwhelming on paper, her Break efficiency is the highest in the game. Her Chaos-aligned attacks ignore a portion of the enemy's Stagger defense. In endgame content where bosses have "Iron Skin" phases, Daffodill is mandatory to shred the shield and initiate the Broken state, where S-Tier carries like Chiz can then deal 200% increased damage.

### Esper Zero: More Than Just a Starter Character
The protagonist, Esper Zero, remains a viable A-Tier choice due to their Cosmos alignment and "Insta-Cycler" passive. This mechanic reduces the swap-in cooldown for all party members whenever Zero hits a critical strike. While Zero lacks the extreme multipliers of Nanally, they act as the perfect "bridge" character, keeping the Esper Cycle moving and ensuring that elemental reactions are triggered as frequently as possible.
