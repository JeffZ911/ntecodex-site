## S Tier

![S Tier](/img/best-dps-characters-nte-ranked-tier-list/inline-2.webp)


Characters in the S Tier are the "gold standard" for damage. They possess high base multipliers, excellent scaling with late-game gear, and mechanics that remain effective regardless of the current anomaly rotation.

### Nanally: The Gold Standard for Carry Potential

Nanally remains the undisputed queen of the NTE meta. As an Anima-element user with a Plasma Arc, she excels in "Anti-Gravity" combat. Her unique mechanic involves the "Underboss" summon, which performs follow-up attacks every time Nanally lands a Critical Hit. 

Nanally’s kit focuses heavily on CRIT DMG scaling. When her "Anti-Gravity" state is active, she gains a [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-17] bonus to her aerial attack speed. This allows her to hover above ground-based threats while raining down Plasma projectiles. For players who pulled on "The Ichi-daime" banner, Nanally provides the most consistent path to clearing floor 12 of the Hethereau Spiral.

### Jiuyuan: Mastering the Lethal Rose Pact

Jiuyuan is an Anima-element burst dealer who specializes in damage-over-time (DoT) that transitions into massive explosive windows. Her "Lethal Rose Pact" applies a unique status effect to enemies. As Jiuyuan attacks, the Rose "blooms," dealing incremental Anima damage. Once the pact reaches maximum stacks, it triggers a "Settlement" phase, dealing a burst of damage equal to a percentage of the total DoT dealt during the window.

Recent bug fixes in version 1.05 have stabilized Jiuyuan’s energy regeneration, ensuring she can maintain her Pact uptime almost indefinitely. She is particularly effective in multi-target scenarios where her roses can spread between enemies upon a target's defeat.

### Hotori: Why Every Team Needs a Mimic

Hotori has revolutionized the sub-DPS role since her debut in the "Misty Tipsy Style" banner. As a Cosmos-element user with a Solid Arc, her primary value lies in her skill mimicry. Hotori can "record" the last skill used by an ally and replay it at [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-17] percent effectiveness.

This ability effectively doubles the burst window for characters like Nanally. ### Sakiri: AoE Excellence

While Sakiri is often categorized as a support due to her team-wide buffs, her personal damage output via the "Kiroumaru" summon is too high to ignore. Sakiri uses the Incantation element to summon Kiroumaru, a spectral entity that mimics her movements. In high-density anomaly waves, Sakiri’s AoE (Area of Effect) coverage is unmatched, making her the premier choice for speed-clearing daily containment missions.

## A Tier

![A Tier](/img/best-dps-characters-nte-ranked-tier-list/inline-3.webp)


A-Tier characters are incredibly powerful and can clear all current content. However, they may require more specific team compositions or higher player skill to match the raw output of S-Tier units.

### Baicang: High Risk, High Reward

Baicang is a high-skill hybrid character utilizing the Incantation element and a Synthesis Arc. His gameplay revolves around the "Power of Words" mechanic, where he stacks different linguistic runes to empower his next "Ultimate Incantation." 

The catch is that Baicang requires precise HP management. His highest damage multipliers trigger when he is below 50% health, necessitating a skilled shielder or a healer who can provide "delayed" healing. For players who can master his rhythm, Baicang’s DoT potential rivals Jiuyuan, but his fragility keeps him out of S-Tier for the general player base.

### Daffodill: The Shield-Breaking Specialist

Daffodill is a Chaos-element specialist and currently the best "Stance Breaker" in Neverness to Everness. Her kit is designed to shred enemy guard bars with exponential efficiency. Once an enemy enters the "Broken" state, Daffodill deals [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-17] increased damage.

She is a boss-slaying specialist. In encounters like the "Goliath Anomaly," Daffodill’s ability to force the boss into a vulnerable state multiple times per fight makes her more valuable than Nanally in specific scenarios. Her lower ranking is only due to her average performance against groups of smaller, low-stance enemies.

### Hathor: Speed and Burst on Two Wheels

Hathor brings a unique flavor to the Lakshana element with her motorcycle-based combat style. Her ultimate ability grants her "Express Delivery Power" stacks, which she consumes to reset the cooldown of her primary combat skills. 

Hathor's mobility is her greatest asset; she can zip across the battlefield to target high-priority snipers or casters that other DPS units might struggle to reach. While her burst is high, she suffers from longer "downtime" periods compared to the S-tier carries, requiring a well-timed rotation to keep her damage consistent.

### Chiz: The F2P Powerhouse

Chiz is the standout free-to-play (F2P) option for Cosmos-element damage. Using her "Yokai Hammer," Chiz builds "Grain" stacks by hitting enemies with heavy attacks. At 10 stacks, her hammer grows in size, granting her massive AoE and increased stagger values.

While her kit is mechanically complex, a fully awakened Chiz (obtainable through event participation and the standard shop) can outperform base-level S-tier units. Her primary limitation is her slow attack speed, which can make her vulnerable to fast-moving bosses if the player does not time her "Grain" consumption correctly.

## B Tier

![B Tier](/img/best-dps-characters-nte-ranked-tier-list/inline-4.webp)


B-Tier characters are reliable foundations for any account. They are often easier to play but lack the specialized scaling or "broken" mechanics that define the top of the meta.

### Esper Zero: The Reliable Foundation

As the protagonist unit, Esper Zero is a Cosmos-element character that every player will have invested in. Their kit is balanced, offering a mix of single-target strikes and moderate AoE. Esper Zero is excellent for the early-to-mid game and remains viable for Psyche-weak anomalies. However, as players reach the level 80 cap, Esper Zero’s lack of specialized Arc scaling becomes apparent, and they are usually replaced by Chiz or Hotori in optimized Cosmos teams.

### Haniel: The Hybrid Buffer

Haniel is primarily used for her "Silent Moonlit Forest Guardian" buff, which increases the team's Elemental Mastery. However, she functions as a respectable sub-DPS because the Guardian performs coordinated attacks based on Haniel’s base ATK. She is a perfect "slot-in" character for teams that need a bit more damage without sacrificing utility, though she cannot carry a team on her own.

### Adler: The Sustain-Focused DPS

Adler uses the Incantation element to apply steady DoT stacks. His primary value lies in the shields he generates for the team while he is attacking. This makes him a "comfy" pick for difficult content where survival is a struggle. Unfortunately, his raw damage numbers are significantly lower than Baicang's, and his DoT takes longer to ramp up compared to Jiuyuan.
