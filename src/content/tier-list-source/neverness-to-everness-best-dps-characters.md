## Methodology

![Methodology](/img/neverness-to-everness-best-dps-characters/inline-1.webp)


The rankings in this guide are derived from rigorous testing within the 'Beyond the Rails' endgame mode and general efficiency during Hethereau exploration. We prioritize characters who provide high "Cycle Intensity," a hidden stat that determines how quickly an Esper can apply their elemental status to a target. Furthermore, "Cycle Rate" is considered, which dictates the internal cooldown of elemental applications.

Our methodology also weighs "Arc Compatibility." Every character is attuned to a specific state of matter—Solid, Liquid, Gas, Plasma, or Synthesis. This determines which weapons provide the most significant scaling. For example, a Plasma-aligned DPS like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) gains unique multiplicative bonuses from Plasma-type Arcs that other units cannot access.

We categorize DPS roles into three distinct types:
1. **Main DPS:** Characters who require maximum on-field time to generate damage.
2. **Sub-DPS:** Characters who provide off-field damage or utility through summons and persistent effects.
3. **Burst DPS:** Characters designed to swap in, execute a high-damage rotation, and swap out.

Finally, the "Esper Wheel" is the core of all team building. Adjacent elements on the wheel, such as Cosmos and Anima, create the "Blossom" reaction, which currently sits at the top of the damage charts.

## S Tier

![S Tier](/img/neverness-to-everness-best-dps-characters/inline-2.webp)


S-Tier characters are the current gold standard for damage in Neverness to Everness. They possess high base scaling, excellent synergy with the current top-tier Arcs, and mechanics that remain relevant regardless of the enemy type.

### Nanally: The Follow-Up Queen
Nanally remains the most sought-after unit following her debut in "The Ichi-daime" banner. As an Anima Esper with Plasma Arc compatibility, she bridges the gap between on-field pressure and off-field support. Her core mechanic revolves around her 'Underboss' summon—a spectral entity that mimics her attacks. This allows her to maintain a high volume of strikes, making her the best character for shredding multi-layered shields.

### Jiuyuan: Ranged Mastery and Blossom Synergy
Jiuyuan is the premier Anima ranged specialist. Her kit is designed to facilitate the "Blossom" reaction by applying Anima stacks rapidly from a distance. Her Skill features a unique gravity-well effect that pulls enemies into a tight cluster, making her the perfect partner for Cosmos units like Chiz or Hotori. In terms of Blossom efficiency, Jiuyuan currently outperforms Nanally in pure AoE scenarios because her projectiles can pierce multiple enemies, applying Anima status to entire groups simultaneously.

### Chiz: The F2P Powerhouse
Obtainable through the Tycoon system, Chiz is a testament to the viability of free-to-play units in NTE. She is a Cosmos/Gas character whose damage hinges on the management of her 'Fons' meter. When the Fons meter is full, her Ultimate transforms her basic attacks into high-velocity energy blasts. While she requires more mechanical skill to play than Nanally, her raw damage ceiling is comparable to limited S-Rank units when her meter is managed correctly.

### Hotori: The Replay Specialist
Released in the May 13 update, Hotori has redefined the Sub-DPS role. Her 'Skill Recording' mechanic allows her to capture the last Esper Ability used by an ally and replay it at [information unavailable]% of its original strength. This effectively allows teams to double their burst windows. As a Cosmos/Solid unit, she provides the necessary Cosmos applications to trigger Blossom while her teammates provide the Anima or Lakshana triggers.

## A Tier

![A Tier](/img/neverness-to-everness-best-dps-characters/inline-3.webp)


A-Tier characters are powerful and capable of clearing all current content, but they often require specific team compositions or higher investment in their Arcs to match the raw output of S-Tier units.

### Baicang: DoT and Execution
Baicang is a unique Incantation/Synthesis hybrid described by many as a "vampire martial artist." His gameplay focuses on the 'Scorch' reaction, which deals massive Damage-over-Time. Baicang's strength lies in his Ultimate, which features an execute mechanic: if an enemy is below a certain health threshold while affected by Scorch, the damage is multiplied significantly.

### Daffodill: The Chaos Breaker
As the only Chaos character currently in the game, Daffodill is the undisputed queen of the Break mechanic. Her Liquid Arc compatibility allows her to deal high "Impact" damage, which depletes enemy Break bars faster than any other unit. She is essential for boss fights where the enemy has a "Nova" or "Discord" vulnerability. However, her personal damage outside of the Break window is lower than that of Main DPS units, necessitating a well-built team to capitalize on the openings she creates.

### Esper Zero: The Instant Cycle Advantage
The protagonist, Esper Zero, remains a staple in the A-Tier due to the 'Instant Cycle' mechanic. Unlike other characters who must build up a meter to use their most powerful Esper Abilities, Esper Zero can trigger an 'Instant Cycle' that skips the buildup. This makes them incredibly valuable for short, high-pressure encounters where immediate damage is required. Recent balance adjustments have improved the recharge rate of this ability, keeping the protagonist competitive with newer limited banners.

## B Tier

![B Tier](/img/neverness-to-everness-best-dps-characters/inline-4.webp)


B-Tier characters are functional but often outclassed by higher-tier units in terms of scaling or ease of use. They are frequently used as niche fillers or for their non-combat utility.

### Hathor: High Burst, Low Sustain
Hathor is a Lakshana character who relies on her motorcycle summon for her primary damage output. During her Ultimate, she deals some of the highest burst damage in the game. However, once the Ultimate ends, her damage drops significantly due to long skill cooldowns and a lack of sustained pressure. Compared to Nanally, who maintains 100% uptime on her Underboss, Hathor’s downtime makes her less efficient for the 'Beyond the Rails' timed challenges.

### Mint: Speed and Exploration Utility
Mint is a dual-blade Anima user known for her incredible mobility. She is the best character for navigating the verticality of Hethereau, but her combat scaling is lower than other Anima units like Jiuyuan. In high-tier endgame content, her speed does not compensate for her lower base attack multipliers.
