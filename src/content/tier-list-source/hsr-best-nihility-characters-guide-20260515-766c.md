S-Tier characters are the "must-haves" of the Nihility Path. They either define an entire team archetype or provide such a massive boost to team damage that they are rarely swapped out.

### Acheron: Still the Ultimate Nuke
Acheron remains a dominant force in the meta due to her unique "Slashed Dream" mechanic. Unlike other characters, she does not use Energy; instead, she relies on debuffs applied by herself or her teammates to charge her Ultimate, "Slashed Dream Operations." At E0, she typically requires two other Nihility characters to maximize her "The Abyss" Trace, which grants a 160% multiplier to her damage. 

Her Ultimate is a multi-hit attack that ignores Weakness Types and reduces enemy Toughness, making her the premier choice for Apocalyptic Shadow. To keep her in S-Tier, pairing her with high-frequency debuffers like Jiaoqiu or Pela is essential.

### The DoT Core: Kafka and Black Swan
Kafka and Black Swan form the most consistent duo in Honkai: Star Rail. Kafka is the "engine" of the team; her Skill, "Caressing Moonlight," immediately triggers a portion of all DoTs currently on the target, while her Ultimate triggers 100% of their damage.

Black Swan complements this by applying "Arcana," a unique Wind DoT that stacks up to 50 times. At 7 or more stacks, Arcana ignores 20% of the target's DEF. The synergy between Kafka’s detonation and Black Swan’s stacking mechanics allows this duo to excel in Pure Fiction, where they can clear waves of enemies without waiting for the enemy's turn.

## A Tier

![A Tier](/img/hsr-best-nihility-characters-guide-20260515-766c/inline-3.webp)


A-Tier characters are highly effective and often essential for specific teams, though they may be slightly more situational or have lower raw damage ceilings than S-Tier units.

### Jiaoqiu: The Premier Acheron Support
Jiaoqiu is the most significant addition to Acheron teams. His kit centers on the "Ashen Roast" stack mechanic, which applies a Vulnerability debuff to enemies. At Level 10, his Ultimate creates a field that increases Ultimate Damage taken by enemies by 15% and has a 100% base chance to apply a stack of Ashen Roast whenever an enemy takes a turn. This allows Acheron to generate "Slashed Dream" stacks even during the enemy's phase, drastically increasing her Ultimate frequency.

### Pela: The Unkillable F2P Staple
Pela remains the most valuable 4-star Nihility character. Her Ultimate, "Zone Suppression," provides a massive 40% AoE DEF shred (at Level 10). When equipped with the "Before the Tutorial Mission Starts" Light Cone or "Resolution Shines As Pearls of Sweat," she becomes a Skill Point (SP) positive machine that can maintain 100% uptime on her debuffs. Her utility in Pure Fiction is particularly high due to the AoE nature of her shred.

B-Tier units are reliable but usually require higher investment or specific elemental weaknesses to compete with S and A-Tier characters.

### Situational Specialists: When to use Guinaifen and Luka
*   **Guinaifen (Fire):** She is a solid DoT sub-DPS who applies the "Firekiss" debuff, increasing the damage enemies take by up to 21% (at 3 stacks). She is an excellent F2P alternative for Kafka/Black Swan teams or as a third Nihility for Acheron.
*   **Sampo (Wind):** While largely overshadowed by Black Swan, Sampo remains a strong option for Pure Fiction due to his multi-hit Skill and Wind Shear application. His Ultimate also increases DoT damage taken by enemies by 30%.
