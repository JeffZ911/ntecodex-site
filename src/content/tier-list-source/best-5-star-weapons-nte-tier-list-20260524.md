## Methodology

Ranking Arcs in NTE requires looking beyond simple damage numbers. Because the game utilizes a complex elemental and state-based combat system, an Arc’s value is determined by how it interacts with an Esper's specific kit and the team's overall synergy.

### Understanding Arc Types and Synergy

Every Arc in the game is categorized into one of five distinct types. Matching the Arc type to the Esper's natural affinity is crucial for unlocking passive bonuses and ensuring optimal stat scaling.

### Stat Priorities: CRIT vs. Charge Efficiency

For most S-Rank Arcs, the primary draw is the massive Base ATK stat, which typically ranges from 500 to over 600 at maximum level. However, the secondary substat often dictates the Arc's longevity in the meta. Currently, Arcs featuring CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG remain the most coveted for primary DPS units like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte). Conversely, supports rely heavily on Charge Efficiency to ensure their Ultimates are available on every rotation, especially in timed Anomaly Commissions.

### The Role of Signature Arcs in the Meta

"Signature Arcs" are weapons specifically designed to complement the kit of a featured 5-star character. While generalist Arcs like [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-24] provide solid stat sticks, signature Arcs often feature "Arc Skills" that fundamentally change how a character plays. These skills can include summoning autonomous units like Officer Whisker or creating environmental zones like the Silent Garden, which provides field-wide buffs or enemy debuffs.

### F2P Accessibility

While gacha-exclusive Arcs from the "Arc Research Program" offer the highest ceiling, NTE provides several farmable S-Rank alternatives. Players can acquire high-tier gear through the City Tycoon progression system (unlocked after reaching certain business milestones) and the Bureau of Anomaly Control rewards.
