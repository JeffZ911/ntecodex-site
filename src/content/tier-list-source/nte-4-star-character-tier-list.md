Maximizing your roster in Neverness to Everness (NTE) requires a deep understanding of how A-Rank characters—commonly referred to as 4-stars—complement the heavy-hitting S-Rank units. While S-Rank characters like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) often dominate the spotlight, the launch meta of Version 1.0.5 has proven that several A-Rank units are indispensable for clearing high-level Anomaly zones and optimizing the "Esper Cycle" combat system. This guide breaks down the current standings of all 4-star units as of May 2026.

## Methodology

![Methodology](/img/nte-4-star-character-tier-list/inline-1.webp)


Ranking characters in NTE involves more than just looking at raw damage output. Because the game blends high-octane combat with deep urban simulation, a character's value is determined by their combat efficiency, elemental synergy, and "Life Skills" that assist in open-world activities like vehicle customization and business management.

The following criteria were used to determine the rankings for the Version 1.0.5 "Hethereau Launch" meta:

*   **Esper Cycle Synergy:** How effectively the unit generates energy and transitions between team members to trigger resonance effects.
*   **Elemental Application:** The reliability of applying Cosmos, Anima, Psyche, Incantation, or Lakshana statuses to trigger elemental reactions.
*   **F2P Viability:** Performance levels when equipped with standard Arcs (weapons) rather than premium, character-specific Arcs.
*   **Life Skill Utility:** The tangible benefits the character provides while exploring Hethereau, such as discounts on car parts or bonuses to urban exploration stamina.

### Combat Efficiency vs. Utility

In the current meta, utility often outweighs pure DPS for A-Rank units. Since most players will focus their primary investments on an S-Rank "Main Carry," the 4-stars who provide buffs, shields, or crowd control tend to sit higher on the tier list. A unit that can stay off-field while providing a 20% boost to the active Esper's damage is significantly more valuable than an A-Rank DPS who requires 100% field time but produces 30% less damage than a standard S-Rank.

### The Importance of Life Skills in the Open World

NTE is unique because character progression is tied to the city itself. Life Skills are passive bonuses that activate when a character is in your active exploration party. For example, some characters reduce the cost of upgrading your vehicle’s engine, while others allow you to find rare materials in the Bridge District more easily. A character might be B-Tier in combat but S-Tier for your overall account progression due to these economic advantages.

## S Tier

![S Tier](/img/nte-4-star-character-tier-list/inline-2.webp)


S-Tier A-Rank units are often described as "S-Ranks in disguise." They possess kits that remain relevant regardless of the power creep introduced by new banners, primarily because they offer unique mechanics that cannot be easily replaced.

### Haniel: The Universal Buffer

Haniel has solidified her spot as the most important A-Rank unit in the game. As a Psyche-element support, her primary role is to enhance the damage of your primary DPS. Her kit revolves around her "Resonance Field," which provides a flat damage increase to any ally standing within its radius.

*   **Element:** Psyche
*   **Combat Role:** Buff/Support
*   **Key Synergy:** Pairs perfectly with Nanally to maximize the "Ichi-daime" burst window.
*   **Buff Percentage:** [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-14]

Haniel was distributed as a 30 million pre-registration milestone reward, meaning almost every player has access to her. Her ability to shred Psyche resistance makes her a staple in any team looking to tackle the late-game Anomaly challenges.

### Adler: Master of Incantation Cycles

Adler brings unparalleled crowd control to the table. Using his signature Cane Sword, he can pull enemies into a central vortex, allowing your DPS to clear waves instantly. His "Cycle Rate" generation is currently the highest among all A-Rank units, meaning he allows your team to use their Ultimate abilities more frequently.

*   **Element:** Incantation
*   **Combat Role:** Support/Crowd Control
*   **Optimal Arc Weapon:** [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-14]

Adler is particularly effective in urban combat scenarios where enemies are spread out across street corners or rooftops. His Life Skill also provides a bonus to "Information Gathering," which speeds up the completion of side quests in Hethereau.

## A Tier

![A Tier](/img/nte-4-star-character-tier-list/inline-3.webp)


A-Tier units are highly capable and can serve as the backbone of your team. They are excellent investments for F2P players who need reliable damage or survival tools.

### Mint: The F2P Speedster

As the poster girl of Neverness to Everness, Mint is designed to be the player's first reliable Main DPS. Her Anima-based kit focuses on rapid strikes and high mobility. Her "Thunderous Whirlwind" ability is a multi-hit AoE tornado that can clear smaller mobs with ease.

*   **Element:** Anima
*   **Combat Role:** Main DPS
*   **Life Skill:** Vehicle modification discount.

Mint is especially useful for players who enjoy the vehicle customization aspect of NTE. Having her in your party provides a significant reduction in the cost of performance parts.

## B Tier

![B Tier](/img/nte-4-star-character-tier-list/inline-4.webp)


B-Tier units are situational. They perform well in specific encounters but are generally outclassed by other units who perform the same role more efficiently.

### Edgar: The Niche Cosmos Striker

Edgar is a Cosmos-element damage dealer who excels in single-target boss fights. However, he faces a major hurdle: the main protagonist (Esper Zero) also uses the Cosmos element and is provided for free with guaranteed progression materials.

*   **Element:** Cosmos
*   **Combat Role:** Single-Target Damage
*   **Comparison:** Edgar provides higher burst damage than Esper Zero in short windows, but lacks the sustained utility and "Break" potential that the protagonist offers.

Edgar requires a high-tier Arc weapon to truly compete with S-Rank damage dealers. Without a high investment in his "Cycle Efficiency" stats, his long cooldowns make him feel sluggish in the fast-paced Esper Cycle meta.
