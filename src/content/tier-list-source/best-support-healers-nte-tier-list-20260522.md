The criteria for this tier list focus on the shifting demands of the post-launch environment. As players move into endgame content like the "Mirage Distortions," the efficiency of a support’s kit is scrutinized under several high-level performance metrics.

*   **Utility and Crowd Control (CC):** Mechanics such as enemy grouping, time-manipulation, and damage mitigation (shields) are weighted heavily.
*   **Accessibility:** We consider the investment required, comparing free-to-play (F2P) friendly A-Rank units against the high-cost requirements of S-Class limited banner characters.
