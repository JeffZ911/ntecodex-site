# Best 5-Star NTE Weapons: The Ultimate Neverness to Everness Arc Tier List
> 📝 **Editorial Note:** This guide is AI-assisted and game data evolves rapidly. Please cross-reference with in-game information. _Updated: 2026-05-22._


Since the official release of Neverness to Everness (NTE) on April 29, 2026, the meta has rapidly evolved around the "Arc" system. These specialized weapon disks are the primary method for enhancing an Esper's combat potential, providing massive stat boosts and transformative passive effects. Unlike traditional weapon systems, Arcs are categorized by their physical states—Plasma, Solid, Gas, Liquid, and Synthesis—each interacting differently with the game's attribute system.

This tier list ranks the current S-Rank (5-star) Arcs available in the Version 1.1 "Scarborough Fair" update. We evaluate these weapons based on their versatility, their synergy with top-tier Espers like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) and Haniel, and their performance in high-level endgame content like the Tower and urban Anomaly hunts.

### Understanding Arc Stats and Substats

Every Arc in NTE comes with a primary stat (Base ATK) and a secondary substat. For 5-star Arcs, these substats are significantly higher than their 4-star counterparts. In the current meta, the most sought-after substats are those that complement the Esper's "Resonance" or "Attribute" damage. While Base ATK is important for early-game progression, the percentage-based scaling of S-Rank Arcs becomes the deciding factor in clearing time-limited challenges.

### Signature vs. Universal Weapons

Signature Arcs are released alongside specific character banners, such as Nanally’s banner, "The Ichi-daime." These weapons are designed to maximize a specific Esper's kit. However, universal Arcs—often found in the standard "Arc Research Program"—provide more flexibility for players who frequently swap their team compositions.
