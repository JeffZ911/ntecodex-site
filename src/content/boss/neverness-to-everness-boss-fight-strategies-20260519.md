## Attack Patterns

![Attack Patterns](/img/neverness-to-everness-boss-fight-strategies-20260519/inline-2.webp)


NTE bosses communicate their next moves through two primary visual indicators: the "Double Red Circle" and the "Red Flash." Recognizing these cues is the difference between a successful counter-attack and a team wipe.

### Arachne Phase 1: Puppet Management
In the initial phase, Arachne remains stationary while summoning puppets that generate circular shockwaves. These waves move outward at high speeds and can overlap, creating a "bullet hell" scenario.
*   **Strategy:** Focus on the puppets first. Wait for the circular wave to approach, then use a Critical Riposte (timed dodge) to close the distance. Avoid staying in the center of the arena, as the overlapping waves are hardest to dodge there.

### Arachne Phase 2: Slash Combos and Parries
Once the puppets are defeated, Arachne enters the field directly. She utilizes rapid 3-hit and 4-hit slash combos.
*   **The Double Red Circle:** This is your window for a Character Swap Parry. When the circles align, swap to your defensive or Break specialist to instantly deplete a large portion of her Break Meter.
*   **The Red Flash:** This indicates an unblockable grab or heavy slam. You must dodge away from the boss; attempting to parry a Red Flash will result in a stagger and heavy damage.

### Arachne Phase 3: The Spotlight Mechanic
In her final phase, the arena darkens, and two spotlights appear—one tracking the player and one tracking the boss.
*   **Spotlight Safe Zone:** You must remain within your designated spotlight. Leaving the light for more than three seconds applies the "Freeze" status effect, leaving you vulnerable to Arachne's teleportation strikes. The boss will frequently teleport out of your reach, so save your gap-closing skills for when she reappears inside the illuminated area.

### Black Tome Mechanics
The Black Tome relies on immobilization and long-range suppression.
*   **Chain Grabs:** The boss fires ethereal chains that can hold up to three characters simultaneously. If your active character is caught, you are forced to swap to a teammate.
*   **Linear Lasers:** It fires high-velocity beams in a straight line. These are preceded by a thin purple tracking line. Wait for the line to stop moving before dodging.

### Justice Executioner Patterns
This boss utilizes vehicle-based transformation attacks. It can shift into a high-speed pursuit mode, attempting to ram the player.
*   **Red Flash Jump-Slam:** When the Executioner leaps into the air, a large red circle appears on the ground. This attack cannot be parried. You must time your dodge for the exact moment of impact to trigger a Time Slow effect, which is the only way to safely land hits on this high-level target.

## Step-by-Step Strategy

![Step-by-Step Strategy](/img/neverness-to-everness-boss-fight-strategies-20260519/inline-3.webp)


The core of NTE combat is the "Break Loop"—a cycle of building pressure, shattering defenses, and unleashing ultimates.

### Maximizing the Break Window
Your primary goal is to deplete the white stability bar (Break Meter) located under the boss’s health. 
1.  **Build Meter:** Use Basic Attack strings to build your Esper Cycle meter.
2.  **Parry/Dodge:** Successfully parrying a "Double Red Circle" attack provides a massive boost to Break damage.
3.  **Break:** Once the bar is empty, the boss enters a "Broken" state, becoming immobile and taking significantly more damage.
4.  **Ultimate Burst:** Save your character Ultimates specifically for this window. Using an Ultimate while the boss is active is often a waste, as they can dash out of the animation range.

### Triggering Trio Elemental Reactions
For bosses with high health pools, dual reactions aren't enough. You should aim for Trio Cycles to maximize DPS.
*   **The Discord Loop:** Combine **Incantation + Chaos + Psyche**. This triggers the "Discord" effect, which continuously drains the enemy's Break bar even when you aren't attacking. This is particularly effective against the Black Tome and Mammon.
*   **The Charge Loop:** Combine **Cosmos + Anima + Lakshana**. This builds the "Blossom" turret while simultaneously applying "Remora" (Slow). This allows you to stay offensive while the boss's movements are hindered.

### Managing Stamina
Aggressive attacking consumes stamina, as does dodging. If you run out of stamina during a boss's enrage phase, you will be unable to avoid "Red Flash" attacks. Always weave in two to three Basic Attacks between dodges to allow your stamina bar to partially recover.

### Top Tier: The Nanally Hypercarry Team
This team focuses on the "Blossom" meta. By combining Nanally (Anima) with Esper Zero (Cosmos), you trigger autonomous turrets that deal AoE damage. 
*   **Nanally:** Acts as the primary on-field DPS, using her "Ichi-daime's Authority" state to land rapid electromagnetic hits.
