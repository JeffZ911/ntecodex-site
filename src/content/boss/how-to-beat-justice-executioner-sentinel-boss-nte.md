# How to Beat the Justice Executioner in Neverness to Everness (Sentinel Boss)
> ⚠️ **Notice:** Auto-generated content. May contain approximations or minor inaccuracies in supporting details. Help us improve via the comment section below. _Last reviewed: 2026-05-16._


The Justice Executioner is the most formidable hidden boss in the version 1.0 release of Neverness to Everness (NTE). Unlike standard world bosses or Anomaly Commissions found in the open world, this mechanical behemoth does not have a fixed spawn point. Instead, it serves as the final deterrent of the Hethereau Security Unit, appearing only when a player reaches the maximum Wanted Level. Defeating it requires not only high-level gear and optimized Esper Cycles but also a deep understanding of the Psyche-based "Discord" meta.

## Boss Stats

![Boss Stats](/img/how-to-beat-justice-executioner-sentinel-boss-nte/inline-1.webp)


The Justice Executioner is a mechanical Anomaly that scales far beyond the typical level caps of the early game. While players might encounter it as early as Appraisal Level 20, the boss itself is locked at Level 99, making it a "gear check" encounter designed for the late game or for players who have mastered the game's parrying and dodging mechanics.

### Level Scaling and Difficulty

Because the Justice Executioner is a fixed Level 99 encounter, it possesses massive defensive modifiers against lower-level characters. Players below Level 60 will find that their Physical and Incantation damage is significantly mitigated by the boss's heavy plating. The primary challenge lies in the Break gauge; the boss has one of the largest white bars in the game, requiring high Break Intensity stats to stagger.

### Elemental Weaknesses

The Justice Executioner is specifically vulnerable to the Psyche element. In the current NTE elemental system, Psyche damage bypasses the "Mechanical Hardening" buff that the boss maintains throughout the fight. Using a Psyche-heavy team is essential because it triggers the **Discord** reaction when combined with Chaos and Incantation elements. Discord is the most effective way to deplete the Break gauge, as it applies a "Cognitive Overload" debuff that increases the effectiveness of all subsequent Break damage by 25%.

Conversely, Physical-based teams will struggle. The boss’s armor reduces incoming Physical DMG by 60% unless the unit is in a "Broken" state. Therefore, your rotation should focus entirely on reaching the stagger window as quickly as possible using elemental reactions.

## Attack Patterns

![Attack Patterns](/img/how-to-beat-justice-executioner-sentinel-boss-nte/inline-2.webp)


The fight is divided into two distinct phases. The Justice Executioner begins as a high-speed pursuit vehicle before transforming into a multi-legged mechanical spider. Understanding the telegraphs for each phase is the difference between a successful clear and a trip to the Hethereau Detention Facility.

### Phase 1: The Novus Enforcer Form

In its initial state, the boss resembles a heavily armed security interceptor. It relies on high-speed kinetic attacks and area-denial tools.

*   **Enforcer Charge:** The boss revs its engine and flashes a bright blue light before charging directly at the player. This move has high tracking. You should dodge toward the boss at the last second to trigger a Critical Dodge, allowing for an immediate counter-attack.
*   **Siren Blast:** A wide-range sonic wave emitted from the boss's roof-mounted speakers. This attack deals low damage but causes a "Stun" effect and drains 15% of your active character's Esper Gauge. To avoid this, watch for the red circular AoE indicator and jump or dodge outside the radius.
*   **Oil Slick / Thermal Trap:** The boss drops canisters that create persistent fire or slowing zones on the ground. Stay mobile and avoid being cornered near city walls.

### Phase 2: The Mechanical Nightmare

Once the boss reaches 60% HP or the first Break window ends, it transforms. It deploys four mechanical legs and becomes much more vertical in its movement.

*   **Grid Lockdown:** The boss slams its central chassis into the ground, creating a digital grid across the floor. Safe zones appear as blue squares within the grid. Standing in the red squares will deal continuous Psyche damage and significantly slow your movement speed.
*   **Predictive Targeting (Railgun):** A red laser sight will lock onto your character. After three seconds, the boss fires a high-velocity railgun shot. This is the best opportunity for a **Parry**. If you time your Parry perfectly, you will reflect a portion of the damage back into the boss's Break gauge, significantly accelerating the stagger process.
*   **Hethereau Reinforcements:** At 50% HP, the Executioner summons four Level 90 Security Drones. While these drones are active, the boss gains a "Shielding Protocol" that reduces all incoming damage by 40%. You must eliminate the drones immediately using AoE abilities from characters like [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) or Aurelia.

### Ultimate Move: Absolute Justice Protocol

When the boss drops below 20% HP, it initiates its ultimate sequence. The sky turns a deep violet, and the boss begins charging a massive energy sphere. You have roughly 15 seconds to deplete the remaining Break gauge. Failure to stagger the boss during this window results in a map-wide explosion that typically results in an instant party wipe for characters under Level 80.

## Step-by-Step Strategy

![Step-by-Step Strategy](/img/how-to-beat-justice-executioner-sentinel-boss-nte/inline-3.webp)


Beating the Justice Executioner requires more than just high stats; it requires a specific sequence of actions to even begin the encounter.

### Triggering the 4-Star Wanted Level

To fight the boss, you must first reach the maximum Heat Level in Hethereau.
1.  **Start the Pursuit:** Attack a Security Officer or steal a civilian vehicle to reach 1 star.
2.  **Escalate:** Destroy city property, such as lamp posts or bus stops, and engage the initial responding drones to reach 2 and 3 stars.
3.  **The Final Star:** Once you reach 3 stars, high-speed interceptors and airborne units will spawn. Continue dealing damage to these units without leaving the "Pursuit Zone."
4.  **The Spawn:** When the 4th star lights up, the screen will flicker with digital noise, and the Justice Executioner will drop from the sky, instantly replacing all other pursuit units.

### Managing the Break Gauge

The most efficient way to win is the **Discord Esper Cycle**. Your rotation should look like this:
1.  Apply an **Incantation** mark using Sakiri’s Support Skill.
2.  Switch to **Fadia** to apply Psyche damage, triggering the **Nova** reaction.
3.  Switch to **Daffodill** and use her Ultimate. Because Daffodill is a Chaos-element Breaker, hitting a Nova-marked enemy triggers **Discord**, which shreds the boss's Break gauge.

### Survival Tips for Low-Level Players

If you are attempting this fight while underleveled, you may want to utilize the **"[Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) Camera Bug."** This is a known exploit as of May 2026 where players open the in-game Camera feature immediately after a boss's attack animation begins. In certain versions, this freezes the boss's AI while allowing the player's active dots (Damage over Time) or summons like Officer Whisker to continue dealing damage. Note that developers are expected to patch this in the version 1.1 update.

## Recommended Team

![Recommended Team](/img/how-to-beat-justice-executioner-sentinel-boss-nte/inline-4.webp)


The current "Psyche Meta" is the gold standard for this fight. Because the boss is mechanical and weak to Psyche, characters that can manipulate the Break gauge are prioritized.

### Best High-End Team (Psyche Focus)

*   **Daffodill (Main Breaker):** Her passive ability increases Break Intensity by 20% against mechanical targets. She is the primary engine for depleting the boss's white bar.
*   **Sakiri (Buffer/Sub-DPS):** Provides the Incantation element needed for Discord reactions and offers a 15% ATK buff to the entire team.
*   **Fadia (Sub-DPS):** Essential for her Mental damage scaling, which ignores the boss's Physical defense. Her "Redirection" skill also helps absorb some of the boss's high-damage railgun hits.
*   **Aurelia (Healer/Support):** Provides sustained healing and can use her jellyfish summons to distract the Phase 2 drones.

### Best F2P Team Composition

If you haven't pulled the S-Rank Psyche units, you can still succeed with a well-built F2P squad:
*   **Esper Zero (Main DPS):** Focused on high-frequency attacks to trigger as many parries as possible.
*   **Chiz (Sub-DPS):** Used primarily for her elemental application to help chip away at the Break gauge.
*   **Mint (Support):** Her healing is vital for surviving the chip damage from the boss's Grid Lockdown attack.

### Key Character Roles

In this fight, **Daffodill** is the undisputed MVP. Her "Still Waters" passive allows her to ignore a portion of the boss's defense during the Break window. When paired with **Hethereau's Keeper** (the Arc dropped by the boss itself upon your first win), she becomes capable of solo-clearing the encounter in subsequent runs.

## Loot

![Loot](/img/how-to-beat-justice-executioner-sentinel-boss-nte/inline-5.webp)


The rewards for defeating the Justice Executioner are among the best in the game's launch version, providing a significant power spike for any account.

### S-Rank Arc: Hethereau's Keeper

This is a guaranteed drop for the first time you defeat the boss and "confess" your crimes at a police station afterward. It is a Solid-type Arc that is best-in-slot for many ATK-scaling characters.

| Level | Base ATK | ATK % | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | 42 | 5.0% | Increases Boss DMG by 15% |
| 40 | 420 | 16.5% | Summons Officer Whisker (100% ATK) |
| 90 | 512 | 27.5% | Summons Officer Whisker (150% ATK) |

The **Officer Whisker** summon is particularly useful for solo players, as the feline officer follows you and fires rapid-fire projectiles at nearby enemies for 30 seconds, providing consistent off-field DPS.

### Ascension Materials and Currency

Beyond the Arc, the boss drops high-grade materials necessary for Level 90+ character ascensions:
*   **Anomaly Shards (S-Rank):** 5–10 per clear.
*   **Polished Consoles:** Used for upgrading mechanical-themed Arcs.
*   **Fons:** 50,000+ (This is the largest single-source Fons drop in the game).
*   **Miracle Box Keys:** A high chance (approx. 30%) to drop a key for the high-tier loot boxes found in the Bureau of Anomaly Control.

### Achievements and Titles

Clearing the Justice Executioner for the first time unlocks the **"Most Wanted"** title, which can be displayed on your player profile. You will also receive the **"Justice Served"** hidden achievement, which rewards 60 Annuliths (premium currency).
