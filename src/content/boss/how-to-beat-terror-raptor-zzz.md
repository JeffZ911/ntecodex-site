# How to Beat Terror Raptor in Zenless Zone Zero: Boss Guide

The Terror Raptor is a specialized combat machine belonging to the Rebel Faction, designed for high-mobility aerial and ground assaults. Encountering this boss usually means dealing with erratic movement, projectile barrages, and a frustrating flight phase that can waste precious seconds in timed challenges. Success hinges on exploiting its mechanical nature and mastering the gold-glint parry windows to force it into a Groggy state.

## Boss Stats

![Boss Stats](/img/how-to-beat-terror-raptor-zzz/inline-1.webp)


The Terror Raptor is classified as a Machine-type enemy. This classification is the most important factor when choosing your Agents, as it dictates which elemental anomalies will be most effective. Its base stats scale significantly with Inter-Knot level, making it a formidable opponent in the endgame.

### Attribute Weaknesses and Resistances

*   **Primary Weakness: Electric.** As a machine, the Terror Raptor is highly susceptible to Shock. Inflicting the Shock anomaly interrupts its actions and deals additional damage over time.
*   **Secondary Weakness: Ether.** This is specifically relevant for the Miasmic Terror Raptor variant often found in late-game Hollow Zero or Shiyu Defense.
*   **Resistances: Fire.** The Raptor features high thermal insulation, making Fire-based teams less efficient.
*   **Defense: Physical.** While it has no innate resistance to Physical damage, its high base defense stats mean Physical Agents require significant penetration investment to maintain high DPS.

### Terror Raptor Variants: Miasmic vs. Standard

The Standard Terror Raptor is the version encountered in early story missions and lower-tier Combat Simulations. It focuses on physical strikes and missile volleys. The Miasmic variant, however, introduces Ether-based corruption mechanics. The Miasmic version has higher aggressive tracking and can deploy specialized drones that provide constant fire support, making the arena much more hazardous.

### Understanding the Daze Bar and Groggy Thresholds

Like all major bosses in Zenless Zone Zero, the Terror Raptor has a Daze bar located directly beneath its HP. Filling this bar triggers the Groggy state. During Groggy, the Raptor becomes immobile for a set duration and takes 200% damage (plus any additional multipliers from your Stun Agents). Because the Raptor moves so quickly, your primary goal is to fill this bar as fast as possible to create a window for your Attack Agents to dump their energy.

## Attack Patterns

![Attack Patterns](/img/how-to-beat-terror-raptor-zzz/inline-2.webp)


The Terror Raptor relies on its "agile legs" and integrated weapon systems to overwhelm players. Recognizing the visual cues for its attacks is the difference between an S-Rank clear and a failed run.

### Melee Combos and Cues

*   **Mechanical Kick:** The Raptor shifts its weight to one side before delivering a swift horizontal strike. This is usually preceded by a **Red Glint**. If you are close to the boss, it may follow this with a drop kick that has a slight delay.
*   **Leap Dash:** The boss jumps back and then lunges across the arena. This covers the entire distance of the battlefield. Look for the **Red Glint** the moment its feet touch the ground after the back-hop.
*   **Spinning Sweep:** A 360-degree tail and leg sweep used when the player stays behind the boss for too long.

### Ranged Weaponry and Projectile Management

*   **Multi-Missile Launch:** The Raptor's back panels open to fire 4 to 6 homing missiles. These do not all land at once. You must perform sequential dodges or use a character with high mobility to circle the boss.
*   **Flame Ball / Special Bomb:** The Raptor charges a glowing projectile in its beak. This is the most important attack to watch for because it features a **Gold Glint**. This indicates a Parrying Support opportunity. Parrying this projectile reflects a portion of the energy back, dealing massive Daze damage.

### The Drone Support Phase

In its Miasmic or high-level variants, the Terror Raptor will deploy six small drones. These drones hover around the perimeter and fire automated lasers. 
*   **Drone Fire:** The lasers are weak but cause "flinch," which can interrupt your EX Special Attacks.
*   **Aerial Invincibility:** During this phase, the Raptor often flies out of reach. While it is airborne, it is immune to most ground-based melee attacks. You must focus on dodging the drones and the Raptor's dive-bombs until it returns to the ground.

## Step-by-Step Strategy

![Step-by-Step Strategy](/img/how-to-beat-terror-raptor-zzz/inline-3.webp)


To beat the Terror Raptor effectively, you must transition from a defensive "reaction" mindset to an aggressive "interruption" mindset.

### Phase 1: Neutral Game and Daze Buildup

At the start of the fight, the Raptor is highly mobile. Do not chase it aimlessly. Instead, stay in the center of the arena and wait for its Leap Dash or Mechanical Kick. 
1.  **Bait the Melee:** Stay within medium range to encourage the Raptor to use its kick or sweep. 
2.  **Stun Agent Priority:** Use Agents like Anby or Qingyi to build Daze. Their Basic Attack strings and EX Special Attacks have high Daze multipliers.
3.  **The Gold Glint Opportunity:** The moment you see the Gold Glint for the Flame Ball, switch to your Support or Defense Agent to trigger a Parrying Support. This is the fastest way to deplete the Daze bar.

### Phase 2: Managing the Aerial Flight and Drones

Once the Raptor reaches approximately 70% HP, it will likely take to the skies.
*   **Stop Attacking:** Unless you are using a ranged Agent like Rina or Billy, do not waste energy trying to hit the Raptor in the air.
*   **Dodge the Dive:** The Raptor will eventually dive back down with a Red Glint. Performing a Perfect Dodge here allows for a Dodge Counter that can transition directly back into your Stun rotation.
*   **Ignore Drones:** In most cases, destroying the drones is a waste of time. Focus your camera on the Raptor itself; the drones will disappear once the boss enters a Groggy state.

### Maximizing Damage During Groggy State

Once the Daze bar is full, the Raptor will collapse. This is your "Burst Window."
1.  **Initiate Chain Attacks:** Trigger the Chain Attack sequence. Ensure your Support Agent (like Nicole or Rina) goes before your main Attack Agent to apply buffs or Shred (Defense reduction).
2.  **Dump EX Specials:** Use all stored Energy for EX Special Attacks.
3.  **Ultimate Timing:** If your Ultimate is ready, use it now. The 200% Groggy multiplier is essential for meeting S-Rank time requirements in Shiyu Defense.

### Checklist for S-Rank Shiyu Defense
*   Finish the encounter in under 2 minutes and 30 seconds.
*   Trigger at least two Groggy states.
*   Successfully perform at least three Parrying Supports on the Flame Ball.
*   Avoid being hit by the missile salvo more than twice (to keep health above the 80% threshold if required).
