Approaching an Elite encounter requires a structured rotation to maximize efficiency. Following a "Setup -> Break -> Burst" flow is the standard for high-level play.

### Phase 1: The Setup and Buffing

Do not lead with your main damage dealer. Instead, initiate combat with a Support or Shield character. Characters like [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-16] are ideal for this phase. Your goal is to:

Once the buffs are active, switch to your "Breaker" specialist. The most effective way to shred the Break Gauge is by triggering elemental reactions. *   Consistently applying the element the boss is weak to.
*   Watching for parry opportunities to deal chunks of Break damage.

### Phase 3: Maximizing the Stagger Window Burst

When the white bar is fully depleted, the Elite will enter the "Broken" or "Staggered" state. This is your window to deal 70-80% of your total damage.
1.  **Switch to your Main DPS:** Characters like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) or [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) should be brought in immediately.

3.  **Use Chain Skills:** Many characters have "Switch Skills" that trigger extra damage when they enter the field while an enemy is Broken.
