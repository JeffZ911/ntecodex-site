# How to Beat the First Major Boss in Neverness to Everness: The Never-Ending Arachne

The Never-Ending Arachne serves as the final encounter of the game's third story chapter and subsequently becomes the first Weekly Boss (Anomaly Pilgrimage) available in the New Herland District. This encounter is a significant difficulty spike that tests your ability to manage the Break bar and execute precise parries. Unlike standard street anomalies, this boss features a three-phase structure that requires both crowd management and high-burst DPS.

## Boss Stats

![Boss Stats](/img/how-to-beat-first-boss-neverness-to-everness/inline-1.webp)


The Never-Ending Arachne is a Class 5 Anomaly that scales with your Appraiser Level. While its base mechanics remain the same, its HP pool and damage output increase significantly at higher World Levels.

### Location and Unlock Requirements

To encounter the Never-Ending Arachne, you must progress the main story until you reach Episode 3: "Deal? Deal!" The fight takes place at the Ebisu Auction House within the Unheard Shores district. Once defeated in the story, the boss arena becomes a permanent challenge node located in the bottom-right sector of the New Herland District.

*   **Unlock Requirement:** Complete Episode 3: "Deal? Deal!"
*   **Challenge Cost:** 60 Character Pixels (to claim rewards).
*   **Weekly Limit:** 3 reward claims per week.

## Attack Patterns

![Attack Patterns](/img/how-to-beat-first-boss-neverness-to-everness/inline-2.webp)


Understanding the visual cues for the Arachne’s attacks is vital for survival. The game uses a specific "Red Shine" and "Closing Circles" system to telegraph incoming high-damage moves.

### Phase 1: The Puppet Dance

In this initial stage, the Never-Ending Arachne remains on the elevated auction stage, making her untargetable. Instead, you must face several dancing puppets on the ballroom floor.

*   **Circular Shockwaves:** Every few seconds, the puppets will pulse with energy, sending out circular shockwaves across the floor. You must time your dash to perform a Critical Dodge.
*   **Spinning Lunge:** One puppet will occasionally be marked with a Red Spark before spinning toward you at high speed. This is the primary window for a Critical Riposte.
*   **Lock-On Laser:** A puppet may target you with a red laser sight. After a two-second delay, it fires a continuous beam. Keep moving in a circle to avoid the damage.

### Phase 2: The Weaver's Slashes

Once the puppets are cleared, the Arachne descends to the floor. This phase is a direct melee confrontation.

*   **Melee Slash Combo:** The boss performs 3 to 4 consecutive slashes with her leg-blades. Note that only the final hit usually features the Red Shine telegraph.
*   **Closing Red Circles (Parry Window):** The boss will occasionally pull back her blades as two red circles close in on her. This is a Parryable Attack. Landing a Basic Attack or Skill exactly when the circles overlap will deal massive Break damage and stun the boss.
*   **Thread Pull:** The boss jumps backward and pulls invisible threads, dragging your character toward her for a follow-up explosion. Dash away immediately when you see the "vacuum" effect.

### Phase 3: The Enraged Anomaly

At 40% HP, the arena dims, and the boss enters her enraged state. This phase introduces the Spotlight mechanic.

*   **The Spotlight:** Two spotlights appear—one on the boss and one on your character. You must remain within your moving spotlight to avoid being frozen.
*   **Teleportation Lunges:** The Arachne will vanish and reappear instantly behind you, performing a Red Shine lunge. You must be ready to parry or dodge the moment she reappears.
*   **Floor Waves:** The boss slams the ground, sending out three waves of energy in rapid succession. These are best handled by jumping or using an Ultimate ability for the invincibility frames.

## Step-by-Step Strategy

![Step-by-Step Strategy](/img/how-to-beat-first-boss-neverness-to-everness/inline-3.webp)


Success against the Never-Ending Arachne relies on the "Break and Burst" cycle. You cannot win this fight by chip damage alone; you must force the boss into a vulnerable state.

### Depleting the Break Bar

The white bar under the boss's HP is your primary target. To deplete it quickly
1.  **Exploit Chaos:** Use Daffodill’s "Still Waters" combo. As a Chaos unit, she deals naturally higher Break damage to this boss.
2.  **Critical Riposte:** When you dodge the Phase 1 shockwaves or Phase 2 slashes, immediately follow up with a Basic Attack. This triggers a Critical Riposte, which deals a fixed, high amount of Break gauge depletion.
3.  **Support Skills:** Swapping characters to use a Support Skill that matches the boss's weakness (Chaos) will provide an additional chunk of Break damage.

### Utilizing Critical Riposte

In Phase 2, the boss is highly aggressive. Do not spam your attacks; wait for the boss to swing first. 
*   **The Timing:** Watch for the red spark on the boss's eyes. 
*   **The Follow-up:** After a successful dodge, your character will glow briefly. Pressing the Basic Attack button now will launch the Riposte. 
*   **The Stagger:** Repeated Ripostes will eventually empty the Break bar. Once it hits zero, the boss will slump over for 4-5 seconds. This is your "Burst Window."

### Surviving the Phase 3 Teleports

Phase 3 is where most players fail due to the Spotlight mechanic. 
*   **Stay in the Light:** If you leave your designated circle for more than three seconds, your character will be frozen, likely leading to a one-shot from the boss's next lunge.
*   **Parry to Win:** The boss teleports frequently in this phase. Instead of chasing her, stand still in your spotlight and wait for her to come to you. She will always perform a parryable lunge after two teleports. Landing this parry is the fastest way to end the fight.

### The Discord Reaction

One of the most effective ways to shred the Break bar is triggering the **Discord** reaction. This occurs when you apply both **Nova** (Chaos + Psyche) and **Scorch** (Incantation + Chaos) to the boss simultaneously.
*   **The Setup:** Use [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) (Incantation) to apply a status, then swap to Haniel (Psyche) to apply the second, and finally bring in Daffodill (Chaos) to trigger the reaction.
*   **The Result:** Discord deals massive AoE Break damage and can often skip half of the Break bar in a single rotation.

### Weekly Reward Caps

You can challenge the boss as many times as you like, but you can only spend **60 Character Pixels** to claim rewards **three times per week**. The rewards reset every Monday at 04:00 server time.

### Farming Dress Sleeves of Vanity

The **Dress Sleeves of Vanity** is the primary "Legendary" drop from this boss. It is a required material for upgrading Esper Abilities (passives) for S-Rank characters like Daffodill and Haniel.

*   **Hunter EXP:** Increases your overall account level.
