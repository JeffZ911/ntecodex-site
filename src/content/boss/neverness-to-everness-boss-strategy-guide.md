## Attack Patterns

![Attack Patterns](/img/neverness-to-everness-boss-strategy-guide/inline-2.webp)


The fight is divided into three distinct phases, each requiring a shift in defensive strategy. Understanding the "Red Shine" cues is the difference between a quick victory and a team wipe.

### Phase 1: The Puppet Master's Dance
Arachne begins the fight perched on the stage of the auction house, remaining invulnerable to direct attacks. Instead, she summons theater puppets to do her bidding.

*   **Circular Shockwaves:** Puppets will slam their feet into the ground, emitting expanding rings of energy. You must jump or dodge through these waves.
*   **String Pull:** Arachne will occasionally fire threads from the ceiling that pull your character toward a puppet currently mid-attack.
*   **Puppet Lunge:** A puppet will glow with a faint red aura before dashing across the stage.

### Phase 2: Blade Symphony and Break Bar
Once the initial puppets are cleared, Arachne descends. This phase is a high-speed melee duel where the Break bar becomes your primary target.

| Attack Name | Visual Cue | Recommended Counter |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Triple Slash | 3 rapid flashes on her blades | Parry first two, Dodge the third |
| Shadow Lunge | Boss vanishes and a red line appears on floor | Critical Dodge (sideways) |
| Web Snare | Boss raises both hands; silk threads hang from ceiling | Sprint away from the center |
| Grand Finale | Red Shine indicator appears above boss's head | Critical Riposte / Heavy Parry |

### Phase 3: The Spotlight Finale
At 30% HP, the arena darkens and two moving spotlights appear on the floor. This is the most dangerous part of the encounter due to the environmental hazards.

*   **Spotlight Mechanic:** You must remain within the circle of a spotlight. Staying in the darkness for more than [information unavailable] seconds applies the "Frozen" status effect, leaving you vulnerable to Arachne's follow-up AoE.
*   **Teleportation Strike:** Arachne will frequently teleport behind the player, immediately followed by a massive AoE wave attack that covers 50% of the arena.
*   **Silk Rain:** Needles fall from the ceiling, targeting the area inside the spotlights, forcing you to constantly micro-adjust your positioning within the safe zone.

## Step-by-Step Strategy

![Step-by-Step Strategy](/img/neverness-to-everness-boss-strategy-guide/inline-3.webp)


### Early Phase: Clearing the Puppets
During Phase 1, do not waste your Esper Abilities on the boss. Focus entirely on the summoned puppets. The key here is to trigger a **Critical Riposte**. By timing a parry against the puppets' lunge attacks, you can trigger a cinematic counter-attack that deals massive damage to all active puppets and forces Arachne to descend to the stage faster.

### Mid-Phase: Breaking Arachne's Defense
In Phase 2, the goal is to deplete the white "Break" bar located under the boss's HP. Using Chaos attribute characters like Daffodil is the most efficient way to achieve this. 
1.  **Parry the Red Shine:** Whenever you see the red visual indicator, use your defensive skill. A successful parry deals significant Break damage.
2.  **Esper Cycle:** Once the bar is empty, Arachne will enter a "Downed" state. This is your window to unleash your full rotation. Use your Psyche and Lakshana abilities in quick succession to trigger the **Nova** reaction, which increases total damage output by 20% for the duration of the stun.

### Final Phase: Surviving the Spotlight
In the final phase, survival is more important than DPS. 
*   **Spotlight Management:** Keep your camera panned out to see where the spotlights are moving. If you are caught in the dark, you will be frozen and likely lose a character to the boss's teleportation strike.
*   **Wait for the Opening:** Arachne teleports so often in this phase that chasing her is counterproductive. Stay in your spotlight and wait for her to come to you. She will eventually perform a parryable lunge. Successfully parrying this in Phase 3 creates a long vulnerability window where the spotlights stop moving, allowing for a safe finish.

**Optimal Combat Rotation for Arachne:**
1.  **Daffodil:** Initiate with Chaos-infused basic attacks to build Break progress.
2.  **Hathor:** Swap in to use Lakshana skills once the Break bar is at 50%.
3.  **Mint:** Use her ultimate to provide a shield and Cycle Rate buff.
4.  **Daffodil:** Return to the field for the final Break and trigger Nova with Hathor's lingering Lakshana stacks.
