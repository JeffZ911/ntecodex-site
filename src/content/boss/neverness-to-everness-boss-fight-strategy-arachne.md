### The Break Bar Mechanic

Arachne features a massive Break Bar located directly beneath her health pool. This bar represents her posture and resistance to heavy damage. While the Break Bar is active, Arachne takes significantly reduced damage and possesses high stagger resistance. Depleting this bar is the primary goal of the fight's first two phases. Once the bar reaches zero, the boss enters a "Broken" state, becoming immobile and vulnerable to massive burst damage for approximately 10 to 12 seconds.

## Attack Patterns

![Attack Patterns](/img/neverness-to-everness-boss-fight-strategy-arachne/inline-2.webp)


The fight is divided into three distinct acts, each themed around a theatrical performance. Understanding the visual "tells" for each phase is the difference between a quick clear and a team wipe.

### Phase 1: Managing the Puppets (The Puppet Waltz)

During the first phase, the Never-Ending Arachne remains off-stage and invulnerable. She summons several dancing puppets into the arena. These puppets do not engage in direct melee combat but instead perform a synchronized waltz that emits circular shockwaves across the floor.

The shockwaves move at high speed and can overlap, creating a "bullet hell" environment. Timing a Critical Dodge through these waves is essential. A successful Critical Dodge not only prevents damage but also triggers a Critical Riposte, which sends a pulse of energy back toward the puppets, draining their shared health pool and Arachne's distant Break Bar.

### Phase 2: Mastering the Parry Windows (The Duel)

Once the puppets are cleared, Arachne descends into the arena for a direct confrontation. This phase is characterized by high-speed mobility and aggressive melee combos. Her primary attack is a 3-4 consecutive slash combo that covers a wide arc.

The key to this phase is identifying the "Red Shine Tells." Before specific heavy attacks, Arachne's blades will flash with a bright red light. This is your cue to perform a Parry or use a Redirect Skill. Successfully parrying these attacks deals massive "Break Damage," allowing you to reach the DPS window much faster.

### Phase 3: The Spotlight Survival Gimmick (The Encore)

When Arachne reaches 40% HP, the arena darkens, and two spotlights appear on the floor. One spotlight follows the player, while the other follows the boss. This is the "Encore" phase, which introduces a lethal environmental mechanic.

Players must stay within their designated spotlight at all times. Leaving the illuminated zone for more than two seconds will rapidly apply a "Chilled" debuff, eventually leading to the Frozen status effect. Being Frozen during this phase usually results in an immediate knockout, as Arachne will teleport to your location for a high-damage execution strike.

## Step-by-Step Strategy

![Step-by-Step Strategy](/img/neverness-to-everness-boss-fight-strategy-arachne/inline-3.webp)


Approaching the Never-Ending Arachne requires a balance of aggression and patience. Follow these steps to ensure a consistent victory.

### Phase 1 Strategy

Focus entirely on the floor. Do not waste your high-cooldown Ultimate skills on the puppets, as they have high damage reduction against standard attacks. Instead, use the puppets' shockwaves to your advantage. By staying near the center and dodging through the waves as they converge, you can trigger multiple Critical Ripostes in a short window. This is the most efficient way to force Arachne onto the stage.

### Phase 2 Strategy

Once Arachne enters the duel, change your focus to aggressive parrying. Characters with high Redirect capabilities are preferred here. Arachne's multi-slash combo is the best time to use these skills. If you miss a parry, do not try to tank the rest of the combo; instead, use a dash to break line-of-sight and reset. 

### Phase 3 Strategy

Prioritize positioning over DPS during the spotlight phase. The spotlights move in a predictable figure-eight pattern. Instead of chasing the boss, stay in your light and wait for Arachne to teleport to you. She will often attempt a parryable strike immediately after teleporting. This allows you to stay safe within the light while still chipping away at her remaining Break Bar.

### Optimal Rotation for the Break Window

Once the Break Bar is depleted, the boss will collapse. This is your window for maximum burst. The optimal rotation involves:
1.  **Buffer Entry:** Switch to [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) to apply ATK buffs.
2.  **Elemental Setup:** Use [Hotori](/characters/hotori-best-team-nte) to apply elemental statuses and trigger the Esper Cycle.
3.  **Main DPS Burst:** Switch to [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte). Use her Time Stop ability to freeze the boss's recovery timer, then unleash her full skill rotation.
4.  **Finisher:** If the boss is still standing, use Chiz to finish with Cosmos damage.

### How to Handle the Frozen Status

If you are caught outside the spotlight and become Frozen, you must rapidly tap the interact key to break free. However, the most effective way to handle this is prevention. Use characters with high movement speed or dashes that cover large distances to ensure you never fall behind the moving spotlight.
