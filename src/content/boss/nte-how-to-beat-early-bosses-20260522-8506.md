### The Headless Rider (Spectral Anomaly)
The Headless Rider is a World Boss found roaming the New Hurland district. As an Anima-element Anomaly, it relies on high mobility and sudden bursts of spectral damage. Players should engage this boss at Level 15 or higher. Its white stagger bar (Break Meter) is particularly susceptible to "Solid" type Arc weapons, which allow you to lock the rider in place for a high-damage window.

### Justice Executioner (The Hethereau Enforcer)
Unlike the spectral threats, the Justice Executioner is a human-adjacent boss triggered by the city's Heat system. When you reach a 4-star "Super Criminal" status, this enforcer spawns to initiate an Anomaly domain. With high physical resistance and a Psyche-element profile, the Executioner punishes players who rely solely on basic attacks without utilizing elemental Esper Cycles.

### The Mourning Star (First Major Story Encounter)
The Mourning Star acts as the gatekeeper for the first major story arc. Located within a distorted reality domain, this Cosmos-element boss features balanced resistances but massive area-of-effect (AoE) potential. Defeating it requires a firm grasp of the "Blossom" reaction (Cosmos + Anima) to maximize damage during its phase transitions.

## Attack Patterns

![Attack Patterns](/img/nte-how-to-beat-early-bosses-20260522-8506/inline-2.webp)


Success in NTE combat is dictated by your ability to read the "Red Flash" cues. These visual indicators signal unblockable or high-damage attacks that, if dodged perfectly, trigger Esper Cycle energy for your team.

### Headless Rider Move Set
The Headless Rider’s combat style is built around hit-and-run tactics.
*   **Spectral Charge:** The Rider leans forward on its motorcycle, and a bright red flash appears. This is an unblockable straight-line dash. You must dodge laterally at the exact moment the flash pulses to gain a Perfect Dodge bonus.
*   **Phantom Scythe Sweep:** A wide, 360-degree AoE attack. The Rider spins its weapon twice. The first hit can be blocked, but the second hit usually requires a jump or a dodge to avoid the knockback.
*   **Blue Mist Teleport:** The boss vanishes into a cloud of azure smoke, reappearing behind the player for a follow-up strike. Watch your mini-map or listen for the engine sound to predict the reappearance point.

### Justice Executioner Move Set
The Executioner uses tactical gear to suppress the player’s movement.
*   **Tactical Lockdown:** The boss fires a projectile that, if it connects, stuns the player for 3 seconds. This move is often preceded by a blue laser sight.
*   **Heavy Baton Slam:** A high-impact physical strike that triggers a Red Flash. Successfully dodging this provides a massive boost to your Break Meter depletion for the next 5 seconds.
*   **Summon Reinforcements:** At 50% HP, the Executioner calls in Hethereau Police drones. These adds should be cleared immediately using an AoE Esper like [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) to prevent being overwhelmed.

### The Mourning Star Phase Transitions
The Mourning Star fight is split into two distinct phases defined by "Reality Warps."
*   **Phase 1 (Starfall):** The boss remains stationary, raining down Cosmos shards. Each impact point is marked on the ground with a purple circle.
*   **Phase 2 (Void Beam):** Upon reaching the 50% HP threshold, the arena shifts into a void space. The boss channels a continuous Void Beam that tracks the player. You must hide behind the floating debris (Anomalous Pillars) to avoid taking lethal damage.

## Step-by-Step Strategy

![Step-by-Step Strategy](/img/nte-how-to-beat-early-bosses-20260522-8506/inline-3.webp)


Each boss encounter requires a specific approach to trigger the fight or manage the environment.

### Strategy: The Headless Rider Chase & Fight
To even begin the fight against the Headless Rider, you must first engage in a high-speed chase through the New Hurland district.
1.  **Locate the Anomaly:** Find the blue spectral trail near the tunnel entrance in New Hurland.
2.  **The Chase:** Use your vehicle to stay within 50 meters of the Rider. If the distance exceeds 100 meters, the Anomaly resets.
3.  **The Ambush:** Once the Rider reaches the dead-end in the industrial zone, it will manifest fully.
4.  **The Fight:** Focus on using Anima-resistant shields (Adler) and chipping away at the Break Meter with Solid-type Arcs. Once the bar is empty, switch to your primary DPS to trigger the "Blossom" reaction.

### Strategy: Surviving the Justice Executioner
The Justice Executioner is a test of your ability to manage crowd control and high-pressure defense.
*   **Triggering the Fight:** Commit crimes or interfere with police activity until your Heat level reaches 4 stars.
*   **Environment Management:** Fight near alleyways to bottleneck the reinforcements.
*   **Break Strategy:** The Executioner has a "Tactical Shield" that reduces incoming damage by [information unavailable]%. Use heavy-hitting Arcs to shatter this shield before attempting an Esper Cycle.

### Strategy: The Mourning Star's Reality Shifts
The Mourning Star is a mechanical check on your team's positioning.
*   **The Break Window:** The Mourning Star has a very short Break window compared to other bosses. You must save your Esper Ultimates for the exact moment the Break state is triggered.
*   **Chaining Reactions:** Use Esper Zero to apply the initial Cosmos mark, then immediately swap to [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) to trigger Blossom. This reaction deals massive damage over time, which is essential for bypassing the boss's high HP pool in Phase 2.
