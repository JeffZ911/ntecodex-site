## Attack Patterns

![Attack Patterns](/img/how-to-beat-nte-world-bosses/inline-2.webp)


Understanding the visual language of NTE is vital for survival. The game utilizes a color-coded telegraph system: purple indicators signify attacks that can be parried or countered using specific Esper abilities, while red indicators signify "Unblockable" attacks that must be avoided via a Perfect Dodge.

### Identifying Red vs. Purple Telegraphs
When a boss prepares a heavy hit, a circular or linear glow will appear on the ground. 
- **Purple Telegraphs:** These are "Resonance Opportunities." If you hit the boss with a Skill or a timed Basic Attack during this window, you deal massive Toughness damage.
- **Red Telegraphs:** These are "Lethal Strikes." Attempting to parry these will usually result in a guard break. You must dodge at the last possible second to trigger a "Time Dilation" effect, allowing for a free counter-attack.

### Morphix's Enraged State Mechanics
Morphix operates in two distinct phases. In Phase 1, it focuses on lunging strikes and "Pollen Bursts." The Pollen Burst is particularly dangerous as it obscures your mini-map and UI, forcing you to rely on visual cues. 

Once Morphix reaches 50% HP, it enters an Enraged State. During this phase, it gains the "Dandelion Storm" ability. This is a sustained AoE attack that pulls all players toward the center of the arena while dealing continuous Wind damage. To survive this, you must either use a movement skill to escape the radius or deploy a shield from a character like Adler.

### Arachne's Web-Slinging Tactics
Arachne’s primary mechanic is the "Silk Trap." She will fire three consecutive web projectiles that root any character they hit. If you are rooted, Arachne follows up with a "Venom Spit" that deals high DoT (Damage over Time). The key to this fight is baiting the Silk Trap and using a Perfect Dodge to trigger the Esper Cycle, which provides a brief window of invulnerability. If the fight exceeds 8 minutes, Arachne enters a "Frenzy" mode, increasing her attack speed by [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-15].

## Step-by-Step Strategy

![Step-by-Step Strategy](/img/how-to-beat-nte-world-bosses/inline-3.webp)


The core of NTE combat is the Esper Cycle. You cannot simply stay on one character and win; you must rotate through your 4-character team to maximize elemental synergy and Stagger potential.

### Mastering the Esper Cycle Rotation
The most effective rotation starts with your Support or Shielder to apply buffs, followed by your Break Specialist to deplete the Toughness bar, and finally your Main DPS.
1. **Opener:** Use Haniel or [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) to deploy ATK buffs or elemental fields.
2. **The Build-Up:** Switch to a character like Jiuyuan to apply Anima stacks.
3. **The Break:** Bring in Daffodill or Fadia. Their skills are specifically designed to shred the white Toughness meter located under the boss's HP bar.
4. **The Punish:** Once the bar is empty, the boss enters a "Stunned" state. This is your window to swap to Baicang or Zero and unleash their Ultimates.

### How to Efficiently Stagger Bosses
The white Toughness bar is the most important UI element in a boss fight. Bosses take 50% less damage while this bar is active. To break it quickly, focus on "Hard Attacks"—these are usually the third or fifth hits in a basic attack string, or specific "Break" skills. Using an element the boss is weak to (e.g., using Incantation against Morphix) will deplete this bar twice as fast.

### Defensive Maneuvers: Parrying and Dodging
While offensive rotations are key, defense wins world boss fights. 
- **Parrying:** If you use an Incantation character like Baicang, his "Counter-Stance" can negate purple attacks and immediately transition into a high-damage strike.
- **Healing:** Do not wait until your HP is red to heal. Edgar’s healing field should be placed near the boss's rear to allow your DPS to stay in the fight without moving away. Adler’s "Karma" stacks can also be consumed to generate a shield that absorbs [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-15] damage based on his Max HP.

## Recommended Team

![Recommended Team](/img/how-to-beat-nte-world-bosses/inline-4.webp)


Building a team in NTE requires looking at the "Elemental Lane"—the connections between characters in your party UI. Neighboring elements can trigger powerful reactions that are essential for high-level world bosses.

### Best Team for Morphix
Since Morphix is weak to Incantation, a team centered around Baicang is the current gold standard.
- **Main DPS:** Baicang (Incantation)
- **Support:** Sakiri (Incantation - provides Resonance buffs)
- **Shielder:** Adler (Lakshana - provides "Karma" shields)
- **Buffer:** Haniel (Cosmos - increases team-wide ATK)
**Synergy:** This team triggers the "Scorch" reaction, which deals percentage-based fire damage over time, effectively bypassing Morphix's high base defense.

### Best Team for Arachne
Arachne requires high Anima application to break her Psyche-based defenses.
- **Main DPS:** Nanally (Anima)
- **Sub-DPS:** Jiuyuan (Anima - applies DoT)
- **Battery:** Zero (Cosmos - recharges team Ultimates)
- **Healer:** Edgar (Anima - provides sustain)
**Synergy:** The "Blossom" reaction triggered by Nanally and Jiuyuan creates small explosions whenever Arachne moves, punishing her for her large hitbox.

### Top F2P Bossing Compositions
For players without multiple S-Rank characters, a reliable "Rainbow" team can clear all world bosses, albeit more slowly.
- **DPS:** Zero (Cosmos - free S-Rank or high-constellation A-Rank)
- **Buffer:** Haniel (A-Rank - easy to obtain)
- **Healer:** Edgar (A-Rank - essential for survival)
- **Breaker:** Fadia (A-Rank - specialized in Toughness damage)
This team focuses on the "Remora" reaction between Cosmos and Lakshana (if using a Lakshana support), which slows boss movement, making telegraphs easier to dodge.
