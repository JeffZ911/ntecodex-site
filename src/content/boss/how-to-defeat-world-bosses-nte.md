### The Never-ending Arachne (New Herland District)
This massive, puppet-like arachnid is often the first major wall for players. Located in the heart of the New Herland District, it serves as a gateway boss for unlocking higher-tier Chaos-type Arcs. Its stats focus heavily on crowd control and area-of-effect (AoE) denial.

### Sea Prisoner (Nautili Tunnel)
Found within the flooded sections of the Nautili Tunnel, the Sea Prisoner is a high-mobility boss that requires players to manage their oxygen levels (if fighting in submerged zones) or utilize vehicle-based lures to bring it to the surface. It is highly resistant to physical strikes but melts under Psyche-based Discord reactions.

### Headless Rider (Fox Window Street)
The Headless Rider is a time-gated boss that typically spawns during the "Midnight" cycle in Fox Window Street. It possesses the highest movement speed of any Version 1.0 boss, making long-range Espers like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) almost essential for maintaining consistent DPS.

### Morphix (Weekly Anomaly Hunt)
Morphix is a unique entity found in the Weekly Anomaly Hunt instances. Unlike static bosses, Morphix copies the elemental profile of your lead Esper, forcing you to rotate your team composition to prevent it from becoming immune to your primary damage source.

## Attack Patterns

![Attack Patterns](/img/how-to-defeat-world-bosses-nte/inline-2.webp)


Understanding the visual cues of an Anomaly is the difference between a successful hunt and a team wipe. Most bosses in NTE utilize a "Red Shine" indicator—a brief flash of light that signals an incoming heavy attack. Timing a dodge during this flash triggers a Critical Dodge, which slows down time and allows for a Critical Riposte.

### Phase 1: Puppet Waves and Circular Shockwaves
During the initial encounter with The Never-ending Arachne, the boss remains relatively stationary but summons waves of smaller "Puppet" adds. The boss will periodically slam its front legs into the ground, sending out three consecutive circular shockwaves. These must be jumped over rather than dodged through, as the lingering hitbox can catch players at the end of a dodge roll.

*   **Parryable:** Frontal leg slams.
*   **Non-Parryable:** The summoned Puppet explosions.

### Phase 2: Laser Barrages and Ground Strikes
Once the Arachne reaches 60% HP, it enters Phase 2. It will retreat to the ceiling of the arena and begin firing laser barrages. These lasers track the player’s movement, requiring a constant sprint. During this phase, the boss is susceptible to ranged attacks. If you deal enough damage to its glowing abdomen, it will fall, providing a massive stagger window.

### Phase 3: The Spotlight and Teleportation Combat
The final phase introduces the "Spotlight" mechanic. The arena dims, and a single pillar of light moves across the floor. Staying outside of this light for more than five seconds applies the "Frozen" status effect, which completely immobilizes your Esper. The boss will teleport rapidly between the shadows, only becoming vulnerable when it enters the spotlight to strike.

**Swallowtail Mechanics:**
Swallowtail requires specific positioning due to its "Wing Shield." Attacking the boss from the front results in a 70% damage reduction. Players must use high-mobility Espers to stay behind the boss or use Adler’s shields to tank the frontal counter-attacks while the Sub-DPS rotates to the flank.

## Step-by-Step Strategy

![Step-by-Step Strategy](/img/how-to-defeat-world-bosses-nte/inline-3.webp)


The core of NTE combat is the "Break Bar"—the white meter located directly beneath the boss’s HP bar. While this bar is active, the boss takes significantly reduced damage and cannot be interrupted.

### Shredding the Break Bar
To effectively shred the Break Bar, you must trigger Discord Reactions. These occur when two or more elements interact on the target.

**The Discord Reaction Chain:**
1.  **Apply Incantation:** Use Adler’s skill to mark the boss.
2.  **Apply Chaos:** Switch to Nanally to trigger the **Scorch** reaction. This deals massive Break Bar damage over time.
3.  **Apply Psyche:** Switch to Fadia or Daffodil to trigger **Nova** (Chaos + Psyche), which causes an immediate explosion of the Break Bar.

### Triggering the Discord Reaction
Once the Break Bar is depleted, the boss enters a "Stagger" state. During this window, all damage is increased by [exact value pending official confirmation], and the boss is completely incapacitated. This is your window to dump all saved Ultimates and Esper Skills.

### Utilizing Stagger Windows for Burst DPS
1.  **Preparation:** Ensure your Esper Meter is full before the Break Bar hits zero.
2.  **Execution:** Use Fadia’s Ultimate first to provide a damage buff to the team.
3.  **Burst:** Switch to Nanally or Daffodil and spam their primary damage rotations.
4.  **Reset:** As the boss recovers, it will often perform a "Wake-up Attack" (a massive AoE knockback). Be ready to dodge immediately as the Break Bar begins to refill.

**Vehicle Combat Strategy:**
For the Sea Prisoner, you must use the Regalia (or any high-speed vehicle) to trigger the boss portal. Driving through the Anomaly Rifts at speeds exceeding 120km/h is required to "crack" the entrance to the Nautili Tunnel boss arena.
