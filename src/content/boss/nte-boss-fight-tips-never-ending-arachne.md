# Neverness to Everness Boss Guide: How to Beat The Never-Ending Arachne

The Never-Ending Arachne serves as one of the primary hurdles for players reaching the mid-game in Neverness to Everness (NTE). Located within the high-stakes environment of the New Herland District, this boss is a multi-phase anomaly that tests your team’s ability to manage the Break Bar while navigating complex floor hazards. Successfully defeating her is essential for obtaining high-tier upgrade materials like the Dress Sleeves of Vanity.

## Boss Stats

![Boss Stats](/img/nte-boss-fight-tips-never-ending-arachne/inline-1.webp)


The Never-Ending Arachne is a weekly boss that scales its difficulty based on your current Hunter Level (HL). As an anomaly tied to the Ebisu Auction House, she utilizes a mix of Psyche and Anima-based attacks to disrupt your positioning. Her most defining feature is a massive Break Bar (Stagger Meter) which provides a significant damage reduction until it is fully depleted.

### Location and Unlock Requirements

To face The Never-Ending Arachne, you must progress through the main story until you reach Chapter 3, titled "Deal? Deal!". This chapter concludes with a confrontation at the Ebisu Auction House in the New Herland District. After your initial victory during the story quest, the boss becomes a permanent weekly challenge located in the bottom right of the New Herland map. You can use the Fast Travel point directly at the auction house once it is unlocked.

### Elemental Weaknesses

The Never-Ending Arachne is notably weak to the Chaos element. Utilizing Chaos-aligned characters like Daffodil is the most efficient way to chip away at her Break Resistance. While she takes standard damage from other elements, her resistance to Psyche and Anima attacks is slightly higher, meaning players should focus on triggering the Discord reaction (Incantation + Chaos + Psyche) to maximize impact.

## Attack Patterns

![Attack Patterns](/img/nte-boss-fight-tips-never-ending-arachne/inline-2.webp)


The fight is divided into three distinct phases, each requiring a different tactical approach. The transition between these phases is usually marked by a brief cinematic or a change in the arena's lighting.

### Phase 1: The Puppet Stage

In the first phase, Arachne remains on the elevated stage, making her untargetable by standard melee attacks. Instead, she controls several "Dancing Puppets" on the floor.

*   **Circular Shockwaves:** The puppets will periodically slam the ground, sending out circular shockwaves. These travel quickly across the floor and must be jumped over or dodged with precise timing.
*   **Puppet Spin:** Occasionally, a puppet will perform a red-marked telegraph before spinning toward your character. This attack tracks your movement, so wait for the final second to dash.
*   **Critical Riposte Window:** During this phase, focus on the floor enemies. Successfully performing a Critical Dodge against the shockwaves allows you to trigger a Critical Riposte, which is the most effective way to clear the puppets and force the boss into the next phase.

### Phase 2: Melee Aggression

Once the puppets are cleared, Arachne descends to the floor. She becomes much more aggressive, utilizing her claws for rapid melee combos.

*   **Consecutive Slashes:** Arachne typically performs a string of 3 to 4 consecutive slash attacks. These do not have red telegraphs, so you must watch her limb movements to time your dodges.
*   **The Red Shine (Parry Window):** The most critical mechanic in Phase 2 is the "Red Shine." Arachne’s claws will glow with a distinct red light before a heavy overhead strike. Landing a parry during this window triggers a Critical Riposte, dealing massive Break damage.
*   **Ground Lasers:** If you stay at a distance, she may fire lasers that travel along the ground. Like the shockwaves in Phase 1, these can be avoided by jumping.

### Phase 3: The Spotlight Finale

The final phase introduces a "Spotlight" mechanic that drastically limits your mobility. The arena darkens, and two spotlights appear—one follows the boss, and one follows the player.

*   **Spotlight Safety:** You must stay within the boundaries of your assigned spotlight. Stepping into the darkness for more than a few seconds will apply the "Frozen" status effect, leaving you vulnerable to a follow-up attack.
*   **Teleportation Barrage:** Arachne teleports frequently in this phase. She often teleports outside your light to bait you into chasing her. Do not follow her; stay in your spotlight and wait for her to teleport back to you for a parryable attack.
*   **Ultimate Timing:** Long-cast Ultimates should be avoided unless the boss has just finished a teleport or is currently in a "Broken" state, as she can easily teleport away mid-animation.

## Step-by-Step Strategy

![Step-by-Step Strategy](/img/nte-boss-fight-tips-never-ending-arachne/inline-3.webp)


Beating The Never-Ending Arachne is a test of patience rather than brute force. If you try to ignore her mechanics, her high damage output will quickly deplete your team's HP.

### Managing the Break Bar

Your primary goal is to deplete the yellow Break Bar located beneath her HP. While the bar is active, the boss takes significantly reduced damage.
1.  **Use Chaos Damage:** Characters like Daffodil should be your primary field units while the Break Bar is up.
2.  **Prioritize Parrying:** In Phase 2 and Phase 3, a successful parry with a character like Daffodil (using her Phantom Step) reduces the Break Bar far more effectively than any standard skill rotation.
3.  **Discord Reaction:** Ensure your team can trigger the Discord Esper Cycle (Incantation + Chaos + Psyche). This reaction deals a massive burst of Stagger damage, often removing 25-30% of the Break Bar instantly.

### Mastering the Spotlight Mechanic

Phase 3 is where most players fail due to the Frozen debuff.
*   **Assigned Spotlight:** Your spotlight moves slowly every few seconds. Focus on staying centered within it.
*   **Managing the Frozen Debuff:** If you do get hit by the Frozen status, immediately swap to a support character like [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) or use a defensive skill to mitigate the incoming damage.
*   **Wait for the Boss:** Because she teleports so often, the best strategy is to stand still in your light and prepare for a parry. Chasing her into the dark is a guaranteed way to get Frozen.

### When to Use Ultimates

Never use your Main DPS Ultimates (like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte)’s or Chiz’s) while the Break Bar is active.
1.  **Wait for the Stun:** Once the Break Bar reaches zero, the boss will enter a "Broken" state and remain motionless for several seconds.
2.  **The DPS Window:** Swap to Nanally or Chiz and unleash your full rotation. This is the only time the boss takes 100% damage.
3.  **Esper Cycle Management:** Use the Cosmos + Anima (Blossom) reaction during this window to spawn turrets that provide passive damage while you execute your character's main skills.

## Recommended Team

![Recommended Team](/img/nte-boss-fight-tips-never-ending-arachne/inline-4.webp)


The ideal team for this fight focuses on a balance between "Breaking" the boss and dealing high burst damage during the vulnerable windows.

### The Meta 'Break' Comp

*   **Break Specialist: Daffodil.** As the premier Chaos character, she is essential for this fight. Her "Still Waters" and "Phantom Step" allow her to shred the Break Bar while remaining invincible during parries.
*   **Support: Sakiri.** Sakiri provides essential ATK buffs and crowd control. Her hammer, Kiroumaru, can help manage any adds in Phase 1 and provides a 30% ATK boost to the team after her Ultimate.
*   **Main DPS: Nanally.** Once the boss is broken, Nanally’s Anima-based burst damage is unrivaled. Her "The Ichi-daime" follow-up attacks can delete the boss's HP once the damage reduction is gone.
*   **Flex Pick: Hathor.** Hathor is excellent for Phase 2. Since the boss attacks 3-4 times in a row, Hathor can easily build her "Express Delivery Power" stacks by taking hits or parrying, allowing her to enter her "Emergency Delivery" state for massive Lakshana damage.

### F2P Friendly Alternatives

If you are a free-to-play player, you can still clear this content using the free S-Rank character provided by the story.
*   **Main DPS: Chiz.** Chiz is a Cosmos-based powerhouse who is given to all players for free. Her "Grain Market" mechanic allows for sustained damage, and she is particularly effective when paired with Sakiri for the Discord reaction.
*   **Support: Mint.** While an A-Class character, Mint provides decent Anima support and can help trigger the Blossom reaction with Chiz.
*   **Shield/Survival: Adler.** Adler is a great F2P option for survivability, providing shields that can protect you if you fail a dodge against the Phase 1 shockwaves.

### Best Arcs and Cartridges for this Fight

*   **Daffodil’s Arc:** *Youthful Fantasy* (S-Rank). This is her signature weapon and provides a massive boost to Break Intensity.
*   **Nanally’s Arc:** *Ready-Ready* (S-Rank). Found in the Tiger Special banner, this Arc maximizes her burst potential during the Break window.
*   **Sakiri’s Arc:** *Good Boy’s Grand Adventure* (S-Rank). This boosts her Ultimate charge speed, ensuring her ATK buffs are always ready.
*   **Cartridge Set:** *Diabolos (4-piece)* is highly recommended for your Chaos characters, as it increases Chaos DMG and shreds enemy Chaos RES after a Nova or Scorch reaction.

## Loot

![Loot](/img/nte-boss-fight-tips-never-ending-arachne/inline-5.webp)


Defeating The Never-Ending Arachne is a lucrative endeavor, but it is governed by the game's weekly reward system.

### Weekly Reward Caps

To claim the rewards after the fight, you must spend **60 Character Pixels**. You can claim loot from this specific boss up to **3 times per week**. After these three claims, you can still fight the boss for practice or to complete District Anomaly progress, but you will not receive progression materials.

### Fons and Material Yields

The rewards scale based on your Hunter Level, with higher levels offering a chance at S-Rank Arcs.

| Material Name | Usage |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Fons** | General currency for upgrades and shop purchases. |
| **Dress Sleeves of Vanity** | High-level material required for character skill upgrades. |
| **Magic Thread** | Used for crafting and refining Arcs. |
| **Beetle Coin** | Currency for the New Herland District specialty shop. |
| **A-Rank Arcs** | Mid-tier weapons (Random drop). |
| **S-Rank Arcs** | Rare high-tier weapons (HL 40+ recommended). |

Defeating Arachne also contributes significantly to the completion percentage of the New Herland District, which unlocks further rewards through the District Progress tracker. By focusing on Chaos damage and mastering the Phase 3 spotlight, you can turn this intimidating encounter into a routine part of your weekly farming.
