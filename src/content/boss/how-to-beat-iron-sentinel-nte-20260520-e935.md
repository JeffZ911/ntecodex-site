## Step-by-Step Strategy

![Step-by-Step Strategy](/img/how-to-beat-iron-sentinel-nte-20260520-e935/inline-3.webp)


Success against the Iron Sentinel depends on your ability to cycle through your team's Esper Abilities effectively. The goal is to keep the boss in a perpetual state of "Break" while avoiding its high-damage telegraphs.

### Phase 1: Breaking the Shield

At the start of the fight, the Iron Sentinel will have a thick white bar under its health—the Break Meter. While this bar is active, the boss takes significantly reduced damage.

1.  **Generate Energy:** Use your active character's normal attacks and skills to fill their Esper Meter (the circular icon next to their portrait).
2.  **Trigger Discord:** Start with an Incantation character like [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) to apply an initial mark, then swap to a Chaos character like Daffodil. This triggers the **Scorch** reaction. Immediately swap to a Psyche character to trigger **Nova** and complete the **Discord** Triple Cycle.
3.  **Perfect Dodge:** Watch for the Red Flash. A successful Perfect Dodge instantly fills the active character's Esper Meter, allowing for a faster rotation.

### Phase 2: Managing Enrage Windows

Once the Break Meter is depleted, the Iron Sentinel will be "Stunned." This is your window for maximum DPS.

*   **Burst Damage:** Switch to your primary Damage dealer, such as [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) or [Hotori](/characters/hotori-best-team-nte).
*   **Ult Management:** Save your Ultimates for this stunned window. If you are using Hotori, her Ultimate can stop time, extending the duration of your other characters' Blossom turrets or follow-up attacks.
*   **Reposition:** When the boss begins to recover, it will often release a high-damage AoE burst. Do not get greedy; dash away as soon as you see the Break Meter beginning to refill.

### Survival Tips and Healing Windows

If your health drops low, look for the "Anomaly Shards" that occasionally drop during combat or use a dedicated Survival character. Mint is an excellent A-Rank choice for players who need consistent healing, while Fadia (Psyche) can provide shields and self-sustain.

*   **Manage Stamina:** Constant dodging will deplete your stamina quickly. Only use the dodge maneuver when you see a telegraph or the Red Flash.
*   **Cycle Rate Stat:** Ensure your characters have high "Cycle Rate" on their Cartridges to ensure your Esper Abilities are ready as soon as you swap.
