# Neverness to Everness: How to Beat the Never-Ending Arachne World Boss

The Never-Ending Arachne serves as a significant hurdle for players transitioning into the mid-game of Neverness to Everness (NTE). Found deep within the New Herland District, this boss is a theatrical nightmare that blends high-speed area-of-effect (AoE) attacks with punishing physical combos. Defeating her is not just a matter of gear but a test of your ability to manage the stagger bar and execute perfect dodges under pressure.

The Never-Ending Arachne is highly resistant to standard physical damage, often shrugging off hits that don't contribute to an Esper Cycle reaction. Her primary weakness is Chaos damage, making characters like Daffodil essential for efficient clears.

*   **Weakness:** Chaos (Deals 20% increased Break damage).
*   **Resistances:** Physical (30% damage reduction), Anima (Slight resistance outside of Blossom reactions).

## Attack Patterns

![Attack Patterns](/img/how-to-beat-world-boss-nte-arachne/inline-2.webp)


The fight is divided into three distinct phases, each requiring a shift in your defensive strategy. Understanding the visual cues for her "Parry Windows" and "Red Aura" attacks is the difference between a successful run and a team wipe.

### Phase 1: The Puppet Waltz

In the opening phase, the Never-Ending Arachne remains on the elevated stage, rendering her invulnerable to direct attacks. Instead, she controls a series of dancing puppets that flood the floor.

*   **Puppet Waltz:** The puppets perform a synchronized dance that generates high-speed circular shockwaves. These waves move outward from the puppets and must be jumped over or dashed through using a Critical Dodge.
*   **Spinning Finale:** Occasionally, a puppet will spin toward the player with a red-marked trajectory. This attack cannot be parried and deals heavy knockback.
*   **Visual Cue:** Watch for the puppets to raise their arms simultaneously; this indicates a wave is about to spawn.

### Phase 2: Mastering the Parry Window

Once the puppets are cleared or a specific health threshold is met, the Arachne joins the field. This is the most dangerous part of the fight, as she uses a mix of rapid slashes and lunges.

*   **3-Hit Slash Combo:** Her most common attack. The first two hits are standard, but the third hit often features a golden or white flash. This is your **Parry Window**. Executing a parry here deals massive Break damage to her stagger bar.
*   **Red Aura Attacks:** Any move where the boss glows with a deep red aura—such as her overhead slam—is unparryable. You must use a Critical Dodge to avoid these.
*   **[Information unavailable] (Move Name):** A lunging thrust that covers the entire length of the arena.
*   **[Information unavailable] (Move Name):** A silk-based tether that pulls the player toward the boss, usually followed by an unparryable explosion.

### Phase 3: Teleportation and Desperation Moves

When her health drops below 30%, the Arachne enters her final phase. She becomes much more mobile, frequently teleporting around the arena to disorient the player.

*   **Spotlight Mechanic:** The arena darkens, and two spotlights appear—one on the boss and one on the player. You must stay within your spotlight or you will be frozen in place, leaving you vulnerable to a follow-up strike.
*   **Teleport Lunges:** She will teleport into the air and perform a downward strike. This move is parryable if you time your counter exactly as she reappears.

## Step-by-Step Strategy

![Step-by-Step Strategy](/img/how-to-beat-world-boss-nte-arachne/inline-3.webp)


Beating the Never-Ending Arachne requires a disciplined rotation. You cannot "brute force" this boss with raw DPS if you ignore the stagger mechanics.

### Survival Tactics for Phase 1

During the Puppet Waltz, your only goal is to survive and build your Esper Cycle meter. Do not waste your Ultimates here, as the boss cannot be damaged. 
1.  Stay in the center of the arena to give yourself maximum time to react to the circular waves.
2.  Use characters with high mobility to dash through the waves, triggering "Critical Ripostes" on the puppets to clear them faster.
3.  Once the puppets are defeated, the boss will jump down, signaling the start of Phase 2.

### Optimizing the Stagger Window

The white bar below the boss's HP is your primary target. In Phase 2, prioritize characters with high "Break Efficiency."
*   **Parry Priority:** Every time you see the golden flash, switch to your "Breaker" (like Daffodil) and perform a parry. This is the fastest way to deplete the bar.
*   **The Downed State:** When the bar is empty, the boss will collapse for approximately 10 seconds. She takes 50% increased damage during this window. 
*   **Time Freezing:** Use your Ultimate abilities during this window. The animation for most Ultimates "freezes" the stagger timer, allowing you to squeeze out more damage than the 10-second window would normally allow.

### Managing the Esper Cycle Meter

To keep the boss's posture low, you must trigger constant reactions. 
*   **Blossom Reaction:** Combine Anima and Cosmos elements to spawn "Vita Buds" (turrets) that deal continuous damage.
*   **Scorch Reaction:** Combine Chaos and Incantation to deal high damage-over-time (DoT) and shred the break bar.

## Recommended Team

![Recommended Team](/img/how-to-beat-world-boss-nte-arachne/inline-4.webp)


Your team should consist of one Main DPS, one Breaker, and two Support/Utility characters. Having a Chaos-element character is highly recommended for this specific boss.

### Top Tier: The Nanally Blossom Comp

This team focuses on overwhelming the boss with constant follow-up attacks and high-frequency hits.
*   **Main DPS:** [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) (Anima) - Excels at chaining hits and maintaining pressure.
*   **Cycle Charge:** Zero (Cosmos) - Quickly fills the Esper Cycle gauge to trigger Blossom reactions.
*   **Control/Support:** Jiuyuan (Anima) - Groups the puppets in Phase 1 and buffs Nanally's damage.
*   **Buffer:** Haniel (Psyche) - Provides massive DMG boosts during the stagger window.

### High Efficiency: The Daffodil Break Comp

This is the most effective team for players struggling with the boss's stagger bar.
*   **Breaker:** Daffodil (Chaos) - The MVP of this fight. Her Chaos attacks shred the Arachne's defenses.
*   **Hyper Carry:** Baicang (Incantation) - Deals massive burst damage once the boss is broken.
*   **Buffer/CC:** [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) (Incantation) - Buffs the team's ATK and triggers the Scorch reaction with Daffodil.
*   **Shielder:** Adler (Incantation) - Provides shields to mitigate damage from missed parries.

### F2P-Friendly Survival Team

If you haven't pulled any S-Rank characters yet, this sustainable composition can still clear the fight.
*   **Main DPS:** Zero (Cosmos) - Can be obtained for free through early-game milestones.
*   **Healer:** Edgar (Anima) - Provides steady healing to recover from the boss's high-damage slashes.
*   **Shielder:** Adler (Incantation) - Essential for surviving the unparryable red aura attacks.
*   **Sub-DPS:** Mint (Anima) - Adds extra AoE damage to help clear the puppets in Phase 1.

## Loot

![Loot](/img/how-to-beat-world-boss-nte-arachne/inline-5.webp)


Defeating the Never-Ending Arachne is the only way to obtain several high-tier materials required for late-game character growth.

### Primary Drops: Dress Sleeves of Vanity

The most sought-after reward is the **Dress Sleeves of Vanity**. This S-Rank material is used to upgrade the "Esper Abilities" (skills and passives) of several powerful characters, including Haniel and [Hotori](/characters/hotori-best-team-nte). High-level upgrades typically require 4-8 Sleeves per level, making weekly clears mandatory for progression.

### Weekly Reward Caps and Reset Times

You can challenge the Never-Ending Arachne as many times as you want, but you can only claim the rewards **3 times per week**. Each claim costs **60 Character Pixels** (the game's stamina equivalent).

The rewards reset every **Monday at 4:00 AM server time**. It is recommended to save your weekly attempts for after you have reached the highest possible World Level for your current Hunter Level, as the quality and quantity of the drops—specifically the Dress Sleeves of Vanity—increase significantly at higher tiers.
