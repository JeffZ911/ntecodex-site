### Location: Ebisu Auction House
The boss is situated in the New Herland District, specifically inside the Ebisu Auction House. After completing the initial story encounter, you can unlock a fast-travel point directly to the arena. This location is part of the weekly Anomaly Pilgrimage, allowing for repeated clears to farm essential ascension materials.

### Elemental Vulnerabilities
Understanding the elemental wheel is mandatory for this fight. The Arachne possesses a unique "Hardened Silk" shield that grants her massive resistance to Physical and Anima damage during her initial phase.

*   **Primary Weakness: Chaos.** The Chaos element is the only way to efficiently deplete her Break Gauge. Characters like Daffodil are specifically designed to counter this boss's defensive mechanics.
*   **Secondary Weakness: Psyche and Incantation.** These elements are required to trigger the "Discord" reaction, which provides a massive multiplier to Stagger damage.
*   **Resistances:** High resistance to Anima and Lakshana. Avoid using primary DPS characters from these elements unless they are heavily over-leveled.

## Attack Patterns

![Attack Patterns](/img/how-to-beat-boss-nte-guide/inline-2.webp)


The fight is divided into three distinct phases, each requiring a shift in your defensive strategy. The transition between these phases is triggered by specific HP thresholds.

### Phase 1: The Puppet Dance (100% to 50% HP)
In this stage, the Arachne remains largely stationary on the auction stage, manipulating puppets to do her bidding.
*   **Circular Shockwaves:** The puppets will emit high-speed circular waves across the floor. You must time your dash to trigger a Critical Dodge. Successfully doing so allows you to perform a Critical Riposte, which deals significant Break damage even while the boss is shielded.
*   **Whirlwind Spin:** Occasionally, a puppet will spin toward the player with a red-marked indicator. This attack cannot be parried and must be dodged laterally.

### Phase 2: The Weaver's Wrath (50% to 25% HP)
At 50% HP, the Arachne descends from the stage and enters a melee-aggressive state.
*   **Red Shine Parry:** Watch for a distinct red glint on her blades. This signals a parryable slash combo. The combo typically consists of 3 to 4 hits. Parrying the first hit with a character like Adler will instantly fill your active character’s Esper Meter, allowing for an immediate counter-attack.
*   **Blink Strike:** The boss frequently teleports (blinks) behind the player. Maintaining a camera lock-on is vital here; if you lose sight of her, the subsequent strike will likely stagger your character and break your Esper Cycle.

### Phase 3: Spotlight Survival (Below 25% HP)
The final phase introduces the most dangerous environmental mechanic in the game.
*   **The Spotlight:** The arena darkens, and a single white spotlight begins to move across the floor. You must stay within this light. Remaining in the darkness for more than three seconds applies the "Frozen" status effect, leaving you vulnerable to the boss's high-damage "Finale" execution.
*   **Teleportation Spam:** The boss will teleport rapidly during this phase, often appearing just outside the spotlight to bait you into the darkness. Do not chase her; wait for her to come to you within the safe zone.

## Step-by-Step Strategy

![Step-by-Step Strategy](/img/how-to-beat-boss-nte-guide/inline-3.webp)


The key to a fast clear is maximizing the "Break" window. When the boss's white stagger bar is depleted, she enters a vulnerable state where all damage is increased by 200%.

### Triggering the Discord Reaction
To deplete the Break Gauge effectively, you must trigger the Discord Triple Cycle. This requires a specific rotation of three elements: Incantation, Chaos, and Psyche.

1.  **Apply Scorch:** Start with an Incantation character (like [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) or Adler) and swap to a Chaos character (Daffodil). This triggers Scorch, a damage-over-time effect that lasts 15 seconds.
2.  **Apply Nova:** While Scorch is active, swap from your Chaos character to a Psyche character (Fadia or Haniel). This triggers Nova, a burst effect that lasts 5 seconds.
3.  **Discord Activation:** If an enemy is affected by both Scorch and Nova simultaneously, the Discord reaction triggers. This reduces the target's Break cap and deals massive Stagger damage.

### Maximizing the Stagger Window
Once the boss is Broken, swap to your highest-damage dealer. If you are using the "Discord Meta" team, this is usually the moment to unleash Daffodil's Ultimate. Because she is a Chaos-element burst DPS, her damage scales significantly against targets already suffering from the Discord debuff.

### Survival Tips for Phase 3
During the Spotlight Finale, your priority must be positioning. 
*   **Don't Greed for DPS:** It is better to lose five seconds of damage than to get Frozen in the dark.
*   **Use Shields:** If you are struggling to stay in the light, use Adler’s "Shield of Blessing" to absorb the chip damage from the boss's teleportation strikes.
*   **Esper Cycle Reset:** Use this phase to recharge your team's Esper Meters by landing safe normal attacks while staying inside the spotlight.
