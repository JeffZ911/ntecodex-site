# How to Beat The Never-ending Arachne: Neverness to Everness World Boss Guide

The Never-ending Arachne serves as one of the first major hurdles for Hunters in Neverness to Everness (NTE). As a Class 5 Anomaly, this boss utilizes a blend of puppet manipulation and area-of-effect (AoE) stage mechanics that can quickly overwhelm an unprepared team. Located deep within the New Herland District, this encounter is the primary source for high-level skill upgrade materials, making it a mandatory stop for anyone looking to optimize their Espers.

## Boss Stats

![Boss Stats](/img/how-to-beat-nte-world-boss-arachne/inline-1.webp)


The Never-ending Arachne is a stationary-to-mobile hybrid boss that relies on its "Puppet Stage" to stay protected during the initial half of the fight. Understanding its elemental profile is the first step to a successful hunt.

*   **Location:** Found at the Ebisu Auction House within the New Herland District. To access the arena, you must enter the Kapé Grand Hotel lobby and speak with Iris, who will teleport you to the B2 Auction Hall.
*   **Unlock Requirement:** You must reach Hunter Level 14 and successfully complete the Episode 3 main story quest: "Deal? Deal."
*   **Attribute Weakness:** Chaos. Currently, Daffodil is the premier specialist for this element, though other Chaos units like Lacrimosa can also fulfill this role.
*   **Stamina Cost:** Requires 60 Character Pixels to claim rewards. You can claim these rewards up to 3 times per weekly reset.
*   **Level Scaling:** The boss’s health and damage output scale dynamically based on your current World Level and Hunter Level, ensuring the challenge remains relevant as you progress.

### Where to Find The Never-ending Arachne

The entrance to the boss arena is tucked away in the southwestern section of the New Herland District. Look for the Kapé Grand Hotel, a landmark building that becomes a central hub during the "Deal? Deal!" questline. After the quest is completed, a gavel icon will appear on your map, indicating the Ebisu Auction House. Once inside the hotel, the NPC Iris serves as the gatekeeper. Interacting with her allows you to descend to the B2 level where the Arachne resides.

## Attack Patterns

![Attack Patterns](/img/how-to-beat-nte-world-boss-arachne/inline-2.webp)


The Never-ending Arachne operates on a strict three-phase cycle. Each phase introduces a new environmental hazard that forces you to change your positioning.

### Phase 1: The Puppet Dance

During the Puppet Stage, the Arachne remains perched on the upper balcony, rendering it untargetable by most melee attacks. Instead, it summons "Vanity Puppets" to do its bidding.
*   **Circular Shockwaves:** The puppets will periodically slam the ground, sending out red circular shockwaves. These must be jumped over or bypassed using a "Phantom Step" dodge.
*   **Puppet String Pull:** If you stay too far from the center, strings will descend to pull you toward the puppets, setting you up for a follow-up strike.

### Phase 2: Breaking the Guard

Once the puppets are cleared, the Arachne descends to the stage. This is the primary combat phase where the boss engages in direct melee.
*   **Triple Slash Combo:** The boss performs three consecutive slashes with high tracking. Watch for a red glint on the boss's weapon—this is your signal to execute a Parry Attack.
*   **Teleport Strike:** The boss vanishes into a cloud of silk and reappears behind you for a wide-area sweep.
*   **Lock-On Lasers:** Three red beams will track your character. You must sprint continuously in a wide circle; dodging too early will cause the second and third beams to hit you as you recover from the animation.

### Phase 3: The Spotlight Finale

When the boss reaches approximately 30% HP, the arena lights dim, and the Spotlight Mechanic begins.
*   **The Spotlight:** Two circular beams of light move across the floor. You must stay within these designated lights. Staying in the darkness for more than 3 seconds will accumulate a "Stage Fright" debuff, eventually resulting in a Freeze status that leaves you vulnerable to the boss's ultimate attack.
*   **Web Snare:** The boss will fire webs that slow your movement speed, making it harder to reach the moving spotlights.

## Step-by-Step Strategy

![Step-by-Step Strategy](/img/how-to-beat-nte-world-boss-arachne/inline-3.webp)


Success against the Never-ending Arachne is a matter of managing your Esper Cycle rotations and respecting the stage mechanics.

### Managing the Break Bar

The goal in Phase 2 is to force a "Break" as quickly as possible. When the boss is in a Broken state, it takes significantly increased damage and remains immobile for several seconds. 
1.  **Lead with Chaos:** Use Daffodil’s Skill or Ultimate to apply "Insight" to the boss. This increases the Break damage taken by the target.
2.  **Trigger Reactions:** Swap to an Incantation character like [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) or Baicang to trigger the "Scorch" reaction. Scorch further erodes the Break Bar over time.
3.  **The Burst Window:** Once the bar is depleted, swap to your main DPS (like Nanally) and unleash all stored Ultimate skills.

### Surviving the Spotlight Freeze

Phase 3 is where most runs fail due to the Freeze debuff. Follow this positioning checklist
*   **Prioritize Movement over DPS:** When the spotlights appear, stop attacking and move into the center of a beam.
*   **Watch the Floor:** The spotlights move in a predictable figure-eight pattern. Position yourself where the two paths intersect to minimize the distance you need to travel when they shift.
*   **Cleansing Debuffs:** If you are caught in the dark, use Adler’s shield or a support skill that provides crowd control (CC) immunity to prevent the Freeze from taking hold.

### Best Times to Use Ultimate Skills

Do not waste your Ultimate skills during Phase 1. The puppets have low HP and can be cleared with basic skills and AoE support. Save your Ultimates for
1.  **The Start of Phase 2:** To quickly build Break gauge.
2.  **The Break Window:** To maximize damage while the boss is stunned.
3.  **Phase 3 Transition:** If you can push the boss from 35% to 0% during a single Break window, you can skip the Spotlight mechanic entirely.

## Recommended Team

![Recommended Team](/img/how-to-beat-nte-world-boss-arachne/inline-4.webp)


Your team composition should focus on high Break efficiency and sustained survivability to handle the puppet waves.

### The Chaos Meta: Why Daffodil is Key

The current "Premium Meta" team consists of **Daffodil, Nanally, and Sakiri**. 
*   **Daffodil (Chaos/Break):** Her "Phantom Step" allows her to ignore the Phase 1 shockwaves and her Chaos attribute shreds the boss's Toughness. Her Ultimate, "Finale," applies the Insight debuff which is essential for a fast clear.
*   **Nanally (Anima DPS):** Once the boss is Broken, Nanally’s follow-up attacks and high raw damage provide the necessary burst to end the fight.
*   **Sakiri (Support/Buffs):** Sakiri’s companion, Kiroumaru, can group the Phase 1 puppets together, allowing you to clear them in seconds. Her Ultimate also provides a substantial ATK buff for the rest of the team.

### Best F2P Alternatives

For players without limited S-Rank units, a highly effective "Free-to-Play" team includes **Zero (MC), Chiz, and Adler**.
*   **Zero (Cosmos/Reaction):** As the protagonist, Zero is a versatile burst dealer. Use their skills to trigger reactions with Chiz.
*   **Chiz (Main DPS):** Chiz is obtainable for free through the City Tycoon system and provides solid consistent damage.
*   **Adler (Shield/Sustain):** Adler is a reliable A-Rank Incantation unit. His shields are invaluable for surviving the Triple Slash combo if your parry timing is off.

### Optimal Cartridge and Arc Loadouts

To maximize your performance, equip your Chaos units with the **Diabolos** Cartridge set. The 4-piece bonus ignores 12% of enemy Chaos RES, which increases to 24% after triggering a Nova or Scorch reaction. 

For Arcs (Weapons)
*   **Daffodil:** Use **Youthful Fantasy**. It boosts ATK and Break Intensity while summoning the "Black Tome" to assist in combat. An A-Rank alternative is **Shiny Days**, which increases damage against Broken units.
*   **Nanally:** Her signature Arc, **The Ichi-daime**, is preferred, but any high-ATK Plasma-type Arc will suffice.
*   **Supports:** Equipping the **Shadow Creed** Cartridge set on Sakiri or Adler will provide team-wide energy regeneration, ensuring your Ultimates are ready for every Phase 2 transition.

## Loot

Defeating the Never-ending Arachne is the only consistent way to obtain the materials needed for end-game Esper Ability progression.

### Farming Dress Sleeves of Vanity

The **Dress Sleeves of Vanity** is the primary S-Rank drop from this boss. You will need approximately 32 of these to fully max out the skills of a single S-Rank character. Because you are limited to 3 claims per week, it is highly recommended to prioritize this boss every Monday.

### Weekly Reward Caps and Resets

*   **Claim Limit:** 3 times per week.
*   **Reset Time:** Every Monday at 04:00 (Server Time).
*   **First-Clear Bonus:** The first time you defeat the Arachne, you receive the **"Your Happiness is Priceless"** Arc, which provides a significant boost to healing received.
*   **Coal Lump's Gift:** This reward container is granted upon every successful claim and has a chance to drop random A-Rank Arcs and Senior Hunter Guides for character EXP.
