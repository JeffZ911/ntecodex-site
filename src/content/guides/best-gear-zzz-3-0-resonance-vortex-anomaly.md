## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/best-gear-zzz-3-0-resonance-vortex-anomaly/inline-4.webp)


While high-end gear can improve your experience, it is not always a necessary purchase for every player.

*   **Existing 144Hz IPS Displays:** If you already own a 144Hz IPS monitor, the jump to OLED is visually stunning but won't fundamentally change your ability to clear content. The pixel response time is better, but the raw refresh rate advantage is marginal unless you are playing at a professional level.
*   **Comfortable Seating:** If your current chair doesn't cause pain after a three-hour session, a $500 Secretlab is a luxury. Ergonomics are highly subjective; if your current setup works for your body type, an upgrade might not offer a noticeable improvement in your gaming performance.
*   **Controller Players:** Avoid upgrading your mouse if you primarily play with a controller. The macro benefits of the [Naga V2 Pro](https://www.razer.com/gaming-mice/razer-naga-v2-pro) are entirely lost if you are using a gamepad. Focus your budget on a high-quality controller with back-paddles instead.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need a 240Hz monitor for ZZZ 3.0 or NTE?
While not strictly necessary, a 240Hz monitor significantly reduces motion blur during high-speed combat. This makes it easier to track enemy movements and time your dodges perfectly, especially in visually dense environments like the city of Hesperia.

### Is the Secretlab chair worth it for shorter gaming sessions?
The benefits of the [TITAN Evo 2022](https://secretlab.co/products/titan-evo-2022-series) are most apparent during sessions lasting four hours or more. If you only play for 30 minutes at a time, the firm cold-cure foam may feel too stiff compared to a softer, cheaper office chair.

### Can I use the Razer Naga V2 Pro on a Mac?
The <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Razer+Naga+V2+Pro&tag=jeffzen911-20" rel="sponsored nofollow noopener" target="_blank">Razer Naga V2 Pro</a> will work as a basic mouse on a Mac, but Razer Synapse software is not officially supported on macOS. This means you will not be able to easily customize the RGB lighting or create complex macros without using third-party software.

### Why is a gasket-mount keyboard better for gaming?
A gasket-mount design, like that found in the Keychron Q1 Pro, provides a slight amount of "flex" when you bottom out a key. This cushioning effect reduces the impact on your fingertips during long, intense combat sessions, leading to less hand fatigue.
