## Overview

![Overview](/img/anton-ivanov-best-build-zzz/inline-1.webp)


Anton Ivanov functions as a primary carry who excels during a target's Stun window. His kit is designed around the "Burst Mode" state, which he enters by consuming Energy through his EX Special Attack. While in this state, his attacks deal Electric damage and gain additional hits known as "Drill Attacks."

His effectiveness is heavily tied to his Core Passive, which allows him to deal additional damage to enemies currently suffering from the Shocked state. This makes him a natural partner for Anomaly Agents who can quickly apply Electric status effects.

### Skill Priority List

To maximize Anton's damage efficiency, prioritize upgrading his skills in the following order:

1.  **Special Attack:** This is the source of his Burst Mode. Higher levels increase the damage multiplier of his drills and the initial Piledriver.
2.  **Basic Attack:** Since Anton spends most of his time in Burst Mode using Enhanced Basic Attacks, this is your primary source of consistent DPS.
3.  **Core Skill:** Essential for increasing the bonus damage dealt to Shocked enemies.
4.  **Ultimate / Chain Attack:** High burst damage, but used less frequently than his Basic/Special rotation.
5.  **Dodge:** Useful for survivability, but provides the lowest contribution to his total damage output.

### Anton's Role and Playstyle

Anton is a "selfish" DPS. He requires a significant amount of field time and Energy to function. Your goal is to keep him off-field while your Stun and Anomaly Agents build up Daze and apply the Shocked status. Once the enemy is stunned and Shocked, Anton enters the fray, activates Burst Mode, and executes his high-damage drill combos. 

### Core Mechanics: Burst Mode Explained

Burst Mode is the defining feature of Anton's gameplay. By using his EX Special Attack, "Mag-Lev Drill-Borne Strike," Anton enters this enhanced state. 

*   **Energy Consumption:** While in Burst Mode, Anton continuously consumes Energy. The mode ends when his Energy reaches zero or when he is swapped out.
*   **Drill Attacks vs. Piledrivers:** During Burst Mode, his Basic Attacks become multi-hit "Drill Attacks." If you continue to tap or hold the attack button, the drill stays active, dealing multiple ticks of Electric damage. The final hit of a combo is usually a "Piledriver," which deals a large burst of damage but ends that specific attack sequence.
*   **Shock Synergy:** Every time a Drill Attack hits a Shocked enemy, Anton's Core Passive triggers, dealing a percentage of his ATK as extra damage. Because the drill hits so many times per second, this "extra" damage becomes his primary source of DPS.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/anton-ivanov-best-build-zzz/inline-5.webp)


Playing Anton effectively requires discipline. You cannot simply stay on him and mash buttons; you must manage his Energy like a resource.

### Optimal Combat Sequence

1.  **Preparation (Stun/Anomaly):** Start the fight with your Stun Agent (like Anby or Koleda). Build the enemy's Daze meter.
2.  **Anomaly Application:** Switch to your Anomaly Agent (like Grace) or use Electric attacks to apply the **Shocked** status.
3.  **The Setup:** Once the enemy is nearly stunned, ensure Anton has at least 50% Energy.
4.  **The Burst:** Trigger a Chain Attack to stun the enemy. Select Anton as the final hit of the Chain Attack sequence.
5.  **Drill Down:** Immediately use Anton's **EX Special Attack** to enter Burst Mode. 
6.  **The Combo:** Hold the Basic Attack button to perform the multi-hit drill. After the third or fourth hit (the Piledriver), reset the combo or use another Special Attack if Energy permits.
7.  **Exit:** Once the enemy recovers from Stun or Anton runs out of Energy, swap him out immediately to begin the cycle again.

### Managing Energy in Burst Mode

Anton’s Energy drains per second while in Burst Mode. To maximize this time, avoid using his Dodge unless absolutely necessary, as it consumes time without dealing optimal damage. If you have his **Mindscape Cinema 1**, hitting Shocked enemies will restore small amounts of Energy, significantly extending your Burst duration. If you find yourself running out of Energy before the enemy is dead, consider switching to an Energy Regen Disc in Slot 6.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/anton-ivanov-best-build-zzz/inline-6.webp)


### Is Anton Ivanov better than other Attack Agents?
Anton is specialized. In terms of raw, consistent Physical damage, Agents like Billy or Nekomata might feel more fluid. However, in an Electric-focused team against enemies weak to Electric, Anton’s burst damage during the Shock window is among the highest in the A-Rank category. He is a "niche king" who excels when the team is built around him.

### What are Anton's most important Mindscape Cinema levels?
*   **M1 (Keep the Spirit High):** This is his most important upgrade. It allows Anton to recover Energy when hitting Shocked enemies in Burst Mode, solving his biggest weakness.
*   **M6 (Limit Break):** This significantly increases the damage of his Drill Attacks during Burst Mode, cementing his role as a top-tier DPS.

### Can Anton be played as a Physical DPS?
It is not recommended. While his non-burst attacks deal Physical damage, his entire scaling, Core Passive, and signature W-Engine focus on Electric damage. Playing him as Physical ignores more than 60% of his potential power.

### Where can I get Anton's upgrade materials?
Anton requires **Augment Chips** (Basic, Advanced, and Specialized Burner Chips) for his skills, which are found in the Combat Simulation training room. His promotion materials include **Attack Seals**, also found in the VR training center. For his higher-level Core Skill upgrades, you will need to defeat the **Weekly Boss** challenges.
