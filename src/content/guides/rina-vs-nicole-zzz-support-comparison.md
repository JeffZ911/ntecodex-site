Choosing the right support can be the difference between a timed finish and a failed run in the high-stakes combat of Zenless Zone Zero.

# Rina vs Nicole: The Ultimate ZZZ Support Comparison Guide

In the current meta of Zenless Zone Zero, the support role is dominated by two primary archetypes: the premium, stat-scaling utility of Alexandrina Sebastiane (Rina) and the accessible, crowd-controlling power of Nicole Demara. While both agents serve to amplify your team's damage, they do so through fundamentally different mechanics—PEN Ratio and DEF Shred—that dictate which teams they belong in and how much investment they require to shine.

## TL;DR Verdict

![TL;DR Verdict](/img/rina-vs-nicole-zzz-support-comparison/inline-1.webp)


If you are looking for a quick answer on which support to prioritize, the choice depends heavily on your current main DPS and your willingness to farm specific Drive Disc stats.

*   **Rina (Alexandrina Sebastiane)** is the premium S-Rank support specializing in PEN Ratio buffs and Electric team synergy. She is best for players who have invested in Victoria Housekeeping units or high-frequency Electric attackers.
*   **Nicole Demara** is the F2P-friendly A-Rank queen of DEF Shred and crowd control. She is essential for Ether-based compositions and remains the most versatile support for burst-heavy rotations.
*   **Choose Rina** if you are running Victoria Housekeeping (Ellen Joe, Von Lycaon) or Mono-Electric (Anton, Grace) teams.
*   **Choose Nicole** for Cunning Hares (Billy, Anby, Nekomata) or high-burst Ether teams featuring Zhu Yuan.
*   **In the current meta**, Nicole's 40% DEF Shred often provides more immediate value for most players, but Rina's PEN Ratio scales better into the late game against enemies with massive defense pools.

### The Case for Rina
Rina is a "hands-off" support who provides massive utility without hogging field time. Her core mechanic allows her to stay off-field while her puppets, Drusilla and Anastella, maintain a PEN Ratio buff for the active agent. Because PEN Ratio is a percentage-based stat that ignores a portion of the enemy's total defense, Rina’s value increases exponentially as you encounter higher-level bosses in Shiyu Defense. Furthermore, her ability to extend the duration of the Shock anomaly makes her the undisputed backbone of any Electric-centric roster.

### The Case for Nicole
Nicole is arguably the most valuable A-Rank agent in the game due to her unique grouping ability and universal DEF Shred. Her EX Special Attack creates a miniature black hole that pulls enemies together, making it easy for your DPS to land AoE hits. Unlike Rina, who requires specific PEN stats on her gear to reach her full potential, Nicole provides a flat 20% to 40% DEF reduction almost immediately. For F2P players or those focusing on the Ether attribute, Nicole is not just a support; she is a core requirement for maximizing damage during a Chain Attack window.

## Detailed Comparison

![Detailed Comparison](/img/rina-vs-nicole-zzz-support-comparison/inline-3.webp)


To truly master the support meta, you must look beyond rarity and understand how PEN Ratio and DEF Shred interact with the game's damage formulas.

### Buffing Mechanics: PEN Ratio vs DEF Shred
The most significant difference between Rina and Nicole is how they mitigate enemy defense. 

Rina’s [Core Passive](https://ntecodex.com/faq), **"Mini-Destructive Partner,"** grants the team a PEN Ratio buff based on her own stats. At maximum level, the calculation is 25% of Rina's PEN Ratio plus a base of 12%, capped at a total of 30%. This means Rina requires heavy investment in PEN Ratio [Drive Discs](https://ntecodex.com/guides/best-nvme-ssd-gacha-gaming-sessions) (specifically in Slot 5) and W-Engines like *Weeping Cradle* or *Slice of Time* to hit her ceiling. PEN Ratio is a "piercing" stat; it allows your damage to bypass a percentage of the enemy's defense. This is statistically superior against "tanky" bosses with high base DEF.

Nicole’s [Core Passive](https://ntecodex.com/faq), **"Mechanical Case,"** applies a DEF Shred debuff to enemies hit by her Energy Field or enhanced bullets. This reduces the enemy's defense by 20% to 40% for 3.5 seconds. While the duration is short, it is easily refreshed by Nicole’s multi-hit Energy Field. DEF Shred is generally more effective in the early-to-mid game because it reduces the enemy's defense value directly before your damage is calculated. 

**Pro Tip:** Do not run Rina and Nicole together in the same team. PEN Ratio and DEF Shred are multiplicative in the ZZZ damage formula. Stacking both leads to diminishing returns, as the PEN Ratio will only calculate its "pierce" based on the already reduced defense provided by the Shred. You are almost always better off picking one and pairing them with a Stun or Anomaly agent.

### Crowd Control and Field Presence
Nicole is the undisputed queen of grouping. Her EX Special Attack, **"Stuffed Sugarcoated Bullet,"** creates a vortex that sucks in smaller mobs and keeps them stationary. This utility is vital for characters like Billy Kid or Zhu Yuan, who rely on hitting multiple targets with their burst skills. Nicole’s field presence is active; you swap her in, fire the vortex, and immediately Quick Assist to your DPS.

Rina, by contrast, provides almost zero grouping but offers incredible mobility. She "floats" during her animations, allowing her to avoid ground-based attacks more easily than most agents. Her puppets, Drusilla and Anastella, act as autonomous units. Once Rina launches an attack, the puppets stay on the field for several seconds even after she swaps out. This allows for a "perpetual" buff uptime if you manage your rotations correctly, as the PEN Ratio buff remains active as long as the puppets are deployed.

### Team Synergy and Faction Bonuses
Faction synergy is a major factor in team building. Rina belongs to **Victoria Housekeeping**, meaning she gains an Additional Ability when paired with Ellen Joe or Von Lycaon. Her **"Banquet of Perfection"** increases the duration of Shock by 3 seconds and boosts the team’s Electric DMG by 10% when Shocked enemies are present. This makes her the perfect partner for Grace or Anton in a "Mono-Electric" or "Shock-Focus" team.

Nicole belongs to the **Cunning Hares**. When paired with another Cunning Hare (like Anby or Nekomata) or another Ether agent (like Zhu Yuan), she activates **"Hare of Many Burrows."** This grants the entire squad a 25% Ether DMG bonus against enemies affected by her DEF Shred. Because she is currently the only Ether support available, she is a non-negotiable inclusion for any Zhu Yuan-led team.

### Best W-Engines and Drive Discs
Investment levels vary wildly between these two. Rina is a "stat-hungry" unit, whereas Nicole is a "cooldown-hungry" unit.

**For Rina:**
*   **Best W-Engine:** *Weeping Cradle* (S-Rank). This is her signature weapon and provides the massive PEN Ratio she needs to reach her 30% buff cap.
*   **A-Rank Alternative:** *Slice of Time*. This provides PEN Ratio and helps with Decibel generation.
*   **[Drive Discs](https://ntecodex.com/guides/best-nvme-ssd-gacha-gaming-sessions):** 4-piece *Swing Jazz* (for team DMG) and 2-piece *Puffer Electro* (for the 8% PEN Ratio). You must have PEN Ratio as the main stat on Disc 5.

**For Nicole:**
*   **Best W-Engine:** *The Vault* (A-Rank). This is her signature weapon and increases the team's DMG when she deals Ether DMG.
*   **S-Rank Alternative:** *Weeping Cradle* can be used on Nicole for the Energy Regen, but *The Vault* is often more practical.
*   **Drive Discs:** 4-piece *Swing Jazz* is standard. For the 2-piece set, *Chaotic Metal* (Ether DMG) or *Swing Jazz* (Energy Regen) works best. Focus on Energy Regen in Disc 6 to ensure her EX Special is always ready.

### Investment and Accessibility
Nicole is a free character given to every player at the start of the game. Her duplicates (Mindscape Cinema) are also easier to acquire. Her **M6 (Corrupting Energy Field)** is a game-changer, providing up to a 15% team-wide CRIT Rate buff when enemies are hit by her energy fields.

Rina is a Standard S-Rank agent. While she is harder to obtain, her **M1 (Dance Duet)** is one of the strongest early duplicates in the game. It increases the duration her puppets stay on the field by 5.5 seconds and boosts her PEN Ratio buff effectiveness to 130% if the active character is within 10 meters of the puppets. This essentially removes the difficulty of managing her buff uptime.

## Recommendation

![Recommendation](/img/rina-vs-nicole-zzz-support-comparison/inline-4.webp)


The "best" support depends on the content you are tackling and the DPS agents you have pulled.

### Best Teams for Rina
*   **The Victoria Housekeeping Meta:** Ellen Joe (DPS), Von Lycaon (Stun), Rina (Support). This is currently one of the highest-performing teams in the game, utilizing Rina's PEN buffs to let Ellen shred through Ice-resistant enemies.
*   **The Mono-Electric Shock Team:** Grace (Anomaly), Anton (DPS), Rina (Support). Rina’s Shock extension is the glue that holds this team together, allowing Anton to stay in his burst state longer.

### Best Teams for Nicole
*   **The Ether Burst Team:** Zhu Yuan (DPS), Anby/Qingyi (Stun), Nicole (Support). Nicole’s grouping and Ether DMG buff are perfectly timed for Zhu Yuan’s massive burst window when an enemy is stunned.
*   **The F2P Physical Team:** Billy/Nekomata (DPS), Anby (Stun), Nicole (Support). Even in non-Ether teams, Nicole’s grouping is often more valuable for clearing waves of small mobs than Rina’s PEN Ratio.

### Final Thoughts on the Support Meta
In the current endgame of Shiyu Defense, you usually need two distinct teams. The most efficient way to build your roster is to use Rina on your Electric or Ice side to take advantage of her high-scaling PEN Ratio, and use Nicole on your Physical or Ether side for her superior crowd control.

**Final Verdict:** 
Build **Rina** if you want a high-ceiling support that scales into the late game and fits perfectly into the Victoria Housekeeping or Electric meta.
Build **Nicole** if you need an immediate, universal support who offers the best grouping utility in the game and provides essential buffs for burst-oriented teams. 

While Rina wins on pure scaling and ease of buff uptime (especially at M1), Nicole wins on utility, accessibility, and the sheer power of her M6 CRIT Rate buff. Most players will eventually want both built to handle the elemental requirements of the endgame.
