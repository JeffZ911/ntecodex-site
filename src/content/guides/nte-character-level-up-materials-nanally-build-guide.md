## Overview

![Overview](/img/nte-character-level-up-materials-nanally-build-guide/inline-1.webp)


Nanally is an S-Rank Anima Esper released during the NTE launch window on April 29, 2026. She excels at dealing sustained area-of-effect (AoE) damage while maintaining high mobility. Unlike traditional RPGs where you can grind levels indefinitely, NTE implements a "Hunter Rank" and "Appraisal Level" system that hard-gates character progression. You cannot simply dump experience into Nanally; you must actively engage with world exploration and specific combat challenges to raise the world's Appraisal Level, which in turn unlocks higher level caps for your roster.

### Nanally Character Profile
Nanally belongs to the Anima element and utilizes Plasma-type Arcs. Her gameplay is defined by the "Ichi-daime's Authority" state, which she enters after using her Skill or Ultimate. In this state, she summons the "Underboss," an autonomous entity that mimics her attacks, effectively doubling her hit frequency. This makes her the perfect candidate for elemental reactions that scale with hit count, such as Blossom (Anima + Cosmos).

### How to Farm Character Level Up Materials
Leveling Nanally requires three tiers of Hunter Guides. These items provide flat experience points to your Espers and are the primary way to reach the current level cap.

## Best Weapons

![Best Weapons](/img/nte-character-level-up-materials-nanally-build-guide/inline-2.webp)


In Neverness to Everness, weapons are known as Arcs. These equippable disks provide a base ATK stat and a unique passive effect that can be refined up to five times (M1–M5). For Nanally, you should prioritize Arcs that offer CRIT Rate or Anima DMG to ensure the Underboss’s follow-up attacks are consistently powerful.

### Top Tier: Ready-Ready (Signature)
**Ready-Ready** is Nanally's signature S-Rank Plasma Arc and is widely considered her Best-in-Slot (BiS) option. It provides a massive CRIT Rate substat (up to 24% at max level), which is essential because Nanally’s kit relies on critical hits to trigger certain passive buffs. 

The unique effect, "Commander Tiger Talisman," increases ATK by 15% and boosts Basic Attack and Critical Riposte damage by 15% for 15 seconds after casting a Skill or Ultimate. If you manage to stack both the "Left" and "Right" Tiger Talismans within 15 seconds, you unlock the full Commander Tiger Talisman effect, which grants an additional 10% damage bonus against Bosses.

### Best F2P Option: Oraora!
For players who did not pull for the signature weapon, **Oraora!** is the premier A-Rank Plasma alternative. While it lacks the CRIT Rate of Ready-Ready, it offers a substantial ATK% substat. Its passive, "Ora Puncher," increases Basic Attack damage by 2% for every hit landed, stacking up to 10 times. Since Nanally’s rotation involves long strings of Basic Attacks while the Underboss is active, she can maintain maximum stacks with ease.

### Alternative S-Rank: Song of the Whale
**Song of the Whale** is a powerful S-Rank Arc that can be obtained for free through high-level world exploration and defeating the "Sea Prisoner" boss in the Nautily Tunnel. It provides a 12% ATK boost and increases damage to enemies in the "Broken" (staggered) state by 12%. It also offers a 30% HP heal upon defeating a Broken enemy, providing Nanally with much-needed sustain during difficult solo challenges.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/nte-character-level-up-materials-nanally-build-guide/inline-3.webp)


NTE utilizes a "Console" system where you equip Cartridges and Modules to boost stats. This isn't just a simple gear slot; it’s a Tetris-style grid where the shape and placement of your Modules matter.

### Best Set: Fireflies and the Forest
The **Fireflies and the Forest** set is the undisputed BiS for Nanally. 
*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Increases Anima DMG by 10%.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** Increases the wearer's CRIT DMG by 8% whenever a nearby enemy takes Anima DMG from the team. This stacks up to 7 times for a total of 56% CRIT DMG. 

Because the Underboss deals frequent Anima DMG even when Nanally is dodging, she can reach max stacks almost instantly. Notably, this buff remains active even if Nanally is swapped out, making her a potent off-field Sub-DPS in certain quick-swap teams.

### The Console System: Type-2 Module Synergy
Every character in NTE has a unique Console passive. Nanally’s passive grants a **6% CRIT Rate bonus for every Type-2 Module** equipped. When arranging your grid, you must first fit the four required pieces for the Fireflies and the Forest set, then fill every remaining slot with Type-2 Modules. A well-optimized grid can provide over 30% "free" CRIT Rate before considering substats.

### Recommended Main Stats and Substats
*   **Cartridge Main Stat:** Anima DMG Bonus (Priority) or CRIT DMG.
*   **Module Fixed Stats:** Flat ATK and Flat HP.
*   **Substat Priority:** CRIT Rate > CRIT DMG > ATK% > Energy Regen.

Aim for a minimum of 75% CRIT Rate to ensure the Fireflies set and Underboss follow-ups trigger reliably.

## Team Comp

![Team Comp](/img/nte-character-level-up-materials-nanally-build-guide/inline-4.webp)


The current meta revolves around the **Esper Cycle**, a mechanic where filling a meter allows you to swap characters and trigger elemental reactions.

### The Meta: Blossom Nuke Team
The "Blossom" reaction occurs when Anima and Cosmos elements collide. It summons a "Blossom Bud" that fires autonomous attacks at enemies.
*   **Main DPS: Nanally (Anima).** Provides the core damage and triggers the Blossom follow-ups.
*   **Battery/Support: Zero (Cosmos).** Essential for his Skill, which instantly fills the Esper Meter, allowing for back-to-back Blossom procs.
*   **Crowd Control: Jiuyuan (Anima).** Groups enemies together so Nanally’s Underboss can hit multiple targets. Jiuyuan also increases the maximum number of Blossom Buds allowed on the field.
*   **Flex Buffer: [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) (Incantation).** Provides a 30% ATK buff and pulls enemies with her "Devour Whole" skill. Swapping from Nanally to Sakiri also triggers **Hexed**, which stores a portion of Nanally's damage and releases it as a massive burst.

### The Boss Shredder: Nanally & Daffodill
For boss encounters with massive stagger bars, swap Sakiri for **Daffodill (Chaos)**. Daffodill possesses high Break Intensity, allowing her to shred shields quickly. Once the boss is "Broken," Nanally can swap in to utilize the damage bonuses from the Song of the Whale Arc or her own Ichi-daime's Authority state.

### F2P Friendly: Nanally, Zero, Edgar, Mint
If you are playing on a budget, use the starter characters. **Zero** remains a top-tier support for Nanally due to the Cosmos element. **Edgar** provides consistent healing, while **Mint** can help trigger Blossom and widen the AoE of your attacks.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/nte-character-level-up-materials-nanally-build-guide/inline-5.webp)


To maximize Nanally’s damage, you must manage her "Ichi-daime's Authority" state and the Esper Cycle meter simultaneously.

### Opening Sequence
1.  Start with **Jiuyuan's Skill** to apply the Lethal Rose Pact and group enemies.
2.  Swap to **Sakiri** and use her hold-skill to pull any remaining stragglers into the center.
3.  Cast **Nanally's Skill (Colucci Howling Technique)**. This triggers her Ichi-daime's Authority, granting her a 30% CRIT Rate boost and summoning the Underboss.

### The Underboss Burst Window
Once the Underboss is active, immediately cast **Nanally's Ultimate (Colucci Ultimate Technique)**. This provides a massive ATK buff and refreshes the duration of her state. Follow this with a full Basic Attack string. Every hit from Nanally will cause the Underboss to strike, doubling your damage output and rapidly stacking the CRIT DMG buff from the Fireflies set.

### Managing the Esper Cycle
When the Esper Meter is full, swap to **Zero**. Use his Skill to instantly refill the meter, then swap back to **Nanally**. This triggers the Blossom reaction, spawning a Bud that assists in the fight. If you are using the Ready-Ready Arc, ensure you perform a **Critical Riposte** (perfect dodge + attack) to maintain your Tiger Talisman stacks.

## FAQ

### Progression Questions
**Where do I find Anomaly Hunt materials?**
These are dropped by world bosses and elite enemies. Use the Exploration Guide in your menu to track specific targets. Bosses like the Sea Prisoner or Houdinii are the most common sources for high-tier Ascension materials.

**How do I unlock the level 40 cap?**
You must reach Hunter Rank 20 and complete the "Hunter Ascension: Part One" quest. This quest involves a combat challenge at Houdinii’s Magic Stage. Once cleared, your Appraisal Level rises to 2, and the level cap for all characters and Arcs increases to 40.

### Gacha and Pity Mechanics
**Is Nanally's signature weapon required?**
No. While Ready-Ready is her best option, a max-refinement (M5) **Oraora!** performs at approximately 84% efficiency compared to the signature weapon. It is a perfectly viable choice for F2P players.

**What is the best way to farm Beetle Coins?**
Houdinii's Magic Stage is the most efficient method for farming currency. Additionally, raising your **City Tycoon** level allows you to generate Fons and Beetle Coins passively over time.

### Resource Management
**Does the Fireflies set work off-field?**
Yes. The 4-piece CRIT DMG buff specifically states that the effect remains active even when the character is off-field. This allows Nanally to act as a powerful Sub-DPS who buffs the team's overall critical potential.

**What is the "Type-2" Module requirement?**
Nanally's Console has a specific synergy with Type-2 Modules (the "L" and "T" shaped pieces). Equipping these gives her a flat 6% CRIT Rate bonus per piece. Always prioritize these shapes when filling the empty spots on your Console grid after completing your 4-piece Cartridge set.
