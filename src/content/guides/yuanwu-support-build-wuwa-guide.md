## Overview

![Overview](/img/yuanwu-support-build-wuwa-guide/inline-1.webp)


Yuanwu is a 4-star Electro Resonator who has carved out a specific niche in the Wuthering Waves meta as a low-field-time support and sub-DPS. Unlike most characters who prioritize Attack, Yuanwu’s kit scales primarily with Defense (DEF), making him exceptionally durable while providing consistent off-field Electro application. His primary contribution to a team is his ability to deploy a "Thunder Wedge" that performs Coordinated Attacks, allowing your Main DPS to trigger elemental reactions and build concerto energy without Yuanwu needing to be active on the field.

One of Yuanwu's most valuable traits is his accessibility. He is a free-to-play friendly unit that players can obtain early on by clearing the Stable Zone of the Tower of Adversity. This makes him a staple for players looking to bolster their second or third teams for endgame content. Furthermore, his ability to deplete an enemy's Vibration Strength (the white stagger bar below the health bar) is among the highest in the game, allowing teams to stun bosses much faster than usual.

### Pros and Cons for Support Yuanwu

**Pros:**
*   **High DEF Scaling:** Naturally tanky and less prone to being one-shot during quick-swap rotations.
*   **Off-Field Presence:** His Resonance Skill provides long-lasting Coordinated Attacks.
*   **Shield Breaking:** Excellent at reducing Vibration Strength, especially with his Outro Skill.
*   **F2P Friendly:** Obtainable for free and works effectively with 3-star and 4-star weapons.
*   **Jinhsi Synergy:** Currently the best partner for Jinhsi to generate Forte stacks.

**Cons:**
*   **Limited Personal DMG:** As a support build, his raw damage output is lower than dedicated sub-DPS units like Yinlin.
*   **Stationary Skill:** The Thunder Wedge has a fixed radius; if a boss moves away, the Wedge must be redeployed.
*   **High Energy Requirements:** Needs significant Energy Regen to maintain his Resonance Liberation uptime.

### Yuanwu's Role in the Meta

In the current meta, Yuanwu functions as a "bridge" support. He is rarely the star of the show in terms of damage numbers, but he enables high-tier DPS characters to reach their maximum potential. His role is defined by his minimal field time requirement. A typical Yuanwu rotation lasts only a few seconds, which is ideal for "hypercarry" setups where the Main DPS wants to stay on the field for as long as possible. His specific interaction with the Vibration Strength mechanic makes him a top-tier pick for boss encounters where staggering the enemy is the only way to bypass difficult phases.

### Key Mechanics: Thunder Wedge and Readiness

The core of Yuanwu’s gameplay revolves around his Resonance Skill, **Leaping Thunder**. Upon activation, he summons a Thunder Wedge. While active, any character within the Wedge’s range who deals damage will trigger a Coordinated Attack from Yuanwu. These attacks scale with his DEF and deal Electro DMG.

His Forte Circuit, **Unyielding Spirit**, involves a "Readiness" bar. When Yuanwu is in the field or when Coordinated Attacks are triggered, he builds Readiness. Once full, he can enter the **Lightning Infused** state, which enhances his Basic Attacks and increases his resistance to interruption. However, in a pure support build, you will rarely use this state, instead focusing on the Wedge's off-field utility.

Choosing a weapon for Yuanwu requires balancing his DEF scaling with his need for Energy Regeneration (ER). Since his Resonance Skill and Resonance Liberation scale off DEF, standard ATK-based gauntlets provide very little value to his actual damage output.

### Top Tier: Abyss Surges

Abyss Surges is the premium choice for Yuanwu, primarily because of its high base stats and the Energy Regen secondary stat. While the ATK% passive is largely wasted on Yuanwu, the 38.8% Energy Regen (at level 90) is invaluable. It ensures that Yuanwu can use his Resonance Liberation every rotation without needing perfect sub-stats on his Echoes. This weapon is recommended if you have an extra copy that is not being used by a Main DPS like Xiangli Yao.

### Best 4-Star Options: Amity Accord vs. Marcato

**Amity Accord** is widely considered the "best in slot" for Yuanwu’s personal damage in a support role. It is one of the few weapons in the game that provides a DEF% secondary stat (61.5% at level 90). The passive further boosts his Resonance Liberation DMG, which is his hardest-hitting ability. If you are looking to maximize Yuanwu’s contribution to team DPS while maintaining his support role, this is the weapon to use.

**Marcato** is the superior choice for utility. It provides Energy Regen as a secondary stat and its passive restores Concerto Energy. This allows Yuanwu to trigger his Outro Skill significantly faster. If your goal is to swap Yuanwu in, drop his skill, and get out as quickly as possible, Marcato is the most efficient tool for the job.

### F2P and Budget Alternatives

**Guardian Gauntlets** are a hidden gem for Yuanwu. As a 3-star weapon, they are incredibly easy to max out (Rank 5). They provide a 38.2% DEF secondary stat, which is higher than many 4-star ATK-based options. For a low-investment Yuanwu, these are often better than 4-star weapons that don't offer DEF or ER.

**Originite: Type IV** is used for a very specific "Healer Yuanwu" build. Because the weapon heals the user on hit, it allows Yuanwu to trigger the 5-piece effect of the **Rejuvenating Glow** Echo set, which grants an ATK buff to the entire team when the wearer heals an ally or themselves. This is a niche but effective way to turn Yuanwu into a pure buffer.

## Best Echoes

![Best Echoes](/img/yuanwu-support-build-wuwa-guide/inline-3.webp)


For a support build, your primary goal with Echoes is to provide team-wide buffs and ensure Yuanwu has enough Energy Regen to sustain his rotation.

### Best Sonata Effects: Moonlit Clouds

The **Moonlit Clouds** (5-piece) set is the definitive choice for a support Yuanwu.
*   **2-Piece Effect:** Increases Energy Regen by 10%.
*   **5-Piece Effect:** Upon using the Outro Skill, the next Resonator to enter the field gains a 22.5% ATK increase for 15 seconds.

This set perfectly aligns with Yuanwu’s quick-swap playstyle. Since he can generate Concerto Energy relatively quickly with his skill and multiple hits from the Thunder Wedge, he can consistently trigger the 22.5% ATK buff for your Main DPS.

### Main Echo Choice: Bell-Borne Geochelone vs. Impermanence Heron

The choice of Main Echo depends on your team's needs:

1.  **Bell-Borne Geochelone:** This is the preferred choice for general support. It provides a shield based on the user's DEF and reduces incoming DMG for the entire team. Since Yuanwu already scales with DEF, the shield is quite sturdy. It also provides a 10% DMG boost while the shield is active.
2.  **Impermanence Heron:** This is used exclusively with the Moonlit Clouds set to maximize damage buffs. When the Heron’s initial hit connects, it restores 10 Resonance Energy to Yuanwu. If he uses his Outro Skill within the next 15 seconds, the next character gains a 12% DMG boost. Pairing this with the Moonlit Clouds 5-piece effect results in a massive buff for your Main DPS.

While **Moonlit Clouds** is the standard, **Rejuvenating Glow** is a viable alternative if you are using the **Originite: Type IV** gauntlets.
*   **Moonlit Clouds:** Provides a 22.5% ATK buff to the *next* character. Better for hypercarries like Jiyan or Jinhsi.
*   **Rejuvenating Glow:** Provides a 15% ATK buff to the *entire* team. This is better in dual-DPS compositions where both characters benefit from the ATK increase.

### The Jinhsi-Yuanwu "Meta" Duo

This is currently Yuanwu’s most popular and effective team. Jinhsi’s Forte Circuit, **Incarnation**, requires her to gain "Incandescence" stacks to deal maximum damage. These stacks are generated whenever her teammates deal damage. Because Yuanwu’s Thunder Wedge triggers a Coordinated Attack every 1.2 seconds for a long duration, he provides a constant stream of stacks for Jinhsi while she is on the field.

*   **Main DPS:** Jinhsi
*   **Sub-DPS/Support:** Yuanwu

In this team, Yuanwu requires almost zero field time. You simply swap him in, drop the Wedge, and swap back to Jinhsi.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/yuanwu-support-build-wuwa-guide/inline-5.webp)


The key to playing Yuanwu effectively is minimizing his field time. Every second Yuanwu spends punching is a second your Main DPS is not dealing damage.

### Standard Support Rotation

1.  **Start with Yuanwu:** If his Resonance Skill is off cooldown, use it immediately to deploy the Thunder Wedge.
2.  **Generate Concerto:** Use his Resonance Skill, followed by a few Basic Attacks if necessary to fill the Concerto bar. If his Resonance Liberation is available, use it now.
3.  **Echo Skill:** Use your Main Echo (Bell-Borne Geochelone or Impermanence Heron).
4.  **Outro Skill:** Swap to your Main DPS to trigger Yuanwu’s Outro Skill and the Moonlit Clouds 5-piece buff.
5.  **Main DPS Window:** Perform your full rotation with your Main DPS. The Coordinated Attacks from the Wedge will trigger automatically.
6.  **Repeat:** Once the Wedge expires or your Main DPS finishes their rotation, swap back to Yuanwu.

### Step-by-Step Quick-swap Rotation Guide

*   **Switch In:** Yuanwu enters the field.
*   **Skill:** Press [E] to deploy **Leaping Thunder**.
*   **Burst:** Press [R] to use **Blazing Fist** (if energy is full). This provides a large chunk of Concerto and deals high Vibration DMG.
*   **Echo:** Use the Echo active ability.
*   **Switch Out:** Immediately swap to the next character in the rotation.

### Maximizing Concerto Energy Generation

Concerto Energy is vital for triggering Yuanwu’s Outro Skill, **Lightning Field**. This skill is essential because it reduces the enemy's Vibration Strength. To build Concerto quickly:
*   Ensure the Thunder Wedge is hitting enemies.
*   Use his Resonance Liberation whenever it is off cooldown.
*   Equip the **Marcato** weapon if you find your Concerto generation is too slow.

### Tips for Positioning the Thunder Wedge

The Thunder Wedge has a respectable radius, but mobile bosses like the **Crownless** or **Tempest Mephis** can easily dash out of it. If the boss moves:
1.  Do not immediately swap back to Yuanwu unless your Main DPS has finished their burst window.
2.  Wait for a natural gap in your rotation to swap Yuanwu back in and reposition the Wedge.
3.  Try to place the Wedge in the center of the arena rather than near the edges to maximize the area covered.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/yuanwu-support-build-wuwa-guide/inline-6.webp)


**Does Yuanwu scale with ATK or DEF?**
Yuanwu scales primarily with DEF for his Resonance Skill (Thunder Wedge) and his Resonance Liberation. While his Basic Attacks scale with ATK, a support build should prioritize DEF to maximize his utility and off-field damage.

**How do I get Yuanwu for free?**
You can obtain a free copy of Yuanwu by completing the **Tower of Adversity: Stable Zone**. This is a permanent challenge available to all players once they reach the required Union Level.

**Is Yuanwu better than Yinlin?**
They serve different purposes. Yinlin is a high-damage Sub-DPS who buffs Electro DMG. Yuanwu is a utility-focused support who excels at breaking enemy shields (Vibration Strength) and providing consistent Coordinated Attacks for characters like Jinhsi. In many endgame scenarios, Yuanwu is preferred for Jinhsi teams, while Yinlin is preferred for Calcharo or Xiangli Yao teams.

**What is Yuanwu's best Resonance Chain?**
*   **S4 (Resonance Chain 4):** Provides a shield to the entire team when Yuanwu uses his Resonance Liberation. This significantly increases team survivability.
*   **S6 (Resonance Chain 6):** Increases the DEF of all nearby party members by 20% for 15 seconds after using Resonance Liberation. This is a massive buff for Yuanwu himself and any other DEF-scaling characters.

**Can Yuanwu be a Main DPS?**
Yes, it is possible by using the **Void Thunder** Echo set and focusing on his **Lightning Infused** state from his Forte Circuit. However, his damage ceilings are generally lower than dedicated 5-star DPS units. His support/sub-DPS build using Moonlit Clouds is considered his most efficient use of resources.
