## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/best-ergonomic-gear-for-long-gacha-mmo-sessions/inline-4.webp)


While high-end gear can significantly improve your experience, it isn't always a necessary purchase. If you do not experience back or wrist pain after 4 hours of play, your current ergonomic setup is likely sufficient. Ergonomics is highly individual, and if your body is already well-supported, a $500 chair may not offer a noticeable improvement in your gameplay.

Avoid upgrading your monitor if your current GPU cannot maintain at least 60 FPS at a higher resolution. Moving to a 1440p Ultrawide like the <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=LG+34GP83A-B&tag=jeffzen911-20" rel="sponsored nofollow noopener" target="_blank">LG 34GP83A-B</a> requires significant graphical power. If your frame rate drops too low, the resulting stuttering can cause more eye strain than a lower-resolution, smoother-running screen.

Furthermore, skip high-end MMO mice if you prefer using a controller. Neverness to Everness and other modern gacha titles offer robust controller support for driving and combat. If you spend most of your time with a gamepad in hand, the macro benefits of a 12-button mouse will be lost, and you would be better off investing that budget into a more comfortable chair or a high-quality wrist rest.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How can I reduce wrist pain during long gacha farming sessions?
Wrist pain is often caused by repetitive clicking and poor angles. ### Does a curved ultrawide monitor really help with eye strain?
Yes, the curve of a monitor like the <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=LG+34GP83A-B&tag=jeffzen911-20" rel="sponsored nofollow noopener" target="_blank">LG 34GP83A-B</a> is designed to keep all parts of the screen at a similar distance from your eyes.
