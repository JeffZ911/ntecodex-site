# Best Build for Mako (Nanally) in Neverness to Everness (NTE)

## Overview

![Overview](/img/best-build-for-mako-nte-20260521/inline-1.webp)


While the community frequently refers to her as "Mako" because of her distinct shark-themed design and sharp-toothed grin, her official name in Neverness to Everness is Nanally Coluccis. She is the first limited S-rank character, appearing on "The Ichi-daime" banner at launch. Nanally is an Anima-element Main DPS belonging to the Plasma archetype. Her kit is designed for high-frequency melee combat, utilizing "Fangs and Claws" to shred through enemy defenses with a mix of rapid strikes and coordinated follow-up damage.

The core of Nanally’s power lies in her ability to maintain pressure both on and off the field. Unlike typical hypercarries who lose all utility when swapped out, Nanally leaves behind a trail of destruction through her passive triggers and her summon, the Underboss. Her mobility is also unmatched; she is currently the only character capable of ignoring gravity during combat, allowing her to transition from ground combos to wall-running strikes seamlessly.

### Why Nanally is the Best Launch DPS

Nanally stands at the top of the launch meta because her [damage scaling](https://ntecodex.com/faq) is exceptionally efficient. As a Plasma-type character, she benefits from offensive stat growth that prioritizes burst potential. Her kit is perfectly tuned for the "Blossom" reaction (Anima + Cosmos), which is currently the most accessible and high-performing elemental interaction for new players. 

Furthermore, her skill floor is relatively forgiving. While her ceiling is high due to the spatial awareness required for her anti-gravity wall-running, her basic "hit everything fast" playstyle produces massive numbers even with moderate investment. She excels in both multi-target mob clearing and single-target boss encounters, making her a "one-size-fits-all" solution for the game’s early challenging content.

### Understanding Ichi-daime's Authority

Nanally’s primary combat state is called "Ichi-daime's Authority," which is activated by her Skill, "Colucci Howling Technique." Upon activation, Nanally deals five instances of AoE Anima damage and enters this empowered state for 12 seconds. 

While in Ichi-daime's Authority, she receives two massive buffs:
1.  **CRIT DMG Bonus:** A flat 30% increase to her Critical Damage.
2.  **Anti-Gravity Mobility:** Nanally can run on walls and ceilings, effectively turning the entire environment into her playground.

This state is the engine of her rotation. It also enables her "Fair Duel" passive, which triggers an extra follow-up attack dealing 60% of her ATK as Anima damage whenever any teammate deals Esper Cycle damage (up to once every 2 seconds). To play Nanally effectively, you must ensure that her Skill is always the first button pressed when she enters the field.

### Signature Arc: Ready-Ready

"Ready-Ready" is Nanally’s signature S-rank Arc and her undisputed best-in-slot weapon. At maximum level, it provides a 24% CRIT Rate substat, which is vital for a character whose kit is built around CRIT DMG buffs. 

The unique passive, "Commander Tiger Talisman," is tailor-made for her rotation. It increases ATK by 15% and grants a specific "Talisman" whenever a Skill or Ultimate is used. If you use both within a 15-second window—which you should always do in her standard rotation—you unlock the full Commander effect, which increases damage dealt to Bosses by an additional 10%. This makes Nanally a premier boss-killer, especially when chaining her "Colucci Howling Technique" into her "Colucci Ultimate Technique."

### Top F2P and Budget Alternatives

For players who did not pull her signature weapon, "Oraora!" is the best A-rank alternative. Because Nanally’s combo strings are long and high-frequency, she can reach the maximum 10 stacks of the "Ora Puncher" passive within seconds of starting her rotation. This provides a consistent 20% Basic Attack damage buff that remains active as long as she stays aggressive.

If you have access to the standard S-rank pool, "Fluff of Fortitude" is a strong generalist choice. It provides a massive 22% damage increase across her entire kit, including her follow-up attacks. This is particularly effective during the latter half of boss fights, where the damage bonus jumps to 28% once the enemy falls below 50% HP, helping her execute targets faster.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/best-build-for-mako-nte-20260521/inline-3.webp)


In Neverness to Everness, your "build" is largely defined by the Cartridges and Modules equipped in your Console. For Nanally, the goal is to stack Anima damage and CRIT DMG to capitalize on her high hit rate.

### The Fireflies and the Forest Set

The "Fireflies and the Forest" set is the definitive choice for Nanally. This Cartridge set is designed specifically for Anima multi-hitters.

*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Increases Anima DMG by 10%.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** Whenever a nearby enemy takes Anima DMG from any team member, the wearer gains 8% CRIT DMG. This effect stacks up to 7 times (for a total of 56% CRIT DMG) and each stack lasts 10 seconds.

The beauty of this set is that the 4-piece effect remains active even when Nanally is off-field. If her Underboss or her "Fair Duel" passive deals Anima damage while you are using your supports, she will already have maximum stacks by the time you swap her back in for her burst window. When combined with the 30% CRIT DMG from her Ichi-daime's Authority state, Nanally gains an 86% CRIT DMG bonus before factoring in any substats.

### Module Optimization and Stat Priorities

When slotting Modules into your Console, prioritize **Type II Modules**. Nanally has a specific Console bonus that provides a 6% CRIT Rate increase for every Type II Module equipped. Filling these slots first is the easiest way to reach the 100% CRIT Rate cap required for her late-game builds.

**Recommended Main Stats:**
*   **Cartridge Slot:** Anima DMG Bonus (or ATK% if Anima is unavailable).
*   **Primary Module Slot:** CRIT DMG.

**Substat Priority:**
1.  **CRIT Rate:** Aim for 70% minimum (the Console bonus and weapon will help cover the rest).
2.  **CRIT DMG:** Since she has so many internal buffs, more CRIT DMG scales her total output exponentially.
3.  **ATK%:** Increases the base of all her multipliers.
4.  **DMG%:** A universal multiplier for all her claw and Underboss strikes.
5.  **Cycle Intensity:** Helps her trigger reactions like Blossom more frequently.

### Top Meta Team: The Blossom Engine

The "Blossom Engine" is currently Nanally’s strongest team composition. It pairs her with Jiuyuan (Anima) and Zero (Cosmos). When Anima hits a Cosmos-affected target, it triggers the Blossom reaction, creating "Lebenstamm" turrets that fire at enemies.

Nanally has a specific passive called "Blossom Enhancement" (part of her "More Than Passionate" talent). This passive doubles the fire rate of these turrets, causing them to fire 10 "Vita Pistils" instead of the standard 5, and reduces the firing interval to just 1 second. This synergy creates a screen-filling amount of projectiles that all scale with the team's Anima and Cosmos investment. Haniel is the ideal fourth slot here for her Psyche-based ATK buffs and energy generation.

### Alternative Comps: Breaking and Buffing

If you need more shield-breaking capability, the "Discord" variant is highly effective. By using Sakiri (Incantation) and Adler (Incantation), you can trigger the "Hexed" and "Scorch" reactions. When these interact with Nanally's Anima attacks, they create "Discord," which deals massive damage to enemy Break meters. This is the go-to team for high-level Anomaly Zones where bosses have thick elemental shields.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/best-build-for-mako-nte-20260521/inline-5.webp)


Nanally is a "selfish" Main DPS who requires roughly 10 to 15 seconds of on-field time to complete her full damage sequence. Her rotation is built around aligning her character buffs with the Underboss summon.

### Maximizing the Underboss Window

1.  **Setup Phase:** Use your supports (like Haniel or Sakiri) to apply any long-term ATK buffs or crowd-control effects.
2.  **Reaction Trigger:** Swap to Zero (Cosmos) and use his active skill to apply the Cosmos status and prep the Esper Cycle.
3.  **Nanally Entry:** Swap to Nanally and immediately cast **"Colucci Howling Technique"** (Skill). This puts her in the Ichi-daime's Authority state, granting her the 30% CRIT DMG buff.
4.  **Burst Window:** Immediately cast **"Colucci Ultimate Technique"** (Ultimate). This summons the Underboss for 6 seconds.
5.  **Combo Execution:** Perform the "3+3" loop. This consists of three Basic Attacks followed by three Charged Attacks (holding the attack button). The Underboss will coordinate with every single hit, effectively doubling your damage output during this 6-second window.
6.  **Sustain Phase:** Continue using Basic Attack combos until the 12-second Authority timer expires, then swap back to your supports to recharge the Esper Meter.

### Esper Cycle Management

Managing the Esper Cycle is crucial because Nanally’s "Fair Duel" passive only triggers when a teammate deals Esper Cycle damage. If you stay on Nanally for too long without refreshing the Cosmos status from Zero, you will lose out on those 60% ATK follow-up hits. A good rule of thumb is to swap back to Zero the moment the Underboss disappears to reset the Blossom reaction.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/best-build-for-mako-nte-20260521/inline-6.webp)


**Is Mako her real name?**
No, her official name in the game is Nanally Coluccis. "Mako" is a popular community nickname derived from her shark-like design elements, such as her teeth and "Underboss" summon.

**How do I unlock Nanally?**
Nanally was the featured S-rank character on the limited-time "The Ichi-daime" banner at the game's launch. undisclosed regarding when her banner will return for a rerun.

**What is her best Awakening level?**
Her A1 (Gang Formation) is the best stopping point for most players, as it provides a significant ATK boost based on her follow-up attacks. Her A5 (Because We're Family) is her "whale" milestone, as it drastically increases the uptime of her Ichi-daime's Authority state.

**Can she really walk on walls?**
Yes. While her Skill "Colucci Howling Technique" is active, Nanally ignores gravity. This allows her to run up skyscrapers or fight from the ceiling for 12 seconds. This is also highly useful for open-world exploration to reach hidden chests.

**Does she work as an off-field DPS?**
Yes, she is surprisingly viable off-field. Her "Fair Duel" passive and the fact that the Underboss remains on the field even if she is swapped out (provided the Ultimate was cast first) allow her to contribute significant Anima damage while your other characters are active.
