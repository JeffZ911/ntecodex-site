### Understanding Fons and Annulith

Fons serves as the standard "gold" equivalent. While it may seem abundant early on, the costs of high-end car modifications and late-game Cartridge (artifact) leveling create a significant bottleneck. Annulith is much rarer, with a hard cap on how much can be earned for free each month. Currently, completing all daily activities and event milestones yields approximately 1,800 Annulith per month, which equates to 10 pulls on a limited banner like "The Ichi-daime."

### The Pink Paw Heist (1M Fons)

The Pink Paw Heist is a specialized challenge located in the financial district of Hethereau. It is a wave-based survival and objective mode where you protect a vault from incoming anomalies. Successfully clearing the highest difficulty rewards exactly 1,000,000 Fons. This event resets every two weeks, making it the single most important task for any player looking to fund their vehicle collection. Failure to complete this before the reset results in a permanent loss of that fortnight's income.

### Cafe Management & Passive Income

The Cafe by Origin provides a steady stream of Fons that does not require City Stamina. By assigning characters with high "Hospitality" stats, you increase the hourly yield. Upgrading the cafe's interior with specific furniture sets also unlocks "Rush Hour" bonuses, which can double income for four hours daily.

### Daily Low-Effort Farms: Nacupeda's Pool

Nacupeda's Pool is a daily interaction point where you exchange minor anomaly drops for Mhm Coins. While the immediate rewards are small, consistent participation is required to save for the "Your Happiness is Priceless" Arc, a powerful S-Rank weapon that costs a significant amount of Mhm Coins.

### Optimal Cartridge Sets for AoE Clearing

The **Anima Cartridge** set is mandatory for your primary damage dealer, typically Nanally. The "Blossom" reaction is the core of the current farming meta. When an Anima-touched enemy is hit by a Cosmos ability, it triggers a chain reaction that clears entire screens of enemies.

The **Cosmos Cartridge** set should be equipped on Zero. This set ensures that his turrets and field effects remain active for the maximum possible duration, providing the "base" for Nanally to trigger reactions.

### Module Substat Priority for Farming

When tuning your Cartridges, prioritize the following substats in order:
1.  **Charge Efficiency:** Essential for keeping Ultimates available for every wave.
2.  **Cycle Intensity:** Increases the damage of elemental reactions (Blossom).
3.  **Break Intensity:** Helps finish off elite enemies (Anomaly Elites) faster by shattering their shields.

To farm Gold-tier Cartridges, you must reach Hunter Level 30 and enter the "Rabbit Hole" domain located in Cape Square. This domain consumes Character Pixels (daily stamina) but is the only reliable source for 5-star Modules.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/neverness-to-everness-currency-farming-guide/inline-6.webp)


**How often does the Pink Paw Heist reset?**
The Pink Paw Heist resets every two weeks on Monday at 04:00 server time. If you do not claim the 1,000,000 Fons reward before this time, it is lost.

**What is the difference between City Stamina and Character Pixels?**
City Stamina is a weekly resource (capped at [information unavailable]) used for hobbies like fishing and racing to earn Fons. Character Pixels are a daily regenerating resource used for combat domains to earn Cartridges and upgrade materials.

**Can I farm Annulith infinitely?**
No. Annulith is capped. You can earn 60 Annulith per day through Daily Commissions. Other sources include one-time exploration rewards (opening chests in Hethereau) and limited-time events.

**How do I get Mhm Coins faster?**
Mhm Coins are primarily obtained through Nacupeda's Pool and daily login rewards. It takes approximately 100 days of consistent logins to exchange for the top-tier "Your Happiness is Priceless" Arc.

**Is Nanally required for currency farming?**
While Nanally is the current meta-standard for the Blossom reaction, any high-AoE Anima character can fill the role. However, Nanally’s synergy with Zero is currently unmatched for speed-clearing.
