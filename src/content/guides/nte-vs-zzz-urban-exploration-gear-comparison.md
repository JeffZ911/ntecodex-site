Urban environments are characterized by verticality and dense assets. An ultrawide monitor provides a significant advantage by expanding the peripheral view, allowing you to see oncoming traffic or hidden Anomalies in NTE without constantly rotating the camera. This reduces repetitive wrist movements and improves spatial awareness.

### Dynamic Lumbar Support
Long sessions of city traversal often lead to poor posture. Chairs with active lumbar adjustment, like the <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Secretlab+TITAN+Evo&tag=jeffzen911-20" rel="sponsored nofollow noopener" target="_blank">Secretlab TITAN Evo</a>, are necessary to maintain the natural curve of the spine. This is especially vital when switching between the relaxed state of driving in NTE and the high-tension combat of ZZZ.

### Multi-Device Connectivity
Players frequently use secondary devices for interactive maps or character build guides. Headsets with simultaneous Bluetooth and 2.4GHz connectivity allow for seamless audio mixing, so you never miss an in-game audio cue while listening to a walkthrough on your phone.

## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/nte-vs-zzz-urban-exploration-gear-comparison/inline-4.webp)


While high-end gear improves the experience, it is not always a necessary investment. If you currently own a 1440p 144Hz IPS monitor, the jump to an Ultrawide panel is largely an aesthetic improvement rather than a functional one. You should keep your current panel if your budget is tight, as the gameplay mechanics themselves do not change.

You should also avoid high-end mechanical keyboards like the Q1 Pro if you primarily play with a controller. The ergonomic benefits of a gasket-mount keyboard are lost if your hands are not on the keys for the majority of your session.

Lastly, avoid the Secretlab TITAN if you are under 5'2". The minimum seat height and the depth of the seat base may prevent your feet from resting flat on the floor, which can cause circulation issues in the legs during long sessions. In this case, a chair specifically sized for smaller frames would be a better ergonomic choice.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the most important piece of gear for long RPG sessions?
The chair is the most critical component because it dictates your overall posture and spinal health. While a better mouse or monitor can improve your in-game performance, a high-quality ergonomic chair prevents long-term physical strain and fatigue during 6+ hour sessions.

### Does an ultrawide monitor give an advantage in Neverness to Everness?
Yes, the 21:9 aspect ratio provides a wider field of view, which is particularly useful for the driving mechanics in NTE. It allows you to see more of the city streets and upcoming turns without needing to adjust the camera manually, leading to a smoother traversal experience.

### Why choose a heavy keyboard like the Keychron Q1 Pro?
The weight of a CNC aluminum keyboard ensures that it does not slide on your desk during intense gameplay. Additionally, the internal gasket-mount construction provides a softer typing feel that reduces finger fatigue, which is essential when you are constantly using WASD to move through large urban maps.

### Are MMO mice like the Razer Naga V2 Pro useful for ZZZ?
Even though Zenless Zone Zero is an action RPG, it features numerous menus for [Hollow Zero](https://zenless.hoyoverse.com/en-us/news/124535) and character management. Mapping your inventory, map, and team menu to the mouse side buttons allows for much faster navigation than using default keyboard shortcuts.
