## What a long-session player actually needs

![What a long-session player actually needs](/img/best-gaming-setup-for-yunli-vs-clara-dps-comparison/inline-2.webp)


Players who spend hours comparing character performance and running simulations have specific requirements that differ from the average casual gamer.

*   **High DCI-P3 Color Gamut:** Modern urban RPGs like NTE rely heavily on visual appeal and "supernatural" effects. A monitor with less than 90% DCI-P3 coverage will make character designs and neon lighting look washed out, losing the artistic intent of the developers.
*   **Adjustable Lumbar Support:** Static lumbar pillows often shift or flatten during long play sessions. Integrated 4-way systems, like those found in the TITAN Evo, are necessary to maintain posture during long theorycrafting sessions where a player might be sitting for four or more hours.
*   **Hot-Swappable Components:** To avoid replacing entire units due to a single failing part, keyboards and mice with modular components provide better long-term value. Keyboards like the Keychron Q1 Pro allow users to replace individual switches, while the Naga V2 Pro offers replaceable side plates to suit different game genres.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the PC requirements for Neverness to Everness?
While official final specifications are being tuned for the 2026-04-29 release, the game utilizes Unreal Engine 5, meaning a modern GPU with ray-tracing capabilities is recommended. For a smooth 1440p experience, an RTX 3070 or equivalent is generally considered the baseline for maintaining high frame rates in the dense urban environment of Hesperia.

### Is a 1440p monitor necessary for character comparisons?
A 1440p monitor provides significantly more screen real estate than 1080p, which is crucial when viewing spreadsheets and game windows side-by-side. The higher pixel density also makes reading small text on character stat screens much easier, reducing eye strain during long analysis sessions.

### Can I use these peripherals on a PlayStation 5?
The SteelSeries Arctis Nova 7 and Razer Naga V2 Pro are compatible with the PS5, though the Naga's advanced macro functions are typically limited to PC software. The Keychron Q1 Pro also works in wired mode, making it a viable option for players who prefer the tactile feel of a mechanical keyboard on a console.

### Why is color accuracy important for Neverness to Everness?
Hotta Studio has emphasized the "supernatural urban" aesthetic of Hesperia, which relies on a specific palette of neon lights and dark shadows. A monitor with high color accuracy ensures that these environmental cues and character designs are displayed correctly, enhancing immersion and making it easier to distinguish between different types of supernatural anomalies.
