Bronya is a 5-star Wind character following the Path of Harmony. While several Harmony units have been released since the game's launch on April 26, 2023, Bronya’s Skill, "Combat Redeployment," remains one of the most powerful abilities in the game. ## Overview

Bronya functions as the ultimate force multiplier. Unlike other supports who provide passive stat buffs, Bronya actively dictates the flow of combat. Her kit is designed around high-frequency Skill usage, which necessitates careful team planning to manage Skill Points (SP).

### Trace Priority List
1.  **Skill (Combat Redeployment):** This is the heart of her kit. Increasing its level boosts the DMG bonus provided to the ally who is advanced.
2.  **Ultimate (The Belobog March):** Provides a team-wide ATK and Crit DMG buff. The Crit DMG buff scales off Bronya’s own Crit DMG.
3.  **Talent (Leading the Way):** After using her Basic ATK, Bronya’s next action is Advanced Forward. This is vital for "Fast Bronya" builds to maintain SP efficiency.
4.  **Basic ATK (Windrider Bullet):** Deals Wind DMG. While her trace "Command" ensures her Basic ATK has a 100% Crit Rate, this is her lowest priority for leveling.

### Why Bronya is a Must-Build Support
Bronya is often cited as the best standard 5-star character because she solves the "turn economy" problem. In turn-based games, the number of actions you take relative to the enemy determines victory. Bronya’s 100% Action Advance effectively grants your DPS an extra turn, bypassing the standard Speed requirements for that unit. Furthermore, her Ultimate provides a rare Crit DMG buff that scales with her own stats, rewarding players who invest heavily in her gear.

### Key Trace Mechanics and Ascension Passives
*   **Command (Ascension 2):** The Crit Rate for Basic ATK increases to 100%. This allows Bronya to contribute chip damage and trigger certain Light Cone effects without needing Crit Rate sub-stats.
*   **Military Might (Ascension 4):** When Bronya is on the field, all allies deal 10% increased DMG. A simple but effective permanent aura.
*   **Formation (Ascension 6):** Increases the DEF of all allies by 20% for 2 turns at the start of the battle. This helps squishier DPS units survive initial enemy onslaughts.

### Top 5-Star Light Cones for Bronya
**But the Battle Isn't Over** is Bronya's signature weapon and remains her definitive best-in-slot. The 10% Energy Regeneration is crucial for reaching specific Ultimate breakpoints. More importantly, the Skill Point recovery (which triggers every two uses of her Ultimate) helps alleviate the SP-heavy nature of her Skill-spamming playstyle.

**A Grounded Ascent** (Sunday's signature) is a powerful alternative. While it doesn't provide the SP recovery of her signature, the raw DMG amplification it provides to the entire team after Bronya uses her Ultimate can result in higher total team DPS, especially in dual-carry setups.

### Best 4-Star and F2P Alternatives
**Past and Future** is the premier 4-star choice. Since Bronya’s Skill always makes an ally act immediately after her, the DMG buff from this Light Cone is guaranteed to apply to your DPS. It can be purchased from the Forgotten Hall store (Light Cone Manifest), making it very accessible.

For players struggling with Energy, **Meshing Cogs** (S5) is a legitimate competitive option despite being a 3-star weapon. When combined with an Energy Regeneration Link Rope, it allows Bronya to achieve a consistent 3-turn Ultimate rotation, which can be more valuable than the raw stats of a 4-star weapon.

## Rotation

The effectiveness of Bronya is entirely dependent on her Speed relative to the Main DPS. There are two primary schools of thought regarding her rotation.

### Understanding Speed Tuning (134 vs 160)
**1. The "Slow Bronya" (-1 SPD Tuning):**
In this setup, your DPS is slightly faster than Bronya. For example, your DPS has 135 SPD and Bronya has 134 SPD.
*   **Sequence:** DPS acts -> Bronya Skills the DPS -> DPS acts again.
*   **Result:** The DPS takes two actions in a single cycle. This is the most SP-intensive build but offers the highest burst damage.

**2. The "Fast Bronya" (Hyperspeed):**
Bronya is built with as much Speed as possible (usually 160 or higher), while the DPS stays at base Speed (around 100-105).
*   **Sequence:** Bronya Basics (advancing herself via Talent) -> DPS acts -> Bronya Skills the DPS -> DPS acts again.
*   **Result:** This is more SP-efficient. Bronya uses her Basic ATK to generate SP and then uses her Skill to bring the slow DPS forward.

### Optimizing Energy and Skill Point Economy
To achieve a **3-turn Ultimate rotation**, Bronya generally needs an Energy Regeneration Rate (ERR) Link Rope and her Signature Light Cone or Meshing Cogs.

**Energy Math for 3-Turn Rotation:**
*   Max Energy: 120
*   Trace (Skill): 30 Energy
*   Trace (Basic): 20 Energy
*   Ultimate: 5 Energy

With a 19.4% ERR Rope and a 10% ERR Light Cone (Total 129.4%):
Three Skills (90) + Ult (5) = 95 base Energy.
95 * 1.294 = **122.9 Energy** (Success).

If you use a Basic ATK in the rotation (Skill-Basic-Skill):
30 + 20 + 30 + 5 = 85 base Energy.
85 * 1.294 = **109.9 Energy** (Fails to reach 120).
In this scenario, you would need to be hit by an enemy or use a Light Cone like Meshing Cogs to bridge the gap.

## FAQ

### Should I get Bronya's E1 or her Signature Light Cone first?
If you have the 300-pull standard selector or enough Undying Starlight, **Eidolon 1 (E1)** is generally the priority. E1 gives a 50% chance to recover 1 SP when using her Skill (with a 1-turn cooldown). This significantly lowers the "SP floor" required to play Bronya effectively. Her signature Light Cone is a close second, but E1's impact on rotation consistency is unmatched.

### Is Bronya powercrept by Sparkle?
No. While Sparkle is better for SP-hungry Crit-based carries (like Dan Heng Imbibitor Lunae or Qingque), Bronya’s **100% Action Advance** is mechanically superior for units like Jingliu and Blade. Bronya also provides a cleanse on her Skill, which Sparkle lacks. They often work best when used together rather than replacing one another.

### How much Crit DMG does Bronya need?
While her Ultimate scales with her Crit DMG, you should not sacrifice Speed or Energy Regeneration for it. Aim for **180% to 200% Crit DMG** as a baseline. Beyond that, the diminishing returns on the buff she provides to allies make it less valuable than ensuring she hits her Speed breakpoints.

### Does Bronya need Wind DMG Bonus?
No. Bronya's personal damage is negligible. Her Sphere should always be **HP% or DEF%** to ensure she survives powerful AoE attacks in high-difficulty content.

### What is the "Slow Bronya" breakpoint?
The most common breakpoint is **134 SPD**. This allows her (and the DPS she is supporting) to act twice in the first cycle of Memory of Chaos, which is essential for "0-cycle" or "1-cycle" clears.
