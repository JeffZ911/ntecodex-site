![Overview](/img/nte-daily-grind-routine-guide-nanally-build/inline-1.webp)


Nanally has quickly established herself as the premier S-Rank Anima/Plasma DPS in Neverness to Everness (NTE). Since the game's launch on April 29, 2026, she has become the gold standard for clearing high-level Anomaly Zones and daily content due to her unique "Underboss" summon and high-mobility "Ichi-daime's Authority" state. Building Nanally correctly is not just about raw power; it is about optimizing her for the "Daily Grind," the core loop of NTE that ensures your account stays ahead of the curve.

The "Daily Grind" in NTE revolves around the efficient management of three critical resources: Character Pixels (stamina), City Energy, and Fons. While Character Pixels are used to farm upgrade materials in combat instances, City Energy fuels your progression in the City Tycoon systems, such as street racing and property management. Nanally’s ability to clear mobs in seconds makes her the most time-efficient choice for burning through these daily requirements.

A complete daily routine in Hethereau involves more than just combat. It includes visiting Nacupeda's Pool for long-term rewards, getting a fortune at the Witch's House for combat buffs, and managing the Origen Cafe to maintain a steady flow of Fons. Nanally's kit, which focuses on follow-up attacks and high-frequency skill usage via her Tiger Talisman mechanic, allows her to excel in both the open-world skirmishes and the structured Anomaly Zone challenges you will face every day.

### Why Nanally for the Daily Grind?

Nanally is uniquely suited for daily tasks because of her "Ichi-daime's Authority" state. Triggered by her Redirect Skill, this state allows her to walk on walls and ceilings, providing unparalleled mobility for navigating the verticality of Hethereau’s districts. In combat, she summons her "Underboss," an autonomous entity that performs follow-up attacks whenever Nanally deals damage. This off-field damage potential means that even while you are dodging or repositioning, your DPS remains high, significantly reducing the time spent on daily combat commissions.

## Best Weapons

Arcs (weapons) in Neverness to Everness are categorized by their elemental and physical properties: Plasma, Solid, Gas, Liquid, and Synthesis. As a Plasma-type character, Nanally requires Arcs that complement her high-frequency hits and crit-scaling passives.

### Signature Weapon: Ready-Ready

Ready-Ready is Nanally's signature S-Rank Plasma Arc and her undisputed Best-in-Slot (BiS) option. This weapon provides a massive 24% CRIT Rate substat at maximum level, significantly easing the burden on your Disk substat rolls. Its unique passive, "Commander Tiger Talisman," is specifically designed for Nanally’s rotation.

When Nanally uses her Redirect Skill, she gains a "Left Tiger Talisman." Using her Ultimate grants a "Right Tiger Talisman." If both are obtained within 15 seconds, the Commander Tiger Talisman effect activates, increasing damage dealt to Boss units by 10% for 10 seconds. Furthermore, the Arc provides a permanent 15% ATK boost and increases Basic Attack and Critical Riposte DMG by 15% for 15 seconds after using a skill or ultimate (stacking up to 2 times).

### Best S-Rank Alternatives

For players who did not pull for her signature weapon, the "Fluff of Fortitude" is the premier alternative. Available through the Battle Pass (Circle Bounty), this Arc offers a high ATK% substat. Its passive, "Jawbreaker's Candy," provides a flat 22% DMG increase to all attacks, which further scales to 28% when the enemy's HP falls below 50%. This makes it exceptionally strong for finishing off elite anomalies and bosses.

### F2P and Budget Arc Options

Its passive, "Ora Puncher," increases Basic Attack DMG by 2.5% every time a Basic Attack lands, stacking up to 10 times. Since Nanally’s rotation involves a 5-hit combo followed by an enhanced derivation, she can reach maximum stacks in less than three seconds.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/nte-daily-grind-routine-guide-nanally-build/inline-3.webp)


In NTE, the "Disk" system consists of Cartridges and Modules. Cartridges provide set bonuses, while Modules are slotted into your Console to provide raw stats and specific elemental boosts.

### Top Cartridge Sets for Anima DPS

The "Fireflies and the Forest" 4-piece Cartridge set is the definitive choice for Nanally. 
*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Increases Anima DMG by 10%.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** Whenever a nearby enemy takes Anima DMG from the team, the wearer gains 8% CRIT DMG for 10 seconds. This effect stacks up to 7 times, for a total of 56% CRIT DMG.

Because Nanally’s Underboss and her Basic Attacks deal constant Anima damage, she maintains maximum stacks of this buff with 100% uptime.

### Optimizing Modules for Crit Scaling

Modules are divided into Types I, II, and III. Nanally’s Console has a specific synergy with Type II Modules; for every Type II Module equipped, she receives a 6% CRIT Rate bonus. To maximize her damage, you should aim for a "Min-Max" Console layout that prioritizes these modules.

**Module Stat Priority:**
1.  **Main Stat:** Anima DMG% (Cartridge) / ATK% or CRIT DMG (Modules).
2.  **Substats:** CRIT Rate (until 70-80%) > CRIT DMG > ATK% > DMG%.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/nte-daily-grind-routine-guide-nanally-build/inline-5.webp)


Nanally’s combat rhythm is built around her 15-second "Tiger Talisman" window. To maximize her output, you must weave her skills and ultimate together to trigger both the weapon-specific and character-specific buffs.

### The Tiger Talisman Combo

1.  **Setup:** Start with your Support characters (like Zero or Sakiri) to fill the Esper Meter. Once the portrait glows, swap to Nanally to trigger her Esper Ability.
2.  **Entry:** Use Nanally’s Redirect Skill (**Colucci Howling Technique**). This deals 5 instances of Anima DMG, grants the "Left Tiger Talisman," and enters "Ichi-daime's Authority" (boosting CRIT DMG by 30%).
3.  **Summon:** Immediately cast her Ultimate (**Colucci Secret Skill: Underboss**). This summons the Underboss for coordinated attacks and grants the "Right Tiger Talisman."
4.  **Activation:** By using the Skill and Ultimate within 15 seconds, you trigger the **Commander Tiger Talisman** (if using her signature Arc), granting 10% extra DMG to bosses.
5.  **Execution:** Spam her Basic Attack combo. Hold the attack button after the third or fifth hit to trigger her enhanced "Street Fighter" derivations.

### Managing Esper Meter and Swaps

Nanally is an on-field DPS who requires roughly 12-15 seconds of field time to exhaust her "Ichi-daime's Authority" duration. Avoid swapping her out early unless you need to refresh a critical shield or heal, as leaving the field ends her Authority state prematurely. Use parries to instantly fill her Esper Meter, allowing you to restart the rotation immediately after the cooldowns reset.

### General Daily Grind Questions

**How fast do Character Pixels refill?**
Character Pixels refill at a rate of 1 every 6 minutes. You receive 240 Pixels per day. It is recommended to spend them on the highest-tier Anomaly Zone stage you have unlocked to maximize the rarity of the drops.

**Where is Nacupeda's Pool located?**
The pool is located in the western park area of the Bridge Crossing district. Interact with it daily and choose "Make a Sincere Wish" to receive Mhm! Coins. These coins are the primary F2P path to obtaining S-Rank Arcs from the exchange shop.

**Is there a limit to Fons farming?**
Yes. While you can earn Fons from many sources, using Chiz's ability to locate "Fons Anomalies" in the city is capped daily. Check the "Abilities" tab in Chiz’s profile to see your current progress toward the daily cap.

### Nanally Build Specifics

**Can I use Nanally without her signature weapon?**
Yes. While "Ready-Ready" is her best weapon, she performs exceptionally well with "Fluff of Fortitude" or even the A-Rank "Oraora!". Her base kit provides enough CRIT DMG and mobility to clear all current 1.0 content.

**What is the "Blossom" reaction exactly?**
Blossom is a Duo-Esper reaction triggered by combining Anima and Cosmos. It creates a "Vita Bud" that fires "Vita Pistils" (projectiles) at nearby enemies. Nanally’s presence on the team makes these turrets fire 10 additional smaller attacks and increases their overall frequency.

**How do I get more Type II Modules for the CRIT bonus?**
Type II Modules are primarily farmed in the "Anomaly Zone: Bubble Can Factory." Ensure you are targeting the stages that drop "Plasma" or "Anima" specific geometries to find the best fits for Nanally's Console.
