# Neverness to Everness Daily Task Guide: Best Nanally Build for Efficient Farming
> 📝 **Editorial Note:** This guide is AI-assisted and game data evolves rapidly. Please cross-reference with in-game information. _Updated: 2026-05-23._


Efficient farming in Neverness to Everness (NTE) is less about how much time you spend and more about the effectiveness of your primary DPS. [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte), the S-Rank Anima DPS and leader of the Ichi-daime gang, currently stands as the most efficient character for clearing daily content. Her ability to automate damage through her "Underboss" summons and her high-mobility wall-running makes her the gold standard for clearing Anomalies and Participation tasks.

## Overview

![Overview](/img/neverness-to-everness-daily-task-guide-build/inline-1.webp)


The daily progression loop in Neverness to Everness revolves around the Participation Guide, a system that rewards consistent activity with premium currency and growth materials. To claim the maximum daily rewards, including 20 Annulith and various EXP items, you must accumulate 100 Participation Points before the server reset.

Nanally is the premier choice for this routine because of her "Ichi-daime's Authority" state. In this mode, her Anima-infused attacks deal massive area-of-effect (AoE) damage, allowing you to wipe out open-world Anomaly mobs in seconds. Her follow-up attack mechanics, triggered by her Ultimate "The Boss is Here," ensure that even when you are moving between objectives, your summons are finishing off stragglers.

### Why Nanally is Best for Daily Clears

Nanally’s kit is designed for high-frequency hits and autonomous damage. Her passive, Fair Duel, triggers a follow-up attack whenever a teammate deals damage while she is in her Authority state. This means you can swap to a support character to group enemies, and Nanally will still be dealing damage from the background. For daily tasks that require defeating a certain number of enemies or clearing specific combat zones, Nanally reduces the "active" play time significantly compared to single-target burst characters like Chiz.

### The 5-Minute Daily Routine Checklist

To hit your 100 Participation Points quickly, follow this optimized path:

1.  **Login and Fortune:** Head to the Witch's House to receive your daily fortune. This provides a minor buff and counts toward your daily activity.
2.  **Cafe Restock:** Visit the City Tycoon Cafe. Collect your accumulated Fons and restock the ingredients. Managing your business is a high-value task that provides passive income while you are offline.
3.  **Spend Character Pixels:** Use at least 180 of your 240 Character Pixels in the Anomaly Zones. This is the most important step for character progression, as it provides the materials needed to upgrade Arcs and Espers.
4.  **Combat Tasks:** Defeat 5 enemies in the open world. With Nanally, this usually takes one "Colucci Howling Technique" skill cast.
5.  **Claim Rewards:** Open the Exploration Guide and claim all five reward chests.

## Best Weapons

![Best Weapons](/img/neverness-to-everness-daily-task-guide-build/inline-2.webp)


In Neverness to Everness, weapons are known as Arcs. Arcs are categorized by types such as Plasma, Liquid, and Gas. Nanally requires Plasma-type Arcs to synergize with her Anima element and high-frequency attack style. Choosing the right Arc determines how quickly her follow-up attacks scale and how much energy she regenerates for her Ultimate.

### Signature Weapon: Ready-Ready

The S-Rank Arc **Ready-Ready** is Nanally's signature weapon and her undisputed best-in-slot option. It features a CRIT Rate substat that reaches 24% at maximum level, which is vital for triggering her Cartridge set bonuses. The unique effect, "Commander Tiger Talisman," provides a 15% ATK boost by default. 

When Nanally uses her Redirect Skill or Ultimate, she gains a 15% damage increase for her Basic Attacks and Critical Ripostes for 15 seconds. This effect stacks twice. If she activates both her skill (granting the Left Tiger Talisman) and her Ultimate (granting the Right Tiger Talisman) within 15 seconds, she unlocks the full Commander state, which increases damage against Boss units by an additional 10%. This makes Ready-Ready not only a farming tool but an essential piece for end-game bossing.

### F2P and Budget Arc Alternatives

For players who did not pull on "The Ichi-daime" weapon banner, there are several viable alternatives that can be obtained through gameplay.

*   **Song of the Whale (S-Rank):** This is the best farmable alternative for Nanally. It can be obtained through overworld exploration and defeating specific world bosses. It provides a 20% ATK bonus and increases damage dealt to "Broken" enemies (enemies whose shield bars have been depleted) by 20%. Crucially for daily farming, it restores 30% of Nanally's HP upon defeating a Broken enemy, providing excellent sustain without needing a dedicated healer.
*   **Oraora! (A-Rank):** A high-value budget option often found in the standard gacha pool. It focuses heavily on boosting Basic Attack damage, which aligns with Nanally's primary damage source during her Authority state.
*   **Fluff of Fortitude (S-Rank):** Available via the premium Battle Pass (Circle Bounty). It acts as a generic stat stick, providing a flat 22% damage increase that rises to 28% when enemies are below 50% HP. It is a reliable choice for players who want a consistent performance boost across all content.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/neverness-to-everness-daily-task-guide-build/inline-3.webp)


The gear system in NTE utilizes "Cartridges" (the primary gear piece) and "Modules" (smaller components that slot into the character's Console). To optimize Nanally for daily tasks, you must focus on sets that reward her high hit count and Anima element.

### Recommended Cartridge Sets

The best-in-slot Cartridge set for Nanally is **Fireflies and the Forest**. This set is specifically designed for Anima-element Espers who deal multi-hit damage.

*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Increases Anima DMG by 10%.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** Whenever a nearby enemy takes Anima DMG from any team member, the wearer gains an 8% CRIT DMG bonus. This effect stacks up to 7 times for a total of 56% CRIT DMG.

Because Nanally’s Ultimate summons the Underboss to coordinate attacks with the team, she can reach maximum stacks almost instantly. This massive surge in CRIT DMG allows her to one-shot most daily mob waves once her Ultimate is active.

### Module Specialization and Stat Priority

Modules are divided into Types (II, III, and IV). For Nanally, prioritizing **Type III Modules** is essential because they unlock her specialization bonus. Equipping Type III Modules in her Console grants a flat 8% CRIT Rate per module. With a full set of high-tier Modules and her signature weapon, Nanally can easily reach a 100% CRIT Rate, ensuring every hit of her follow-up attacks deals maximum damage.

**Optimal Module Substat Priority:**
1.  **CRIT DMG:** Since Nanally gets plenty of CRIT Rate from her weapon and specialization, stacking CRIT DMG is the best way to scale her output.
2.  **ATK%:** Increases the base damage of her Anima follow-ups.
3.  **Anima DMG:** Provides a multiplicative bonus to all her skills.
4.  **Energy Regeneration:** Helpful for maintaining 100% uptime on her Ultimate during long farming sessions.

## Team Comp

![Team Comp](/img/neverness-to-everness-daily-task-guide-build/inline-4.webp)


Nanally performs best when paired with characters who can trigger the "Blossom" reaction. Blossom occurs when Anima and Cosmos elements interact, creating a "Bud" that deals automated damage based on the team's hit frequency.

### The Blossom Meta Team

The current meta for efficient daily farming consists of Nanally, [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide), Esper Zero, and a flexible support slot.

*   **Nanally (Main DPS):** Provides the Anima application and the bulk of the team's damage through her Authority state.
*   **Sakiri (Support/Crowd Control):** Sakiri is indispensable for daily tasks. Her skill, "Devour Whole," acts as a powerful vacuum, pulling all nearby enemies into a tight cluster. This allows Nanally to hit the entire wave simultaneously.
*   **Esper Zero (Cosmos Enabler):** As the protagonist, Esper Zero provides the Cosmos element necessary for the Blossom reaction. Their Redirect Skill instantly fills the Esper Meter, allowing for immediate reaction triggers.
*   **Jiuyuan (Support/Healer) or Haniel (Buffer):** Jiuyuan provides additional Anima synergy and healing, while Haniel offers massive team-wide ATK buffs. For daily tasks where survival isn't an issue, Haniel is preferred for the extra speed.

### F2P-Friendly Daily Task Team

If you are a new player or haven't pulled S-Rank supports, use this configuration:
*   **Nanally** (Main DPS)
*   **Esper Zero** (Cosmos/Reaction)
*   **Mint** (Anima/Sub-DPS)
*   **Haniel** (ATK Buffs)

This team focuses on the basic Blossom interaction. Mint provides extra Anima hits to help Nanally maintain her Fireflies and the Forest stacks, while Haniel keeps the team's damage high enough to clear Anomalies without needing optimized gear.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/neverness-to-everness-daily-task-guide-build/inline-5.webp)


To clear daily content as fast as possible, you must manage the "Ichi-daime's Authority" uptime and ensure the Blossom reaction is always active.

### Optimal Combat Flow for Daily Mob Clears

1.  **Start with Sakiri:** Use Sakiri’s skill (Hold version) to group all enemies in the area. This ensures no time is wasted chasing down individual mobs.
2.  **Swap to Esper Zero:** Use their Redirect Skill to apply the Cosmos element to the grouped enemies. This primes the field for the Blossom reaction.
3.  **Enter Nanally’s Authority:** Switch to Nanally and immediately use her Skill, "Colucci Howling Technique." This enters her Ichi-daime's Authority state and applies Anima damage, triggering the first Blossom explosion.
4.  **Activate Ultimate:** If the enemies are elite or have high HP, use her Ultimate "The Boss is Here." This summons the Underboss, who will automatically attack any enemy Nanally targets.
5.  **Enhanced Basics:** Spam Nanally’s Enhanced Basic Attacks. Each hit will trigger the Fireflies and the Forest CRIT DMG stacks. Because the enemies are grouped and affected by Blossom, they will melt within seconds.

### Managing Ichi-daime's Authority Uptime

The Authority state lasts for 15 seconds. In the open world, you can extend this duration by unlocking Nanally’s A6 Awakening ("WE are Family"), which increases the duration to 20 seconds when out of combat. This is particularly useful for moving between different mob camps in an Anomaly Zone without having to restart your rotation. Always keep an eye on the purple gauge above her health bar; if it runs low, swap back to Zero to refill the Esper Meter before re-entering the state.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/neverness-to-everness-daily-task-guide-build/inline-6.webp)


### Daily Reset and Participation Questions

**What time does Neverness to Everness reset?**
The game resets at 5:00 AM server time. This includes daily tasks, login rewards, and the restocking of the City Tycoon Cafe.

**How do I get 100 Participation Points quickly?**
The fastest method is to log in, restock your cafe ingredients, spend 180 Character Pixels in any Anomaly Zone, and defeat 5 enemies in the open world. This routine takes approximately five minutes and guarantees the full 20 Annulith reward.

**Can I skip the daily login animation?**
Currently, the Witch's House fortune and the initial login transition are unskippable. However, you can speed up the process by teleporting directly to the Cafe from the map immediately after the fortune is read.

### Resource Management and Farming Tips

**What are Character Pixels?**
Character Pixels are the combat stamina system in NTE. They are required to claim rewards from Anomaly Zones, which provide character EXP, Arc ascension materials, and Cartridges. You regenerate 1 Pixel every 6 minutes, capping at 240.

**Are Arcs farmable?**
While S-Rank signature Arcs like Ready-Ready are gacha-exclusive, others like Song of the Whale are farmable. You can obtain them by completing high-level exploration challenges and defeating overworld bosses.

**What is the best way to spend Fons?**
Early on, prioritize spending Fons on upgrading your Arcs and leveling up Nanally. Once your main team is stable, invest your Fons into purchasing new properties and furniture for your apartment, as this increases your total Character Pixel cap over time.
