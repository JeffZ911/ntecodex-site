To begin ascending your roster, you must first reach Hunter Rank 10. This is achieved by accumulating Hunter EXP through the completion of main story chapters and side commissions throughout Hethereau. Once you hit this milestone, the Ascension tab unlocks within the character menu. ### Nanally Character Profile & Role

Nanally is an S-Class Anima/Plasma Esper who functions as a hyper-carry Main DPS. Her kit revolves around the "Ichi-daime's Authority" state, which transforms her combat style and allows her to summon a spectral Underboss. This Underboss performs coordinated follow-up attacks whenever Nanally lands a critical hit, making her one of the fastest damage dealers in the game. Because her damage scales heavily with her presence on the field, she requires a team built entirely around supporting her burst windows.

### Stat Priority: CRIT Rate vs. Anima DMG

When building Nanally, the priority is balancing her CRIT Rate with Anima DMG bonuses. Because the Underboss follow-up attacks only trigger on critical hits, a low CRIT Rate significantly hampers her total DPS output. 

1. **CRIT Rate:** Aim for a minimum of 75% to ensure the Underboss is active during almost every attack string.
2. **Anima DMG:** This multiplicative stat increases the base damage of her Plasma-type abilities and the Underboss's strikes.
3. **ATK%:** While important, ATK% has diminishing returns compared to the raw multiplier provided by Anima DMG.
4. **CRIT DMG:** Once your CRIT Rate is stable, stack CRIT DMG to capitalize on the "Ichi-daime's Authority" buffs.

## Best Weapons

![Best Weapons](/img/nte-character-ascension-guide-nanally/inline-2.webp)


In Neverness to Everness, weapons are categorized as "Arcs." Each Esper is compatible with a specific Arc type based on their elemental resonance—Plasma, Liquid, Gas, Solid, or Synthesis. As a Plasma Esper, Nanally must be equipped with a Plasma Arc to activate her passive skills and stat bonuses.

### Top Tier: Ready-Ready (Signature)

Ready-Ready is Nanally’s signature S-Rank Plasma Arc and is widely considered her best-in-slot weapon. It features a high base ATK and a CRIT Rate substat that eases the burden on your Disk (artifact) farming. Its unique passive increases Nanally’s total DMG by 22% at M1, rising to 28% at higher refinement levels. Furthermore, it grants a stacking buff that increases the Underboss’s attack speed every time Nanally uses a Charged Attack.

### Alternative S-Rank: Fluff of Fortitude

If you do not have Ready-Ready, Fluff of Fortitude is the strongest alternative. This Arc focuses on execution damage. It provides a massive ATK boost when attacking enemies with less than 50% HP. While it lacks the CRIT Rate substat of the signature weapon, its raw damage ceiling in boss fights is comparable, especially during the final phase of Anomaly Pilgrimages.

### Best F2P Arc: Hethereau's Keeper

For players looking for a budget-friendly option, Hethereau's Keeper is the premier F2P choice. It can be obtained through world exploration and by completing specific Hunter Union challenges. It offers a consistent ATK buff and increases the damage of follow-up attacks, which synergizes directly with Nanally's Underboss mechanic.

| Arc Name | Rarity | Substat | Key Passive Effect |

| **Ready-Ready** | S-Rank | CRIT Rate | +22-28% DMG; Increases Underboss ATK SPD |

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

The gear system in NTE is managed through the "Console," which consists of Cartridges (set pieces) and Modules (individual stat boosters). To maximize Nanally’s potential, you must focus on sets that enhance her elemental damage and her critical hit consistency.

### Best Set: Fireflies and the Forest

The "Fireflies and the Forest" 4-piece set is the mandatory endgame choice for Nanally. 
*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Increases Anima DMG by 10%.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** When the character deals Anima DMG, they gain a stack of "Luminescent Drift." Each stack increases CRIT DMG by 7%, stacking up to 8 times (56% total). 

Because Nanally deals multi-hit Anima damage through her Underboss, she can reach maximum stacks within the first three seconds of her burst rotation.

### Module Optimization & Type-2 Synergy

The Console layout allows for the insertion of Modules. Nanally benefits most from Type-2 Modules. When your Console contains at least three Type-2 Modules, Nanally triggers a unique passive that grants a flat 10% CRIT Rate increase. This is vital for reaching the 75% threshold required for consistent Underboss triggers.

**Optimal Console Layout:**
*   **Cartridge 1 (Top):** Main Stat ATK (Fixed).
*   **Cartridge 2 (Right):** Main Stat Anima DMG Bonus.
*   **Cartridge 3 (Left):** Main Stat CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG (depending on your weapon).

## Team Comp

![Team Comp](/img/nte-character-ascension-guide-nanally/inline-4.webp)


Nanally thrives in teams that can trigger the "Blossom" reaction. Blossom occurs when Anima (Nanally’s element) reacts with Cosmos. This reaction creates "Genesis Buds" on the field, which explode for massive Area of Effect (AoE) damage when struck by an Esper's Ultimate.

### The Blossom Nuke Meta

The current gold standard for Nanally is the "Blossom Nuke" team. This composition focuses on maximizing Nanally's burst window while ensuring she has 100% uptime on her Ultimate.

1. **Nanally (Main DPS):** The primary damage dealer.
2. **Esper Zero (Cosmos Sub-DPS):** Essential for providing the Cosmos element needed for Blossom reactions. Esper Zero's off-field abilities generate Genesis Buds constantly.
3. **[Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) (Incantation Support):** Provides a massive ATK buff based on 30% of her own base ATK after using her Ultimate. She also provides energy regeneration for the team.
4. **Jiuyuan (Anima/CC):** Provides crowd control to group enemies together, making it easier for Nanally’s Underboss and Blossom explosions to hit multiple targets.

### Support Synergies: Sakiri vs. Haniel

While Sakiri is the top-tier support for her raw ATK buffs, Haniel is a viable alternative for players who need more survivability. Haniel provides shields and minor healing, though she offers significantly less offensive utility than Sakiri. In the current meta, Sakiri is preferred because Nanally’s high damage output usually allows players to end fights before healing becomes necessary.

### F2P-Friendly Team Options

If you are a free-to-play player and do not have the full S-Class roster, this team provides excellent synergy:
*   **Nanally** (Main DPS)
*   **Esper Zero** (Cosmos Sub-DPS - obtained via story)
*   **Haniel** (Support/Healer)
*   **Adler** (Utility/Breaker)

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/nte-character-ascension-guide-nanally/inline-5.webp)


To achieve maximum DPS, you must follow a specific skill priority that ensures all buffs are active before Nanally enters her "Ichi-daime's Authority" state.

### The Opening Setup

1.  **Start with Sakiri:** Cast her Ultimate to apply the team-wide ATK buff.
2.  **Switch to Jiuyuan:** Use her Skill to pull enemies into a tight cluster.
3.  **Switch to Esper Zero:** Use her Skill and Ultimate to apply the Cosmos element and generate Genesis Buds.

### Burst Window: Underboss Summoning

Once the setup is complete, swap to Nanally to begin the damage phase:
1.  **Cast Skill (Colucci Howling Technique):** This allows Nanally to enter the "Ichi-daime's Authority" state, granting her an immediate CRIT DMG buff.
2.  **Cast Ultimate (Colucci Ultimate Technique):** This summons the Underboss. The Underboss will remain active for 6 seconds, performing follow-up attacks on every critical hit.

### Sustained DPS: The Charged Attack Loop

During the 6 seconds the Underboss is active, use the following attack string:
*   **3 Standard Basic Attacks**
*   **3 Charged Attacks (Hold)**

Repeating this loop maximizes the number of hits Nanally lands, which in turn maximizes the number of Underboss follow-up triggers. The Charged Attacks are particularly important if you are using the "Ready-Ready" Arc, as they increase the Underboss's attack speed.

### General Ascension Questions

**Where can I farm Hunter Guides?**
Hunter Guides (Rising, Senior, and Elite) are the primary EXP materials for Espers. They are farmed at Houdinii's Magic Stage. Entering this stage costs Character Pixels (Stamina). Higher difficulty stages yield Senior and Elite guides more frequently.

**How do I get boss ascension materials?**
Each character requires specific drops from Anomaly Pilgrimages. These are world bosses located throughout the map. You can find the exact location of the boss you need by checking the "Exploration Guide" in your main menu and filtering by "Ascension Materials."

**Can I buy ascension materials?**
Yes. If you prefer not to farm, you can visit the Mall's Mode Shop. You can purchase a limited number of ascension materials weekly using Peril Hunt Medals, which are earned by completing the weekly endgame boss resets.

While Mint is a very capable Anima DPS for early to mid-game, Nanally has significantly higher damage scaling and better synergy with the Blossom reaction. If you have both, Nanally should be your priority for investment.
