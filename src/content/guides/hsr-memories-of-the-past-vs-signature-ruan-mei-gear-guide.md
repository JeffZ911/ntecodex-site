# HSR Memories of the Past vs Signature Ruan Mei: Best Gear for Long Gacha Grinding Sessions

Quick Answer: For the best visual fidelity during long sessions, the LG 27GP850-B is the top pick. For physical endurance, the Secretlab TITAN Evo 2022 offers the best lumbar support for 4+ hour grinds.

The launch of Neverness to Everness (NTE) on April 29, 2026, has pushed the demands of gacha gaming hardware to a new peak. Much like the classic debate of using the 4-star Memories of the Past versus the 5-star signature Past Self in Mirror for [Ruan Mei](https://ntecodex.com/guides/hsr-dahlia-vs-ruan-mei-comparison-20260713) in Honkai: Star Rail, choosing gaming gear is a balance between cost-efficiency and absolute performance. While the former provides essential Energy Regeneration for a functional build, the signature option offers the damage buffs and skill point recovery needed for ceiling-level play. This guide applies that same rigorous optimization logic to your physical setup, ensuring you can navigate the Unreal Engine 5 streets of Hesperia without fatigue.

**Verdict:** The TITAN Evo 2022 is built for postural longevity. By using [ADC12 aluminum](https://secretlab.co/products/titan-evo-2022-series) in its construction, it offers a level of durability and stability that prevents the "wobble" common in cheaper alternatives.

## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/hsr-memories-of-the-past-vs-signature-ruan-mei-gear-guide/inline-4.webp)


Not every new piece of hardware is a necessary pull for your setup. You should consider skipping these upgrades if:

*   **You already have a 1440p IPS display:** If your current monitor runs at 144Hz or higher, the jump to 165Hz or 180Hz is visually negligible. In a game like NTE, which features a mix of exploration and combat, you won't notice the extra frames as much as you would in a competitive shooter.
*   **Your current chair has adjustable lumbar:** If your chair already allows you to move the lumbar support up and down, you might be better off buying a $30 memory foam seat cushion rather than a whole new $500 chair.
*   **You only play in a fixed position:** Wireless headsets are convenient, but if you never leave your desk, a wired headset like the Sennheiser HD 560S will offer significantly better audio quality for the same price.

## What we didn't include and why

![What we didn't include and why](/img/hsr-memories-of-the-past-vs-signature-ruan-mei-gear-guide/inline-5.webp)


We excluded several popular items that are often marketed to gamers but fail to meet the standards for long-session health:

*   **DXRacer and similar "Bucket" seats:** These designs are based on car racing seats intended to hold a driver in place during high-G turns. In a home setting, the flared "wings" at the shoulders force your body into a rounded-shoulder posture, which leads to upper back pain over time.
*   **Generic "Gaming" Headsets under $50:** These typically use low-quality 40mm drivers that overemphasize bass. This "bloated" sound masks the mid-range details of orchestral soundtracks, making the game's audio environment sound muddy.
*   **Blue Yeti Microphone:** While a staple for many, its condenser capsule is extremely sensitive. Without a treated room, it will pick up every click of your Keychron K2 V2, which can be annoying for your teammates during co-op sessions.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does Neverness to Everness support 4K resolution?
Yes, Neverness to Everness is built on Unreal Engine 5 and supports 4K resolution. However, for most players, 1440p remains the "sweet spot" for maintaining high frame rates during fast-paced car customization and driving segments.

### Which character in NTE is best for beginners?
Characters like Nanally and Mint are often recommended for early-game exploration due to their versatile Esper abilities. Their skills allow for efficient management of the Anomalies found throughout Hesperia.

### How much storage does NTE require on PC?
Official storage requirements haven't been finalized, but given the massive urban open world and high-fidelity assets, players should expect to reserve at least 100GB of space. Using an SSD is highly recommended to handle the seamless transitions between the city and indoor environments.
