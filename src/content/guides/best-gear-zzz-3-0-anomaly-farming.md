## What an anomaly farmer actually needs

![What an anomaly farmer actually needs](/img/best-gear-zzz-3-0-anomaly-farming/inline-2.webp)

Combat in modern urban gacha games like Neverness to Everness is fast and unforgiving. To efficiently farm Anomalies, your hardware must meet three specific criteria:

*   **High-Precision Sensors:** Combat requires frame-perfect dodges and swaps. A sensor like the [Focus Pro 30K](https://www.razer.com/gaming-mice/razer-naga-v2-pro) ensures that your mouse movements are translated with zero jitter, which is critical when navigating the Scarborough Fair gacha board.
*   **Lumbar Support:** Long sessions of City Tycoon management can lead to poor posture. An [integrated lumbar system](https://secretlab.co/products/titan-evo-2022-series) is required to maintain spinal alignment and prevent "gamer lean."
*   **Input Density:** A mouse with multiple side buttons allows for faster menu navigation and character swapping. Mapping your Solid Dice pulls or Arc Research menus to your mouse can significantly speed up your daily routine.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the official PC requirements for Neverness to Everness?
The recommended specs for NTE include an Intel Core i7-12700 (12th Gen) or above, combined with an NVIDIA GeForce RTX 3060 or AMD Radeon RX 6700. For a minimum experience, you will need at least an Intel Core i7-10700 and a GTX 1660 or equivalent.

### How do I get free S-Class Arcs (weapons) in the game?
Players can obtain S-Class Arcs for free through various beginner rewards and the Standard Board. After your first 50 rolls, you can access a free S-Class selector to choose a top-tier character or weapon for your roster.

### Is the Razer Naga V2 Pro worth it for gacha games?
Yes, because the [12-button side plate](https://www.rtings.com/mouse/reviews/razer/naga-v2-pro) allows you to map character swaps and skills to your thumb. This is particularly useful in games like NTE where character swaps are essential for maintaining combat flow and triggering Esper Cycles.

### Can I play NTE on a 1080p monitor?
Absolutely. While the game looks stunning at 1440p, a high-quality 1080p monitor like the <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=ASUS+TUF+VG279Q1A&tag=jeffzen911-20" rel="sponsored nofollow noopener" target="_blank">ASUS TUF VG279Q1A</a> provides a smooth [165Hz experience](https://www.asus.com/us/displays-desktops/monitors/tuf-gaming/tuf-gaming-vg279q1a/) that is more than sufficient for competitive play.
