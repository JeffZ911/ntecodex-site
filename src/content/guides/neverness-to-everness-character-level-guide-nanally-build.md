## Overview

![Overview](/img/neverness-to-everness-character-level-guide-nanally-build/inline-1.webp)


Nanally Coluccis is an Anima-type damage dealer whose primary role is to act as the main carry for your squad. Her combat identity revolves around the "Underboss" mechanic—a series of follow-up strikes that trigger automatically when specific conditions are met during her skill windows. Unlike many other DPS units, Nanally does not rely solely on her Ultimate; her sustained damage output during her "Ichi-daime’s Authority" state is what truly sets her apart in the 1.0 meta.

Character progression in Neverness to Everness is a multi-layered system. You cannot simply grind experience to reach the maximum level immediately. Instead, your progress is gated by two primary factors: Hunter Rank and Appraisal Level. Hunter Rank serves as your account-wide experience level, while Appraisal Level acts as the World Level, determining the difficulty of enemies and the quality of loot dropped in the open world.

### Who is Nanally Coluccis?

Lore-wise, Nanally is a prominent figure in the Hethereau underworld, and her gameplay reflects this "boss" persona. She utilizes dual-wielded Plasma Arcs to shred enemy resistances. Her primary strength is her ability to deal consistent AoE (Area of Effect) Anima damage, making her exceptionally efficient at clearing groups of enemies gathered by supports like [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide).

To level Nanally effectively, you must increase your Hunter Rank by completing urban requests, story missions, and Side Tycoon activities. Every 10 Hunter Ranks, you will face an Appraisal Trial. ## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/neverness-to-everness-character-level-guide-nanally-build/inline-3.webp)


The Disk system in NTE is the equivalent of artifacts or gear sets in other RPGs. It consists of a Main Cartridge and several Modules that are slotted into a "Console." For Nanally, the goal is to maximize her Anima DMG and CRIT stats.

### Top Cartridge Sets

The undisputed best set for Nanally is the "Fireflies and the Forest" Cartridge set. This set is specifically tuned for Anima DPS characters who hit multiple times per second.

*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Increases Anima DMG by 10%.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** Provides a stacking CRIT DMG buff every time an Anima hit is recorded. This can stack up to a maximum of 56% CRIT DMG, which Nanally can reach within the first few seconds of her rotation.

### Module Stat Priority

When slotting Modules into Nanally's Console, you must pay attention to "Module Geometry." Nanally has a unique passive that grants her a 7.5% CRIT Rate bonus if you prioritize Type III Modules (triangular shapes) in her grid.

1.  **Main Cartridge Stat:** Anima DMG Bonus.
2.  **Module Substats:** CRIT DMG > CRIT Rate > ATK% > Energy Regeneration.

### Farming Disks in Hethereau

High-tier "Fireflies and the Forest" Disks are farmed in the late-game rift challenges located in the outskirts of the city. It is recommended to wait until Appraisal Level 4 before using your limited stamina to farm these, as the drop rate for S-Rank Disks increases significantly at that threshold.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/neverness-to-everness-character-level-guide-nanally-build/inline-5.webp)


To get the most out of Nanally, you must follow a strict combat rotation. This ensures that all buffs are active when she enters her high-damage state.

### Opening Sequence

1.  **Haniel:** Start the fight by casting Haniel's Ultimate. This applies the ATK buff immediately.
2.  **Sakiri:** Quickly swap to Sakiri and use "Devour Whole." This will pull all nearby mobs into a single point.
3.  **Zero:** Switch to Zero and use his primary skill to trigger an Esper Cycle. This generates the energy needed for Nanally’s expensive Ultimate.

### Maximizing the Underboss Window

Once the enemies are grouped and the buffs are active, swap to Nanally.

1.  **Skill Activation:** Use Nanally's Skill to enter the "Ichi-daime's Authority" state.
2.  **Ultimate:** Immediately cast her Ultimate to apply Anima Shred to the enemies.
3.  **Optimal Combo:** Perform 3 Standard Basic Attacks followed by 3 Charged Attacks (Hold). This specific sequence triggers the maximum number of "Underboss" follow-up strikes within the skill window.

### Energy Management and Cooldowns

Nanally’s biggest weakness is her high energy requirement. If you find yourself unable to cast her Ultimate every rotation, consider swapping one Module for an Energy Regeneration stat or ensuring that Zero is staying on field long enough to generate Anima particles.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/neverness-to-everness-character-level-guide-nanally-build/inline-6.webp)


### Leveling and Progression FAQs

**How do I unlock Ascension?**
You must reach Hunter Rank 10 and gather district-specific materials. Once you hit the level cap (20, 30, 40, etc.), the Ascension button will appear in the character menu.

**Can I reset character levels?**
No, there is currently no way to reset character levels or recover spent materials. However, materials become much more abundant as your Appraisal Level rises, so low-level investments are not a major loss in the long run.

### Nanally Build FAQs

**What are Mind Shards?**
Mind Shards are duplicate character copies. They are used in the 6-level Awakening system. For Nanally, her first two Awakenings are significant power spikes, increasing the damage of her Underboss strikes by a substantial margin.

**Is CRIT Rate or ATK% better for Nanally?**
Because Nanally hits so frequently, CRIT Rate is generally superior. You want to aim for a 1:2 ratio of CRIT Rate to CRIT DMG. Her innate 7.5% bonus from Type III Modules makes reaching a 60%+ CRIT Rate much easier.

### Boss and Material Farming

**Where is the Headless Rider?**
The Headless Rider is a world boss located South of Illusion Town. It is a unique encounter: it spawns only at night, and you must be driving a car in the designated area to trigger the boss's appearance. Defeating it is the only way to get the "Raging Flames" Arc for free.

**How do I get more Cartridges?**
Cartridges and Modules are primarily obtained from the "Hethereau Cleanup" missions and high-level rift challenges. You can also craft specific sets at the workshop using materials found in the City Tycoon mode.
