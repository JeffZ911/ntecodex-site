## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does Neverness to Everness support ultrawide resolutions on both Steam and Epic?
Yes, Neverness to Everness is built on Unreal Engine 5, which natively supports 21:9 and 32:9 aspect ratios. Whether you launch via Steam or Epic Games, you can adjust the resolution in the settings to fill an ultrawide monitor like the LG 34GP83A-B.

### Can I use a controller for NTE on PC?
NTE features full controller support, which is particularly useful for the vehicle customization and driving mechanics. Steam users can further customize their controller layouts using Steam Input to add macros or adjust stick sensitivity.

### Is a mechanical keyboard necessary for NTE combat?
While not strictly required, a mechanical keyboard like the Keychron Q1 Pro provides much better tactile feedback for the game's combat system. This helps in timing Esper abilities and ensures that every keypress is registered during intense encounters with Anomalies.

### Will my PC run NTE better on Steam or Epic Games?
Performance is generally identical between the two platforms as they use the same game files. However, some players prefer Steam for its shader pre-caching, which can reduce stuttering in Unreal Engine 5 titles during initial exploration of large cities like Hethereau.
