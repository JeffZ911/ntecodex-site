## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/gilgamesh-vs-archer-free-character-choice-guide-20260711/inline-4.webp)


While high-end gear can improve the experience, it isn't always the right choice for every player.

*   **Mobile-Only Players:** If you primarily play in short 15-minute bursts on a mobile device, a high-end ergonomic chair like the Secretlab will not provide a significant return on investment.
*   **Small Desk Spaces:** If your desk is less than 30 inches deep, the LG Ultrawide's stand and screen size may cause more eye strain than it solves. The screen will be too close to your face to comfortably view the entire horizontal plane.
*   **FPS Specialists:** Avoid the Razer Naga if you have small hands or prefer lightweight mice for competitive shooters. Its 134g bulk is tailored for MMO/JRPG comfort rather than the rapid "flick" movements required in games like *Valorant* or *Counter-Strike*.
