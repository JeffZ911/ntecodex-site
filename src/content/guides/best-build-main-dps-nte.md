## Overview

![Overview](/img/best-build-main-dps-nte/inline-1.webp)


Nanally is currently the strongest Main DPS in Neverness to Everness (NTE) following the April 2026 launch. As the featured S-Rank character on the debut "The Ichi-daime" banner, she has set a high bar for damage dealers in the urban environment of Hethereau. She is an Anima character of the Plasma Arc type, specializing in high-frequency follow-up attacks that can shred both elite shields and mob groups with ease.

What sets Nanally apart from other damage dealers is her unique mechanic: Anti-Gravity Mobility. This allows Nanally to fight on vertical surfaces, including walls and ceilings. In the dense urban combat of Hethereau, this provides a massive tactical advantage, allowing players to bypass ground-based hazards or target enemy weak points from angles other characters cannot reach. Her core strength lies in her "Underboss" summon, a spectral entity that coordinates with her every strike. This summon deals massive off-field damage even during burst windows, ensuring that her DPS output remains consistent throughout the fight.

As a primary on-field carry, Nanally scales exceptionally well with CRIT stats and the Blossom reaction. When paired with Cosmos-type characters, she triggers elemental interactions that multiply her damage output significantly.

### Why Nanally is the Meta Main DPS

In the current 1.0 version, Nanally dominates the meta due to her sheer versatility. Unlike other DPS units that require specific conditions to deal maximum damage, Nanally’s kit is self-contained. Her ability to stay mobile while maintaining a high damage-per-second floor makes her the safest yet most aggressive choice for clearing high-level Commissions and the City Mirage endgame content.

### Core Mechanics: Follow-up Attacks & Anti-Gravity

Nanally’s playstyle revolves around "Colucci Howling Technique," her primary skill that triggers the Ichi-daime's Authority state. While in this state, her Basic Attacks are converted into Plasma strikes that trigger the Underboss to perform "Critical Riposte" follow-up attacks. These follow-ups do not just deal damage; they also regenerate Energy, allowing for nearly 100% uptime on her Ultimate if built correctly.

## Best Weapons

![Best Weapons](/img/best-build-main-dps-nte/inline-2.webp)


Choosing the right Plasma Arc is vital for maximizing Nanally’s potential. Because she hits so frequently, weapons that provide stacking buffs or CRIT stats are prioritized over flat ATK weapons.

### Signature S-Rank: Ready-Ready

Ready-Ready is Nanally's signature weapon and the undisputed Best-in-Slot (BiS) option. It provides a massive CRIT Rate substat, which alleviates the pressure on your Disk substat rolls. The weapon’s unique Arc Skill, "Commander Tiger Talisman," boosts Basic Attack and Critical Riposte DMG by up to 30% for 12 seconds after casting a Redirect Skill. Since Nanally’s entire kit revolves around these two damage types, the synergy is perfect.

### Best F2P & Budget Alternatives

For players who did not pull for the signature weapon, there are several competitive options available:

*   **Fluff of Fortitude (S-Rank):** This is the premium Battle Pass option. It serves as a strong "stat stick" by providing a high base ATK and a passive that increases overall DMG by 22%. This bonus rises to 28% against enemies with less than 50% HP, making it an excellent "execution" weapon for boss fights.
*   **Oraora! (A-Rank):** The best F2P alternative. This weapon stacks Basic Attack DMG every time a hit lands, up to 10 times. Given Nanally’s high-frequency combo strings, she can reach max stacks in less than three seconds. At Max Resonance (M5), Oraora! can rival S-Rank weapons in sustained combat.
*   **Song of the Whale (S-Rank):** A farmable overworld option found through exploration and high-level crafting. It provides solid ATK scaling and is a great placeholder for players focusing on overworld exploration rather than gacha pulls.
*   **Us (B-Rank):** A viable early-game starter. It offers a flat ATK boost and a minor enhancement to Basic Attacks. Replace this as soon as you acquire an A-Rank Plasma Arc.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/best-build-main-dps-nte/inline-3.webp)


Disks (also known as Cartridges) are the primary way to customize Nanally’s stats. For a Main DPS, you want a set that rewards her for being on the field and dealing elemental damage.

### Top Choice: Fireflies and the Forest

The **Fireflies and the Forest** (4-Piece Legendary) is currently the gold standard for Anima DPS units. The 2-piece bonus provides a 10% Anima DMG bonus. The 4-piece bonus is where the set truly shines: it stacks CRIT DMG (up to 56%) whenever any team member deals Anima DMG. Since Nanally and her Underboss deal Anima DMG constantly, she can maintain the maximum stack count indefinitely.

**Alternative Set: Lost Radiance**
The **Lost Radiance** (4-Piece) set is a specialized alternative for boss encounters. It allows the wearer to ignore 25% of the enemy's DEF for 20 seconds after casting an Ultimate. If you find yourself struggling against high-armor targets in the later floors of the City Mirage, this set can outperform Fireflies and the Forest in short burst windows.

**General DPS Set: Devil's Blood: Curse**
If you are still farming for legendary sets, **Devil's Blood: Curse** is a reliable fallback. It provides a flat 18% DMG increase. This bonus doubles to 36% if the target is currently affected by the Nova or Stain status effects, making it a strong choice if you run Nanally with specific elemental sub-DPS units.

### Module Layout & Sub-stat Priority

Nanally’s Console layout is unique. To maximize her efficiency, you should focus on **Type II Modules**. Nanally has a hidden passive that grants her a 6% CRIT Rate bonus for every Type II module equipped in her Console. A full Type II layout can provide a "free" 30% CRIT Rate, allowing you to focus entirely on CRIT DMG in your Disk substats.

**Sub-stat Priority:**
1.  **CRIT Rate:** Aim for 70%+ (including Console bonuses).
2.  **CRIT DMG:** As high as possible; 160%+ is the baseline for endgame.
3.  **Anima DMG%:** Essential on the main slot of the Disk.
4.  **ATK%:** Provides the base for all other multipliers.
5.  **Cycle Intensity:** Helps with elemental reaction damage and energy regeneration.

Nanally shines brightest when her teammates can set up elemental reactions and provide grouping for her AoE attacks.

### The Blossom Meta: Nanally & Esper Zero

The most powerful team composition at launch is the **Blossom Core**. This involves pairing Nanally (Anima) with Esper Zero (Cosmos). When Anima and Cosmos elements interact, they trigger the **Blossom** reaction. This reaction spawns "Vita Buds" on the battlefield that deal additional follow-up damage whenever Nanally attacks.

*   **Main DPS:** Nanally
*   **Sub-DPS:** Esper Zero (Provides Cosmos application and off-field support)
*   **Support:** [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) (Incantation). Sakiri is the premium support choice because she can group enemies into a tight cluster. This allows Nanally’s Underboss to hit every enemy at once, maximizing her Plasma Arc's AoE potential.

### Flexible Support & Sustain Options

If you do not have Sakiri, you can use **Jiuyuan** (Anima). Jiuyuan provides Anima RES shred, which directly increases Nanally’s damage. She also offers quick-swap burst damage to fill the 2-3 seconds of downtime when Nanally’s skill is on cooldown.

For the sustain slot, **Adler** or **Fadia** are the top choices. Adler provides powerful shields and adds a Damage-over-Time (DOT) effect to enemies, while Fadia is a dedicated healer who offers defensive stability for high-risk commissions where one hit can be fatal.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/best-build-main-dps-nte/inline-6.webp)


### Common Build Questions

**Is Nanally worth pulling for F2P players?**
Yes. Nanally is the most versatile DPS available at launch. Her synergy with Esper Zero—who is provided for free to all players—makes her incredibly accessible. She does not require high Awakening levels (duplicates) to be effective, though her A3 and A5 provide significant power spikes.

**What is the "Anti-Gravity" combat benefit?**
Beyond looking stylish, Anti-Gravity allows you to avoid ground-based AoE attacks from bosses like the Headless Rider. By fighting from the walls of the arena, you can continue your DPS rotation while the boss performs ground-clearing mechanics that would force other melee characters to retreat.

**Can I use Nanally without her signature weapon?**
Absolutely. While Ready-Ready is her best option, the A-Rank weapon "Oraora!" at Max Resonance (M5) is highly competitive. Many players find that a Max Resonance Oraora! outperforms a base Resonance S-Rank weapon like Song of the Whale.

### Advanced Combat Tips

**How do I trigger follow-up attacks?**
Follow-up attacks are triggered automatically in two ways. First, by using her Ultimate, which summons the Underboss to mimic her moves. Second, her A3 and A5 awakenings allow her Underboss to strike whenever a teammate deals damage, significantly increasing her off-field presence.

**What is the best main stat for the Cartridge?**
Always aim for **Anima DMG%** on the main slot of your primary Disk. While ATK% might seem comparable in the early game, the elemental damage multiplier is calculated separately, leading to much higher total damage in the endgame when your base ATK is already high.

**Comparison of Nanally at A0 vs. A3 Awakening:**
*   **A0 (Base):** Full access to Anti-Gravity and Underboss summon during Ultimate.
*   **A3 (Awakened):** Adds a passive where the Underboss performs a "Sync-Strike" every 4 seconds when Nanally is off-field, making her a viable Sub-DPS/Burst-Support if you pull a stronger Main DPS in the future.
