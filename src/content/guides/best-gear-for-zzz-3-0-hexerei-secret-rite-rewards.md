## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/best-gear-for-zzz-3-0-hexerei-secret-rite-rewards/inline-4.webp)


While high-end gear can improve your experience, it is not always a necessary investment.

*   **Short Sessions**: If your current sessions are under 2 hours, a $500 ergonomic chair will not provide a noticeable health benefit over a standard office chair. Ergonomics is a cumulative benefit that manifests over long durations.
*   **GPU Limitations**: Don't upgrade to a 1440p monitor if your GPU cannot maintain at least 60 FPS at that resolution. In a game like *Neverness to Everness*, which features Ray Tracing, the performance hit at 1440p is significant. Stuttering caused by an underpowered GPU will cause more eye strain than a lower resolution.
*   **Audio Preferences**: Skip the high-end wireless headsets if you only play in a quiet room. A wired pair of open-back headphones, such as the [Sennheiser HD 560S](https://www.rtings.com/headphones/reviews/sennheiser/hd-560s), will offer superior audio fidelity and a wider soundstage for the same price, provided you don't mind the cable.

## What we didn't include and why

The weight savings are less important than the utility of having your shortcuts accessible at your thumb.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why is 1440p recommended over 1080p for Neverness to Everness?
NTE uses Unreal Engine 5, which features high-density assets and complex lighting effects. A 1440p resolution provides 77% more pixels than 1080p, allowing you to see finer details in the supernatural "Anomalies" and urban architecture without the blurry edges common on lower-resolution screens.

### Do I really need an MMO mouse for an urban RPG?
While not strictly required, an MMO mouse like the Naga V2 Pro significantly reduces the number of keyboard stretches required to open menus, inventory, and character screens. Over a 6-hour session, this reduction in hand movement can prevent repetitive strain in your non-mouse hand.

### How does a footrest help with back pain?
When your feet are not firmly planted, your lower back compensates by tensing to maintain balance. A footrest ensures your weight is distributed correctly through your hips and thighs, allowing the lumbar support in your chair to work as intended.

### Is the Secretlab chair too firm for comfort?
The cold-cure foam used by Secretlab is designed for support rather than plushness. While it may feel stiff initially, it prevents you from "bottoming out" against the frame of the chair, which is what causes soreness during long-session grinds for rewards.
