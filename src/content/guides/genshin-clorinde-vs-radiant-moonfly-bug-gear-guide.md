## Frequently Asked Questions

### How does a high refresh rate monitor help in gacha games?
A high refresh rate, such as the 180Hz offered by the LG 27GP850-B, reduces motion blur and makes character movement feel significantly smoother. This is particularly useful in open-world games with fast camera rotations, as it helps reduce eye strain during long farming sessions.

### Is the Razer Naga V2 Pro too heavy for casual play?
At 134g, it is heavier than specialized FPS mice, but this weight provides stability for precise clicking in menus and skill bars. For players who aren't performing rapid "flick" shots, the extra weight is rarely an issue and is a fair trade-off for the 12-button side plate.

### Why is firm foam better for a gaming chair?
Soft, plush foam feels comfortable initially but often collapses under your weight after an hour, leaving you sitting against the hard internal frame. Firm cold-cure foam, like that in the Secretlab TITAN Evo, is designed to support your weight evenly for many hours without losing its shape.

### Can the Keychron K2 V2 connect to multiple devices?
Yes, the Keychron K2 V2 can pair with up to three devices via Bluetooth and switch between them using a key combination. This is ideal for players who want to use the same keyboard for their gaming PC, a tablet for guides, and a work laptop.
