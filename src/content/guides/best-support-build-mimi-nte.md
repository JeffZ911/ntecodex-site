# Best Support Build for Mimi (Haniel) in Neverness to Everness

## Overview

![Overview](/img/best-support-build-mimi-nte/inline-1.webp)


Haniel (frequently referred to as Mimi by the community due to her similar support role to characters in other Hotta titles) is the premier A-Rank support in Neverness to Everness. While the community often focuses on her aesthetic appeal, her mechanical value lies in her ability to provide massive, team-wide offensive buffs that scale with her own progression.

She is a dedicated ATK buffer whose utility scales directly with her own base ATK attribute. Unlike other supports who may rely on complex elemental setups, Haniel’s primary contribution is a flat percentage increase to the active character's offensive power. This makes her exceptionally valuable for high-damage carries who can leverage every point of extra attack.

Her primary role is to enable high-damage carries like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte). While Haniel herself is a Psyche-element character, she is the cornerstone of the popular "Blossom" team architecture. By providing consistent team-wide buffs, she ensures that the elemental reactions triggered by her teammates reach their maximum damage potential.

Haniel is highly accessible, being a [free character](https://ntecodex.com/guides/gilgamesh-vs-archer-free-character-choice-guide-20260711) obtained through the gacha unlock and pre-registration rewards. This accessibility, combined with her low-investment requirements, makes her a staple for both new players and veteran "sweaty" optimizers looking to push the 1.0 meta.

### Role in the 1.0 Meta
In the launch version of Neverness to Everness, the meta is heavily defined by "Follow-up" damage and elemental reactions like Blossom. Haniel fits into this ecosystem as the ultimate "stat stick" support. She doesn't need to be on the field for long; her kit is designed to deploy a buff and immediately swap out, allowing your main DPS to enjoy increased damage windows. Her presence is almost mandatory in any team looking to maximize the "Esper Cycle" damage.

### Why Haniel is the Ultimate F2P Support
For Free-to-Play (F2P) players, Haniel is a godsend. Because she is distributed for free through pre-registration milestones (specifically the 30 million global mark), every player has access to her. Furthermore, her best-in-slot weapons and gear are often obtainable through in-game activities or the standard recruitment pool. She requires very little "Resonance" (duplicate) investment to be effective, though her higher resonance levels do provide significant boosts to team CRIT DMG.

## Best Weapons

![Best Weapons](/img/best-support-build-mimi-nte/inline-2.webp)


In Neverness to Everness, weapons are referred to as Arcs. For a support like Haniel, you want Arcs that either boost her Base ATK (to increase her buff scaling) or provide utility to the rest of the team while she is off-field.

### Top Tier: S-Rank Arcs
*   **Blow up the Crowd (S-Rank):** The absolute best-in-slot Arc for Haniel. This weapon provides massive ATK% scaling that directly enhances her support buffs. Its unique passive, "Beat King," increases the active character's ATK by 10% while the wearer is off-field. Additionally, it increases the active character's ATK by an extra 2% whenever the wearer deals damage (up to 4 stacks), making it perfect for Haniel's summoned Hootie speaker.
*   **Day Off (S-Rank):** A powerful alternative that grants a significant flat ATK increase. While it is the signature weapon for the protagonist (Zero), it serves as an excellent "stat stick" for Haniel. Its "Eclipse" effect allows for energy restoration, which helps Haniel maintain her Ultimate uptime in longer encounters.
*   **Hethereau's Keeper (S-Rank):** Excellent for sustained boss fights. It offers a 15% ATK boost and a 15% damage increase against bosses. More importantly, it unlocks the "Officer Whisker" Arc, summoning a companion that deals 100% of the wearer's ATK as damage per hit for 30 seconds. This allows Haniel to contribute meaningful off-field damage while her buffs are active.

### F2P Alternatives: A-Rank and B-Rank Arcs
*   **Time Bandit (A-Rank):** The best F2P weapon for Haniel in the early game. While its primary stat is HP%, its passive increases Break Intensity by 90 for 10 seconds after casting a Redirect Skill. This helps Haniel contribute to the team's "Break" goals before swapping to a DPS.
*   **Forgotten (B-Rank):** undisclosed.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/best-support-build-mimi-nte/inline-3.webp)


Disks, or Cartridges, are the primary way to customize Haniel's stats. For a support build, your goal is to maximize "Charge Efficiency" and team-wide offensive bonuses.

### Recommended Cartridge Sets
*   **Speedy Hedgehog (4-Piece Set):** The definitive support Cartridge set in NTE. The 2-piece bonus provides a 12% increase to Charge Efficiency, allowing Haniel to reach her Ultimate faster. The 4-piece bonus is the star: it increases all allies' ATK by 15% for 20 seconds after the wearer casts their Ultimate. This effect does not stack, so you only need one character on the team running this set.
*   **Fireflies and the Forest (2-Piece Set):** A viable hybrid option if you want Haniel to contribute more personal damage. The 2-piece bonus provides a 10% Anima DMG boost (though Haniel is Psyche-element, this set is often paired with others in hybrid builds).
*   **Shadow Creed (2-Piece or 4-Piece):** A strong alternative that focuses purely on ATK. The 2-piece gives +10% ATK, and the 4-piece grants a 25% ATK bonus for 20 seconds after casting a Skill. This is easier to trigger than the Speedy Hedgehog bonus but lacks the team-wide utility.

### Optimal Stat Distribution and Breakpoints
To maximize Haniel's potential, you must prioritize her Base ATK. Her buffs do not scale with "Total ATK" from all sources in the same way they scale with her core attributes, so focus on the following:

1.  **Main Stat Priority:**
    *   **Sands/Goblet:** ATK% is mandatory. This is the primary driver of her buffing capability.
    *   **Circlet:** CRIT Rate or ATK%. CRIT Rate is useful for triggering certain energy-regeneration procs from weapons or other team passives.
2.  **Sub-stat Focus:**
    *   **Energy Recharge / Charge Efficiency:** Aim for 160% or higher. You need her Ultimate available every rotation.
    *   **ATK% / Flat ATK:** Any additional attack directly increases the team's damage ceiling.
    *   **Break Intensity:** Useful for helping the team stagger bosses faster.

## Team Comp

![Team Comp](/img/best-support-build-mimi-nte/inline-4.webp)


Haniel’s value is maximized when she is paired with characters who can take full advantage of her ATK buffs and provide the elemental triggers she lacks.

### The Blossom Meta: Nanally & Haniel
The most powerful team composition in the 1.0 meta revolves around the **Blossom** reaction. Blossom is triggered by combining **Anima** and **Cosmos** elements. While Haniel is a **Psyche** character, her universal ATK buffs are so potent that she is the preferred third slot for the Nanally-Zero core.

*   **Nanally (Main DPS):** An Anima-element powerhouse. She provides the bulk of the damage and benefits immensely from Haniel's ATK scaling.
*   **Zero (Sub-DPS/Enabler):** A Cosmos-element character who triggers the Blossom reaction with Nanally. Zero also generates "Cycle Meter" quickly, allowing for frequent swaps.
*   **Haniel (Buffer):** Deploys Hootie to buff both Nanally and Zero, while contributing off-field Psyche damage.

### F2P Friendly Team Structures
For players who did not pull for Nanally, Haniel can still lead a very effective team:
*   **Zero (DPS):** Using the protagonist as your main damage dealer.
*   **Haniel (Support):** Providing the necessary ATK boost to keep Zero's damage relevant.
*   **Edgar (Healer):** An A-Rank Cosmos healer who ensures the team stays alive in difficult "Beyond the Rails" undisclosed content.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/best-support-build-mimi-nte/inline-5.webp)


To get the most out of Haniel, you must follow a strict rotation that ensures her buffs are active when your Main DPS takes the field.

### Standard Combat Opening
1.  **Opening Sequence:** Start with Haniel and cast her Ultimate, **'A Melody Named Haniel'**. This immediately applies the team-wide ATK bonus and enters the "Paranormal Ace" state.
2.  **Skill Chaining:** Follow the Ultimate immediately with her Redirect Skill, **'Silent Moonlit Forest Guardian'**. This throws her speaker, Hootie, onto the field. Hootie will begin granting ATK buffs and building "Four-Part Harmony" stacks.
3.  **The Swap:** Immediately switch to **Zero** or your Cosmos enabler. This swap triggers the **Esper Cycle**, consuming the full meter to deal burst damage and trigger the **Blossom** reaction (if swapping to an Anima character).
4.  **Execution Phase:** Bring in **Nanally**. With Haniel's ATK buff at its peak and Hootie triggering "Ensemble" follow-up attacks, Nanally can unleash her skills and Ultimate to shred the enemy.

### Maximizing the Esper Cycle Meter
The "Esper Cycle" is the circular meter next to character portraits. Haniel builds this meter through her basic attacks and Hootie's hits. To optimize your rotation, ensure that Haniel's meter is full before you begin your burst window. If the meter is not full, use a "Perfect Dodge" or "Parry" with Haniel to instantly refill it before swapping.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/best-support-build-mimi-nte/inline-6.webp)


### Common Player Questions
**Is Mimi a playable character in Neverness to Everness?**
Yes, but her official name in the game is **Haniel**. The community calls her "Mimi" due to her visual similarities to a character from Tower of Fantasy, another Hotta Studio game.

**Does Haniel's buff snapshot her current ATK?**
undisclosed. Most players recommend casting her Ultimate while all temporary self-buffs are active to be safe.

**What is the best F2P weapon for Haniel?**
**Time Bandit** is the most reliable A-Rank choice, but **Blow up the Crowd** can be obtained for free by completing the "Rhythm Master" Anomaly Commission in Illusion Town.

**How do I unlock Haniel's Resonance levels?**
As an A-Rank character, Haniel's "Resonances" (duplicates) can be obtained through the standard recruitment banner or as occasional rewards from city exploration and "City Tycoon" rank milestones.

### Troubleshooting Your Support Build
If your Haniel doesn't seem to be providing enough of a boost, check two things: her **Base ATK** and her **Charge Efficiency**. If her Ultimate isn't ready every time you swap to your DPS, you are losing a significant portion of your team's damage potential. Switch to a **Speedy Hedgehog** 2-piece set or an energy-focused Arc if you find yourself waiting on cooldowns.
