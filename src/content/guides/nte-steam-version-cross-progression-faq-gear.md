## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/nte-steam-version-cross-progression-faq-gear/inline-4.webp)

Not every player needs a top-of-the-line setup to enjoy their time in Hethereau. There are several scenarios where your current gear might already be sufficient.

*   **Existing High-Refresh Monitors:** If you already own a 1440p monitor with at least a 144Hz refresh rate, the jump to 160Hz on the LG UltraGear is negligible for this specific gameplay style.
*   **Gamepad Preference:** Don't buy an MMO mouse like the Naga if you primarily play with a controller. The game is heavily optimized for gamepads, and many players find the controller experience more intuitive for driving and combat.
*   **Functional Ergonomics:** If your current chair has a mesh back and adjustable lumbar support that isn't sagging, a gaming chair upgrade may actually feel too firm. High-end office chairs often provide better breathability than the synthetic leather found on many gaming models.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the Steam version of Neverness to Everness support cross-progression?
Yes, the Steam version [fully supports cross-progression](https://store.steampowered.com/news/app/2992810/view/4243036666838384241) with existing accounts on mobile and the standalone PC launcher. You can log in using your existing credentials to sync your progress, characters like Nanally, and inventory across all platforms.

### When is the Neverness to Everness Steam release date?
The game officially launches on Steam on [July 8, 2026](https://store.steampowered.com/news/app/2992810/view/4243036666838384241). This version will launch directly into the [Version 1.2 content](https://store.steampowered.com/news/app/2992810/view/4243036666838384241), including new characters and updates that were introduced after the initial April launch.

### What are the minimum PC requirements for the Steam version?
To run the game, you will need at least an Intel Core i7-10700 CPU and an NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1660 GPU. Additionally, 16GB of RAM and approximately 60GB of storage space are required for the installation.

### Can I use a controller on the Steam version?
Yes, the Steam version includes full controller support. The developers have implemented optimizations for controller responsiveness, making it a viable alternative to keyboard and mouse for both combat and urban exploration.
