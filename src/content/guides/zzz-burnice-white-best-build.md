# Zenless Zone Zero Burnice White Best Build Guide

## Overview

![Overview](/img/zzz-burnice-white-best-build/inline-1.webp)


Burnice White is an S-Rank Fire Anomaly Agent belonging to the Sons of Calydon faction. Unlike traditional on-field attackers, Burnice excels as an off-field Sub-DPS who applies the Scorched effect to enemies. This unique playstyle allows her to maintain constant elemental pressure without hogging field time, making her the perfect partner for aggressive on-field carries.

Her kit revolves around the Heat mechanic. By consuming Energy through her various attacks, Burnice builds up Heat. Once she reaches 50 Heat, she enters the Nitro-Fuel Cocktail state. In this state, her attacks apply the Scorched effect. When a teammate hits an enemy afflicted by Scorched, it triggers Afterburn DMG. This Afterburn DMG is categorized as Assist Attack DMG and scales directly with Burnice's ATK and Anomaly Proficiency.

### Burnice's Role in the Meta

In the current Zenless Zone Zero meta, Burnice White occupies a top-tier slot for Anomaly-focused compositions. While characters like Grace or [Jane Doe](https://ntecodex.com/guides/yanagi-vs-jane-doe-anomaly-comparison) require significant field time to build up their respective anomalies, Burnice does the heavy lifting from the sidelines. She is the first character specifically designed to facilitate "Disorder" damage—a massive burst of damage triggered when a second elemental anomaly is applied to an enemy already suffering from one.

Her presence allows players to run dual-anomaly setups without the clunkiness of constant manual swapping. By simply building her Heat and switching out, she provides consistent Fire application that bridges the gap between different elemental rotations.

### Understanding the Heat & Scorched Mechanics

The core of any Burnice White best build is mastering her Heat management. At the start of a combat encounter, Burnice typically begins with 100 Heat (at Mindscape 0). This allows her to immediately enter the Nitro-Fuel Cocktail state.

- **Nitro-Fuel Cocktail:** While in this state, Burnice's Fire Anomaly Buildup is increased.
- **Scorched:** Applied by Burnice's Basic Attacks, Dash Attacks, or EX Specials while Nitro-Fuel is active.
- **Afterburn DMG:** When an ally hits a Scorched enemy, an explosion occurs. This can trigger once every 1.5 seconds. It consumes a small amount of Heat each time.
- **Heat Depletion:** If Heat reaches 0, the Nitro-Fuel Cocktail state ends, and Burnice can no longer apply Scorched or trigger Afterburn until she builds more Heat by consuming Energy.

## Best Weapons

![Best Weapons](/img/zzz-burnice-white-best-build/inline-2.webp)


Choosing the right W-Engine is vital for maximizing Burnice's off-field damage and ensuring she can maintain her Heat levels throughout long battles.

### Top-Tier S-Rank W-Engines

**Flamemaker Shaker (S-Rank)**
This is Burnice's signature W-Engine and is undeniably her best-in-slot option. It provides a massive Base ATK of 713 and an Advanced Stat of 25% Energy Regen. Its passive effect increases Fire DMG by 15% while the wearer is off-field. Furthermore, using an EX Special Attack or triggering an Assist Attack grants the wearer a DMG buff that stacks up to 10 times, significantly boosting the potency of her Afterburn explosions.

**Fusion Compiler (S-Rank)**
Originally Grace’s signature weapon, this is a phenomenal alternative for Burnice. It offers 684 Base ATK and a 24% PEN Ratio. The passive grants a stackable Anomaly Proficiency buff whenever the user uses a Special Attack or EX Special Attack. Since Burnice relies heavily on her EX Special to dump Energy and build Heat, she can maintain these stacks easily.

### Best A-Rank and F2P Alternatives

**Roaring Ride (A-Rank)**
This W-Engine is tailored for the Sons of Calydon. It provides ATK% as an advanced stat. When the user triggers an EX Special Attack, it grants a random buff: either an ATK boost, an Anomaly Proficiency boost, or an Anomaly Buildup Rate boost. All three of these stats are highly beneficial for Burnice's kit.

**Rainforest Gourmet (A-Rank)**
The best craftable F2P option available at the Gadget Store. It rewards Burnice for her high Energy consumption. For every 10 Energy consumed, the user gains a stack of ATK% buff (up to 10 stacks). Since Burnice’s primary rotation involves dumping 40 to 80 Energy in a single EX Special sequence, she can reach max stacks almost instantly.

**Weeping Gemini (A-Rank)**
A solid choice for long-form content like Hollow Zero or Shiyu Defense. It provides Anomaly Proficiency that increases every time a team member triggers an Elemental Anomaly. This synergizes perfectly with Disorder teams where anomalies are being popped every few seconds.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/zzz-burnice-white-best-build/inline-3.webp)


[Drive Discs](https://ntecodex.com/guides/best-nvme-ssd-gacha-gaming-sessions) are where you fine-tune Burnice’s stats to ensure her Burn damage and Afterburn triggers hit as hard as possible.

### Recommended Drive Disc Sets

**4-Piece Chaos Jazz**
This is the definitive set for Burnice White.
- **2-Piece:** Increases Anomaly Proficiency by 30.
- **4-Piece:** Increases Fire DMG by 15%. While the character is off-field, EX Special Attack and Assist Attack DMG increase by 20%. When the character swaps back to the field, this DMG bonus persists for an additional 5 seconds.
Since almost all of Burnice’s damage comes from Afterburn (Assist DMG) while she is off-field, this set provides the most significant mathematical advantage.

**2-Piece Freedom Blues**
For the remaining two slots, Freedom Blues is the standard choice. It provides an additional 30 Anomaly Proficiency, which is crucial for reaching her passive stat caps. If you already have over 300 Anomaly Proficiency from substats and weapons, you can opt for **Swing Jazz** (10% Energy Regen) or **Inferno Metal** (10% Fire DMG).

### Main Stat and Substat Optimization

Burnice's damage does not crit. Therefore, you should ignore CRIT Rate and CRIT DMG in favor of raw ATK and Anomaly stats.

**Drive Disc Stat Priority:**
- **Partition 4:** Anomaly Proficiency (Mandatory)
- **Partition 5:** Fire DMG% or ATK% (Fire DMG is generally superior)
- **Partition 6:** Anomaly Mastery or Energy Regen (Anomaly Mastery is preferred for faster Burn application)

**Substat Weight Breakdown:**
1. **Anomaly Proficiency:** Highest priority until 300+, then still valuable.
2. **ATK%:** Increases the base damage of Burn and Afterburn.
3. **PEN:** Helps bypass enemy DEF.
4. **Flat ATK:** Minor contribution to overall damage.

### The 300 Anomaly Proficiency Cap Explained

Burnice's [Core Passive](https://ntecodex.com/faq), "Fueling the Fire," provides a direct bonus to her Afterburn DMG based on her Anomaly Proficiency. For every 1 point of Anomaly Proficiency Burnice has, the DMG of her Afterburn increases by 1%, up to a maximum of 300%.

This means that hitting exactly 300 Anomaly Proficiency is the "sweet spot" for her build. Any points beyond 300 will still increase the damage of the Burn status effect itself, but they will no longer boost the Afterburn explosion modifier. If you find yourself at 350+ Anomaly Proficiency, you may want to swap an Anomaly Proficiency main stat for ATK% to achieve a better damage balance.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/zzz-burnice-white-best-build/inline-5.webp)


To get the most out of Burnice, you must understand how to efficiently dump her Energy and swap her out. She is not meant to stay on the field for more than a few seconds at a time.

### Opening Sequence and Heat Management

At the start of the battle, you should immediately switch to Burnice if she isn't your lead character.
1. **Initial Burst:** Use her EX Special Attack: Intense Heat Stirring Method. If you hold the button, she will perform a "Double Shot," spraying fire from both flamethrowers. This consumes Energy rapidly and ensures your Heat bar is full.
2. **Apply Scorched:** Ensure you land at least one hit while her Heat is above 50 to apply the Scorched status to all nearby enemies.
3. **Quick Assist:** Burnice’s EX Special often triggers a Quick Assist. Use this to instantly swap to your Main DPS (like [Jane Doe](https://ntecodex.com/guides/zzz-3-0-velina-vs-jane-doe-gear-comparison) or Piper).

### Off-Field Application Strategy

Once Burnice is off-field, your goal is to trigger Afterburn as often as possible.
- Use your Main DPS to attack aggressively. Every 1.5 seconds, Burnice will automatically contribute a Fire explosion.
- Watch the Heat bar (located under her health bar in the party UI). When it drops below 50, her portrait will stop glowing. This is your cue that Nitro-Fuel Cocktail is ending.
- When Heat is low, wait for Burnice’s Energy to recharge or for a Chain Attack opportunity.

### Ultimate and Quick Assist Timing

Burnice’s Ultimate, **Glorious Inferno**, is a powerful tool for Heat management.
1. **Heat Refresh:** Using her Ultimate immediately restores 50 Heat. This is perfect for when you have run out of Heat but don't have enough Energy for a full EX Special sequence.
2. **Chain Attacks:** During a Chain Attack, always pick Burnice near the end of the chain. Her Chain Attack applies significant Fire buildup and keeps the Scorched effect active.
3. **Defensive Assist:** If your Main DPS is about to be hit, swapping to Burnice via a Defensive Assist is a great way to get her back on the field to refresh Scorched without losing momentum.

**Rotation Flowchart:**
- **Step 1:** Burnice EX Special (Hold) -> Reach Max Heat.
- **Step 2:** Trigger Quick Assist -> Switch to Jane Doe/Piper.
- **Step 3:** Main DPS builds Anomaly -> Trigger Assault/Burn -> Trigger Disorder.
- **Step 4:** Heat drops -> Swap Burnice in for Chain Attack or Ultimate -> Repeat.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/zzz-burnice-white-best-build/inline-6.webp)


**Is Burnice White worth pulling?**
Yes. Burnice is currently the only S-Rank off-field Anomaly Agent. Her ability to enable Disorder teams makes her one of the most future-proof units in the game, as she will likely pair well with any future Anomaly characters of different elements.

**How much Anomaly Proficiency does Burnice need?**
You should aim for at least 300 Anomaly Proficiency. This maximizes her Core Passive bonus for Afterburn DMG. While you can go higher, the returns start to diminish compared to investing in ATK% or Energy Regen.

**Does Afterburn crit?**
No. Afterburn DMG is considered Anomaly-related damage. In Zenless Zone Zero, Anomaly damage (Burn, Shock, Assault, etc.) cannot crit. You should focus entirely on ATK, Anomaly Proficiency, and Fire DMG% for her build.

**What is the best Mindscape for Burnice?**
Mindscape 1 (M1) is a fantastic stopping point as it increases her Heat cap to 140 and allows her to start battles with maximum Heat. Mindscape 2 (M2) is also highly valuable, as it provides a 20% Fire RES shred to enemies afflicted by Scorched, significantly boosting the entire team's Fire damage.

**Can she be played as a Main DPS?**
While you can keep Burnice on the field to use her Basic Attack strings, her kit is heavily optimized for off-field support. Her Basic Attack damage is relatively low compared to the damage potential of her Afterburn triggers and the Disorder reactions she enables while off-field.
