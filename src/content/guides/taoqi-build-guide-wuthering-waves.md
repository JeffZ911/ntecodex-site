## Overview

![Overview](/img/taoqi-build-guide-wuthering-waves/inline-1.webp)


Taoqi is a 4-star Havoc Resonator who wields a Broadblade. Unlike the majority of the roster that relies on Attack (ATK) to drive their damage, Taoqi is a DEF-scaling specialist. This unique scaling allows her to reach absurd levels of survivability while still contributing meaningful damage through her "Timed Counters."

Her primary role in the current meta is a Hybrid Support/Tank. By utilizing her Outro Skill, she provides a massive "Deepen" buff to the next character's Resonance Skill damage. In Wuthering Waves, "Deepen" effects are multiplicative, making them significantly more powerful than standard percentage damage increases. This makes Taoqi the best-in-slot partner for any character whose primary damage source is labeled as Resonance Skill damage.

### Skill Upgrade Priority

To maximize Taoqi's efficiency, you should prioritize her Forte Circuit, as it governs her shield strength and counter-attack damage.

1.  **Forte Circuit (Power of Precision):** This is the core of her kit, defining her "Resolving Caliber" stacks and counter-attack scaling.
2.  **Resonance Skill (Fortified Defense):** Increases the damage reduction and initial shield provided when she enters her defensive stance.
3.  **Resonance Liberation (Unshakable Might):** Provides a large burst of Havoc damage that scales with DEF.
4.  **Intro Skill (Defense Formation):** Useful for initial positioning and stack generation.
5.  **Basic Attack (Rocksteady Shield):** Lowest priority as most of her damage comes from timed counters triggered via her Forte Circuit.

### Taoqi's Role in the Meta

Taoqi occupies a niche but vital position. She is not a "plug-and-play" support like Verina, who fits into every team. Instead, Taoqi is a specialist. She excels in high-difficulty content like the Tower of Adversity, where boss attacks can one-shot squishier Resonators. Her ability to parry and mitigate damage allows players to ignore certain boss mechanics that would otherwise require perfect dodging.

### Pros and Cons of Playing Taoqi

**Pros:**
*   **Extreme Survivability:** High base DEF and damage reduction shields make her nearly impossible to kill.
*   **Unique Buffing:** Her 38% Resonance Skill DMG Deepen is one of the strongest specific buffs in the game.
*   **Parry Mechanics:** Her Resonance Skill allows for a "Strategic Parry" that can interrupt boss attack strings.
*   **Low Investment:** Because she uses DEF% stats, she is often easier to gear than characters competing for high ATK% and Crit Echoes.

**Cons:**
*   **Slow Concerto Generation:** Her biggest weakness is how long it takes to fill her Concerto bar to trigger her Outro Skill.
*   **Niche Synergy:** She only provides maximum value to characters who rely on Resonance Skill damage.
*   **Slow Gameplay:** Her animations and parry-heavy playstyle can feel sluggish compared to the high-speed combat of other Havoc units.

### Top Tier: Dauntless Evernight & Discord

**Dauntless Evernight** is widely considered Taoqi's Best-in-Slot (BiS) for general use. It provides a massive 54% DEF secondary stat at level 90, which directly boosts her damage, shield strength, and healing. The passive further increases her DEF when she uses her Resonance Skill, which aligns perfectly with her rotation.

**Discord** is the preferred choice for players using Taoqi strictly as a "Buff Bot." Since Taoqi's main contribution is her Outro Skill, and her Concerto Energy generation is naturally slow, the Concerto Energy restoration from Discord allows her to swap out much faster. This weapon is essential for maintaining smooth rotations in time-limited content like the Tower of Adversity.

### Alternative & F2P Broadblade Options

For players who have purchased the Insider Channel (Battle Pass), **Autumntrace** is a viable option for a Sub-DPS Taoqi build. While the ATK stacks are largely wasted, the Crit Rate secondary stat helps her Timed Counters and Resonance Liberation hit significantly harder.

For F2P players, **Broadblade of Voyager** is a hidden gem. It provides much-needed Energy Regen and helps her keep her Resonance Liberation available on every rotation, which is her primary way of dealing AoE Havoc damage.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/taoqi-build-guide-wuthering-waves/inline-3.webp)


In Wuthering Waves, the equipment system is known as the **Echo System**. For Taoqi, your choice of Echo set will determine if she functions as a team-wide buffer or a solo tank.

### Optimal Echo Sets: Moonlit Clouds vs. Sun-sinking Eclipse

**1. Moonlit Clouds (5-Piece Set) - Best for Support**
*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Increases Energy Regen by 10%.
*   **5-Piece Bonus:** Upon using the Outro Skill, the ATK of the next Resonator is increased by 22.5% for 15 seconds.
*   **Why use it:** This is Taoqi's best set. It stacks the 22.5% ATK buff on top of her 38% Resonance Skill Deepen buff, making the incoming DPS character incredibly powerful.

**2. Sun-sinking Eclipse (5-Piece Set) - Best for Sub-DPS**
*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Increases Havoc DMG by 10%.
*   **5-Piece Bonus:** Increases Havoc DMG by 7.5% after using Basic or Heavy attacks, stacking up to 4 times.
*   **Why use it:** If you find yourself leaving Taoqi on the field for extended periods to tank hits, this set maximizes her personal Havoc damage.

### Recommended Echo Stats and Substats

Taoqi’s stat priority is unique because of her DEF scaling. You should avoid ATK% main stats entirely.

*   **4-Cost Echo:** DEF% or Crit Rate (Use Bell-Borne Geochelone).
*   **3-Cost Echo:** Havoc DMG% or DEF%.
*   **1-Cost Echo:** DEF%.

**Substat Priority:**
1.  **DEF%:** Increases every aspect of her kit.
2.  **Energy Regeneration:** Crucial for using her Liberation.
3.  **Crit Rate / Crit DMG:** To boost her counter-attack damage.
4.  **Flat DEF:** A secondary boost to her scaling.
5.  **Resonance Skill DMG%:** Increases the damage of her parries.

### Recommended Main Echo Skills

*   **Bell-Borne Geochelone:** This is the best Main Echo for Taoqi. Its active skill provides a shield based on DEF and grants the entire team 50% Damage Reduction and a 10% DMG Boost. Since Taoqi is already building DEF, the shield becomes massive.
*   **Impermanence Heron:** A secondary choice for the Moonlit Clouds set. It provides extra Concerto Energy and increases the damage of the next Resonator by 12% if Taoqi uses her Outro Skill shortly after using the Heron's skill.

### The Danjin & Taoqi Synergy

The synergy between Danjin and Taoqi is currently one of the strongest pairings in Wuthering Waves. Danjin is a "glass cannon" who consumes her own HP to deal massive Havoc damage through her Resonance Skill. 

Taoqi solves both of Danjin's problems:
1.  **Protection:** Taoqi provides shields and damage reduction, preventing Danjin from being one-shot while at low HP.
2.  **Damage:** Taoqi’s Outro Skill grants a 38% Resonance Skill DMG Deepen. Since almost 80% of Danjin’s damage comes from her Resonance Skill, this buff results in a massive DPS increase.

### Universal Support Team Variations

Taoqi can also be paired with **Jiyan**. While Jiyan is often paired with Mortefi for Heavy Attack buffs, Taoqi is a safer alternative for players struggling with boss mechanics. Jiyan’s ultimate state converts his attacks into Resonance Skill damage (in certain mechanical interpretations) or benefits from the survivability Taoqi provides during his long attack animations.

In a **Havoc Rover** team, Taoqi provides the Havoc DMG synergy. While Havoc Rover’s main nuke is their Resonance Liberation, their enhanced Basic Attacks during the Dark Surge state are often supplemented by Skill usage, which Taoqi buffs.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/taoqi-build-guide-wuthering-waves/inline-5.webp)


The goal of a Taoqi rotation is to build three stacks of "Resolving Caliber" as quickly as possible to trigger her Timed Counters, fill her Concerto bar, and swap out.

### Standard Support Rotation

1.  **Intro Skill:** Switch to Taoqi when her Intro is available. This immediately grants her one stack of Resolving Caliber.
2.  **Resonance Skill (Fortified Defense):** Hold the skill to enter the parry stance. If the enemy attacks, Taoqi performs a Strategic Parry, generating significant Concerto Energy and granting shields.
3.  **Basic Attacks (Timed Counters):** After a parry or using her skill, Taoqi’s basic attacks change. Tap the basic attack button with proper timing (when the circle closes) to consume Resolving Caliber stacks and perform powerful counter-attacks.
4.  **Resonance Liberation (Unshakable Might):** Cast her ultimate to deal a large burst of AoE Havoc damage. This should be done while her DEF buffs from weapons like Dauntless Evernight are active.
5.  **Main Echo Skill:** Use Bell-Borne Geochelone to apply the team-wide damage reduction and DMG boost.
6.  **Outro Skill:** Once the Concerto bar is full, switch to your Main DPS (e.g., Danjin) to pass on the Resonance Skill Deepen buff.

### Advanced Parry and Counter Techniques

To speed up Taoqi's slow Concerto generation, you must master the **Strategic Parry**. Instead of just holding her Resonance Skill, try to time it exactly as an enemy strike lands. A successful parry generates more energy than simply standing in the defensive stance. 

Additionally, you can "dash-cancel" the recovery frames of her third Timed Counter. As soon as the damage numbers appear for the final swing, dash or jump to immediately transition into your Echo skill or Resonance Liberation, saving roughly 0.5 to 1 second of field time.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/taoqi-build-guide-wuthering-waves/inline-6.webp)


### Common Build Questions

**Is Taoqi better than Jianxin for shielding?**
Taoqi and Jianxin serve different purposes. Taoqi offers more raw damage reduction and a specific Skill DMG buff, making her better for bossing. Jianxin provides better Crowd Control (CC) and a generic Resonance Liberation buff, making her superior for clearing waves of smaller enemies.

**Does Taoqi need ATK?**
No. Taoqi’s Resonance Skill, Forte Circuit counters, and Resonance Liberation all scale with her DEF. Building ATK on Taoqi is a significant loss in both damage and survivability.

**What is the best Echo for her?**
For team utility, **Bell-Borne Geochelone** is unmatched. If you are running a triple-Havoc meme team or soloing content, you could use **Dreamless** for high burst damage, but Geochelone remains her most consistent option.

### Gameplay and Mechanic Clarifications

**How do I fix her slow Concerto Energy?**
The best way to fix her energy issues is to equip the **Discord** Broadblade and prioritize **Energy Regen** substats on your Echoes. Aim for at least 140-150% Energy Regen to ensure her ultimate is available and her Concerto bar fills in a single rotation.

**How long does her Outro buff last?**
Taoqi’s Resonance Skill DMG Deepen buff lasts for 14 seconds. However, it will end prematurely if you switch the character who received the buff out for someone else. Ensure your Main DPS stays on the field for the full duration to maximize the value.

**What is "Resolving Caliber"?**
Resolving Caliber is Taoqi's unique resource. You can see the stacks as small pips above her HP bar. These stacks are consumed to perform her "Timed Counters," which are her strongest attacks. You gain stacks through her Intro Skill and by successfully blocking hits with her Resonance Skill.
