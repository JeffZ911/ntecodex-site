### Optical Switches vs. Mechanical Switches
Traditional mechanical switches rely on metal-on-metal contact. Over time, these contacts can oxidize or bend, leading to "chatter" or double-clicking. Optical sensors use a beam of light to detect a keypress, which is not only faster but immune to the physical wear that causes input errors.

### The Importance of Rapid Trigger
In games where a "perfect dodge" triggers a slow-motion window or a powerful counter, the reset point of your keyboard key is just as important as the actuation point. Rapid Trigger technology allows the key to deactivate the moment it moves upward by a fraction of a millimeter, letting you press it again sooner than a traditional keyboard would allow.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I really need an MMO mouse for Neverness to Everness?
While not strictly required, an MMO mouse like the Razer Naga V2 Pro significantly simplifies combat by allowing you to map Resonance Skills and character swaps to your thumb. This keeps your left hand free to focus entirely on movement and dodging, which is crucial during high-difficulty Anomaly encounters.

### Is 1440p better than 4K for gacha gaming?
For most players, 1440p is the "sweet spot" because it allows for higher refresh rates (165Hz+) without requiring an ultra-expensive GPU. In fast-paced games, the motion clarity provided by a higher refresh rate is generally more beneficial than the extra pixel density of 4K.

### How does Rapid Trigger help with dodging?
Rapid Trigger allows a key to reset as soon as it travels upward, rather than waiting for it to pass a fixed reset point. This means you can tap the dodge key multiple times in much quicker succession, which is vital for avoiding multi-hit attacks from Hesperia's supernatural bosses.

### Will a gaming chair actually improve my gameplay?
An ergonomic chair like the Secretlab TITAN Evo won't directly increase your in-game stats, but it reduces the physical fatigue and back pain that can cause your performance to drop after several hours of play. Proper lumbar support helps you maintain focus during long farming loops.
