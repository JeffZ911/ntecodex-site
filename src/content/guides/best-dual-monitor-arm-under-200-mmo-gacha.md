## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/best-dual-monitor-arm-under-200-mmo-gacha/inline-4.webp)


While a dual monitor arm can significantly improve your gaming experience in Neverness to Everness, there are situations where you should avoid these specific models:

*   **If you have a glass desk:** Most C-clamps under $200 exert enough concentrated pressure to shatter tempered glass. Unless you use a <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=reinforcement+plate&tag=jeffzen911-20" rel="sponsored nofollow noopener" target="_blank">reinforcement plate</a>, you risk a catastrophic desk failure.
*   **If your monitors lack VESA holes:** Many budget or ultra-slim monitors require expensive third-party adapters. These adapters often shift the center of gravity, which can compromise the stability of the gas-spring arm.
*   **If you use a desk with a back-panel:** If your desk has a "modesty panel" or a built-in cable tray that prevents a clamp from reaching at least 2 inches inward, these standard C-clamps will not fit.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can these arms hold a curved monitor?
Yes, most dual monitor arms can hold curved monitors as long as they are VESA compatible. However, the curve changes the center of gravity, so you may need an arm with a higher weight rating, such as the AVLT or Amazon Basics Premium, to prevent the screen from tilting downward.

### Do I need a special desk for a dual monitor mount?
You need a desk with a sturdy, flat surface and an edge that allows for a C-clamp or a hole for a grommet mount. Avoid thin particle board or glass desks without reinforcement, as the weight of two monitors concentrated on one point can cause the desk to warp or break.

### Is vertical stacking better than side-by-side?
Vertical stacking is better for reducing horizontal neck strain and saving desk space, making it ideal for monitoring Discord or guides while playing NTE. Side-by-side is better for immersive gaming or tasks that require you to look at both screens equally.

### How do I stop my monitor arm from wobbling?
Wobble is usually caused by either a loose desk clamp or a desk that is too light. Ensure the clamp is tightened fully and that your desk is positioned against a wall for extra stability. Higher-quality arms like the Amazon Basics model also use thicker materials that naturally dampen vibrations.
