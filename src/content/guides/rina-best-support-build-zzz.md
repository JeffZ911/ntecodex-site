## Overview

Rina is an Electric-attribute Support agent who commands two puppets, Drusilla and Anastella, to attack from a distance. She is designed to be played as a "quick-swap" or off-field support. Her gameplay involves deploying her puppets via her Special Attacks or Chain Attacks and then immediately swapping to a damage dealer while the puppets remain on the field to provide buffs.

The effectiveness of Rina is tied directly to her Core Passive, which transfers a portion of her own PEN Ratio to her teammates. This makes her one of the most stat-hungry supports in the game, as her utility scales linearly with her gear investment.

### Role and Rarity

As an S-Rank agent, Rina possesses higher base stats and more potent modifiers than her A-Rank counterparts. Her role is strictly supportive; while she can deal decent Electric DMG and contribute to Anomaly Buildup (Shock), her main contribution is the team-wide PEN Ratio buff. She is currently the only agent in Zenless Zone Zero who provides this specific stat in such large quantities, making her a unique asset for any team struggling against high-DEF bosses.

### Core Skill: Mini Destruction Partner

Rina's Core Skill, "Mini Destruction Partner," is the heart of her kit. When Rina launches an attack that sends out Drusilla and Anastella, the PEN Ratio of all squad members is increased. The buff duration is tied to how long the puppets remain on the field. 

The amount of PEN Ratio granted is calculated as a base percentage plus a portion of Rina's own PEN Ratio. Because there is a hard cap on how much PEN Ratio she can share (30%), players must balance her stats to reach this ceiling without over-investing. If the puppets return to Rina's side, the buff ends immediately, which is why managing puppet uptime is the most critical aspect of her high-level play.

## Rotation

Using Rina effectively requires understanding the "Quick Assist" mechanic. You should never spend more than a few seconds with Rina on the field.

### The Quick Assist Loop

The standard combat loop for Rina follows these steps:
1.  **Engage with Stunner:** Use your Stun agent (like Lycaon or Anby) to build up the enemy's Daze meter.
2.  **Rina's Entry:** Once you have enough Energy, swap to Rina and use her **EX Special Attack: Diminuendo**. This sends Drusilla and Anastella out to perform a multi-hit circular attack.
3.  **Quick Assist:** Immediately after the EX Special, a "Quick Assist" prompt will often appear (if the move triggers it) or you can manually swap. The puppets will stay on the field for several seconds even after Rina leaves.
4.  **DPS Window:** Your main DPS enters the field while the puppets are still spinning. The PEN Ratio buff is active during this entire window.

### Chain Attack Order

During a Chain Attack (triggered by hitting a Dazed enemy with a heavy attack):
*   Always place Rina **before** your primary DPS. 
*   The ideal sequence is: **Stunner -> Rina -> DPS**.
*   This ensures that the DPS agent benefits from the PEN Ratio buff for their entire duration on the field, including their Ultimate if it is available.

### Puppet Uptime Management

To track your buffs, look at the character icons near the health bar or watch the physical models of Drusilla and Anastella. If the puppets are floating near Rina while she is off-field, the buff is inactive. If they are hovering near the enemy or in the middle of the arena, the buff is active. At Mindscape Cinema 1 (M1), this management becomes significantly easier as the puppets stay out for an additional 5.5 seconds.

## FAQ

### Is Rina better than Soukaku?

Rina and Soukaku serve different purposes. Soukaku provides a massive flat ATK buff and is the premier support for Ice teams. Rina provides PEN Ratio and is more universal. In an Ellen Joe team, Rina is often preferred for speedrunning and high-DEF bosses, whereas Soukaku is better for general play and higher raw ATK scaling.

### What is the PEN Ratio cap?

Rina's Core Passive caps the shared PEN Ratio at 30%. To reach this cap, Rina needs approximately 72% total PEN Ratio (including her base stats and gear). Any PEN Ratio Rina gains beyond 72% will still increase her personal damage but will not provide additional benefits to her teammates.

### Best Bangboo for Rina?

The **Butler** Bangboo is widely considered the best for Rina, especially in Victoria Housekeeping teams. It provides vital Energy Regeneration, which helps Rina maintain her EX Special Attack uptime. For Mono-Electric teams, **Plugboo** is a strong alternative for faster Shock procs.

### Does PEN Ratio stack with flat PEN?

Yes. In Zenless Zone Zero, the damage formula applies PEN Ratio first (reducing the enemy's DEF by a percentage) and then subtracts flat PEN from the remaining value. This makes PEN Ratio exceptionally powerful against bosses with massive DEF pools, while flat PEN is more effective against smaller, "trash" mobs.

### Is Rina's M1 (Mindscape Cinema 1) worth it?

Rina's M1 is one of the most impactful early Mindscapes in the game. It increases the duration the puppets stay on the field by 5.5 seconds and increases the buff conversion rate. This effectively removes the "uptime" struggle, allowing her PEN buff to remain active almost permanently during a standard rotation.
