## Overview

Huohuo is a 5-star Wind character following the Path of Abundance, but she functions very differently from traditional healers like Natasha or Luocha. While most Abundance units focus purely on survival, Huohuo acts as a hybrid Support, bridging the gap between healing and the offensive utility typically found in the Path of Harmony. Her kit revolves around her "Divine Provision" effect, which provides passive healing and cleansing, alongside an Ultimate that recharges her allies' Energy.

### Huohuo's Role in the Meta

In the current meta, Huohuo is often considered the best-in-slot sustain for teams that rely on high Ultimate uptime or Attack (ATK) scaling. Because her Ultimate provides a percentage-based Energy restoration (rather than a flat value), she scales exceptionally well with characters who have high Energy costs, such as Argenti or Yunli. Furthermore, her ability to cleanse debuffs every time an ally takes a turn or uses an Ultimate makes her the ultimate counter to "debuff hell" scenarios, such as those involving the Kafka boss or the Aurumaton Gatekeeper.

### Pros and Cons of Huohuo

**Pros:**
*   **Massive Offensive Utility:** Provides a team-wide ATK buff and Energy restoration.
*   **High-Frequency Cleansing:** Her Talent can cleanse up to 6 debuffs (or more with Eidolons) per Skill use, triggered by ally turns or Ultimates.
*   **Passive Healing:** Once her Skill is active, she heals allies without needing to spend more Skill Points (SP) immediately.
*   **Wind Typing:** Useful for contributing to Toughness depletion against Wind-weak enemies.

**Cons:**
*   **High Energy Cost:** Her Ultimate requires 140 Energy, making Energy Regeneration Rate (ERR) mandatory.
*   **SP Intensive:** Unlike Luocha, she generally needs to use her Skill every two or three turns to maintain her Talent uptime.
*   **No Emergency Ultimate Heal:** Her Ultimate does not heal; if your team is at critical HP, you must wait for her turn or an ally's turn/Ultimate to trigger passive healing.

## Best Weapons

Selecting the right Light Cone for Huohuo is primarily about solving her 140 Energy cost requirement. While her signature weapon is undeniably powerful, several 4-star options provide comparable utility for specific team archetypes.

### Top 5-Star Light Cones

**Night of Fright (Signature)**
This is Huohuo's best-in-slot weapon. It provides a 12% Energy Regeneration Rate, which is vital for her Ultimate rotation. Additionally, when an ally uses an Ultimate, the wearer heals the ally with the lowest HP percentage by 10% of their Max HP. It also provides a stacking ATK buff (up to 5 stacks, 2.4% each at S1) to the ally being healed. This weapon turns Huohuo into a triple-threat: a healer, an energy battery, and a stacking ATK buffer.

**Time Waits for No One**
Bailu's signature weapon is a solid stat-stick for Huohuo. It increases Max HP and Outgoing Healing. It also records the amount of healing processed and deals a portion of it as additional damage when an ally attacks. While it lacks Energy Regeneration, the sheer HP boost makes Huohuo incredibly tanky.

### Best 4-Star and F2P Alternatives

**Post-Op Conversation (S5 Recommended)**
At Superimposition 5, this Light Cone provides an 8% to 12% Energy Regeneration Rate and increases Outgoing Healing when using an Ultimate. For Huohuo, the ERR is the main draw, as it allows for more consistent 3-turn or 4-turn Ultimate rotations. It is often considered her best 4-star option.

**Quid Pro Quo (S5)**
This is the premier F2P option, obtainable through the Forgotten Hall's Light Cone Store. At S5, it regenerates 16 Energy for a random ally (excluding the wearer) whose Energy is currently below 50% at the start of Huohuo's turn. In teams with high-cost Ultimates, this provides an incredible amount of extra Energy, though it does not help Huohuo charge her own Ultimate.

**Shared Feeling**
This increases Outgoing Healing and provides a flat Energy boost (2 to 4 Energy) to all allies whenever Huohuo uses her Skill. This is excellent for SP-neutral playstyles where Huohuo uses her Skill frequently.

**Hey, Over Here**
An event-exclusive Light Cone from the "A Foxian Tale of the Haunted" event. It increases Max HP and Outgoing Healing for 2 turns after using a Skill. It is a reliable F2P stat-booster if you missed other options.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

Huohuo’s relic selection should focus on three goals: reaching Speed (SPD) breakpoints, maximizing HP for healing scaling, and ensuring she has enough Energy Regeneration.

### Recommended Relic Sets

**4pc Messenger Traversing Hackerspace**
This is the standard set for Huohuo. The 2-piece effect increases her SPD, while the 4-piece effect increases the SPD of all allies for 1 turn whenever Huohuo uses her Ultimate on them. Since Huohuo's Ultimate targets the whole team, she triggers this buff reliably, helping her team cycle turns faster.

**2pc Messenger Traversing Hackerspace + 2pc Passerby of Wandering Cloud**
A highly efficient "rainbow" build. This provides a 6% SPD boost and a 10% Outgoing Healing boost. This is often easier to farm with good substats than a full 4-piece set and offers a more balanced approach to healing and speed.

**2pc Longevous Disciple + 2pc Passerby of Wandering Cloud**
This combination maximizes Huohuo's raw healing output and survivability by providing 12% HP and 10% Outgoing Healing. This is recommended if you find your Huohuo is struggling to stay alive or if your teammates are taking massive damage in content like Gold and Gears.

### Best Planar Ornaments

**Fleet of the Ageless**
The gold standard for Huohuo. It increases her HP by 12% and grants the entire team an 8% ATK buff once Huohuo reaches 120 SPD. Since Huohuo already provides ATK buffs, this set stacks perfectly to further empower your DPS.

**Broken Keel**
An excellent choice for teams that rely on Critical Damage (Crit DMG). It grants 10% Effect RES to Huohuo and provides a 10% Crit DMG buff to the whole team once she hits 30% Effect RES. Huohuo has Effect RES in her Traces, making this threshold very easy to reach.

**Lushaka, the Sunken Seas**
This newer set from the Divergent Universe provides a 5% Energy Regeneration Rate to the wearer. If the wearer is not the first character in the team lineup, it increases the ATK of the first character in the team by 12%. This is a niche but powerful choice for Hypercarry teams where you want to funnel every possible buff into a single DPS like Jingliu or Dan Heng - Imbibitor Lunae.

## Team Comp

Huohuo shines in compositions where her ATK buffs and Energy restoration are fully utilized. She is less effective in teams that do not care about ATK (such as Blade or HP-scaling units) or teams that are extremely SP-starved.

### The DoT Core: Kafka & Black Swan

Huohuo is the undisputed best sustain for Damage over Time (DoT) teams. DoT damage scales heavily off ATK, and characters like Kafka and Black Swan rely on their Ultimates to apply or detonate DoT stacks. Huohuo provides the ATK they crave and the Energy they need to maintain their rotation.
*   **Premium DoT Team:** Kafka, Black Swan, Ruan Mei, Huohuo.
*   **Alternative DoT:** Kafka, Sampo/Guinaifen, Asta, Huohuo.

### The Ultimate Battery: Argenti & Yunli

Characters with high-cost, high-impact Ultimates benefit the most from Huohuo. Argenti, who has a massive 180 Energy cap for his enhanced Ultimate, and Yunli, who relies on her Ultimate for counters, both find Huohuo indispensable.
*   **Physical Hypercarry:** Argenti/Yunli, Tingyun, Sparkle/Hanya, Huohuo.
*   **The Energy Engine:** By pairing Huohuo with Tingyun, you create a "battery" that can fill a DPS's Energy bar from zero to full in a very short window.

### F2P-Friendly Team Options

Huohuo can sustain almost any traditional Hypercarry team. Even F2P units like Dan Heng or Serval benefit from her cleansing and buffs.
*   **F2P Hypercarry:** Dan Heng, Tingyun, Asta, Huohuo.
*   **Dual DPS:** Dr. Ratio, Moze, Robin, Huohuo. (Note: Robin and Huohuo together provide an immense amount of team-wide ATK).

## Rotation

Managing Huohuo's "Divine Provision" stacks is the key to her effectiveness. Divine Provision is the buff Huohuo gains after using her Skill, lasting for 2 of her turns.

### Managing Divine Provision Stacks

When Divine Provision is active, an ally will be healed when their turn starts or when they use an Ultimate. This also triggers a cleanse.
*   **Standard SP-Neutral Rotation (Skill -> Basic):** This ensures 100% uptime on Divine Provision. Use the Skill on turn 1, Basic on turn 2, and Skill again on turn 3. This is the safest way to play Huohuo in high-difficulty content.
*   **SP-Positive Rotation (Skill -> Basic -> Basic):** Use this when your team is healthy and you need Skill Points for your DPS. This results in 1 turn where Divine Provision is inactive. Only do this if you are confident the enemy will not land a CC debuff or massive damage during that "gap" turn.

### Optimizing Ultimate Usage for Energy

Huohuo’s Ultimate restores Energy based on the target's Max Energy (15% to 20%). To maximize this:
1.  **Wait for the DPS to use their Ultimate first:** If your DPS has a full Energy bar, Huohuo's Ultimate will be wasted on them. Always use the DPS Ultimate, then follow up with Huohuo's Ultimate to immediately jump-start their next cycle.
2.  **The "Emergency" Cleanse:** While her Ultimate doesn't heal, it does trigger her Talent's passive healing if Divine Provision is active. If an ally uses an Ultimate while Divine Provision is up, they get a small heal and a cleanse. Use this to clear debuffs out of turn.

**Energy Regeneration Rotation Chart (with ERR Rope + S5 Post-Op):**
*   **3-Turn Ult:** Requires being hit or triggering specific Light Cone effects (like Shared Feeling) alongside a Skill-heavy rotation.
*   **4-Turn Ult:** Easily achievable with a Skill -> Basic -> Basic -> Basic rotation or a more standard SP-neutral mix.

## FAQ

### Is Huohuo better than Luocha or Aventurine?

Huohuo is generally better for offensive pressure and cleansing. Luocha is superior for SP generation (he is almost entirely SP positive) and "emergency" auto-healing. Aventurine is the king of shields and prevents damage entirely, making him better for teams that can't afford to take HP damage (like those using Yanqing). Choose Huohuo if your team needs Energy and ATK to finish the fight faster.

### How much Speed does Huohuo need?

At a minimum, aim for 134 SPD. However, because Huohuo needs to take turns to generate SP and refresh her Talent, higher SPD is always better. 143 is a very comfortable middle ground, while 160 is recommended for players looking to 0-cycle or 1-cycle Memory of Chaos stages.

### Is her E1 worth pulling?

Huohuo's E1 (Anchor of Spirit and Spear of Demon) is one of the best first eidolons in the game. It increases the duration of Divine Provision by 1 turn and increases all allies' SPD by 12%. This makes her much more SP-efficient (allowing for a Skill -> Basic -> Basic -> Basic rotation with 100% uptime) and provides a permanent SPD buff to the team.

### Can she solo sustain in high-difficulty content?

Yes. While she doesn't have a "panic button" heal on her Ultimate, the combination of her high Skill heal and the constant "ticking" heals from her Talent is more than enough to keep a team alive in MoC 12 or Apocalyptic Shadow. The key is ensuring she has enough HP (at least 6,000+) and an Outgoing Healing body piece.

**Eidolon Priority List:**
1.  **E1:** Massive SP efficiency and team SPD.
2.  **E2:** Provides a "revive" mechanic (up to 2 times per battle), which is a huge safety net.
3.  **E6:** Increases DMG dealt by allies receiving healing by 50% for 2 turns—essentially turning her into a top-tier Harmony unit.
