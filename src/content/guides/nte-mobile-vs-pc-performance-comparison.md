# Best Gaming Gear for Neverness to Everland Players Balancing Mobile and PC Performance

For the best NTE experience, the LG 34GP83A-B offers the superior field-of-view PC players crave, while the SteelSeries Arctis Nova 7 is the top choice for its seamless PC-to-mobile wireless switching.

Neverness to Everness (NTE) represents a significant leap in the urban open-world genre, utilizing Unreal Engine 5 to deliver a dense, supernatural depiction of the city of Hethereau. For players performing 4+ hour daily sessions, the hardware requirements are demanding. This guide evaluates the best peripherals and gear to bridge the gap between a high-end PC experience and the portability of mobile play, ensuring that whether you are customizing vehicles or engaging in fast-paced Esper combat, your performance remains consistent.

## Comparison summary

The performance gap between PC and mobile in Neverness to Everness is most evident in visual density and input precision. PC setups dominate in environmental detail, thanks to the ability to run Unreal Engine 5 at higher resolutions and frame rates. Using an ultrawide monitor and a multi-button MMO mouse provides a level of control and situational awareness that is simply not possible on a handheld device.

However, mobile setups offer unmatched portability for time-sensitive tasks like the daily commissions or limited-time farming events. The middle ground is found in "hybrid" gear. Products like the SteelSeries Arctis Nova 7 and the ASUS ZenScreen allow mobile players to borrow the strengths of a PC setup—better audio and larger screens—without sacrificing the ability to play anywhere.

## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/nte-mobile-vs-pc-performance-comparison/inline-4.webp)


Not every player needs to rush out and buy the latest hardware. If your current PC setup already includes an RTX 30-series or 40-series GPU and a 1440p monitor, the jump to a slightly higher refresh rate or a slightly wider screen will offer diminishing returns. The visual improvements of UE5 are best realized at 1440p; moving to 4K or 240Hz provides a marginal benefit that may not justify the cost for a supernatural RPG.

Similarly, do not invest in a high-end gaming chair if your primary playstyle involves sitting on a couch or bed. In those cases, the ergonomic benefits of a TITAN Evo are lost. Instead, a quality tablet stand or a lap desk would be a more effective use of your budget. Finally, if your mobile device is more than three years old, an external monitor or high-end headset won't fix the underlying frame rate drops. In that scenario, your funds are better spent upgrading the phone or tablet itself to a device with a modern Snapdragon or Apple Silicon chip.

## What we didn't include and why

![What we didn't include and why](/img/nte-mobile-vs-pc-performance-comparison/inline-5.webp)


In our research, several popular products were excluded because they do not align with the specific needs of an NTE player. We omitted the DXRacer Racing Series and similar "bucket-style" gaming chairs. While they look the part, the aggressive side wings are designed to hold a driver in place during high-G turns—something that doesn't happen at a desk. These wings often push the shoulders forward, leading to poor posture during the relaxed, long-duration grinds common in open-world RPGs.

We also excluded the Logitech G Pro X Superlight 2. While it is arguably the best mouse for competitive shooters, it lacks the side buttons necessary for efficient MMO/JRPG hotkey mapping. In NTE, you frequently need to access Esper skills, swap characters, and trigger vehicle gadgets. A two-button mouse forces too much reliance on the keyboard, which can be less efficient than a dedicated thumb grid. Lastly, we avoided generic $50 gaming headsets. These budget options typically use low-quality pleather ear pads that cause significant heat buildup and discomfort during sessions exceeding two hours.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does Neverness to Everness support cross-save between PC and mobile?
Yes, Neverness to Everness is designed as a multi-platform title with full cross-save support. This allows you to progress through the story of Hethereau on your PC and continue exactly where you left off on your mobile device using the same account.

### Can I use a controller for NTE on mobile?
Official support for controllers on the mobile version of NTE has been a priority for Hotta Studio. Most standard Bluetooth controllers, such as the Xbox Wireless Controller or DualSense, are compatible, providing a more tactile experience than touch controls.

### What are the minimum PC requirements for Unreal Engine 5 in NTE?
To run NTE effectively on PC, a modern quad-core processor and at least an NVIDIA GTX 1660 or equivalent are recommended for 1080p play. For those wanting to experience Lumen lighting and high-resolution textures, an RTX 3060 or higher is suggested.

### Is there a significant graphical difference between the PC and mobile versions?
While the core gameplay remains the same, the PC version offers superior visual fidelity including better draw distances, higher resolution textures, and more advanced lighting effects. Mobile versions are optimized for performance and battery life, which often results in lower shadow quality and simplified reflections.
