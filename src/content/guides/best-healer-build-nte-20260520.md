# Best Healer Build Guide for Neverness to Everness (NTE)

## Overview

![Overview](/img/best-healer-build-nte-20260520/inline-1.webp)


In Neverness to Everness (NTE), healers are categorized as "Sustain" or "Survival" Espers. Their primary function is to manage the team's HP pool and provide defensive utility to prevent one-shots during high-intensity combat. Unlike traditional MMOs where healers stay on the field, NTE healers rely on the "Esper Cycle" mechanic. This system encourages quick-swapping, where a healer enters the field just long enough to deploy their skills before rotating back to the main damage dealer.

Currently, the healer roster is divided between dedicated medics and hybrid sustain units. Edgar (Cosmos, A-rank) serves as the game’s primary dedicated healer, offering straightforward HP-based restoration. Fadia (Psyche, S-rank) functions as a high-tier sustain tank, utilizing damage redirection and self-healing to keep the team upright. Jiuyuan (Anima, S-rank) provides role compression by offering ranged DPS alongside consistent healing through her unique marking mechanics.

### How Healing Scales in NTE

Healing efficiency in NTE is typically tied to one of two factors: the healer's Max HP or the target ally's Max HP. For Edgar, his "Wild Current" skill restores health based on his own Max HP, making HP% stats the highest priority for his build. Fadia also scales heavily with HP, as her damage redirection threshold is calculated based on her total health pool. 

Understanding the "Esper Cycle" is critical for these units. Healers often possess "Redirect Skills" that allow them to heal the active character even after they have been swapped out. Mastering the timing of these swaps ensures your DPS characters maintain their momentum without risking a wipe during "Nova" or "Scorch" elemental states.

## Best Weapons

![Best Weapons](/img/best-healer-build-nte-20260520/inline-2.webp)


Weapons in Neverness to Everness are known as "Arcs." For healers, Arcs are not just stat sticks; they provide essential passives like Healing Bonus, Energy Recharge, and Max HP boosts. Selecting the right Arc can be the difference between a heal that barely moves the needle and one that fully restores a critical teammate.

### S-Tier: The Best Signature Arcs

**Eternal Waltz (S-Rank)**
This is the premium choice for healers and high-HP sustain units like Fadia. It provides a base Max HP boost of 20%, which can scale up to 32% at max refinement. Additionally, it increases Mental DMG by 10% for 10 seconds after the wearer casts an Ultimate. While the damage boost is secondary for pure healers, the massive HP increase is unmatched for scaling Edgar and Fadia’s recovery output.

**Day Off (S-Rank)**
A versatile option often found through high-level Anomaly challenges, "Day Off" provides a significant 30% ATK boost. More importantly, its "Eclipse" effect restores 6 to 10 Ultimate Energy for every enemy defeated. This makes it an excellent choice for healers who rely on their Ultimates, such as Jiuyuan, ensuring they have their healing domains ready for every encounter.

### A-Tier & F2P Alternatives

**Call of the Twisted City (A-Rank)**
This is arguably the best accessible option for F2P players. It focuses entirely on "Healing Efficiency," providing a boost whenever the wearer uses a Redirect Skill. Since Edgar’s entire kit revolves around holding his Redirect Skill to cast "Wild Current," this Arc synergizes perfectly with his gameplay loop.

**Hethereau's Keeper (S-Rank)**
While classified as a "Solid" Arc, this weapon is often used on hybrid healers like Jiuyuan for off-field utility. It summons "Officer Whisker" to assist in combat, providing extra pressure while the healer is tagged out. It is best suited for players who want their sustain units to contribute to the team's overall Break damage.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

In NTE, your equipment sets are called "Cartridges" (sometimes referred to as Disks). These are slotted into your character's Console and powered by Modules. To maximize a healer's effectiveness, you must match the Module types to the Cartridge requirements to activate set bonuses.

### Top Cartridge Sets for Sustain

**Tiny Big Adventure**
This set is the gold standard for HP-scaling healers. 
*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Increases HP by 10%.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** Increases the wearer's Max HP by 4% whenever they take damage, stacking up to 10 times (40% total). Casting an Ultimate instantly grants all 10 stacks.
This is particularly powerful on Fadia, who naturally takes damage through her redirection mechanic, and Edgar, whose healing output scales directly with these HP stacks.

**Thea's Night Tavern**
For players focusing on pure healing throughput rather than raw HP, this set is the superior choice.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** Increases the wearer's Healing Bonus by a flat 20%.
This set is ideal for Edgar when paired with modules that already provide high flat HP, as the percentage-based healing bonus acts as a final multiplier for his "Wild Current" and "Finnegan's Wake."

### Substat Priority and Optimization

When farming Modules for your healer's Console, prioritize the following stats
1.  **HP%:** The most important stat for Edgar and Fadia.
2.  **Energy Recharge:** Essential for keeping Ultimates like "Finnegan's Wake" or "Final Reckoning" available.
3.  **Healing Bonus%:** Usually found as a main stat on Type III or IV Modules.
4.  **Flat HP / DEF%:** General survivability to ensure the healer doesn't get caught out during a swap.

## Team Comp

![Team Comp](/img/best-healer-build-nte-20260520/inline-4.webp)


Healers in NTE act as "Flex Picks." Because they don't usually interfere with elemental reactions like "Blossom" (Anima + Cosmos) or "Scorch" (Incantation + Chaos), they can be slotted into almost any team that needs a safety net.

### Best F2P Healer Team
*   **Main DPS:** Zero (Cosmos)
*   **Healer:** Edgar (Cosmos)
*   **Buffer/Support:** Haniel (Psyche)
This team utilizes the "Charged" reaction between Cosmos and Psyche to generate massive amounts of Ultimate Energy. Edgar keeps Zero healthy, while Haniel provides ATK buffs that scale Zero's damage.

### Meta Endgame Compositions

**Anima Blossom Team**
*   **Main DPS:** [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) (Physical/Anima)
*   **Hybrid Healer:** Jiuyuan (Anima)
*   **Support:** Haniel or Hathor
This team focuses on the "Blossom" reaction. Jiuyuan provides crowd control and healing through her "Lethal Rose Pact," allowing Nanally to stay aggressive. Jiuyuan’s healing is particularly effective here because it happens passively as the team deals damage to marked targets.

**Chaos/Psyche Nova Team**
*   **Main DPS:** Baicang (Incantation/Chaos)
*   **Sustain:** Fadia (Psyche)
*   **Break Support:** Daffodil (Chaos)
Fadia is the star here. Her damage redirection protects Baicang, who often needs to stay on-field for long durations to complete his "Power Word" rotations. The interaction between Psyche and Chaos triggers "Discord," which significantly accelerates the enemy's Break Gauge depletion.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/best-healer-build-nte-20260520/inline-5.webp)


The key to effective healing in NTE is minimizing the healer's field time. You want to execute a "Quick-Swap" to trigger their healing abilities and then immediately return to your DPS to continue the elemental cycle.

### The Quick-Swap Healing Method

**Edgar Rotation:**
1.  Wait for an ally’s HP to drop below 60%.
2.  **Tag in Edgar.**
3.  **Hold Skill (Wild Current):** Use this for 3-5 seconds to rapidly top off the lowest HP ally. Note that the cooldown increases the longer you hold it.
4.  **Cast Ultimate (Finnegan's Wake):** If the whole team is damaged, deploy this domain.
5.  **Tag out immediately** to your Main DPS. The domain will continue to heal the active character.

**Fadia Rotation:**
1.  **Start the fight with Fadia's Skill (Existence):** This activates her damage redirection and applies "Destructive Experience" to enemies.
2.  **Swap to DPS:** Fadia will now absorb 50% of the damage your DPS takes while off-field.
3.  **Tag Fadia back in** when her HP is low or her Ultimate is charged.
4.  **Cast Ultimate (Agony to Euphoria):** This enters her "Lilith state," which fully restores her HP and boosts her damage.
5.  **Execute follow-up attacks** then swap back to maintain pressure.

### Managing the Esper Cycle with Healers

Always save your healer’s Ultimate for "Stagger Windows." When a boss’s Break Gauge is depleted, they enter a vulnerable state. Using a healer's Ultimate during this window ensures your team is at full health before the boss recovers and enters its next "Nova" or "Scorch" phase. If you are using Jiuyuan, ensure you apply "Lethal Rose Pact" marks with her skill before using her Ultimate to maximize the "Pact Settlement" healing.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/best-healer-build-nte-20260520/inline-6.webp)


**Does Edgar need ATK for healing?**
No. Edgar’s healing through "Wild Current" and "Finnegan's Wake" scales based on his Max HP. Building ATK on Edgar is generally considered a waste of resources unless you are attempting a niche "Speedy Hedgehog" DPS build.

**Is Fadia better than Edgar?**
In the endgame, Fadia is generally considered superior because she provides damage redirection and contributes to "Discord" reactions. However, Edgar is the better "pure" healer for early-game progression and Anomaly Hunts where you simply need raw HP recovery without complex mechanics.

**Where do I farm Arcs?**
S-rank Arcs like "Day Off" and "Eternal Waltz" can be obtained through high-level Anomaly Zones, Field Bosses, and specific gacha banners. "Eternal Waltz" is often featured on Fadia's signature banner, "The Ichi-daime."

**Can I play without a healer?**
It is possible if you utilize high-skill mechanics like "Perfect Dodge" and "Critical Riposte." Additionally, characters like Adler provide powerful shields that can replace the need for a healer in shorter fights. However, for "Beyond the Rails" mode and weekly world bosses, at least one sustain unit is highly recommended.

**How do I get Jiuyuan to heal?**
Jiuyuan's base kit focuses on damage and marks. To unlock her team-wide healing, you typically need her first Awakening (A1), which restores HP to the team based on the damage accumulated by her "Lethal Rose Pact" whenever "Pact Settlement" is triggered.
