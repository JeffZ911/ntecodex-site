## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/zzz-3-0-velina-vs-jane-doe-gear-comparison/inline-4.webp)


Not every player needs to drop thousands on a new setup. In fact, some upgrades might be counterproductive depending on your current hardware.

*   **Existing Office Gear:** If you currently own a high-end ergonomic office chair from brands like Steelcase or Haworth, a "gaming" chair will likely be a downgrade in build quality. These chairs are designed for 40-hour work weeks and offer superior ergonomics to most gaming-branded seats.
*   **GPU Bottlenecks:** Don't upgrade your monitor if your GPU cannot consistently push 1440p at over 100 FPS. In a fast-moving game like NTE, the visual stutter caused by low frame rates will negate the benefit of a higher refresh rate monitor.
*   **Controller Players:** Avoid buying "MMO mice" if you primarily play with a controller. The extra side buttons will go unused and add unnecessary weight, which can lead to faster wrist fatigue in the few moments you do use the mouse.
