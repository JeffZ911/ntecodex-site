## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/norma-stun-efficiency-comparison-zzz-3-0-gear-guide/inline-4.webp)


While high-end gear offers clear benefits, it is not always necessary for every player. If you primarily play on mobile with a controller, a high-end mechanical keyboard like the Huntsman V3 Pro will offer zero efficiency gains for your ZZZ 3.0 sessions. The game's mobile interface is optimized for touch or gamepad, and the "Rapid Trigger" benefits do not translate through most mobile controller bridges.

Furthermore, do not buy the LG 27GP850-B if you already own a 1440p IPS monitor with a refresh rate above 144Hz. The jump from 144Hz to the 180Hz overclocked peak of the LG is rarely noticeable in gacha combat, which is often capped at 60 or 120 FPS by the game engine itself. The visual fidelity of Neverness to Everness is stunning, but a standard high-quality IPS panel will likely suffice unless you are experiencing significant ghosting.

Finally, avoid the Secretlab TITAN if you are under 5'2". Even the "Small" variant has a seat depth that may cause circulation issues for very petite users because their feet may not reach the floor comfortably, even at the lowest setting. In such cases, an ergonomic office chair with a shorter seat pan is a better investment for long-session health.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does "Rapid Trigger" actually help in Zenless Zone Zero 3.0?
Yes, Rapid Trigger allows for faster consecutive inputs by resetting the key the moment you lift your finger. This is particularly effective for characters like Norma or Nanally who rely on rapid skill cancels and frame-perfect dodges to maintain their stun efficiency.

### Is an MMO mouse with 12 buttons too heavy for action games?
While the <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Razer+Naga+V2+Pro&tag=jeffzen911-20" rel="sponsored nofollow noopener" target="_blank">Razer Naga V2 Pro</a> is heavier than a standard competitive mouse, the trade-off is worth it for efficiency. The ability to map your entire skill bar to your thumb reduces the strain on your left hand and keeps your movement keys (WASD) free for dodging and positioning.

### Why is 1440p 165Hz considered the "sweet spot" for gacha grinding?
At 1440p, UI elements like stun bars and health meters are much sharper than at 1080p, making them easier to track in peripheral vision. The 165Hz refresh rate ensures that fast-moving combat in Hethereau remains fluid, which reduces eye strain during long farming sessions.

### Can I use the Secretlab TITAN Evo for office work too?
The TITAN Evo is designed for long-term support, making it excellent for both gaming and office work. However, its firm [cold-cure foam](https://secretlab.co/products/titan-evo-2022-series) is meant for posture correction rather than "couch-like" comfort, so it may feel stiff if you prefer a very soft seat.
