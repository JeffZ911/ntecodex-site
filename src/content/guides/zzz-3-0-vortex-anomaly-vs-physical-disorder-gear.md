## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/zzz-3-0-vortex-anomaly-vs-physical-disorder-gear/inline-4.webp)


While high-end gear offers clear advantages, it isn't always necessary for every player.

*   **Existing High-Refresh Monitors:** If you already own a 144Hz IPS monitor, the jump to 240Hz OLED is visually stunning but won't fundamentally change your combat performance. The diminishing returns after 144Hz are real for all but the most competitive players.
*   **Controller Players:** Don't buy a high-button-count mouse if you primarily use a controller. The ergonomic and utility benefits of the Razer Naga are lost if you aren't using the side plates for your primary inputs.
*   **Functional Ergonomics:** If your current chair has a functional lumbar pillow and you don't experience back pain after 4 hours of play, a $500 ergonomic upgrade is likely unnecessary. Focus on your desk height and monitor positioning first.
