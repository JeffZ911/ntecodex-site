## Overview

![Overview](/img/nte-character-ascension-materials-farming-guide-20260523/inline-1.webp)


Nanally’s kit focuses on high-frequency Anima hits and summoning the "Underboss" for coordinated follow-up attacks. Unlike traditional burst-heavy characters, Nanally relies on sustained aggression, where her basic attack strings trigger additional hits from her summoned entity. This synergy makes her particularly effective at shredding enemy shields and maintaining constant elemental pressure.

Character progression in Neverness to Everness requires three main pillars: Hunter Guides (EXP), Beetle Coins (Currency), and Ascension Materials (Boss/World drops). To break level caps, players must reach Hunter Rank 10 and farm specific Anomaly Pilgrimages. These pilgrimages are localized combat challenges that consume Character Pixels (stamina) and reward the unique materials needed to push a character toward the current level 80 cap.

### Nanally's Role in the 1.0 Meta

In the current version of the game, Nanally occupies the "Hyper-Carry" slot. Her Anima element is essential for triggering the Blossom and Hexed reactions, which are currently the most efficient ways to clear timed content like the Peril Hunts. Because her "Underboss" summon scales directly with her own offensive stats, investing in her ascension is a priority for any account that pulled her from "The Ichi-daime" banner.

## Best Weapons

![Best Weapons](/img/nte-character-ascension-materials-farming-guide-20260523/inline-2.webp)


The weapons in Neverness to Everness are known as Arcs. These items provide both raw stats and unique passive abilities that can drastically alter a character's playstyle. For Nanally, the priority is finding an Arc that rewards her high hit count and maximizes the damage of her Underboss.

### Top-Tier S-Rank Arcs

**Ready-Ready (S-Rank)**
This is Nanally's signature Arc and is widely considered her Best-in-Slot (BiS) option. Ready-Ready provides a massive ATK% buff and features a unique synergy for off-field follow-up damage. When Nanally is on the field, every third hit triggers a resonance that increases the Underboss's damage by [exact value pending official confirmation]. This makes it the most consistent damage source for her kit.

**Fluff of Fleetness (S-Rank)**
A powerful alternative to her signature weapon, Fluff of Fleetness grants stacking ATK% bonuses while the character remains on-field. Since Nanally rarely swaps out during her burst window, she can easily maintain the maximum stacks of this Arc. While it lacks the specific Underboss synergy of Ready-Ready, the raw stat increase is high enough to make it a top contender for pure DPS.

### Best F2P and Budget Options

**Oraora! (A-Rank)**
For players who did not pull on the weapon banner, Oraora! is the best F2P-friendly option. It focuses on boosting Basic Attack damage, which is perfect for Nanally’s long combo strings. At maximum refinement, the damage bonus from Oraora! can rival unrefined S-Rank Arcs in specific scenarios.

**Clear Skies (A-Rank)**
Clear Skies serves as a solid early-game bridge. It provides Anima DMG bonuses and helps with Ultimate energy regeneration. While it falls off in the late game compared to Oraora!, it is an excellent choice for players struggling to keep Nanally’s Ultimate uptime at 100%.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/nte-character-ascension-materials-farming-guide-20260523/inline-3.webp)


Disks are the primary way to customize Nanally’s stats. They are divided into three slots: Cartridge, Console, and Module. Each set provides a 2-piece and 4-piece bonus, and for an Anima DPS like Nanally, the choice of set can change her damage output by over 30%.

### Recommended Cartridge Sets

**Fireflies and the Forest (4-Piece)**
This is the definitive BiS set for Anima characters. The 2-piece bonus provides a flat Anima DMG increase, but the 4-piece bonus is where the power lies. It allows the wearer to stack Crit DMG up to 56% through multi-hits. Given that Nanally hits dozens of times within a single combo string, she reaches the maximum stack count almost instantly.

**Street Boxer (4-Piece)**
Street Boxer is a strong alternative for players who are focusing on a raw Basic Attack or physical-hybrid scaling build. It provides a flat physical damage boost and increases ATK every time the character takes or deals damage. While it doesn't scale as well as Fireflies and the Forest in the endgame, it is easier to farm in the early stages of Hethereau.

### Console and Module Stat Priorities

When farming Disks, you must look for specific main stats on each piece:
*   **Cartridge:** Anima DMG% (This is non-negotiable for her damage scaling).
*   **Console:** Crit Rate or Crit DMG (Use Crit Rate until you hit 60%, then swap to Crit DMG).
*   **Module:** ATK% (Provides the baseline for her summon's damage).

For substats, prioritize Crit Rate until you reach the 60% comfort zone. Once that is achieved, dump all remaining resources into Crit DMG and Universal DMG%. Avoid flat HP or DEF substats, as Nanally relies on her dodge-cancel and healers like Jiuyuan for survivability.

## Team Comp

![Team Comp](/img/nte-character-ascension-materials-farming-guide-20260523/inline-4.webp)


Nanally shines brightest when paired with characters who can trigger powerful elemental reactions. In Neverness to Everness, the interaction between Anima and other elements creates secondary effects that are vital for clearing high-level content.

### The 'Hyper Nanally' Meta Team

The most effective team for Nanally consists of Nanally (Main DPS), Jiuyuan (Healer/Anima), Zero (Cosmos/Sub-DPS), and [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) (Incantation/Support). This team focuses on two primary reactions:

*   **The Blossom Reaction:** Triggered by combining Anima and Cosmos. This creates auto-attacking entities on the field that scale directly with Nanally's hit count. With Zero providing constant Cosmos application, the field becomes crowded with additional damage sources.
*   **The Hexed Reaction:** Triggered by Anima and Incantation. This reaction replicates a portion of Nanally's damage in an area of effect, making it the premier choice for clearing waves of smaller enemies. Sakiri is the best enabler for this, as her kit also provides grouping capabilities.

### F2P Friendly Team Variations

For players without a full roster of S-Rank characters, a highly effective F2P team includes:
1.  **Nanally** (Main DPS)
2.  **Mint** (Anima Support/Healer)
3.  **Zero** (Cosmos Sub-DPS - often available through early game rewards/selectors)
4.  **Haniel** (Psyche Support)

This team maintains the Blossom reaction while using Haniel to provide utility and crowd control. While it lacks the raw damage replication of the Hexed reaction, it is more than enough to clear the main story and early Hunter Rank challenges.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/nte-character-ascension-materials-farming-guide-20260523/inline-5.webp)


To get the most out of Nanally, you cannot simply mash the attack button. You must manage her summon and her ability to dodge-cancel without breaking her combo sequence.

### Optimal Combat Sequence

1.  **Opener:** Start with Sakiri. Use her skill to group enemies and apply the Incantation status.
2.  **Setup:** Swap to Zero. Use his skill to trigger the Cosmos element and fill the Esper Meter instantly.
3.  **Burst Window:** Swap to Nanally. Immediately cast her Ultimate to summon the Underboss.
4.  **Execution:** Use her Skill to enter her aggressive combo state. Perform the 5-hit Basic Combo, but branch into "Hold" attacks during the third and fifth hits for maximum multipliers.

### Managing the Underboss Follow-up Window

The Underboss remains active for a set duration after the Ultimate is cast. During this window, every action Nanally takes—including dodges—will trigger a coordinated strike from the summon. A key mechanic to master is dodge-canceling. In Neverness to Everness, Nanally can dodge mid-animation to avoid damage, and it will not break her attack sequence. This allows you to stay aggressive even in the face of heavy boss attacks.

Skill leveling priority should always be: **Ultimate > Basic Attack > Skill > Passive**.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/nte-character-ascension-materials-farming-guide-20260523/inline-6.webp)


**Where is the best place to farm Hunter Guides?**
Houdinii's Magic Stage is the most efficient use of Character Pixels (Stamina). Higher difficulty tiers unlock as your Hunter Rank increases, providing better XP-to-Pixel ratios.

**How do I get more Beetle Coins?**
The most consistent way to earn Beetle Coins is by completing Daily Commissions and participating in high-risk Peril Hunts throughout Hethereau. Selling unwanted lower-tier Disks can also provide a small influx of currency.

**Can I use Nanally without her signature weapon?**
Yes. While Ready-Ready provides a significant boost to her Underboss damage, the A-Rank Arc "Oraora!" is a highly competitive substitute that is much easier to obtain and refine.

**What is the level cap for Ascension?**
Currently, character levels are capped at Level 80 for the 1.0 launch version. Future updates are expected to raise this cap as new regions of the city are unlocked.

**Is Crit Rate or ATK% more important for Nanally?**
Because Nanally hits so many times, Crit Rate is vital for consistency. You should aim for at least 60% Crit Rate before focusing heavily on ATK% or Crit DMG.
