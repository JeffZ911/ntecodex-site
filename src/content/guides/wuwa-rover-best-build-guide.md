## Best Echoes (Artifacts)

![Best Echoes (Artifacts)](/img/wuwa-rover-best-build-guide/inline-3.webp)


In Wuthering Waves, the Echo system replaces traditional gear. You must balance "Sonata Effects" (set bonuses) with the active skill of your primary Echo.

### Havoc Rover: Sun-sinking Eclipse & Dreamless

For Havoc Rover, the goal is maximizing Havoc DMG and Burst potential.

*   **Sonata Effect:** 5-Piece **Sun-sinking Eclipse**. This set provides a 10% Havoc DMG boost, plus an additional 30% Havoc DMG boost that stacks when using Basic or Heavy attacks.
*   **Primary Echo:** **Dreamless**. This is the mandatory choice. The Dreamless Echo skill deals massive Havoc DMG, and its damage is increased by an additional 50% if used shortly after the Rover casts their Resonance Liberation: Deadening Abyss.
*   **Main Stats:**
    *   **Cost 4:** Crit Rate or Crit DMG
    *   **Cost 3:** Havoc DMG% / Havoc DMG%
    *   **Cost 1:** ATK% / ATK%

### Spectro Rover: Celestial Light & Mourning Aix

Spectro Rover functions best as a mix of DPS and utility.

*   **Sonata Effect:** 5-Piece **Celestial Light**. Increases Spectro DMG by 10% and grants an additional 30% Spectro DMG boost for 15 seconds after using a Resonance Skill.
*   **Alternative Set:** 5-Piece **Moonlit Clouds**. Use this if you are running Rover purely as a Sub-DPS/Buffer, as it increases Energy Regen and buffs the next character's ATK upon swapping.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/wuwa-rover-best-build-guide/inline-5.webp)


Mastering the Rover requires understanding their Forte Gauge (the bar above the HP bar), which dictates when they can enter their enhanced states.

### Mastering the Havoc Dark Surge Combo

1.  **Build Umbra:** Use Basic Attacks and Resonance Skills until the Umbra gauge is full.
2.  **Transform:** Hold Heavy Attack to consume the Umbra gauge and enter the **Dark Surge** state.
3.  **Burst:** Immediately cast **Resonance Liberation: Deadening Abyss**.
4.  **Echo Skill:** Use the **Dreamless Echo** skill immediately after the Liberation animation to benefit from the 50% DMG multiplier.
5.  **Skill Spam:** Use the enhanced Resonance Skill (Wingblade) while in Dark Surge.
6.  **Outro:** Swap to your next character to trigger the Rover's Outro Skill, which deals a final burst of Havoc DMG.

### Spectro Time-Stop and Outro Timing

1.  **Intro:** Swap Rover in via Intro Skill to trigger a small Spectro explosion.
2.  **Combo:** Perform Basic Attack 3 followed by a Heavy Attack to trigger **Aftertune**.
3.  **Enhanced Skill:** Use the Resonance Skill while the "Aftertune" buff is active to deal increased damage and generate more Concerto Energy.
4.  **Liberation:** Cast Resonance Liberation to slow enemies in a wide radius.
5.  **Swap:** Once Concerto Energy is full, swap to your Main DPS to trigger the Spectro Rover's Outro Skill, which creates a stasis field that slows enemies for 3 seconds.

### Switching Attributes and Skills

To switch the Rover's element, open the **Character Menu**, select the Rover, and look for the **circular "Switch" icon** located next to their name or the "Resonator Info" tab. Switching attributes will change your active skills and Forte Circuit, but your character level and base stats remain the same. You will, however, need to equip different Echo sets to match the new element.

### Rover Resonance Chain (Sequences) Guide

Rover's Wavebands (Constellations/Sequences) are unique because they are not pulled from the Gacha. Instead, they are earned through gameplay:

*   **S1-S2:** Usually obtained through Main Story progression (Act I through VI).
*   **S3-S5:** Obtained by reaching specific milestones in **Casket Delivery** (handing in Sonance Caskets to the Relic Merchant in Jinzhou).
*   **S6:** Typically rewarded for completing high-level Union Level milestones or late-game story chapters.

**Key Sequences for Havoc Rover:**
*   **S1 (Cryptic Sound):** Increases Resonance Skill DMG.
*   **S2 (Mute Insight):** Restores Energy when entering Dark Surge.
*   **S6 (Illusive Moon):** Increases Havoc DMG during Dark Surge, significantly boosting endgame performance.

### Is Rover better than limited 5-stars?

While limited units like Jiyan or Jinhsi often have higher "floor" damage, a fully built S6 Havoc Rover is competitive with S0 limited 5-stars. Their ease of access and the fact that their "Wavebands" are free makes them the most reliable DPS option for F2P players.
