# Gepard Best Build Guide: The Ultimate Preservation Tank

Gepard Landau is the Captain of the Silvermane Guards and a 5-star Ice character following the Path of Preservation. While many newer sustain units have been introduced, Gepard’s ability to provide a massive, team-wide shield through his Ultimate makes him a unique asset for players tackling difficult endgame content like Memory of Chaos and Pure Fiction. Unlike other Preservation units that rely on damage reduction or single-target shields, Gepard focuses on raw defensive stats to absorb incoming damage for the entire party.

## Overview

![Overview](/img/gepard-best-build-honkai-star-rail/inline-1.webp)


Gepard serves as a "pure tank." His kit is designed around high survivability and crowd control. His primary value is his Ultimate, "Vow of Protection," which applies a shield to all allies for three turns. Because this shield scales directly with Gepard's DEF stat, building him involves stacking as much Defense as possible while maintaining enough Speed to refresh the shield before it expires.

His Skill, "Daunting Smite," offers a reliable way to Freeze enemies, providing utility by delaying enemy turns. Furthermore, his Talent, "Unyielding Will," acts as a safety net, allowing him to revive once per battle if he takes a killing blow. This makes him exceptionally difficult for bosses to remove from the field.

### Trace Priority
To maximize Gepard’s efficiency, prioritize his traces in the following order:
1.  **Ultimate (Vow of Protection):** This is his most important trace. It dictates the strength of his team-wide shield.
2.  **Talent (Unyielding Will):** Increasing this improves the amount of HP he restores upon reviving.
3.  **Skill (Daunting Smite):** Leveling this increases the damage and the base chance to Freeze, though the Freeze chance is the primary focus.
4.  **Basic ATK:** This is a low priority, used mainly for generating Skill Points.

### Gepard's Role in the Meta
In the current meta, Gepard is valued for his "solo-sustain" capabilities. This means he can keep a team alive without needing an additional healer (Abundance character). While he lacks the cleansing abilities of units like Huohuo or the offensive buffs of Aventurine, his raw shield capacity is often higher. He is particularly effective in teams that do not want to take any chip damage, such as those featuring Yanqing.

### Core Mechanics: Shielding and Freezing
Gepard’s shielding mechanic is reactive. Because his shield is tied to his Ultimate, he requires Energy Regeneration to be effective. If his shield drops and he doesn't have his Ultimate ready, the team becomes vulnerable. This is why Speed (SPD) and Energy Regeneration Rate (ERR) are just as vital as DEF%. 

His Freeze mechanic serves as a secondary layer of defense. By freezing an elite enemy or boss, Gepard effectively "mitigates" damage by preventing the enemy from attacking at all. This is especially useful against enemies with powerful AoE wind-up attacks.

## Best Weapons

![Best Weapons](/img/gepard-best-build-honkai-star-rail/inline-2.webp)


Selecting the right Light Cone for Gepard is a balance between increasing his DEF and managing enemy aggro. Since Gepard has a naturally high Taunt value, Light Cones that further increase the likelihood of him being attacked are beneficial, as they help him generate Energy faster.

### Top 5-Star Light Cones
**Moment of Victory** is Gepard's signature Light Cone and remains his absolute Best-in-Slot (BiS) option. It provides a massive 24% increase to DEF and Effect Hit Rate (EHR). Furthermore, it increases the wearer's chance to be attacked. When the wearer is attacked, they receive an additional 24% DEF boost until the end of their turn. This double-dipping into DEF stats makes his shields nearly unbreakable.

**Texture of Memories** is a top-tier Free-to-Play (F2P) alternative available through Herta’s Store in the Simulated Universe. While it doesn't provide a team-wide shield boost, it grants Gepard a personal shield and reduces damage taken when he is shielded. This makes him even more unkillable, though it does less for the rest of the team compared to Moment of Victory.

### Best 4-Star and F2P Alternatives
**Landau's Choice** is often considered the best 4-star option. It significantly increases the wearer's Taunt value and reduces damage taken. By forcing enemies to hit Gepard, you ensure he gains Energy quickly to keep his Ultimate active.

**Day One of My New Life** provides a flat DEF increase and, more importantly, increases the DMG RES of all allies. This effectively makes Gepard’s shields "thicker" because the damage hitting the shield is reduced by the DMG RES buff.

**Trend of the Universal Market** is a niche but powerful choice, specifically for teams featuring Acheron. It provides DEF and has a chance to apply a Burn debuff to enemies that attack Gepard. Since Acheron requires debuffs to build her Ultimate stacks, Gepard becomes a battery for her while still performing his tanking duties.

To maximize Gepard’s utility, you must focus on the Knight of Purity Palace set. Currently, no other relic set provides the same level of direct synergy with his kit.

### Top Relic Sets (Cavern of Corrosion)
**Knight of Purity Palace (4-pc):** This is the definitive set for Gepard. The 2-piece bonus increases DEF by 15%, and the 4-piece bonus increases the max DMG that can be absorbed by the shield created by the wearer by 20%. This 20% multiplier is calculated after all other DEF bonuses, making it a massive upgrade for his Ultimate.

**Guard of Wuthering Snow (2-pc) + Knight of Purity Palace (2-pc):** If you are struggling with Gepard’s personal survival in extreme endgame content, this hybrid set provides 15% DEF and 8% Damage Reduction. However, the full Knight set is generally preferred for team-wide safety.

### Best Planar Ornaments (Simulated Universe)
**Belobog of the Architects:** This is the standard choice for a pure tank build. It provides a 15% DEF boost. If Gepard’s Effect Hit Rate is 50% or higher, he gains an additional 15% DEF. While reaching 50% EHR can be difficult without his signature Light Cone, the base 15% is still valuable.

**Broken Keel:** For players who have reached a point where Gepard’s shields are "strong enough," Broken Keel is the superior support choice. It increases the team’s CRIT DMG by 10% as long as Gepard has 30% or more Effect RES. Since Gepard gains Effect RES through his minor traces, reaching this threshold is relatively easy.

## Team Comp

Gepard is a flexible unit that can fit into almost any team needing a sustain. However, he has specific synergies that elevate him from a "good tank" to a "best-in-slot" partner.

### The Yanqing-Gepard Core
Yanqing is a high-damage Ice DPS who relies on his "Soulsteel Sync" buff. This buff is lost immediately if Yanqing takes damage. Since Gepard’s shield absorbs damage entirely (preventing it from touching Yanqing's HP), Gepard is the only sustain unit that can reliably keep Yanqing’s buff active 100% of the time. This pairing is the gold standard for Ice-weak content.

### Gepard in Acheron Debuff Teams
As mentioned in the Light Cone section, Gepard is a premier choice for Acheron teams when equipped with **Trend of the Universal Market**. Because Acheron gains Ultimate points whenever an enemy receives a debuff, and the Light Cone applies Burn when Gepard is hit, Gepard’s high Taunt value turns enemy attacks into offensive momentum for Acheron.

### F2P-Friendly Team Options
Gepard is excellent for F2P players because he can solo-sustain a team of 4-star characters. A standard F2P lineup would include:
*   **Gepard:** Sustain/Tank
*   **Dan Heng:** Main DPS (Wind)

*   **Asta:** Support (SPD and ATK buffs)

In this team, Gepard uses his Basic ATK most of the time to generate Skill Points for Dan Heng and Serval, only using his Skill if a specific enemy needs to be frozen.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/gepard-best-build-honkai-star-rail/inline-5.webp)


Mastering Gepard requires understanding the rhythm of combat. He is a Skill Point (SP) positive character, meaning he usually generates more points than he consumes.

### Pre-battle: Technique
Always use Gepard's Technique, **Comradery**, before entering a fight. This grants the entire team a shield equal to 24% of Gepard's DEF plus a flat value for 2 turns. This is critical because it protects the team during the first cycle while Gepard builds up the Energy needed for his actual Ultimate.

### Optimal Skill Point Usage
During combat, your goal is to use Gepard's Basic ATK as much as possible. This serves two purposes: it generates SP for your high-damage dealers and builds Energy for his Ultimate. 

You should only use his Skill, **Daunting Smite**, in two scenarios:
1.  **Emergency Energy:** If Gepard is only a few points away from his Ultimate and an enemy is about to unleash a massive attack, use the Skill to get the extra Energy immediately.
2.  **Crowd Control:** If a dangerous enemy (like a Decaying Shadow or a boss with a charge-up state) is about to act, use the Skill to attempt a Freeze.

### Maximizing Ultimate Uptime
The most common mistake with Gepard is "over-shielding." Do not immediately press his Ultimate button just because it is glowing. 
*   **Wait for the current shield to expire:** Gepard's Ultimate does not stack; it refreshes. If your allies still have a full shield, wait until the enemy attacks and wears it down.
*   **Wait for the ally's turn:** Shields in HSR tick down based on the *target's* turn. If your DPS is about to act and their shield has 1 turn left, the shield will disappear after they move. Cast Gepard's Ultimate *after* they finish their turn but *before* the enemy attacks to maximize the shield's duration.

### Step-by-Step Combat Flow
1.  **Start:** Activate Technique (Comradery).
2.  **Turn 1:** Basic ATK to generate SP.
3.  **Turn 2:** Basic ATK. If hit by enemies, Ultimate should be nearly ready.
4.  **Enemy Turn:** If the enemy prepares an AoE, trigger Ultimate (Vow of Protection).
5.  **Repeat:** Maintain the 3-turn rotation of Basic ATKs while monitoring the shield duration on your teammates.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/gepard-best-build-honkai-star-rail/inline-6.webp)


### Is Gepard's E1 worth it?
Yes, Gepard’s first Eidolon (E1) is one of the best in the game for a standard character. It increases the base chance of his Skill to Freeze enemies by an additional 35%, bringing the total base chance to 100%. This allows him to reliably lock down bosses, provided you have a decent amount of Effect Hit Rate from your gear.

### Should I use DEF% or SPD boots?
SPD boots are almost always better. While DEF% boots give you a larger shield, Gepard is naturally slow (92 Base SPD). Without SPD boots, enemies will often take two turns for every one turn Gepard takes, causing his shield to expire before he can refresh it. 134 SPD is the recommended target.

### How does Gepard compare to Aventurine?
Aventurine is a more offensive Preservation unit who provides shields via his Skill and follow-up attacks. Gepard provides a much larger single shield via his Ultimate. Aventurine is better for follow-up teams (like Topaz/Dr. Ratio), whereas Gepard is better for pure defensive needs or teams that cannot afford to use Skill Points on their tank.

### Can Gepard solo sustain in Memory of Chaos?
Absolutely. With an Energy Regeneration Rate rope and at least 3,000 DEF, Gepard can comfortably solo-sustain even the highest floors of Memory of Chaos. The key is ensuring he has enough SPD to keep the Energy loop consistent.

### What is the EHR requirement for Belobog of the Architects?
To trigger the secondary 15% DEF bonus from the Belobog of the Architects Planar set, you need 50% Effect Hit Rate. This is easily achievable if you have his signature Light Cone, **Moment of Victory**. If you do not have it, you may need an EHR Body piece, though this will result in a weaker shield overall. Many players choose to ignore the secondary bonus and just take the base 15% DEF.

### Eidolons and Investment
Gepard is highly effective at E0, but his Eidolons provide significant utility:
*   **E1:** Increases Freeze chance (High value).
*   **E2:** Slows enemies after they unfreeze (Medium value).
*   **E4:** Increases Effect RES for all allies (High value for team survivability against CC).
*   **E6:** Gepard’s revive triggers an immediate action and restores more HP (High value for "clutch" moments).
