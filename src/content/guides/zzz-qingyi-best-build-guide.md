# Zenless Zone Zero Qingyi Best Build Guide
> ⚠️ **Notice:** Auto-generated content. May contain approximations or minor inaccuracies in supporting details. Help us improve via the comment section below. _Last reviewed: 2026-05-13._


Qingyi is an S-Rank Electric Agent within the Stun specialty, serving as a cornerstone for high-tier team compositions in Zenless Zone Zero. As a member of the Criminal Investigation Special Response Team (PubSec), her kit is designed around high field time, rapid-fire strikes, and a unique debuff mechanic that significantly amplifies the damage enemies take while stunned. Unlike traditional stunners who may swap out quickly, Qingyi demands more active play to maximize her "Subjugation" stacks, making her a "Main Stunner" who bridges the gap between support and damage dealer.

## Overview

![Overview](/img/zzz-qingyi-best-build-guide/inline-1.webp)


Qingyi’s kit revolves around the "Flash Connect Voltage" mechanic. By performing her multi-hit Basic Attacks—specifically the third hit of her combo—she builds up a Voltage meter. Once this meter exceeds 75%, she can unleash her "Enchanted Moonlit Blossoms" attack by holding the Basic Attack button. This move is her primary tool for applying "Subjugation" stacks to enemies. Each stack increases the Stun DMG Multiplier, allowing your Attack-type Agents to deal devastating damage once the enemy finally breaks.

### Qingyi's Role in the Meta

In the current Zenless Zone Zero meta, Qingyi occupies a top-tier position among Stun Agents. While Anby is the accessible F2P Electric Stunner, Qingyi outclasses her in both Daze generation speed and team utility. Her ability to increase the Stun DMG Multiplier by up to 80% is a unique multiplier that stacks multiplicatively with other buffs, making her indispensable for clearing high-difficulty content like Shiyu Defense. She is particularly favored in teams that require a high-frequency attacker to trigger assist follow-ups or specific passive conditions.

### Core Mechanics: Flash Connect Voltage and Subjugation

The defining feature of Qingyi's gameplay is the **Flash Connect Voltage**. This meter is displayed below her health bar.
*   **Voltage Accumulation:** Qingyi gains Voltage primarily through her Basic Attack: Moonlit Lotus. The third hit of this sequence involves a rapid series of staff thrusts; this is the most efficient way to charge the meter.
*   **Enchanted Moonlit Blossoms:** When Voltage is at 75% or higher, holding the Basic Attack button triggers a channeled multi-hit rush. This move consumes Voltage and applies the **Subjugation** debuff.
*   **Subjugation Debuff:** This debuff increases the Stun DMG Multiplier of the target. While most enemies have a base Stun DMG Multiplier of 150%, a fully debuffed enemy under Qingyi's influence can reach up to 230%. This effect persists for the entire duration of the enemy's stun state.

### Skill Upgrade Priority

To optimize your resources, focus on upgrading Qingyi’s skills in the following order:
1.  **Core Skill:** This upgrades her Impact-to-ATK conversion and the maximum Stun DMG Multiplier bonus.
2.  **Basic Attack:** This is her primary source of Daze and Voltage.
3.  **EX Special Attack:** Provides a significant burst of Daze and helps maintain Voltage.
4.  **Dodge:** Improves her Dodge Counter, which is useful for maintaining field presence.
5.  **Chain Attack / Ultimate:** While powerful, these are secondary to her role as a Daze-builder.
6.  **Assist:** Useful for switching in, but lower priority for overall performance.

## Best Weapons

![Best Weapons](/img/zzz-qingyi-best-build-guide/inline-2.webp)


Choosing the right W-Engine for Qingyi is critical for reaching her Impact thresholds. Her passive ability, "Archaic Enticement," converts her Impact stat into a flat Attack bonus, capping at 600 ATK when she reaches 220 Impact. Therefore, any W-Engine that boosts Impact is inherently superior.

### Top Tier: S-Rank W-Engines

**Ice-Jade Teapot (Signature)**
This is Qingyi’s best-in-slot weapon. It provides a massive 18% Impact secondary stat. Its passive, "Tea-riffic," triggers when the wearer hits an enemy with a Basic Attack, granting a stack of "Tea-riffic" (up to 30 stacks). Each stack increases the wearer's Impact. Furthermore, when the wearer gains a certain number of stacks, the entire team receives a 20% DMG bonus. This weapon perfectly aligns with Qingyi’s high-frequency Basic Attack playstyle.

**The Restrained (Lycaon’s Signature)**
If you happened to pull Lycaon’s signature engine, it is an excellent alternative. It also provides 18% Impact and increases the Daze and DMG of Basic Attacks. Since Qingyi spends the majority of her time using Basic Attacks to build Voltage, she makes full use of this passive.

### Best A-Rank and F2P Options

**Steam Oven (Best F2P)**
The Steam Oven is the premier craftable option for Qingyi. It provides Energy Regen as a secondary stat, but its passive is the real draw: for every 10 Energy accumulated, the wearer’s Impact increases (stacking up to 8 times). At high refinement (R5), this can rival S-Rank engines in pure Daze potential, provided you manage your Energy levels effectively.

**Demara Battery Mark II**
Anby’s signature weapon is a solid budget choice. It increases Electric DMG and Energy Generation. While it doesn't provide the raw Impact of the Steam Oven, it helps Qingyi use her EX Special more frequently, which in turn helps build Voltage faster.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/zzz-qingyi-best-build-guide/inline-5.webp)


Using Qingyi effectively requires understanding how to stay on the field without taking damage. Her combos have built-in "Dodge Offsets," meaning you can dodge an incoming attack and then immediately continue your Basic Attack sequence without starting from the first hit.

### The Flash Connect Combo

1.  **Initiation:** Start with Basic Attack 1 and 2 to get into the rhythm.
2.  **The Spam Phase:** Reach Basic Attack 3. This is the multi-hit staff thrust. Continue tapping the Basic Attack button to extend this phase. This builds Voltage rapidly.
3.  **The Finisher:** Use Basic Attack 4 to conclude the sequence if you need to reposition.
4.  **Voltage Release:** Once your meter is above 75%, hold the Basic Attack button. Qingyi will enter the "Enchanted Moonlit Blossoms" state. She will dash forward and perform a flurry of hits.
5.  **Perfect Timing:** During the final hit of "Enchanted Moonlit Blossoms," if you time it correctly or trigger a Perfect Dodge, you can instantly maximize the Subjugation stacks on the enemy.

### Maximizing the Stun DMG Multiplier

The goal is to have the enemy reach 100% Daze (Stun) just as you reach the maximum Subjugation stacks.
*   **Step 1:** Build Voltage to 100%.
*   **Step 2:** Use EX Special Attack to deal a massive chunk of Daze.
*   **Step 3:** Trigger "Enchanted Moonlit Blossoms" to finish the Daze bar and apply the debuff.
*   **Step 4:** Once the "Chain Attack" prompt appears, cycle through your Support (Nicole/Rina) and end with your Attacker (Zhu Yuan/Anton).
*   **Step 5:** Unleash your Attacker's ultimate and skills while the 80% multiplier is active.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/zzz-qingyi-best-build-guide/inline-6.webp)


**Is Qingyi better than Anby?**
Yes. While Anby is a fantastic F2P unit, Qingyi offers significantly faster Daze accumulation and a unique Stun DMG Multiplier debuff that Anby cannot replicate. Qingyi also has better defensive utility through her long invulnerability frames during her EX Special and Chain attacks.

**What is the Impact cap for Qingyi?**
Qingyi's passive, "Archaic Enticement," scales her ATK based on her Impact. The cap is reached at 220 Impact, which provides a 600 ATK bonus. To reach this, you will need a Level 60 Qingyi, an Impact% Slot 6 Drive Disc, and either her signature weapon or a high-refinement Steam Oven.

**Can I play Qingyi as a Main DPS?**
While Qingyi has high personal damage for a Stunner, she is not designed to be a Main DPS. Her kit is optimized to stay on the field *only* until the enemy is stunned. Once the enemy is stunned, her damage contribution drops significantly compared to an Attack-type Agent like Zhu Yuan or Ellen.

**Is her signature weapon necessary?**
It is not necessary for her to function, but it is a significant upgrade. The "Ice-Jade Teapot" provides a team-wide damage buff that makes her a pseudo-Support Agent. However, an R5 "Steam Oven" is a very competitive F2P alternative that allows her to stun enemies almost as quickly.

**How does her Dodge Offset work?**
If you are in the middle of her Basic Attack 3 (the staff spam) and an enemy attacks, you can press the Dodge button and then immediately press Basic Attack again. Qingyi will resume the staff spam exactly where she left off, rather than restarting the combo from the first hit. This allows you to maintain Voltage generation even against aggressive bosses.
