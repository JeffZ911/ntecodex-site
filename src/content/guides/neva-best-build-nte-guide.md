# Neva (Hotori) Best Build Guide: Neverness to Everness (NTE)

## Overview

![Overview](/img/neva-best-build-nte-guide/inline-1.webp)


Neva, officially recognized in the game files and story as Hotori, has rapidly ascended to the top of the tier lists following the April 29, 2026, launch. As an S-Class Anima element character, she occupies a unique niche that blurs the lines between a traditional Burst DPS and a high-utility Support. Her kit is centered around the manipulation of the battlefield's temporal flow, a mechanic that is currently unmatched by any other Esper in the roster.

Her primary value in high-level play, such as the Beyond the Rail endgame mode, stems from her ability to effectively "pause" the game. While other characters rely on dodging and positioning, Neva simply halts the enemy's progress, allowing for uninterrupted damage windows. This is facilitated by her unique resource system, the "Non-closed Time Piece," which serves as the engine for her most devastating abilities.

### Core Mechanics: Frozen Snow Ritual
The "Frozen Snow Ritual" is the centerpiece of Neva’s gameplay. By accumulating energy within her "Non-closed Time Piece" through basic attacks and Esper Skills, she can trigger a state that slows time to a near standstill for all enemies on the field. Unlike standard "slow" debuffs found in other RPGs, Neva’s ritual affects the actual stage timer in specific challenge modes, making her the gold standard for players aiming for three-star clears in time-sensitive content. During this window, her damage profile shifts, granting her massive multipliers on her heavy attacks.

### Why the Community Calls Her Neva
While the character's name is officially Hotori, the global community almost exclusively refers to her as "Neva." This nickname originated during the early closed beta tests and was reinforced by her signature umbrella-sword's design, which features aesthetic flourishes reminiscent of "Neverness." Additionally, the shorthand is frequently used in theorycrafting circles to differentiate her from other Anima units that share similar naming conventions in regional localizations. When searching for community-made spreadsheets or rotation clips, using "Neva" will yield significantly more results than "Hotori."

## Best Weapons

![Best Weapons](/img/neva-best-build-nte-guide/inline-2.webp)


Choosing the right Arc for Neva is critical because her damage scales heavily with Cycle Intensity and Anima DMG bonuses. Because she spends a significant portion of her rotation in a time-stopped state, weapons that provide "flat" damage increases are often outperformed by those that offer scaling buffs during Esper-active windows.

### Top Tier: Rain that Shook the World (S-Rank)
The "Rain that Shook the World" is Neva’s signature S-Rank Arc and is indisputably her best-in-slot option. This weapon is designed specifically to capitalize on her "Frozen Snow Ritual." It provides a massive base increase to Anima DMG, but its true power lies in its passive ability. After Neva casts an Esper Skill, the weapon grants a unique buff to Cycle Intensity. This allows her to regenerate energy for her next "Non-closed Time Piece" much faster, effectively shortening the downtime between her time-stop windows. For players looking to tackle the highest floors of Beyond the Rail, pulling for this Arc is highly recommended.

### F2P Alternative: Umbrella (A-Rank)
For players who did not pull on the weapon banner, the A-Rank "Umbrella" (Synthesis type) is the most viable alternative. While it lacks the raw offensive power of the signature S-Rank, it offers a substantial HP scaling bonus. This is particularly useful for Neva because her survival can be precarious during the wind-up phase of her ritual. Furthermore, the "Umbrella" provides a boost to shield strength, which synergizes well if you are running her with a defensive support like Fadia. It ensures that Neva isn't interrupted while she is building her stacks.

### Other Options: Ready-Ready
If you are playing Neva in a role that focuses more on her time-stop utility rather than her personal damage, "Ready-Ready" is an interesting choice. This Arc triggers a team-wide ATK% buff whenever the wearer uses an ultimate. Since Neva’s rotation always ends with her "Echoes in the Vessel" ultimate, she can reliably maintain high uptime on this buff for your other party members.

## Team Comp

![Team Comp](/img/neva-best-build-nte-guide/inline-4.webp)


Neva does not operate in a vacuum. To truly unlock her potential, she needs teammates who can provide the energy she needs and capitalize on the openings she creates. The current meta revolves around elemental reactions, specifically the interaction between Anima and Cosmos.

### The Meta Blossom Team
The "Blossom Team" is currently the highest-performing composition in Neverness to Everness. This team utilizes the Blossom reaction, which occurs when Anima and Cosmos elements interact.
*   **Neva (Hotori):** The primary Burst DPS and time-manipulator.
*   **Zero (Cosmos):** Zero is the lynchpin of this team. His Esper Skill allows for instant Esper Meter refills for the entire party. In this comp, Zero acts as a "battery," allowing Neva to trigger the Blossom reaction almost instantly and tripling the number of Blossom triggers per minute.
*   **Jiuyuan:** Jiuyuan serves as the primary crowd control specialist. Her ability to group enemies into a tight cluster is essential because Neva’s ultimate and heavy attacks have a specific AOE (Area of Effect) radius. By grouping enemies first, Jiuyuan ensures that Neva hits every target on the field during her "Frozen Snow Ritual."

### F2P-Friendly Composition
For players without Zero or Jiuyuan, Neva can still lead a formidable team.
*   **Neva:** Main DPS.
*   **Fadia:** Fadia provides incredible defensive utility. She can redirect damage away from Neva and provide near-immortal survivability through her damage-sharing mechanic. This allows Neva to stay on the field longer without fear of being interrupted during her attack animations.
*   **undisclosed:** A secondary Anima or support unit to provide elemental resonance.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/neva-best-build-nte-guide/inline-5.webp)


To achieve the maximum damage numbers seen in community showcases, you must follow a strict rotation. Neva’s damage is heavily back-loaded, meaning the majority of her impact happens at the very end of her sequence.

### Opening Sequence
1.  **Start with Zero:** Immediately use Zero’s Esper Skill. This is the most important step as it fills the team’s Esper Meter to 100%.
2.  **Swap to Neva:** Switch to Neva (Hotori) to trigger the Blossom reaction immediately upon entry. This reaction provides an initial burst of damage and sets the stage for her ritual.
3.  **Activate Esper Skill:** Use Neva’s Esper Skill to consume the meter and activate the "Frozen Snow Ritual." This will stop the stage timer and freeze enemies in place.

### Maximizing the Time-Stop Window
Once the "Frozen Snow Ritual" is active, the clock is literally on your side.
1.  **Hold Basic Attack:** Execute Neva’s "Rippling Waves." By holding the basic attack button during the time-stop, she unleashes a series of high-velocity slashes. Because the enemies cannot move, every hit of this multi-stage attack is guaranteed to connect.
2.  **Unleash Ultimate:** Just before the ritual window expires, cast Neva’s Ultimate, "Echoes in the Vessel." This deals massive AOE Anima damage and consumes any remaining "Non-closed Time Piece" energy to multiply the final blow's damage.
3.  **Cleanup:** Swap to Jiuyuan or your secondary DPS to clean up any remaining low-health enemies while Neva’s cooldowns reset.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/neva-best-build-nte-guide/inline-6.webp)


**Is Neva a [free character](https://ntecodex.com/guides/gilgamesh-vs-archer-free-character-choice-guide-20260711)?**
No, Neva (Hotori) is a limited S-Rank character. She was featured on the "Echoes in the Vessel" debut banner. Players must use their premium currency to pull for her, and she is not currently available in the standard recruitment pool.

**What is Neva's official name?**
Her official in-game name is Hotori. The name "Neva" is a community-driven nickname that has become the standard way to refer to her in guides, Discord servers, and Reddit threads.

**Does her time-stop work on bosses?**
Yes. One of Neva’s greatest strengths is that her "Frozen Snow Ritual" affects all enemies, including major Anomalies and world bosses. While some bosses have high resistance to standard stuns, Neva’s temporal manipulation is a field effect that slows the boss's internal logic, making their attacks much easier to dodge once the ritual ends.

**What is her best element reaction?**
Blossom is currently her strongest reaction. By combining Neva’s Anima element with a Cosmos element character like Zero, you trigger an explosion of energy that deals AOE damage and significantly boosts the party's energy regeneration rate.

**What are her talent priorities?**
You should prioritize leveling her "Frozen Snow Ritual" (Skill) first, followed by "Echoes in the Vessel" (Ultimate). Her basic attack, "Rippling Waves," should be leveled third, as its damage is heavily dependent on the multipliers provided by her skill.
