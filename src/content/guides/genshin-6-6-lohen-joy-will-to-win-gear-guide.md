## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/genshin-6-6-lohen-joy-will-to-win-gear-guide/inline-4.webp)


Not every player needs to overhaul their setup for a new update. You should consider skipping these upgrades if:

*   **You already own high-end office furniture:** If you currently own a high-end ergonomic office chair from brands like Steelcase or Herman Miller, most gaming chairs will likely feel like a downgrade in build quality and long-term spinal support.
*   **Desk space is limited:** Players using 1440p 27-inch monitors may not see a significant benefit in upgrading to an ultrawide if their desk depth is less than 24 inches. At that distance, the edges of a 34-inch screen may fall outside the user's peripheral vision, causing neck strain from constant side-to-side movement.
*   **You prefer lightweight mice:** If you do not use macros or complex keybindings, the Razer Naga's weight (134g) may actually hinder your experience compared to a lighter, more agile mouse designed for fast-paced shooters.

### What are the minimum PC requirements for Neverness to Everness?
While official final specifications can vary, the use of Unreal Engine 5 suggests that players will need a modern multi-core processor and a GPU capable of handling ray-tracing features for the best experience. ### How do I unlock car customization in Hethereau?
In Neverness to Everness, car customization is a core feature that allows players to modify their vehicles for both performance and aesthetics. ### How can I reduce eye strain during 8-hour gaming sessions?
Reducing eye strain involves a combination of proper lighting and the "20-20-20 rule," which suggests looking at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds every 20 minutes. Using a light bar like the BenQ ScreenBar Halo helps by providing ambient desk light and reducing the contrast-related fatigue caused by a bright monitor in a dark room.
