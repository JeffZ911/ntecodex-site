## Overview

![Overview](/img/nte-stamina-management-tips-mint-build/inline-1.webp)


Neverness to Everness (NTE) distinguishes itself from other open-world RPGs by implementing a dual stamina system that separates your combat potential from your lifestyle progression. Navigating the supernatural urban landscape requires a firm grasp of how Character Pixels and City Stamina interact. While one dictates how many rewards you can claim from Anomaly Zones, the other limits your ability to engage in the city's secondary economy.

Character Pixels serve as the primary gatekeeper for character progression. These are consumed when players clear challenges, farm for Arcs, or hunt for high-tier Cartridges. For players focusing on Mint, the game’s premier Anima-element DPS, stamina is more than just a currency—it is a direct combat resource. Mint’s unique kit revolves around her "Minty Whirlwind" charged attack, which consumes stamina to deal sustained AoE damage. Mastering her build requires a strategy that balances maximum stamina capacity with rapid recovery.

### Understanding Character Pixels vs. City Stamina

Character Pixels are the "energy" or "resin" equivalent in NTE. You start with a base cap of 240, and they regenerate automatically over time. City Stamina, conversely, is a weekly resource used for non-combat activities. This separation ensures that players do not have to choose between upgrading their characters and enjoying the "City Tycoon" aspects of the game.

| Stamina Type | Purpose | Recovery Rate | Usage Examples |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Character Pixels** | Combat progression and rewards | 1 every 6 minutes | Anomaly Zones, Bosses, Cartridge farming |
| **City Stamina** | Urban activities and hobbies | Weekly reset | Truck deliveries, Fishing, Bank heists |

### How to Increase Your Maximum Stamina Cap

One of the most effective ways to optimize your daily gameplay is to increase the ceiling of your Character Pixels. This is achieved through the Hethereau housing system. By purchasing properties and placing specific high-value furniture, you provide your characters with better "rest conditions," which translates to a higher stamina cap.

The standard cap of 240 is sufficient for casual play, but dedicated players should aim for the 280 cap. This is primarily achieved by acquiring the Fengan Villa and outfitting it with the following items:

*   **The Piano:** Provides a significant boost to mental recovery stats.
*   **The Luxury Bed:** The primary source of cap expansion.

### Why Mint is the Meta Choice for Stamina Management

Mint is currently the only character in the NTE 1.0 meta whose damage output is directly proportional to her stamina pool. Her "Minty Whirlwind" is a high-velocity spinning attack that shreds enemy shields and applies the Anima status effect. Because this move drains stamina continuously, players who have invested in stamina cap increases see a literal increase in their "burst window" duration. Furthermore, Mint’s ability to reset her skill cooldowns through successful dodges makes her the most efficient user of the stamina bar in high-difficulty content.

## Best Weapons

![Best Weapons](/img/nte-stamina-management-tips-mint-build/inline-2.webp)


In NTE, weapons are referred to as "Arcs." These are categorized into three compatibility types: Liquid, Solid, and Gas. Mint is a Liquid-type compatible character, meaning she gains specific resonance bonuses when equipped with Liquid Arcs. Choosing the right Arc is the difference between a Mint that struggles to maintain momentum and one that clears entire waves in a single rotation.

### S-Rank Choice: Fluff of Fleetness

Fluff of Fleetness is the definitive Best-in-Slot (BiS) Arc for Mint. It is designed specifically to synergize with her high-frequency hitting style. The weapon provides a massive 44% Crit DMG substat at level 90, which is vital for scaling the "Minty Whirlwind" damage. Its passive effect, "Aero-Dynamic Grace," grants a stacking ATK buff every time the user consumes 20 stamina. Since Mint is constantly draining her bar, she reaches maximum stacks within the first few seconds of her rotation.

### Best F2P Arcs: Mind Royale and Clear Skies

For players who did not pull for Fluff of Fleetness, Mind Royale is the strongest four-star alternative. This Arc focuses on Break Intensity. In NTE, staggering an enemy (breaking their posture) is essential for dealing true damage. Mind Royale allows Mint to stagger elite enemies faster, which is particularly useful in the higher floors of the Anomaly Zones.

Clear Skies serves as a reliable early-game or budget option. Its primary benefit is Ultimate Energy regeneration. When Mint performs a Redirect Skill (her parry-like mechanic), Clear Skies restores a portion of her energy, ensuring her Ultimate is always ready for the next wave.

### Arc Compatibility: Why Liquid Arcs Matter for Mint

Equipping a Liquid Arc on Mint activates her "Fluid Motion" resonance. This hidden mechanic reduces the stamina consumption rate of her charged attacks by [information unavailable] and increases her movement speed while spinning. Using a Solid or Gas Arc on Mint is generally discouraged, as the loss of resonance bonuses results in a roughly 15-20% decrease in overall DPS efficiency.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/nte-stamina-management-tips-mint-build/inline-3.webp)


The "Disk" system in NTE is composed of Cartridges and Console Modules. These are the primary ways to customize Mint’s stats. For a stamina-heavy build, you must prioritize sets that reward high activity and frequent dodging.

### Recommended Cartridge Set: Fireflies and the Forest

The "Fireflies and the Forest" set is the gold standard for Anima-type damage dealers. It addresses Mint's two biggest needs: raw elemental damage and critical consistency.

*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Increases Anima DMG by 15%.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** After consuming 50 stamina, the user gains "Forest’s Haste," increasing Crit Rate by 20% and Basic Attack DMG by 30% for 10 seconds.

This set is perfectly tuned for Mint because her "Minty Whirlwind" triggers the 4-piece bonus almost instantly. This ensures that the majority of her spin duration benefits from the increased Crit Rate.

### Console Modules: Maximizing Crit and Break Stats

Console Modules act as secondary modifiers for your Cartridges. Each Cartridge can hold up to three Modules. For Mint, you should hunt for Type III Modules, which offer the highest stat ceilings in the game.

*   **Focus:** Type III Crit Modules (8% Crit Rate per module).
*   **Secondary:** Type III Break Modules (Increases stagger damage).
*   **Utility:** Type III Stamina Recovery Modules (Increases passive stamina regen by 5%).

### Farming Strategy: When to Spend Character Pixels on Disks

A common mistake is spending Character Pixels on Cartridge farming too early. In NTE, the drop rate for S-Rank (Gold) Disks significantly improves at Hunter Level 40. Before reaching this milestone, your Pixels are better spent on guaranteed upgrades:

1.  Character Leveling Materials.
2.  Arc Ascension Materials.
3.  Skill Tree (Pixel) Upgrades.

Once you hit Hunter Level 40, you can begin "burning" your De-noise Solutions to optimize your Fireflies and the Forest set.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/nte-stamina-management-tips-mint-build/inline-5.webp)


To maximize Mint's damage, you must follow a strict rotation that ensures all buffs are active before she begins her stamina-draining spin.

### The Opening Buff Sequence

1.  **Start with Sakiri:** Cast her Skill to deploy her slime companion and apply the defense shred to the target.
2.  **Switch to Zero/Jiuyuan:** Use their off-field abilities to prime the "Esper Meter." This meter, once filled, increases the team's elemental reaction damage.
3.  **Switch to Mint:** Use her Skill, "Overdrive: Super Claw," immediately followed by her Ultimate. This ensures her Ultimate benefits from any "on-skill" Arc buffs.

### Mastering the Critical Riposte Reset

Mint’s most powerful mechanic is "Caramel Crisp." When Mint performs a Critical Dodge (dodging right before an attack hits), she can follow up with a Riposte attack. This Riposte deals high Anima damage and, crucially, resets the cooldown of "Overdrive: Super Claw." This allows Mint to stay in her empowered state much longer than her base cooldowns would suggest.

### Managing Stamina During the Minty Whirlwind

Once the buffs are active, hold the Basic Attack button to enter the "Minty Whirlwind." 
*   **The 5-Second Rule:** Do not spin until your stamina is completely empty. It is mathematically optimal to spin for exactly 5 seconds, then perform a Critical Dodge to trigger a Riposte. This restores a small chunk of stamina and keeps the "Forest’s Haste" buff active.
*   **The Reset:** If you are hit during the spin, you lose your momentum. Use Adler’s shield or Sakiri’s crowd control to ensure Mint remains undisturbed.

**Optimal Combat Flow:**
1. Sakiri Skill -> 2. Jiuyuan Ultimate -> 3. Mint Skill -> 4. Mint Ultimate -> 5. Minty Whirlwind (5s) -> 6. Critical Dodge -> 7. Repeat.

## FAQ

### How do I get more De-noise Solution?

De-noise Solution is the consumable item used to refill 60 Character Pixels instantly. You can obtain these through:
*   **Weekly Tasks:** Completing city errands.
*   **Battle Pass:** Both the free and premium tiers offer several solutions.
*   **Level Up Rewards:** Reaching new Hunter Levels often grants a bundle of solutions.

### Does City Stamina affect my combat performance?

No. City Stamina is strictly for "lifestyle" content. While participating in truck deliveries or bank heists can earn you Credits (the main currency), it does not directly increase Mint’s damage or your Character Pixel count. However, the Credits earned can be used to buy furniture, which *does* increase your stamina cap.

### What is the fastest way to reach Hunter Level 40?

The most efficient way to gain Hunter EXP is by running Anomaly Zones. Every 10 Character Pixels spent grants a fixed amount of EXP. To level up quickly, ensure you never let your Pixels sit at the 240/240 cap. If you are short on time, you can quickly spend them by "condensing" them into reward multipliers at the Anomaly terminal.

### What are the Annulith refill costs?

If you run out of De-noise Solutions, you can spend Annuliths (premium currency) to refill 60 Character Pixels. This can be done up to 6 times per day, with the cost increasing each time.
