## Overview

![Overview](/img/lingyang-build-guide-wuthering-waves/inline-1.webp)


Lingyang is a 5-star Glacio Resonator who utilizes Gauntlets to deliver rapid-fire strikes. While many players initially find his aerial combat daunting, he serves as a powerful Main DPS capable of shredding elite enemies and bosses. His kit is heavily reliant on his Forte Circuit, which transforms his standard moveset into the "Striding Lion" state. During this window, Lingyang takes to the skies, performing a series of attacks that count as Basic Attack damage, making him a unique hypercarry in the current meta.

### Lingyang's Core Mechanics: Lion's Spirit and Striding Lion

The heart of Lingyang’s gameplay is the "Lion's Spirit" gauge, located above his health bar. This gauge is filled by performing Basic Attacks, using his Resonance Skill "Ancient Arts," or triggering his Intro Skill. Once the gauge is full, Lingyang can perform a Heavy Attack to enter the "Striding Lion" state.

In the Striding Lion state, Lingyang leaps into the air and gains a completely new set of animations. His Basic Attacks become "Feral Kicks," and his Resonance Skill transforms into "Mountain Roamer." This state lasts for 5 seconds by default, but its duration is significantly extended if you activate his Resonance Liberation, "Strive: Lion's Vigor," immediately before entering the state. While airborne, Lingyang is immune to most ground-based shockwaves, providing a defensive advantage alongside his offensive output.

### Is Lingyang Worth Building?

Lingyang is worth building if you enjoy high-mobility gameplay and are looking for a dedicated Glacio carry. He excels in single-target encounters where his "sticky" targeting allows him to stay glued to a boss's weak points. However, he requires more investment than some other 5-star units. To feel "complete," Lingyang needs a solid amount of Energy Regeneration and a dedicated Sub-DPS like Sanhua to amplify his Basic Attack damage. If you prefer wide-reaching AoE attacks or a simpler "button-mashing" playstyle, Lingyang’s technical requirements might feel restrictive.

### Top Tier: Abyss Surges

Abyss Surges is the definitive best-in-slot weapon for Lingyang. As a 5-star weapon, it offers the highest base ATK, but its secondary stats and passives are what truly make it shine. The built-in Energy Regeneration helps Lingyang reach the 130% threshold required to keep his Resonance Liberation on a consistent loop. Furthermore, the passive creates a "ping-pong" buff effect: using his Resonance Skill buffs his Basic Attacks (his primary damage source in the air), and his Basic Attacks buff his Resonance Skill. This perfectly mirrors his optimal rotation.

### Best 4-Star Options: Stonard and Hollow Mirage

Stonard is the premier 4-star choice, specifically for players who purchase the Insider Podcast (Battle Pass). Its Crit Rate secondary stat makes building Lingyang’s Echoes significantly easier, allowing you to focus on Crit DMG sub-stats. The passive boost to Resonance Liberation DMG is also highly effective, as Lingyang’s ultimate accounts for a respectable portion of his total burst.

Hollow Mirage is a strong alternative for players who can maintain high uptime on its stacks. Since Lingyang spends much of his time in the air, he is naturally shielded from many melee-range ground attacks, making it easier to keep the "Iron Armor" stacks active. However, if you find yourself getting knocked out of the air frequently, the value of this weapon drops significantly.

### F2P and Budget Alternatives

For players looking for craftable or easily accessible options, Gauntlets 21D provides much-needed Energy Regeneration. While its passive is somewhat clunky to trigger during an aerial combo, the base stats help with ultimate uptime. If you are still in the early game, the 3-star Gauntlets of Night can serve as a placeholder due to its straightforward ATK% boost upon using an Intro Skill.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/lingyang-build-guide-wuthering-waves/inline-3.webp)


In Wuthering Waves, Lingyang’s power is dictated by his Echo configuration. As a Glacio DPS, his primary goal is to maximize Glacio DMG Bonus and Crit stats while ensuring he has enough Energy to transform into his "Striding Lion" state every rotation.

### Optimal Set: 5-Piece Freezing Frost

The 5-piece Freezing Frost set is non-negotiable for a high-end Lingyang build.
*   **2-Piece Effect:** Glacio DMG +10%.
*   **5-Piece Effect:** Upon using Basic Attack or Heavy Attack, Glacio DMG increases by an additional 10%, stacking up to 4 times for 15 seconds.

Since Lingyang’s entire aerial kit consists of rapid Basic Attacks, he can trigger and maintain the full 40% Glacio DMG bonus with ease. This set provides significantly more value than generic ATK sets like Lingering Tunes.

### Alternative Set: 5-Piece Lingering Tunes

If you have not yet farmed a good Freezing Frost set, Lingering Tunes is a viable temporary option. It provides a stacking ATK% buff and increases Outro Skill DMG. While Lingyang’s Outro Skill is not his main source of damage, the raw ATK% can help bridge the gap during the mid-game.

### Main Echo Choice: Lampylumen Myriad vs. Mech Abomination

The **Lampylumen Myriad** is the essential 4-cost Echo for Lingyang. When activated, it transforms Lingyang into the beast, performing three consecutive attacks. Each hit deals Glacio DMG and increases the current character's Glacio DMG and Resonance Skill DMG by 12% for 15 seconds. It also has a high chance to freeze non-boss enemies, providing valuable crowd control.

**Mech Abomination** is occasionally used in "quick-swap" teams for its massive ATK boost and coordinated attack, but for a dedicated Lingyang carry build, the Glacio buffs from Lampylumen Myriad are superior.

### The Premium Hypercarry Team: Lingyang + Sanhua + Verina

This is the gold standard for Lingyang players. Sanhua is the most important piece of this puzzle. Because Lingyang’s aerial attacks are classified as Basic Attack damage, Sanhua’s 38% Basic Attack DMG Deepen buff is a massive multiplicative multiplier. Verina rounds out the team by providing universal DMG buffs and ensuring Lingyang stays healthy while airborne.

### The Glacio Resonance Team: Lingyang + Zhezhi + Shorekeeper

This team focuses on maximizing Glacio-specific synergies. Zhezhi provides a 20% Glacio DMG Deepen and a 25% Resonance Skill DMG Deepen. While the Resonance Skill buff is less impactful for Lingyang than Sanhua's Basic Attack buff, Zhezhi’s personal damage is much higher. Shorekeeper replaces Verina here to provide superior Crit scaling, which is vital for Lingyang's high-frequency hits.

### F2P Friendly Team: Lingyang + Sanhua + Baizhi

Sanhua is given to all players for free via the login event, making her the perfect F2P partner. Baizhi serves as a reliable healer. While this team lacks the raw power of the Verina variant, it still follows the correct mechanical logic: Sanhua buffs Lingyang, and Baizhi keeps the team alive.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/lingyang-build-guide-wuthering-waves/inline-5.webp)


Lingyang’s rotation is more rigid than other characters because you must ensure his Resonance Liberation and Forte Circuit are synchronized.

### Step-by-Step Rotation Flowchart

1.  **Opening:** Start with Verina. Use her Resonance Skill and Liberation to trigger her buffs and the Rejuvenating Glow Echo set.
2.  **Buffing:** Swap to Sanhua. Perform her combo (Skill -> Liberation -> Heavy Attack) to build Concerto energy. Use her Outro Skill to swap into Lingyang.
3.  **Burst Window:**
    *   Cast Lingyang’s Resonance Liberation (**Strive: Lion's Vigor**) immediately.
    *   If the Forte gauge is full, use a Heavy Attack to enter the **Striding Lion** state.
    *   While in the air: Perform 4 Basic Attacks followed by the Resonance Skill (**Mountain Roamer**). Repeat this loop.
4.  **Reset:** Once the Striding Lion state ends, use Lingyang's Echo (Lampylumen Myriad) to bridge the gap while swapping back to your supports to restart the cycle.

### Building Lion's Spirit: Grounded Combos

If you start a fight without a full Forte gauge, use Lingyang's ground combo: Basic Attack 1-5 followed by the Resonance Skill "Ancient Arts." Using the Resonance Skill after the 3rd, 4th, or 5th Basic Attack allows you to skip animations and build gauge faster.

### The Burst Window: Resonance Liberation and Aerial Loop

It is vital to use your Resonance Liberation **before** entering the Striding Lion state. The ultimate increases Lingyang’s Glacio DMG and, crucially, extends the duration of the Striding Lion state to 10 seconds. If you enter the state without the ultimate, you only get 5 seconds of aerial combat, which is often not enough to justify the swap-in.

### Swap-Canceling Tips for Maximum Efficiency

Lingyang’s Resonance Liberation animation is somewhat long. You can "swap-cancel" this by pressing the Liberation button and immediately switching to another character to perform a quick skill, then switching back. However, be careful not to trigger an Outro Skill prematurely, as Lingyang needs to be the active character to enter his aerial state.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/lingyang-build-guide-wuthering-waves/inline-6.webp)


### Common Gameplay Questions

**How do I stay in the air longer?**
The "Striding Lion" state has a natural timer, but using the Resonance Skill "Mountain Roamer" pauses your descent. If you find yourself falling too early, ensure you are weaving the Skill into your combo after every 4th Basic Attack.

**What is the best F2P weapon?**
Gauntlets 21D is the best craftable option. If you have the "4-star Weapon Box" from early-game rewards, choosing the Gauntlets (Hollow Mirage) is a significant upgrade over craftables.

**Which skills should I prioritize leveling?**
1.  **Forte Circuit:** This scales your "Striding Lion" damage, which is your primary source of DPS.
2.  **Basic Attack:** Increases the damage of your ground and aerial kicks.
3.  **Resonance Liberation:** Increases the Glacio DMG buff provided during your burst window.
4.  **Resonance Skill:** Provides a smaller portion of overall damage.
5.  **Intro Skill:** Lowest priority.

### Lingyang vs. Other 5-Star DPS

**Is Lingyang better than Calcharo?**
Lingyang and Calcharo serve different purposes. Calcharo has higher AoE (Area of Effect) potential and a higher damage ceiling in "perfect" play scenarios. Lingyang, however, is much more mobile and suffers less from "boss mobility." If a boss jumps across the arena, Lingyang can usually stay attached to them, whereas Calcharo might lose his ultimate stacks.

**Lingyang vs. Jiyan?**
Jiyan is generally considered the stronger DPS due to his massive AoE and ease of use. However, Lingyang is a standard banner character, making him much easier to obtain and potentially gain Resonance Chains (duplicates) for over time. In Glacio-weak content, a well-built Lingyang can rival limited units.
