## Overview

![Overview](/img/piper-wheel-best-build-zzz/inline-1.webp)


Piper Wheel is an A-Rank Physical Anomaly Agent who specializes in rapidly accumulating Physical Anomaly Buildup to trigger the Assault effect. Unlike traditional Attack Agents who rely on critical hits, Piper’s damage scales primarily through Anomaly Proficiency, which increases the damage of the Assault proc itself. Her kit is designed around the "Power" stack mechanic, which significantly boosts her efficiency the longer she stays in the fight.

At Level 60, Piper possesses the following base stats:

| Stat | Base Value (Level 60) |
| :--- | :--- |
| HP | 6,976 |
| ATK | 683 |
| DEF | 612 |
| Anomaly Mastery | 116 |
| Anomaly Proficiency | 118 |
| Impact | 86 |
| Energy Regen | 1.2 |

### Role and Playstyle
Piper functions best as an on-field Anomaly DPS or a "Disorder" enabler. Her primary goal is to use her EX Special Attack: Engine Spin to hit enemies rapidly, building up both Physical Anomaly and Power stacks. Once an enemy is afflicted with Assault, they take a massive burst of Physical damage and have their Daze increased. In multi-element teams, Piper acts as the "closer" who triggers Disorder—a mechanic where applying a new Anomaly to an enemy already suffering from one causes an immediate, high-damage explosion.

### Core Passive: Power Charge
Piper’s Core Passive is the engine of her kit. Whenever her EX Special Attack or Ultimate hits an enemy, she gains stacks of "Power," up to a maximum of 20 stacks (increasing to 30 at Mindscape 1). Each stack of Power increases Piper’s Physical Anomaly Buildup Rate by 4%. At maximum stacks, she gains an 80% to 120% boost to her buildup speed, allowing her to trigger Assault almost instantly against most elite enemies.

### Additional Ability: Engine Overhaul
When Piper is paired with another Physical Agent or a member of the Sons of Calydon faction (such as Lucy or Caesar King), her Additional Ability activates. While she has 20 or more stacks of Power, the entire squad receives an 18% DMG increase for 12 seconds. This makes Piper not just a damage dealer, but a potent offensive support for her teammates.

### Top Choice: Roaring Ride
Roaring Ride is Piper’s signature W-Engine and remains her most consistent option. Its passive is tailor-made for her multi-hit EX Special Attack. Every 0.3 seconds that her spin hits an enemy, it randomly triggers one of three buffs: an ATK increase, an Anomaly Proficiency boost, or an Anomaly Buildup Rate increase. Because Piper hits so frequently, she can maintain all three buffs simultaneously throughout her rotation, maximizing both her raw damage and her speed of application.

### Premium Alternative: Fusion Compiler
While originally designed for Grace Howard, the Fusion Compiler is a top-tier stat stick for Piper. The high base ATK and PEN Ratio are beneficial, but the real draw is the passive that grants up to 75 Anomaly Proficiency (at R1) when using Special Attacks. Since Piper's gameplay involves holding her Special Attack for extended periods, she can easily maintain max stacks of this buff.

### Best F2P Options: Weeping Gemini & Rainforest Gourmet
For players looking for craftable or accessible options, Weeping Gemini is the gold standard for Anomaly Agents. In long boss fights, the stacking Anomaly Proficiency (up to 120-192 depending on Rank) significantly outscales other A-Rank options. Alternatively, Rainforest Gourmet is excellent if you find yourself struggling with energy, as it converts the energy Piper spends during her spin into a substantial ATK buff.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/piper-wheel-best-build-zzz/inline-3.webp)


Drive Disc selection for Piper is straightforward: you want to maximize the damage of the Assault proc and the speed at which you reach it.

### Recommended 4-Piece Sets
*   **Fanged Metal (4-Piece):** This is the definitive set for a main DPS Piper build. The 2-piece bonus provides a 10% Physical DMG boost. The 4-piece bonus is the star: whenever any squad member inflicts Assault, the wearer deals 35% additional DMG to that target for 12 seconds. Since Piper is usually the one triggering Assault, this buff has near-permanent uptime.
*   **Freedom Blues (4-Piece):** This set is preferred if Piper is playing a secondary role in a Disorder team. The 4-piece effect reduces the enemy’s Anomaly Buildup RES to the wearer’s attribute by 20% for 8 seconds after hitting them with an EX Special. This allows Piper to set up Physical Anomaly even faster, though it sacrifices her personal damage compared to Fanged Metal.

### Best 2-Piece Set Options
*   **Freedom Blues (2-Piece):** Provides +30 Anomaly Proficiency. This is the best secondary set for any Piper build.
*   **Hormone Punk (2-Piece):** Provides a 10% ATK boost, which increases the scaling of the Assault proc.
*   **Swing Jazz (2-Piece):** Increases Energy Regeneration by 20%. This is highly recommended for players who want to use Piper’s EX Special spin more frequently.

### Main Stat and Substat Optimization
To reach the endgame requirements for Piper, you should aim for specific main stats on your 4, 5, and 6 slots.

## Team Comp

![Team Comp](/img/piper-wheel-best-build-zzz/inline-4.webp)


Piper excels in teams that can either amplify her Physical damage or provide a second element for Disorder procs.

### The Sons of Calydon Meta Team
*   **Anomaly:** Piper Wheel
*   **Support:** Lucy
*   **Defense/Support:** Caesar King
*   **Bangboo:** Bangvolver

This is currently Piper’s strongest team. Caesar King provides a massive shield and an ATK buff that prevents Piper from being interrupted during her spin. Lucy provides additional ATK buffs through her boars and triggers Piper’s "Engine Overhaul" passive. Bangvolver is the preferred Bangboo as it contributes significantly to Physical Anomaly buildup.

### The Disorder Explosion Team
*   **Anomaly:** Piper Wheel
*   **Anomaly:** Grace Howard or Burnice White
*   **Support:** Rina or Lucy
*   **Bangboo:** Magnetiboo or Rocketboo

In this composition, you use Grace (Electric) or Burnice (Fire) to apply their respective Attribute Anomaly first. Once the enemy is Shocked or Burned, you switch to Piper and use her EX Special to trigger Assault. This triggers "Disorder," dealing a massive chunk of damage based on the remaining duration of the previous Anomaly. Magnetiboo is useful here for grouping enemies so Piper can hit multiple targets at once.

### F2P and Budget Friendly Teams
*   **Anomaly:** Piper Wheel
*   **Stun:** Anby Demara
*   **Support:** Nicole Demara
*   **Bangboo:** Bagboo

This team utilizes the free characters provided at the start of the game. Anby serves to Daze the enemy, creating a window for Piper to spin without fear of retaliation. Nicole’s EX Special groups enemies and shreds their DEF, making Piper’s Assault procs hit significantly harder.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/piper-wheel-best-build-zzz/inline-5.webp)


Mastering Piper requires understanding her "Quick Launch" mechanic and managing her energy effectively.

### Optimal Combat Combo
1.  **Initiate:** Start with your Support or Stun Agent to build Daze or apply an initial element.
2.  **Quick Launch:** Switch to Piper. Perform three Basic Attacks or a Dash Attack.
3.  **Engine Spin:** Immediately hold the Special Attack button after the third hit or dash. This skips the slow wind-up of her spin, entering the high-speed "Engine Spin" phase instantly.
4.  **EX Finish:** Continue holding until you have reached max Power stacks or are low on energy, then release to perform the "Really Heavy" downward smash. This move has high burst damage and is invulnerable during the animation.

### Managing Power Stacks
You must keep Piper’s Power stacks above 20 to maintain the 18% team DMG buff. If you are at Mindscape 1, aim for 30 stacks to maximize your Anomaly Buildup Rate. In combat, you can check the number of stacks via the icon above Piper’s health bar. If stacks are falling off, a quick tap of her EX Special or using her Ultimate is the fastest way to refresh the duration.

### Triggering Disorder Efficiently
When playing a Disorder team, do not stay on Piper too long. Once the Disorder explosion occurs, the previous Anomaly is consumed. You should immediately switch back to your other Anomaly Agent (like Grace or Burnice) to re-apply their element before switching back to Piper to "detonate" it again.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/piper-wheel-best-build-zzz/inline-6.webp)


**Is Piper Wheel better than Jane Doe?**
Jane Doe is an S-Rank Physical Anomaly Agent and generally has higher ceiling damage and better mobility. However, Piper remains a top-tier choice due to her superior AoE capabilities and the fact that she provides a team-wide DMG buff, which Jane does not. Piper is often the better choice for content with many small enemies, while Jane excels in single-target boss fights.

**What is the best Mindscape for Piper?**
Mindscape 1 (M1) is her most important upgrade, as it increases the Power stack limit to 30 and gives her a 50% chance to gain extra stacks. Mindscape 6 (M6) is also significant, as it increases the maximum duration of her EX Special spin by 2 seconds, allowing for even more Anomaly buildup in a single window.

**Does Piper need Crit stats?**
No. Attribute Anomaly damage (Assault) cannot crit. While her physical hits (the actual slashes of the axe) can crit, they represent a small fraction of her total damage output. Investing in Anomaly Proficiency and ATK% will always yield better results than Crit Rate or Crit DMG.

**How much Anomaly Proficiency does Piper need?**
For endgame content like Shiyu Defense (Ambush), you should aim for at least 350 Anomaly Proficiency. Optimally, a well-built Piper with a signature W-Engine and Level 15 Drive Discs should sit between 400 and 450 Anomaly Proficiency.
