1.  **Knee-to-Hip Alignment:** To prevent lower back strain, your knees should be slightly higher than your hips. If your chair's lowest setting still leaves your feet dangling or barely touching the floor, a footrest is not optional—it is essential for spinal health.

## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/best-ergonomic-footrests-for-wfh-gacha-grinders/inline-4.webp)


Not everyone needs a footrest, and in some cases, adding one can actually cause harm.
*   **Natural Alignment:** If your feet already rest flat on the floor with your knees at a 90-degree angle, adding a footrest may push your knees too high, potentially introducing hip impingement or reducing circulation to your lower legs.
*   **Standing Desks:** If you use a standing desk for more than 70% of your session, a standard footrest is a tripping hazard rather than an ergonomic aid. In this case, an anti-fatigue mat is a better investment.
*   **High-End Ergonomic Chairs:** If you have a chair with a deep seat pan and a waterfall edge that already supports your thighs fully, you may only need a very thin mat to protect against cold floors rather than a 5-inch riser.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can a footrest help with lower back pain during long gaming sessions?
Yes, a footrest helps by allowing you to sit all the way back in your chair, which engages the lumbar support. By lifting your feet, you reduce the pressure on your hamstrings and help maintain the natural curve of your spine.

### Should I choose foam or plastic for my footrest?
Foam is generally better for comfort and barefoot use, as it provides a softer surface for your arches. Plastic is superior if you want mechanical adjustability, such as specific tilt angles or multiple height increments that foam cannot provide.

### How do I know what height footrest I need?
Sit in your office chair with your back against the lumbar support. Measure the distance from the back of your knee to the floor. If your feet don't reach comfortably while maintaining a 90-degree bend, the gap between your feet and the floor is the minimum height your footrest should provide.

### Is a rocking footrest better than a stationary one?
Rocking footrests are excellent for improving circulation. If you find your legs feel restless or "heavy" after several hours of farming Anomalies in Neverness to Everness, a rocking model like the Mount-It! MI-7802 can help keep your blood flowing.
