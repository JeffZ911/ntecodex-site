## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the most important piece of gear for hitting high breakpoints?
While many focus on the mouse for input, the monitor is arguably more important for high-level optimization. A high-color-accuracy screen like the LG UltraGear allows you to quickly identify gear stats and rarity colors, reducing eye strain during long inventory management sessions.

### Is a 12-button mouse necessary for Neverness to Everness?
It is not strictly necessary, but it is highly recommended for players who want to manage shops and car customization while simultaneously handling combat. Mapping these shortcuts to your thumb allows for a much smoother transition between the game's various urban systems.

### Why choose a 1440p monitor over a 4K one for gacha gaming?
1440p is currently the "sweet spot" for performance and clarity. It allows you to maintain high frame rates (144Hz+) even on mid-range hardware, which is essential for the fast-paced Esper combat and driving mechanics of NTE without requiring a top-of-the-line GPU.

### How does the Herman Miller Embody help with long-term gaming?
