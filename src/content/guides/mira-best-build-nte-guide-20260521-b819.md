## Overview

Mira is a high-mobility Anima-element DPS known for her rapid-fire dual blade attacks and exceptional field presence. The core of Mira's gameplay is her 'Minty Whirlwind' (Hold Basic Attack). This mechanic allows her to enter a sustained spinning state, dealing continuous AoE Anima damage while moving freely. ### Mira's Role in the Meta

Since the official launch of Neverness to Everness on April 29, 2026, Mira has emerged as the most reliable A-Rank DPS for tackling the Anomaly Zones. Her Anima alignment allows her to bypass the physical resistances of many robotic enemies found in the city's industrial sectors. While S-Rank units might offer higher single-hit numbers, Mira’s hit frequency is so high that she triggers elemental reactions faster than almost any other character currently in the roster.

She functions best as a Main DPS who demands significant field time. However, her "Burst Carry" potential shines when her Cycle Gauge is full, allowing her to unleash her Ultimate, 'Thunderous Whirlwind Slash,' which converts her physical strikes into pure Anima energy for a limited duration.

### Skill Priority and Leveling

To maximize Mira's efficiency, focus on her Basic Attack and Skill first, as these comprise the bulk of her active combat time.

1.  **Basic Attack (Minty Whirlwind):** Priority 1. This is your primary source of damage and Cycle Gauge generation.
2.  **Skill (Super Claws):** Priority 2. This provides the necessary gap-closer and burst damage to finish off elite targets.
3.  **Ultimate (Thunderous Whirlwind Slash):** Priority 3. While powerful, its effectiveness is gated by energy costs and cooldowns.
4.  **Passive/Dodge (Critical Riposte):** Priority 4. Vital for high-level play, but the damage scaling is secondary to the utility of the cooldown reset.

### Fluff of Fleetness (S-Rank)
This is Mira's signature weapon and her absolute best-in-slot option. The high Crit DMG secondary stat perfectly complements Mira’s multi-hit kit. Since 'Minty Whirlwind' hits enemies multiple times per second, Mira can reach the maximum 10 stacks of the ATK buff almost instantly. The 20% Anima DMG bonus at max stacks provides a massive scaling boost that keeps her competitive with S-Rank damage dealers.

### Mind Royale (A-Rank)
For F2P players or those who haven't pulled an S-Rank dual blade, Mind Royale is the premier choice. It addresses one of Mira’s few weaknesses: her Ultimate Energy generation. By restoring energy upon skill use, Mind Royale ensures Mira can use 'Thunderous Whirlwind Slash' more frequently during a single encounter.

### Clear Skies (A-Rank)
Clear Skies is a specialized utility weapon. While it offers less raw damage than Mind Royale, the boost to Redirect Skill potency makes Mira much harder to kill in high-difficulty content. The movement speed bonus also synergizes with her "always moving" playstyle.

In Neverness to Everness, Disks (also known as Cartridges) provide the most significant boost to a character's endgame power. For Mira, the goal is to maximize her Anima DMG and her ability to crit on her frequent small hits.

### 4-Piece Set: Fireflies and the Forest
This is the definitive set for Mira. The 2-piece bonus provides a flat increase to Anima DMG, while the 4-piece bonus is tailor-made for her kit.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** Every time an Anima hit deals damage, the character gains a 2% Crit DMG buff for 5 seconds, stacking up to 15 times.
Because Mira’s 'Minty Whirlwind' and 'Super Claws' hit so many times, she maintains maximum stacks of this buff with 100% uptime, effectively giving her a permanent 30% Crit DMG boost during combat.

### 2-Piece Hybrid: Fireflies + Lost Radiance
If you are still farming for a perfect 4-piece set, combining two pieces of 'Fireflies and the Forest' with two pieces of 'Lost Radiance' is a strong transition build. 'Lost Radiance' provides a 15% ATK boost, which helps stabilize Mira's damage if your Crit Rate is still low.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/mira-best-build-nte-guide-20260521-b819/inline-5.webp)


To get the most out of Mira, you must follow a specific rotation that ensures all buffs are active before she takes the field.

### Step-by-Step Rotation Flowchart

1.  **Opener (Jiuyuan):** Start the fight with Jiuyuan’s Skill. This groups the enemies into a tight cluster and applies the 'Lethal Rose Pact' vulnerability.
2.  **Setup (Sakiri):** Immediately switch to Sakiri. Unleash her Ultimate to grant the 30% Team ATK buff. If her Skill is available, use it to ensure the enemies remain grouped.
3.  **Reaction Trigger (Esper Zero):** Swap to Esper Zero. Use her Skill to hit the grouped enemies. This will interact with the Anima marks left by Jiuyuan or prepare the field for Mira, triggering 'Blossom' and filling your Cycle Gauge.
4.  **Burst Window (Mira):** Swap to Mira. Immediately use 'Super Claws' to gap-close and trigger her initial damage buffs. Follow up by holding the Basic Attack button to initiate 'Minty Whirlwind.'
5.  **Ultimate Execution:** Once the Cycle Gauge is full and the enemies are still debuffed, unleash 'Thunderous Whirlwind Slash.'
6.  **Maintenance:** If an enemy attempts to counterattack, use Mira’s 'Critical Riposte' (Perfect Dodge + Basic Attack). This will reset the cooldown of 'Super Claws,' allowing you to restart your burst sequence immediately.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/mira-best-build-nte-guide-20260521-b819/inline-6.webp)


### Is Mira (Mint) worth building?
Yes. Mira is widely considered one of the most efficient A-Rank DPS units in Neverness to Everness. Her ease of use, combined with the high power of the Anima element in the early 2026 patches, makes her a safe investment for both new and veteran players.

### What is the best F2P weapon for her?
Mind Royale is highly recommended. It is easily obtainable through the standard weapon banner or the in-game shop and provides the energy regeneration Mira needs to maintain her Ultimate uptime.

### How do I trigger her Critical Riposte?
You must perform a 'Critical Dodge' (a perfect dodge timed just before an enemy hit lands) and then immediately press the Basic Attack button. If timed correctly, Mira will perform a high-damage counter-slash that resets her Skill cooldown.

### Which Awakening level is most important?
Awakening Level 1 (A1) is her most crucial milestone. It allows her 'Super Claws' skill to reset upon a Critical Riposte or a kill, which significantly increases her mobility and DPS uptime during mob-heavy encounters.
