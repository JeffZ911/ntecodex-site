## Overview

![Overview](/img/neverness-to-everness-daily-routine-guide/inline-1.webp)


Nanally arrived with the version 1.0 launch of Neverness to Everness as the definitive S-Rank Anima (Plasma) DPS. Her gameplay loop is distinct from other damage dealers due to her reliance on the Underboss, a spectral summon that mirrors her movements and executes follow-up attacks. Unlike traditional melee characters, Nanally utilizes wall-running and verticality to reposition while the Underboss maintains constant pressure on the ground.

Building Nanally is a long-term commitment that forms the backbone of a player's daily activities. Because she scales heavily with Crit Rate and Anima DMG, your daily routine must prioritize the acquisition of high-tier Modules (Disks) and weapon upgrade materials. Efficiently spending your Character Pixels (the game's stamina system) is the difference between a mediocre Nanally and one that can clear endgame Anomaly Zones with ease.

### Who is Nanally?

Nanally is the leader of the Ichi-daime, a faction within Hethereau that blends urban street culture with high-stakes supernatural combat. In the meta, she functions as a "Hyper-Carry." This means the entire team is usually built to buff her personal damage rather than contributing significant off-field DPS. Her "Ichi-daime's Authority" state transforms her basic attacks into multi-hit Plasma strikes, making her exceptionally effective at breaking enemy shields.

Nanally’s weapon category is known as Arcs. Her performance fluctuates wildly based on the passive effects of her equipped Arc, specifically those that enhance follow-up attack damage or Basic Attack speed.

### Signature Weapon: Ready-Ready

Ready-Ready is Nanally's signature S-Rank Arc, specifically designed to synergize with her "Ichi-daime's Authority" state. Its primary passive, "Commander Tiger Talisman," triggers whenever Nanally uses her Ultimate. This effect grants a massive boost to Basic Attack damage and increases the attack frequency of the Underboss. If you are looking to maximize her ceiling, this weapon is non-negotiable.

If you missed the signature weapon banner, the best alternative is the "Fluff of Fortitude." Available through the premium tier of the Battle Pass, this Arc provides a universal damage multiplier. ## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/neverness-to-everness-daily-routine-guide/inline-3.webp)


In Neverness to Everness, the artifact system is comprised of Modules, often referred to by the community as "Disks." These items fit into a Tetris-like grid, where the shape and placement of the block determine the bonuses you receive. For Nanally, you are looking for specific shapes that allow you to fit the "Fireflies and the Forest" set.

### Recommended Set: Fireflies and the Forest

The "Fireflies and the Forest" 4-piece set is the undisputed Best-in-Slot (BiS) for Nanally. 
- **2-Piece Bonus:** Increases Anima DMG by 10%.
- **4-Piece Bonus:** After using a Skill, increases Crit DMG by [Information unavailable]% for 12 seconds. This effect can stack twice.

This set is specifically tuned for Nanally’s "Colucci Howling Technique," which she uses frequently to refresh her combat state.

### Optimal Module Main Stats

Modules are divided into three types: Cartridges, Modules, and Blocks. Each has a specific pool of potential main stats.

1. **Cartridge (Offensive Slot):** Anima DMG Bonus is the priority here. If you cannot find an Anima DMG Cartridge, ATK% is a temporary substitute.
2. **Module (Utility Slot):** Crit Rate or Crit DMG. You should aim for a Crit Rate Module until you reach at least a 70% Crit Rate through substats, then swap to Crit DMG.
3. **Block (Defensive/Hybrid Slot):** ATK%. While this slot can roll HP or DEF, Nanally needs every bit of ATK to ensure the Underboss’s hits are impactful.

### Substat Priority and Breakpoints

Farming for the perfect "Tetris" shapes is difficult, so prioritize the correct substats first.
- **Priority 1:** Crit Rate (Target 70%).
- **Priority 2:** Crit DMG (Target 160%+).
- **Priority 3:** ATK%.
- **Priority 4:** Universal DMG Bonus.

Nanally cannot carry alone; she requires supports that can group enemies and trigger elemental reactions. The most powerful reaction for her is "Blossom," which occurs when Anima (Nanally) interacts with Cosmos elements.

### The Blossom Reaction Team (Meta)

The current gold standard for Nanally teams involves Sakiri and a Cosmos-element flex slot (usually Zero or [Hotori](/characters/hotori-best-team-nte)). 
- **Sakiri (S-Rank Support):** Sakiri is essential. Her Skill pulls enemies into a tight cluster, allowing Nanally’s Underboss to hit multiple targets simultaneously. Her Ultimate also provides a 30% ATK buff that lasts long enough for Nanally’s entire burst window.
- **Zero/Hotori (Cosmos Support):** These characters apply the Cosmos status off-field. When Nanally hits these enemies with Anima damage, it triggers the Blossom reaction, dealing continuous area-of-effect damage.

If you prefer a more straightforward playstyle, a Triple-Anima team utilizing Nanally, Jiuyuan, and a healer is viable. Jiuyuan provides "Anima Resonance," which increases the team’s Anima DMG by a flat percentage. This team lacks the grouping capabilities of Sakiri but offers much higher single-target shred for boss fights.

### F2P Friendly Team Alternatives

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/neverness-to-everness-daily-routine-guide/inline-5.webp)


Understanding the combat flow is vital. Nanally’s damage is back-loaded, meaning her strongest hits come at the end of her combo and during her summoned Underboss's active duration.

### Opening Buff Sequence

Always start the encounter with your supports. 
1. Switch to **Sakiri**. Use her Skill to gather the enemies.
2. Immediately use **Sakiri’s Ultimate**. This triggers the team-wide ATK buff and ensures that the enemies are debuffed before Nanally takes the field.
3. If using a Cosmos character like **Zero**, tap their Skill to apply the Cosmos element to the grouped enemies.

### The Underboss Burst Window

Once the buffs are active, swap to Nanally.
1. Use Nanally’s **Redirect Skill (Colucci Howling Technique)**. This puts her into the "Ichi-daime's Authority" state and positions her for a wall-run if necessary.
2. Activate **Nanally’s Ultimate**. This summons the Underboss. If you have "Ready-Ready" equipped, the "Commander Tiger Talisman" effect will now be active.
3. Begin the Basic Attack combo.

### Managing Ichi-daime's Authority Uptime

Do not just mash the attack button. You must pay attention to the 5th hit of her combo. The 5th hit is a finisher that deals massive Break damage. Additionally, if an enemy prepares a heavy attack, use Nanally’s **Critical Riposte (Caramel Crisp)**. This move not only negates the incoming damage but also triggers an immediate follow-up from the Underboss, shredding the enemy's Break meter instantly.

**Step-by-Step Combat Rotation Flowchart**
- **Step 1:** Support Skill (Group Enemies).
- **Step 2:** Support Ultimate (Buff ATK).
- **Step 3:** Swap to Nanally -> Redirect Skill.
- **Step 4:** Nanally Ultimate (Summon Underboss).
- **Step 5:** 5-Hit Basic Combo -> Critical Riposte (as needed).

### Where to Farm Nanally Materials?

Nanally’s progression materials are split between open-world exploration and Anomaly Zones.

Investing your daily resources into her build ensures you can participate in high-level "Anomaly Containment" events, which are the primary source of premium currency.
