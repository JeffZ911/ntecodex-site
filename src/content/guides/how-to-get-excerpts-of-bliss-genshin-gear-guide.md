Selecting gear for a gacha-heavy lifestyle requires looking beyond standard "gaming" benchmarks like high polling rates or RGB lighting. We prioritized ergonomic data from [r/ergonomics](https://www.reddit.com/r/ergonomics/) and [RTINGS](https://www.rtings.com/) to ensure gear can handle 6+ hour sessions without causing physical fatigue.

Monitors were selected based on DCI-P3 color gamut coverage to match the vibrant art styles of modern gacha titles. Games like NTE and Genshin Impact rely heavily on color-coded visual cues for their elemental systems and supernatural effects, making color accuracy more important than extreme 360Hz refresh rates.

Audio picks focused on 'breathability' and weight to prevent ear fatigue and 'hot ears' during long exploration loops. We specifically looked for headsets that offer fabric or mesh earcups, as these dissipate heat better than the leatherette found on budget models. Finally, mice were evaluated on their macro capabilities and switch durability, as farming often involves thousands of repetitive clicks that can wear down inferior mechanical switches.

## What a long-session player actually needs

![What a long-session player actually needs](/img/how-to-get-excerpts-of-bliss-genshin-gear-guide/inline-2.webp)


When your gameplay loop involves traversing massive open worlds, your needs shift toward endurance and comfort.

*   **Lumbar Support:** Fixed-back chairs lead to slouching during long grinds; active or adjustable lumbar is non-negotiable for spinal health. Without proper support, the lower back begins to round, leading to long-term issues like sciatica or chronic tension.
*   **High Refresh Rate with Color Accuracy:** 1440p at 144Hz+ is the 'sweet spot' for PC gacha gaming. It balances sharp visuals with smooth movement, which is particularly beneficial in NTE when driving vehicles at high speeds through the city.
*   **Multi-Device Connectivity:** Headsets with simultaneous Bluetooth allow players to follow guides or watch videos while farming without losing game audio. This is vital when searching for hidden collectibles like the Excerpts of Bliss where a video guide is often running on a secondary device.

## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/how-to-get-excerpts-of-bliss-genshin-gear-guide/inline-4.webp)


Not every player needs a $2,000 setup to enjoy exploration-heavy games.

If you already own a high-quality ergonomic office chair (like a Steelcase or Herman Miller), do not switch to a 'gaming' chair; you already have superior support. Most gaming chairs are modeled after bucket seats in racing cars, which are designed to hold you in place during high G-force turns, not to provide ergonomic support for typing or clicking.

If you primarily play on mobile or tablet, high-end PC monitors and MMO mice will offer zero benefit to your gameplay loop. In this case, investing in a high-quality power bank or a dedicated mobile controller like the Backbone One would be a much better use of your budget. Additionally, don't upgrade your headset if your current one is comfortable; audio quality is secondary to physical comfort for exploration-heavy games where you are often listening to ambient wind or background music rather than critical footsteps.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I get Excerpts of Bliss in Genshin Impact?
Excerpts of Bliss are collected in the Simulanka region by finding Joyous Letters and opening various chests scattered across the map. These items act as a progression currency for the "Summertide Lore and Tales" event, allowing players to unlock rewards such as Kirara's character outfit.

### Is a gaming chair better than an office chair for long sessions?
Generally, a high-end ergonomic office chair provides better spinal support than a budget gaming chair. However, premium gaming chairs like the <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Secretlab+TITAN+Evo&tag=jeffzen911-20" rel="sponsored nofollow noopener" target="_blank">Secretlab TITAN Evo</a> have integrated adjustable lumbar systems that rival mid-range office chairs, making them a viable choice for those who prefer the aesthetic.

### Why is 1440p considered the best resolution for gacha games?
1440p offers a significantly sharper image than 1080p without the extreme hardware requirements of 4K. This allows players to maintain high frame rates even on mid-range GPUs, which is essential for smooth camera movement while exploring large open worlds like those in Neverness to Everness.

### Do monitor light bars actually reduce eye strain?
Yes, by illuminating the area behind and in front of your monitor, light bars like the BenQ ScreenBar Halo reduce the contrast between the screen and the surrounding environment. This prevents your pupils from constantly dilating and contracting, which is the primary cause of eye fatigue during night sessions.
