# NTE Daily Tasks Guide: Best Nanally Build for Efficient Farming

The daily gameplay loop in Neverness to Everness (NTE) is designed to reward consistent progression through urban exploration, combat challenges, and city management. To maximize your efficiency, especially when farming for high-tier resources, having a specialized character build is essential. [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) stands out as the premier choice for daily clearing due to her unique mobility options and high-impact area-of-effect (AoE) damage. This guide covers the essential daily tasks you must complete and the optimal Nanally build to streamline your farming process.

## Overview

![Overview](/img/nte-daily-tasks-guide-nanally-build/inline-1.webp)


In Neverness to Everness, account progression is tied directly to your daily activity. Because the game features a vast urban environment, the time spent traveling between objectives can add up. Efficient players focus on a specific checklist to ensure no resources are left on the table while using high-mobility characters like Nanally to minimize downtime.

The daily routine centers on several key systems: the Exploration Guide, City Tycoon management, and Character Pixel (stamina) expenditure. Completing these tasks consistently provides the materials needed to upgrade Arcs, Cartridges, and character levels, which are the primary drivers of your power level.

### Why Nanally is the Best Character for Dailies

Nanally is widely considered the "queen of efficiency" for daily tasks for two main reasons: her anti-gravity mobility and her burst damage profile. 

1.  **Mobility:** Nanally’s ability to navigate the verticality of Hethereau without relying solely on traditional climbing or vehicles allows her to reach Participation Task locations faster than almost any other character. Her movement skill ignores many of the environmental obstacles that slow down other units.
2.  **AoE Burst:** Most daily combat encounters involve waves of smaller "Broken" enemies. Nanally’s Anima-based attacks cover a wide radius, allowing her to wipe out entire groups in a single rotation. This turns what could be a three-minute combat encounter into a ten-second clear, which is vital when you are running multiple bounty missions or farming world bosses.

## Team Comp

![Team Comp](/img/nte-daily-tasks-guide-nanally-build/inline-4.webp)


Nanally performs best when paired with characters who can trigger the "Esper Cycle" reactions. In NTE, combining different elements creates explosive effects that can clear entire rooms.

### The Premium Blossom Team

The most powerful team for Nanally currently revolves around the **Blossom** reaction. This is triggered by combining the **Anima** (Nanally) and **Cosmos** elements.

*   **Nanally (Main DPS / Anima):** Provides the bulk of the damage and the Anima application.
*   **Zero (Sub-DPS / Cosmos):** Zero is essential for triggering Blossom. His off-field Cosmos application ensures that every time Nanally attacks, an explosion occurs.
*   **[Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) (Support / Incantation):** Sakiri provides the utility needed for fast clears. Her skill, "Devour Whole," groups enemies into a tight cluster, making Nanally’s AoE much more effective. Her Ultimate also provides a team-wide ATK buff.

For players with a limited roster, a balanced team of Nanally, Zero, and **Haniel** (Psyche) is highly effective. Haniel provides necessary healing and defensive utility, which is helpful if you are tackling higher-level world bosses or anomalies before your gear is fully optimized.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/nte-daily-tasks-guide-nanally-build/inline-5.webp)


To get the most out of Nanally’s kit during your daily tasks, you should follow a specific combat rotation. This ensures all buffs are active before Nanally enters her primary damage state.

### Standard Combat Rotation

1.  **Setup with Zero:** Start the encounter by using Zero’s Skill. This applies the Cosmos element to enemies and primes the Esper Meter.
2.  **Crowd Control with Sakiri:** Switch to Sakiri and use her "Devour Whole" skill to pull all enemies into a single point. If her Ultimate is available, cast it now to trigger the 30% ATK buff for the team.
3.  **Nanally’s Entry:** Switch to Nanally and use her Skill, **Colucci Howling Technique**. This allows her to enter the **Ichi-daime's Authority** state, enhancing her basic attacks and increasing her resistance to interruption.
4.  **The Finisher:** Cast Nanally’s Ultimate to summon the Underboss. While the Underboss is active, spam Nanally’s basic attacks. The Underboss will perform coordinated follow-up attacks that deal massive Anima damage in a wide area.

### Daily Farming Optimization

When moving between multiple small encounters in the open world, you may not need to use your full rotation. For standard "Participation Task" mobs, simply using Sakiri’s grouping skill followed by Nanally’s enhanced basic attacks is usually enough to clear the wave. Save your Ultimates for "Elite" anomalies or world bosses to ensure you always have the energy when it matters most.

### Managing Ichi-daime's Authority

Nanally’s power is heavily concentrated in her "Authority" state. Keep an eye on the gauge above her health bar. If the gauge is low, switch to your sub-DPS or support characters to generate energy particles before initiating the next fight. Proper energy management can shave minutes off your total daily farming time.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/nte-daily-tasks-guide-nanally-build/inline-6.webp)


**What time is the Neverness to Everness daily reset?**
The daily reset for all servers occurs at 5:00 AM local server time. This is when your Participation Tasks refresh and your City Energy is replenished.

**How do I unlock the Participation Tasks?**
You must complete the main story prologue and reach the first major hub in Hethereau. Once done, the Exploration Guide menu will unlock, allowing you to track and complete daily tasks for rewards.

**Can I farm S-Rank Arcs for free?**
Yes. While some S-Rank Arcs are exclusive to the gacha banners, others, such as those dropped by the Headless Rider and other world bosses, can be farmed by spending Character Pixels.

**What is the best way to spend Character Pixels daily?**
Your priority should generally follow this order: Character Level materials > Weapon Level materials > Skill Upgrade materials. Once your main team is maxed out, you can shift your focus to farming high-tier Cartridges.

**Is Nanally worth pulling for F2P players?**
Nanally is currently one of the most valuable pulls in the game. Her high damage ceiling and unmatched utility in world exploration make her an excellent investment for both new and veteran players.
