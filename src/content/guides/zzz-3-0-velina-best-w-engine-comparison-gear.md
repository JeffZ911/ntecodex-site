# ZZZ 3.0 Velina Best W-Engine Comparison: The Ultimate Build Guide

**Quick Answer:** The Joyau Dore is the undisputed best-in-slot W-Engine for Velina, offering a massive 70 Anomaly Proficiency boost and a team-wide buff. For players seeking budget alternatives, Rainforest Gourmet and Boisterous Echoes provide excellent stacking bonuses that synergize with her high-frequency cyclone attacks.

As urban supernatural RPGs like Neverness to Everness continue to expand their rosters in 2026, Zenless Zone Zero has introduced its most complex Anomaly unit yet. Velina Airgid, the head of the External Strategy Department, specializes in dealing sustained Wind DMG and triggering the Vortex status effect. This comparison analyzes the top gear options to ensure you maximize her sub-DPS potential during marathon farming sessions.

### Joyau Dore (S-Rank)
*   **Best for:** Maximum Anomaly damage and team utility.
*   **Key Stats:** +70 Anomaly Proficiency.
*   **Effect:** Increases Vortex and Windswept DMG by 7% for 40 seconds when an EX Special Attack deals Wind DMG. At two stacks, all squad members gain 60 Anomaly Proficiency.
*   **Verdict:** This is Velina's signature W-Engine and is designed to fix her energy regeneration issues while providing the highest possible damage ceiling. The team-wide AP buff makes it essential for high-end Abloom nuke compositions.

### Bellicose Blaze (S-Rank)
*   **Best for:** Raw Wind DMG output.
*   **Key Stats:** High Base ATK and Wind DMG Bonus.
*   **Effect:** Provides a flat increase to Elemental DMG and enhances the Sweeping Cyclone multipliers.
*   **Verdict:** While it lacks the utility of Joyau Dore, Bellicose Blaze is a powerful alternative for players who want Velina to carry more of the direct damage load rather than relying solely on Anomaly procs.

### Boisterous Echoes (S-Rank/F2P)
*   **Best for:** Free-to-play players and accessibility.
*   **Key Stats:** Energy Regen and Anomaly Proficiency.
*   **Effect:** Increases Anomaly buildup when Basic Attacks hit enemies.
*   **Verdict:** Labeled as the primary F2P option in the 3.0 update, this W-Engine allows for consistent Cyclone uptime without requiring premium Signal Searches.

### Rainforest Gourmet (A-Rank)
*   **Best for:** Energy-efficient ATK stacking.
*   **Key Stats:** +30 Anomaly Proficiency.
*   **Effect:** For every 10 energy consumed, the equipper gains a 2.5% ATK buff for 10 seconds, stacking up to 10 times.
*   **Verdict:** This is the most reliable A-Rank option for Velina. Since her kit revolves around summoning cyclones through EX Special Attacks, she spends energy frequently enough to maintain high stacks.

### Weeping Gemini (A-Rank)
*   **Best for:** Long-duration boss fights.
*   **Key Stats:** Anomaly Proficiency.
*   **Effect:** Increases Anomaly Proficiency each time a squad member triggers an Attribute Anomaly.
*   **Verdict:** Testing indicates that at R5, Weeping Gemini achieves roughly 90% of the performance of Velina's signature S-Rank weapon, making it a stellar fallback for dedicated grinders.

## What a Velina build actually needs

![What a Velina build actually needs](/img/zzz-3-0-velina-best-w-engine-comparison-gear/inline-2.webp)


Optimizing Velina requires balancing three core pillars of her kit: Anomaly Proficiency, Energy Management, and Team Synergy.

*   **Anomaly Proficiency (AP):** This is the single most important stat for Velina. It determines the damage of her Vortex and Abloom effects. High AP allows her to shred enemy Attribute Anomaly Resistances by up to 14%, creating massive openings for main DPS units.
*   **Vortex and Wind Energy:** Velina's gameplay loop involves accumulating 90 Wind Energy to activate her Gale Field. W-Engines with Energy Regen substats are highly valued because they ensure she can summon Sweeping Cyclones more frequently.
*   **Attribute Infusion:** One of Velina's unique strengths is her ability to infuse her cyclones with other attributes. This makes her the perfect partner for Anomaly characters like Promeia or Grace, as she can help trigger multiple Disorder effects in a single rotation.
