# Neverness to Everness Best F2P Build Guide: Characters, Arcs, and Teams

## Overview

![Overview](/img/best-f2p-build-neverness-to-everness-guide/inline-1.webp)


The free-to-play (F2P) experience in Neverness to Everness is surprisingly robust, offering a path to high-tier power without requiring a single pull on the limited banners. Unlike many contemporary gacha titles, NTE provides a full, viable team through story progression and side activities like the City Tycoon management sim. To succeed as an F2P player, you must master the "Elemental Lane" system—a hexagonal reaction wheel that dictates how your characters interact during combat.

At the heart of any F2P build is the **Esper Cycle**. This mechanic rewards you for swapping characters when their portraits "glow," triggering unique Esper Skills that lead into powerful elemental reactions. For those starting their journey, the game provides a solid foundation with **Zero (the MC)**, **Mint**, **Adler**, and **Haniel**, eventually culminating in the acquisition of **Chiz**, an S-Rank powerhouse who serves as the cornerstone of the best F2P damage build.

### Understanding the Esper Cycle

The Elemental Lane consists of six core attributes: **Cosmos, Anima, Incantation, Chaos, Psyche, and Lakshana**. Reactions only occur between neighboring elements on the wheel. For example, swapping from a Cosmos character to an Anima character triggers **Blossom**, while moving from Anima to Incantation triggers **Hex**. Mastering these "Duo Cycles" and the advanced "Trio Cycles" is essential for maximizing your DPS.

### The Power of Free S-Rank Characters

You start with Zero, an S-Rank Cosmos unit with high versatility. However, the real game-changer for F2P players is the **S-Rank Selector Box**, typically awarded after 50 pulls on the standard banner. This allows you to hand-pick a meta-defining unit like [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide/) or Fadia to round out your free roster. Additionally, reaching Level 18 in the **City Tycoon** mode unlocks Chiz, arguably the strongest F2P-accessible main DPS in the game at launch.

## Best Weapons

In Neverness to Everness, weapons are known as **Arcs**. These are classified by their physical state: **Solid, Plasma, Gas, Liquid, and Synthesis**. For an F2P player, the goal is to secure S-Rank Arcs that are farmable through Anomaly Commissions and world bosses, rather than relying on the "Arc Research" (gacha) system.

### Top Farmable S-Rank Arcs

The "Solid" type Arcs are currently the most accessible and powerful for the F2P roster.

*   **Day Off (Solid):** This is the premier F2P Arc for Zero or Chiz. It features a base ATK of 512 and a Charge Efficiency substat of 33%. Its passive, **Eclipse**, increases the wearer's ATK by up to 50% at max refinement and restores 10 Ultimate Energy whenever an enemy is defeated during the Eclipse window.
*   **Hethereau's Keeper (Solid):** An excellent choice for boss encounters. It provides a massive 52.5% ATK boost (combining its passive and substat) and summons **Officer Whisker**, a combat companion that deals off-field damage equal to [information unavailable]% of the wearer's ATK.

### Best A-Rank Alternatives for F2P Players

Before you can farm S-Rank Arcs, you will rely on A-Rank "bridge" weapons.

*   **Dangerous Game (Synthesis):** A versatile Arc for Adler or Haniel. It focuses on [information unavailable] and provides a steady damage floor for early-game progression.
*   **Us (Plasma):** Primarily used for its [information unavailable] utility, making it a decent placeholder for support units like Mint or Haniel.
*   **Blow up the Crowd (Solid):** An A-Rank standout that boosts the active character's ATK by 10% while the wearer is off-field, making it perfect for sub-DPS units like Zero.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/best-f2p-build-neverness-to-everness-guide/inline-3.webp)


NTE uses a gear system called **Console Architecture**, which consists of **Cartridges** (the set) and **Modules** (the individual pieces). To build a top-tier F2P character, you must farm the **Rabbit Hole Anomaly Zone** in Cape Square to acquire the right Cartridge sets.

### Top Cartridge Sets for F2P Damage

For Cosmos-element damage dealers like Zero and Chiz, there is only one clear winner: **Lost Radiance**.

*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Cosmos DMG +10%.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** Ignores 25% of enemy DEF for 20 seconds after the wearer casts an Ultimate.

This set is essential because it scales directly with the high-frequency Ultimates used by the F2P team. For Mint (Anima), look for the **Fireflies and the Forest** set, which provides a stacking CRIT DMG buff (8% per hit, up to 7 stacks) whenever nearby enemies take Anima damage.

### Utility Sets for Supports and Cyclers

Supports like Adler and Haniel should focus on sets that provide team-wide buffs or increase the frequency of the Esper Cycle.

*   **Shadow Creed:** This set is excellent for Haniel, providing a 25% ATK boost for 20 seconds after casting a Skill.
*   **Cycle Efficiency Sets:** Prioritize any Cartridge that offers **Cycle Intensity** or **Charge Efficiency** as a 2-piece bonus to ensure your Esper Meter fills quickly.

## Team Comp

![Team Comp](/img/best-f2p-build-neverness-to-everness-guide/inline-4.webp)


Building the best F2P team in Neverness to Everness requires balancing the Elemental Lane to ensure you can trigger reactions on every swap. If you use the Neverness to Everness tier list as a reference, you will see that even free units rank highly when paired correctly.

### The Ultimate F2P Starter Team

This team is available to every player within the first few hours of gameplay
1.  **Zero (Cosmos):** Main/Sub-DPS and Cycle Anchor.
2.  **Mint (Anima):** AOE DPS and Blossom trigger.
3.  **Adler (Incantation):** Shielder and Hex trigger.
4.  **Haniel (Psyche):** ATK Buffer and support.

**Synergy Logic:** Zero and Mint are adjacent on the wheel, triggering **Blossom**, which spawns an automated turret. Mint and Adler are also adjacent, triggering **Hex**, which deals bonus damage based on the number of hits landed in the last 12 seconds. This creates a loop where Blossom's high hit rate feeds into Hex's damage multiplier.

### Scaling with Chiz and the S-Rank Selector

Once you unlock **Chiz** from City Tycoon and use your **S-Rank Selector**, you can upgrade to a more advanced "City Tycoon" team.

*   **Chiz (Main DPS):** Replaces Mint or Adler as the primary damage dealer. Her kit uses "Grain" stacks to unleash massive Cosmos bursts.
*   **Zero (Sub-DPS/Cycler):** Zero’s Ultimate instantly fills the Esper Meter, allowing you to swap back to Chiz immediately for a second reaction.
*   **Sakiri (Support - Selector Choice):** If you pick Sakiri from the selector, replace Adler. Sakiri provides team-wide ATK buffs and triggers Hex more efficiently.
*   **Haniel (Support):** Remains the best F2P buffer.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/best-f2p-build-neverness-to-everness-guide/inline-5.webp)


The key to high damage in NTE isn't just pressing buttons; it's managing the **Glow** mechanic. You should rarely stay on one character for more than 10 seconds.

### The Core Combat Loop

1.  **Opener:** Start with **Haniel**. Cast her Ultimate and Skill to apply a team-wide ATK buff.
2.  **Reaction 1 (Blossom):** Swap to **Zero**. Use his Skill to instantly fill his Esper Meter, then swap to **Mint** (her portrait will be glowing). This triggers **Blossom**, spawning the turret.
3.  **Reaction 2 (Hex):** While the turret is firing, build Mint's meter and swap to **Adler**. This triggers **Hex**, which will now benefit from the high hit rate of the Blossom turret.
4.  **Burst:** Swap to **Chiz** (or return to Zero). Use her Ultimate during the Hex window to maximize damage.
5.  **Reset:** Use Zero's Ultimate to refill the team's Esper Meters and repeat.

### Maximizing Damage During Stagger Windows

Every boss has a **Break Meter** (white bar under HP). Use **Adler’s parry** (switching to him just as the enemy's red circle closes) to instantly fill his meter and deal massive Break damage. Once the boss is Staggered, dump all Ultimates. If you have the right components, you can trigger **Discord** (Incantation + Chaos + Psyche) to extend the stagger duration and shred the boss's defense.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/best-f2p-build-neverness-to-everness-guide/inline-6.webp)


**Who should I pick from the S-Rank Selector as an F2P player?**
We recommend **Sakiri** or **Fadia**. Sakiri is a top-tier buffer who fits perfectly into the Incantation slot, while Fadia offers unparalleled survivability by redirecting team damage to herself, which is vital for high-level Anomalies. Check our NTE reroll guide if you want to aim for a specific starting account.

**Can I beat endgame Anomalies with only free Arcs?**
Yes. Arcs like **Day Off** and **Hethereau's Keeper** are competitive with signature weapons when fully refined (Rank 5). Focus on clearing your daily commissions to get the materials needed for refinement.

**How do I get more Tri-Keys for Arc Research without spending?**
Tri-Keys are primarily earned through Hunter Level rewards, clearing the **Fracture Circle** (NTE’s version of a battle tower), and redeeming active NTE codes.

**Is the Male or Female Zero better?**
The choice is purely cosmetic. Both versions share the same Cosmos element, base stats, and skill multipliers. Pick the one whose design you prefer for your urban exploration.
