### Farming Currency: Shell Credits vs. Fons

Players must distinguish between the two primary currencies in Hethereau. Shell Credits (also known as Beetle Coins) are the standard currency used for character and weapon upgrades, including leveling up Arcs and performing Breakthroughs. Fons, on the other hand, are the urban currency used for purchasing high-end assets like luxury cars, real estate, and business investments. While you can occasionally exchange Fons for materials at specific vendors, Shell Credits are the primary resource you will farm in Anomaly Zones to sustain your weapon growth.

### Cartridge Set Recommendations

Cartridges come in 2-piece and 4-piece sets. You should begin farming these in the **Rabbit Hole Anomaly Zone** once you reach Hunter Level 30, as this is when S-Rank (Gold) Cartridges begin to drop.

*   **Fireflies and the Forest:** The premier set for Anima DPS like Nanally. It increases Anima DMG and provides a stacking CRIT DMG buff whenever the team deals Anima damage.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/nte-weapon-upgrade-materials-farming-guide/inline-5.webp)


Mastering the rotation in NTE is about more than just pressing buttons; it requires managing unique energy systems and watching for the "Glowing" indicator that signals the optimal time to swap characters.

### The Esper Cycle Mechanic

When you use an Esper's skill, you contribute to the Esper Cycle meter. Once the meter is full, the next character you swap to will perform an "Instant Cycle" attack, dealing massive elemental damage and applying a debuff to the enemy. The goal of any rotation is to trigger this cycle as frequently as possible.

### Advanced Combat Tips: Parrying and Redirects

*   **Hotori's Rotation:** Hotori's gameplay revolves around her "Non-Closed Time Piece" energy. You should use her Redirect Skill, **Present Replay**, to enter a 5-second recording window. During this time, swap to your teammates and use their skills. When Hotori uses her Ultimate, **World's Tide**, she stops time and re-executes every recorded skill simultaneously.
*   **Nanally's Follow-up Loop:** Nanally enters the **Ichi-daime's Authority** state for 12 seconds after using her Redirect Skill. During this window, her Basic Attacks are enhanced, and she triggers follow-up attacks from her **Vita Pistils**. The rotation involves entering this state, summoning her Underboss with her Ultimate, and then spamming her Basic Attack string to maximize the Underboss's coordinated strikes.
