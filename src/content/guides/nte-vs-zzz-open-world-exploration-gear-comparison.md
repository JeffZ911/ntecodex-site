## What 'Urban Exploration' players actually need

![What 'Urban Exploration' players actually need](/img/nte-vs-zzz-open-world-exploration-gear-comparison/inline-2.webp)


Urban exploration in 2026 titles differs significantly from traditional fantasy RPGs. Games like Neverness to Everness (NTE) and Zenless Zone Zero (ZZZ) demand specific hardware capabilities to match their unique gameplay loops.

### Horizontal FOV and Scale
The [city of Hethereau](https://www.ign.com/games/neverness-to-everness) in NTE is a seamless, large-scale environment where players spend a significant amount of time driving high-end vehicles. An ultrawide monitor with a 21:9 aspect ratio is not just a luxury here; it is a functional advantage. It allows players to see more of the city architecture and incoming traffic without constantly panning the camera. This increased field of view (FOV) enhances the sense of scale that Hotta Studio designed into the urban landscape.

### Visual Contrast and Clarity
In contrast, [Zenless Zone Zero](https://zenless.hoyoverse.com/) focuses on New Eridu, an urban hub that serves as a gateway to Hollow exploration. ZZZ's visual style is defined by high-contrast neon signs and dark, shadowy alleys. An OLED panel is critical for this aesthetic because it provides true blacks. When a Proxy enters a dark zone, the infinite contrast ratio of an OLED ensures that details aren't lost in a "grey wash" typical of cheaper IPS or VA panels.

### Ergonomic Adaptability
Long sessions—often exceeding 6 hours—require hardware that adapts to the player's posture. Exploration often involves cycles of high-intensity combat followed by low-intensity traversal. A chair like the Secretlab TITAN Evo supports this by offering a [4-way lumbar system](https://secretlab.co/products/titan-evo-2022-series) that can be adjusted as the player shifts from a focused combat stance to a more relaxed exploration posture.

### Input Versatility
Modern urban RPGs are menu-heavy. Whether you are managing Nanally's banner pulls in the "The Ichi-daime" event or navigating the Proxy interface in ZZZ, having a mouse with multiple side buttons allows for faster navigation. This reduces repetitive wrist strain by moving frequent UI interactions to the thumb.

The choice between an ultrawide setup and a high-refresh OLED setup depends largely on which title dominates your playtime.

The **LG 34GP83A-B** is the superior choice for NTE players. The driving mechanics and the sheer verticality of the [Hethereau districts](https://www.ign.com/games/neverness-to-everness) benefit immensely from the extra horizontal space. Seeing the reflection of neon lights on your car's hood while maintaining a view of the street ahead creates a level of immersion that a standard 16:9 monitor cannot replicate.

The **ASUS ROG Swift OLED PG27AQDM** is the better fit for ZZZ enthusiasts. Because ZZZ is a [character action game](https://www.ign.com/articles/zenless-zone-zero-review) at its core, the 0.03ms response time of the OLED panel is a game-changer for perfect dodges and frame-perfect counters. The vibrant, stylized colors of [New Eridu](https://zenless.hoyoverse.com/) pop with a level of saturation that makes the game look like a living anime.

For the physical setup, the **Secretlab TITAN Evo** remains the industry standard for both titles. Whether you are grinding Anomalies in NTE or clearing Hollow Zero in ZZZ, the high-density cold-cure foam prevents the "bottoming out" feeling that leads to tailbone pain during marathon sessions.

Finally, the **SteelSeries Arctis Nova 7** serves as the perfect bridge for the multi-device nature of these games. Many players use mobile apps or community wikis to track character builds while playing on PC. The Nova 7’s ability to play game audio via 2.4GHz while simultaneously connected to a phone via Bluetooth allows you to hear Discord notifications or guide videos without removing your headset.

## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/nte-vs-zzz-open-world-exploration-gear-comparison/inline-4.webp)


While high-end gear improves the experience, it isn't always a necessary investment. 

If you currently own a high-quality 1440p IPS monitor with a refresh rate of 144Hz or higher, the jump to OLED is a luxury. While the colors and contrast are better, they do not provide a competitive advantage in games like NTE or ZZZ, which are primarily [PvE-focused](https://www.ign.com/articles/zenless-zone-zero-review). You should only consider the upgrade if you find the "IPS glow" in dark scenes particularly distracting.

Avoid "racing style" chairs if you already own a high-end mesh office chair from brands like Herman Miller or Steelcase. While the Secretlab TITAN Evo is excellent for a gaming-branded product, high-end ergonomic office chairs often provide better long-term spinal health through superior [mesh breathability and dynamic tension](https://www.hermanmiller.com/products/seating/office-chairs/aeron-chairs/).

Do not upgrade your mouse if you primarily play with a controller. Both Neverness to Everness and [Zenless Zone Zero](https://zenless.hoyoverse.com/) feature robust controller support and are optimized for gamepad navigation. If you prefer the feel of a joystick for [driving in NTE](https://www.ign.com/games/neverness-to-everness), a high-button-count mouse will sit idle on your desk.

## What we didn't include and why

![What we didn't include and why](/img/nte-vs-zzz-open-world-exploration-gear-comparison/inline-5.webp)


Several popular products were excluded from this guide because they do not align with the specific needs of long-session urban exploration.

The **DXRacer Formula Series** was omitted because its aggressive "bucket seat" design features prominent side wings that push the shoulders forward. This posture is detrimental for the relaxed, multi-hour sessions typical of RPG exploration. We prioritized chairs like the Secretlab TITAN Evo, which features a [flatter pebble seat base](https://secretlab.co/products/titan-evo-2022-series) that allows for more movement.

The **Logitech G Pro X Superlight 2** is an elite mouse for competitive shooters, but we excluded it here. Its extreme focus on weight reduction means it lacks the side buttons and [scroll wheel versatility](https://www.razer.com/gaming-mice/razer-naga-v2-pro) required for shortcut-heavy urban RPGs. In NTE and ZZZ, the utility of having 6 to 12 thumb buttons outweighs the benefit of a 60g chassis.

Finally, we excluded **generic $100 mesh chairs** found on major retail sites. While they may look ergonomic, these models often lack the [seat pan depth](https://secretlab.co/products/titan-evo-2022-series) and adjustable lumbar tension required to support a user for more than 2 or 3 hours. Using these for a 6-hour session often results in significant lower back and tailbone pain due to the thinness of the materials used.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is an ultrawide monitor better for NTE or ZZZ?
An ultrawide monitor is significantly better for Neverness to Everness because its seamless open world and [driving mechanics](https://www.ign.com/games/neverness-to-everness) benefit from the wider field of view. Zenless Zone Zero is more hub-based and combat-focused, making a high-refresh 16:9 OLED monitor a better choice for visual clarity and response time.

### Why is OLED recommended for ZZZ exploration?
ZZZ features a stylized, high-contrast aesthetic with many [nighttime and neon-lit environments](https://zenless.hoyoverse.com/). OLED panels provide infinite contrast and true blacks, which prevent the "grey" look in dark areas and make the [flashy combat effects](https://www.ign.com/articles/zenless-zone-zero-review) stand out more vividly than on standard IPS panels.

### Do I need a special mouse for Neverness to Everness?
While not strictly required, a mouse with multiple side buttons like the Razer Naga V2 Pro is highly recommended for NTE. The game features complex systems for vehicle customization and business management, and mapping these menus to thumb buttons makes long exploration sessions much more efficient.

### How does the Secretlab TITAN Evo help with long sessions?
The TITAN Evo uses [high-density cold-cure foam](https://secretlab.co/products/titan-evo-2022-series) that is firmer than standard office chairs, which prevents the user from "bottoming out" over several hours. Its [4-way L-ADAPT lumbar system](https://secretlab.co/products/titan-evo-2022-series) also allows you to adjust the support as your posture changes during different gameplay phases.
