## Overview

![Overview](/img/best-nte-healer-build-for-abyss/inline-1.webp)


In the fast-paced, "Tag-Team" combat of Neverness to Everness (NTE), the role of a healer has evolved beyond simple HP restoration. Because the "Console" system allows for off-field support through Hootie deployments and damage redirection, healers now function as the primary engine for team-wide stat amplification. Unlike traditional RPGs where healers are reactive, NTE rewards proactive sustain units that can trigger "Esper Cycles" while maintaining high uptime on offensive buffs.

The current meta for the late-game Abyss—known as "Beyond the Rails" or the Fracture Circle—is dominated by two distinct Psyche-element units: Haniel and Fadia. While Haniel is an A-Rank Psyche Solid character, she is considered a mandatory "meta-pick" due to her unique "It's Friendship!" passive. This ability drains a portion of the enemy's Base ATK and redistributes it to the entire team, effectively neutering boss damage while skyrocketing your own. Fadia, an S-Rank Psyche Synthesis character, serves a different purpose as the ultimate "face-tank." Her kit allows her to absorb 50% of the damage taken by her active teammates, making her indispensable for floors where enemies possess "one-shot" mechanics.

Healing in NTE primarily scales through two distinct paths: Max HP and Healing Bonus. For Fadia, Max HP is the priority, as her damage redirection and "Lilith" state bursts are tied directly to her total health pool. For pure healers like Edgar, Healing Bonus modules are often preferred to maximize the throughput of every "Esper Cycle" trigger.

### Why You Need a Healer in Abyss
The Fracture Circle introduces "Anomaly Pressure," a mechanic that slowly erodes your team's Break resistance over time. Without a dedicated sustain unit, your Main DPS will eventually be staggered, leading to a total loss of DPS during critical burst windows. Healers provide the necessary "Plot Armor" and "Prayer" stacks to keep your rotation fluid, ensuring that your "Esper Cycle" gauge never stalls.

### Haniel vs. Fadia: Which One to Build?
Choosing between these two depends on your team's offensive ceiling. Haniel is the superior choice for players who have mastered "Perfect Dodges" and want to clear floors as quickly as possible using her ATK-buffing speaker, Hootie. Fadia is the better investment for players struggling with survivability, as her ability to redirect damage ensures that even glass-cannon DPS units like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) can stay on the field during aggressive boss phases.

## Best Weapons

![Best Weapons](/img/best-nte-healer-build-for-abyss/inline-2.webp)


Selecting the right Arc (weapon) for your healer is a matter of balancing "Charge Efficiency" with utility. In the Abyss, your healer's personal damage is secondary to their ability to fire off Ultimates as frequently as possible to refresh team buffs. Most high-end S-Class Arcs are farmed through Anomaly Commissions such as "Ride Assault" or obtained via the Tri-Key gacha system.

### Top S-Rank Arcs for Healers
For Fadia, the **Eternal Waltz** is the gold standard. This S-Rank Arc provides a massive Max HP percentage boost, which is the foundation of her damage redirection kit. Furthermore, it increases Mental DMG by 10%–18% for 10 seconds after an Ultimate, allowing Fadia to contribute significant Cognito damage during her Lilith transformation. An alternative is **Your Happiness is Priceless**, which offers a random supportive effect—such as a team-wide shield or a targeted heal for the lowest-HP ally—every time an Ultimate is cast.

For Haniel, **Blow up the Crowd** is the preferred S-Rank choice. Since Haniel scales her team-wide buffs based on her own Base ATK, this weapon's high ATK% substat is invaluable. Its passive effect also enhances the ATK of the active character while Haniel is benched, perfectly complementing her off-field playstyle.

### Best 4-Star (A-Rank) Weapons for F2P Players
F2P players should prioritize the **Speedy Hedgehog** Arc for Haniel. This weapon is specifically designed to maximize Charge Efficiency, allowing Haniel to keep her "Paranormal Ace" state active for nearly the entire fight. For Fadia, **The Good, The Bad, The Bitter** is a solid A-Rank placeholder that increases DEF by 26% when taking damage, helping her survive the 50% damage she absorbs from allies.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/best-nte-healer-build-for-abyss/inline-3.webp)


The "Console" system in NTE is a two-layer artifact system consisting of **Cartridges** (set bonuses) and **Modules** (individual stat pieces). To activate a Cartridge's Legend Effect, you must arrange Modules on a grid in a Tetris-like configuration that matches the Cartridge's required pattern.

### Optimal Cartridge Sets for Abyss
For Haniel, the **Speedy Hedgehog** Cartridge set is the current meta. The 2-piece bonus grants 12% Charge Efficiency, while the 4-piece Legend Effect increases all allies' ATK by 15% for 20 seconds after the wearer casts an Ultimate. This effect does not stack, making it the definitive choice for a primary support.

For Fadia, the **Tiny Big Adventure** set is unmatched. It provides a 10% HP boost at 2 pieces. The 4-piece bonus is even more potent: whenever Fadia takes damage (including redirected damage), her Max HP increases by 4%, stacking up to 10 times. Since her Ultimate immediately grants all 10 stacks, she can enter her burst phase with a massive 40% HP bonus.

### How to Optimize Your Module Grid (Tetris Strategy)
Every character has a "Preferred Module Shape." Haniel prefers Type III Modules (3-block pieces), which grant her a 10% ATK bonus per module when correctly slotted. Fadia utilizes Type II Modules (2-block pieces) to maximize her HP scaling. When building for the Abyss, you should prioritize **Wastetime** modules in the remaining grid spaces. These modules provide "DEF Ignore" buffs that are critical for breaking through the high-armor bosses found in the later Fracture Circle floors.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/best-nte-healer-build-for-abyss/inline-5.webp)


Mastering the rotation is the difference between a timed out floor and a 3-star clear. You must manage your "Wastetime" stacks and "Cycle Gauge" to ensure buffs never drop.

### Haniel Support Rotation
1.  **Start with Zero:** Use the "Redirect" skill (Appraise and Engrave) to instantly fill 100% of the Cycle Gauge.
2.  **Swap to Haniel:** Immediately cast her Skill to deploy **Hootie**. This initiates the ATK drain from her "It's Friendship!" passive.
3.  **Basic Attack Weaving:** Perform Haniel’s airborne plunge or charged "Paranormal Cannon" to build **Plot Armor** stacks.
4.  **Trigger Ultimate:** Once energy is capped, activate **Paranormal Ace**. This triggers the "Speedy Hedgehog" 15% ATK buff.
5.  **Swap to Nanally:** Unleash your Main DPS burst while the enemy's ATK is drained and your team's ATK is at its peak.

### Fadia Emergency Healing Sequence
1.  **Passive Management:** Keep Fadia in the party to maintain the 50% damage redirection.
2.  **Swap-In Trigger:** When the active ally falls below 50% HP, swap Fadia in and use her Skill to deploy her **Cross-Shield**.
3.  **Ultimate Timing:** Wait until Fadia has taken several hits (building **Tiny Big Adventure** stacks) before casting her Ultimate.
4.  **Lilith Burst:** Enter the **Lilith state** to deal Cognito damage and recover 30% of Fadia's Max HP instantly.
5.  **Perfect Dodge:** Use Fadia’s "Critical Riposte" to reduce the enemy's Break bar before swapping back to your DPS.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/best-nte-healer-build-for-abyss/inline-6.webp)


**Can I use Edgar in the late-game Abyss?**
Edgar is viable as a niche swap-healer for floors with "Cosmos" elemental weakness. However, because he lacks the offensive buffs of Haniel or the damage redirection of Fadia, he is often replaced by players looking to maximize their clear speeds.

**How do I get more Fons to upgrade my healer's Arcs?**
The most efficient way to farm Fons is through the "Owner’s Selection" and "City Delivery" tasks. Additionally, Haniel’s Life Skills (Level 5) can increase dish prices in your restaurant, providing a passive income of Fons over time.

**What is the best way to heal without a dedicated healer?**
You can use **Food Items** from your inventory or visit **ReroRero Phone Booths** and Wertheimer Towers in the open world. However, these methods are disabled inside the Fracture Circle (Abyss), making dedicated healers or shielders like Adler mandatory.

**Do healer buffs stack in NTE?**
Most ATK buffs from Cartridges (like Speedy Hedgehog) do not stack. However, character-specific passives, such as Haniel's "It's Friendship!" and [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide)'s Ultimate buff, do stack, allowing for massive damage multipliers.

**How does 'Appraisal Level' affect healing output?**
In NTE, your **Appraisal Level** acts as a multiplier for all "Esper Cycle" effects. A higher level increases the amount of HP recovered during **Blossom** reactions and improves the efficiency of ATK-draining mechanics.
