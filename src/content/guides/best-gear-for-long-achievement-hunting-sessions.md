## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/best-gear-for-long-achievement-hunting-sessions/inline-4.webp)


Not every "gaming" product is an upgrade for every user. 

If you currently use a high-end mesh office chair like a Herman Miller Aeron, switching to a "gaming" chair like the Secretlab may feel like a downgrade in breathability. Mesh is superior for heat dissipation, which is a major factor in comfort during 8-hour sessions in warmer climates.

Furthermore, do not upgrade your mouse if you primarily play on a controller. While Neverness to Everness has a robust PC interface, players using a gamepad for the driving mechanics will find that a high-end mouse like the Naga V2 Pro provides zero benefit during those gameplay segments.

Finally, consider your desk space. If your desk is less than 30 inches deep, an ultrawide monitor may be too close to your eyes. This can cause more ocular strain and neck pain than a standard 27-inch display, regardless of the refresh rate or panel quality.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How to unlock ZZZ Officer Mewmew medals?
To unlock Officer Mewmew medals in Zenless Zone Zero, you must find the character Officer Mewmew in various locations like Sixth Street or Lumina Square. You earn medals by completing specific neighborhood tasks, such as finding lost cargo or helping citizens with "Anomalous" problems. Once a task is finished, talk to the officer to claim your reward and increase your reputation.

### Is Neverness to Everness harder on the body than other gacha games?
Because Neverness to Everness features a seamless open world with high-speed driving and vertical exploration, players tend to stay "locked in" for longer periods compared to stage-based games. The constant camera movement and complex Esper ability rotations put more strain on the wrists and neck, making ergonomic gear more of a necessity than a luxury.

### Why is an ultrawide monitor better for urban exploration?
In a city like Hesperia, collectibles are often hidden behind crates, on balconies, or in narrow gaps. An ultrawide monitor with a [21:9 aspect ratio](https://www.lg.com/us/monitors/lg-34gp83a-b-gaming-monitor) allows you to see these details in your peripheral vision without having to manually rotate the camera as often. This reduces the total number of mouse movements you make over an 8-hour session.

You should not stay in one fixed position for the entire 8-hour grind.
