### Resonance Chain (S1-S6) Priority List

Unlocking Chixia's Resonance Chains significantly boosts her damage floor and ceiling. Below is the priority for her sequences:

1.  **S4 (Hero's Heart):** This is her most impactful upgrade. It resets the cooldown of her Resonance Skill, "Whiz-Kid," whenever she uses her Resonance Liberation. This allows for back-to-back "Daka Daka" rotations.
2.  **S1 (Land of My Heart):** Ensures her Resonance Skill "Whiz-Kid" (and the subsequent "Boom Boom" finisher) always deals Critical Hits. This greatly reduces the Crit Rate requirements in her Echo sub-stats.
3.  **S6 (Miracle Girl):** Provides a 25% ATK buff to the entire team for 15 seconds after she uses her "Boom Boom" finisher. This transforms her into a pseudo-support for dual-DPS comps.
4.  **S3 (Eternal Flame):** A straightforward 40% damage increase to her Resonance Liberation, "Blazing Flames."
5.  **S5 (Unstoppable Justice):** Increases the damage of her "Daka Daka" state, further cementing her sustained DPS role.
6.  **S2 (Leaping Fire):** Restores 5 Stamina for every hit during her Resonance Skill channel, which is helpful for dodging but less impactful for raw damage.

### Chixia's Role in the Meta

Chixia currently sits as a premier 4-star Main DPS. While she may be overshadowed by 5-star units like Jiyan or Encore in terms of raw burst, her S4 and S6 upgrades allow her to compete in the Tower of Adversity. She is particularly effective against bosses with predictable patterns, as she can safely fire from a distance while circling the enemy.

### Stat Priority: Crit vs. Fusion DMG

For Chixia, the goal is to balance her high attack speed with heavy-hitting critical strikes. Because her S1 guarantees Crits on her finisher, many players focus heavily on Crit DMG. However, to maintain consistent damage during her normal "Daka Daka" hits, a healthy Crit Rate is still required.

*   **Primary Stats:** Crit Rate / Crit DMG > Fusion DMG% > ATK%
*   **Secondary Stats:** Resonance Skill DMG% > Resonance Liberation DMG% > Flat ATK

### Top Tier: Static Mist Analysis

Static Mist is the definitive best weapon for Chixia. Its primary stat is Crit Rate, which allows you to focus almost exclusively on Crit DMG and Fusion DMG in your Echo sub-stats. The passive effect increases Energy Regeneration by 12.8% (at Rank 1), which is crucial for keeping her Resonance Liberation ready. Furthermore, it provides a 10% ATK buff to the next Resonator when Chixia uses her Outro Skill, adding utility to her high damage.

### Best 4-Star Options for F2P Players

If you do not have access to 5-star weapons, **Undying Flame** is the premier 4-star choice. Its passive specifically increases Resonance Skill DMG by 20% for 15 seconds after a Resonance Skill is cast. Since Chixia's entire kit revolves around her "Daka Daka" (which counts as Resonance Skill DMG), this buff has nearly 100% uptime.

**Thunderbolt**, obtainable via the Pioneer Podcast (Battle Pass), is another strong contender. It provides a stacking Resonance Skill DMG buff (up to 21% at Rank 1) that triggers whenever her skill hits an enemy. Given how many hits Chixia lands per second, she reaches max stacks almost instantly.

### Early Game Weapon Recommendations

For new players, **Pistols#26** is a reliable 3-star weapon. It grants a 5% ATK increase that stacks up to three times when the user takes no damage. Since Chixia is a ranged character, maintaining these stacks is relatively easy in the early game.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/chixia-dps-build-wuthering-waves/inline-5.webp)


Mastering Chixia's rotation is about managing her Thermopile stacks and ensuring she is fully buffed before entering her "Daka Daka" state.

### Managing Thermopile Stacks

Chixia can hold up to 30 Thermopile stacks. You generate these by:
*   **Basic Attacks:** Each hit of her normal attack string generates stacks.
*   **Resonance Skill (Whiz-Kid):** Using the initial dash of her skill generates stacks.
*   **Intro Skill:** Entering the field via an Intro Skill provides immediate stacks.

### Optimal Opening Sequence

1.  **Support/Sub-DPS Setup:** Start with Verina or Changli. Use their skills and ultimates to build Concerto energy. 
2.  **Outro Transition:** Trigger the Outro Skill of your support to buff Chixia as she enters the field.
3.  **Echo Skill:** Immediately use **Inferno Rider**. Complete the three slashes to trigger the 12% Fusion and Resonance Skill DMG buffs.
4.  **Building Stacks:** If you don't have 30 Thermopile stacks, use a few Basic Attack strings.
5.  **The "Daka Daka":** Hold the Resonance Skill button. Chixia will begin her rapid-fire channel, consuming Thermopile stacks.
6.  **The Finisher (Boom Boom):** Once all 30 stacks are consumed, release the Skill button to trigger "Boom Boom," a powerful explosive shot.
7.  **Resonance Liberation:** Use "Blazing Flames" immediately after "Boom Boom." If you have S4, this will reset your Skill cooldown, allowing you to repeat the "Daka Daka" process if you have more stacks.

### Animation Canceling

Chixia is vulnerable during her "Daka Daka" channel. If an enemy is about to hit you, you can dash or jump to cancel the animation. However, if you have already consumed 30 stacks, try to release the skill button first to ensure the "Boom Boom" finisher lands before you dodge.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/chixia-dps-build-wuthering-waves/inline-6.webp)


### Is Chixia worth building over 5-star DPS units?
Chixia is worth building if you enjoy her playstyle or if you have her at S4 or higher. At S4, her damage output is competitive with several S0 5-star units, especially in Fusion-favored content.

### How many Thermopile stacks does Chixia need?
You should always aim for 30 stacks before holding her Resonance Skill. The "Boom Boom" finisher, which deals the highest portion of her skill damage, only triggers if you have consumed stacks during the channel.

### Can Chixia be played as a Sub-DPS?
Chixia is strictly a Main DPS. Her kit requires significant field time to consume Thermopile stacks and perform her full rotation. She offers very little utility or off-field damage to other units unless she is S6.

### What is the best Resonance Chain level for Chixia?
S4 is her most critical milestone, as the cooldown reset on her Resonance Skill fundamentally changes her rotation and doubles her burst potential.

### How to dodge while using her Resonance Skill?
Chixia can move slowly while in her "Daka Daka" state. You can use the directional keys to strafe. If you need to perform a hard dodge, pressing the Dodge button will cancel the skill. If you have S2, this is less punishing as your stamina will likely be full.
