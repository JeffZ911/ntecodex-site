By pairing her with the protagonist, Zero, players can create a devastating elemental loop that clears mobs and bosses alike with minimal resource investment.

### Why Mint is the Best Starter Choice

Mint is awarded to every player upon the completion of the "Prologue: Hethereau Anomaly" questline. This accessibility, combined with her "Liquid" compatibility, makes her incredibly easy to gear. Most early-game rewards provide the materials needed to ascend Liquid Arcs, ensuring Mint stays ahead of the power curve. Her "Transform! Super Mint!" passive provides a flat ATK percentage increase whenever she triggers a reaction, meaning her floor for damage is significantly higher than other A-Class alternatives like Edgar or Adler.

### Core Mechanics: Critical Riposte and Skill Resets

The defining feature of Mint’s kit is the "Critical Riposte." When an enemy initiates an attack, performing a Perfect Dodge (known as a Critical Dodge in NTE) triggers a brief window where Mint can counter-attack. Successfully landing this Riposte instantly resets the cooldown of her Elemental Skill. For skilled players, this creates a loop where Mint never has to rely on her Basic Attack string, leading to one of the highest sustained DPS windows in the game.

### Rerolling and Character Acquisition

**How do I get Mint?**
Mint is not a gacha-exclusive character for your first copy. She is a guaranteed reward for completing the "Prologue: Hethereau Anomaly" questline, which takes approximately 45 to 60 minutes of gameplay.

**Is Mint better than Zero?**
They serve different roles. Mint is a dedicated Main DPS who needs field time to deal damage. Zero is a Sub-DPS who excels at supporting others. You should run them together rather than choosing one over the other.

**Which S-Class should I pick from the selector for Mint?**
Sakiri is the most popular choice because her crowd control (CC) solves Mint’s biggest weakness: hitting scattered enemies. However, if you want pure damage, Jiuyuan is the better long-term investment for an Anima-focused team.

### Resource Management for Starters

**What is the best way to farm Fireflies Cartridges?**
In the early game, do not spend your "Stamina" (Battery) on low-level Disk farming. Use the placeholder Disks provided by the "Urban Guide" missions. Once you reach Account Level 35, you can begin farming the "Fracture Circle" challenges for 5-star Fireflies and the Forest pieces.

**Common New Player Mistakes with Mint:**
*   **Ignoring the Liquid Arc requirement:** Using a Solid Arc because it has "higher ATK" will actually result in lower damage because Mint loses her elemental synergy.
*   **Mashing Dodge:** If you dodge too early, you won't trigger the Critical Riposte. Wait for the yellow flash on the enemy's weapon.
*   **Forgetting Zero:** Mint’s damage drops by nearly 40% if she isn't triggering Blossom reactions. Always keep Zero’s Cosmos field active.
