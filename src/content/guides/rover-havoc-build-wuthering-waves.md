## Overview

Rover (Havoc) is a high-tier Main DPS unlocked after completing Chapter 1 Act VI: Grand Warstorm. This transformation changes the Rover's element from Spectro to Havoc and provides an entirely new kit focused on aggressive, fast-paced combat. The Rover's base stats at Level 90 are as follows:

*   **Base HP:** 10125
*   **Base ATK:** 412
*   **Base DEF:** 1115

The character’s strength lies in their Forte Circuit, which allows them to enter a transformed state to deal massive Havoc damage. Unlike the Spectro version, which focuses on time-stop mechanics and slowing enemies, the Havoc version is designed for pure destruction.

### Skill Priority
To maximize your investment, prioritize leveling the Rover's skills in the following order:
1.  **Resonance Liberation (Deadening Abyss):** This is your primary source of burst damage and triggers the Dreamless Echo's massive damage bonus.
2.  **Forte Circuit (World in a Grain of Sand):** Governs the damage of your Dark Surge state and Enhanced Basic Attacks.
3.  **Resonance Skill (Wingblade / Lifetaker):** Essential for building Umbra and dealing consistent damage during the Dark Surge window.
4.  **Basic Attack (Tune of Cosmos):** Important for building Umbra gauge outside of the burst window.
5.  **Intro Skill (Ominous Shatter):** Provides a solid entry hit and helps transition into the main rotation.

### How to Unlock Rover (Havoc)
Players do not need to pull for this version of the Rover on any banner. You unlock the Havoc element automatically upon completing the "Grand Warstorm" quest at the end of Chapter 1, Act VI. Once unlocked, you can toggle between Spectro and Havoc forms in the character menu, though they require separate leveling for their skills (Resonance Skills, Forte, etc.) and use different Echo sets.

### Core Mechanics: Umbra and Dark Surge
The Rover's gameplay revolves around the **Umbra** gauge, located above the health bar. 
*   **Building Umbra:** You accumulate Umbra by landing hits with Basic Attacks, the Resonance Skill (Wingblade), and the Intro Skill.
*   **Entering Dark Surge:** Once the Umbra gauge is full, perform a Heavy Attack. This triggers "Devastation," consuming all Umbra to enter the **Dark Surge** state.
*   **Dark Surge State:** While in this state, your Basic Attacks are replaced with Enhanced Basic Attacks, and your Resonance Skill transforms into "Lifetaker." This state lasts for a fixed duration or until the gauge depletes. Your primary goal is to trigger this state as often as possible to capitalize on the increased Havoc DMG multipliers.

## Best Weapons

The Havoc Rover uses a Sword. Because they scale heavily with Attack and Critical stats, five-star weapons significantly outperform four-star alternatives, though there are several viable F2P paths.

### Top 5-Star Weapon Options
**Emerald of Genesis** is widely considered the best-in-slot weapon for Havoc Rover. It provides a massive 24.3% Crit Rate substat at level 90, which makes balancing your Echo substats much easier. Its passive increases Energy Regeneration by 12.8% and boosts ATK by 6% (stacking twice) whenever a Resonance Skill hits an enemy. Since the Rover uses their skill frequently to build Umbra, this buff maintains near-permanent uptime.

**Blazing Brilliance** (Changli’s signature weapon) is another top-tier contender. It offers high Base ATK and Crit DMG. While its passive is specifically tailored for Resonance Skill DMG, the raw stats alone make it a powerhouse for a Main DPS Rover.

**Red Spring** is a strong alternative that provides Crit Rate and increases damage after using a Resonance Skill, aligning perfectly with the Rover's rotation.

### Best 4-Star and F2P Swords

It features an ATK% substat and a passive that grants a 15% ATK increase for 15 seconds after using an Intro Skill. Since Rover should always enter the field via an Intro Skill to maximize Concerto energy, this buff is highly reliable.

**Lumingloss**, available through the Insider Channel (Battle Pass), is excellent for a Main DPS playstyle. It increases Basic Attack and Heavy Attack damage by 20% for 10 seconds after using a Resonance Skill. This synergizes directly with the Dark Surge state, where the Rover relies on Enhanced Basic Attacks.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

In Wuthering Waves, "Disks" are referred to as Echoes. For the Havoc Rover, the goal is to stack Havoc DMG and Critical stats while ensuring enough Energy Recharge to use the Resonance Liberation every rotation.

### Best Echo Set: Sun-sinking Eclipse
The **Sun-sinking Eclipse** (5-piece set) is the mandatory choice for a Havoc Rover build.
*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Havoc DMG +10%.
*   **5-Piece Bonus:** After using a Basic Attack or Heavy Attack, Havoc DMG increases by 7.5%. This effect stacks up to 4 times and lasts for 15 seconds.
This set provides a total of 40% Havoc DMG bonus when fully stacked, which is significantly higher than any 2-piece/2-piece combination.

### Main Echo: The Dreamless Synergy
The **Dreamless** is the definitive Main Echo (4-cost) for the Havoc Rover. It possesses a unique interaction: if the Dreamless Echo skill is used within 5 seconds after the Rover casts their Resonance Liberation (Deadening Abyss), the Dreamless damage is increased by 50%. This creates a massive burst window that can delete elite enemies and bosses instantly.

An alternative is the **Crownless**. While the Crownless provides a longer-lasting Havoc DMG and Resonance Skill DMG buff (12% each for 15 seconds), its animation is much longer and it lacks the explosive synergy that the Dreamless offers. Use Crownless only if you have significantly better substats on it compared to your Dreamless.

### Recommended Stats and Sub-stats
To optimize your damage, use a **4-3-3-1-1** Echo cost distribution.

*   **4-Cost Echo (Dreamless):** Crit Rate or Crit DMG (aim for a 1:2 ratio).
*   **3-Cost Echoes:** Havoc DMG% / Havoc DMG%.
*   **1-Cost Echoes:** ATK% / ATK%.

**Sub-stat Priority:**
1.  Crit Rate / Crit DMG
2.  Energy Recharge (Target: 120% – 140%)
3.  ATK%
4.  Resonance Liberation DMG%
5.  Basic Attack DMG%

Having at least 120% Energy Recharge is vital. If you cannot use your Ultimate every rotation, you lose the 50% damage bonus on your Dreamless Echo, which drastically lowers your overall DPS.

## Team Comp

The Havoc Rover functions best in a "Hypercarry" or "Duo DPS" setup where supports provide DMG Deepen buffs rather than just raw Attack.

### The Havoc Duo: Rover + Danjin
**Danjin** is the undisputed best partner for the Havoc Rover. Her Outro Skill provides a **23% Havoc DMG Deepen** buff for 14 seconds to the next character. "Deepen" is a separate multiplicative layer in the damage formula, making it far more valuable than standard ATK% buffs. Danjin can also act as a secondary DPS, filling in the gaps when the Rover's Umbra gauge is empty.

### Best Supports and Healers
**Verina** is the gold standard for the healer slot. She provides a 20% ATK buff to the entire team and a 15% All-Type DMG Deepen buff through her Outro Skill and inherent skills. If Verina is unavailable (e.g., used in another Tower of Adversity team), **Baizhi** is a solid F2P alternative, offering similar ATK buffs, though her Deepen buff is slightly lower and limited to the next character swapped in.

**Sanhua** is a niche but effective support. Her Outro Skill provides a 38% Basic Attack DMG Deepen buff. This is particularly effective if your Rover is using the Lumingloss sword and focuses heavily on the Enhanced Basic Attacks during the Dark Surge state.

**Zhezhi** can also be utilized for her 20% Resonance Skill DMG Deepen and coordinated attacks, which help the Rover clear mobs more efficiently.

## Rotation

Mastering the Havoc Rover requires precise timing to ensure the Dreamless Echo receives its 50% damage bonus.

### Umbra Building Phase
1.  **Start with your Supports:** Use Verina to apply her marks and buffs. Switch to Danjin, use her Resonance Skill to build Concerto Energy, and trigger her Outro Skill.
2.  **Intro into Rover:** Switch to Rover via Intro Skill (Ominous Shatter).
3.  **Fill the Gauge:** Use Resonance Skill (Wingblade) followed by Basic Attacks. If your Resonance Skill is off cooldown, use it again immediately.

### The Dark Surge Burst Window
1.  **Transform:** Once the Umbra gauge is full, hold the Basic Attack button to perform **Devastation**. The Rover will enter the Dark Surge state.
2.  **The Ultimate:** Immediately cast Resonance Liberation (Deadening Abyss).
3.  **The Echo:** Immediately after the Ultimate animation ends, cast the **Dreamless Echo** skill. This ensures the Echo benefits from the 50% DMG boost.
4.  **Sustain Damage:** Use the transformed Resonance Skill (Lifetaker) and spam Enhanced Basic Attacks.
5.  **Exit:** Once the Dark Surge state ends or your Concerto Energy is full, swap back to your supports to restart the cycle.

**Animation Canceling Tip:** You can cancel the end of the Resonance Skill (Lifetaker) animation by using the Dreamless Echo or swapping characters, though you must ensure the damage numbers appear before the cancel.

## FAQ

### Is Havoc Rover better than Spectro Rover?
In terms of raw damage output, Havoc Rover is significantly stronger. Spectro Rover is better suited for a Sub-DPS or support role due to their ability to slow down time and provide minor healing/utility. If you need a Main DPS to clear timed content like the Tower of Adversity, Havoc Rover is the superior choice.

### Can I use Havoc Rover with Calcharo?
While you can put them on the same team, it is not optimal. Both characters want to spend a significant amount of time on the field as the Main DPS.
