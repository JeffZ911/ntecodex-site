## What a long-session grinder actually needs

![What a long-session grinder actually needs](/img/zzz-1-0-vs-1-1-polychrome-rewards-gear-comparison-20260701/inline-2.webp)


Grinding for Polychromes in ZZZ or navigating the urban sprawl of Hethereau in Neverness to Everness places specific demands on your body and your hardware.

*   **Lumbar Support:** Static pillows are insufficient for 4+ hour sessions; active or 4-way adjustable systems are required to prevent lower back fatigue. As players move from the discovery-based play of 1.0 into the repetitive daily tasks of 1.1, the risk of "slumping" increases.
*   **Input Efficiency:** Gacha titles often involve heavy menu navigation. Mice with high button counts reduce repetitive strain on the keyboard hand by allowing users to map frequently used shortcuts—like the Inter-Knot Guide or the inventory screen—to thumb buttons.
*   **Visual Clarity:** High DCI-P3 color coverage is necessary to maintain visual engagement during repetitive daily tasks without causing eye strain. The vibrant, neon-soaked aesthetics of games like Neverness to Everness and ZZZ require monitors that can distinguish subtle color gradients in dark environments.

## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/zzz-1-0-vs-1-1-polychrome-rewards-gear-comparison-20260701/inline-4.webp)


Not every player needs to rush out and buy a $600 chair or a $400 monitor.

*   **Existing Office Gear:** If you already own a high-end ergonomic office chair like a Steelcase or Herman Miller, "gaming" chairs will likely feel like a downgrade in adjustability and long-term comfort.
*   **Competitive FPS Focus:** If your primary focus is competitive FPS, the 134g weight of the Razer Naga V2 Pro will hinder your performance compared to a sub-60g lightweight mouse. The Naga is for efficiency, not flick shots.
*   **Hardware Bottlenecks:** Skip the 1440p monitor upgrade if your GPU cannot maintain a stable 60 FPS. The increased resolution will not compensate for stuttering during combat in games like Neverness to Everness, which features high-fidelity urban environments.
