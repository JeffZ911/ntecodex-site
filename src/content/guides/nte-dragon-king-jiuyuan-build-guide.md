### Jiuyuan's Role and Playstyle

Jiuyuan operates best as a "Quick-Swap" or "Main DPS" depending on your team composition. Her primary goal is to stack "Rose Pact Bullets" and apply marks to as many targets as possible. This makes her exceptionally strong in mobbing scenarios where multiple enemies can be detonated simultaneously, creating a chain reaction of Anima explosions.

The Console system allows you to slot in Modules to further refine Jiuyuan's stats. Jiuyuan has a specific Console bonus that rewards using Type II Modules. For every Type II Module equipped, her base CRIT Rate increases by 6%. By filling all five slots with Type II Modules, you can achieve a 30% base CRIT Rate boost before considering substats.
