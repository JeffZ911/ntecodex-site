## Overview

Support characters in Neverness to Everness (NTE) act as the glue that holds a high-performing squad together. While your main DPS focuses on raw numbers, supports are responsible for ATK buffing, crowd control (CC), and enabling the complex reactions found within the Esper Wheel. Without a dedicated support, players often find themselves stuck with long cooldowns and insufficient energy to maintain their burst windows during difficult encounters in "Beyond the Rails" content.

Sakiri currently sits at the top of the meta as the S+ tier premium support. Her kit is designed around high-efficiency grouping and a massive 30% ATK buff that scales with her total Charge Efficiency. On the other end of the spectrum, Haniel serves as the premier A-rank universal support. She is highly accessible for F2P players and provides consistent, team-wide buffs that remain relevant even in the late game.

To build these characters effectively, you must prioritize specific stats that allow them to cycle their Ultimates as frequently as possible.

### Core Support Stats Priority

When farming for gear, supports generally follow a strict stat priority to ensure they can sustain the team's momentum:

1.  **Charge Efficiency:** This is the most vital stat for any support. It dictates how quickly your Esper Meter fills, allowing for more frequent Ultimates.
2.  **Cycle Intensity:** This stat increases the damage and effectiveness of reactions triggered via the Esper Wheel (such as Blossom and Scorch).
3.  **ATK% / HP%:** Depending on whether the support's buffs scale with their offensive or defensive stats.
4.  **Flat HP/DEF:** For survivability in high-level anomalies.

### The Role of Supports in the NTE Meta
In the current version of NTE, the meta revolves around "Burst Windows." This is a period where all buffs are active, and the enemy is CC'd or debuffed. Supports facilitate this by using their skills to group enemies, followed by an Ultimate that triggers a team-wide damage multiplier. The Esper Wheel mechanics further emphasize the need for supports to match elements with their DPS to trigger powerful reactions.

The strength of a support is dictated by the team they enable. In Neverness to Everness, team building is centered around the Esper Wheel—a system where specific elemental combinations trigger massive damage events.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/nte-support-character-build-guide/inline-5.webp)


A proper rotation ensures that your buffs are active when your main DPS is ready to unleash their highest-damaging abilities.

### Sakiri Rotation: Step-by-Step
1.  **Swap In:** Bring Sakiri onto the field as soon as the enemy wave spawns.
2.  **Devour Whole (Skill):** Use this to pull all enemies into a tight cluster.
3.  **Ultimate:** Activate Sakiri's Ultimate to trigger the 30% ATK buff and the Speedy Hedgehog 4-piece bonus.
4.  **Swap to Main DPS:** Immediately switch to Nanally or your primary damage dealer.
5.  **Burst Window:** Execute the DPS combo while the Sakiri buff is active (usually a 10-12 second window).

### Haniel's Quick-Swap Buffing Loop
Haniel is designed for a faster, more fluid rotation.
1.  **Ultimate:** Haniel should often start the rotation with her Ultimate to set the team-wide damage floor.
2.  **Silent Moonlit Forest Guardian (Skill):** Use this to generate energy particles for the rest of the team.
3.  **Swap to Sub-DPS:** Switch to your secondary damage dealer to set up elemental primers.
4.  **Swap to Main DPS:** Finish the rotation with your heaviest hitter to trigger the Esper Cycle.

### Timing Your Esper Cycles for Maximum Damage
Always monitor the Esper Meter located at the bottom of the UI. You should never use a support's Ultimate if the meter is less than 80% full, unless you are in desperate need of emergency healing or CC. The goal is to have the support's buff and the Esper Cycle trigger at the exact same time. This "Double Peak" strategy is how top players achieve sub-one-minute clears in endgame content.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/nte-support-character-build-guide/inline-6.webp)


### Does Sakiri's ATK buff stack with Haniel's?
Yes, they do stack, but with diminishing returns. Because both buffs apply to the "Base ATK" stat, having both characters on the same team is usually less efficient than having one support and one Sub-DPS who can trigger more reactions on the Esper Wheel.

### What is the difference between Charge Efficiency and Cycle Intensity?
Charge Efficiency solely affects how fast you gain energy for your Ultimate. Cycle Intensity increases the actual damage numbers of reactions like Blossom, Scorch, and [information unavailable]. For supports, Charge Efficiency is generally the priority until you can cast your Ultimate on cooldown.

### Can I use Mint as a solo support in endgame content?
Mint can solo support in the mid-game, but for "Beyond the Rails" and high-tier Urban Shadow, her lack of significant offensive buffs makes it difficult to meet the DPS checks. She is best used alongside a character like Haniel in a dual-support "turtle" comp.

### How do I unlock Chiz for free to pair with my supports?
Chiz can be obtained for free by completing the "Hethereau Urban Legend" questline, which becomes available after reaching Interknot Level 30. He serves as a fantastic F2P Sub-DPS that pairs exceptionally well with Haniel's energy-regen capabilities.
