### The Advantage of the No 50/50 System

One of the most significant reasons to reroll in NTE is the "No 50/50" system implemented for limited character banners. In traditional gacha games, pulling an S-Rank on a limited banner only offers a 50% chance of receiving the featured character. NTE removes this layer of RNG for its limited boards. If you trigger an S-Rank pull on a limited banner, such as Hotori’s current board, you are guaranteed to receive that specific character.

This system makes an early S-Rank pull extremely high value. By rerolling until you hit an S-Rank within the first 20 to 30 pulls, you effectively bypass the need to spend months of accumulated Annulith (premium currency) to guarantee a meta-defining unit.

### Maximizing the Beginner S-Rank Selector

The Standard Board in NTE features a 20% discount on the first 50 pulls. This means you only need 40 Fabricated Dice to reach the 50-pull milestone, which triggers a one-time S-Rank selector or guaranteed random S-Rank (depending on the current seasonal event).

## How to Reroll

![How to Reroll](/img/nte-reroll-guide-fast-start/inline-2.webp)


The reroll process in Neverness to Everness takes approximately 18 to 22 minutes depending on your platform and skip speed. The goal is to reach Hunter Level 5, which unlocks the mail system and the Scarborough Fair dice board.

### The Salted Email Method

To avoid creating dozens of unique email addresses, use the "salted" email method. If your email is `username@gmail.com`, you can register with `username+1@gmail.com`, `username+2@gmail.com`, and so on. The NTE account system treats these as unique IDs, but all verification codes and receipts will be sent to your primary inbox. This is the most efficient way to manage multiple accounts during a reroll session.

### Speedrunning the Prologue

Follow this checklist to minimize your time spent in the prologue:

1.  **Launch and Login:** Log in using a salted email.
2.  **The Opening Sequence:** Complete the initial combat tutorial. The opening boss intro lasts approximately 60 seconds and is currently unskippable.
3.  **Dialogue Skips:** Use the "Skip" button in the top right corner for all non-essential cutscenes.
4.  **Navigation:** Once you reach the Hethereau city streets, do not engage with side NPCs or optional Anomalies. Head directly to the objective markers to reach the Eibon Antique Shop.
5.  **Unlock Gacha:** Complete the quest "Prologue: A New Destination." This quest concludes once you speak to the manager at the Eibon Antique Shop and unlock the Scarborough Fair menu.
6.  **Claim Rewards:** Open your in-game mail to claim pre-registration rewards, including the Fabricated Dice and Annulith.

### Navigating the Scarborough Fair Dice Board

The gacha in NTE is stylized as a board game. Instead of a simple animation, you roll dice to move a piece (the Chuppa) across tiles containing rewards. 
*   **Limited Board:** Spend your initial Annulith or Limited Fabricated Dice here if you want Hotori.
*   **Standard Board:** Spend your Standard Fabricated Dice here. Ensure you utilize the 20% discount (40 pulls for the price of 50).
*   **Board Modification:** If you do not hit an S-Rank by pull 70, the "Board Modification" system activates, significantly increasing the probability of an S-Rank on every subsequent roll until pull 90.

## Best Starters

![Best Starters](/img/nte-reroll-guide-fast-start/inline-3.webp)


When rerolling, your priority should always be a high-impact DPS or a universal support that fits into multiple team compositions.

### S+ Tier: The God-Tier Pulls

*   **Hotori (S-Rank, Cosmos):** Currently the most sought-after unit in the version 1.0/1.1 meta. Her ultimate ability creates a "Chronos Fracture," effectively stopping time for all enemies within a specific radius. This utility is unmatched for clearing high-tier boss content where enemy attack patterns are difficult to dodge.
*   **Jiuyuan (S-Rank, Anima):** The premier standard DPS. Jiuyuan excels at raw damage output and has an incredibly high scaling factor for her Anima-based attacks. She is the best character for fast mob clearing during city exploration.

### S Tier: Top Standard Alternatives

*   **Sakiri (S-Rank, Incantation):** A top-tier support unit. Sakiri provides a consistent 30% ATK buff to the active character and possesses essential crowd control skills that pull enemies together. She is a "future-proof" unit that will remain relevant even as newer DPS characters are released.
*   **Daffodill (S-Rank, Chaos):** The best "Break" specialist in the game. In NTE, bosses have a stability bar that must be broken to deal significant damage. Daffodill reduces this bar faster than any other character, making early-game boss fights significantly shorter.

### A Tier: When to Settle

*   **[Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) (S-Rank, Physical):** A solid Physical DPS who is available on the "The Ichi-daime" banner. While strong, she lacks the elemental utility of Hotori or Jiuyuan.
*   **A-Rank Characters:** You should generally avoid rerolling specifically for A-Rank characters like Haniel or Adler. These characters have higher pull rates and will naturally join your roster over time.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/nte-reroll-guide-fast-start/inline-5.webp)


### Does pity carry over between limited banners?
Yes. If you perform 40 pulls on Hotori's banner but do not get an S-Rank, those 40 pulls will count toward the 90-pull hard pity for Lacrimosa's banner in version 1.1.

### Can I reroll on PS5?
Rerolling on PS5 is not recommended. The game links your progress directly to your PlayStation Network (PSN) ID. To reroll, you would need to create multiple PSN accounts, which is time-consuming and violates Sony's terms of service if done excessively. It is better to reroll on PC or Mobile and then link the "God Account" to your PS5 later.

### What is the hard pity?
Hard pity is 90 pulls. However, the "Board Modification" (soft pity) mechanic begins at 70 pulls, where the chance of landing on an S-Rank tile on the Scarborough Fair board increases significantly with each roll.

### Is the protagonist (Esper Zero) worth using?
Esper Zero is a very capable unit and is unique because they can switch elemental alignments as you progress through the story. However, they do not replace the specialized roles of units like Hotori or Jiuyuan. Use them as a filler until you pull a dedicated elemental DPS.

### Account Binding and Security
Once you are satisfied with your pulls, navigate to the "User Center" in the settings menu and bind your account to a permanent email or third-party login (such as Google or Apple ID). Salted emails are useful for rerolling, but you should secure the final account to prevent any loss of progress during future updates.

### Server Choice and Ping Considerations
Neverness to Everness is a fast-paced action RPG. While much of the game is single-player, the "Anomaly Co-op" modes require a stable connection. Ensure you reroll on the server closest to your physical location (NA, EU, or ASIA) to avoid input lag during combat, as your character's dodge timing is processed server-side.
