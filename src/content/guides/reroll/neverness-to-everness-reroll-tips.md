## Why Reroll

![Why Reroll](/img/neverness-to-everness-reroll-tips/inline-1.webp)


Rerolling in Neverness to Everness is a strategy used by players to optimize their initial character roster by taking advantage of the generous launch rewards. Because the game features high-difficulty urban anomalies and boss encounters, starting with a top-tier S-class character like [Hotori](/characters/hotori-best-team-nte) or [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) provides a massive power spike that carries through the mid-to-late game content.

The most compelling reason to reroll is the unique nature of the "Scarborough Fair" gacha system. Unlike many other modern gacha titles, Neverness to Everness does not utilize a 50/50 mechanic on its limited banners. When you trigger an S-class pull on a featured board, you are guaranteed the featured character. This makes early S-class pulls exceptionally impactful because there is no risk of "losing" the pull to a standard pool character.

For Free-to-Play (F2P) players, the launch window offers between 80 and 90 free pulls through various milestones. This volume of currency is enough to guarantee at least one S-class through the Standard Board's discounted starter system while leaving enough Fabricated Dice to attempt a pull on the current limited character.

### The Advantage of No 50/50

The absence of a 50/50 system in the Scarborough Fair gacha changes the value proposition of every pull. In other games, you might need to save enough currency for 160 or 180 pulls to guarantee a character. In Neverness to Everness, securing a limited character like Nanally on her "The Ichi-daime" banner is much more straightforward. Rerolling allows you to "fish" for these high-value targets early, ensuring that your account starts with the best possible offensive or utility foundation.

### Maximizing the Standard Board Selector

The Standard Board offers a specific incentive for new players: an S-class selector available after 50 pulls. To make this even more accessible, the first 50 pulls on this board are discounted by 20%, meaning you only need 40 Solid Dice to reach this milestone. By rerolling effectively, you can secure a powerful limited character first, then use your Standard Board pulls to select a complementary unit. For example, if you pull a powerful DPS from the limited banner, you can use the selector to grab a "Breaker" like Daffodill or a "Support" like [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) to round out your team composition.

### F2P vs. P2W Rerolling Benefits

For F2P players, rerolling is almost mandatory for a competitive start. It bridges the gap between those who can afford to buy extra currency and those who rely on earned rewards. Since the game provides Chiz as a free S-class character, a successful reroll ensures you have a two-S-class foundation within the first hour of play. P2W (Pay-to-Win) players might also reroll to "double-dip," securing a limited character for free before spending on duplicates (Resonance levels) or Arcs (weapons).

## How to Reroll

![How to Reroll](/img/neverness-to-everness-reroll-tips/inline-2.webp)


The reroll process in Neverness to Everness is relatively streamlined, but it does require creating new account instances. The goal is to reach the point where the Scarborough Fair gacha system unlocks, which occurs during the early stages of the main story.

### The Salting Method Explained

The most efficient way to reroll without creating dozens of entirely new email addresses is the "Salting Method." This works primarily with Gmail accounts. By adding a plus sign and a number after your username (e.g., username+1@gmail.com, username+2@gmail.com), the game treats each variation as a unique account, but all verification emails are sent to your primary inbox.

**Reroll Workflow Checklist:**
1.  Launch the game and select "Register Account."
2.  Use a salted email (username+1@gmail.com).
3.  Complete the "Prologue: A New Destination" questline.
4.  Reach the Eibon Antique Shop to unlock the gacha menu.
5.  Check the in-game Mailbox for pre-registration and launch rewards.
6.  Spend all Fabricated Dice on the Limited Banner.
7.  Spend Solid Dice on the Standard Board (aiming for the 40-pull discount).
8.  If unsatisfied, log out and repeat with "username+2@gmail.com."

### Unlocking the Scarborough Fair Gacha

The gacha system, known as Scarborough Fair, is not available immediately upon starting the game. You must follow the narrative until you reach the Eibon Antique Shop. This involves several combat tutorials and dialogue sequences. To speed this up, utilize the skip button located in the top right corner during dialogue. While some cinematic cutscenes are unskippable, most in-engine dialogue can be fast-forwarded.

### How to Reset Your Account Efficiently

There is no "Delete Account" button that works instantly, as most deletions have a 15-to-30-day grace period. This is why the salting method or using multiple guest accounts (on mobile) is preferred. If you are playing on PC, you can simply log out of the current salted account and log in with the next one. On mobile, clearing the app cache can sometimes reset the guest login, but the salted email method remains the most reliable way to ensure you can keep the account once you hit a successful roll.

### Comparison of Account Methods

*   **Salted Email:** Best for PC/Mobile. Easy to manage, easy to bind later, and keeps all communications in one inbox.
*   **Guest Account:** Fastest for mobile, but carries the risk of data loss if the app crashes or updates before you can bind it to a permanent login.
*   **Social Media Binding:** Not recommended for rerolling. Only bind your social media (Google, Apple ID, Facebook) once you have secured the characters you want.

## Time Estimate

![Time Estimate](/img/neverness-to-everness-reroll-tips/inline-4.webp)


Rerolling is a game of patience versus efficiency. A single reroll cycle in Neverness to Everness takes approximately 15 to 20 minutes depending on your familiarity with the controls and how quickly you navigate the Eibon Antique Shop unlock.

### Tutorial Speedrunning Tips

To hit the 15-minute mark, you must be aggressive with your movement and dialogue skipping.
*   **Ignore Exploration:** During the "Prologue: A New Destination," do not stop to open optional chests or investigate side-interacts. These add minutes to your run and provide negligible rewards for the initial gacha pull.
*   **Combat Efficiency:** Use Chiz’s skills as soon as they are off cooldown to clear tutorial mobs instantly. The faster the combat ends, the sooner the next dialogue trigger appears.
*   **Menu Navigation:** Familiarize yourself with the shortcut keys for the Mailbox and the Scarborough Fair menu to shave off precious seconds.

### Timeline: 0-20 Minutes of an Optimized Reroll Run

*   **0-5 Minutes:** Account creation, opening cinematics (skip where possible), and initial movement tutorial.
*   **5-12 Minutes:** Combat encounters in the city and progression through the "A New Destination" quest.
*   **12-15 Minutes:** Arriving at the Eibon Antique Shop and completing the gacha introduction.
*   **15-18 Minutes:** Claiming all mail rewards and performing pulls on the Limited and Standard boards.
*   **18-20 Minutes:** Evaluating the account and deciding whether to bind or reset.

### When to Give Up on a Run

If you have used all your Fabricated Dice and Solid Dice and have not secured at least one S-class character (other than the guaranteed ones), it is usually best to reset. A "perfect" reroll account typically has the limited character (Hotori or Nanally) and one additional S-class from the Standard Board within the first 40 pulls.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/neverness-to-everness-reroll-tips/inline-5.webp)


### Is rerolling necessary to clear endgame content in NTE?
It is not strictly necessary, as the free S-class character Chiz and the A-class units are capable of clearing the main story. However, rerolling makes endgame activities like the "Anomaly Rifts" much more manageable and reduces the pressure to spend money later.

### Can I get banned for using the salting method?
As of 2026-05-24, there have been no reports of players being banned specifically for using salted email addresses. This is a common practice in global gacha games, though you should always avoid using automated scripts or third-party "reroll bots," as these *can* trigger anti-cheat bans.

### Does pity carry over between the Limited and Standard boards?
Pity is separate for each board type. Fabricated Dice used on the Limited Banner will carry over their pity count to the *next* Limited Banner, but they do not count toward the Standard Board's S-class selector.

### How do I transfer a successful reroll account to PlayStation 5?
Once you have a successful reroll on PC or Mobile, bind the account to a permanent email or a developer account. On your PlayStation 5, launch Neverness to Everness and select "Link Account" at the login screen. Enter the credentials used for your successful reroll to sync your progress.

### Should I pull for Arcs (weapons) while rerolling?
Generally, no. Characters are far more important for account progression than Arcs. An S-class character with an A-class Arc is significantly better than an A-class character with an S-class Arc. Only consider pulling for Arcs once you have a solid team of at least three high-tier characters.
