# Neverness to Everness Reroll Guide 2026: Best Characters to Target

Securing a powerful start in Neverness to Everness (NTE) significantly impacts your efficiency in clearing high-level Anomalies and navigating the open-world challenges of Hethereau. With the game’s official release on April 29, 2026, the community has identified the most streamlined methods to capitalize on the generous launch rewards.

## Why Reroll

![Why Reroll](/img/neverness-to-everness-reroll-guide-2026/inline-1.webp)


Rerolling in Neverness to Everness is a strategic choice aimed at maximizing the value of your initial Fabricated Dice and Annulith. Because the game features a "No 50/50" system on limited character banners, landing an early S-Rank on the "Misty Tipsy Style" banner guarantees the featured character, Hotori, without the risk of pulling a standard pool unit.

Early-game progression relies heavily on your primary damage dealer's ability to clear waves of enemies and handle boss mechanics. By rerolling for a top-tier Esper like Hotori or Sakiri, you ensure that your account has the raw power needed to bypass early-game bottlenecks. Furthermore, the game provides a "Standard S-Rank Selector" after 50 pulls on the "Strange Encounters" banner. Rerolling allows you to save this selector to fill a specific elemental or role gap in your team later, rather than using it on your first carry.

Optimizing your start also preserves your long-term resource economy. By starting with a "God Roll" account, you can hoard Annulith for future power-creep characters like Lacrimosa, rather than spending resources to fix a weak initial roster.

### The Value of S-Rank Espers in Hethereau

S-Rank Espers in NTE possess unique mechanics that A-Rank characters cannot replicate. For example, Hotori’s time-manipulation abilities allow for precise combat control, while Sakiri provides the highest ATK buffs currently available. These characters do not just deal more damage; they fundamentally change how you interact with the game's combat systems and environmental puzzles.

### Maximizing the 1.0 Launch Generosity

The launch period offers a unique window where pre-registration rewards and "Day 1" mail bonuses provide enough currency for approximately 41 pulls almost immediately. This is significantly higher than the standard currency income for new accounts created later in the game's lifecycle. Utilizing these pulls through rerolling is the most effective way to "buy" power without spending real money.

### Strategic Use of the S-Rank Selector

The 50-pull selector on the "Strange Encounters" banner is a safety net. If you reroll and successfully pull a limited character like Hotori, you can then use your selector to pick a complementary support like Sakiri. This creates a synergistic team core that can carry an account through the first several months of content updates.

## How to Reroll

![How to Reroll](/img/neverness-to-everness-reroll-guide-2026/inline-2.webp)


The most efficient way to reroll in Neverness to Everness on PC and Mobile is the "Salting Method." This technique allows you to create multiple game accounts using a single email address, bypassing the need to register dozens of unique emails.

### The Email Salting Method (+ and .)

To use this method, take your primary email address (e.g., `player@gmail.com`) and add a plus sign and a number before the "@" symbol (e.g., `player+1@gmail.com`, `player+2@gmail.com`). Most email providers, including Gmail and Outlook, treat these as the same inbox, but the NTE login system recognizes them as unique accounts. Alternatively, you can insert periods into the address (e.g., `p.layer@gmail.com`), which functions similarly.

### Step-by-Step Reroll Walkthrough

1.  **Launch and Login:** Start the game and log in using a salted email address.
2.  **Tutorial Skip:** Once the game begins, look for the fast-forward button in the top-right corner during dialogue sequences. While some cutscenes are unskippable, most non-essential dialogue can be bypassed.
3.  **Combat Basics:** Complete the initial combat tutorial at Bridge Crossings. Focus on using your character's Basic Attacks and Resonance Skills to finish encounters quickly.
4.  **Reach Mint:** Progress through the story until you meet the NPC Mint. This is the trigger point that unlocks the Mailbox and the "Scarborough Fair" gacha system.
5.  **Claim Rewards:** Open your Mailbox and claim all pre-registration and launch rewards. This should net you roughly 41 Fabricated Dice and a significant amount of Annulith.
6.  **Enter Promo Codes:** Go to the Settings menu and enter active promo codes like `NTENOWTOENJOY` and `NTE0429` for additional pulls.
7.  **Pulling Strategy:** Spend your Fabricated Dice on the "Misty Tipsy Style" limited banner if you want Hotori, or use the discounted pulls on the "Strange Encounters" banner (8 Dice per 10-pull) to hit your first S-Rank faster.
8.  **Evaluate:** If you get the desired S-Rank, bind the account. If not, log out and repeat from Step 1 with a new salted email.

### Mobile vs. PC Rerolling Tips

PC users have the advantage of faster loading times and more precise controls during the mandatory combat sections. However, mobile users can often bypass certain account linking steps if they use "Guest Accounts" (though salting is still recommended for security). Avoid rerolling on PlayStation 5 for your initial attempts, as PSN accounts are difficult to unbind and the process is significantly more time-consuming.

### How to Unbind and Reset Accounts

If you are using the official Hotta account system, there is no "instant delete" button. You must log out and use a new salted email. Once you find an account you want to keep, navigate to the Account Center in the settings and link it to your permanent social media or primary email to ensure your progress is saved across platforms.

## Best Starters

![Best Starters](/img/neverness-to-everness-reroll-guide-2026/inline-3.webp)


When rerolling, your primary targets should be S-Rank characters that offer long-term meta viability. Characters that provide unique utility or massive area-of-effect (AoE) damage are prioritized over single-target specialists.

### S+ Tier: The 'God Roll' Targets

*   **Hotori (Limited S-Rank):** Hotori is the premier target for any reroll attempt. Her time-manipulation mechanics are unparalleled for both combat and open-world exploration. She can slow down enemy movements and environmental hazards, making her a "cheat code" for difficult Anomaly containment missions.
*   **Sakiri (Standard S-Rank):** Sakiri is currently the best ATK buffer in the game. She scales exceptionally well into the late game because her buffs are percentage-based, meaning she only gets stronger as your other characters' base stats increase.

### S Tier: Strong Meta Alternatives

*   **Jiuyuan (Standard S-Rank):** A high-mobility burst DPS who provides excellent crowd control. Jiuyuan is perfect for early-game mob clearing and is very beginner-friendly due to her large attack hitboxes.
*   **[Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) (Limited S-Rank):** Featured on "The Ichi-daime" banner, Nanally offers high sustained damage and strong elemental application. While Hotori is often preferred for her utility, Nanally is a top-tier damage dealer that can carry an account through all current content.

## Time Estimate

![Time Estimate](/img/neverness-to-everness-reroll-guide-2026/inline-4.webp)


A standard efficient reroll run in Neverness to Everness takes approximately 15 to 20 minutes. This includes the time required for account creation, the tutorial sequence, and the initial pulling phase.

### Average Run Duration

The bulk of your time will be spent in the initial tutorial and the walk to Bridge Crossings. By using the skip button and ignoring side NPCs, you can shave several minutes off the clock. The actual combat encounters are brief, provided you use your Resonance Skills as soon as they are off cooldown.

If you are rerolling on multiple accounts simultaneously, using a multi-instance manager on an emulator (if supported) can allow you to run 3-4 accounts at once, though this requires a high-end PC.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/neverness-to-everness-reroll-guide-2026/inline-5.webp)


Navigating the account systems and gacha mechanics of a new release can be confusing. Below are the most common questions regarding the Neverness to Everness reroll process.

### Account Safety and Ban Risks

Rerolling manually using the salting method is considered low risk. Developers generally do not ban players for creating multiple accounts to get a better start. However, avoid using automated scripts or third-party "reroll bots," as these can trigger anti-cheat systems and lead to permanent hardware or IP bans.

### Cross-Platform Progression FAQ

Neverness to Everness supports full cross-progression. If you reroll a successful account on PC or Mobile, you can link that Hotta account to your PlayStation Network (PSN) ID later. Keep in mind that once a Hotta account is linked to a PSN ID, it is often permanent, so only link your "final" successful reroll account.

### Banner and Pity Mechanics

The "No 50/50" system is the standout feature of NTE. When you pull an S-Rank on a limited banner like "Misty Tipsy Style" or "The Ichi-daime," you are guaranteed the featured character. Pity also carries over between limited banners; if you do 40 pulls on Nanally's banner and don't get an S-Rank, those 40 pulls count toward your pity on Hotori's banner.
