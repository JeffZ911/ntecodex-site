# Neverness to Everness Reroll Guide: How to Get the Best Start

## Why Reroll

![Why Reroll](/img/neverness-to-everness-reroll-guide-20260516/inline-1.webp)


Rerolling in Neverness to Everness is a strategy used to maximize the initial free resources provided at launch. Since the game features a unique board-game-style gacha system called Scarborough Fair, starting with a powerful S-Rank Esper like Hathor or Baicang ensures you can clear high-level Anomaly Investigations without hitting early power walls.

One of the primary reasons to reroll in NTE is the "No 50/50" guarantee on limited banners. Unlike many other gacha titles, if you land on an S-Rank tile on a limited board, you are 100% guaranteed to receive the featured character, such as [Hotori](/characters/hotori-best-team-nte). By rerolling, you can secure these limited units early and save your premium Solid Dice for upcoming Version 1.1 characters like Lacrimosa and Chaos.

### The Value of S-Rank Espers in NTE

S-Rank Espers possess unique passive abilities and higher base stats that A-Rank characters cannot match. For example, Hathor’s mobility allows for faster exploration of Hethereau, while Baicang’s shield-breaking capabilities are essential for endgame bosses. Starting with these units allows you to ignore the weapon (Arc) banners early on, as S-Rank characters provide a more significant power spike than high-tier gear.

### F2P Efficiency and Resource Management

For free-to-play players, rerolling is the most effective way to manage the Annulith economy. By getting your target character within the first 15-20 minutes, you can begin hoarding Fabricated Dice and Solid Dice for future updates. This efficiency is further bolstered by the Standard Banner S-Rank Selector, which becomes available after 50 pulls. If you reroll into one S-Rank and then use the selector for another, you can start the game with two of the best units in the game.

## How to Reroll

![How to Reroll](/img/neverness-to-everness-reroll-guide-20260516/inline-2.webp)


The reroll process in Neverness to Everness is relatively player-friendly because the game allows for "salted" email addresses and features a skippable tutorial. A successful reroll run takes approximately 15 to 20 minutes if you optimize your movement and skip dialogue.

### The Salted Email Method Explained

NTE accounts are tied to an email login. To avoid creating dozens of actual email accounts, use the "salting" technique. If your email is `username@gmail.com`, the game treats `username+1@gmail.com`, `username+2@gmail.com`, and so on, as unique accounts. However, all verification codes and communications will still go to your primary `username@gmail.com` inbox. This allows you to cycle through accounts rapidly until you hit your desired S-Rank.

### Navigating the Scarborough Fair Board Game Gacha

The gacha system, Scarborough Fair, functions like a board game. You roll dice to move a piece called a "Chuppa" across tiles. Each tile provides a reward.
1. **Apprentice Chests:** Common tiles with a 0.2% S-Rank chance.
2. **Hero Chests:** Gold tiles with a 3% S-Rank chance.
3. **Journey Together:** Character portrait tiles that guarantee the unit if you land on them.
4. **Board Modification:** At 70 pulls, the board transforms, turning many tiles into S-Rank tiles and increasing the success rate to 19.59%.

### Step-by-Step Reroll Checklist
1. **Create Account:** Sign up using a salted email address.
2. **Prologue:** Play through "Episode 0: Unforeseen Yet Foretold." Skip all cutscenes using the fast-forward button in the top right.
3. **Combat Tutorial:** Clear the first Anomaly encounter. Focus on parrying and dodging to finish the fight faster.
4. **Reach Hethereau:** Follow the objective markers to the Bridge Crossings area.
5. **Talk to Mint:** Interact with Mint near the Eibon Antique Shop to unlock the Scarborough Fair menu and the Mailbox.
6. **Claim Rewards:** Open your Mailbox to claim pre-registration rewards, launch bonuses, and any active codes.
7. **Redeem Codes:** Enter active promo codes in the settings menu (see the list below).
8. **Spend Dice:** 
   - Use **Fabricated Dice** on the "Strange Encounters" Standard Board (first 50 pulls are discounted).
   - Use **Solid Dice** or **Annulith** on the Limited Board (currently featuring Hotori).
9. **Check Results:** If you did not get an S-Rank, log out and repeat from step 1.

### PC vs. Mobile Rerolling Efficiency
PC is generally faster for rerolling due to shorter loading times and more precise combat controls. However, mobile players can use "Guest Accounts" to skip the initial email setup, though you must bind the account to a salted email immediately if you decide to keep it. PlayStation 5 players cannot easily reroll due to PSN account linking; it is recommended to reroll on mobile or PC first and then link that account to your PS5.

## Best Starters

![Best Starters](/img/neverness-to-everness-reroll-guide-20260516/inline-3.webp)


When rerolling, your primary goal is to secure an S-Rank character that remains viable into the late game. Because the Standard S-Rank Selector is available at 50 pulls, you should aim for a character that complements the unit you plan to pick later.

### S+ Tier: The 'God Start' Targets
*   **Hathor (S-Rank, Lakshana):** Hathor is currently the most sought-after reroll target. She utilizes the Plasma Arc type and specializes in massive AOE damage through her "Blade Wings" ability. Her high mobility makes navigating the verticality of Hethereau much easier.
*   **Hotori (S-Rank, Cosmos):** As the featured limited banner character, Hotori is a top-tier support/survival unit. Her time-stop mechanic allows you to freeze bosses in place, creating massive windows for your DPS to deal damage. Since she is limited, getting her early is a huge win for F2P accounts.

### S Tier: Excellent Alternatives
*   **Baicang (S-Rank, Incantation):** A Damage-role Esper who uses Synthesis Arcs. Baicang is a hybrid fighter capable of switching between melee and ranged attacks. He is specifically designed to break elemental shields, which is a core mechanic in the Anomaly Investigations.
*   **Jiuyuan (S-Rank, Anima):** A Solid Arc compatible DPS. Jiuyuan excels in sustained combat. Her kit revolves around building stacks to unleash a high-damage burst, making her one of the best characters for clearing waves of enemies.

### A-Rank Consolidation Prizes
If you pull these A-Rank units while chasing an S-Rank, your account is in a good spot:
*   **Haniel (Psyche):** An excellent ATK buffer who synergizes well with any DPS.
*   **Adler (Incantation):** A reliable shield provider who can keep your team alive in high-difficulty content.

## Time Estimate

![Time Estimate](/img/neverness-to-everness-reroll-guide-20260516/inline-4.webp)


The time required to reroll depends on how much of the story you are willing to skip and your familiarity with the combat system.

*   **Standard Reroll (15–20 minutes):** This involves following the quest markers naturally, participating in the mandatory combat tutorials, and navigating the menu to claim rewards.
*   **Speedrun Reroll (10–12 minutes):** By utilizing the fast-forward button for all dialogue and using high-damage combos to end the tutorial fights in seconds, you can reach the Scarborough Fair screen in roughly 10 minutes.
*   **Unlocking the S-Rank Selector (45–60 minutes):** If you decide to push an account further to reach the 50-pull milestone for the selector, expect about an hour of gameplay. This involves clearing the first set of Anomaly commissions for the photography studio.

### How to Speed Up the Tutorial
To maximize efficiency, do not explore Hethereau during the reroll. Stick strictly to the golden objective markers. Use the "Dodge Offset" mechanic during the combat tutorial to maintain your attack strings while avoiding enemy hits, as this reduces the time spent in combat encounters.

### When to Stop Rerolling
The ideal stopping point is when you pull any S-Rank from the Limited Board (Hotori) or a top-tier DPS from the Standard Board (Hathor or Baicang). Once you have one of these, use your Fabricated Dice to reach the 50-pull milestone on the standard banner. Use the resulting S-Rank Selector to pick a character that fills the missing role in your team (e.g., if you pulled Hathor, you might select a survival unit like Fadia or a secondary DPS like Jiuyuan).

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/neverness-to-everness-reroll-guide-20260516/inline-5.webp)


### Can I get banned for rerolling?
The risk is extremely low. Developers typically allow rerolling as it inflates early player numbers. However, avoid using automated scripts or macros to perform the reroll for you, as third-party software is a violation of the Terms of Service and can lead to an account ban.

### Does NTE have a guest account option?
Yes, the mobile version of Neverness to Everness offers a guest account feature. This is the fastest way to start a reroll. If you get a good result, you must go to the "User Center" in the settings and bind the guest account to a permanent email to ensure you don't lose your progress.

### Should I pull for Arcs (weapons) while rerolling?
No. You should prioritize characters during the reroll process. Arcs provide stat boosts, but a new character provides an entirely new playstyle and elemental coverage. Furthermore, high-quality Arcs can be earned through world exploration, chest hunting, and in-game events.

### What is the pity system in NTE?
Neverness to Everness uses a 90-pull hard pity system. Soft pity, known as Board Modification, begins at 70 pulls. On limited banners, there is no 50/50 mechanic; the first S-Rank you pull is guaranteed to be the featured character.

### Can I play on PS5 with a rerolled mobile account?
Yes. Neverness to Everness supports full cross-progression. Once you have finished rerolling on mobile or PC, go to the User Center and ensure your account is bound to a Hotta/Perfect World ID. You can then log in with those credentials on your PlayStation 5 to continue where you left off.
