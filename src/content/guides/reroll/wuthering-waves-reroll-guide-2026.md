# Wuthering Waves Reroll Guide 2026: How to Get the Best 5-Star Starters

Rerolling in Wuthering Waves remains the most effective strategy for players who want to jumpstart their journey with the strongest possible Resonators without spending a dime. As of May 14, 2026, the game is currently in the height of its Version 3.3 "Reverbs From the End of Galaxies" update, celebrating two years since its global launch. With the introduction of meta-defining units like Hiyuki and Denia, the value of a fresh account has skyrocketed. This guide covers everything you need to perform a fast, efficient reroll to secure the best 2026 starters.

## Why Reroll

![Why Reroll](/img/wuthering-waves-reroll-guide-2026/inline-1.webp)


Rerolling is the process of creating new accounts to use the initial free pulls (Convenes) provided to new players until you land a high-tier 5-star character. In 2026, the power gap between early 1.0 units and modern 3.0 powerhouses is significant, making a "perfect start" more desirable than ever.

### The 2nd Anniversary Advantage
The 2nd Anniversary window (Version 3.3) is arguably the best time in the game's history to reroll. Kuro Games has distributed a massive hoard of rewards to celebrate the milestone. Currently, new players can access over 50 free pulls almost immediately upon reaching Jinzhou. This includes:
*   **20x Radiant Tides:** Distributed across two phases in the mail.
*   **10x Radiant Tides:** Earnable via the "Bountiful Waves" event.
*   **10x Forging Tides & 10x Lustrous Tides:** Unlocked through the "Second Coming of Solaris" collaboration season.
*   **1,200+ Astrite:** From the "Star Bouncing" anniversary event.

Starting now allows you to dump these resources into the limited banners for Hiyuki and Denia, who are currently dominating the endgame meta.

### Power Creep and the 2026 Meta
While Wuthering Waves has maintained a respectable level of balance, the "Glacio Chafe" and "Fusion Burst" mechanics introduced in the 3.0 era have shifted the meta. Characters like Hiyuki provide significantly higher damage ceilings than older standard units. Rerolling ensures you aren't left behind by the increasing difficulty of the Tower of Adversity and the new Calamity Class weekly bosses.

| Character Type | Power Level (2026) | Sustainability |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Limited (Hiyuki/Denia)** | S+ Tier (Meta-Defining) | High (New Mechanics) |
| **Standard (Verina)** | S Tier (Universal Support) | Eternal (Best Healer) |
| **Standard (Encore/Calcharo)** | A Tier (Solid DPS) | Moderate (Outclassed) |

Additionally, rerolling now prepares your account with a solid foundation of resources for the confirmed Version 3.4 collaboration with Cyberpunk: Edgerunners, which features Lucy and a free 5-star Rebecca.

## How to Reroll

![How to Reroll](/img/wuthering-waves-reroll-guide-2026/inline-2.webp)


The reroll process in 2026 has been slightly optimized due to engine updates, but the core path remains the same. You will need roughly 30 to 45 minutes per attempt.

### The Dot-Salted Email Trick
To avoid creating dozens of new email addresses, use the "dot-salted" method. If your email is `rover@gmail.com`, Wuthering Waves treats `r.over@gmail.com`, `ro.ver@gmail.com`, and `rov.er@gmail.com` as unique accounts, but all verification codes will be sent to your primary `rover@gmail.com` inbox. This allows you to cycle through dozens of rerolls using a single email address.

### Optimized Speedrun Path
1.  **Skip Everything:** Use the "Skip" button in the top right corner for every dialogue sequence possible.
2.  **Combat Efficiency:** During the initial tutorial fights, focus on using your Resonance Skill immediately to clear groups.
3.  **The Crownless Trick:** In the first major boss fight against the Crownless, you can speed up the encounter by focusing on parrying (clashing). Successfully parrying the Crownless's dive attacks reduces its Vibration Strength significantly, leading to a faster transition to the final cutscene.
4.  **Movement:** Use the dash-jump-plunge mechanic to move across the map faster toward Jinzhou. Do not stop for chests or Echoes during a reroll run; focus only on the main quest markers.

### Unlocking the Convene System
The goal is to reach the first meeting with Madam Magistrate in Jinzhou and complete the "First Resonance" quest. Once you finish the dialogue at the Academy, the "Convene" (gacha) menu will unlock. 

**2026 Reroll Checklist:**
*   [ ] Complete the opening tutorial and reach Jinzhou.
*   [ ] Unlock the Convene menu (Level 5-8 typically).
*   [ ] Check the Mailbox for all 2nd Anniversary Tides.
*   [ ] Navigate to the "Events" tab and claim any "7-Day Login" or "Bountiful Waves" rewards.
*   [ ] Redeem active gift codes (Check the "Redeem Code" section in Settings).

## Best Starters

![Best Starters](/img/wuthering-waves-reroll-guide-2026/inline-3.webp)


Choosing who to keep is the most important part of the reroll. In 2026, the priority has shifted toward the new anniversary units and the upcoming collaboration synergy.

### S-Tier: The Anniversary Elites
*   **Hiyuki (Glacio, Sword):** She is the current gold standard for Main DPS. Her "Glacio Chafe" mechanic allows her to deal exponential damage to frozen or slowed targets. If you pull Hiyuki, your reroll is an immediate success.
*   **Denia (Fusion, Rectifier):** A premier Sub-DPS/Support. Denia specializes in "Fusion Burst," which amplifies the Resonance Liberation damage of the entire team. She is the perfect partner for high-burst units and is expected to have massive synergy with the upcoming Lucy from the Cyberpunk collab.

### A-Tier: Strong Standard Options
If you are pulling on the Standard/Beginner banner after using your limited Tides, these are the targets:
*   **Verina:** Despite being a launch character, Verina remains the #1 support in 2026. Her coordinated attacks and universal DMG buffs are still unmatched by any standard unit.
*   **Jinhsi & Shorekeeper:** These units from the 1.x and 2.x eras remain highly viable. Jinhsi’s Spectro burst is still top-tier for AoE clearing, while Shorekeeper provides unique utility that newer units haven't fully replaced.

### B-Tier: Niche but Viable
*   **Encore / Calcharo:** While still great for early-game carrying, they have been noticeably power-crept by the 3.0 Resonators. Keep them only if you also landed a top-tier limited unit on the same account.

## Time Estimate

![Time Estimate](/img/wuthering-waves-reroll-guide-2026/inline-4.webp)


Rerolling is a test of patience. Depending on your platform and skill, times will vary.

*   **Average Reroll Time:** 35–45 minutes. This accounts for unskippable cutscenes and the trek to Jinzhou.
*   **Speedrun Record (2026):** ~28 minutes. This requires perfect movement and utilizing the "Character Menu" skip to bypass certain transition animations.

### Efficiency Tips for PC and Mobile
*   **PC Advantage:** PC players have significantly faster loading times and better control for combat skips. If you are serious about rerolling, do it on PC first, then link the account to mobile later.
*   **Multi-Instance (PC Only):** Using emulators to run 3-4 instances of Wuthering Waves simultaneously is the most efficient method. You can sync your mouse movements to play four accounts at once, effectively cutting your reroll time to 10 minutes per account.
*   **Mobile Performance:** On mobile, ensure your "Graphics" settings are set to low during the reroll to prevent crashes or lag during the Jinzhou entry, which can waste several minutes.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/wuthering-waves-reroll-guide-2026/inline-5.webp)


### Account Safety
**Is rerolling bannable?**
No, rerolling manually is not a bannable offense. Kuro Games has never banned players for using the "dot-salted" email method. However, avoid using automated scripts or third-party "reroll bots," as these can trigger anti-cheat flags and lead to account termination.

### Banner Priority
**Which banner should I spend my Radiant Tides on?**
Prioritize Hiyuki’s limited banner first. A top-tier Main DPS will carry your account much further in the early game than a support. Once you have Hiyuki, you can spend remaining resources on Denia or save for the Version 3.4 Cyberpunk collaboration.

### Platform Differences
**Can I reroll on PS5?**
Rerolling on PS5 is difficult because accounts are often tied to your PSN ID. It is highly recommended to reroll on a mobile device or PC using a Kuro Games account, then link that account to your PlayStation once you are satisfied with the results.

**Is it worth rerolling for 5-star weapons?**
Generally, no. In the 2026 meta, characters provide much more utility and power than weapons. You can eventually earn the "5-Star Weapon Box" through standard gameplay and reaching Union Level milestones. Focus your reroll efforts on Hiyuki or Denia.
