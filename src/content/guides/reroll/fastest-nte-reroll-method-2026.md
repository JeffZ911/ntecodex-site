# Neverness to Everness Reroll Guide: The Fastest Method to Get S-Rank Espers

Starting your journey in the urban sprawl of Hethereau can be a daunting task, especially when the difficulty of Oddity encounters begins to spike after the first few chapters. In Neverness to Everness (NTE), your initial roster of Espers dictates how efficiently you can clear content and how much premium currency you can save for future updates. While the game provides several free units, the power gap between a standard A-Class Esper and a top-tier S-Rank like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) is significant. This guide provides the most efficient, time-tested reroll strategy for 2026 to ensure you start with a "God Account" without spending hours in the prologue.

## Why Reroll

![Why Reroll](/img/fastest-nte-reroll-method-2026/inline-1.webp)


Rerolling is the process of creating new accounts to use the initial free summons provided to players, repeating the process until a high-value character is obtained. In NTE, this is particularly valuable due to the way the Scarborough Fair gacha system interacts with early-game progression.

### The Value of S-Rank Espers in NTE

S-Rank Espers aren't just stat sticks; they possess unique mechanics that fundamentally alter combat. For instance, Nanally’s "Underboss" follow-up attack mechanic allows for consistent off-field damage that A-Rank units cannot replicate. Similarly, [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) provides a level of crowd control that makes high-level Oddity encounters significantly safer. By securing these units early, you bypass the "wall" many players hit around Level 20, where standard gear and low-rarity units begin to struggle against elite enemies.

### F2P Efficiency and the Scarborough Fair System

NTE utilizes a "No 50/50" gacha system for its limited banners, which is a departure from many other titles in the genre. When you pull an S-Rank on a limited banner like "The Ichi-daime," you are guaranteed to get the featured character. However, the currency required—Annulith and Solid Dice—is finite for Free-to-Play (F2P) players. Rerolling allows you to secure a limited S-Rank using only the initial launch rewards, effectively "saving" your earned Annulith for future power-creep or upcoming characters like [Hotori](/characters/hotori-best-team-nte).

## How to Reroll

![How to Reroll](/img/fastest-nte-reroll-method-2026/inline-2.webp)


The goal of a successful reroll in NTE is to reach the gacha menu as quickly as possible. Currently, the most efficient path takes approximately 10 to 12 minutes once you are familiar with the map layout of Hethereau.

### Step-by-Step Speedrun Path

1.  **Account Creation:** Do not use a guest account if you are on PC, as these can be difficult to bind later. Instead, use the "Salted Email" method. If your email is `username@gmail.com`, register your first account as `username+1@gmail.com`. The game treats this as a unique ID, but all verification codes go to your primary inbox.
2.  **The Prologue:** Skip every cutscene and dialogue exchange. The "Skip" button is located in the top right corner. During the initial combat tutorial, focus on using the protagonist's skill to break enemy shields quickly.
3.  **Reaching Bridge Crossings:** Follow the navigation marker until you reach the "Bridge Crossings" area. This is the first major hub where the open-world elements of Hethereau become accessible.
4.  **Locating Mint:** Look for the NPC named Mint near the central kiosk. Speaking with Mint is the trigger that unlocks the "Scarborough Fair" menu and grants access to your in-game Mailbox.
5.  **Claiming Rewards:** Open your Mailbox and "Claim All." As of May 2026, launch rewards include 20 Fabricated Dice and the A-Class Esper Haniel.
6.  **The Summoning Session:** 
    *   First, use your 20 Fabricated Dice on the "Strange Encounters" (Standard) banner. This banner features a 20% discount, meaning 8 Dice covers a 10-pull.
    *   Second, use any accumulated Annulith to purchase Solid Dice. These should be spent exclusively on the Limited Banner (currently Nanally’s "The Ichi-daime").
7.  **Evaluation:** If you do not pull an S-Rank within these initial 25–30 pulls, log out and repeat the process with `username+2@gmail.com`.

### Unlocking the Scarborough Fair Gacha

The Scarborough Fair isn't just a menu; it's a Monopoly-style board where you spend Dice to progress and earn rewards. When rerolling, don't get distracted by the secondary rewards on the board. Focus entirely on the "Instant Pull" button to see your results. If you land on a "Golden Space" during your rolls, you may receive additional Annulith, which can be funneled back into more Solid Dice for the limited banner.

### PC Multi-Instance Sync Strategy

If you are playing on PC using an emulator or the official launcher with multi-instance support, you can significantly cut down your reroll time. By running four instances of NTE simultaneously and using "Sync Input," you can perform the prologue actions on one screen while the other three mirror your movements. This brings your effective reroll time down to roughly 3 minutes per account.

## Best Starters

![Best Starters](/img/fastest-nte-reroll-method-2026/inline-3.webp)


Knowing when to stop is the hardest part of rerolling. In the current 2026 meta, certain characters provide significantly more value than others for a fresh account.

### S+ Tier: The "Must-Haves"

*   **Nanally (S-Rank, Anima):** Nanally is the current gold standard for Main DPS. Her "Underboss" mechanic triggers a powerful follow-up attack whenever an ally uses a skill, making her damage output passive and massive. She is the primary target for any serious reroller.
*   **Sakiri (S-Rank, Incantation):** If you prefer a tactical advantage, Sakiri is the best choice. She offers top-tier crowd control, capable of freezing Oddities in place, while simultaneously providing a team-wide ATK buff that scales with her Incantation level.

### S Tier: Strong Alternatives

*   **Hotori (S-Rank, Cosmos):** While often used as a sub-DPS, Hotori’s time-stop ability is invaluable for boss fights in the "Beyond the Rails" endgame mode. Her skill-mimicry allows her to copy the last ability used by your team, doubling your utility or burst damage.

### Who to Pick with the S-Rank Selector

NTE features a "Beginner's Choice" system where you can select a specific S-Rank after 50 pulls on the standard banner. 
1.  **Hathor:** If you pulled Nanally, pick Hathor. Her defensive shields perfectly complement Nanally's aggressive playstyle.
2.  **Jiuyuan:** If you pulled Sakiri, Jiuyuan is the better choice. Jiuyuan provides elemental shred that allows Sakiri’s Incantation damage to bypass enemy resistances.

### Account Security and Binding

**Is rerolling bannable?**
As of May 2026, there have been no reports of players being banned for manual rerolling or using the salted email method. Developers generally tolerate rerolling as it inflates early player numbers. However, avoid using third-party automated scripts that play the game for you, as these trigger anti-cheat software.

**How do I bind my "God Account"?**
Once you are satisfied with your pulls, navigate to Account Settings. Select "Bind Account" and link it to a permanent email address or a social media profile. This ensures you don't lose access if you clear your browser cache or uninstall the game.

### Gacha Mechanics and Pity Rules

**Does pity carry over between limited banners?**
Yes. If you spend Solid Dice on Nanally’s banner but fail to reach an S-Rank, your progress will carry over to the next Phase banner (e.g., Hotori’s upcoming banner). This makes every pull valuable even if you don't get the character immediately.

**Can I change my protagonist gender later?**
No. The choice you make during the initial tutorial is permanent for that account. If you realize you prefer the other protagonist's design or voice acting, you will need to start a new reroll cycle.
