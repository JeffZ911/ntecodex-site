# Zenless Zone Zero Reroll Guide: Best S-Rank Starters & Fast Method

Zenless Zone Zero (ZZZ) offers a high-octane combat experience where team synergy and character roles define your success. For many players, the journey begins long before the first high-level Hollow exploration—it starts at the Signal Search screen. Rerolling is the practice of restarting your account multiple times to utilize initial free pulls and secure a top-tier character. While HoYoverse has made the process more time-consuming than in some other gacha titles, the long-term benefits of a strong start can significantly impact your progression through the Inter-Knot levels and endgame content like Hollow Zero.

## Why Reroll

![Why Reroll](/img/zzz-reroll-guide-beginners-20260514/inline-1.webp)


Rerolling in Zenless Zone Zero is primarily about efficiency and power scaling. While every Agent in the game is viable with the right build and team composition, S-Rank Agents possess fundamentally higher base statistics and unique "Core Passives" that provide utility unavailable to A-Rank characters. Securing a top-tier S-Rank early allows you to save your precious Polychromes for future limited-edition banners rather than spending them to fill gaps in a weak roster.

### The 50-Pull Guarantee Explained

The most compelling reason to reroll is the "Stable Channel" (Standard Banner) mechanic. For new accounts, the Stable Channel features a permanent 20% discount on the first 50 pulls. This means a 10-pull only costs 8 Master Tapes. More importantly, the game guarantees an S-Rank Agent within these first 50 pulls. 

By rerolling, you are essentially trying to "snipe" the specific S-Rank Agent that best fits your desired playstyle or future team plans. If you pull an S-Rank early (for example, in your first 10 or 20 pulls), the guarantee resets to the standard 90-pull pity, effectively giving you a massive "head start" on your next S-Rank acquisition.

### F2P vs. P2W: Is Rerolling Necessary?

For Free-to-Play (F2P) players, rerolling is a strategic move to bridge the gap with players who can afford to purchase pulls. A "perfect" reroll account might start with two S-Rank Agents (one from the 50-pull guarantee and one lucky early pull), which would normally cost thousands of Polychromes. 

However, rerolling is not strictly necessary to enjoy the game. The starting trio—Anby (Stun), Billy (Attack), and Nicole (Support)—is remarkably well-balanced. Anby, in particular, remains a staple for many players well into the mid-game due to her reliable daze generation. If you prefer to experience the story without repetition, you can safely skip the reroll process.

### Long-term Account Value

The value of an S-Rank Agent increases as you reach the "Shiyu Defense" and "Hollow Zero" endgame modes. These modes often require two distinct teams. Having a foundational S-Rank like Von Lycaon or Alexandrina Sebastiane (Rina) ensures that your secondary team is as capable as your primary one. Furthermore, certain S-Ranks provide elemental shred or buffs that are "future-proof," meaning they will likely remain relevant even as newer characters are released.

## How to Reroll

![How to Reroll](/img/zzz-reroll-guide-beginners-20260514/inline-2.webp)


The reroll process in Zenless Zone Zero requires creating a new HoYoverse account for each attempt. Because there is no "Guest Account" feature, you must manage multiple sets of login credentials. The most efficient way to do this is through the "salted email" method.

### The Salted Email Method

If you use a Gmail or Outlook account, you can create "aliases" that HoYoverse recognizes as unique emails, but all point to your primary inbox. 
1. If your email is `proxyname@gmail.com`, register your first account as `proxyname+1@gmail.com`.
2. For the next attempt, use `proxyname+2@gmail.com`.
3. All verification codes sent to these aliases will arrive in your main `proxyname@gmail.com` inbox, allowing you to manage dozens of accounts without creating new email addresses.

### Speedrunning the Tutorial

A standard reroll run takes about 20 to 30 minutes. To minimize time, follow this optimized route:
1. **Dialogue Skipping:** Spam the "Skip" button in the top-right corner during all non-cinematic scenes.
2. **Combat Efficiency:** Use Billy’s dash-attacks and Anby’s EX Special to clear early mobs quickly. Do not worry about "perfect" play; focus on clearing the room.
3. **The "Random Play" Milestone:** Follow the quest markers until you reach the video store, "Random Play."
4. **Unlock Signal Search:** Complete the "The Proxy and the Hare" story commission. Once you return to the shop and talk to your sibling (Wise or Belle), the "Signal Search" (Gacha) menu will unlock (shortcut: F4 on PC).
5. **Claim Rewards:** Open your Mailbox to claim pre-registration rewards, which typically include at least 20 Master Tapes. Check the "Events" tab for any additional "Road to Proxy Greatness" rewards.

### Clearing Registry and Cache (PC & Mobile)

If you are rerolling on PC, you do not need to reinstall the game. Simply log out of the current account and log in with the next salted email. However, if you encounter "Too many attempts" errors, you may need to clear your local cache.
*   **PC:** Close the game and the HoYoverse launcher. Navigate to `C:\Users\[YourName]\AppData\LocalLow\Unity\HoYoverse_Zenless Zone Zero` and delete the folder.
*   **Mobile:** Go to Settings > Apps > Zenless Zone Zero > Storage > Clear Cache. Do not "Clear Data" unless you want to redownload the entire game.

### When to Stop: The 'Good Enough' Account

The "perfect" account is an S-Rank within the first 10–20 pulls, allowing you to save the rest of your 50-pull guarantee for later. However, for most players, simply getting **Von Lycaon** or **Rina** within the 50-pull guarantee is a successful run. If you hit any S-Rank that you personally enjoy playing, it is usually better to start playing the game rather than spending another 5 hours in the tutorial.

## Best Starters

![Best Starters](/img/zzz-reroll-guide-beginners-20260514/inline-3.webp)


When rerolling, you are pulling from the standard pool of six S-Rank Agents. While all are strong, some provide significantly more utility in the current meta.

### S-Tier: The Must-Haves

*   **Von Lycaon (Stun / Ice):** Lycaon is widely considered the best standard S-Rank. He provides massive Daze generation and possesses a unique passive that shreds Ice Resistance and increases the Stun DMG Multiplier. He is the backbone of the "Mono-Ice" team, which remains one of the strongest compositions in the game.
*   **Alexandrina Sebastiane (Rina) (Support / Electric):** Rina provides a powerful PEN Ratio buff to the entire team based on her own stats. Because PEN Ratio ignores a percentage of the enemy's defense, her value scales incredibly well into late-game content where enemies have massive defensive stats.

### A-Tier: Solid Alternatives

*   **Grace Howard (Anomaly / Electric):** Grace is the queen of "Shock" application. She doesn't rely on raw attack power as much as she does on triggering Attribute Anomalies. She is essential for "Disorder" teams that cycle through different elemental effects for massive burst damage.
*   **Koleda Belobog (Stun / Fire):** Koleda offers explosive Stun potential. When paired with Ben Bigger, her animations change, providing more invincibility frames and higher damage. She is a top-tier pick for Fire-weak content.

### Synergy Spotlight: Building Around Your First S-Rank

Your first S-Rank should dictate your early team building. 
*   If you pull **Lycaon**, pair him with **Soukaku** (A-Rank) and a DPS like **Ellen Joe** (if available) or **Corin**.
*   If you pull **Grace**, pair her with **Anton** (A-Rank) and **Anby** for a powerful Mono-Electric squad.
*   If you pull **Soldier 11**, you will need **Ben** and **Lucy** (A-Rank) to maximize her Fire damage potential.

### Limited Banner Rerolling: High Risk, High Reward

Some players choose to reroll for the current "Exclusive Channel" character (e.g., Ellen Joe or [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-14]). This is much harder because the base S-Rank rate is only 0.6%, and you do not have a 20% discount. You will typically only have enough Polychromes for 1 to 3 pulls on the limited banner after 30 minutes of play. This is only recommended for the most dedicated players.

## Time Estimate

![Time Estimate](/img/zzz-reroll-guide-beginners-20260514/inline-4.webp)


Rerolling is a test of patience. The time investment varies based on how "deep" you want to go into the account progression before deciding to reset.

### Efficiency Tips for Faster Runs

To hit the 20-minute mark, you must be disciplined:
1.  **Ignore Side NPCs:** Only talk to characters with the red "!" story icon.
2.  **Fast-Forward TVs:** In the Hollow Deep Dive (HDD) segments, use the acceleration button to speed up the Bangboo's movement across the grid.
3.  **Level Up Anby:** Spend a few resources to level Anby to 10 immediately. This makes the mandatory combat encounters significantly faster.

### Probability and Luck: Managing Expectations

With a 0.6% base rate for an S-Rank, the math is sobering. On average, you have a:
*   **5.8% chance** to see an S-Rank within your first 10 pulls.
*   **11.3% chance** within 20 pulls.
*   **26% chance** within 50 pulls (excluding the guarantee).

Most players will rely on the 50-pull guarantee. To get a *specific* character among the six available, you have a 16.6% chance per successful S-Rank pull. Statistically, you may need to reach the 50-pull guarantee on 6 different accounts to guarantee the specific Agent you want.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/zzz-reroll-guide-beginners-20260514/inline-5.webp)


### Account Security and Bans

**Can I get banned for rerolling?**
Generally, no. HoYoverse does not ban users for manually creating multiple accounts to reroll. However, using automated scripts, macros, or third-party software to "multi-box" or automate the tutorial can lead to a ban. Additionally, buying or selling rerolled accounts is against the Terms of Service and often results in the account being reclaimed by the original creator or banned.

### Platform Differences (PC vs. Mobile vs. PS5)

**Which platform is best for rerolling?**
PC is the fastest due to faster loading times and easier account switching. Mobile is convenient but can be slowed by thermal throttling and touch-screen navigation. PS5 is the most difficult to reroll on, as your game data is tied to your PlayStation Network (PSN) ID. To reroll on PS5, you would need to create multiple PSN accounts, which is highly inefficient.

### The 300-Pull Selector: A Safety Net

**What if I never get the character I want?**
Zenless Zone Zero includes a "Additional Recruitment" feature on the Stable Channel. After 300 total pulls (which may take several months for F2P players), you can choose one of the six standard S-Rank Agents to add to your roster for free. This is a one-time bonus. If you fail to reroll for Lycaon or Rina, you can eventually guarantee them through this system.

### Should I reroll for W-Engines?

**Are weapons worth rerolling for?**
No. In the early game, a new Agent provides a much larger power spike and opens up new gameplay styles. W-Engines (weapons) are stat sticks that enhance existing Agents. You should always prioritize getting a diverse roster of Agents before worrying about S-Rank W-Engines.
