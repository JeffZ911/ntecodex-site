# Neverness to Everness Reroll Guide: Fastest Method & Best Starters

Securing a powerful start in Neverness to Everness (NTE) can drastically change your long-term resource management. Because the game features a unique board-game style gacha system known as Scarborough Fair, understanding how to navigate the initial pulls is vital for any player looking to stay competitive without spending significant amounts of Annulith.

## Why Reroll

![Why Reroll](/img/how-to-reroll-neverness-to-everness/inline-1.webp)


Rerolling is the process of creating new accounts until your initial free summons yield a high-tier character. In Neverness to Everness, this strategy is more effective than in many other urban open-world RPGs due to the specific structure of the limited banners.

### Is Rerolling Necessary in NTE?

While you can complete the main story using the protagonist, Esper Zero, and the free A-Rank units provided during the prologue, rerolling offers a massive strategic advantage. The most compelling reason to reroll is the "no 50/50" guarantee on Limited Boards. Unlike other titles where you might lose a "coin flip" to a standard character, if you trigger an S-Rank on a limited banner like "The Ichi-daime," you are guaranteed to receive the featured unit. 

Starting with a Limited S-Class character like Nanally or Hotori allows you to save your future Annulith (the premium currency) for upcoming patches. Furthermore, the early game power spike provided by T0 characters like Jiuyuan or [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) allows you to breeze through "Beyond the Rails" challenges and high-level Hethereau combat encounters that would otherwise require significant grinding.

### The Value of Limited vs. Standard Characters

Every new account has access to a Standard Board that guarantees an S-Rank character within 50 pulls. By rerolling for a Limited S-Rank first using your initial Fabricated Dice, you can then use the Standard Board's selector to pick a unit that perfectly complements your team. This dual-threat setup is the most efficient way to build a foundation for your account.

## How to Reroll

![How to Reroll](/img/how-to-reroll-neverness-to-everness/inline-2.webp)


The reroll process in Neverness to Everness takes approximately 15 to 20 minutes once you become familiar with the skip points. Since the game does not currently offer a "Guest Account" option on most platforms, you will need to utilize specific account creation strategies.

### The Email Salting Method Explained

Email salting is the most effective way to manage multiple accounts without creating dozens of unique email addresses. This method works primarily with Gmail and certain other providers. By adding a plus sign and a number after your username (e.g., username+1@gmail.com, username+2@gmail.com), the game treats each as a unique registration, but all verification codes are sent to your primary username@gmail.com inbox. 

Alternatively, you can use the "dot" method (e.g., u.sername@gmail.com), which functions similarly. This allows you to bypass the lack of guest accounts and quickly cycle through new registrations until you hit your desired S-Rank.

### Unlocking the Gacha: The Eibon Antique Shop Milestone

To reach the Scarborough Fair gacha menu, you must progress through the prologue until you reach the Eibon Antique Shop. Follow these steps for the fastest route:

1.  **Launch and Login:** Log in using a salted email address.
2.  **Prologue Combat:** Complete the initial combat tutorial as Esper Zero. Focus on aggressive combos to clear waves faster.
3.  **Skip Dialogue:** Tap the skip button in the top right corner for all non-mandatory cutscenes.
4.  **City Navigation:** Use the sprint and dodge mechanics to move through the Hethereau streets toward the quest markers. Avoid unnecessary side fights with street anomalies.
5.  **The Eibon Meeting:** Enter the Eibon Antique Shop and complete the dialogue with the shopkeeper. This unlocks the Scarborough Fair menu.
6.  **Claim Mail:** Open your in-game mailbox to claim pre-registration rewards, launch milestones, and any active redeem codes. This should provide you with enough Solid Dice and Fabricated Dice for your first several rounds on the board.

### Account Binding and Security

Once you successfully pull a target like Hotori, stop rerolling immediately. Navigate to the account settings and ensure your salted email is properly secured. undisclosed regarding the ability to unbind or change the primary email address, so choose an email you intend to keep.

### The Scarborough Fair Mechanic

The gacha in NTE functions like a board game. You use Solid Dice (Standard) or Fabricated Dice (Limited) to move across tiles.
*   **Apprentice Chests:** These tiles offer a small chance at A-Rank items or characters.
*   **Hero Chests:** These are the primary targets, offering the highest probability for S-Rank characters.
*   **Movement:** Certain dice allow for fixed movement, while others are randomized. Use fixed dice to land precisely on Hero Chests whenever possible.

## Best Starters

![Best Starters](/img/how-to-reroll-neverness-to-everness/inline-3.webp)


When rerolling, your priority should always be the Limited S-Class characters currently on rotation. As of May 2026, the meta is dominated by high-utility DPS units that can manipulate the flow of combat.

### Top Priority: Limited Banner Targets

**Hotori (S-Class, Limited)**
Hotori is currently the most sought-after unit in the game. Her "Misty Tipsy Style" kit allows her to trigger a time-stop effect upon a successful perfect dodge or counter-attack. This utility is invaluable for boss fights in "Beyond the Rails," where enemy attack patterns are fast and punishing.

**Nanally (S-Class, Limited)**
Featured in "The Ichi-daime" banner, Nanally focuses on high-scaling follow-up damage. She excels in teams that utilize frequent ability rotations, as her passive allows her to strike enemies whenever an ally uses a Resonance Skill.

### Best Standard Characters for the 50-Pull Selector

If you successfully pull a limited character, use your 50-pull selector on the Standard Board for one of these two:

*   **Jiuyuan (S-Class, Standard):** The premier AoE DPS. Her burst covers a massive radius and applies a debuff that increases the damage enemies take from elemental reactions.
*   **Sakiri (S-Class, Standard):** The best offensive support in the current version. Sakiri provides a flat attack buff and increases the crit rate of the active character, making her a staple for any end-game team composition.

### Underrated A-Rank Units to Watch For

While S-Ranks are the goal, these A-Rank units provide excellent support and make the early game much smoother:

*   **Haniel (A-Rank, Support):** Provides consistent healing and energy regeneration.
*   **Adler (A-Rank, Sustain):** A defensive specialist who can generate shields based on his maximum health, which is vital for players still learning boss mechanics.
*   **Daffodill (A-Rank, Sub-DPS):** Offers Chaos-elemental off-field damage that pairs well with Esper Zero.
*   **Baicang (A-Rank, Ranged):** A solid ranged burst option for flying enemies or distant targets.

## Time Estimate

![Time Estimate](/img/how-to-reroll-neverness-to-everness/inline-4.webp)


Rerolling is a test of patience. Understanding the time commitment helps you decide if you want to settle for a "good" account or hold out for a "perfect" one.

### Speedrunning the Prologue

A standard run from account creation to the Eibon Antique Shop takes roughly 17 minutes. This includes mandatory combat and the time it takes to navigate the menu systems. 

*   **Tutorial & Intro:** 3 Minutes
*   **Prologue Combat & Movement:** 7 Minutes
*   **Dialogue & Cutscenes (Skipped):** 2 Minutes
*   **Unlock Eibon Antique Shop:** 3 Minutes
*   **Gacha Navigation & Pulls:** 2 Minutes

### PC vs. Mobile Reroll Efficiency

If you have access to a PC, it is highly recommended to perform your rerolls there. PC loading times are significantly faster, and the precision of a [mouse and keyboard](https://ntecodex.com/guides/wuthering-waves-controller-vs-mouse-and-keyboard) makes the initial combat sequences quicker. Some players utilize multi-instance managers on PC to run three or four accounts simultaneously, though this requires a robust system and may be subject to future developer restrictions.

The "Hunter Level 15" trap is something to avoid. Some guides suggest playing until Hunter Level 15 to get more dice. However, the time required to reach Level 15 (roughly 2 hours) is better spent performing six or seven 15-minute reroll cycles. Efficiency is about the number of Hero Chests you can open per hour, not the total number of pulls on a single mediocre account.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/how-to-reroll-neverness-to-everness/inline-5.webp)


**Can I get banned for rerolling or salting emails?**
Historically, developers like Hotta Studio have not banned players for email salting or manual rerolling. However, using automated scripts or third-party software to automate the process is a violation of the Terms of Service and can result in a permanent ban.

**How do I reroll on PlayStation 5?**
Rerolling on PS5 is difficult because the game binds directly to your PSN account. The most efficient method is to reroll on a PC or mobile device using a salted email. Once you get the character you want, bind that account to a Level Infinite or official developer ID, and then log in using that ID on your PlayStation 5.

**What is the account deletion cooldown?**
If you choose to delete an account rather than using a salted email, be aware there is a 7-day processing period. During this time, the email address cannot be reused for a new account. This is why the salting method is preferred.

**Do pre-registration rewards expire?**
Most launch rewards in Neverness to Everness are available for the duration of the 1.0 version. However, specific "Launch Week" bonuses may expire within 14 days of the April 29th release. Check the expiration timer on individual mail items in your inbox.

**Can I unbind a salted email later?**
undisclosed. Most modern gacha games allow you to link a secondary social media account (like X or Google) and eventually change the primary email, but you should treat the initial email as permanent for safety.
