### The Beginner Banner Guarantee Explained
Zenless Zone Zero features a "Stable Channel" (Standard Banner) with a specific mechanic designed for new players. For the first 50 pulls on this banner, you are guaranteed to receive one of the six standard S-Rank Agents. This guarantee disappears once the first S-Rank is pulled, reverting the pity to the standard 90-pull cycle.

Furthermore, this beginner phase offers a 20% discount on pulls. Instead of the usual 10 Master Tapes required for a 10-pull, you only need 8. This means you can hit the 50-pull guarantee using only 40 Master Tapes. Rerolling allows you to "fish" for the specific S-Rank that fits your preferred playstyle or provides the highest meta value.

## How to Reroll

Rerolling in Zenless Zone Zero is more time-consuming than in some other gacha games because there is no "Guest Account" option. Every reroll attempt requires a unique HoYoverse ID, which means you must register a new email address for every run.

### The Salted Email Strategy
To avoid creating dozens of entirely new email accounts, you can use the "salted email" method. This works primarily with Gmail. By adding a plus sign and a number after your username (e.g., `username+1@gmail.com`), HoYoverse treats it as a new registration, but all verification codes are sent to your primary `username@gmail.com` inbox.

1.  Go to the HoYoverse registration page.
2.  Enter your email with a suffix (e.g., `myemail+zzz1@gmail.com`).
3.  Verify the account and log into Zenless Zone Zero.
4.  If the pulls are unsatisfactory, log out and repeat with `myemail+zzz2@gmail.com`.

### Fastest Route to Inter-Knot Level 5
The gacha system, known as "Signal Search," is not available immediately. You must progress through the tutorial until you reach Inter-Knot Level 5.

*   **Prologue:** Complete the initial combat tutorial with Anby and Billy.
*   **The Video Store:** Follow the story prompts to unlock the Random Play video store and meet Enne.
*   **First Commission:** Complete the "Hollow Disaster" commission.
*   **Unlock Signal Search:** Once you return to the city and reach Inter-Knot Level 5, the "Signal Search" menu will appear in your HUD.

### Maximizing Your Starting Master Tapes

1.  **Mailbox:** Claim all pre-registration rewards, which include 20 Master Tapes and other currency.

## Best Starters

When rerolling, your goal depends on whether you are aiming for a standard S-Rank or the current limited-time character on the "Exclusive Channel."

### S-Tier: The Must-Haves (Lycaon & Ellen Joe)
**Von Lycaon** is widely considered the best standard S-Rank to pull. As a Stun Agent, his ability to quickly incapacitate bosses is unmatched. He also provides a significant Ice Resistance shred and DMG bonus to stunned enemies. He is the perfect partner for **Ellen Joe**, the first limited S-Rank.

**Ellen Joe** is the primary target for "Deep Rerollers." Since she is on the Exclusive Channel, you must use Encrypted Master Tapes (or Polychromes) to get her. She is a top-tier Ice Attacker with high mobility and massive burst damage. If you manage to pull Ellen Joe early, stop rerolling immediately—your account is already in the top 1%.

### A-Tier: Strong Foundations (Rina & Grace)
**Rina** is the game's premier Electric Support. She provides a team-wide Penetration Ratio buff based on her own stats. Because Penetration becomes more valuable as enemy defense levels increase in late-game content, Rina is an incredibly safe long-term investment.

**Grace Howard** is an Anomaly Agent. Unlike traditional attackers, she focuses on applying the "Shock" status effect. She is essential for "Disorder" team comps, which rely on stacking different elemental anomalies to trigger massive burst damage.

### B-Tier: Solid DPS Options (Soldier 11 & Nekomata)
**Soldier 11** and **Nekomata** are pure Attack Agents. While they deal excellent damage, they are ranked slightly lower because pure DPS characters are the most likely to be powercrept by future limited banners.
*   **Soldier 11:** Requires precise timing on her basic attacks to trigger Fire damage. She is excellent for players who enjoy rhythm-based combat.
*   **Nekomata:** A high-speed Physical attacker who excels at backstabbing enemies for increased damage.

### Understanding Agent Roles and Elements
To build a functional team, you must understand the roles:
*   **Attack:** Primary damage dealers.
*   **Stun:** Focuses on filling the enemy's Daze bar to trigger Chain Attacks.
*   **Support:** Provides buffs to the team or energy regeneration.
*   **Anomaly:** Specializes in elemental debuffs and status effects.
*   **Defense:** Focuses on parrying and damage mitigation.

A standard effective team usually consists of **1 Attacker + 1 Stunner + 1 Support/Anomaly**.

### Tips to Speed Up the Tutorial
*   **Skip Dialogue:** While not all scenes are skippable, look for the "Skip" button in the top right corner during non-rendered cutscenes.
*   **Sprint Constantly:** Use the dash button (Right Click/Shift/RB) while moving through the city to save seconds.
*   **Efficient Combat:** Use Anby’s "Bolt" (Basic Attack x3 + Special) to clear mobs quickly. Don't spend too much time on small enemies; focus on moving to the next objective.

## FAQ

### Can I get banned for rerolling?
Manual rerolling is generally safe. HoYoverse has historically not banned players for creating multiple accounts to reroll. However, using automated scripts or third-party "reroll bots" is a violation of the Terms of Service and will likely result in a permanent ban. Also, avoid buying "starter accounts" from third-party sites, as these are often reclaimed or banned.

### Do I get pre-registration rewards every time?
Yes. As of the July 2024 launch period, every new account that reaches Inter-Knot Level 5 can claim the pre-registration rewards from the in-game mailbox. These rewards are not tied to a specific "pre-registered" email address but are given to all players during the launch window.

### How do I reroll on PS5?
Rerolling on PlayStation 5 is significantly more difficult. Since your HoYoverse account is linked to your PlayStation Network (PSN) ID, you would need to create a new PSN account for every reroll attempt. It is much faster to reroll on a PC or mobile device and then link the successful HoYoverse account to your primary PSN ID.

### What is the 300-pull selector?
Zenless Zone Zero features a one-time "Additional Rewards" milestone on the Stable Channel. After performing a total of 300 pulls on the standard banner, you are allowed to choose one of the six standard S-Rank Agents for free. This acts as a safety net, ensuring that even if your reroll luck is poor, you will eventually get the character you want (like Lycaon) after a few months of play.
