## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/fastest-nte-reroll-strategy-guide/inline-5.webp)


### Is rerolling bannable?
No, Hotta Studio has not historically banned players for manual rerolling or using the salted email method. However, you should avoid using automated scripts or third-party macros to play the game for you, as these can be detected by the anti-cheat system and lead to account termination.

### Can I reroll on PlayStation 5?
Rerolling on PS5 is extremely difficult because the game automatically links to your PlayStation Network (PSN) ID. To reroll effectively, it is recommended to perform the process on a PC or mobile device first. Once you have secured the account you want, you can then use the "Account Link" feature in the settings to bind that specific Hotta account to your PSN ID.

### Does pity carry over?
Yes. If you pull on a limited character board like "Misty Tipsy Style" but do not reach an S-Rank, your progress (pity) will carry over to the next limited character board. This is tracked via **Solid Dice** usage. Note that pity on the "Strange Encounters" standard banner is separate and does not interact with limited banners.

### Should I pull for Arcs (weapons)?
During the rerolling phase, you should prioritize characters. While 5-star Arcs provide significant stat boosts, a new character provides entirely new gameplay mechanics and elemental coverage. You can earn strong 4-star Arcs through in-game activities and urban exploration rewards.

### What is the S-Rank drop rate and pity?
The base rate is 0.99%. Soft pity begins at **70 pulls**, where the rate for an S-Rank increases significantly with every subsequent pull until a 5-star is guaranteed at **80 pulls** (hard pity). Because the "Strange Encounters" banner is discounted, you can reach that first 50-pull selector milestone very quickly through active play.
