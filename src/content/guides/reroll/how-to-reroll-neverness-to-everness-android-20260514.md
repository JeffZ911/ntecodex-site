### The Salting Method Explained

Gmail ignores any characters after a plus sign (+) in the username. If your email is `example@gmail.com`, you can register for NTE using `example+1@gmail.com`, `example+2@gmail.com`, and so on. The game treats these as separate accounts, but all verification emails and password reset links are sent to your primary `example@gmail.com` inbox.
