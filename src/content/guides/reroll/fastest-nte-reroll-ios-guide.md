### Account Management & Safety

**Can I get banned for rerolling?**
Historically, Hotta Studio has not banned players for using the salted email method. It is a common practice in their previous titles. However, using automated scripts or macros to reroll on an emulator (if you are bridging from iOS) can lead to account flags. Stick to manual rerolling on your iPhone or iPad.

**Is there a guest account option on iOS?**
No. Neverness to Everness requires a valid login (Email, Apple ID, or Social) to enter the game. This is why the salted email method is the only viable way to reroll without creating multiple Apple IDs.

**How do I delete an account on iOS?**
Navigate to the User Center in the settings menu and select "Account Deletion." Be aware that there is a 7-day processing period. This is why we recommend salted emails instead of deletion; you can simply abandon the "bad" email and start a new one instantly.

### Currency Management Tips

**Should I pull for Arcs (Weapons) while rerolling?**
No. Arcs are significantly easier to obtain through gameplay and farming later in the game. In the early stages, having a diverse and powerful character roster is much more important for account progression than having a signature weapon.

**Does pity carry over?**
Yes. If you pull 40 times on Nanally's banner and don't get an S-rank, those 40 pulls will count toward the 90-pull pity on the next limited character banner. This makes "failed" rerolls that you decide to keep still somewhat valuable.
