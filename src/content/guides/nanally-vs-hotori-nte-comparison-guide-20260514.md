Her Plasma Esper type also makes her highly effective against enemies weak to high-frequency disruption.

### Who is Hotori Best For?

Because her value is tied to the strength of the skills she "records," she is widely considered a future-proof unit that will only get stronger as more powerful characters are released.

### Damage Output and Playstyle
Nanally operates on a high-octane "Follow-up" system governed by her **Ichi-daime's Authority** mechanic. In combat, Nanally builds stacks through her basic attacks and the **Colucci Howling Technique**. Once these stacks reach a threshold, she triggers automatic follow-up strikes that deal massive Anima damage. This playstyle is relatively "selfish," meaning Nanally wants to stay on the field for as long as possible to maximize her damage windows. Her "Underboss" summons act as autonomous turrets, providing consistent pressure even while she is repositioning.

Hotori, conversely, manages the **Non-Closed Timepiece** energy system. She does not deal the same level of sustained raw damage as Nanally. Instead, her contribution comes in concentrated bursts of utility. Her playstyle revolves around "recording" the state of the battlefield and replaying high-impact skills. This makes her a technical Sub-DPS; you swap her in, execute a precise sequence to manipulate time, and swap back to your Main DPS to capitalize on the opening she creates.

### Skill Synergy and Team Roles
The most significant mechanical difference lies in their specific active skills

*   **Nanally's Colucci Howling Technique:** This skill launches a series of rapid-fire strikes. Its primary purpose is to accelerate the accumulation of Ichi-daime stacks. When her Ultimate is active, the frequency of these strikes increases, turning her into a blur of Anima energy.
*   **Hotori's Present Replay:** This is arguably the most unique skill in NTE. It allows Hotori to "record" a previously used skill and replay it. In high-level play, this is used to double-cast powerful buffs or high-damage abilities from other team members.
*   **Hotori's World's Tide:** This Ultimate creates a localized time-stop field. While enemies are frozen, your team can reposition or build up "Blossom" stacks without fear of retaliation.

In terms of team synergy, the "Blossom" reaction (Anima + Cosmos) is currently one of the strongest elemental interactions in the game. ### Investment and F2P Friendliness
Nanally is exceptionally F2P-friendly because her signature Arc, **Ready-Ready**, provides a straightforward boost to her follow-up damage. Pull Hotori if you are looking to build a "top-tier" competitive team for the months to come.
