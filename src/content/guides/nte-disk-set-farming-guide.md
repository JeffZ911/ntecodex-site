## Overview

The progression system in Neverness to Everness (NTE) centers on the Console, a hardware-themed interface where players equip gear to enhance their Espers. Unlike traditional RPG gear slots, the Console uses a spatial management system where the physical arrangement of components matters as much as their individual stats.

### Understanding the Console Layout

Each character possesses a unique Console board, often resembling a handheld gaming device or a retro computer interface. This board features a central slot for a Cartridge and a grid-based area for Modules. The shape of the grid varies between characters, influencing which Modules can be effectively slotted. High-tier builds require balancing the powerful set bonuses of Cartridges with the geometric puzzle of fitting high-stat Modules into the available space.

### Cartridges vs. Modules

Cartridges and Modules serve distinct purposes within the Console system. Cartridges are the primary source of set bonuses, functioning as the "core" of the build. Modules, on the other hand, provide the granular stat boosts needed to hit critical thresholds for CRIT Rate, ATK, or Elemental DMG.

| Feature | Cartridges | Modules |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Shape** | Circular (Fixed) | Tetris-shaped (Variable) |
| **Primary Function** | 2-piece and 4-piece Set Bonuses | Individual Stat Scaling |
| **Rarity Range** | C-Rank to S-Rank | C-Rank to S-Rank |
| **Acquisition** | Rabbit Hole Domains | Rewind Menu / Carrota Coins |
| **Equip Limit** | 1 per Console | Grid-dependent (usually 4-6) |

### How to Unlock the Rabbit Hole

The Rabbit Hole serves as the primary farming instance for high-rarity Cartridges. To access it, you must progress through the main urban chronicle until you reach Hunter Level 20. The entrance is physically located in the Cape Square district, marked by a shimmering Anomaly rift near the Midas ARC Workshop. While the Rabbit Hole is accessible early, S-Rank (Gold) gear drops are locked behind Hunter Level 30. Attempting the Rabbit Hole consumes Character Pixels, the game's stamina currency.

**Hunter Level Gear Milestones:**
*   **Level 1-19:** Mostly B-Rank and C-Rank drops.
*   **Level 20:** A-Rank (Purple) gear begins appearing.
*   **Level 30:** S-Rank (Gold) gear becomes a guaranteed drop from high-difficulty floors.
*   **Level 45+:** Increased drop rate for "Radiant" sub-stats on S-Rank gear.

### Module Shaping and Tetris Optimization

**Sub-stat Priority by Role:**
*   **Main DPS:** CRIT Rate > CRIT DMG > Elemental DMG % > ATK %.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/nte-disk-set-farming-guide/inline-5.webp)


Mastering the rhythm of swapping characters is the key to maximizing the "Stagger" window on bosses.

### Mastering the Esper Cycle

The Esper Cycle wheel indicates which elements are adjacent. Triggering reactions between adjacent elements generates Concentration. Once the Concentration Meter is full, your off-field characters' Intro Skills become available. 

### Maximizing the Stagger Window

When an enemy's white Break Gauge is depleted, they enter a "Staggered" state. During this window, all damage taken is multiplied by 2.0x.
1.  **Setup:** Use Sakiri to group enemies and apply resistance shreds.

3.  **Burst:** Swap to Nanally and immediately use her Ultimate. The animation lock of the Ultimate is ignored during the Stagger window, allowing for a follow-up Skill chain.

### Farming and Stamina Management

**Where is the Rabbit Hole?**
The Rabbit Hole is located in the Cape Square district of Hethereau. It is a physical door south of the Midas ARC Workshop. You can fast-travel to the nearby "Cape Square Central" anchor point for quick access.

**What are Character Pixels?**
Character Pixels are the stamina currency in NTE. You have a daily cap of 240 Pixels. They regenerate at a rate of 1 Pixel every 6 minutes. Farming a single Rabbit Hole floor typically costs 40 Pixels.

| Activity | Pixel Cost | Primary Reward |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Rabbit Hole (Floor 1-5) | 40 | Cartridges |
| Anomaly Zone (Boss) | 60 | Arc Ascension Materials |
| Rewind Simulation | 20 | Carrota Coins |

### Gear Rarity and Progression

**Can I farm S-Rank gear early?**
No. While you can enter the Rabbit Hole at Hunter Level 20, S-Rank (Gold) Cartridges only have a reliable drop rate once you reach Hunter Level 30. It is recommended to spend your Pixels on character and Arc ascension materials until you hit the Level 30 milestone.

**How do I get more Modules?**
Modules are primarily obtained by spending Carrota Coins in the "Rewind" menu. You can also find them as rewards for exploring the open world and completing "Urban Oddity" side quests.

### Gacha and Currency Tips

**Is there a 50/50 in the gacha?**
Neverness to Everness uses a "Fair" board system. There is no 50/50 chance to lose the featured character. Instead, you are guaranteed the featured S-Rank character at exactly 90 pulls. If you pull an S-Rank earlier, it will always be the featured unit, and the counter resets.
