## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does a higher refresh rate actually help with NTE Chaos reactions?
Yes, a higher refresh rate reduces the "input lag" between your mouse click and the Esper ability appearing on screen. This is critical for Chaos and Psyche reactions that rely on specific timing windows to maximize damage multipliers.

### Is 1440p better than 4K for Neverness to Everness?
For most players, 1440p is the "sweet spot" because it allows for high frame rates without requiring a top-tier GPU. Maintaining a stable 144+ FPS at 1440p provides a smoother experience for tracking Anomaly effects than a fluctuating 60 FPS at 4K.

### Can I use a vertical mouse for high-level NTE combat?
While the Logitech MX Vertical is excellent for ergonomics, it has a lower polling rate than dedicated gaming mice. It is best suited for the "grind" phase of the game—such as car customization or material farming—rather than frame-perfect boss encounters.

### Why is the Secretlab chair recommended over a mesh office chair?
Mesh chairs are excellent for breathability, but many players find the firm, multi-layered foam of the TITAN Evo 2022 better for the "active" seating positions used during intense gaming. The integrated 4-way lumbar support is specifically designed to support the spine during the repetitive motions of Esper combat.
