## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the most important piece of gear for long sessions?
The chair is the most critical investment for anyone playing more than six hours a day. While a better monitor improves the visual experience, a high-quality ergonomic chair like the Secretlab TITAN Evo prevents long-term physical issues like lower back pain and poor posture.

### Is a 1440p monitor worth it for gacha games?
Yes, because most modern titles use high-resolution assets and intricate character designs that are often blurred on 1080p screens. A 1440p display provides a 77% increase in pixel density over 1080p, making the urban environments of Hethereau look significantly sharper.

### Why use an MMO mouse for an action RPG?
Action RPGs often require managing skills, items, and menu shortcuts simultaneously. An MMO mouse like the Razer Naga V2 Pro allows you to map these functions to your thumb, reducing the "keyboard dance" and allowing your left hand to focus entirely on character movement and dodging.

### How often should I replace my gaming chair?
A high-quality chair with cold-cure foam should last between 5 and 7 years. Budget chairs using standard sponge foam often lose their structural integrity within 12 months, leading to a "bottoming out" feeling where you can feel the metal frame beneath the seat.
