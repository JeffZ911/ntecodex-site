## Overview

![Overview](/img/nte-character-progression-guide-20260521/inline-1.webp)


Character progression in Neverness to Everness is a multi-layered process that balances your account-wide standing with the individual growth of your Espers. Unlike traditional RPGs where level is the only metric, NTE utilizes a dual-leveling system that dictates your access to gear, story content, and character power ceilings.

### Hunter Level and Appraisal Milestones

Your progression is governed primarily by your **Hunter Level**, which represents your overall account experience. As your Hunter Level increases, you will reach specific **Appraisal Milestones**. Increasing your Appraisal Level raises the "World Level," making enemies in the open world and Anomaly Zones more difficult while significantly improving the rarity and drop rate of rewards.

It is vital to complete Appraisal Breakthrough missions as soon as they appear. Delaying these missions caps your Espers' maximum level, preventing you from tackling higher-tier content like the "Rabbit Hole" or advanced "Houdini's Magic Stage" challenges.

### Character Pixels Management

**Character Pixels** serve as the primary stamina resource in NTE. Every major progression activity—from farming EXP materials to hunting for high-end Cartridges—requires the consumption of Pixels.

*   **Regeneration:** Pixels regenerate over time at a fixed rate.
*   **Cap:** [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-21].
*   **Priority:** During the early game, focus your Pixels on "Houdini's Magic Stage" for character EXP and "Bubble Can Factory" for currency. Once you reach Hunter Level 40, shift your focus to Anomaly Zones for 5-star Cartridges.

### The Awakening & Bond Systems

The **Awakening system** is NTE’s version of a talent tree. By obtaining duplicates of a character or using rare universal materials, you unlock nodes that provide flat stat boosts or mechanical changes to skills. A player-friendly feature of NTE is the ability to **re-spec Awakening nodes at any time for zero cost**, allowing you to swap between a "Main DPS" setup and a "Support/Breaker" setup depending on your current team needs.

The **Bond System** is progressed by using characters in combat or interacting with them in the city. Higher Bond levels unlock unique character stories and provide minor permanent stat buffs that contribute to your overall "City Tycoon" standing.

**Leveling Priority:** 
1. **Main DPS:** Always keep your primary damage dealer at the maximum level allowed by your Appraisal Level.

3. **Supports/Utility:** Can generally function effectively even if they are 15-20 levels below the cap, as their primary value comes from skill utility rather than raw stats.

## Best Weapons

In NTE, weapons are referred to as **Arcs**. These are high-tech devices that channel an Esper's energy. Arcs are classified into five distinct types: **Plasma, Solid, Gas, Liquid, and Synthesis**. For maximum efficiency, you must match the Arc type to the Esper’s specialization to trigger unique synergy bonuses.

### Understanding Arc Types

Each Arc type favors a specific combat style
*   **Plasma:** Generally focuses on CRIT Rate and high-frequency attacks.

*   **Gas:** Optimizes Elemental Mastery and reaction damage.
*   **Liquid:** Focuses on energy regeneration and Ultimate cooldowns.
*   **Synthesis:** Hybrid Arcs that provide versatile buffs based on team composition.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/nte-character-progression-guide-20260521/inline-3.webp)


NTE utilizes a unique gear architecture consisting of **Cartridges** and **Modules**. This system allows for high customization, but requires careful stat balancing to reach end-game viability.

### Cartridge Set Bonuses

Cartridges come in sets. Equipping 2 or 4 pieces of the same set activates powerful passive bonuses.

| Set Name | 2-Piece Bonus | 4-Piece Bonus | Recommended Roles |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fireflies and the Forest** | +15% Anima DMG | Increases ATK by 20% for 10s after using an Entry Attack. | Anima DPS ([Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide), Mint) |

| **Static Haze** | +15% Chaos DMG | Triggering "Scorch" reduces enemy DEF by 15%. | Chaos Sub-DPS |

### Module Specialization Explained

Each Cartridge has three **Module slots (Type I, II, and III)**. While Type I and II modules usually provide flat HP and ATK, **Type III Modules** are the most critical.
*   **Type III Specialization:** If you equip a Type III Module that matches your character's specialization (e.g., a "Striker" module on a Main DPS), you unlock a hidden **Bonus CRIT Rate** or **Elemental DMG** modifier.
*   **Main Stat Priority:** For damage dealers, you should prioritize **CRIT DMG** on the Type III slot and **Elemental DMG%** (matching the character's element) on the Type II slot.

### End-game Stat Optimization

Once you reach the "Rabbit Hole" Anomaly Zone, your goal is to farm for Cartridges with optimal sub-stats. The "Golden Ratio" for most DPS characters in NTE is a 1:2 ratio of CRIT Rate to CRIT DMG. Aim for at least 60% CRIT Rate before focusing entirely on CRIT DMG and Elemental Mastery.

## Team Comp

![Team Comp](/img/nte-character-progression-guide-20260521/inline-4.webp)


The core of NTE’s combat is the **Elemental Reaction** system. Building a team that can consistently trigger high-damage reactions is more important than simply putting four S-Rank characters together.

### Elemental Reactions: Scorch, Nova, and Discord

Understanding how elements interact is the key to mastering Hethearau's combat
*   **Scorch (Incantation + Chaos):** Deals sustained damage over time and reduces enemy Break resistance.
*   **Nova (Chaos + Psyche):** Triggers a high-damage explosion that deals AoE damage based on the character's ATK.
*   **Discord:** This is the pinnacle reaction. By applying **Scorch** and then triggering **Nova** on the same target, you create a **Discord** state. This state completely shreds enemy "Break bars," leaving them vulnerable to massive damage.

### The 'Break Discord' Meta Team

This is currently the most popular high-end composition for clearing difficult content
*   **Main DPS:** Nanally (Incantation) - Provides the primary damage and applies Incantation status.
*   **Sub-DPS/Breaker:** Daffodil (Chaos) - Used to trigger Scorch and shred Break bars.
*   **Support:** Haniel (Psyche) - Triggers Nova to complete the Discord reaction and provides healing.
*   **Flex/Support:** Sakiri or Jiuyuan - Provides Anima-based grouping or additional defensive utility.

### Hyper Chiz Team (Cosmos Focus)

Chiz is a unique character unlocked for free by reaching **City Tycoon Level 18**. As a Cosmos element user, Chiz interacts differently with the elements
*   **Remora (Cosmos + Lakshana):** Creates a vacuum effect that pulls enemies together.
*   **Hyper-Carry Setup:** Pair Chiz with characters who provide off-field elemental application (like Edgar or Sakiri) to keep the Cosmos reactions cycling.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/nte-character-progression-guide-20260521/inline-5.webp)


Mastering the "Quick-swap" flow is essential for maintaining the Discord reaction and maximizing your damage output.

### The Quick-Swap Flow

In NTE, you do not stay on one character for long. You should be constantly cycling through your team to utilize **Entry Attacks** and **Redirect Skills**.
1.  **Setup:** Start with your Support (e.g., Haniel) to drop buffs or deploy off-field Psyche application.
2.  **Status Application:** Swap to your Chaos character (e.g., Daffodil) to trigger the initial Scorch reaction.
3.  **The Burst:** Swap to your Main DPS (e.g., Nanally). Her **Entry Attack** will often deal bonus damage to Scorched enemies. Use her full skill combo while the enemy's Break bar is low.
4.  **The Discord Finish:** Use your Psyche or Cosmos character to trigger the final reaction, resetting the cycle.

### Maximizing Break Intensity

When an enemy's Break bar (the white bar under their HP) is depleted, they enter a "Stunned" state. During this window, all damage taken is increased by [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-21]. 

To maximize Break Intensity
*   Use **Heavy Attacks** or specific "Breaker" class skills.
*   Trigger **Critical Riposte**: By dodging an enemy attack at the last second, you can trigger a Riposte (like Mint’s "Caramel Crisp" move), which deals massive Break damage and often resets your skill cooldowns.

### Ultimate Timing and Energy Recovery

Do not use all four Ultimates at once. Instead, stagger them to ensure you always have an "i-frame" (invincibility frame) available to dodge unavoidable boss attacks. Use Arcs with "Energy Recovery" passives on your Supports to ensure their Ultimates are ready every rotation.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/nte-character-progression-guide-20260521/inline-6.webp)


**How do I unlock Chiz for free?**
You can unlock Chiz by reaching **City Tycoon Level 18**. This is achieved by completing urban tasks, side quests, and participating in the city's economy (buying cars, decorating your apartment, and managing businesses).

**What is the Hunter Level cap?**
As of the current version, the Hunter Level cap is **Level 60**. Reaching this cap allows you to ascend your characters to their final "Breakthrough" stage.

**Can I re-spec my character's Awakening?**
Yes. NTE allows you to re-allocate your Awakening nodes at any time for zero cost. This encourages experimentation with different builds for the same character.

**Where do I spend Character Pixels?**
Pixels should be spent in **Anomaly Zones**. For EXP, head to "Houdini's Magic Stage." For gear, farm the "Rabbit Hole" or specific elemental zones.

**Is there a 50/50 in the gacha?**
No. Neverness to Everness features a **100% guarantee** on limited character banners. If you reach the pity threshold (typically 80 pulls), you are guaranteed to receive the featured S-Rank character.
