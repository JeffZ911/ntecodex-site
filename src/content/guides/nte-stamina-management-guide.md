# Neverness to Everness Stamina Management Guide: Best Mint Build

## Overview

![Overview](/img/nte-stamina-management-guide/inline-1.webp)


Progression in Neverness to Everness (NTE) is governed by two distinct energy systems that dictate how often you can farm combat upgrades and how quickly you can expand your urban empire. Understanding the interplay between Character Pixels and City Stamina is essential for any Appraiser looking to optimize their daily routine.

### Understanding Character Pixels

Character Pixels serve as your primary combat stamina. These are consumed whenever you enter Anomaly Zones to farm Esper EXP, Arc ascension materials, or Cartridge sets. Unlike many contemporary gacha titles, Pixels in NTE are consumed immediately upon entering a stage, meaning failure or abandonment results in a total loss of the spent resource.

The regeneration rate for Character Pixels is fixed at 1 Pixel every 6 minutes, totaling 240 Pixels over a 24-hour period. Because the cap is set at 240, you must log in at least once per day to spend your Pixels and prevent them from capping, which halts natural regeneration.

### The City Stamina System

City Stamina is the lifeblood of the City Tycoon system. This resource is used for non-combat activities such as street racing, delivery missions, and fishing. Unlike Pixels, City Stamina does not regenerate on a minute-by-minute basis; instead, it resets fully at the start of each week.

As you increase your Tycoon Level by earning Fons and completing urban milestones, your City Stamina cap increases. For example, while you start with a cap of 100 at Level 1, reaching Tycoon Level 10 increases this cap to 350, allowing for significantly more weekly activity.

### Mint: The Stamina-Based DPS

Mint is an A-Class Anima Esper whose combat efficacy is tied directly to your stamina management. Her signature move, the Minty Whirlwind (often referred to as the "Mint Tornado"), is a charged Basic Attack that transforms her into a spinning vortex of Anima damage. This state continuously drains your combat stamina bar. To maximize Mint's DPS, you must enter the field with a full stamina bar and maintain high recovery rates through external buffs or efficient dodging.

| Resource Type | Recovery Rate | Cap (Base) | Primary Uses |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Character Pixels** | 1 per 6 Minutes | 240 | Anomaly Zones, Arcs, Cartridges |
| **City Stamina** | Weekly Reset | 100 (Scales) | Racing, Deliveries, Tycoon Levels |

*   **Initial Drain:** 10 Stamina upon activation.
*   **Sustained Drain:** [Information unavailable] Stamina per second.
*   **Movement Penalty:** 5% additional drain while moving during the tornado state.

## Best Weapons

![Best Weapons](/img/nte-stamina-management-guide/inline-2.webp)


Selecting the right Arc (weapon) for Mint requires a focus on her extended field time. Because she needs to stay active to deplete her stamina bar during her whirlwind, weapons that provide stacking buffs are superior to those offering short-term bursts.

### Best-in-Slot: Fluff of Fleetness

The **Fluff of Fleetness** is an S-Class Liquid Arc and stands as Mint’s undisputed best-in-slot option. Its unique passive, **Motor Candy**, provides a 5% ATK increase every 1 second the wearer remains on the field. This effect stacks up to 5 times for a total 25% ATK bonus. Since Mint’s rotation involves staying on-field for the entirety of her stamina bar, she easily maintains max stacks for the duration of her damage window.

### Top F2P Arcs

For players who haven't secured an S-Class Liquid weapon, **Clear Skies** is the premier A-Class alternative. This Arc increases Anima DMG for Redirect Skills and Ultimates by 20%. While it doesn't buff her Basic Attack tornado directly, it significantly boosts her "Overdrive: Super Claw" and "Thunderous Whirlwind Slash" damage, making her burst phases much more potent.

Another viable option is **Shiny Days**, which focuses on **Break Intensity**. This is particularly useful in high-difficulty Anomaly Zones where shielding prevents Mint from effectively grouping enemies with her tornado.

### Weapon Stat Priority

When leveling and tuning your Arcs, prioritize the following attributes to maximize Mint's output:
1.  **CRIT Rate:** Essential for triggering "Caramel Crisp" ripostes.
2.  **Anima DMG%:** Direct multiplier for her entire kit.
3.  **ATK%:** Scales her high base attack values.
4.  **Break Intensity:** Useful for secondary DPS roles.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/nte-stamina-management-guide/inline-3.webp)


In Neverness to Everness, "Disks" are referred to as **Cartridges** and are slotted into the **Console** interface. For an Anima DPS like Mint, the focus should be on maximizing her critical hit consistency and elemental scaling.

### Recommended Cartridge Sets

The **Fireflies and the Forest** set is the gold standard for Mint.
*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Increases Anima DMG by 10%.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** Increases the wearer's CRIT DMG by 8% whenever a nearby enemy takes Anima DMG from any team member. This stacks up to 7 times (56% total CRIT DMG) and lasts 10 seconds.

This set is specifically designed for multi-hit Anima characters. Since Mint's whirlwind hits multiple times per second, she reaches maximum stacks almost instantly after starting her rotation.

### Module Stat Priority

The Console uses a grid-based system where you must fit different "Modules" to activate set bonuses. For Mint, you should prioritize **Type III modules** to fill the Console grid efficiently.
*   **Main Stat (Console Headpiece):** CRIT Rate is mandatory here. Mint has high natural CRIT DMG through her set bonuses and passives, so balancing that with a high CRIT Rate is the only way to ensure consistent DPS.
*   **Sub-stat Tier List:** CRIT DMG > ATK% > Universal DMG% > Flat ATK.

### Console Optimization

To activate the most powerful Console bonuses, ensure you are utilizing the **Module Configuration** that favors Type III placements. By aligning three Type III modules in a vertical or horizontal row (depending on your specific Console model), you can trigger a hidden bonus that provides up to a 30% CRIT Rate increase, though this requires precise placement on the 20-square grid.

**Fireflies and the Forest Set Bonuses**
*   **2-Piece:** Anima DMG +10%.
*   **4-Piece:** +8% CRIT DMG per Anima hit (Max 7 stacks, 56% total). Stacks last 10s and persist off-field.

## Team Comp

![Team Comp](/img/nte-stamina-management-guide/inline-4.webp)


Mint’s elemental affinity allows her to participate in some of the strongest elemental reactions in the current meta. Her primary goal is to trigger **Blossom** or **Hexed** reactions to shred enemy defenses and boost her own ATK.

### Premium Blossom Team

The "Blossom Meta" involves pairing Mint (Anima) with **Zero** (Cosmos). When Anima and Cosmos interact, they trigger the Blossom reaction, which creates a "Vita Pistil" that deals additional area damage. 
*   **Main DPS:** Mint (Anima)
*   **Sub-DPS:** Zero (Cosmos) - Triggers instant Esper Cycles.
*   **Support:** Jiuyuan (Anima) - Provides Anima resistance shred and grouping.
*   **Healer/Sustain:** Edgar (Cosmos) - Provides healing and maintains the Blossom resonance.

### F2P-Friendly Composition

If you are playing with the starter roster, Mint still shines when paired with **[Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide)** (Incantation).
*   **Main DPS:** Mint
*   **Support:** Sakiri - Triggers **Hexed** (Anima + Incantation), which grants a flat 20% DMG boost to the active character.
*   **Sustain:** Adler - Provides shields and damage-over-time (DoT) to keep enemies staggered.
*   **Flex:** Haniel - Adds off-field damage and helps with energy regeneration.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/nte-stamina-management-guide/inline-5.webp)


To maximize Mint's damage, you must follow a strict rotation that ensures all team buffs are active before she begins her stamina-intensive whirlwind.

### The Perfect Opener

1.  **Setup:** Start with **Jiuyuan**. Use her Redirect Skill to pull enemies into a tight cluster. This is vital because Mint's tornado has a limited radius.
2.  **Buffing:** Swap to **Sakiri** (or your chosen support) and cast her Ultimate. This provides the team-wide ATK buff necessary for the next phase.
3.  **Priming:** Switch to **Zero**. Use his Skill to instantly fill the Esper Meter. This ensures that the moment Mint takes the field, the Blossom reaction is ready to trigger.

### Stamina Management in Combat

Once the field is set, swap to **Mint**. 
*   Immediately use her Skill, **Overdrive: Super Claw**, to dash through the enemy pack. 
*   If an enemy attacks, perform a **Perfect Dodge**. This triggers her "Caramel Crisp" riposte, which instantly resets the cooldown of "Super Claw."
*   After the second Skill use, **Hold Basic Attack** to enter the Minty Whirlwind. 
*   Stay in this state until your stamina bar is nearly empty. Do not let it reach zero, or Mint will enter a "Fatigued" animation that leaves her vulnerable for 2 seconds.

### Maximizing Ultimate Damage

Cast Mint’s Ultimate, **Thunderous Whirlwind Slash**, just as her stamina is about to run out. The Ultimate animation provides "Invincibility Frames" (I-frames), allowing your stamina to begin its natural recovery while Mint is performing her final high-damage burst.

**Rotation Flowchart:**
Jiuyuan Skill (Group) -> Sakiri Ultimate (Buff) -> Zero Skill (Cycle) -> Mint Skill -> Perfect Dodge -> Minty Whirlwind (Drain Stamina) -> Mint Ultimate (Finisher).

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/nte-stamina-management-guide/inline-6.webp)


### General Stamina Questions

**How do I recover Character Pixels faster?**
Character Pixels recover at a natural rate of 1 every 6 minutes. To speed this up, you can use **Pixel Refills** (De-noise Solutions) which grant 60 Pixels, or spend **Annulith** (premium currency) for up to 6 refills per day.

**Does sprinting use the same stamina as Mint's tornado?**
Yes. Sprinting, dodging, and Mint's "Minty Whirlwind" all draw from the same combat stamina bar. If you sprint too much before swapping to Mint, you will significantly reduce her DPS window.

**What is the maximum Tycoon level for City Stamina?**
In Version 1.0, the Tycoon level is currently capped at **Level 50**. Reaching this cap requires significant investment in properties like the Fengan Villa and maintaining a high Comfort Value through furniture.

### Mint Build Specifics

**Can I use [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte)'s weapon on Mint?**
You can, but it is not optimal. Nanally's signature weapon, **Ready-Ready**, is specifically tuned for follow-up attack damage. While Mint can use the base stats, she cannot trigger the follow-up buffs as effectively as Nanally can.

**Where do I farm the Fireflies and the Forest set?**
This set is farmed in the **Anomaly Containment** zones located in the northern districts of Hethereau. Each run costs 40 Character Pixels. It is recommended to only farm the S-Rank (Legendary) versions once you reach Account Level 45.

### Resource Farming Tips

To optimize your account, always prioritize spending your Character Pixels on **Arc Ascension** materials before farming Cartridges. Cartridges have high RNG (randomness) regarding sub-stats, whereas Arc levels provide guaranteed, flat power increases. For City Stamina, focus on **Racing** early on, as it provides one-time Fons rewards that help you reach Tycoon Level 4 (unlocking the Cafe) much faster.
