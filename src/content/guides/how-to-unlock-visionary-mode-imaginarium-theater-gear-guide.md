Modern titles like *Neverness to Everness* rely on vibrant visual effects and detailed urban environments created in Unreal Engine 5. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does Neverness to Everness have a Visionary Mode?
No, "Visionary Mode" and "Imaginarium Theater" are features specific to *Genshin Impact*. In *Neverness to Everness*, players should focus on reaching high-level milestones in urban anomaly investigations to experience the most challenging content.

### What is the most important piece of gear for long NTE sessions?
The chair is the most critical investment for long-session health. A chair with [4-way adjustable lumbar support](https://secretlab.co/products/titan-evo-2022-series) ensures your spine remains in a neutral position, preventing the chronic pain often associated with high-intensity grinding.

### Why is 1440p recommended over 4K for gacha games?
1440p offers the best balance between visual clarity and performance. It allows you to see fine details in character models and environments without requiring the extremely expensive hardware needed to run 4K at high refresh rates.

### Is a mechanical keyboard actually better for gaming?
Yes, keyboards like the Keychron Q1 Pro use gasket mounts and high-quality switches that provide a more consistent and cushioned feel. This reduces the impact on your fingers during repetitive combat inputs, which is vital for long-term comfort.
