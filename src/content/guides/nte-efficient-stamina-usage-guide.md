Mastering your progression in the sprawling urban landscape of Hethereau requires a disciplined approach to resource management. With the launch of Neverness to Everness (NTE) on April 29, 2026, players have quickly discovered that while the city offers endless distractions, your growth as an Appraiser is strictly gated by several energy systems. To reach the endgame efficiently, you must prioritize where every point of stamina goes, particularly when building a top-tier DPS like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte).

# NTE Efficient Stamina Usage Guide: Best Nanally Build & Farming Strategy

## Overview

![Overview](/img/nte-efficient-stamina-usage-guide/inline-1.webp)


Efficient progression in Neverness to Everness hinges on understanding that not all "stamina" is created equal. The game utilizes a tripartite energy system designed to balance combat progression, city management, and tactical movement. Misallocating these resources early on can lead to a significant bottleneck once you reach Hunter Level 40, where upgrade costs for Arcs and Cartridges spike dramatically.

### The Dual Stamina System Explained

At the heart of your daily routine are **Character Pixels** and **City Stamina**. While they may look similar in the UI, their recovery rates and primary uses are distinct.

*   **Character Pixels:** This is your primary combat stamina. It is consumed whenever you enter an Anomaly Zone to farm character EXP, Arc materials, or Cartridges. 
*   **City Stamina:** This resource is dedicated to the "City Tycoon" side of NTE. It fuels your hobbies—such as racing, fishing, and managing businesses—which in turn generate Fons (the primary gold currency) and increase your Tycoon Level.
*   **Combat Stamina Gauge:** Unlike the previous two, this is a real-time gauge used during combat for sprinting, dodging, and specific heavy attacks. Managing this is a matter of skill rather than time-gated recovery.

| **Daily/Weekly Cap** | 240 (Standard) | Resets Weekly (Mondays 4:00 AM UTC+8) |
| **Primary Use** | Anomaly Zones, Bosses, Gear Farming | Tycoon Hobbies, Shop Management, Racing |

### Why Nanally is the Best Stamina Investment

During the launch meta, Nanally has emerged as the most efficient character for your Character Pixel investment. As an S-Rank Anima DPS, her kit is uniquely suited for both mob clearing and boss suppression. Investing in her early allows you to clear higher-difficulty Anomaly Zones faster, which improves the "loot-per-pixel" ratio of your farming. Her ability to walk on walls and ignore gravity during her "Ichi-daime's Authority" state also makes overworld exploration and Anomaly containment significantly less time-consuming.

### Stamina Spending Priorities by Hunter Level

To maximize efficiency, follow this spending roadmap to avoid wasting Pixels on low-tier gear:

1.  **Hunter Level 1-20:** Focus exclusively on Character EXP and Arc Ascension materials. Do not farm Cartridges; the game provides enough via story rewards to get through early content.
2.  **Hunter Level 20-30:** Begin farming for specific A-Rank Arcs if you haven't pulled a signature weapon. Continue prioritizing character levels to keep up with World Level increases.
3.  **Hunter Level 30-40:** This is the "pre-farm" phase. Accumulate enough materials to instantly push Nanally to Level 60 the moment you hit the next cap. Avoid spending Pixels on "The Rabbit Hole" (Cartridge farming) until you hit HL 40.
4.  **Hunter Level 40+:** Shift your focus to Gold Cartridges. At HL 45, the drop rate for Gold Cartridges in the Rabbit Hole doubles, making this the most efficient window to burn saved Pixel recovery items (De-noise Solutions).

## Best Weapons

![Best Weapons](/img/nte-efficient-stamina-usage-guide/inline-2.webp)


Nanally utilizes Plasma-type Arcs, which typically scale with offensive stats like CRIT Rate and Basic Attack damage. Choosing the right weapon is the difference between a functional DPS and a meta-defining powerhouse.

### Top Tier: Ready-Ready

**Ready-Ready** is Nanally's signature S-Rank Plasma Arc and her undisputed Best-in-Slot (BiS). It is designed to reward her specific combat loop of alternating between Skills and Ultimates.

*   **Base ATK:** 570 (at Max Level)
*   **Substat:** CRIT Rate (24% at Max Level)
*   **Passive Effect (Commander Tiger Talisman):** Increases ATK by 15%. When casting a Skill or Ultimate, Basic Attack and Critical Riposte damage increase by 15% for 15 seconds (stacks up to 2 times). Additionally, using a Skill grants a "Left Tiger Talisman" and an Ultimate grants a "Right Tiger Talisman." Having both active increases damage against Bosses by an additional 10%.

### Best F2P Options: Song of the Whale & Oraora!

If you are saving your Annuliths and skipping the weapon banner, NTE offers excellent alternatives that can be acquired through gameplay.

**Song of the Whale** is the premier F2P S-Rank alternative. It is obtainable by completing specific overworld Anomaly challenges in the Hethereau district. While it lacks the specific "Tiger Talisman" boss-shredding capability of Ready-Ready, its raw stat line is formidable for a free weapon.

**Oraora! (A-Rank)** is a high-value budget option frequently found in the standard gacha pool. It excels on Nanally because its passive stacks damage with multi-hit attacks. Since Nanally’s basic attack string is a rapid 5-hit combo, she can reach max stacks in seconds.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/nte-efficient-stamina-usage-guide/inline-3.webp)


In NTE, your "Disks" are known as **Cartridges**, which are slotted into your character's Console. For an Anima DPS like Nanally, the choice is clear.

### Recommended Set: Fireflies and the Forest

The **Fireflies and the Forest** Cartridge set is the gold standard for Nanally. It directly amplifies her elemental damage and rewards her high-frequency hit rate.

*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Anima DMG +10%.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** Whenever a nearby enemy takes Anima DMG from any team member, the wearer gains 8% CRIT DMG for 10 seconds. This effect stacks up to 7 times, providing a massive 56% CRIT DMG boost at full uptime.

### Module Selection and Stat Priority

The Console system requires you to fit geometric **Modules** into a grid to activate your Cartridge's set bonuses. To maximize Nanally, you should aim for a 75% CRIT Rate threshold. This ensures her passives, such as "Fair Duel," trigger consistently.

**Sub-stat Priority for Nanally:**
1.  **CRIT Rate** (Until 75% threshold)
2.  **Anima DMG Bonus** (Main stat on the Cartridge)
3.  **CRIT DMG**
4.  **ATK%**
5.  **Cycle Intensity** (Boosts the power of the Blossom reaction)

**Efficiency Tip:** Do not be afraid to upgrade purple (A-Rank) Cartridges early in your journey. NTE features a generous 100% XP refund system when you feed an upgraded lower-tier Cartridge into a higher-tier Gold Cartridge later. This allows you to stay powerful during the leveling process without "wasting" Character Pixels.

## Team Comp

![Team Comp](/img/nte-efficient-stamina-usage-guide/inline-4.webp)


Nanally shines brightest when paired with characters that can trigger the **Blossom Reaction**. This reaction occurs when Anima (Nanally's element) interacts with Cosmos, spawning "Vita Buds" that deal explosive AoE damage.

### The Meta Blossom Team

The most potent team in the 1.0 meta consists of:
*   **Nanally (Main DPS):** The primary source of Anima damage and follow-up triggers.
*   **Zero (Cosmos Support):** Essential for her ability to immediately fill the team's Esper Meter with her Skill, allowing for instant reaction setups.
*   **Jiuyuan (Anima Sub-DPS):** Provides crowd control and increases the count of Vita Buds spawned during Blossom reactions.
*   **[Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) (Flex Support):** Provides team-wide ATK buffs and can trigger "Hexed" reactions to shred enemy resistances.

| Character | Role | Element | Synergy Note |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Nanally** | Main DPS | Anima | Triggers Blossom follow-ups; high on-field uptime. |
| **Zero** | Battery | Cosmos | Instant Esper Meter refill; triggers Blossom. |
| **Jiuyuan** | Crowd Control | Anima | Doubles Vita Bud count via "Seize the Moment." |
| **Sakiri** | Buffer | Incantation | Massive team ATK buffs; groups enemies for AoE. |

### F2P-Friendly Team Variations

If you are missing the premium supports, you can achieve high efficiency by swapping Sakiri or Jiuyuan for **Haniel** or **Adler**. Haniel provides necessary shielding and sustain, which is vital for clearing high-level Anomaly Zones where enemies can one-shot glass-cannon builds. Adler serves as a "connective tissue" support, helping with energy generation if your team lacks the "Instant-Cycle" capability of Zero.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/nte-efficient-stamina-usage-guide/inline-5.webp)


To squeeze every drop of value out of your Character Pixels, you must execute Nanally's rotation correctly. A sloppy rotation leads to longer clear times and higher resource consumption in the long run.

### Optimal Combat Sequence

The goal is to maximize the uptime of the **Underboss** and the **Ichi-daime's Authority** state.

1.  **The "Insta-Cycle" Opener:** Start with **Zero**. Use her Skill to immediately fill the team's Esper Meter.
2.  **Buff Layering:** Swap to **Sakiri** and **Jiuyuan**. Cast their Ultimates to layer ATK buffs and group enemies.
3.  **The Authority Loop:** Swap to **Nanally**. Activate her Skill (**Colucci Howling Technique**) to enter the Ichi-daime's Authority state. This grants her a 30% CRIT DMG bonus and enables her follow-up attacks.
4.  **The Finisher:** Immediately follow up with her Ultimate (**Colucci Ultimate Technique**) to summon the **Underboss**.
5.  **Sustained Pressure:** For the next 6-12 seconds, perform Nanally's 5-hit basic attack string. The Underboss will mirror every attack, and your high CRIT Rate will trigger "Fair Duel" follow-ups every 2 seconds.

### Managing the Underboss Uptime

The Underboss only remains active for 6 seconds, while the Ichi-daime's Authority lasts for 12. It is crucial not to waste the Underboss window by dodging excessively. Use Nanally's "Fair Duel" follow-ups to maintain damage while repositioning. Because her basic attack string is not interrupted by dodging, you can stay aggressive even during complex boss mechanics.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/nte-efficient-stamina-usage-guide/inline-6.webp)


**How do I recover Character Pixels?**
Character Pixels recover naturally at a rate of 1 every 6 minutes. You can also use **De-noise Solutions** to restore 60 Pixels instantly or spend **Annuliths** for up to 6 refills per day.

**What is the daily cap for Character Pixels?**
The standard cap is 240 Pixels. However, you can raise this cap by placing specific Anomaly Furniture, such as the **Dream Weaver's Knot**, in your properties.

**Should I spend City Stamina on fishing or racing?**
Early on, prioritize activities that raise your **Tycoon Level** the fastest. This unlocks the **Hunter Exchange**, where you can trade Fons for essential character progression items, effectively turning your "hobby time" into combat power.

**When does City Stamina reset?**
City Stamina is a weekly resource that resets every Monday at 4:00 AM UTC+8.

**Is it worth spending Annuliths to refresh stamina?**
For most players, no. Annuliths are better spent on character banners like "The Ichi-daime." Refreshing is only recommended for competitive players aiming for world-first clears of endgame content like "Beyond the Rails: Prime Circle."
