## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/best-gear-genshin-6-6-meta-shifts/inline-4.webp)


Not every player needs to overhaul their setup for a version update. You should consider skipping the upgrade if:

*   If your current chair has a functioning, adjustable lumbar system and you don't experience numbness after 4 hours, the jump to a $1,600 Embody may not be necessary.
*   Avoid upgrading your mouse if you primarily play with a controller; the Naga's 12-button plate is useless for gamepad-centric players who prefer the tactile feel of a joystick.
*   If you already own a 144Hz IPS monitor from the last two years, the visual shifts in version 6.6 won't be drastic enough to justify a new display unless you are moving to OLED for perfect blacks.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is a 1440p monitor worth it for Genshin 6.6?
Yes, moving from 1080p to 1440p offers a 77% increase in screen real estate, which is vital for managing the cluttered UI during complex combat. The [Nano IPS technology](https://www.lg.com/us/monitors/lg-27gp850-b-gaming-monitor) also ensures that the new environments are rendered with high color accuracy.

### Do I really need 12 buttons on a mouse for Neverness to Everness?
While not strictly required, having extra buttons allows you to map Esper Abilities and menu shortcuts to your thumb. This reduces the strain on your keyboard hand, which is often busy with WASD movement and dodging Anomalies.

### Why is the Herman Miller Embody so much more expensive than a Secretlab chair?
The Embody uses a [unique support matrix](https://www.hermanmiller.com/products/seating/gaming-chairs/embody-gaming-chair/) rather than traditional foam. This allows for better airflow and pressure distribution, and the [12-year warranty](https://www.hermanmiller.com/products/seating/gaming-chairs/embody-gaming-chair/) covers the cost of the chair for over a decade of use.

### Can I use the SteelSeries Arctis Nova 7 with my phone and PC at the same time?
Yes, the Arctis Nova 7 supports simultaneous 2.4GHz and Bluetooth audio. This means you can hear your game audio on your PC while listening to a podcast or taking a call on your phone.
