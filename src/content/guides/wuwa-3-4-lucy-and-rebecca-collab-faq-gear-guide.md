While new gear is exciting, it isn't always necessary for every player. If you already own a high-end ergonomic office chair, such as a Herman Miller Aeron or a Steelcase Gesture, do not switch to a "gaming" chair. You already have superior support designed for 12-hour workdays that translates perfectly to gaming.

If you primarily [play Neverness to Everness](/faq) with a controller, the Razer Naga’s 12-button side plate will be useless to you. In that case, you should stick with a lightweight FPS mouse for general navigation and save your budget for other upgrades. Additionally, don't upgrade to an Ultrawide if your GPU is older than an RTX 3060 or RX 6600 XT. The increased pixel count of a [3440 x 1440 resolution](https://www.lg.com/us/monitors/lg-34gp83a-b-gaming-monitor) will tank your frame rates in the 3.4 update, leading to a choppy experience that negates the benefits of the 160Hz refresh rate.
