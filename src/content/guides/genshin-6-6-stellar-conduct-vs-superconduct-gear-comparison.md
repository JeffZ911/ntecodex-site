## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/genshin-6-6-stellar-conduct-vs-superconduct-gear-comparison/inline-4.webp)

Not every player needs to overhaul their setup for the 6.6 update. If you currently own a 1440p IPS monitor with at least 144Hz, the jump to 160Hz or 175Hz is visually negligible for gacha games. The difference in motion clarity is most noticeable in competitive shooters, whereas open-world exploration benefits more from color depth and resolution.

Don't buy an OLED monitor if you leave your game on a static "AFK" screen for 10+ hours a day; IPS is safer for that specific use case to avoid permanent image retention. Furthermore, if your current chair has a functional, adjustable lumbar pillow and the foam hasn't bottomed out, a $500 upgrade won't magically fix poor posture habits. Ergonomics is as much about how you sit as what you sit on.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is an ultrawide monitor better for Genshin Impact 6.6?
Yes, an ultrawide monitor provides a 21:9 field of view that allows you to see more of the battlefield, which is helpful for tracking the wide-reaching effects of Stellar-Conduct. It also provides extra space to keep a browser window open for walkthroughs without tabbing out of the game.

### Will an OLED monitor burn in if I play gacha games daily?
While OLED burn-in is a risk due to static UI elements like health bars and mini-maps, modern monitors like the Alienware AW2725DF include a 3-year burn-in warranty. Using built-in maintenance features like "Pixel Refresh" significantly reduces the risk for most users.

### Do I really need an MMO mouse for Neverness to Everness?
An MMO mouse like the Razer Naga V2 Pro is not strictly required, but it greatly improves efficiency. Mapping your character skills and menu shortcuts to the side buttons reduces hand movement and allows for faster reaction times during high-level anomaly encounters.

### Why is the Secretlab chair better than a standard office chair?
The Secretlab TITAN Evo 2022 is specifically designed for gamers who spend 4+ hours in a single position. Its L-ADAPT lumbar system and high-density cold-cure foam provide more targeted support than most entry-level office chairs, which often use lower-quality foam that flattens over time.
