## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/nte-sakiri-vs-jiuyuan-comparison-best-gaming-chairs/inline-4.webp)


Not every investigator needs a $500 chair to enjoy Neverness to Everness. You should consider skipping an upgrade if:

*   **Minor Discomfort:** If your current chair has a functional gas lift and you only experience minor discomfort, a high-quality memory foam lumbar pillow can often extend its life for another year at a fraction of the cost.
*   **Alternative Gaming Stations:** Don't buy a high-end ergonomic chair if you primarily game from a couch or bed. The ergonomics of these chairs are strictly designed for a desk-height setup with a 90-degree leg angle.
*   **Sizing Constraints:** Skip the upgrade if you are under 5'2" or over 6'6" without checking for specific "Small" or "XL" sizing. A standard ergonomic chair will often miss your spinal curve entirely if you fall outside the average height range, potentially causing more harm than good.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is a gaming chair or an office chair better for Neverness to Everness?
For most players, a high-end gaming chair like the Secretlab TITAN Evo provides better neck and head support for reclined play. However, if you prioritize breathability and have a smaller budget, an ergonomic office chair like the Branch provides better value and adjustability for the price.

### How does lumbar support help during long NTE sessions?
Lumbar support maintains the natural inward curve of your lower spine. Without it, your muscles must work harder to hold your posture, leading to the fatigue and "slumping" often seen during long grinds for character materials in Hethereau.

### Do I need 4D armrests for gacha gaming?
4D armrests are highly beneficial if you switch between devices. They allow you to bring the armrests inward to support your elbows while holding a phone or controller, which significantly reduces the strain on your neck and upper back.

### How long should a gaming chair last?
A high-quality chair from a brand like Secretlab or Steelcase should last between 5 and 12 years. Budget chairs typically begin to show significant wear, such as foam compression or hydraulic failure, within 1 to 3 years of heavy daily use.
