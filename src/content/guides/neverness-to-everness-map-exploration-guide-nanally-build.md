## Overview

![Overview](/img/neverness-to-everness-map-exploration-guide-nanally-build/inline-1.webp)


Nanally is the premier S-Rank Anima DPS and the undisputed queen of map exploration in Neverness to Everness. Since the game’s launch on April 29, 2026, she has remained the benchmark for high-frequency follow-up damage and vertical mobility. Unlike traditional ground-based fighters, Nanally utilizes urban architecture as her personal playground, making her indispensable for players looking to uncover every secret in the neon-lit districts of Hethereau.

Her unique "Anti-Gravity" mechanic is unlocked via her Redirect Skill, **Colucci Howling Technique**. This state allows her to run on walls and ceilings, bypassing traditional parkour obstacles and reaching high-altitude anomalies that other characters simply cannot access without complex gadgetry. As an Anima-element character, she excels in "Blossom" reaction teams, where she provides both high on-field burst and consistent off-field follow-up damage through her summoned Underboss.

### Exploration Perks

Nanally’s utility extends far beyond her damage numbers. Her exploration-specific passives make her the most efficient choice for open-world traversal:

*   **Wall-Running Duration:** Entering the **Ichi-daime's Authority** state grants 12 seconds of gravity-defying movement. This duration is extended to 20 seconds when out of combat, allowing for massive vertical climbs.
*   **Stamina Consumption Reduction:** While running on vertical surfaces, Nanally consumes 30% less stamina compared to standard sprinting, enabling her to scale the tallest skyscrapers in the Harbor District.
*   **Plunge Mastery:** Her "Grand Entrance!" plunge attack scales damage based on fall height, capping at a 200% damage bonus. This makes initiating combat from a skyscraper roof a viable tactical choice.

### Why Nanally is Essential for Exploration

In the dense urban environment of Hethereau, many Anomaly Chests and data fragments are hidden on the undersides of bridges or the vertical faces of buildings. Nanally’s ability to "stick" to these surfaces removes the need for precision gliding or external traversal tools. By activating her skill, she can sprint horizontally across a glass facade to reach a ledge that would otherwise require a long detour.

### Core Combat Mechanics: Follow-up Attacks

Nanally’s combat identity revolves around her "Follow-up Attacks." Her passive, **Fair Duel**, triggers an additional instance of Anima DMG whenever a teammate deals Esper Cycle DMG while she is in her enhanced state. This means she effectively doubles the team's hit count, which is vital for breaking enemy shields and triggering the Blossom reaction.

The **Ready-Ready** Arc is Nanally’s signature S-Rank weapon and her undisputed best-in-slot choice. It provides a massive 24% CRIT Rate substat at Level 80, significantly easing the burden on your Module substat rolls. Its unique passive, **Commander Tiger Talisman**, triggers when you cast both her Redirect Skill and Ultimate within 15 seconds of each other. For players who did not pull on "The Ichi-daime" weapon banner, the A-Rank Arc **Oraora!** is an exceptional alternative. Given that Nanally’s standard combo involves a rapid 5-hit string, she reaches maximum stacks in under three seconds.

The **Song of the Whale** is a hidden S-Rank Arc found as a reward for the "Deep Sea Echoes" anomaly quest in the Nautily Tunnel. While its raw damage is roughly 84% of Ready-Ready, it provides unparalleled overworld utility. It restores 30% HP upon defeating a "Broken" enemy, allowing Nanally to sustain herself during long exploration sessions without a dedicated healer.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

In Neverness to Everness, your build is defined by your Cartridge sets and Module configurations. Nanally requires a balance of Anima DMG and CRIT stats to ensure her follow-up attacks hit with sufficient impact.

### Recommended Cartridge: Fireflies and the Forest

The **Fireflies and the Forest** set is the definitive BiS for Nanally. The 2-piece bonus provides a flat 10% Anima DMG boost, but the 4-piece bonus is where the set truly shines. Every time a nearby enemy takes Anima DMG (which includes Nanally’s own follow-ups and the Underboss’s strikes), the wearer gains 8% CRIT DMG for 10 seconds. This effect stacks up to 7 times, providing a maximum of 56% bonus CRIT DMG.

### Optimal Module Configurations

Modules provide the granular stats needed to hit your targets. You should aim for a "Type II" Module focus to gain extra CRIT Rate bonuses.

*   **Main Stat Priority:** Anima DMG (Slot 1) > CRIT DMG (Slot 2) > ATK% (Slot 3).
*   **Substat Tier List:**
    1.  **CRIT Rate:** Priority until you reach 85%.
    2.  **CRIT DMG:** The primary scaling stat once CRIT Rate is stable.
    3.  **ATK%:** Essential for increasing the base of her follow-up multipliers.
    4.  **Universal DMG Bonus:** A rare but powerful multiplier for all skill types.

### Stat Thresholds for Endgame

To clear the highest floors of the Bureau of Anomaly Control, Nanally should aim for at least 2,200 ATK and a CRIT ratio of 1:2 (e.g., 80% CRIT Rate and 160% CRIT DMG). Because the Fireflies set provides 56% CRIT DMG, your sheet stats only need to show around 100% CRIT DMG to reach the effective ceiling during combat.

## Team Comp

![Team Comp](/img/neverness-to-everness-map-exploration-guide-nanally-build/inline-4.webp)


Nanally’s effectiveness is multiplied by the "Esper Cycle" system. She belongs to the **Top Trio** (Lakshana, Cosmos, Anima), meaning she reacts most powerfully with Cosmos and Lakshana elements.

### The Blossom Hypercarry Team

The current "Blossom Meta" consists of **Nanally, Jiuyuan, Zero, and [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide)**. This team focuses on the **Blossom reaction** (Anima + Cosmos), which spawns "Vita Buds" on the field. These buds fire "Vita Pistils"—exploding projectiles that deal AoE Anima DMG.

*   **Jiuyuan (Anima):** Provides the "Anima Resonance" buff and uses her skill to gather enemies into a tight cluster. Her passives also increase the maximum number of Vita Buds on the field from 3 to 6.
*   **Zero (Cosmos):** Acts as the reaction bridge. Zero’s unique "Insta-Cycle" skill instantly fills the Esper Meter, allowing for rapid swaps that trigger the Blossom effect multiple times in a single rotation.
*   **Sakiri (Incantation):** While Incantation is on the "Bottom Trio," Sakiri is used as a flex support. She triggers the **Hexed** reaction (Anima + Incantation), which deals bonus damage based on 20% of the Anima damage taken by the enemy over the last 12 seconds.

### F2P Exploration-Focused Team

If you are just starting out, a highly effective F2P team is **Nanally, Mint, Zero, and Adler**.

*   **Mint (Anima):** Provides basic healing and helps generate Anima particles for Nanally’s Ultimate.
*   **Adler (Incantation):** Provides a team-wide shield, which is essential for surviving high-altitude exploration where falling is a risk.

### Elemental Synergy: Why Cosmos and Anima Rule

The **Blossom** reaction (Anima + Cosmos) is currently the highest DPS duo-reaction in the game because it operates independently of the active character's attacks. Once the Vita Buds are spawned, they continue to fire even if Nanally is dodging or repositioning on a wall, providing 100% damage uptime.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/neverness-to-everness-map-exploration-guide-nanally-build/inline-5.webp)


Mastering Nanally’s rotation is about managing the uptime of **Ichi-daime's Authority** and the **Underboss** summon.

### The Opening Sequence

1.  **Sakiri:** Use Skill to gather enemies and Ultimate to apply the team-wide ATK buff.
2.  **Zero:** Switch to Zero and use their Skill. This instantly fills the Esper Cycle Meter.
3.  **Nanally:** Swap to Nanally. This triggers the **Blossom** reaction, spawning Vita Buds. Immediately cast **Colucci Howling Technique** (Skill) to enter her enhanced state.

### Maximizing Underboss Uptime

Once in the Ichi-daime's Authority state, cast **Colucci Ultimate Technique** to summon the Underboss. For the next 6 seconds, the Underboss will mirror every Basic Attack Nanally performs. 

*   **The Combo:** Perform the full 5-hit **Colucci Secret Skill** string. Do not interrupt the combo with dodges unless necessary; Nanally’s passive allows her to continue her combo even after a dash.
*   **Critical Riposte:** If an enemy attacks, use a **Critical Dodge** followed immediately by a Basic Attack to trigger **Can't Touch This!** (a powerful roundhouse kick). This reduces the enemy's Break meter significantly and triggers an additional follow-up strike from the Underboss.

### Exploration Rotation: Infinite Wall-Running

To maintain vertical movement indefinitely:
1.  Enter **Ichi-daime's Authority** via her Skill.
2.  Sprint up the wall.
3.  When the timer at the bottom of the screen reaches 2 seconds, use her **Ultimate**. This refreshes the gravity-defying state for an additional 6 seconds.
4.  If the surface has a ledge, use the **Grand Entrance!** plunge to land safely and reset your stamina.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/neverness-to-everness-map-exploration-guide-nanally-build/inline-6.webp)


**How do I unlock Nanally's wall-running?**
Nanally’s wall-running is not a passive that is always active. It is tied to her Redirect Skill, **Colucci Howling Technique**. You must use the skill to enter the **Ichi-daime's Authority** state before you can run on vertical surfaces.

**Is Nanally better than Mint for exploration?**
Yes. While Mint has a high movement speed and a low-cooldown dash, she cannot run vertically. Nanally’s anti-gravity allows her to reach rooftop anomalies that Mint would have to find a stairs or elevator to reach.

**What is the hard pity for Nanally's banner?**
Neverness to Everness utilizes a 90-pull hard pity system for S-Rank characters. Notably, the game does not feature a 50/50 system; if you hit an S-Rank on the limited banner, it is guaranteed to be the featured character.

**Where can I find the Song of the Whale Arc?**
The **Song of the Whale** is a reward for completing the "Deep Sea Echoes" anomaly quest. To find it, drive into the **Nautily Tunnel** in the Harbor District until an anomaly portal appears. Defeating the "Sea Prisoner" boss inside will grant the weapon.
