## Overview

Nanally is an S-Rank Anima character who utilizes the Plasma arc type. In the current Patch 1.0 environment, she functions as a high-frequency Main DPS whose primary value comes from her ability to trigger coordinated attacks through her summoned companion and elemental reactions. 

The core of Nanally’s gameplay revolves around the "Ichi-daime's Authority" state. Triggered by her Redirect Skill, this state provides a significant 30% boost to her Crit DMG and unlocks her signature "Anti-Gravity Walking" ability. This allows Nanally to transition seamlessly from ground combat to fighting on walls and ceilings, which is essential for dodging ground-based AoE attacks from high-level bosses. Furthermore, her synergy with the Blossom reaction makes her the centerpiece of the current "Premium Blossom" team composition, where she supercharges Vita buds to deal massive area damage.

### Nanally's Skill Kit Breakdown

*   **Basic Attack: Colucci Secret Skill** – A flexible 5-hit combo that deals Anima DMG. A key feature of this skill is that dodging does not interrupt the combo chain, allowing Nanally to reposition without losing her high-multiplier finishers.
*   **Charged Attack: Heavy Hitter!** – By holding the Basic Attack button, Nanally executes a 3-hit heavy combo. This can be derived from various points in her standard combo or after a Critical Riposte.
*   **Redirect Skill: Colucci Howling Technique** – Nanally deals five instances of AoE Anima DMG and enters the **Ichi-daime's Authority** state for 12 seconds. This state is the engine of her kit, providing the Crit DMG buff and wall-walking mobility.
*   **Ultimate: Colucci Ultimate Technique** – Nanally deals seven instances of massive AoE Anima DMG and summons the **Underboss**. For 6 seconds, the Underboss coordinates with every one of Nanally's attacks, adding follow-up Anima hits and creating a small pull effect to keep enemies clustered.
*   **Passive: More Than Passionate** – This is her signature reaction enhancement. It buffs the Blossom reaction, causing Vita buds to fire ten Vita Pistils instead of the standard five, effectively doubling the reaction's damage output.

### Skill Upgrade Priority

To maximize Nanally's damage ceiling, you should prioritize her Ultimate and Basic Attack, as these constitute the bulk of her active field time damage.

1.  **Colucci Ultimate Technique (Ultimate):** The primary source of follow-up damage and raw Anima multipliers.

3.  **Colucci Howling Technique (Skill):** Important for the initial burst and maintaining the Authority state.

## Best Weapons

Nanally requires an Arc that complements her high Crit scaling and frequent use of both skills and ultimates. Because she is a Plasma-type character, she can only equip Plasma Arcs. Her signature weapon, Ready-Ready, was featured on the limited weapon banner "The Ichi-daime" and remains her best-in-slot (BiS) option by a significant margin.

### Top-Tier S-Rank Arcs

**Ready-Ready (Signature)**
This S-Rank Arc is tailor-made for Nanally's rotation. It provides a base ATK of 570 and a 24% Crit Rate substat at level 60. Its unique effect, **Commander Tiger Talisman**, increases ATK by 15% permanently. Additionally, casting a Redirect Skill or Ultimate increases Basic Attack and Critical Riposte DMG by 15% for 15 seconds (stacking up to 2 times). Obtaining both the "Left" and "Right" Talismans within 15 seconds unlocks a 10% damage bonus against Bosses, perfectly aligning with her standard burst window.

**Fluff of Fortitude**
The best alternative S-Rank Arc, providing a 27.5% ATK substat. Its passive, **Jawbreaker's Candy**, provides a flat 22% damage increase to all attacks. This bonus jumps to 28% when the enemy's HP falls below 50%, making it an excellent choice for phase-based boss fights.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

In Neverness to Everness, the "gear" system utilizes Cartridges and Modules placed within a Console grid. For Nanally, the goal is to maximize her Anima DMG and Crit DMG to take advantage of her high hit rate.

### Recommended Cartridge Sets

The **Fireflies and the Forest** Cartridge set is currently the only viable choice for an endgame Nanally build.
*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Anima DMG +10%.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** Increases the wearer's Crit DMG by 8% whenever a nearby enemy takes Anima DMG from any team member. This stacks up to 7 times (56% total Crit DMG) and lasts 10 seconds. Crucially, this effect remains active even when Nanally is off-field, allowing her follow-up attacks to maintain the buff.

### Module Stat Priority & Grid Optimization

When slotting Modules into your Console, prioritize the following stats:
1.  **Main Stat:** Anima DMG Bonus (Cartridge)
2.  **Substats:** Crit Rate > Crit DMG > ATK% > Cycle Intensity

Nanally has a unique Console passive that increases her **Crit Rate by 6% for every Type II Module equipped**. To optimize her build, you should aim for a grid layout that maximizes the number of Type II Modules while still fulfilling the shape requirements for the Fireflies and the Forest 4-piece set.

### Optimal Console Grid Layout
*   **Cartridge:** Fireflies and the Forest (Liquid Type)
*   **Required Modules:** 1x Type II (2-cell), 2x Type III (3-cell), 1x Type IV (4-cell).
*   **Optimization Tip:** Fill all remaining empty slots with 2-cell Type II Modules to maximize the Crit Rate passive. With a fully optimized grid, you can gain upwards of 24-30% "free" Crit Rate from the Console system alone.

## Rotation

Mastering Nanally requires a strict adherence to her buff windows. Because her Ichi-daime's Authority state ends if she is swapped out, she should always be the final character brought onto the field in a rotation.

### Opening Burst Sequence

1.  **Support Prep:** Start with **Sakiri** or **Haniel**. Cast their skills and ultimates to apply ATK buffs and resistance shreds.
2.  **Reaction Setup:** Switch to **Zero**. Use her skill to immediately charge the Esper Meter and trigger the Blossom state.
3.  **The Entry:** Swap to **Nanally**. If you have her A1 unlocked, this swap will immediately trigger a follow-up hit.
4.  **Authority State:** Immediately use **Colucci Howling Technique** (Skill). This puts Nanally into the Ichi-daime's Authority state, granting her the 30% Crit DMG bonus.
5.  **Summon Underboss:** Cast **Colucci Ultimate Technique** (Ultimate). This summons the Underboss and triggers the **Commander Tiger Talisman** boss-damage buff from her signature weapon.

### Sustained DPS Combo

Once the Underboss is active (6-second window), use the following combo to maximize follow-up triggers:
*   **3x Basic Attack -> 3x Charged Attack (Heavy Hitter!)**
*   Repeat this sequence until the Underboss disappears.
*   If the enemy attempts a ground-based AoE, use the wall-walking mechanic to run up a nearby building or vertical surface, then use **Grand Entrance!** (Plunge Attack) to dive back into the fight for up to 200% bonus damage based on height.

### Is Nanally Worth Pulling?
Yes. As the first limited S-Rank DPS, Nanally is designed to be the "benchmark" for damage in the early versions of Neverness to Everness. Her ability to deal damage off-field through the Underboss and her unique mobility make her a safe investment for both F2P and whale players. ### Awakening Priority (A1 - A6)
If you are aiming for duplicates, here is the priority for Nanally’s Awakenings:
*   **A1: Gang Formation (High Priority):** Grants 2.5 Ultimate Energy for every follow-up attack. This is vital for maintaining 100% uptime on her Underboss summon.
*   **A2: Second Member:** Increases Underboss damage by 30%.
*   **A5: Followers Everywhere (High Priority):** Stacks ATK by 2% for every follow-up hit, up to 20%. This provides a massive boost during long boss encounters.
*   **A6: Because We're Family (Best Value):** Extends the duration of Ichi-daime's Authority to 15 seconds in combat (up to 20 seconds for exploration). This significantly widens her burst window.

### Common Troubleshooting & Tips
*   **Crit Overcapping:** Nanally gains a lot of Crit Rate from her Console (Type II Modules) and her signature weapon. If you find yourself over 100% Crit Rate, swap your Cartridge main stat from Crit Rate to Crit DMG.
*   **Energy Issues:** If you cannot cast her Ultimate every rotation, ensure you are running **Zero** in your team. Zero is the best battery for Anima characters.
*   **Wall-Walking in Combat:** Don't forget that wall-walking isn't just for exploration. You can use it to avoid "Floor is Lava" mechanics in boss fights while still being able to use her aimed gun attack to maintain damage stacks.
