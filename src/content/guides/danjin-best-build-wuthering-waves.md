## Overview

![Overview](/img/danjin-best-build-wuthering-waves/inline-1.webp)


Danjin is a 4-star Havoc Resonator who utilizes a Sword to deliver rapid, devastating strikes at the cost of her own survivability. As a member of the Midnight Rangers, her lore reflects her intense personality—a trait that translates directly into her "Berserker" playstyle. Every time Danjin uses her Resonance Skill, she consumes a portion of her own health to fuel her attacks, making her one of the most fragile yet hardest-hitting characters in the game.

Her kit is built around the "Ruby Blossom" Forte Circuit. By consuming HP through her Resonance Skill, she builds up stacks that can be unleashed via a Heavy Attack to deal massive Havoc damage and provide a self-heal. This creates a gameplay loop where the player must constantly balance on the edge of death to maximize damage output.

### Danjin's Role: Main DPS vs. Sub-DPS

Danjin is unique because she excels in two distinct roles depending on your team composition. As a Main DPS, she remains on the field for extended periods, cycling through her Resonance Skill combos to maintain high uptime on her Havoc damage buffs. Her fast attack animations and ability to parry effectively make her a formidable duelist against bosses.

Alternatively, Danjin is widely considered the premier Sub-DPS for Havoc-based teams. This is due to her Outro Skill, Duality, which grants the next character a 23% Havoc DMG Deepen buff for 14 seconds. When paired with a powerful Havoc unit like Havoc Rover, this buff allows for some of the highest burst damage windows currently achievable in Wuthering Waves.

### Skill Priority: Forte Circuit and Resonance Skill

To maximize Danjin's effectiveness, you must prioritize her skill upgrades correctly. Her damage is heavily weighted toward her Forte Circuit and her Resonance Skill.

1.  **Forte Circuit (Ruby Blossom):** This is the heart of her kit. Upgrading this increases the damage of her "Chaoscleave" (Heavy Attack) and "Scatterbloom" (Follow-up attack), which are her primary sources of burst.
2.  **Resonance Skill (Carmine Gleam):** Since Danjin uses her skill repeatedly with no cooldown to build Forte, increasing its base damage significantly impacts her overall DPS.
3.  **Basic Attack:** While she uses basic attacks in her combos, they are often secondary to the skill-interwoven strikes.
4.  **Resonance Liberation (Crimson Bloom):** Her ultimate deals a large burst of Havoc damage, but it is not her primary focus.
5.  **Intro Skill:** Provides a small entry hit; lowest priority for investment.

## Best Weapons

![Best Weapons](/img/danjin-best-build-wuthering-waves/inline-2.webp)


Danjin benefits most from weapons that provide Critical stats or flat Attack increases to complement her high base scaling. Because she drains her own HP, weapons that offer defensive utility or healing are niche but can be useful for players struggling with her survival.

### Top Tier: 5-Star Sword Options

The **Emerald of Genesis** is the gold standard for Danjin. As a standard 5-star weapon, it is accessible through the Permanent Weapon Convene and provides a massive Crit Rate substat. Its passive increases Energy Regen by 15% and grants a stacking ATK% buff whenever a Resonance Skill is used. Since Danjin spams her Resonance Skill, she maintains maximum stacks of this buff effortlessly.

**Blazing Brilliance** is another high-tier 5-star option. While it is the signature weapon for Changli, its high Crit DMG substat and ATK% bonuses make it a powerful stat stick for Danjin. It rewards the user for dealing damage with Resonance Skills, which aligns perfectly with Danjin's Carmine Gleam spam.

### Best 4-Star and F2P Alternatives

For players without 5-star weapons, **Lumingloss** is the best 4-star choice, available through the Insider Podcast (Battle Pass). It provides an ATK% substat and a passive that increases Basic and Heavy Attack DMG by 20% for 15 seconds after using a Resonance Skill.

**Commando of Conviction** is the best gacha 4-star for pure damage. It increases ATK by 15% for 15 seconds when the Intro Skill is triggered. This is particularly effective in quick-swap teams where Danjin enters the field frequently.

For a more defensive approach, the craftable **Sword#18** offers a unique passive that heals the user when their HP drops below 40%. While this lowers her damage potential compared to offensive swords, it provides a safety net for players still learning her complex dodge timings.

## Best Echoes (Artifacts)

![Best Echoes (Artifacts)](/img/danjin-best-build-wuthering-waves/inline-3.webp)


Choosing the right Echo set is vital for Danjin, as her damage is split between sustained Havoc pressure and buffing her teammates.

### Best Sonata Effects for Danjin

The **Sun-sinking Eclipse (5-piece set)** is the mandatory choice for a Main DPS Danjin.
*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Havoc DMG +10%.
*   **5-Piece Bonus:** Havoc DMG +10% after using a Basic or Heavy Attack. This effect stacks up to 4 times, providing a total of 40% additional Havoc DMG. Since Danjin uses Heavy Attacks to consume her Ruby [Blossom stacks](https://ntecodex.com/guides/camellya-crimson-blossom-stacks-explained), she can maintain this buff with ease.

For a Sub-DPS or "Buffer" build, the **Moonlit Clouds (5-piece set)** is an alternative. This set focuses on Energy Regeneration and provides a 22.5% ATK buff to the next character after using an Outro Skill. This is only recommended if you are using Danjin exclusively to setup a hyper-carry like Havoc Rover or a future Havoc DPS.

### Main Echo Selection: Crownless vs. Dreamless

The choice of your primary Echo (the 4-cost slot) depends on your team's rotation speed.

1.  **The Crownless:** This is the best Main Echo for Danjin's personal damage. Its active skill deals Havoc damage and increases Danjin's Havoc DMG and Resonance Skill DMG by 12% for 15 seconds. Because Danjin spends a significant amount of time on the field, she benefits from the full duration of these buffs.
2.  **Dreamless:** This Echo is better for quick-swap Havoc teams. Its animation is much faster than The Crownless, and it deals increased damage if used shortly after the Havoc Rover’s Resonance Liberation. If you are playing the Danjin/Havoc Rover duo, Dreamless is often the smoother choice.

### Optimal Echo Stats and Substats

To reach her maximum potential, Danjin requires a traditional DPS stat spread. You should aim for a 4-3-3-1-1 Echo cost distribution.

*   **4-Cost Echo:** Crit Rate or Crit DMG (aim for a 1:2 ratio).
*   **3-Cost Echoes:** Havoc DMG% / Havoc DMG%.
*   **1-Cost Echoes:** ATK% / ATK%.

**Recommended Substat Priority:**
1. Crit Rate / Crit DMG
2. ATK%
3. Resonance Skill DMG Bonus
4. Heavy Attack DMG Bonus
5. Flat ATK
6. Energy Regeneration (Low priority)

## Team Comp

![Team Comp](/img/danjin-best-build-wuthering-waves/inline-4.webp)


Danjin thrives in environments where she can either be the star of the show or the ultimate enabler for another Havoc unit.

### The Havoc Duo: Danjin and Havoc Rover

This is currently one of the most powerful synergies in Wuthering Waves. Danjin’s Outro Skill provides a 23% Havoc DMG Deepen buff, which is a multiplicative damage increase. When she swaps into the Havoc Rover, the Rover's already massive burst damage is amplified to extreme levels. In this team, Danjin acts as a "Burst Support" who builds her Forte quickly, executes her combo, and then passes the torch.

### Sustain Options: Healers and Shielders

Because Danjin spends most of her time at low HP, a healer is almost mandatory for most players. **Verina** is the best choice due to her ability to provide off-field healing, a "cheat death" passive at S0, and global ATK buffs. If Verina is unavailable, **Baizhi** is a solid F2P alternative who offers consistent healing and an ATK buff through her own Outro Skill.

**Jianxin** is an interesting alternative for more skilled players. She provides a shield that can protect Danjin's fragile HP bar while also offering crowd control and Resonance Liberation DMG buffs.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/danjin-best-build-wuthering-waves/inline-5.webp)


Mastering Danjin requires understanding her "Sanguine Pulse" combos and how they interact with her Forte bar.

### Mastering the Incinerating Will Mark

When Danjin hits an enemy with her Resonance Skill, she applies the "Incinerating Will" mark. This mark is essential because hitting marked enemies with her Resonance Skill generates more Forte (Ruby Blossom) and deals additional damage. 

### Forte Circuit Management (Ruby Blossom)

The goal of Danjin's field time is to fill the Ruby Blossom bar as quickly as possible.
1.  **Skill Chain 1:** Resonance Skill -> Basic Attack -> Resonance Skill.
2.  **Skill Chain 2 (Sanguine Pulse):** 3 Basic Attacks followed by 3 Resonance Skills. This is her most efficient way to generate Forte.
3.  **The Finisher:** Once the Forte bar is full (red), hold the Basic Attack button to perform **Chaoscleave**. Follow this up immediately with another click to trigger **Scatterbloom**. This combo consumes all stacks, deals massive damage, and heals Danjin based on the stacks consumed.

### Step-by-Step Rotation (Sub-DPS/Buffer)

1.  **Intro Skill:** Enter the field to trigger initial Havoc damage.
2.  **Resonance Skill x2:** Apply the Incinerating Will mark.
3.  **Sanguine Pulse Combo:** Basic Attack 3 times -> Resonance Skill 3 times.
4.  **Repeat:** Continue until the Forte bar is full.
5.  **Main Echo:** Use The Crownless or Dreamless.
6.  **Forte Finisher:** Hold Heavy Attack (Chaoscleave) -> Scatterbloom.
7.  **Resonance Liberation:** Use Crimson Bloom if available.
8.  **Outro Skill:** Switch to your Main DPS (e.g., Havoc Rover) to pass the 23% Havoc Deepen buff.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/danjin-best-build-wuthering-waves/inline-6.webp)


### Is Danjin better than 5-star characters?
In terms of raw single-target damage, Danjin can outperform several 5-star characters, especially if she has high Resonance Chain (Sequence) levels. However, her lack of survivability and small attack range mean she requires much higher player skill to achieve those results compared to a character like Jiyan or Calcharo.

### How do I keep Danjin alive?
Survival on Danjin is a matter of "don't get hit." You must become proficient at parrying and dodging. Since her Resonance Skill has no cooldown, you can use it to cancel animations into a dodge at any time. Bringing a healer like Verina or a shielder like Jianxin is highly recommended for high-difficulty content like the Tower of Adversity.

### What are Danjin's best Resonance Chains?
Danjin gains significant power from her Sequences.
*   **S1:** Increases damage reduction while using Resonance Skill, helping with survival.
*   **S2:** Increases Resonance Skill DMG against enemies with the Incinerating Will mark.
*   **S5:** Increases Havoc DMG when HP is below 60%.
*   **S6:** Increases the ATK of all party members by 20% after Danjin uses her Heavy Attack "Scatterbloom," making her an even better Sub-DPS.

### Does Danjin need Energy Regen?
Danjin's Energy Regen needs are minimal. Most of her damage comes from her Resonance Skill and Forte Circuit, which do not require Energy. While her Resonance Liberation is a nice burst of damage, you should prioritize offensive stats like Crit and ATK over Energy Regen in your Echo substats.
