## Overview

![Overview](/img/zzz-caesar-king-best-build/inline-1.webp)


Caesar King is an S-Rank Physical Defense Agent who redefined the role of protective characters upon her release. Unlike traditional tanks that merely absorb damage, Caesar functions as a hybrid force. She provides the "Radiant Aegis" shield, which not only protects the active character from damage but also grants a significant 1,000 ATK buff and high Anti-Interrupt resistance. This makes her a mainstay in high-difficulty content like Hollow Zero and Shiyu Defense, where maintaining offensive momentum is as important as survival.

Her playstyle is built around reactive combat. By utilizing her Defensive Assist and EX Special Attack, she can parry incoming strikes and immediately transition into a counter-offensive. Because her shield strength and offensive buffs scale with her Impact stat—a stat usually reserved for Stunners—she effectively doubles as a Daze-builder, allowing teams to skip a traditional Stunner in favor of more damage or anomaly application.

### Role and Playstyle

Caesar is best described as a "Frontline Enabler." You do not bring her just to soak up hits; you bring her to ensure your Main DPS never has to stop attacking. Her Radiant Aegis shield is the core of her kit, providing a flat 1,000 ATK increase to any character currently protected by it. Because this buff is flat rather than percentage-based, it is exceptionally powerful for characters with high damage multipliers but lower base stats.

In combat, Caesar stays off-field until an enemy attacks. By switching her in during a yellow flash (Defensive Assist), she triggers her parry, refreshes her shield, and applies debuffs to the enemy. This "swap-parry-swap" loop allows your DPS to remain on the field with near-permanent uptime on her buffs.

### Core Passive: Radiant Aegis

The Core Skill, **Barrier of Resilience**, is what defines Caesar’s utility. When Caesar uses her EX Special Attack, Chain Attack, or Ultimate, she grants the entire team the Radiant Aegis shield.

*   **Shield Value:** The shield's durability is calculated as 1,400% of Caesar's initial Impact + 1,400 flat value (at max level).
*   **ATK Buff:** While the shield is active, the protected character gains 1,000 ATK.
*   **Duration:** The shield lasts for 60 seconds or until its durability is exhausted.
*   **Anti-Interrupt:** While shielded, characters gain Level 2 Anti-Interrupt, meaning they cannot be staggered by most standard enemy attacks.

### Additional Ability: Battle Spirit

When Caesar is paired with another member of the Sons of Calydon faction or any Agent capable of a Defensive Assist, her **Battle Spirit** ability activates. This ability triggers when Caesar performs a Perfect Block, Defensive Assist, or hits an enemy with certain shield-based attacks. It applies a debuff to all enemies within a 7-meter radius, increasing the DMG they take by 25% for 30 seconds. This is a massive multiplicative boost to team damage that makes her competitive with top-tier Supports like Rina or Soukaku.

## Best Weapons

![Best Weapons](/img/zzz-caesar-king-best-build/inline-2.webp)


Choosing the right W-Engine for Caesar is straightforward: you want Impact. Because her shield strength and her very identity as a hybrid Stunner rely on her Impact stat, weapons that provide this stat are prioritized above all else.

### Top Tier: Tusks of Fury

The **Tusks of Fury** is Caesar King's signature S-Rank W-Engine and is indisputably her best-in-slot option. It provides a base Impact percentage that significantly boosts her shield durability. 

*   **Effect:** Increases the wearer's Impact by 18%. When the wearer triggers a Perfect Dodge or Interrupt (Defensive Assist), the entire team's DMG is increased by 15% and Daze dealt is increased by 20% for 20 seconds.
*   **Why it works:** This engine perfectly aligns with Caesar's "reactive" playstyle. Since you will naturally be triggering Defensive Assists to refresh her shield, you will have near 100% uptime on the team-wide damage and daze buffs.

### Best F2P Option: Original Transmorpher

For players who did not pull for her signature weapon, the **Original Transmorpher** (A-Rank) is the premier free-to-play choice. It is currently the only other Defense-class W-Engine that provides an Impact bonus.

*   **Effect:** Increases Max HP by 8%. When the wearer is attacked, their Impact is increased by 10% for 12 seconds.
*   **Why it works:** While it requires Caesar to take a hit or perform a block to trigger the Impact bonus, it is the only A-Rank engine that directly scales her shield strength via the Impact stat. It can be purchased from the Gadget Store using Signal Shop currency.

### Alternative W-Engines

If you lack the options above, you can look toward energy-focused or damage-reduction engines, though they are significantly less effective for her specific scaling.

*   **Peacekeeper - Specialized (A-Rank):** This engine provides Energy Regeneration and increases Anomaly Buildup when the wearer is off-field. This is a niche pick for "Anomaly" focused teams where Caesar is used primarily to trigger Physical Anomaly (Assault) and then swap out.
*   **Spring Embrace (B-Rank/Battle Pass):** This provides a small amount of Energy Generation for the team and reduces damage taken. It is a "comfy" pick but does nothing to improve her shield value.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/zzz-caesar-king-best-build/inline-5.webp)


Mastering Caesar requires understanding that she is a "reactive" character. You do not want her on the field for long periods; you want her to enter, parry, buff, and leave.

### Opening Sequence
1.  Start with your Main DPS or Support to bait an enemy attack.
2.  When the gold flash appears, trigger a **Defensive Assist** to bring Caesar in.
3.  Immediately follow up with her **EX Special Attack: Parry Counter**. This refreshes the Radiant Aegis shield and applies the 1,000 ATK buff.
4.  Use the follow-up **EX Special Attack: Overpowered Shield Bash** to deal massive Daze.
5.  Swap back to your Main DPS.

### Maintaining Radiant Aegis
The shield lasts 60 seconds, but it can be broken by heavy hits. To maintain 100% uptime:
*   Use Caesar’s **Chain Attack** whenever a Stun is triggered. This refreshes the shield for free without consuming Caesar's energy.
*   If the shield breaks early, wait for a Defensive Assist window or manually swap to Caesar and use her EX Special Attack.

### Perfect Block and Counter Timing
Caesar can manually block by holding the Special Attack button. If you release it just as an attack hits, she performs a **Perfect Block**. This is useful for bosses with slow, predictable hits where a Defensive Assist might not be available or if you have already used your Assist Points.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/zzz-caesar-king-best-build/inline-6.webp)


### Does Caesar King's shield stack with Seth's?
No. In Zenless Zone Zero, shields do not stack. If you apply Caesar's shield while Seth's shield is active, the most recent shield will replace the previous one. Generally, you want to use Caesar's shield as the primary buff due to the 1,000 ATK bonus.

### Why is Impact important for a Defense character?
Caesar is a hybrid. Her "Barrier of Resilience" Core Skill explicitly states that the shield's HP is based on her Impact stat. Furthermore, higher Impact allows her to stun enemies faster, fulfilling the role of a Stunner while occupying a Defense slot.

### Is her signature W-Engine mandatory?
It is not mandatory for clearing content, but it provides a significant power spike. The 18% Impact from "The Tusks of Fury" is much higher than the 10% provided by the "Original Transmorpher," resulting in a noticeably stronger shield and faster Daze application.

### What are her best Mindscape stopping points?
*   **M1 (Greatest Hits):** Reduces the target's Attribute Resistance and Defense, making it a very strong supportive upgrade.
*   **M2 (In the Name of the King):** Increases the flat ATK buff from 1,000 to 1,500 and increases the buff duration. This is her most powerful offensive milestone.
