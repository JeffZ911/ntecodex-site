## What a high-DPS player actually needs

![What a high-DPS player actually needs](/img/changli-vs-encore-dps-gear-comparison/inline-2.webp)


When performing a Changli vs Encore DPS comparison, the technical ceiling of your hardware often dictates your actual output. High-tier characters require more than just the right build; they require an environment that supports high-APM execution.

### Rapid Trigger Functionality
Standard mechanical switches have a fixed reset point; a key must travel back up past a certain threshold before it can be pressed again. Rapid Trigger, found on the Razer Huntsman V3 Pro, allows for faster repeated inputs because the key resets the moment you start lifting your finger. This is critical for dodging and animation cancels, especially for Changli's Flaming Sacrifice combo, which requires precise timing to maximize the damage of her Ultimate ability.

### Consistent Ergonomic Support
High-APM play increases the risk of repetitive strain. A chair with internal lumbar support, like the Secretlab TITAN Evo, and a mouse with a natural grip are non-negotiable for long-term health. When grinding for Arcs in *Neverness to Everness* or Echoes in other titles, poor posture leads to fatigue, which directly reduces your reaction time and DPS accuracy after the first two hours of play.

### High Refresh Rate Clarity
When comparing fast-moving characters, a 240Hz+ or OLED display reduces the "smearing" of visual effects. This makes it significantly easier to time parries and counters. In a Changli vs Encore DPS comparison, visual clarity is vital because Changli’s animations are fast and flashy, potentially obscuring enemy telegraphs on lower-quality monitors. The [Alienware AW2725DF](https://www.rtings.com/monitor/reviews/dell/alienware-aw2725df) provides the near-instant response time needed to see through the "Fusion" visual effects and react to enemy AI.

## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/changli-vs-encore-dps-gear-comparison/inline-4.webp)


While top-tier gear improves the Changli vs Encore DPS comparison results, it is not always a necessary investment.

*   **Monitor Caps:** If you are currently using a 144Hz IPS monitor, the jump to 360Hz is noticeable but won't fundamentally change your DPS output if your frame rate is capped by your CPU. High-APM games like *Neverness to Everness* are often CPU-intensive; ensure your processor can push 300+ FPS before buying a 360Hz display.
*   **Switch Preference:** Avoid upgrading your keyboard if you do not use "Rapid Trigger" features. Standard mechanical switches are sufficient for casual play. If you do not actively practice animation canceling with Changli, the analog switches of the Huntsman V3 Pro may feel overly sensitive.
*   **Sensor Saturation:** If your current mouse has a flawless sensor (like the Logitech Hero 25K or Razer Focus+), a new mouse will only benefit you if you specifically need more buttons for macros. The raw tracking accuracy of a 20,000 DPI sensor is already beyond human perception in most action RPGs.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is Changli or Encore better for overall DPS?
Changli generally offers higher peak burst damage and synergy with Fusion sub-DPS units due to her True Sight mechanics. Encore provides more consistent sustained damage and is easier to play for those who prefer ranged-to-melee transitions using her Resonance Skill.

### Do I need a 360Hz monitor for Neverness to Everness?
While not strictly required, a 360Hz monitor like the Alienware AW2725DF significantly reduces motion blur during high-speed vehicle Arcs and supernatural combat. It allows you to track fast-moving enemies in the city of Hethereau with much higher precision than a standard 60Hz or 144Hz screen.

### Does Rapid Trigger actually increase my DPS?
Rapid Trigger increases your "effective" DPS by allowing you to input commands faster and more reliably. By reducing the time it takes for a key to reset, you can execute animation cancels—such as those required for Changli’s optimal rotations—more consistently, leading to higher damage over a fixed combat window.

### Can I use macros for Encore’s Dissonance gauge?
While many mice like the Razer Naga V2 Pro support macros, you should check the terms of service for your specific game. Generally, mapping a single key to a mouse button is safe, but automating a full rotation for Encore may be flagged by anti-cheat systems.
