## Overview

![Overview](/img/laura-build-guide-nte-20260518/inline-1.webp)


Nanally functions as a sustained S-Rank Anima DPS who relies on "Blossom" reactions to multiply her damage. Unlike burst-heavy characters who rely on a single hit, Nanally’s strength lies in her "Ichi-daime's Authority" state. In this mode, her basic attacks and skills generate Vita Pistils—autonomous combat units that mirror her attacks and provide consistent pressure even while she is repositioning.

Her mobility is a standout feature in the open-world urban environment of Hesperia. She can transition from ground combat to wall-running seamlessly, allowing her to avoid ground-based boss telegraphs without losing DPS uptime. This makes her particularly effective against large-scale bosses like the "Anomaly: Weaver" where verticality is a tactical advantage.

### Core Mechanics: Blossom and Vita Pistils
The core of Nanally’s kit is the **Vita Pistil** system. When Nanally enters her enhanced state, every third hit of her basic attack or any skill usage generates a Pistil. These Pistils stay on the field for 8 seconds, firing Anima needles at the target Nanally is currently attacking. 

The **Blossom** reaction occurs when Anima damage hits a target already affected by a "Cosmos Mark" (typically applied by characters like Zero or Jiuyuan). This reaction causes the Vita Pistils to "bloom," dealing a localized AoE explosion and resetting the duration of the Pistil. Managing these durations is the difference between a mediocre Laura player and a master.

If you are just starting out, **The Umbrella** (B-Rank) is a functional placeholder, but you should aim to replace it as soon as you pull any A-Rank umbrella.

Refinement priority for A-Rank weapons should always favor **First Step to Success**, as its Refinement 5 (R5) performance can rival an R1 S-Rank weapon in specific sustained combat scenarios.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/laura-build-guide-nte-20260518/inline-6.webp)


### How do I get Laura (Nanally)?
Nanally is a limited S-Rank character. She is currently obtainable through her featured banner, **"The Ichi-daime,"** which runs alongside her signature weapon banner, "Scarborough Fair." As a limited character, she will not be added to the standard "Urban Recruitment" pool until a future update.

### Is she better than Baicang?
Nanally and Baicang serve different roles. Nanally is the queen of sustained AoE and follow-up damage, making her better for clearing waves and multi-part bosses. Baicang, however, focuses on single-target execution and "True Damage" bursts. In the current 1.0 abyss-style content, Nanally is generally considered more versatile.

### Can I play her without her signature weapon?
Yes. While Scarborough Fair is a significant power spike, she is perfectly viable with **Ready-Ready** or **Fluff of Fleetness**. F2P players can find success using **First Step to Success**, though you will need to focus more on Energy Regeneration substats on your Disks to compensate for the lack of the signature's passive.

### What is the "Ichi-daime" state?
This is Nanally's enhanced combat state, triggered by her Skill or Ultimate. While in this state, her umbrella glows with a teal Anima aura. It grants her a [Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-18]% Crit DMG bonus and is the only time she can generate Vita Pistils. Maintaining 100% uptime on this state is the primary goal of her rotation.

### Troubleshooting Damage Output
If your Nanally feels like she isn't doing enough damage, check the following
*   **Pistil Count:** Are you maintaining at least 3 Pistils? If not, check your rotation.
*   **Reaction Trigger:** Are you applying Cosmos with Zero or Jiuyuan? Without the Blossom reaction, your damage will drop by nearly 40%.
*   **Disk Levels:** Ensure your Slot 4 (Anima DMG) Disk is maxed out, as this is her most important scaling factor.
