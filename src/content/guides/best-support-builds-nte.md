In the supernatural urban landscape of Hesperia, your team's success depends on how efficiently you can cycle through abilities and trigger elemental reactions. While DPS units like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) or Jiuyuan grab the headlines, the backbone of any high-tier squad is the support roster. ## Overview

![Overview](/img/best-support-builds-nte/inline-1.webp)


Support characters in NTE are generally categorized into three distinct roles: Buffers, Sustain, and Enablers. Buffers like [Sakiri](/characters/sakiri-nte-free-5-star-guide) and Haniel focus on increasing the offensive stats of the active character, while Sustain units like Adler or Mint ensure the team survives aggressive anomaly encounters. Enablers are specialized units that facilitate rapid "Cycle Intensity," allowing the team to trigger elemental reactions more frequently.

The primary goal of any support build is to maximize Charge Efficiency. This stat dictates how quickly a character can regain energy for their Ultimate. Because most top-tier support buffs are tied to Ultimate abilities, failing to reach specific Charge Efficiency thresholds can lead to "dead time" in your rotation where your DPS is hitting for significantly less damage.

Additionally, NTE utilizes a "Redirect Skill" system, often referred to as QTEs. Supports are designed to be swapped in, use their skills instantly, and then be swapped out. This off-field presence allows your main DPS to maintain field time while benefiting from the persistent buffs provided by the support's equipment and abilities.

### The Role of Buffers vs. Sustain

Choosing between a Buffer and a Sustain unit depends on the difficulty of the content. In Time Trials or the "Hesperia Deep Dive" challenges, Buffers are prioritized to meet strict DPS checks. Characters like Sakiri provide raw percentage increases to ATK and Elemental Damage. Conversely, in high-hazard open-world exploration or weekly boss fights, Sustain units like Adler become mandatory. Adler’s ability to generate shields based on his DEF stat prevents your glass-cannon DPS units from being one-shot during complex boss patterns.

### Understanding Charge Efficiency

Charge Efficiency is the most critical stat for any support. It determines the rate at which "Esper Particles" generated by skills translate into Ultimate Energy. For most S-Rank supports, a Charge Efficiency of 160% to 180% is considered the "sweet spot" to ensure their Ultimate is ready every time their cooldown expires. Without this, your rotation will stall, leading to a significant drop in total team damage per second.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/best-support-builds-nte/inline-3.webp)


The "Disk" system in NTE, specifically the Cartridges and Modules, allows for deep customization of support capabilities. The Console Tetris system requires players to fit different Module shapes into a grid, with set bonuses triggering when enough pieces of the same set are equipped.

### The Speedy Hedgehog Meta

The **Speedy Hedgehog** Cartridge set is the absolute meta-defining choice for supports. Its 4-piece set bonus provides a non-stackable 15% ATK buff to the entire team. Because this effect does not stack, you should only ever have one character per team running this set. Usually, this is assigned to your primary Buffer (e.g., Sakiri or Haniel).

### Niche Support Sets: Tiny Big Adventure & Shadow Creed

While Speedy Hedgehog is universal, some supports benefit from more specialized sets:

*   **Tiny Big Adventure:** This set is essential for Fadia or any support that scales with Max HP. It increases the wearer's HP and converts a portion of that HP into healing effectiveness.
*   **Shadow Creed:** A strong A-Tier secondary option. It grants a 25% ATK boost for 20 seconds specifically after using a Skill. This is useful for teams that rely on long DPS windows where the Speedy Hedgehog buff might expire too early.

### Priority Sub-stats for Support Modules

When slotting Modules into your Console, prioritize the following sub-stats to ensure your supports function at peak efficiency:

1.  **Charge Efficiency:** The highest priority. Essential for Ultimate uptime.
2.  **Cycle Intensity:** Increases the speed at which the Esper Meter fills, allowing for more frequent elemental reactions.
3.  **Flat HP / DEF:** Important for Sustain units like Adler or Fadia whose shields and heals scale with these stats.
4.  **Elemental Resistance:** Helps supports survive stray hits during the brief window they are on the field.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/best-support-builds-nte/inline-5.webp)


Mastering the rotation is what separates casual players from those clearing the hardest "Anomaly Zones." NTE's combat is built around the 20-second buff window provided by most Arcs and Cartridges.

### Standard Support-to-DPS Rotation

1.  **Start with the Buffer:** Switch to Sakiri or Haniel. Immediately use their Skill and then their Ultimate. This triggers the **Speedy Hedgehog** set bonus and the **Good Boy's Grand Adventure** weapon buff.
2.  **Trigger the Enabler:** Swap to Zero or Hotori. Use their Skill to apply elemental status (Anima or Cosmos) to the enemy. This primes the target for an Esper Cycle.
3.  **The DPS Window:** Swap to your Main DPS (e.g., Nanally). Use their Ultimate and spam their primary damage combo. This should occur within the first 10-12 seconds of the rotation to benefit from all active buffs.
4.  **Energy Reset:** Once the buffs expire, use your supports' Parries or basic attacks to catch any generated particles. This refills the Esper Meter for the next cycle.

### Maximizing Esper Cycles

An **Esper Cycle** occurs when you fill the circular meter at the center of the UI. During this state, elemental reactions are significantly amplified. To maximize this, always ensure your supports are of different elements (e.g., one Anima, one Psyche). This variety allows you to trigger multiple reaction types, such as "Blossom" or "Scorch," which deal massive percentage-based damage to bosses.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/best-support-builds-nte/inline-6.webp)


**Does the Speedy Hedgehog 4-piece effect stack?**
No. If two characters on the same team wear the Speedy Hedgehog set, you will still only receive a 15% ATK buff. It is more efficient to put a different set, like Shadow Creed, on your second support.

**Who is the best free support?**
Haniel is widely considered the best A-Class support. Her buffs are universal, meaning they work for any DPS regardless of their element, making her an invaluable asset for new players.

**What is the difference between Charge Efficiency and Cycle Intensity?**
Charge Efficiency affects how quickly an individual character's Ultimate energy bar fills. Cycle Intensity affects the speed at which the entire team's shared Esper Meter (used for elemental reactions) fills.

**Is Mint a good support?**
Mint is a very capable healer for the early and mid-game. However, in the current endgame meta, she is often replaced by Sakiri for more damage or Adler for better defensive utility. Mint lacks the offensive scaling found in S-Rank supports.

**What is the "Redirect Skill" mechanic?**
This is NTE's version of a swap-in attack. When a support's icon flashes, clicking it will cause them to enter the field, perform a specific move, and apply their buffs instantly, often without interrupting your main DPS's positioning.
