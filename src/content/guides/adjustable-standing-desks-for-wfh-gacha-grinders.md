## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/adjustable-standing-desks-for-wfh-gacha-grinders/inline-4.webp)


Not every gamer needs a full standing desk replacement. There are specific scenarios where an upgrade might be counterproductive.

If your current desk is stable and you only want to stand occasionally, a standing desk converter (like the VariDesk Pro Plus) is a more cost-effective solution than a full desk replacement. This allows you to keep your existing furniture while gaining the ergonomic benefits of standing.

Don't upgrade if your flooring is high-pile carpet. Even the most stable standing desks, including the FlexiSpot E7, will exhibit significant wobble on soft surfaces at standing heights. If you are on carpet, you will need a heavy-duty floor mat to stabilize the base.

Furthermore, if you are under 5'2", many standard standing desks (like the Vari) have a minimum height that is too tall for ergonomic sitting. In these cases, you should check for "low-range" or "extended-range" models specifically designed for shorter statures.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is a standing desk worth it for gacha gaming?
Yes, because gacha games often require long periods of "active idling" or repetitive farming. Switching between sitting and standing helps prevent the physical fatigue associated with long sessions in [Hethereau](https://www.ign.com/games/neverness-to-everness), keeping you more alert for daily commissions.

### How much weight capacity do I really need?
While a standard monitor and keyboard weigh very little, a full WFH setup with a desktop PC, three monitors, and studio speakers can easily exceed 100 lbs. Choosing a desk with at least 200 lbs of capacity, like the Vari Electric, ensures the motors aren't strained, extending the life of the desk.

### Will my monitors shake when I type or play?
All standing desks have some degree of "wobble" when fully extended. However, desks with stability braces or T-style legs, such as the <a href="https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Uplift+V2&tag=jeffzen911-20" rel="sponsored nofollow noopener" target="_blank">Uplift V2</a>, minimize this. To further reduce shake, ensure your monitor arms are high-quality and your desk is on a hard surface.

### Can I use a standing desk on carpet?
You can, but it will be less stable than on a hard floor. High-pile carpet allows the desk feet to shift, which is amplified at standing heights. Using a rigid chair mat or a specialized "desk feet" stabilizer can help maintain the precision needed for Esper Ability timing.
