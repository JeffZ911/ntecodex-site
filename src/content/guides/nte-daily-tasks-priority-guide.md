## Best Weapons

![Best Weapons](/img/nte-daily-tasks-priority-guide/inline-2.webp)


In Neverness to Everness, weapons are known as "Arcs." These items provide more than just raw stats; they offer unique "Arc Skills" that can fundamentally change a character's rotation. For Nanally, the goal is to find an Arc that complements her high Crit DMG scaling and rewards her for staying on the field.

### Signature Weapon: Fluff of Ferocity
Fluff of Ferocity is the definitive S-Rank Arc for Nanally. It features a Crit Rate substat, which is vital because Nanally’s kit focuses heavily on Crit DMG. The Arc Skill increases ATK by a set percentage every time a Basic Attack hits an enemy, stacking up to five times. At max stacks, it grants an additional Anima DMG bonus, making it the highest damage-ceiling option available.

### Best F2P Alternatives: Oraora! and Song of the Veil
For players who did not pull on the weapon banner, "Oraora!" is an exceptional A-Rank alternative. This Arc can be refined using materials found in daily Anomaly Zones. At max refinement (R5), it provides a 36% damage bonus to Basic Attacks, which directly buffs Nanally's whirlwind combo. "Song of the Veil" is another viable choice if you lack Crit Rate, though it falls behind in raw output compared to Oraora!.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/nte-daily-tasks-priority-guide/inline-3.webp)


The artifact system in NTE is divided into two parts: Cartridges (the set bonuses) and Blocks (the Tetris-style modules that provide raw stats). To optimize Nanally, you must balance the set bonuses with the spatial constraints of the Tetris grid.

### Top Cartridge Set: Fireflies and the Forest
The "Fireflies and the Forest" set is the Best-in-Slot (BiS) option for any Anima Main DPS.
*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Increases Anima DMG by 10%.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** Increases Crit DMG by 14% every time an "Esper Cycle" reaction is triggered, stacking up to 4 times (56% total).

Because Nanally triggers Blossom and Hexed constantly when paired with the right teammates, she can maintain the maximum 56% Crit DMG bonus throughout the entire fight.

### Optimizing the Tetris Block System
Each Cartridge has a specific shape. When placing them into Nanally's "Console," you must fit them together without overlapping. To maximize efficiency, focus on "Type III Modules." These specific blocks provide the highest stat density. Activating the "Console Bonus" requires filling at least 80% of the grid with matching module types, which grants a flat 8% Crit Rate increase.

### Recommended Substats Priority
When farming for modules in daily Anomaly Zones, look for the following priority:
1.  **Crit DMG:** Nanally's most important stat due to her built-in buffs.
2.  **Crit Rate:** Necessary to ensure her high-damage hits actually crit.
3.  **Anima DMG%:** Increases the base damage of all her abilities.
4.  **ATK%:** Provides the foundation for her damage scaling.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/nte-daily-tasks-priority-guide/inline-5.webp)


The key to maximizing Nanally's damage is ensuring all buffs and elemental fields are active before she uses her Ultimate. Following a strict rotation will significantly increase your clear speeds in daily Anomaly Zones.

### Standard Combat Opener
1.  **Sakiri Skill:** Start with Sakiri to deploy her Incantation field and trigger her ATK-boosting passive.
2.  **Jiuyuan Skill:** Swap to Jiuyuan to group the enemies into the center of Sakiri's field.
3.  **Zero Skill:** Use Zero’s skill to apply the Cosmos status to all grouped enemies.
4.  **Nanally Entry:** Swap to Nanally and immediately use her Skill (E) to activate "Ichi-daime's Authority."
5.  **Nanally Burst:** Use Nanally's Ultimate (Q) to summon the Underboss.
6.  **Arc Skill:** If using Fluff of Ferocity, activate the Arc Skill (R) now.
7.  **Combo:** Hold the Basic Attack button to perform the whirlwind attack, triggering Blossom and Hexed simultaneously on all enemies.

### Maximizing Burst Damage Windows
During the "Underboss" duration, avoid dodging unless absolutely necessary. Every second Nanally is not attacking is a loss of potential damage from her summon. Use Adler’s shield or Haniel’s healing to tank through minor hits so Nanally can finish her full whirlwind rotation.

### Common Rotation Mistakes to Avoid
*   **Using Nanally's Ultimate before her Skill:** You lose the Crit DMG buff from "Ichi-daime's Authority."
*   **Forgetting the Cosmos Field:** Without Zero's Cosmos application, you cannot trigger Blossom, which is your main source of DEF shred.
*   **Swapping out Nanally too early:** Her Underboss summon disappears if Nanally leaves the field before the duration ends.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/nte-daily-tasks-priority-guide/inline-6.webp)


### How do I unlock Nanally?
Nanally is a limited S-Rank character. She is currently only obtainable through the "Scarborough Fair" gacha banner. This banner uses "Gold Records" and has a 90-pull pity system. If you miss her now, you will have to wait for a future rerun, as she is not added to the standard "Urban Resonance" pool.

### What are the most important daily tasks for Nanally?
To keep Nanally at peak performance, you must complete the following daily:
*   **Anomaly Zones:** Farm these to obtain "Bubble Cans" (level-up material) and "Fireflies and the Forest" Cartridges.
*   **Beetle Coin Farming:** Participate in city activities and combat challenges to earn Beetle Coins, which are needed to buy refinement materials for the Oraora! Arc.
*   **Beyond the Rail:** Complete your weekly attempts for high-tier Blocks to optimize your Tetris grid.

### Can Nanally walk on walls without her skill?
No. The wall-walking ability is tied directly to the "Ichi-daime's Authority" buff. If the buff expires while you are on a wall, Nanally will fall. Always keep an eye on the green Anima gauge above her health bar to ensure you have enough duration to reach your destination.

### Is her signature weapon necessary?
While "Fluff of Ferocity" provides roughly a 20% damage increase over F2P options, Nanally is perfectly viable with "Oraora!". The most important factor is her Cartridge set and team composition. If you are F2P, focus on getting her 4-piece "Fireflies and the Forest" set before worrying about her S-Rank Arc.

### Daily Resource Farming Priority for Nanally
1.  **Bubble Cans (Anima):** Required for every 10 levels of character progression.
2.  **Type III Modules:** Focus on the "Triangle" and "L-Shape" blocks as they are the easiest to fit into the BiS grid.
3.  **Arc Experience Chips:** Arcs require a significant amount of XP to reach level 80.
4.  **Hethereau Credits:** The standard currency for all upgrades; you will run out quickly during the mid-game (Levels 40-60).
