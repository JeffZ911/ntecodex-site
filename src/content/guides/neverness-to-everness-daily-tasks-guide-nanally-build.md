### Who is Nanally?

Nanally is an S-Class Anima Esper belonging to the Plasma archetype. Released during the version 1.0 launch banner, "The Ichi-daime," she is characterized by her high mobility and "anti-gravity" combat style. Her signature mechanic, "Ichi-daime's Authority," allows her to walk on walls and ceilings while gaining a massive 30% CRIT DMG bonus. In combat, she summons her "Underboss"—a spectral entity that mimics her attacks—to deal coordinated follow-up damage. This makes her exceptionally strong against bosses with high break gauges, as her sheer hit count can deplete shields faster than almost any other character.

### Managing Character Pixels and City Energy

In Neverness to Everness, stamina is split into two categories: Character Pixels and City Energy. 

**Character Pixels** are your primary resource for combat progression. They regenerate at a rate of 1 pixel every 6 minutes, totaling 240 pixels per day. To build Nanally, you should spend these exclusively in the **Anomaly Zones**. Focus on stages that drop Anima-type upgrade materials and "Fireflies and the Forest" Cartridges. Always challenge the highest difficulty level available to you, as drop rates for S-Rank Modules scale significantly with difficulty.

**City Energy** is used for the City Tycoon systems, such as deliveries and business management. While not directly tied to combat stats, spending City Energy increases your Tycoon Level, which eventually unlocks higher-tier shops where you can purchase rare Arcs and refinement materials.

## Best Weapons

![Best Weapons](/img/neverness-to-everness-daily-tasks-guide-nanally-build/inline-2.webp)


Nanally requires **Plasma-type Arcs** to function. Equipping the wrong Arc type will disable her passive synergies, severely neutering her damage output. Arcs in NTE function as your primary weapon, providing a base ATK stat, a substat, and a unique passive effect.

### Top Tier: Ready-Ready (Signature)

The **Ready-Ready** Arc is Nanally’s signature S-Rank weapon and is undeniably her best-in-slot (BiS) option. It provides a massive boost to CRIT Rate, which is essential for triggering her high-damage follow-up attacks. 

*   **Substat:** CRIT Rate (up to 24% at Max Level).
*   **Passive Effect (Commander Tiger Talisman):** Increases ATK by 15%. Casting a Redirect Skill or Ultimate increases Basic Attack and Critical Riposte damage by 15% for 15 seconds (stacks twice). Additionally, it grants a "Tiger Talisman" that increases damage to Bosses by 10% for 10 seconds.

### Alternative S-Rank: Blow up the Crowd

If you haven't pulled Ready-Ready, **Blow up the Crowd** is a viable S-Rank alternative. While it is technically Haniel’s signature, its Plasma-type compatibility and focus on Ultimate DMG make it a strong secondary choice for Nanally players who prefer a burst-heavy playstyle.

### Best F2P and Budget Arcs

For free-to-play players, the **Raging Flames** Arc is the gold standard. You can obtain this S-Rank Arc for free by clearing the "Ride Assault" Anomaly Commission at Bridge Crossings after 8:00 PM in-game time. It provides a flat 15% Lakshana/Anima DMG bonus and increases Redirect Skill damage after using an Ultimate.

If you are early in the game, the A-Rank Arc **Oraora!** is surprisingly effective. It grants a 2% Basic Attack DMG increase after every hit, stacking up to 10 times. Since Nanally attacks so frequently, you will maintain maximum stacks throughout every encounter.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/neverness-to-everness-daily-tasks-guide-nanally-build/inline-3.webp)


The gear system in Neverness to Everness is called the **Console**, which consists of **Cartridges** (set bonuses) and **Modules** (individual stat blocks). Modules come in different "Tetris" shapes (geometries) and must be fitted into a grid to activate the Cartridge's full potential.

### Recommended Cassette Sets

The absolute best set for Nanally is **Fireflies and the Forest**. This set is tailor-made for Anima damage dealers who hit multiple times per second.

*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Anima DMG +10%.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** Whenever a nearby enemy takes Anima DMG from the team, the wearer’s CRIT DMG increases by 8%, stacking up to 7 times (56% total). This effect stays active even if Nanally is off-field.

### The Block and Module System Explained

Nanally has a unique interaction with the Console grid: she gains a **6% CRIT Rate bonus for every Type II Module** equipped. When building her grid, your goal is to complete the 4-piece Fireflies and the Forest set using as few squares as possible, then fill every remaining slot with Type II Modules. This strategy allows Nanally to reach nearly 100% CRIT Rate without relying on perfect substat rolls.

### Optimal Stat Distribution

When farming Modules, prioritize the following stats:

1.  **Main Stat (Cartridge):** Anima DMG Bonus.
2.  **Main Stat (Modules):** CRIT DMG (if CRIT Rate is over 70%) or ATK%.
3.  **Substat Priority:** CRIT Rate > CRIT DMG > ATK% > Flat ATK.

## Rotation

To maximize Nanally’s damage, you must align her "Underboss" window with your team's buffs. Nanally’s damage is heavily back-loaded, meaning her strongest hits come at the end of her combo strings.

### Opening Sequence

1.  **Buff Phase:** Start with **Sakiri**. Use her Ultimate to apply the team-wide ATK buff.
2.  **Enabler Phase:** Switch to **Esper Zero**. Cast Zero’s Redirect Skill to apply the Cosmos element and prepare for the Blossom reaction.
3.  **State Activation:** Switch to **Nanally**. Immediately cast her Skill, **Colucci Howling Technique**. This enters the **Ichi-daime's Authority** state, granting her 30% CRIT DMG and enhanced mobility.

### Maximizing the Underboss Window

Once Nanally is in her Authority state, cast her Ultimate, **Colucci Ultimate Technique**. This summons the **Underboss** for 6 seconds. During this 6-second window, you must execute the following combo:

*   **Combo String:** 3-Basic Attacks -> 3-Charged Attacks (Hold).
*   **Why this works:** The Underboss coordinates with every hit Nanally lands. By using the 3-Basic + 3-Charged combo, you trigger the maximum number of follow-up instances while the 56% CRIT DMG buff from the "Fireflies and the Forest" set is at peak stacks.

### Energy Management and Cooldowns

Nanally’s Ultimate has a 15-second cooldown. If you find yourself with downtime, do not switch Nanally out immediately. Her "Ichi-daime's Authority" state ends if she leaves the battlefield. Instead, use her **Vita Pistils (Gun Special)** to maintain damage from a distance or use her wall-running to reposition while waiting for your support cooldowns to refresh.

## FAQ

### General Daily Task Questions

**What time do daily tasks reset in Neverness to Everness?**
Daily tasks and server resets occur at 5:00 AM UTC+8. For US players, this is typically 4:00 PM EST or 1:00 PM PST the previous day.

**How do I get more Mhm! Coins?**
You get one Mhm! Coin per day by visiting **Nacupeda's Pool** in the Bridge Crossing district. You must select the "Make a Sincere Wish" dialogue option. Trading these in at the fountain's exchange shop is the only way to get a free S-Rank Arc without using the gacha system.

**Is the Cafe by Origen worth managing daily?**
Yes. The Cafe is your primary source of passive Fons (gold). Upgrading your recipes and restocking ingredients daily ensures a steady flow of cash for leveling up Nanally’s Arcs and Modules, which become extremely expensive after Level 60.

### Nanally Build Specifics

**Can I use a Liquid or Gas type Arc on Nanally?**
Technically yes, but it is highly discouraged. Nanally’s core passives only trigger when a **Plasma-type Arc** is equipped. Using other types will result in a roughly 40% loss in total damage output.

**What is the "Underboss"?**
The Underboss is Nanally's spectral summon from her Ultimate. It is not a separate pet; it is a follow-up trigger. Every time Nanally attacks, the Underboss deals a corresponding instance of Anima damage. This damage is considered "Follow-up Attack Damage" and benefits from specific Module bonuses.

### Resource Farming Tips

**Where is the best place to farm Fireflies and the Forest Cartridges?**
These are found in the **Anomaly Zone: Verdant Echoes**. It is recommended to save your "Double Drop" vouchers specifically for this zone if you are a Nanally main.

**How do I increase my Participation Points quickly?**
The fastest way to hit 100 points is to spend all 240 Character Pixels first, then complete three "City Deliveries" using City Energy. This usually takes less than 10 minutes and completes the daily requirement.
