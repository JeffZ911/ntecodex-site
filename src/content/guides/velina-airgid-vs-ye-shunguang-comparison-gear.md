## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/velina-airgid-vs-ye-shunguang-comparison-gear/inline-4.webp)


Not every new piece of hardware is a necessary purchase. You should consider skipping an upgrade if your current setup already meets certain thresholds:

* **Monitor Refresh Rates:** If you currently own a 1440p IPS monitor with at least a 144Hz refresh rate, the jump to a 165Hz or 180Hz IPS panel will be negligible. The human eye struggles to perceive these small increments unless you are jumping to 240Hz or switching panel technologies (like moving to OLED).
* **Chair Ergonomics:** Don't buy a premium gaming chair if your current office chair already has a dedicated, adjustable lumbar support system. Many high-end office chairs provide better ergonomic health than "gaming" branded seats that prioritize aesthetics over spinal alignment.
* **Mouse Sensors:** Avoid upgrading your mouse if your current sensor is already a modern optical model (e.g., PMW3360 or better). The human hand cannot perceive the difference in DPI beyond 1600 for most action-RPG titles, and higher numbers are often just marketing.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the best monitor for Neverness to Everness?
The best monitor for this title is one that can handle high-contrast urban environments and fast-paced combat. An OLED panel like the ASUS ROG Swift PG27AQDM is ideal because it provides true blacks and near-instant response times, which are critical for the game's aesthetic and mechanics.

### Is a gaming chair actually better for my back?
Not necessarily. A chair is only "better" if it offers adjustable lumbar support and fits your body size. The Secretlab TITAN Evo 2022 is recommended because its L-ADAPT system allows for precise adjustments that most entry-level gaming chairs and standard office chairs lack.

### Do I need a high-DPI mouse for action-RPGs?
While high-DPI sensors are common, you rarely need more than 1600 DPI for precision in action-RPGs. The more important feature is the number of programmable buttons, which allows you to map your Resonance Skills and items to your mouse for faster access during combat.

### Can I use the same headset for my PC and my phone?
Yes, if the headset supports simultaneous wireless or has both Bluetooth and 2.4GHz connectivity. The SteelSeries Arctis Nova 7 is a top choice for this because it allows you to stay connected to both devices at once, making it easy to switch between platforms.
