## Overview

Jianxin is a 5-star Aero Resonator who utilizes Gauntlets to manipulate the flow of Chi. While many players initially view her as a standard Sub-DPS, her true value lies in her ability to act as a "comfy" sustain unit. She is a hybrid Support and Sustain character capable of grouping enemies with her Resonance Liberation and providing a persistent shield that heals the active character over time.

The core of Jianxin’s kit is her Forte Circuit: Primordial Chi Spiral. Unlike traditional tanks in other action RPGs, Jianxin does not rely on HP to bolster her defenses. All of Jianxin's utility—including the strength of her shields and the potency of her healing—scales directly with her ATK stat. This makes her gearing process unique, as you must balance offensive stats with utility stats like Energy Recharge and Concerto Energy.

Focusing on a shield-heavy support build allows Jianxin to replace a dedicated healer like Verina or Baizhi in high-level content like the Tower of Adversity. This frees up those top-tier healers for your other team compositions. However, the trade-off is that Jianxin requires significant field time to channel her shield, which can lower the overall team DPS if not managed correctly.

### How Jianxin's Shield Works

Jianxin’s shield is generated through her Forte Circuit, "Primordial Chi Spiral." When her Chi gauge is full (120 units), holding the Heavy Attack button enters the "Zhoutian Progress" state. This is a multi-stage channeling process:

1.  **Zhoutian Minor:** Provides a basic shield.
2.  **Zhoutian Major:** Increases the shield strength.
3.  **Zhoutian Grand:** Reaches the maximum shield capacity and triggers significant healing.

The shield persists for 30 seconds, even if Jianxin is swapped out. While the shield is active, it restores HP to the current character every 6 seconds. If the shield is broken, the healing effect ends immediately.

### Why ATK Scaling Matters for Shielding

The most common mistake when building Jianxin is stacking HP. Because her shield scales with ATK, a higher ATK stat directly translates to a thicker damage barrier.

**Shield Scaling Percentages (Forte Level 10):**
*   **Minor Shield:** 16.08% ATK + 120
*   **Major Shield:** 32.16% ATK + 241
*   **Grand Shield:** 64.32% ATK + 482

By reaching high ATK thresholds, Jianxin can generate a shield that absorbs thousands of points of damage, effectively making your Main DPS immune to interruption and chip damage.

## Best Echoes

In Wuthering Waves, the Echo system replaces traditional artifact sets. For a Jianxin build focused on shielding and team support, you have two primary set options depending on your team's needs.

### Optimal Echo Sets for Shielding

1.  **Rejuvenating Glow (5-Piece Set):** This is the premier choice for a support Jianxin. The 2-piece bonus increases Healing Bonus by 10%. The 5-piece bonus is the real prize: when Jianxin heals an ally (which happens automatically while her shield is active), the entire team receives a 15% ATK boost for 30 seconds.
2.  **Moonlit Clouds (5-Piece Set):** If your team already has a healer using Rejuvenating Glow, Jianxin should use Moonlit Clouds. This set focuses on Energy Recharge and provides a 22.5% ATK buff to the next character swapped in via Outro Skill. This is excellent for hypercarry teams featuring Jiyan or Calcharo.

### Main Echo Selection: Bell-Borne vs. Heron

Your primary Echo (the top slot) determines your active Echo skill.

*   **Bell-Borne Geochelone:** This is the mandatory choice for a shield-focused build. When activated, it provides a shield that reduces incoming damage by 50% and increases the current character's DMG by 10%. This damage reduction stacks effectively with the damage negation provided by Jianxin's own shield, making your team virtually unkillable.
*   **Impermanence Heron:** Use this only if you are running Moonlit Clouds and want to maximize the buffing potential for your Main DPS. It provides Energy Recharge and further buffs the next character's damage.

### Stat and Substat Priority

To maximize the shield, you need to prioritize ATK and Energy Recharge. Jianxin’s channeling is vulnerable to interruption, and her Resonance Liberation is expensive.

*   **4-Cost Echo:** Healing Bonus or ATK%
*   **3-Cost Echo:** Energy Recharge (at least one) and ATK%
*   **1-Cost Echo:** ATK%
*   **Substat Priority:** Energy Recharge > ATK% > Flat ATK > Heavy Attack DMG > Resonance Liberation DMG.

**Target Stat Benchmarks:**
*   **Energy Recharge:** 140% - 160% (Crucial for consistent rotations).
*   **ATK:** 2,000+ (Mid-to-late game goal).

## Rotation

The key to playing Jianxin effectively is minimizing her field time while ensuring the "Grand Zhoutian" shield is fully formed.

### Chi Generation Phase

Jianxin starts with zero Chi. You must fill the gauge to 120 units before you can channel the shield.
1.  **Resonance Skill (Calming Air):** Use this to parry an incoming attack. A successful parry generates a significant amount of Chi and Concerto Energy.
2.  **Basic Attack Combo:** Perform the full basic attack string. The final hits of her combo generate the most Chi.
3.  **Resonance Liberation:** Use her ultimate to group enemies. This also contributes to Chi generation.

### Executing the Full Shield Channel

Once the gauge is full, find a safe window (or use her Resonance Skill parry first).
*   **Hold Heavy Attack:** Jianxin will begin channeling. You will see three segments fill up on the screen.
*   **Stages:** Do not release the button until the third stage (Grand Zhoutian) is reached. Releasing early results in a much weaker shield and no healing.
*   **Interruption:** If an enemy is about to hit you, you can release the button to trigger a Chi blast, but it is better to wait for the shield to form.

### Switching and Buff Alignment

1.  **Jianxin enters:** Use Resonance Skill and Basic Attacks to fill Chi.
2.  **Ultimate:** Group enemies and shred Aero resistance (if Resonance Chains are unlocked).
3.  **Channel Shield:** Hold Heavy Attack until the Grand Zhoutian shield appears.
4.  **Echo Skill:** Use Bell-Borne Geochelone for the damage reduction buff.
5.  **Outro Skill:** Swap to your Main DPS once the Concerto gauge is full to pass the 38% Liberation Deepen buff.

## FAQ

### Does Jianxin's shield stack with other shields?
In Wuthering Waves, shields generally do not stack their HP pools. If you have multiple shields active (e.g., Jianxin's shield and the Bell-Borne Geochelone shield), they will deplete simultaneously as you take damage. However, the damage reduction from Bell-Borne makes Jianxin's shield last significantly longer because it reduces the raw damage the shield takes.

### Is Jianxin better than Verina or Baizhi for healing?
Jianxin offers lower "burst" healing than Verina or Baizhi. She is a preventative sustain unit. If your team is already at 10% HP, Jianxin will struggle to bring them back quickly. However, if you use her shield proactively, your team will rarely take damage in the first place.

### How much Energy Regen does Jianxin actually need?
For a smooth rotation where you use her Ultimate every time she takes the field, aim for 150% Energy Recharge. If you are using the Marcato weapon, you can drop this slightly to 140%.

### Can Jianxin be played as a Main DPS?
While possible, her damage scaling on Basic Attacks is lower than dedicated DPS units like Jiyan or Rover. If you choose to play her as a Main DPS, focus on the **Sierra Gale** Echo set and stack Crit Rate/Crit DMG, but be aware that her shielding will be slightly weaker than a dedicated ATK/ER build.

### What is the best Resonance Chain (Duplicate) for shielding?
Her **Sequence 4 (S4)** is the most impactful for a shield build. It increases the shield's damage absorption by a significant margin and prevents Jianxin from being interrupted while channeling her Forte Circuit. **Sequence 6 (S6)** also provides a massive boost to her Chi generation, making her rotations much faster.
