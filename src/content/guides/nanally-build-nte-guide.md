Nanally is an S-Rank Anima-element Main DPS who serves as a cornerstone of the Eibon Antique Shop faction. Her playstyle is defined by high-octane physical-arc combat, where she swings a massive Plasma Arc to crush enemies while commanding her spectral "Underboss" to perform coordinated follow-up attacks. ### Core Mechanics: Ichi-daime's Authority
The "Ichi-daime's Authority" is Nanally’s signature combat state. Activating this state—usually through her Skill, the Colucci Howling Technique—transforms her movement and attack patterns. While under the influence of Authority, Nanally gains a massive 40% boost to her Crit DMG and ignores a portion of the enemy’s physical defense. 

Furthermore, this state enables her "Skating" movement, allowing her to glide across the battlefield with her Plasma Arc. This isn't just for show; successful dodges while skating grant Nanally "Authority Stacks," which further increase the damage of her next Heavy Hitter (Charged Attack).

### Skill Upgrade Priority
To get the most out of Nanally, you should prioritize your resources on her Ultimate and Skill first. Her Basic Attacks are important, but they gain most of their value from the multipliers provided by her active states.

1.  **Colucci Ultimate Technique (Ultimate):** This is her highest priority. It summons the Underboss, which accounts for nearly 50% of her total damage output in high-end rotations.
2.  **Colucci Howling Technique (Skill):** Upgrading this increases the Crit DMG bonus provided by the Ichi-daime's Authority state.
3.  **Basic Attack (Plasma Swing):** Essential for her 3-hit combo chains, but secondary to her state-based multipliers.
4.  **Passive (Antique Legacy):** Provides minor utility and energy regeneration.

### Ready-Ready (Signature Weapon)
Ready-Ready is Nanally's Best-in-Slot (BiS) weapon for a reason. Its Crit Rate substat makes reaching the 75% threshold significantly easier, allowing you to focus your Disk substats on Crit DMG. The passive specifically buffs Basic Attacks, which is the primary way Nanally interacts with the Underboss during her burst window.

### Song of the Whale (F2P S-Rank)
For players who skipped the weapon banner, Song of the Whale is the premier free-to-play alternative. It can be obtained through the Standard Resonance or via high-level world exploration rewards. While it lacks the Crit Rate of Ready-Ready, its high base ATK and HP sustain make Nanally much more durable in the "Hollow" endgame zones.

### Oraora! (A-Rank Alternative)
If you are just starting out, Oraora! is a fantastic A-Rank choice. Nanally hits very quickly during her Authority state, meaning she can max out the 10-stack ATK buff in less than three seconds. It is a reliable, high-floor weapon that remains viable until you pull an S-Rank Plasma Arc.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/nanally-build-nte-guide/inline-3.webp)


Disks function as the primary equipment system in Neverness to Everness. For a Main DPS like Nanally, the goal is to maximize her Anima DMG and ensure her critical hits are as impactful as possible.

### Fireflies and the Forest (End-game Set)
This is the mandatory 4-piece set for any serious Nanally build.
*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Increases Anima DMG by 15%.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** When the wearer deals Anima damage, Crit DMG is increased by 14%, stacking up to 4 times (56% total). This effect lasts for 15 seconds and can be refreshed.

Since Nanally deals Anima damage with every swing of her Plasma Arc and every strike from her Underboss, she maintains the maximum 56% Crit DMG bonus with 100% uptime.

### Shadow Creed (Early-game Set)
If you haven't farmed a good Fireflies set yet, Shadow Creed is a viable bridge.
*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Increases ATK by 12%.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** Increases Basic and Charged Attack DMG by 25%.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/nanally-build-nte-guide/inline-5.webp)


To maximize Nanally's damage, you must coordinate her Ultimate with her Authority state. The goal is to have the Underboss active while Nanally is in her empowered form.

### Step-by-Step Combat Flowchart
1.  **Support Setup:** Start with Sakiri or Haniel. Use their Skills to apply long-term buffs or shields to the active character.
2.  **Cycle Activation:** Switch to [Zero](#TODO:keyword:zero-build-nte) and use his Resonance Skill to trigger the Cosmos Esper Cycle. This ensures Nanally enters the field with maximum energy potential.
3.  **Enter Authority:** Switch to Nanally and immediately cast **Colucci Howling Technique (Skill)**. This puts her into the Ichi-daime's Authority state, boosting her Crit stats.
4.  **Summon Underboss:** Cast **Colucci Ultimate Technique (Ultimate)**. The Underboss will now appear and begin mirroring your attacks.
5.  **The Combo Loop:** Execute the **3-hit Basic -> 3-hit Charged (Heavy Hitter)** combo. This loop takes approximately 6 seconds, which perfectly aligns with the Underboss's duration.
6.  **Refresh:** Once the Underboss vanishes, swap back to your supports to regenerate energy and restart the cycle.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/nanally-build-nte-guide/inline-6.webp)


**Is Nanally worth pulling?**
Yes. Nanally is the definitive Patch 1.0 DPS benchmark. Her ability to deal sustained damage without relying on complex animation cancels makes her both beginner-friendly and incredibly potent in the late game.

**Which Awakening is best?**
Her **A1 (First Awakening)** is the most significant for quality of life, as it provides energy sustain whenever the Underboss hits an enemy. **A3** is the major power spike, adding extra follow-up hits to her Heavy Hitter attacks, which effectively increases her burst damage by 25%.

**Can she be played without her signature weapon?**
Absolutely. While Ready-Ready is her best option, **Song of the Whale** is a very competitive F2P alternative that provides enough raw power to clear all current endgame content, including the higher floors of the Mirage Tower.

**What exactly is the 'Underboss'?**
The Underboss is a summon unique to Nanally. It is not a separate entity with its own HP bar; rather, it is a visual manifestation of her "Authority." It mirrors her attacks with a slight delay. Crucially, the damage dealt by the Underboss is classified as "Follow-up Damage," which allows Nanally to benefit from specific gear and buffs that other DPS characters cannot utilize.
