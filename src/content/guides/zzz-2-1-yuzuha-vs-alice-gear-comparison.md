## When to skip the upgrade

![When to skip the upgrade](/img/zzz-2-1-yuzuha-vs-alice-gear-comparison/inline-4.webp)


Not every player needs to rush out and buy a $900 monitor. There are several scenarios where your current gear is likely sufficient.

If you currently own a high-quality 1440p IPS monitor with at least 144Hz, the jump to a 165Hz or 180Hz IPS like the LG 27GP850-B is negligible. You would be better off saving for a true upgrade to an OLED panel where the difference in contrast and response time is actually transformative.

Don't buy an OLED monitor if you leave your game on the same static menu screen for hours while AFK. Games like Neverness to Everness often have bright, static HUD elements or shop menus. If you are the type of player who leaves the game running in the background while doing other tasks, an OLED will significantly accelerate burn-in. In this case, a high-end IPS or Mini-LED monitor is a safer choice.

If you primarily play on mobile or tablet, high-end PC peripherals won't translate to a better experience unless you use a dedicated dock. While NTE is a cross-platform title, the visual fidelity on a mobile device is capped by the hardware's thermal limits and screen size. Investing in a high-end PC monitor only makes sense if you have a GPU capable of pushing 1440p at high frame rates.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which monitor is best for seeing character details in Neverness to Everness?
The ASUS ROG Swift OLED PG27AQDM is the best choice because its OLED panel provides infinite contrast. This allows you to see fine textures in both bright highlights and deep shadows, which is essential for the Unreal Engine 5 visuals in the city of Hethereau.

### Do I need a 360Hz monitor for RPGs like ZZZ or NTE?
While not strictly necessary, a 360Hz monitor like the Alienware AW2725DF provides the smoothest possible animation tracking. If you are someone who analyzes frame data or animation cancels, the increased motion clarity can be a significant advantage.

### How can I prevent burn-in on an OLED monitor while playing gacha games?
To prevent burn-in, you should hide your Windows taskbar, use a screen saver, and utilize the monitor's built-in pixel cleaning features. Additionally, avoid leaving static game menus open for extended periods when you are not actively playing.

### Is a multi-button mouse like the Razer Naga useful for Neverness to Everness?
Yes, because NTE and similar games have many sub-menus for characters, vehicles, and inventory. Mapping these to the side buttons of a mouse reduces the need for keyboard shortcuts and makes long farming sessions much more comfortable.
