### Top Tier: Blow up the Crowd (Signature)
Blow up the Crowd is Haniel's signature S-Rank Arc and her undisputed best-in-slot option. This weapon is tailor-made for her kit, offering a significant boost to Psyche DMG which enhances Hootie's pulses. ### Best Alternatives: Stellar Vile & Fluff of Fortitude
If you do not have her signature Arc, Stellar Vile is a top-tier alternative. It focuses heavily on maximizing team-wide utility, ensuring that Haniel’s buff uptime remains at 100% during long boss encounters. 

For players who want Haniel to contribute more personal damage, Fluff of Fortitude (a Plasma-type Arc) is a strong offensive choice. F2P players should prioritize A-Rank Arcs that offer flat ATK% or Charge Efficiency. ## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/best-build-hannah-nte/inline-5.webp)


To maximize Haniel's impact, you must follow a specific rotation that ensures her buffs are active when your Main DPS takes the field.

1.  **Opener:** Swap to Haniel and immediately use her Skill (**Silent Moonlit Forest Guardian**) to deploy Hootie. This starts the off-field Psyche application and begins generating Ensemble Stacks.
2.  **Energy Gain:** Use a single Charged Basic Attack (**Paranormal Cannon**) if you need extra energy. This attack triggers ricochet damage that can hit multiple enemies, generating extra Esper Meter.
3.  **Buff Phase:** Once her Ultimate is ready, activate **Paranormal Ace**. This enters Haniel into her enhanced state and triggers the 4-piece Speedy Hedgehog set bonus (if equipped).
4.  **Execution:** Immediately swap to your Main DPS (e.g., Nanally or Daffodil). You now have a 12-to-20-second window where your ATK is significantly boosted.
5.  **Reset:** Once the buffs expire or Hootie disappears, swap back to Haniel and repeat the cycle.

### Standard Support Rotation Flow
**Haniel Skill -> 1-2 Basic Attacks -> Haniel Ultimate -> Swap to DPS -> DPS Burst -> Reset.**

### Maximizing the Paranormal Ace State
During the Paranormal Ace state, Haniel provides her maximum buffing value. It is critical that you do not stay on Haniel during this window unless you are forced to dodge. The goal is to transfer those stats to your primary damage dealer as quickly as possible.
