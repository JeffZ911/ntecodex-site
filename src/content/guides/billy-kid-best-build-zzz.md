## Overview

![Overview](/img/billy-kid-best-build-zzz/inline-1.webp)


Billy Kid is an A-Rank Physical Attack Agent who specializes in high-frequency ranged attacks and massive Area of Effect (AoE) damage. Unlike melee-focused Attackers, Billy relies on positioning and maintaining his "Crouching Shot" stance to maximize his damage output. He is a member of the Cunning Hares faction, which allows him to easily trigger faction-based synergies with other early-game units.

His gameplay revolves around the balance between mobility and stationary firepower. While he can use "Rolling Shot" to move across the battlefield and avoid incoming fire, his highest damage potential is unlocked when he stands still, engaging his Core Passive. This makes him an excellent choice for clearing groups of smaller enemies (mobs) and dealing sustained damage to bosses during their Daze windows.

### Billy Kid's Role in the Meta

In the current Zenless Zone Zero meta, Billy Kid serves as a primary Physical DPS, particularly effective in the early to mid-game. While S-Rank characters like Nekomata may offer higher burst potential in single-target scenarios, Billy’s ability to hit almost every enemy on the screen with his Ultimate, "Starlight, Shine Bright," gives him a unique niche in multi-target combat. He is often used in "Hypercarry" setups where the rest of the team focuses on stunning the enemy and shredding their defenses so Billy can unload his pistols during the burst window.

### Core Passive: Steady Shot Explained

Billy’s Core Passive, Steady Shot, is what separates a casual player from a master. When Billy remains stationary while performing his Basic Attack, he enters the "Crouching Shot" state. While in this state, his DMG is increased by a significant 25%. 

This mechanic rewards players who can predict enemy movements. If you move while shooting, you transition into "Rolling Shot," which is great for repositioning but sacrifices that 25% damage bonus. To play Billy optimally, you must find windows where you can remain stationary for as long as possible. Note that the damage bonus applies to his Basic Attacks and certain follow-up shots, making it the cornerstone of his sustained DPS.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/billy-kid-best-build-zzz/inline-5.webp)


Mastering Billy's rotation is about timing your entry into the field to coincide with the enemy's Daze state.

### Optimal Combat Combo

1.  **The Opener:** Start with **Anby**. Use her Basic Attack combo into her EX Special to build Daze. Your goal is to trigger a Chain Attack.
2.  **The Setup:** When the Chain Attack triggers, select **Nicole**. Her Chain Attack will gather enemies and apply a DEF shred debuff.
3.  **The Execution:** Switch to **Billy** for the final hit of the Chain Attack. 
4.  **The Burst:** Immediately hold the Basic Attack button to enter the **Crouching Shot** stance. If you have Energy, weave in his **EX Special Attack: Clearance Time**.
5.  **The Finisher:** If the Ultimate is available, use **Starlight, Shine Bright** while the enemies are still grouped and stunned.

### Mastering the Crouching Shot Stance

The biggest mistake players make is constantly moving. While Billy's "Rolling Shot" (moving while shooting) looks cool, it lowers your DPS. To maximize "Steady Shot," you should only move when an enemy telegraphs an attack. Use a Dodge to reposition, then immediately let go of the directional stick and resume firing to re-enter the Crouching Shot stance as quickly as possible.

### Skill Upgrade Priority

Focus your resources on Billy's damage-dealing skills first.
1.  **Core Skill:** This upgrades his base stats and the "Steady Shot" bonus.
2.  **Basic Attack:** This is his primary source of damage during field time.
3.  **Ultimate:** His most powerful AoE tool for clearing waves.
4.  **Special Attack:** Useful for burst, but secondary to Basic Attacks.
5.  **Dodge:** Upgrade this last as it provides the least offensive scaling.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/billy-kid-best-build-zzz/inline-6.webp)


### Is Billy Kid good for end-game content like Shiyu Defense?
Yes, Billy Kid is viable for Shiyu Defense, especially in stages with high enemy counts. However, as an A-Rank character, he requires high investment in his Drive Discs (specifically Crit stats) to keep up with S-Rank DPS units in the later "Critical Nodes." His ability to clear mobs quickly saves valuable time for the boss encounters.

### What is the optimal range for Billy Kid's attacks?
Billy is a mid-to-long-range fighter. His "Sweet Spot" is approximately 6 to 10 meters away from the target. If you are too far, you will experience damage fall-off. If you are too close, you risk being interrupted and losing your Crouching Shot bonus.

### Which Mindscape Cinema levels are most important for Billy?
Billy's Mindscape Cinema (MC) provides significant power spikes.
*   **M1:** Provides Energy generation, helping him use his Ultimate more frequently.
*   **M2:** Increases DMG after a Dodge Counter, rewarding skilled play.
*   **M4:** Increases the range and damage of his "Crouching Shot," making his positioning more flexible.
*   **M6:** Provides a stacking ATK buff (up to 5 stacks) when hitting enemies, massively increasing his sustained DPS.

### Can I build Billy Kid as a Physical Anomaly character?
While Billy deals Physical damage, he is not an Anomaly specialist. His "Anomaly Proficiency" and "Anomaly Mastery" stats are naturally lower than characters like Piper. It is much more effective to build him for traditional Crit and ATK to leverage his high attack frequency.
