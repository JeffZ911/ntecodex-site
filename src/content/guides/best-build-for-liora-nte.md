### Hathor's Role in the Meta

In the current 1.0 meta, Hathor sits at the top of the Burst DPS rankings. Her ability to trigger the Remora reaction (Lakshana + Cosmos) allows her to maintain high critical hit consistency, which is vital for clearing timed challenges like the Anomaly rifts. While characters like [Nanally](/characters/nanally-guide-nte) offer sustained Anima damage, Hathor provides the "finisher" potential needed to execute bosses during their stagger windows. She is not intended to be a "selfish" carry; rather, she is the explosive payoff for a well-executed team rotation.

### Key Combat Mechanics: Express Delivery Power

The core of Hathor's gameplay revolves around "Express Delivery Power." These are stacks displayed near her character icon that dictate the strength of her abilities. 

1.  **Stack Generation:** Hathor generates these stacks primarily through her Skill, **Aerial Command**. Using this skill while grounded or in mid-air allows her to dash toward enemies, gaining stacks upon successful hits.
2.  **The Burst State:** Once Hathor reaches 6 stacks, she can activate her Ultimate, **Emergency Delivery**. This transforms her basic attacks and skills into enhanced versions.
3.  **Consumption:** During her Ultimate, Hathor can spam **Cyclone Strike**, an empowered version of her skill that consumes stacks to deal massive AoE Lakshana damage. Managing these stacks ensures that no damage is wasted during her limited burst window.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

![Best Disks (Artifacts)](/img/best-build-for-liora-nte/inline-3.webp)


In Neverness to Everness, "Disks" are referred to as Cartridges, which are slotted into your character's gear console. For Hathor, there is one set that stands head and shoulders above the rest: the Street Boxer set.

### Best Set: Street Boxer

The Street Boxer cartridge set is specifically designed for Lakshana characters who rely on critical hits and elemental reactions. 

*   **2-Piece Bonus:** Increases Lakshana DMG by 10%. This is a straightforward multiplicative boost to Hathor's entire kit.
*   **4-Piece Bonus:** Increases CRIT Rate by 14%. This bonus is doubled to 28% for 20 seconds whenever the team triggers a **Remora** (Lakshana + Cosmos) or **Stain** (Lakshana + Psyche) reaction.

Because Hathor is almost always paired with a Cosmos character like Zero, she can maintain the 28% CRIT Rate bonus indefinitely. This allows players to focus almost entirely on CRIT DMG in their sub-stats, leading to massive damage numbers.

### Module Placement & Console Optimization

When equipping your Street Boxer set, pay attention to the Module Placement on your console. You want to prioritize the "Offensive" slot for your highest-rarity Lakshana DMG% cartridge. 

*   **Main Slot (Offensive):** Lakshana DMG% (Mandatory)
*   **Secondary Slot (Utility):** Cycle Intensity (Helps with Ultimate uptime)
*   **Tertiary Slot (Defense):** ATK% or HP% (ATK% is preferred to maximize scaling)

### Recommended Main and Sub-stats

To reach the "Golden Ratio" of 1:2 (CRIT Rate to CRIT DMG), aim for the following stats on your cartridges:

1.  **Main Stats:** Lakshana DMG% (Slot 1), CRIT Rate or CRIT DMG (Slot 2, depending on your weapon), ATK% (Slot 3).
2.  **Sub-stats Priority:** 
    *   CRIT DMG (Highest Priority)
    *   CRIT Rate (Until you reach ~50% unbuffed)
    *   ATK%
    *   Cycle Intensity (To ensure you can Ultimate every rotation)

## Team Comp

![Team Comp](/img/best-build-for-liora-nte/inline-4.webp)


Hathor’s effectiveness is dictated by the elements of her teammates. In NTE, elemental reactions (called "Resonances") are the key to clearing high-level content. Hathor thrives in "Charge" reaction teams.

### The Charge Meta Team

The "Charge" reaction is a trio-element synergy that occurs when you combine **Lakshana, Cosmos, and Anima**. This is currently the strongest team archetype for Hathor.

*   **Hathor (Main/Burst DPS):** Provides the Lakshana application and the primary damage during the burst window.
*   **Nanally (Sub-DPS/Anima):** Nanally provides off-field Anima damage. Her banner, "The Ichi-daime," introduced her as a premier partner for Hathor. She triggers the **Blossom** reaction with Cosmos, which seeds enemies for Hathor to detonate.
*   **Zero (Cycler/Cosmos):** The Main Character (Zero) is mandatory. Zero’s Cosmos element triggers **Remora** with Hathor, boosting CRIT Rate. More importantly, Zero possesses the "Instant Cycle" mechanic, which allows the team to skip portions of the Esper Cycle and get Hathor back into her burst state faster.
*   **Haniel (Support/Psyche):** Haniel provides healing and Psyche-based buffs. Her presence allows for the **Stain** reaction, which provides additional crowd control and shred.

### F2P Friendly Composition

If you do not have Nanally or Haniel, Hathor can still excel with the starting roster:

*   **Hathor:** Burst DPS.
*   **Zero (MC):** Cosmos support and Cycle management.
*   **Jiuyuan:** Anima application and shield utility.
*   **Haniel:** Healer (obtainable via early progression rewards).

### Synergy with Zero (MC)

Zero is the "glue" that holds Hathor teams together. Hathor’s main weakness is her long cooldown after her Ultimate ends. Zero’s "Instant Cycle" mechanic essentially recharges a portion of the team's energy and reduces active cooldowns when a specific elemental sequence is met. By swapping from Hathor to Zero immediately after the "Emergency Delivery" state ends, you can significantly reduce the downtime between Hathor's bursts.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/best-build-for-liora-nte/inline-5.webp)


Executing the correct rotation is the difference between Hathor feeling like a top-tier DPS or a mid-range attacker. The goal is to maximize the time spent in her amped state while benefiting from all team buffs.

### Step-by-Step Rotation Flowchart

1.  **Setup Phase:** Start with **Haniel** to drop her healing field and apply Psyche marks. Swap to **Zero** and use his Skill to apply Cosmos and build the Esper Cycle meter.
2.  **Stack Building:** Swap to **Hathor**. Use her Skill, **Aerial Command**, twice (or three times if necessary) to reach 6 stacks of Express Delivery Power. Use her basic aerial string to stay mobile while building these stacks.
3.  **The Burst Window:** Once at 6 stacks, activate Hathor’s Ultimate, **Emergency Delivery**. This will pull nearby enemies in and trigger a massive Lakshana explosion.
4.  **Damage Maximization:** While the Ultimate is active, spam the enhanced Skill, **Cyclone Strike**. Do not use basic attacks unless your Skill is on its (very short) internal burst cooldown. Each Cyclone Strike consumes stacks to deal AoE damage.
5.  **Cycle Reset:** Once the stacks are gone and the Ultimate ends, swap immediately to **Zero**. Use Zero’s burst to trigger "Instant Cycle," which will refund energy to Hathor and prepare the next rotation.

### Building Express Delivery Power

It is important to note that you should never use Hathor's Ultimate before reaching 6 stacks. Using it at 3 or 4 stacks significantly reduces the duration of her amped state and the number of Cyclone Strikes she can perform. If you find it difficult to build stacks, ensure you are hitting multiple enemies with Aerial Command, as each enemy hit contributes to stack generation.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/best-build-for-liora-nte/inline-6.webp)


**Is Liora a real character in NTE?**
Liora is not the official name of any character in the current release. The community used the name "Liora" to describe the character now officially known as Hathor during the pre-release and beta periods. If you are looking for a "Liora build," this Hathor guide is exactly what you need.

**How do I get Raging Flames for free?**
You must defeat the **Headless Rider** Anomaly boss. This boss is located in the Hethereau City outskirts. It is a level-gated challenge, so ensure your team is at least level 40 before attempting it. Once defeated, the weapon is a guaranteed first-time clear reward.

**What is the best element to pair with Hathor?**
Cosmos is her most essential partner. The **Remora** reaction (Lakshana + Cosmos) is what triggers the massive CRIT Rate buff from the Street Boxer cartridge set. Without a Cosmos character like Zero on the team, Hathor’s damage becomes much less consistent.

**Can Hathor be played as a Main DPS?**
Hathor is best described as a "Burst DPS." While she deals the most damage on the team, she lacks the sustained presence to stay on the field for 100% of the fight. She is most effective when you cycle through your other three characters to set up buffs, then bring her in to "nuke" the enemies.

**What stat should I prioritize if I don't have good Street Boxer pieces?**
If you are still farming for the Street Boxer set, prioritize **ATK%** and **Lakshana DMG%**. While CRIT is her best late-game stat, early-game progression is much smoother if you have a high flat ATK value to carry you through the story missions.
