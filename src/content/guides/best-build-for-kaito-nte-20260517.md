### Kaito (Zero) Role and Playstyle

Kaito’s playstyle revolves around his "Redirect Skill" and his Ultimate. His primary role is "Instant Cycle," meaning he can bypass the standard buildup time required to fill the Esper Meter. By utilizing his core skill, Kaito applies the Cosmos element to enemies while simultaneously spiking the team's energy reserves. This allows players to swap to a secondary element, such as Anima or Lakshana, to trigger immediate reactions.

In the early game, Kaito’s Solid-archetype attacks provide enough raw Cosmos DMG to carry players through the main story. However, as players reach the Hethereau Anomaly endgame, Kaito shifts into a "Reaction Enabler." His Cosmos application is remarkably consistent, making him the perfect partner for "Follow-up" specialists. Because Cosmos is a "Solid" element, it interacts uniquely with "Liquid" and "Bose" archetypes, creating a battlefield control loop that keeps enemies staggered.

## Best Disks (Artifacts)

The gear system in NTE consists of **Cartridges** and **Modules**, which are slotted into the character's **Console**. Cartridges provide powerful set bonuses, while Modules provide the raw stats and determine the shape of your build on the Console grid.

### Optimal Cartridge Sets

For a damage-oriented Kaito build, the **Lost Radiance** set is the gold standard. 
*   **2-Piece Bonus (Epic):** Increases Cosmos DMG by 10%.
*   **4-Piece Bonus (Legendary):** Ignores 25% of enemy DEF for 20 seconds after the wearer casts their Ultimate.

This set is particularly potent because the DEF-ignore effect is near-unconditional as long as Kaito maintains his Ultimate uptime. For a more support-focused Kaito, the **Speedy Hedgehog** set is the superior choice.
*   **2-Piece Bonus (Epic):** Increases Charge Efficiency by 12%.
*   **4-Piece Bonus (Legendary):** Increases all allies' ATK by 15% for 20 seconds after the wearer casts their Ultimate.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/best-build-for-kaito-nte-20260517/inline-5.webp)


Mastering Kaito requires understanding the timing of the Esper Cycle. You should never sit on his Redirect Skill; it should be used as soon as it is off cooldown to maximize the team's energy flow.

### Standard Combat Loop

1.  **Opener:** Use Kaito's Redirect Skill ([Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-17]) to hit the maximum number of enemies. This will instantly jump-start your Esper Meter.
2.  **Buffing:** Swap to your buffer (e.g., Sakiri or Haniel) to activate team-wide damage increases.
3.  **Reaction Phase:** Swap to your Main DPS (e.g., Nanally). Use their elemental skills to trigger Blossom. If Kaito is equipped with "The Rain That Shook the World," your reaction damage will be significantly enhanced during this window.
4.  **Burst Window:** Once the Esper Meter is full, swap back to Kaito and cast his Ultimate. This will shred enemy DEF (if using Lost Radiance) or buff the team (if using Speedy Hedgehog).
5.  **Support Loop:** While Kaito is off-field, his Support Skill ([Information not yet publicly available as of 2026-05-17]) will continue to apply Cosmos, allowing your Main DPS to maintain reaction triggers.

### Advanced Meter Management

In high-difficulty content like the Anomaly Containment zones, you must manage the "Cycle Intensity" stat. Kaito has the highest natural Cycle Rate in the game, but his damage scales with Cycle Intensity. To optimize this, ensure Kaito is the one who "closes" the reaction if your team needs a massive burst of damage, or use him as the "opener" if you need to sustain a long chain of follow-up attacks.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/best-build-for-kaito-nte-20260517/inline-6.webp)


**How do I get Kaito (Zero)?**
Kaito is the free starting protagonist of Neverness to Everness. While you receive his base S-Rank version at the start of the game, his "Awakenings" (ascensions) are earned by progressing through the main story chapters in Hethereau.

**Is Kaito male or female?**
Players can choose either a male or female avatar at the beginning of the game. Both versions are officially known as Esper Zero and share the exact same skills, stats, and Cosmos elemental alignment.

**What is the best element for Kaito?**
Kaito is locked to the Cosmos element. This is actually a major advantage, as Cosmos is one of the most versatile elements for triggering high-damage reactions like Blossom and Charged.

**Is Kaito better as a DPS or Support?**
He is a hybrid. In the early game (Levels 1-50), he can easily function as your primary DPS. In the late-game meta (Level 70+), his most effective role is a meter-generating support who enables high-tier limited characters like Nanally or [Hotori](/characters/hotori-best-team-nte).

**Which banner should I pull on for Kaito's gear?**
You should look for the "Arc Research Program" banner featuring **The Rain That Shook the World**. This Arc is a massive power spike for Kaito and is highly recommended if you plan to keep him in your main rotation.
