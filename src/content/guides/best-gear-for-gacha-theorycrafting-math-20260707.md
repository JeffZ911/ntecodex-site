### ASUS ROG Swift OLED PG27AQDM 27" 1440p 240Hz QD-OLED
The ASUS PG27AQDM is widely regarded as one of the most responsive displays available, making it ideal for the fast-paced Esper abilities used in NTE combat. The [0.03ms response time](https://www.rtings.com/monitor/reviews/asus/rog-swift-oled-pg27aqdm) virtually eliminates motion blur, which is crucial when you are tracking multiple enemy Anomalies on screen. ## What a theorycrafter actually needs

![What a theorycrafter actually needs](/img/best-gear-for-gacha-theorycrafting-math-20260707/inline-2.webp)

Theorycrafting is a distinct style of play that combines high-level mechanical execution with academic-level data analysis. To do this effectively, your setup needs:

1.  **High-Contrast Displays:** When comparing zzz 3.0 energy regen vs impact math, you are often looking at small font sizes in tables. High contrast, such as that found on the [ASUS PG27AQDM](https://www.rtings.com/monitor/reviews/asus/rog-swift-oled-pg27aqdm), reduces eye strain by making characters pop against the background.
2.  **Macro Support:** Games like NTE involve repetitive menu navigation for car customization and gear management. A mouse with a side-grid, like the Razer Naga V2 Pro, allows you to automate these clicks, saving your wrist from repetitive strain.
3.  **Open-Back Audio:** Closed-back headphones trap heat and create "clamping" pressure. Open-back designs, like the Sennheiser HD 560S, allow for better airflow and a more natural sound, which is essential for players who spend their entire day in-game.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the difference between energy regen and impact in ZZZ 3.0?
Energy Regen determines how quickly a character's energy bar fills, allowing for more frequent use of EX Special attacks. Impact, on the other hand, scales the amount of Daze a character applies to enemies, which is essential for triggering the "Stun" state on bosses.

### Does a 240Hz monitor help with gacha spreadsheets?
While the high refresh rate is primarily for smooth combat, it also makes scrolling through long spreadsheets and PDF guides much smoother. This reduces the "jitter" that can cause eye strain when you are quickly looking for a specific stat value in a large table.

### Why is open-back audio better for long sessions?
Open-back headphones allow air to pass through the ear cups, which prevents heat and moisture buildup. They also create a wider soundstage that feels less "inside your head," reducing the mental fatigue that comes from concentrated listening during 8+ hour gaming marathons.

### Is the Razer Naga V2 Pro good for small hands?
The Naga V2 Pro is a medium-to-large mouse, and the 12-button side plate can be difficult to navigate for players with smaller hands. However, the interchangeable side plates include a 2-button and 6-button option, which are much easier to handle if the full grid feels overwhelming.
