## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/hsr-relic-farming-guide-2026/inline-5.webp)


Operating The Herta at peak efficiency requires more than just hitting the "Auto-Battle" button. Her damage is conditional on enemy health states and Memosprite timing.

### Standard Combat Sequence
1.  **Setup Phase:** Use Sunday’s Skill to boost The Herta’s Action Value and DMG.
2.  **Threshold Trigger:** Use The Herta’s Skill to hit as many enemies as possible. If any enemy falls below the 50% HP threshold, her follow-up attack will trigger.
3.  **Memosprite Management:** In the Amphoreus region, ensure your Memosprite is active before casting The Herta’s Ultimate. This maximizes the buffs from the BananAmusement Park ornament set.
4.  **Ultimate Timing:** Save The Herta’s Ultimate for the start of a new wave or immediately after Robin activates her Concerto. This ensures the maximum number of hits are amplified by support buffs.

### Advanced Energy Management
Managing Skill Points (SP) is crucial when running high-speed supports like **Yao Guang**. Yao Guang is SP-positive, which allows The Herta to use her Skill every turn. Sunday’s Ultimate should be timed to "refill" The Herta’s energy bar right after she casts her Ultimate, potentially allowing for back-to-back Ice nukes in a single Action Value cycle.

**Is the 'Eagle of Twilight Line' set still worth farming in 2026?**
No. While the Action Forward mechanic was niche for certain 0-cycle builds in the past, the newer Scholar and Sacerdos sets provide significantly higher raw stat value and synergy with 4.x mechanics. For non-whales, farming Eagle is considered a "trap" that yields diminishing returns compared to the Path of the Cloud-Hymn.

**How to use Self-Modeling Resin efficiently for Energy Regeneration Ropes?**
In Version 4.2, Energy Regeneration Rate (ERR) Ropes for the **Arcadia** or **Bananamusement** sets are the highest priority. The Herta requires high Ultimate uptime to maintain her Scholar stacks, making an ERR rope almost mandatory if you aren't running a dedicated battery like Sunday or Huohuo.

**What is the best way to spend triple drops during the 'Realm of the Strange' event?**
Always prioritize the Path of the Cloud-Hymn. Because this cavern drops both the best DPS set (Scholar) and the best Support set (Sacerdos), it has the highest "per-stamina" value in the game. Even "bad" rolls on Sacerdos can often be used as off-set pieces for high-speed supports.

**Can The Herta clear Apocalyptic Shadow difficulty A8 without her signature weapon?**
Yes, but it requires a highly optimized Sunday or Robin. Without "Into the Unreachable Veil," you will need to compensate for the missing Crit DMG through superior substat rolls on your Scholar relics. Achieving the 180 SPD breakpoint is more important for clearing A8 than having the signature weapon itself.
