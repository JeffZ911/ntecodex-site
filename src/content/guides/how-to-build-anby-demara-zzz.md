## Overview

![Overview](/img/how-to-build-anby-demara-zzz/inline-1.webp)


Anby Demara is a high-mobility Stun specialist who utilizes Electric-infused sword strikes to accumulate Daze. Unlike Attack agents who focus on raw numbers, Anby’s value is measured by how quickly she can fill the yellow bar beneath an enemy's health. She is remarkably F2P-friendly, as every player receives her for free during the prologue, and her kit remains competitive even in late-game Hollow Zero or Shiyu Defense stages.

Her Core Passive is the heart of her gameplay. It significantly boosts the Daze dealt by her "Thunderbolt" attack—a special move triggered by using her Special Attack immediately after the third hit of her Basic Attack combo. Mastering this timing is the difference between a mediocre Anby and a professional Stunner.

### Skill Priority List
To optimize your resources, focus on upgrading Anby's skills in the following order:
1. **Basic Attack:** This is her primary method of generating Daze and the foundation of her Thunderbolt combo.
2. **Special Attack:** Increases the damage and Daze scaling of her Thunderbolt and EX Special Attack.
3. **Core Passive:** Directly improves her Daze multipliers and provides essential stat boosts.
4. **Dodge:** Enhances her Dodge Counter, which is vital for maintaining pressure.
5. **Chain Attack / Ultimate:** These provide high burst but are less important for her primary role as a Stunner.

### Anby's Role in the Meta
In the current Zenless Zone Zero meta, Anby sits as the premier accessible Stunner. While S-Rank characters like Von Lycaon or Koleda Belobog might offer higher Daze ceilings or specific faction synergies, Anby’s Electric attribute allows her to fit into Mono-Electric teams or Cunning Hares compositions with ease. She is often used to "bridge" the gap until a player pulls a premium Stunner, but with high Cinema Mindscape investment, she can easily clear the game's hardest content.

### Core Mechanics: Thunderbolt and Daze
Anby’s gameplay revolves around the **Thunderbolt** mechanic. Her Basic Attack combo consists of four hits. However, if you press the Special Attack button after the third hit (the one where she performs a wide horizontal slash), she will skip her fourth Basic hit and instead perform an upward Electric strike called Thunderbolt. This move deals significantly more Daze than her standard combo. When her Energy is full, this becomes the **High Voltage Thunderbolt**, which provides invulnerability frames (i-frames) and massive Electric Daze.

Choosing the right W-Engine for Anby requires balancing two stats: Impact and Energy Regen. Impact directly increases the Daze she deals with every hit, while Energy Regen ensures she can frequently use her EX Special Attack to sustain her combo and grant her safety through i-frames.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/how-to-build-anby-demara-zzz/inline-5.webp)


Playing Anby effectively requires more than just button mashing. You must respect the rhythm of her sword swings to trigger her Daze multipliers.

### Mastering the Thunderbolt Combo
The most efficient way to play Anby is the "3-Hit Combo" into Special Attack.
1. Perform **three** Basic Attacks. Anby will perform two quick slashes followed by a larger horizontal swing.
2. Immediately after the third hit connects, press the **Special Attack** button.
3. Anby will perform **Thunderbolt**, jumping into the air and slamming her sword down for massive Daze.
4. If your Energy bar is glowing, this will automatically become **High Voltage Thunderbolt**, which deals even more Daze and grants you invulnerability during the animation.

### Optimal Chain Attack Entry
Once the enemy's Daze bar reaches 100%, Anby will trigger a Chain Attack. 
*   If you are fighting a boss, try to trigger the Stun using Anby's **EX Special Attack**. This ensures she contributes the maximum possible Daze and damage before the Chain Attack sequence begins.
*   In the Chain Attack UI, always swap to your Support (like Nicole) first to apply debuffs, and end the chain with your primary DPS (like Billy or Anton) to maximize the damage window.

### Energy Management and EX Special Timing
Do not waste your EX Special Attack on small enemies. Save your energy for Elite enemies and Bosses. Because Anby’s EX Special (High Voltage Thunderbolt) has significant i-frames, you can use it to "tank" through an enemy's red-flash attack while simultaneously dealing heavy Daze. This allows you to stay aggressive without needing to dodge away and reset your combo.

## FAQ

![FAQ](/img/how-to-build-anby-demara-zzz/inline-6.webp)


### Is Anby better than Lycaon?
Von Lycaon is generally considered the superior Stunner due to his higher Daze multipliers and Ice resistance shred. However, Anby is easier to play and is essential for Electric-weak enemies. If you are running an Ice DPS like Ellen Joe, Lycaon is better. If you are running Anton or Billy, Anby is a fantastic choice.

### What is the best F2P W-Engine for Anby?
The **Steam Oven** is the best craftable F2P option. It provides a reliable Impact boost that scales with your energy. If you have the "Demara Battery Mark II" from gacha pulls, use that instead as it is her signature weapon.

### How do I unlock Anby's Cinema Mindscape upgrades?
Cinema Mindscapes are unlocked by obtaining duplicate copies of Anby through the Signal Search (gacha) system. As an A-Rank character, she frequently appears as a featured rate-up unit on Limited Banners, making it relatively easy to reach her maximum Mindscape (M6) over time.

### Cinema Mindscape Priority List
*   **M1 (Rapid Charging):** Increases Energy Regen when hitting with the 3rd hit of her combo or Thunderbolt. This is a huge quality-of-life upgrade.
*   **M2 (Power Surge):** Increases Daze dealt by Thunderbolt if the enemy is Shocked. Great for Mono-Electric teams.
*   **M4 (Lightning Conduct):** Provides energy to Electric teammates when Anby uses Thunderbolt. Excellent for supporting Anton.
*   **M6 (Overcurrent Flow):** Significantly buffs her damage and energy generation after using Thunderbolt. This turns Anby into a "Sub-DPS" who can deal respectable damage.

### Should I build Anby for Damage or Stun?
Always prioritize **Stun (Impact)**. While Anby can deal decent damage at M6, her primary job is to get the enemy into the Stunned state as fast as possible so your main DPS can do the heavy lifting. A "DPS Anby" is usually less efficient for clearing hard content than a "Stun Anby."
