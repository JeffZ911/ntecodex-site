Nanally is currently the definitive benchmark for Anima damage in Hethereau. In the context of daily quests, Nanally shines because her Ultimate summons an "Underboss" that mirrors her attacks, providing autonomous damage that cleans up stragglers while you focus on the primary objective.

The daily quest system in NTE is divided into three primary pillars: **Participation Tasks**, **Circle Bounties**, and the **City Tycoon** activities. By using a Nanally-centered build, you can complete the combat-related portions of these tasks—such as clearing Anomaly Zones or defeating overworld thugs—in a fraction of the time required by other units.

### Why Nanally for Daily Quests?
Nanally’s primary strength for daily content is her "Ichi-daime's Authority" state. Activated by her skill, this state grants her a 30% Crit DMG bonus and allows her to ignore gravity, making her incredibly mobile for urban traversal. When paired with a Cosmos support like Zero, she triggers the Blossom reaction, which spawns autonomous "Vita Buds" that fire at enemies automatically. This "set and forget" damage style is ideal for clearing waves of daily mobs.

### Signature Weapon: Ready-Ready
"Ready-Ready" is Nanally’s signature S-Class Arc and her undisputed Best-in-Slot (BiS) option. It provides a massive 24% Crit Rate substat, which is vital for reaching the 85% Crit Rate threshold required for endgame consistency.

*   **Passive Effect:** Increases ATK by 15%. When casting a Redirect Skill or Ultimate, Basic Attack and Critical Riposte damage increase by 15% for 15 seconds (up to 2 stacks). 
*   **Unique Mechanic:** Obtaining both the "Left Tiger Talisman" (from Skill) and "Right Tiger Talisman" (from Ultimate) within 15 seconds unlocks the "Commander Tiger Talisman," increasing damage against Bosses by 10%.

2.  **Raging Flames (F2P S-Rank):** Obtained by completing the "Ride Assault" and "Bridge Crossings" anomaly commissions. It offers a 24% Crit Rate substat and boosts Lakshana/Anima damage after an Ultimate.

## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/neverness-to-everness-daily-quest-guide-nanally-build/inline-5.webp)


Maximizing Nanally’s damage involves a specific loop to ensure her "Ichi-daime's Authority" and "Underboss" are always active during your burst window.

### The Blossom Burst Combo
1.  **Start with Zero:** Use Zero's skill to instantly fill the team's Esper Meter.
2.  **Switch to Nanally:** Swapping to Nanally while the meter is full triggers her Support Skill, which immediately initiates a Blossom reaction.
3.  **Activate Skill:** Use Nanally's skill ("Colucci Howling Technique") to enter the Ichi-daime's Authority state. This grants her a 30% Crit DMG buff.
4.  **Cast Ultimate:** Use her Ultimate to summon the Underboss. For the next 6 seconds, the Underboss will mirror every attack Nanally makes.
5.  **Basic Attack Chain:** Perform three standard basic attacks followed by a charged attack (Hold Basic). Repeat this until the Underboss expires.

### Managing the Underboss
The Underboss is your best friend for daily clearing. It has a small gravity-well effect that pulls enemies toward Nanally. To maximize efficiency, try to position yourself in the center of a mob wave before casting your Ultimate. This ensures the Underboss’s AoE hits the maximum number of targets.

**Tip for Character Pixels:** 
During daily farming, you may need to use "Minty Whirlwind" (if using Mint) or Nanally's dash attacks to move between packs. Be mindful of your Character Pixels (Stamina); running out of stamina during a rotation will cancel Nanally’s Ichi-daime state, significantly lowering your DPS.
