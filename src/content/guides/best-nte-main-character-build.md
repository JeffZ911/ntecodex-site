## Rotation

![Rotation](/img/best-nte-main-character-build/inline-5.webp)


Mastering the rotation is what separates a casual player from a top-tier NTE pilot. Zero’s rotation is designed to maximize the uptime of the Instant Cycle state and ensure that your Main DPS or Zero themselves can burst during the "Break" window.

### Standard Combat Loop

1.  **Opener:** Start with Zero’s Skill. This immediately triggers the "Instant Cycle" and begins filling the party's Esper Meters.
2.  **Setup:** Swap to your Support (e.g., Haniel or Sakiri). Apply buffs to the party and inflict elemental statuses on the enemies.
3.  **Application:** Bring in your Sub-DPS (e.g., Hotori). Use their off-field abilities or summons to ensure constant elemental application.
4.  **Burst:** Once the enemy's Break gauge is low, switch back to Zero or your primary damage dealer (e.g., Nanally). Unleash your Ultimate to shatter the gauge and deal massive damage during the vulnerable window.
5.  **Reset:** Use Zero’s basic attacks during the Break window to finish off the Esper Meter refill and prepare to restart the loop.

### Maximizing the Esper Cycle

The goal is to never have Zero on the field without the Instant Cycle active unless you are specifically waiting for a cooldown. If the Esper Meter is empty, use Zero’s "Resonant Dodge" to trigger a perfect dodge, which provides a significant boost to energy regeneration. This allows you to get back into the cycle faster without relying solely on the Skill's cooldown.

### Ultimate Timing and Break Windows

Do not waste Zero’s Ultimate on enemies with full Break gauges unless you are certain it will trigger the Break. The Ultimate deals significantly more damage to enemies in the "Shattered" state. Timing the Ultimate to hit just as the gauge breaks will result in a damage multiplier that can bypass even the most difficult phase transitions in boss fights.
