## Effect

![Effect](/img/best-closed-back-headphones-competitive-mmo-players-under-200/inline-2.webp)


The true power of Blow up the Crowd lies in its unique passive, which allows it to function as both a personal damage booster and a team-wide utility tool.

### The 'Beat King' Passive Explained

The signature effect of these "headphones" is known as Beat King. This passive is split into two distinct parts:

1.  **Off-field Utility:** When the character wearing Blow up the Crowd is swapped out, the active character on the field receives a flat 10% ATK buff. This makes the weapon incredible for sub-DPS characters or supports who only stay on the field for a short time to cast their abilities.
2.  **On-field Buff:** While the wearer is on the field, they receive an immediate 12% increase to their Psyche DMG. Furthermore, every Basic Attack landed by the wearer grants an additional 2% damage stack. These stacks can be triggered every 2 seconds and can stack up to 10 times, leading to a massive total damage increase during prolonged fights.

### Mixing Levels and Refinement Bonuses

Mixing (the game's version of weapon refinement) allows players to fuse duplicate copies of the Arc to enhance the Beat King passive. For competitive players, reaching Mixing Level 5 is the goal, as it significantly boosts the percentages provided by the weapon.

### Off-Field vs. On-Field Performance

In a competitive environment, rotation efficiency is key. Blow up the Crowd is unique because it rewards both styles of play. For a main DPS who stays on the field, the stacking mechanism ensures that their damage ramps up as the fight continues. ## How to Get

![How to Get](/img/best-closed-back-headphones-competitive-mmo-players-under-200/inline-4.webp)


One of the reasons Blow up the Crowd is so highly recommended is that it is surprisingly accessible for a top-tier S-Rank weapon.

### Unlocking the Rhythm Master Commission

The most reliable way to get your first copy of this weapon for free is through the Rhythm Master Commission. This is a special questline located in **Illusion Town**. To unlock it, players must reach a specific Urban Level and complete the prerequisite story chapters associated with the district's anomalies. Once unlocked, the commission tasks the player with a series of rhythm-based challenges and combat encounters that culminate in a final confrontation.

### Beat King Boss Strategy Guide

The final step of the Rhythm Master Commission involves defeating the Beat King boss in Illusion Town. This anomaly uses sound-based attacks that can disorient the player if they aren't careful. 

*   **Phase 1:** The boss sends out circular shockwaves. You must jump or dash through these to avoid taking heavy damage.
*   **Phase 2:** The boss creates "Sound Zones" on the ground. Staying inside these will drain your energy, so constant movement is required.
*   **Weakness:** The Beat King is particularly vulnerable to Psyche-type damage. Using Haniel or other Psyche characters will allow you to break the boss's posture bar much faster, leading to a massive damage window.

Defeating this boss for the first time guarantees a drop of the Blow up the Crowd Arc, making it a "must-do" for any competitive player.

### Gacha and Pity Mechanics

Beyond the free copy, additional copies for Mixing can be obtained through the Miracle Box Gacha. It is part of the standard pool, meaning it can appear on both the standard and limited Arc banners. 

For those who want to guarantee their refinements, the **Arc Research Program** allows players to target specific S-Rank weapons. Under this system, you are guaranteed an S-Rank Arc within 60 pulls, and you can set your "Path" to prioritize Blow up the Crowd, ensuring you don't waste pulls on gear you don't need.
