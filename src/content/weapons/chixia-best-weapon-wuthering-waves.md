### Primary Stats for Main DPS Chixia

Chixia’s stat priority should always follow this hierarchy: **Crit Rate > Crit DMG > Fusion DMG% > ATK% > Energy Regeneration**.

The reason Crit Rate takes precedence is due to the nature of her Forte Circuit. During her "DAKA DAKA" state, Chixia fires 30 bullets in rapid succession. If your Crit Rate is low (e.g., 30%), only about 9 of those bullets will deal critical damage. By pushing Crit Rate toward 60% or 70% with weapons like **Static Mist**, you ensure that the majority of her burst window is maximized. 

Critical Damage is the second most important stat, as it scales the ceiling of her Fusion damage. Since Chixia gains some ATK% from her inherent leveling nodes and potential Echo substats, weapons that provide offensive utility or specific damage type bonuses (like Skill DMG) are generally more valuable than those providing flat ATK alone.

### Base ATK vs. Substat Scaling

In Wuthering Waves, the gap between 5-star and 4-star weapons is significant due to the Base ATK difference. A Level 90 **Static Mist** boasts 587 Base ATK, whereas most 4-star pistols like **Undying Flame** sit at 412. Because all ATK% bonuses from Echoes and passives are calculated based on this Base ATK, the 5-star option provides a much higher "floor" for your total damage.

However, a Rank 5 (R5) 4-star weapon can often compete with a Rank 1 (R1) 5-star weapon in very specific scenarios. For Chixia, an R5 **Undying Flame** provides a massive 40% Resonance Skill DMG bonus, which directly buffs her primary source of damage. While the 5-star weapon will still offer higher total ATK, the specialized bonus of the 4-star can make it a very close second for players who haven't pulled on the weapon banner.

## Effect

A weapon’s passive effect is just as important as its raw numbers. For Chixia, the goal is to find effects that trigger upon using an Intro Skill or Resonance Skill, as her rotation typically involves swapping in, using her Intro Skill to gain Forte stacks, and immediately entering her "DAKA DAKA" state.

### Synergy with Forte Circuit (DAKA DAKA!)

Chixia’s Forte Circuit, **Tenacity**, is the core of her gameplay. When she has at least 30 "Thermite" stacks, holding the Resonance Skill button enters this state. The damage dealt during this time is considered Resonance Skill DMG.

*   **Static Mist:** While the passive focuses on Energy Regeneration and a supportive ATK buff for the next character, the raw Crit Rate substat is the real draw. It allows Chixia to focus her Echo substats on Crit DMG and ATK%, leading to a more balanced and powerful build.
*   **Undying Flame:** This is widely considered Chixia's "signature" 4-star weapon. The passive grants a 20% Resonance Skill DMG bonus (at Rank 1) for 15 seconds after she performs her Intro Skill. Since Chixia almost always enters the field via Intro Skill to maintain her rotation, this buff has nearly 100% uptime during her most important damage windows.
*   **Novaburst:** This weapon rewards a high-skill playstyle. It grants ATK stacks when the user performs a successful Dodge or Dodge Counter. Given Chixia’s mobility and the need to reposition while firing, this can be effective, but it is less consistent than the "fire and forget" nature of Undying Flame.

### Energy Regeneration and Rotation Uptime

Chixia’s Resonance Liberation, **Blazing Flames**, deals massive AoE Fusion damage and is an essential part of her burst. However, she can sometimes struggle with Energy Regeneration if her field time is cut short. 

**Static Mist** provides 12.8% Energy Regen at base, which helps Chixia get her Ultimate back faster without sacrificing offensive Echo slots for Energy stats. If you find your rotations are stalled because Chixia’s Ultimate isn't ready, switching to a weapon with Energy Regen or looking for more ER substats on her Echoes is necessary. Generally, Chixia needs around 120%–130% Energy Regeneration to feel smooth in a standard team featuring Mortefi or Verina.
