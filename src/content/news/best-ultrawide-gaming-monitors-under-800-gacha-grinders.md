# Best Ultrawide Gaming Monitors Under $800 for Gacha Grinders: Neverness to Everness Edition

The Alienware AW3423DWF and Gigabyte M34WQ are the top ultrawide picks under $800 for NTE players, offering the high contrast and color depth needed to make Hesperia's supernatural neon aesthetic pop. ## What Happened

![What Happened](/img/best-ultrawide-gaming-monitors-under-800-gacha-grinders/inline-1.webp)


The launch of Neverness to Everness on April 29, 2026, marked a significant shift in the urban open-world RPG landscape. Developed by Hotta Studio using Unreal Engine 5, the title immediately drew millions of players into the supernatural city of Hesperia. However, the scale of the city and its dense verticality quickly exposed the limitations of traditional widescreen monitors, pushing the community toward 21:9 and 32:9 aspect ratios to better capture the "urban supernatural" atmosphere.

### The Hesperia Immersion Factor

Hesperia is designed with a focus on both high-speed traversal and intricate street-level detail. The official reveal trailer showcased a world where players transition seamlessly from car customization shops to high-stakes combat against anomalies. This immersion is heavily dependent on field of view (FOV). Ultrawide monitors allow players to see the peripheral neon signage and looming skyscrapers that define the Hesperia aesthetic, which are often cut off on 16:9 screens.

The recent Misty Tipsy Style banner featuring the character [Hotori](/characters/hotori-best-team-nte) further emphasized this need. Hotori’s combat kit relies on time-manipulation effects that ripple across the battlefield. On a standard monitor, players often lose track of these visual cues during intense encounters. A 21:9 display provides the necessary screen real estate to keep Hotori’s Anima effects within view while tracking multiple enemies.

### Initial Launch Resolution Hurdles

Despite the visual fidelity, the 1.0 launch was not without technical friction. Many players reported that the settings menu lacked native support for common ultrawide resolutions like 3440x14400 or the super-ultrawide 5120x1440. This led to a surge in community-driven fixes to bypass the software caps.

The introduction of the new S-Class character, **Lacrimosa** (Chaos/Liquid type), serves as a benchmark for modern display hardware. Lacrimosa’s abilities involve sprawling area-of-effect (AoE) puddles and liquid-based "Chaos" geometry that can cover a significant portion of the arena. ## Player Reactions

![Player Reactions](/img/best-ultrawide-gaming-monitors-under-800-gacha-grinders/inline-3.webp)


The community response to NTE’s visual demands has been a mix of technical ingenuity and debate over "competitive" advantages. On the [r/NevernesstoEverness](https://www.reddit.com/r/NevernesstoEverness/) subreddit, users have shared extensive guides on how to modify the game's `Engine.ini` files to force custom resolutions.

### The Community Fix Era

"Finally seeing the full width of the Hesperia docks without the black bars makes this feel like a completely different game," one top-voted Reddit comment reads. Another user pointed out that "The Scarborough Fair gacha board looks incredible on 21:9; you can actually see the entire dice-roll path without the screen scrolling constantly."

However, not all feedback is positive. Players on 32:9 "Super Ultrawide" monitors have expressed frustration with the placement of UI elements. Even with the 1.1 optimizations, some claim that the health bars are located too far in their peripheral vision, making it difficult to monitor stats during high-risk anomaly hunts.

### The 'Pay-to-See' Advantage in Hesperia

A debate has emerged regarding whether ultrawide monitors provide a "pay-to-see" advantage in the City Tycoon hobbies and competitive anomaly hunts.

**Pros and Cons of 21:9 vs 32:9 for Gacha Grinding**

> **21:9 Aspect Ratio (The Sweet Spot)**
> *   **Pros:** Better peripheral vision in combat; fits most desk setups; native support in most modern patches.
> *   **Cons:** Slightly less vertical FOV than 16:9 in some non-optimized titles.
>
> **32:9 Aspect Ratio (The Enthusiast Choice)**
> *   **Pros:** Total immersion in Hesperia’s cityscapes; massive advantage in tracking multiple targets.
> *   **Cons:** Requires high-end GPUs (RTX 4080+); UI elements can be hard to see; higher risk of "fish-eye" distortion.
