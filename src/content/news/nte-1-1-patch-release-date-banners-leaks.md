### The Porsche Collaboration and Vehicle Monetization

One of the most surprising announcements for the 1.1 update is the official collaboration with Porsche. This partnership marks a shift in how NTE handles its urban lifestyle elements. Starting in Version 1.1, a dedicated Porsche dealership will open its doors in the Central Business District of Hethereau.

The centerpiece of this collaboration is the Porsche 918 Spyder. Unlike standard vehicles found throughout the city, the 918 Spyder is a high-performance licensed vehicle that players can acquire and customize. This introduces a new layer of monetization to the game. While characters remain the primary draw for the gacha system, vehicles like the Porsche 918 Spyder will offer premium cosmetic and performance upgrades. 

Players can visit the dealership to test drive the car, customize its livery, and even participate in time-trial challenges around the city. Hotta Studio has clarified that while these cars offer superior handling and speed for city traversal, they do not provide combat advantages, keeping the "power" monetization focused on the esper roster.

### Quality of Life: Housing and Mini-game Improvements

The housing system, a core part of the "Everness" side of the game, is receiving a major overhaul in 1.1. Players have previously noted that the furniture placement was somewhat restrictive and lacked interactive depth. The update will introduce "Hethereau Hobbies," a series of mini-games and activities that can be performed within player-owned apartments.

**List of Housing System Optimizations:**
*   **Free-Grid Placement:** Removal of the strict snapping tool to allow for more creative furniture arrangements.
*   **Interactive Furniture:** Items like the arcade cabinet and the music player will now be fully functional.
*   **Pet System Integration:** Players can now house and interact with "Red Jelly" variants and other non-hostile anomalies found in the world.
*   **Visitor Rewards:** Inviting friends to your housing instance will provide small amounts of currency and social experience points.
*   **Expanded Customization:** New wallpapers, flooring, and lighting options inspired by the ETD-4 and 6th Platoon aesthetics.

Finally, Hotta Studio is addressing the "AI Asset" controversy. During the 1.0 launch, several background environmental assets were flagged by the community as being AI-generated without proper manual refinement. In 1.1, the studio is beginning a rolling replacement program, swapping these assets for handcrafted models and textures to ensure the visual fidelity of Hethereau remains consistent.
