A recent thread on Reddit titled "Is 1.1 Too Much for Controllers?" has garnered over 500 upvotes, with many players complaining that the materialization mechanics of Lacrimosa are nearly impossible to manage on a standard controller. ### Why Raid Leaders are Ditching Controllers

Raid leaders, in particular, find the lack of buttons on standard peripherals a major hindrance. Coordinating a 12-man squad for Prime Circle requires the ability to quickly toggle voice channels, ping locations, and check the status of teammates. MMO mice allow these functions to be mapped to the thumb grid, keeping the left hand free for movement—a critical advantage when dodging the Corridor of Echoes bosses' area-of-effect attacks.

### Community Consensus: Is Hardware the New Meta?

Positive feedback has focused on the increased tactical depth that multi-button mice allow. Skilled players have demonstrated that with a 12-button side grid, they can manage 4-character squad rotations with near-perfect efficiency, often clearing Beyond the Rails stages significantly faster than those on standard setups. This has led to a community consensus that while a high-end mouse isn't required to enjoy the story, it is a "soft requirement" for those aiming for the top 1% of the leaderboards.
